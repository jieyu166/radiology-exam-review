# meningioma-recurrence

**Meningioma 復發風險因子包括 bone invasion、low ADC value、proximity to major sinuses、mushroom shape。年齡（age）不是復發風險因子。**

## Summary
- **Bone invasion**：復發風險增加 [^1]
- **Low ADC value**：提示高 cellularity/high grade [^1]
- **Proximity to major sinuses**：手術難以完全切除 [^1]
- **Mushroom shape**：higher grade 表現（narrow dural base + lobulated irregular outline） [^1]
- **Age 非風險因子** [^1]

[^1]: 2019 交換考詳解。
