# pyogenic-liver-abscess-ultrasound

**化膿性肝膿瘍超音波：variable echogenicity、irregular border、thick wall with vascularity on color Doppler。Posterior acoustic shadow 非典型表現（那是 solid lesion/calcification 特徵）。**

## Summary
- **Variable echogenicity**（因內容物不同）。[^1]
- **Well-defined to irregular border**。[^1]
- **Thick wall with vascularity** on color Doppler。[^1]
- **Posterior acoustic shadow → NOT typical**（為 solid lesion 特徵）。[^1]

### 參考來源
[^1]: 2019 交換考詳解；Ahuja A. Diagnostic Imaging: Ultrasound. Amirsys; 2007. p 1-42~1-47。
