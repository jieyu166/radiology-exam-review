# chronic-pulmonary-thromboembolism

**慢性肺栓塞 CT 徵象包括：鈣化血栓、transpleural systemic collateral vessels（如 intercostal arteries）、bilateral lower lobe peripheral linear opacities（= organized infarcts/fibrosis）。**

## Summary
- **Calcified thrombus** in pulmonary arteries [^1]
- **Transpleural systemic collateral vessels**（intercostal / internal mammary arteries）代償 [^1]
- **Multiple peripheral linear opacities** in lower lobes（organized pulmonary infarcts） [^1]
- 其他：mosaic perfusion、web/band in PA、PA enlargement、RV hypertrophy [^1]
