# synthetic-mammography

**Synthetic Mammography (SM) 由 DBT 資料重建，不需額外輻射劑量。優點：降低劑量、縮短檢查時間、提高鈣化/星芒邊緣/結構扭曲偵測。缺點：pseudocalcification false positive、motion artifact 評估困難。**

## Summary
- SM 由 DBT 重建，**不需額外 radiation exposure** [^1]
- 偽影：blurring subcutaneous tissue、axilla resolution loss (MLO)、pseudocalcifications、foreign body 附近解析度下降 [^1]
- 優點：reduced dose、shorter time vs combined FFDM+DBT、increased conspicuity of calcifications/speculated margins/architectural distortion [^1]
- 缺點：pseudocalcification false positives、motion artifact 評估困難 [^1]
