# pediatric-presacral-masses

**兒童薦前腫塊要點：sacrococcygeal germ cell tumor最常見為teratoma(多良性、非皆惡性)；anterior sacral meningocele是最常見的先天脊椎脊膜膨出，可為ASP/Currarino triad(anorectal malformation + sacrococcygeal osseous defect + presacral mass)之一部分；tailgut cyst可任何年齡(常30-60歲)非僅兒童；cystic lymphatic malformation呈均質高T2訊號(fluid-fluid level僅見於出血時)。本題正解B。**

## Summary
- **Sacrococcygeal germ cell tumor (SC-GCT)**：**最常見為teratoma(畸胎瘤，多behave as benign，少數惡性)**；最少見的SC-GCT為endodermal sinus tumor(EST，典型惡性)。故「SC-GCT are malignant germ cell tumors」為偽。[^1]
- **Anterior sacral meningocele（本題正解）**：**最常見的先天脊椎脊膜膨出**；可為**ASP/Currarino triad(anorectal malformation、sacrococcygeal osseous defect、presacral mass)**之一部分。[^1]
- **Tailgut cyst(retrorectal cystic hamartoma)**：**可於任何年齡發生(presentation常30-60歲)**，非僅兒童；多囊薄壁含黏液。[^1]
- **Cystic lymphatic malformation**：呈**均質高T2訊號、T1呈液體訊號**；**fluid-fluid level僅見於急性出血**（非常規表現，且訊號為均質非heterogeneous）。[^1]

> [!note] 考點：關於兒童薦前區病灶，何者正確？
> **Anterior sacral meningocele is most common type of congenital spinal meningocele, may occur as part of ASP triad（B）**。A(SC-GCT多為良性teratoma非皆惡性)、C(tailgut cyst可任何年齡)、D(cystic lymphatic malformation為均質高T2，fluid-fluid僅見於出血)皆為偽。[^1]
