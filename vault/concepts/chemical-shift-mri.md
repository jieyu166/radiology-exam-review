# chemical-shift-mri

**In-phase imaging 顯示 magnetic susceptibility artifact（iron、hemorrhage、metal、melanin），因水脂訊號同相位加總後更凸顯 susceptibility 效應；gross fat（macroscopic fat）不會在 in-phase 表現 susceptibility，而是在 opposed-phase 產生 India ink artifact（化學位移邊界）。**

## Summary
- In-phase susceptibility：iron deposition、hemorrhage、metal clips、melanin [^1]
- **Gross fat 不表現 in-phase susceptibility**（為干擾選項）[^1]
- Opposed-phase：偵測 intracellular/microscopic fat（signal drop）[^1]
