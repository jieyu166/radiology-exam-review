# bronchiolitis-imaging

**Bronchiolitis 為累及細支氣管（bronchioles）和/或肺泡管的發炎或纖維化；CT 幾乎總能顯示異常，包括 centrilobular micronodules、bronchial wall thickening、bronchiolar dilatation、mosaic attenuation；infectious bronchiolitis 為最常見類型。**

## Summary
- 定義：累及 bronchioles 和/或 alveolar ducts 的 inflammation/fibrosis [^1]
- CT：centrilobular micronodules、tree-in-bud、bronchial wall thickening、mosaic attenuation [^1]
- 最常見類型：**infectious bronchiolitis** [^1]

### 參考來源
[^1]: RadioGraphics 2017;37:778；Bronchiolitis: A Practical Approach.
