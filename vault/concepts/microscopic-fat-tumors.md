# microscopic-fat-tumors

**含微量脂肪（microscopic/intracellular fat）的腫瘤可在 chemical shift MRI 表現 signal drop on opposed-phase；腎上腺病灶：adrenal adenoma（最常見）、adrenocortical carcinoma、pheochromocytoma 皆可含 intracellular lipid；HCC 亦可含 intracellular fat。** 判讀分水嶺：**microscopic fat ≠ macroscopic fat——前者用 chemical shift（in/opposed phase）偵測，後者用 fat saturation 偵測；adrenal adenoma 的 signal drop > 20% (ASI) 高度提示良性，但 ACC 與 pheo 也可有少量 intracellular lipid。**

## Summary
- **偵測方法**：Chemical shift imaging（in-phase vs opposed-phase）偵測 intracellular/microscopic fat [^1]
- **腎上腺**：Adrenal adenoma（70% 含豐富 intracellular lipid）、adrenocortical carcinoma、pheochromocytoma 皆可含 microscopic fat [^1]
- **肝臟**：HCC 可含 intracellular fat，呈 opposed-phase signal drop [^1]
- **與 macroscopic fat 區別**：Macroscopic fat（如 AML、脂肪瘤）用 fat saturation 可見 signal drop；microscopic fat 用 chemical shift [^1]

### 參考來源
[^1]: RadioGraphics 2016;36:718；Schieda N et al. Update on CT and MRI of Adrenal Nodules. AJR 2017;208:1206-1217.
