# breast-mri-enhancement-patterns

**乳房 MRI 強化模式判讀：circumscribed mass with non-enhancing dark internal septation 提示良性（如 fibroadenoma）。Rim enhancement、enhancing internal septation、clumped/linear non-mass enhancement 均提示惡性。**

## Summary
- **良性提示**：circumscribed mass + **non-enhancing dark internal septation**（典型 fibroadenoma）。[^1]
- **惡性提示**：[^1]
  - **Rim enhancement** of solid mass（非典型囊腫時惡性率~40%）
  - **Enhancing internal septation**（強化隔膜通常為惡性特徵）
  - **Non-mass enhancement with clumped pattern**
  - **Non-mass enhancement with linear distribution**（high-grade DCIS）
  - Central enhancement → high-grade ductal cancer

### 參考來源
[^1]: 2019 交換考詳解；ACR BI-RADS ATLAS 2013, MRI, p14-15。
