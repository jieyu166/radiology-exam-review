# prostate-cancer-mri-treatment

**前列腺癌三大重點記憶：移行區癌MRI呈「erased charcoal」（均質低T2、邊界不清、低ADC）；Epstein主動監測納入標準為Gleason ≤6、≤2 cores、cancer involvement ≤50%；根治性切除術後最常見復發部位是膀胱尿道吻合處（vesicourethral anastomosis），非淋巴結或前列腺床。**

## Summary

### 移行區前列腺癌MRI特徵
- **T2WI表現**：**均質低訊號（"erased charcoal" sign）**、**邊界不清（ill-defined margins）**、缺乏包膜（lack of capsule）、呈**lenticular形狀**，可合併或不合併anterior fibromuscular stroma侵犯。[^1]
- **ADC**：**低ADC值**。[^1]
- **顯影時相**：**早期顯影（early enhancement）**。[^1]

### Active Surveillance納入標準（多系統比較）
| 系統 | Clinical stage | PSA level | PSA density | Gleason score | Amount of cancer |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Epstein/Johns Hopkins** | T1c | <10 ng/mL | <0.15 ng/mL | ≤6 | 1-2 positive cores; ≤50% cancer in any core |
| Sunnybrook | T1c/T2 | <15 ng/mL | -- | ≤6* | 個案評估 |
| PRIAS | T1c/T2 | ≤10 ng/mL | <0.2 ng/mL | ≤6 | 1-2 positive cores |

（*Sunnybrook族群歷史上可接受Gleason 3+4=7於精心篩選之個案）[^1]

- **Epstein inclusion criteria核心**：**Gleason score ≤6、two or fewer positive cores、PSA density ≤0.15 ng/mL、cancer involvement ≤50% of any core**。[^1]

### 根治性前列腺切除術後復發
- **最常見復發部位**：**Vesicourethral anastomosis（膀胱尿道吻合處）**——非lymph nodes、incision/port site、prostatic bed。[^1]
- **典型影像**：環繞膀胱及/或membranous urethra之**顯影軟組織結節**，T1WI與肌肉等訊號、T2WI較肌肉**略高訊號**。[^1]
- **最有幫助之序列**：**DCE（動態對比增強）與T2WI**，對偵測吻合處、anterolateral手術邊緣、精囊內、膀胱與直腸間之復發病灶最有幫助。[^1]

> [!note] 考點一：移行區前列腺癌之典型MRI特徵？
> **邊界不清、低T2訊號、早期顯影、低ADC（C）**——非well-defined、非high ADC。[^1]

> [!note] 考點二：Epstein主動監測納入標準？
> **Gleason ≤6、≤2 positive cores、cancer involvement ≤50%（D）**。[^1]

> [!note] 考點三：根治性前列腺切除術後最常見復發部位？
> **Vesicourethral anastomosis（C）**——DCE與T2WI為最佳偵測序列。[^1]

## 題目
> [!question]- What are the characteristics of a typical transitional zone prostate cancer? (2020-249)
> **Ill-defined, low T2 signal intensity, early enhancement, and low ADC（C）**。[^1]

> [!question]- Which of the following best describes the Epstein inclusion criteria for active surveillance? (2020-250)
> **Gleason score ≤6, two or fewer cores with cancer, and ≤50% cancer involvement of any core（D）**。[^1]

> [!question]- What is the most common site of recurrence after radical prostatectomy? (2020-251)
> **Vesicourethral anastomosis（C）**。[^1]
