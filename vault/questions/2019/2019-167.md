#交換 #2019交換 #Breast Which one is not the usually benign calcification of breast?
(A) pleomorphic type calcification
(B) milk of calcium
(C) suture calcification
(D) eggshell-type calcification
??
**Ans: A**
正確答案為 **A**。Pleomorphic（細多形）為可疑鈣化型態（BI-RADS 4），非良性。Milk of calcium、suture calcification、eggshell-type皆為典型良性鈣化型態。詳見 [[breast-calcifications]]。
