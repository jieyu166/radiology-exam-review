#交換 #2019交換 #MSK Concerning entrapment syndrome and associated nerve, which one is **false** as follow?
(A) Quadrilateral space syndrome - axillary nerve
(B) Pronator syndrome – radial nerve
(C) Cubital tunnel syndrome – ulnar nerve
(D) Kiloh-Nevin syndrome – anterior interosseous nerve
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**（錯誤敘述）。Pronator syndrome壓迫的是**median nerve**，並非radial nerve；最常見成因為median nerve在pronator teres肌兩頭之間受壓迫。Quadrilateral space syndrome-axillary nerve、cubital tunnel syndrome-ulnar nerve、Kiloh-Nevin syndrome-anterior interosseous nerve皆為正確配對。詳見 [[nerve-entrapment-syndromes]]。
