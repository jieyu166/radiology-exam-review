#交換 #2019交換 #CV Which of the following statements is **true** regarding coronary artery calcium scoring (CACS)?
(A) Coronary calcification frequently occurs in the absence of coronary atherosclerosis
(B) A CACS of zero excludes the presence of any coronary artery disease
(C) Extensive coronary calcification is associated with a low risk of myocardial infarction
(D) Repeat coronary calcium testing is not considered appropriate in patients with prior CACS>0
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。CACS 分數 **>0** 之病人依現行建議（如 SCCT 指引）並**不建議常規重複**鈣化分數檢測（重掃間隔建議主要針對 CACS=0 者，於數年後追蹤是否轉為 >0）；冠狀動脈鈣化通常反映動脈粥樣硬化存在，CACS=0 不能完全排除 CAD（尤其軟斑塊），而廣泛鈣化通常與較高（非低）心肌梗塞風險相關。詳見 [[calcium-score]]。
