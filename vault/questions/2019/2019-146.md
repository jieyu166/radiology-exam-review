#交換 #2019交換 #PED Which of the following is not correct about Moyamoya Disease?
(A) Progressive occlusion of the terminal segments of the intracranial internal carotid arteries and compensatory development of tortuous, dilated collateral networks.
(B) Moyamoya vessels depict signal voids within the basal ganglia and thalami.
(C) On T2WI, diffuse leptomeningeal collateral vessels may appear as sulcal hyperintensity ("ivy sign")
(D) SW imaging may depict microbleeding in 15%-44% of patients.
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**（錯誤敘述）。Ivy sign（軟腦膜側支血流之腦溝高訊號）應見於**FLAIR**序列，而非T2WI。終端頸內動脈進行性阻塞合併側支網形成、基底節/視丘之flow void訊號缺失、SWI微出血發生率15-44%等敘述皆正確。詳見 [[moyamoya]]。
