#交換 #2019交換 #NR Which of the following descriptions about cerebral proliferative angiopathy (CPA) is **WRONG**?
(A) Large, diffuse vascular lesion involving multiple vascular territories
(B) Transdural arterial supply is common
(C) Lower hemorrhage risk compared to typical AVM
(D) No normal brain tissue is present between the abnormal vessels
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**（錯誤敘述）。CPA 與典型 AVM 的關鍵差異在於 CPA **含有正常腦實質夾雜於異常血管之間（interposed normal brain parenchyma）**，典型 AVM 的 compact nidus 則無。A（大範圍瀰漫性）、B（transdural supply 常見）、C（出血風險較低）皆為正確描述。
