#交換 #2019交換 #PE Umbilical venous catheter (UVC) does NOT traverse which of the following structures?
(A) Ductus venosus
(B) Portal vein
(C) Portal sinus
(D) Inferior vena cava
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。UVC 正常路徑：umbilical vein → portal sinus → ductus venosus → IVC。Portal vein（右門脈分支等）不在正常路徑上，進入 portal vein 為 malposition。詳見 [[uvc-catheter-route]]。
