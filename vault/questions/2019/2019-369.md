#交換 #2019交換 #Breast 有關 vascular calcification，以下何者錯誤?
(A) Dense, linear, parallel, or "tram track-like" calcification
(B) Most common in postmenopausal women; When seen in younger premenopausal women, it is often in patients with diabetes or in those with underlying renal disease.
(C) In some women, this is a reversible process.
(D) The calcification may not simulating those occurring in DCIS, even when only a portion of the arterial wall is affected, or when small vessels are involved.
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**（錯誤敘述）。乳房血管鈣化在僅部分動脈壁鈣化或累及小血管時，**可能模擬 DCIS 鈣化**。選項 D 說「不會模擬」是錯誤的。A（tram track 型態）、B（停經後好發）、C（部分可逆）皆正確。
