#交換 #2019交換 #NR Which is not the risk factor of recurrence in meningiomas?
(A) Bone invasion
(B) Low ADC value
(C) Proximity to major sinuses
(D) Mushroom shape
(E) Age
??
**Ans: E**
正確答案為 **E**。Age 不是 meningioma 復發風險因子。Bone invasion、low ADC、proximity to sinuses、mushroom shape 皆為復發風險因子。詳見 [[meningioma-recurrence]]。
