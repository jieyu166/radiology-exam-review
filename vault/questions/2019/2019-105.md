#交換 #2019交換 #MSK 請問下列對於骨纖維發育不良 fibrous dysplasia(FD)的敘述何者錯誤?
(A) The radiographic appearance of monostotic form: the replacement of medullary bone by fibrous tissue leads to a loss of the trabecular pattern, giving the lesions a ground-glass, milky, or smoky appearance
(B) Malignant transformation is rare, with a frequency about < 1%.
(C) If FD involves the femoral neck, it can lead to the deformity called "shepherd's crook".
(D) The associated syndromes are known as Albright-McCune Syndrome and Jaffe-Campanacci syndrome.
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**（錯誤敘述）。FD 相關症候群為 **McCune-Albright syndrome**（皮膚咖啡牛奶斑、性早熟）與 **Mazabraud syndrome**（合併肌內黏液瘤）；**Jaffe-Campanacci syndrome 實為多發 non-ossifying fibroma（NOF）相關症候群，與 FD 無關**，為本題陷阱所在。單骨型 ground-glass 外觀、惡性轉化率 <1%、股骨頸受侵犯致 shepherd's crook 畸形等敘述皆正確。詳見 [[fibrous-dysplasia]]。
