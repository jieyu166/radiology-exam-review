#交換 #2019交換 #MSK Which of the statements about discoid meniscus is **WRONG**?
(A) It is much more commonly involved in the lateral meniscus than in the medial meniscus.
(B) It is seen in 1%-5% of knees
(C) Discoid meniscus is diagnosed when the body of the meniscus measures 5 mm or more on a midline coronal image.
(D) Discoid meniscus is diagnosed when three or more bow-tie shapes are identified on contiguous sagittal (4-mm-thick) images
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**。Discoid meniscus 的 MRI 冠狀面診斷標準為半月板體寬度 **≥15 mm**（非 5 mm）。選項 C 將門檻誤寫為 5 mm，為錯誤敘述。
