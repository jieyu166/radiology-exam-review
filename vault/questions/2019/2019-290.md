#交換 #2019交換 #GU Which of the following is NOT a typical image finding of ovarian mature cystic teratoma?
(A) High signal intensity on T1WI without fat saturation
(B) Shadowing on T2WI
(C) Wall calcifications on CT
(D) Fat-fluid level
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。T2WI shadowing（shading sign）為**子宮內膜異位瘤（endometrioma）**特徵（含鐵血黃素致 T2 訊號降低），非成熟囊性畸胎瘤。
