#交換 #2016交換 #2019交換 #ABD The "gut signature" is disrupted in which of the following diseases?
(A) Colon carcinoma
(B) Early Crohn's disease
(C) Ulcerative colitis
(D) Diverticulosis
??
**Ans: A**
正確答案為 **A**。Colon carcinoma 侵犯腸壁全層，破壞正常 gut signature（五層結構消失）。Early Crohn's、UC、diverticulosis 雖影響腸壁但仍保留基本層狀結構。詳見 [[bowel-wall-layers]]。
