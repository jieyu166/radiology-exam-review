#交換 #2019交換 #Neuro Which of the following intracranial lesions is not presented with high signal intensity at T1-weighted imaging?
(A) Meningeal Melanomatosis
(B) Epidermoid Cysts
(C) Intracranial teratoma
(D) Hepatic Encephalopathy
??
**Ans: **
Epidermoid cysts 在 T1 呈低訊號（類似 CSF），T2 高訊號，DWI 受限。與含脂肪成分的 dermoid 不同。
