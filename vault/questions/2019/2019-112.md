#交換 #2019交換 #NR Which is INCORRECT for multiple sclerosis?
(A) Most MS plaques are supratentorial and more than 50% occur in the posterior fossa.
(B) EBV virus, chemicals, smoking, diet and geographic variability all contribute to MS risk.
(C) Ependymal dot-dash sign
(D) Transient enhancement during active demyelination.
??
**Ans: A**
正確答案為 **A**（自相矛盾之錯誤敘述）。MS 斑塊絕大多數位於幕上（supratentorial，尤其腦室周圍），並非「超過50%位於後顱窩」。EBV/化學物質/抽菸/飲食/地理變異為風險因子、ependymal dot-dash sign、活動性去髓鞘期間短暫強化等敘述皆正確。詳見 [[multiple-sclerosis-imaging]]。
