#交換 #2019交換 #NR Which following statement is **false** in most patients with ischemic stroke:
(A) Non-contrast CT findings is more delayed than MR findings
(B) Findings on FLAIR images are positive 12-24 hours after onset of symptoms.
(C) High signal intensity is not usually seen at T2-weighted imaging until at least 8 hours after the initial ischemic insult.
(D) If parenchymal enhancement persists longer than 8-12 weeks, a diagnosis other than ischemic stroke should be sought.
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。FLAIR 影像在缺血性中風症狀發生後 **6-12 小時**即轉為陽性，而非 12-24 小時。DWI-FLAIR mismatch（DWI 陽性但 FLAIR 陰性）提示發病 <6 小時。選項 A、C、D 皆為正確敘述。
