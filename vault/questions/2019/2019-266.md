#交換 #2019交換 #NR Which one is **WRONG** about Fahr Disease?
(A) Bilateral symmetric calcifications in basal ganglia.
(B) The calcification is usually hyperintense on T1WI.
(C) There is no infantile form.
(D) Onset of clinical symptoms is typically 30-60 years.
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**（錯誤敘述）。Fahr disease 有文獻報告存在 infantile form，"no infantile form" 為不正確。A（雙側對稱基底核鈣化）、B（T1WI 高訊號）、D（30-60 歲發病）皆為正確敘述。詳見 [[fahr-disease]]。
