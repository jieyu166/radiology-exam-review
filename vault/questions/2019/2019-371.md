#交換 #2019交換 #MSK The in-phase and opposed-phase imaging of MRI can be used to characterize tissue composition. The following components show magnetic susceptibility on in-phase imaging, EXCEPT?
(A) Iron deposition in solid organ
(B) Hemorrhage
(C) Metal clips or implants
(D) Melanin
(E) Gross fat
??
**Ans: E**
正確答案為 **E**（Gross fat）。In-phase imaging 凸顯 magnetic susceptibility artifact：iron deposition(A)、hemorrhage(B)、metal(C)、melanin(D) 皆會。Gross fat（巨觀脂肪）不產生 in-phase susceptibility，而是在 opposed-phase 產生 India ink artifact（水脂界面化學位移）。
