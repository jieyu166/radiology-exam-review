#交換 #2019交換 #US 1 atmosphere (14.7 psi) = 101325 Pa, which is closest to:
(A) 10kPa
(B) 50kPa
(C) 75kPa
(D) 100kPa
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D（100kPa）**。1 atmosphere = 101,325 Pa = 101.325 kPa ≈ 100 kPa。詳見 [[ultrasound-physics-basics]]。
