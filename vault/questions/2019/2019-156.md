#交換 #2019交換 #NR Which of the following description about cerebral pseudoaneurysm is **wrong**?
(A) Focal arterial dilatation not contained by layers of normal arterial wall
(B) On DSA, contrast rapidly fills and empties the aneurysmal cavity.
(C) Trauma is the most common cause
(D) Treatment options include endovascular stenting and surgical occlusion
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**（錯誤敘述）。DSA上應呈延遲廓清（delayed washout），而非快速填充與排空。局部動脈擴張未被正常動脈壁包覆、外傷為最常見病因、治療含血管內支架與手術阻斷等敘述皆正確。詳見 [[cerebral-pseudoaneurysm]]。
