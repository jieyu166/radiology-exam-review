#交換 #2019交換 #ABD A large infiltrative hepatic mass demonstrating early enhancement and portal vein invasion is most consistent with which of the following?
(A) Cholangiocarcinoma
(B) Biliary cystadenoma
(C) Primary hepatic lymphoma
(D) Hepatocellular carcinoma
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**（肝細胞癌）。HCC 典型特徵為動脈期早期增強 (arterial enhancement) 合併門脈侵犯 (portal vein invasion/tumor thrombus)。Cholangiocarcinoma 通常延遲增強；lymphoma 通常低血管性；biliary cystadenoma 為囊性病灶。
