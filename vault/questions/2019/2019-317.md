#交換 #2019交換 #PED Regarding esophageal atresia, which of the following is correct?
(A) Associated with VACTERL and CHARGE associations
(B) Polyhydramnios with small or absent stomach bubble on prenatal US
(C) Presence of bowel gas indicates fistula status
(D) All of the above
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**（以上皆是）。EA 與 VACTERL/CHARGE 相關(A)、產前 polyhydramnios + 胃泡縮小/消失(B)、腹部有腸氣代表有 distal TEF(C) 皆正確。
