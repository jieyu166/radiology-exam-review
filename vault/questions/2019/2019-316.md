#交換 #2019交換 #PED Which of the following statements about pediatric hepatic neoplasms is INCORRECT?
(A) Hepatic neoplasms are the most common intraabdominal tumor in children
(B) Hepatoblastoma is the most common malignant hepatic tumor in children < 5 years
(C) AFP is elevated in hepatoblastoma
(D) HCC is more common in older children with underlying liver disease
??
**Ans: A**
正確答案為 **A**（錯誤敘述）。兒童最常見腹腔內腫瘤為**神經母細胞瘤 (neuroblastoma)**，非肝臟腫瘤。
