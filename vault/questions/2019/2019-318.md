#交換 #2019交換 #PED Regarding vesicoureteral reflux (VUR), which statement is INCORRECT?
(A) VCUG is the standard imaging for diagnosis and grading
(B) VUR grading ranges from I to V
(C) It is NOT necessary to evaluate bowel and bladder dysfunction
(D) BBD affects spontaneous resolution rate
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**（錯誤敘述）。評估 BBD 是 VUR 管理中**必要**的一環，BBD 存在會降低 VUR 自然緩解率。
