#交換 #2019交換 #CH 有關 bronchiolitis，以下何者正確?
(A) Bronchiolitis refers to inflammation and/or fibrosis involving airways, typically bronchioles and/or alveolar ducts.
(B) CT images, almost always demonstrate abnormal findings that include centrilobular micronodules, bronchial wall thickening, bronchiolar dilatation and mosaic attenuation.
(C) Infectious bronchiolitis is by far the most common type of bronchiolitis.
(D) All of the above.
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**（以上皆正確）。Bronchiolitis 累及細支氣管(A)；CT 幾乎必有異常(B)；infectious bronchiolitis 為最常見類型(C)。
