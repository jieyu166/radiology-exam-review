#交換 #2019交換 #GU Renal venous thrombosis is sometimes seen in radiological studies. Which of the following statement regarding to the cause of renal venous thrombosis is incorrect?
(A) Renal vein thrombosis in adults usually is due to a hypercoagulable state, most commonly nephrotic syndrome.
(B) In children, it is usually associated with sepsis and dehydration
(C) Neoplastic invasion of the renal vein is more common in metastatic renal tumors than in primary renal tumors.
(D) Mechanical compression of renal vein from abscess, tumor, lymphadenopathy, and aneurysm will cause renal venous thrombosis.
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**（錯誤敘述）。腫瘤性腎靜脈侵犯在**原發性腎腫瘤（RCC）**比轉移性腎腫瘤更常見，選項 C 敘述反了。
