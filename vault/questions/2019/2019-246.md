#交換 #2019交換 #US Which of the following is NOT a typical ultrasound finding of cholelithiasis?
(A) Posterior acoustic shadow
(B) Echogenic foci
(C) Comet-tail artifact
(D) Gravity dependent movement
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C（Comet-tail artifact）**。Comet-tail artifact 為 adenomyomatosis 的特徵，非膽結石典型表現。膽結石典型三聯徵：echogenic foci + posterior acoustic shadow + gravity-dependent movement。詳見 [[cholelithiasis-ultrasound]]。
