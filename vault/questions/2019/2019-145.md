#交換 #2019交換 #PED Which of the following is not correct about Dandy-Walker Malformation?
(A) The most common posterior fossa malformation and typically occurs only sporadically, with a low overall risk of recurrence (1%-5%).
(B) The majority of patients with DWM present before 1 year of age with signs and symptoms of increased intracranial pressure.
(C) The key neuroimaging features of DWM are hypoplasia of the cerebellar vermis and dilatation of the cystic-appearing fourth ventricle.
(D) Microcephaly is the most common manifestation, affecting 90%-100% of children during the first months of life.
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**（錯誤敘述）。DWM最常見表現為**巨頭畸形（macrocephaly）**，並非小頭畸形（microcephaly）。最常見後顱窩畸形、多偶發性且復發風險低（1-5%）、多數1歲前以顱內壓升高表現、關鍵影像特徵為小腦蚓部發育不全與囊狀擴大第四腦室等敘述皆正確。詳見 [[dandy-walker-malformation]]。
