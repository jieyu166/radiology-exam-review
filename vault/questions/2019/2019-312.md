#交換 #2019交換 #CH Which choice is **false** about mediastinal and pleural MR imaging?
(A) Early arterial phase images are critical for detection of enhancement of mediastinal soft-tissue lesions.
(B) Lesions that demonstrate foci of enhancement, whether nodular, circumferential, septate, diffuse, or heterogeneous, cannot be considered benign mediastinal cysts by their MR appearance.
(C) The two key MR imaging features that can be used to direct biopsy are areas of enhancement and diffusion restriction.
(D) Enhancement within a thrombus should be considered diagnostic of tumor and is best identified in the portal venous phase.
(E) Signal intensity index was recently shown to be of benefit in thoracic MR imaging for evaluating thymic hyperplasia.
??
**Ans: A**
正確答案為 **A**（錯誤敘述）。縱膈腔軟組織病灶的增強偵測不依賴早期動脈相 — 延遲相更重要。B/C/D/E 皆為正確敘述。SII = [(tSIin - tSIop) / tSIin] × 100%。
