#交換 #2019交換 #MSK 下列何種疾病和 fibrous dysplasia 無關?
(A) McCune-Albright syndrome
(B) Mounier-Kuhn syndrome
(C) Mazabraud's syndrome
(D) Activating mutations in the GNAS1 gene
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。Mounier-Kuhn syndrome（氣管支氣管擴大症）為獨立疾病實體，與fibrous dysplasia無關。McCune-Albright syndrome、Mazabraud's syndrome、GNAS1基因活化突變皆與FD相關。詳見 [[fibrous-dysplasia]]。
