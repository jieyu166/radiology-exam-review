#交換 #2019交換 #US Which of the following types of tissue would have the lowest mean propagation velocity?
(A) Air
(B) Fat
(C) Blood
(D) Bone
??
**Ans: A**
正確答案為 **A（Air, 330 m/sec）**。超音波傳播速度：air(330) < fat(1450) < water(1480) < liver(1550) < kidney(1560) < blood(1570) < muscle(1580) < bone(4080 m/s)。詳見 [[ultrasound-physics-basics]]。
