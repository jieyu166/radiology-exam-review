#交換 #2019交換 #Neuro Which of the following entity is associated with cytotoxic lesions of the corpus callosum (CLOCCs)?
(A) Antiseizure drugs
(B) Diffuse axonal injury
(C) Meningoencephalitis
(D) Hepatic encephalopathy
(E) All of above
??
**Ans: E**
正確答案為 **E**。CLOCCs（cytotoxic lesions of the corpus callosum）可見於多種病因：抗癲癇藥物驟停（最經典）、瀰漫性軸索損傷、腦膜腦炎、肝性腦病變等，以上皆是。
