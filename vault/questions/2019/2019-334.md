#交換 #2019交換 #NR About MR Elastography, which one is **wrong**?
(A) Can detect brain stiffness
(B) An invasive MRI exam
(C) Edge effects, partial volume, CSF contamination need to be minimized
(D) In NPH, MRE showed increased brain stiffness in cerebrum, especially occipital lobe
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**（錯誤敘述）。MR Elastography 為**非侵入性 (non-invasive)** MRI 技術，利用外部振動源產生 shear wave 量測組織硬度。
