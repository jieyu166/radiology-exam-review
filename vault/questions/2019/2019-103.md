#交換 #2019交換 #MSK A 30-year-old male presents with a well-defined, osteolytic lesion at proximal tibia. The lesion is eccentric, extending to subarticular margin of tibia. What is the most likely diagnosis of this lesion?
(A) Osteoblastoma
(B) Giant cell tumor
(C) Ewing sarcoma
(D) Metastasis
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。骨骺閉合後（30歲成人）、長骨末端偏心、延伸至軟骨下關節面之界線清楚溶骨性病灶，為 giant cell tumor (GCT) 的典型表現。詳見 [[giant-cell-tumor]]。
