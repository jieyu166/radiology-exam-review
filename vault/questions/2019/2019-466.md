#交換 #2019交換 #CV 有關 coronary artery aneurysm(CAA)之敘述，下列何者為非?
(A) Atherosclerotic disease is the most common and accounts for at least 50% of CAAs diagnosed in adults.
(B) The most common vasculitis to cause acquired CAAs is Kawasaki disease.
(C) Congenital CAAs may be associated with other abnormalities, such as a coronary artery fistula.
(D) Mycotic CAAs are frequently associated with sepsis, bacteremia, or endocarditis, and Epstein-Barr virus is reported to be the most common cause.
??
**Ans: **
Mycotic CAAs 最常見的原因是細菌感染（bacterial agents），而非 Epstein-Barr virus。EBV 屬病毒感染，在免疫缺陷患者中可見，但並非 mycotic CAA 最常見病因。
