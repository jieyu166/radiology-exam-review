#交換 #2019交換 #GI 下列關於 closed-loop obstruction 敘述，何者錯誤?
(A) A segment of bowel that is obstructed at two points along its course
(B) Impair venous return resulting in ischemia
(C) Twist or volvulus due to peristalse around its narrow point of obstruction
(D) Most commonly, the closed loop is caused by defects in the mesentery or omentum
??
**Ans: **
Closed-loop obstruction 最常見原因是 adhesion（沾黏），而非 mesentery 或 omentum 的缺損。
