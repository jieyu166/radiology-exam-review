#交換 #2019交換 #CH 有關 pulmonary tuberculosis，以下何者錯誤?
(A) Mediastinal and hilar lymphadenopathy is the most common radiologic manifestation of primary tuberculosis.
(B) The cavitation pattern in both primary and post-primary tuberculosis demonstrate an upper lung zone predominance.
(C) Pleural effusion is less common in children.
(D) None of the above.
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**（錯誤敘述）。Cavitation 的上肺分佈為 **post-primary（reactivation）TB** 特徵，並非 primary TB（primary TB 空洞少見且不具上肺偏好）。A（LAD為primary TB最常見表現）正確；C（兒童較少肋膜積液）正確。
