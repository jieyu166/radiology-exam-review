#交換 #2019交換 #NR About inflammatory lesions of the temporal bone, which one is correct?
(A) External auditory canal cholesteatoma is a rare lesion with an incidence of approximately 1%-5%.
(B) Keratosis obturans often occurs in younger patients and tends to be bilateral.
(C) Necrotizing or malignant otitis externa most commonly occurs in young female without underlying disease.
(D) External auditory canal cholesteatoma can be easily differentiated from entities such as carcinoma and otitis externa.
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。Keratosis obturans 好發 <40 歲年輕人，50% 雙側。A 錯（EAC cholesteatoma 發生率約 0.1-0.5%，非 1-5%）。C 錯（necrotizing OE 好發老年糖尿病/免疫低下，非年輕女性）。D 錯（影像非特異性，可被 carcinoma 和 OE 模擬）。詳見 [[temporal-bone-inflammatory]]。
