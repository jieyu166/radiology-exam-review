#交換 #2019交換 #Chest Regarding pulmonary complications of hematologic malignancy. Which is NOT **true**?
(A) Pulmonary hemorrhage, leukostasis, pneumonia are the most common pulmonary complications
(B) Leukostasis is difficult to differentiate from pulmonary hemorrhage radiologically
(C) CMV and RSV viral pneumonia are usually found during the period of 31-100 days after transplantation
(D) Findings of CMV pneumonia include bilateral parenchymal opacities and innumerable nodules
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。Leukostasis 以 interstitial pattern 為主，pulmonary hemorrhage 以 alveolar consolidation 為主，影像上**可以區分**。詳見 [[hematologic-malignancy-pulmonary]]。
