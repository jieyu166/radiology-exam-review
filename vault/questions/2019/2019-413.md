#交換 #2016交換 #2019交換 #MSK Which type of osteosarcoma has the best prognosis?
(A) Conventional (intramedullary)
(B) Parosteal
(C) Periosteal
(D) Telangiectatic
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。Parosteal osteosarcoma 為 low-grade surface osteosarcoma，預後最好（5年存活率 >90%）。Conventional 與 telangiectatic 為 high-grade。詳見 [[parosteal-osteosarcoma]]。
