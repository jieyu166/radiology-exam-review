#交換 #2019交換 #MSK 關於 Avascular necrosis of the knee at MRI 之影像表現，下列何者為非?
(A) Absent marrow signal in necrotic marrow
(B) Edema spares the devascularized infarcted segment
(C) Double-line sign
(D) Development of a collapse
??
**Ans: **
AVN 壞死區骨髓保留原始脂肪訊號，不會呈現 absent marrow signal。Absent marrow signal 是錯誤描述。
