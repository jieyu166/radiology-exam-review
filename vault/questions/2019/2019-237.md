#交換 #2019交換 #CV A patient with multiple myeloma and heart failure is referred for a CMR study. Which of the following findings would be most consistent with cardiac amyloidosis?
(A) Dilated and hyperdynamic left ventricle with small atria
(B) Thickened pericardium
(C) Asymmetric septal hypertrophy
(D) Patchy myocardial uptake of contrast, with difficult to completely suppress myocardial signal with inversion-recovery sequences obtained 20 minutes after contrast administration
(E) Focal subendocardial delayed contrast enhancement with regional wall motion abnormality
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。Cardiac amyloidosis 特徵為 global/diffuse subendocardial LGE + 異常 gadolinium kinetics（心肌 T1 與血池相近，null point 難以設定，IR 序列難以完全抑制心肌訊號）。E 為 ischemic pattern。詳見 [[cardiac-mri-delayed-enhancement]]。
