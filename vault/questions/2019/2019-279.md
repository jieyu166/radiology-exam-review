#交換 #2019交換 #GU What imaging modality is best for evaluation of urethral injury?
(A) CT with intravenous contrast material, delayed phase
(B) CT cystography
(C) Fluoroscopic retrograde urethrography
(D) Fluoroscopic cystography
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**（透視引導逆行性尿道攝影）。RUG 為評估尿道損傷之 gold standard。CT cystography / fluoroscopic cystography 用於膀胱損傷。
