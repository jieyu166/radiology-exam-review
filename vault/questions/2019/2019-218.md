#交換 #2019交換 #GU Which one of the following is unlikely to be a 'primary retroperitoneal malignant tumour'?
(1) Liposarcoma
(2) Malignant fibrous histiocytoma
(3) Leiomyosarcoma
(4) Myelolipoma
??
**Ans: 4**
正確答案為 **4（Myelolipoma）**。Myelolipoma為良性腫瘤（成熟脂肪+造血組織），最常見於腎上腺，不屬於原發後腹腔惡性腫瘤。Liposarcoma、MFH/UPS、Leiomyosarcoma皆為常見之原發後腹腔肉瘤。詳見 [[retroperitoneal-tumors]]。
