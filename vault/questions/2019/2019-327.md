#交換 #2019交換 #NR A patient presents with thumb pain due to nerve compression. Which nerve root is most likely involved?
(A) C5
(B) C6
(C) C7
(D) C8
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B（C6）**。拇指皮節對應 C6 神經根。C5 = 上臂外側（deltoid area），C7 = 中指，C8 = 小指。
