#交換 #2019交換 #ABD The statement of melioidosis, which one is **WRONG**?
(A) The "honeycomb" appearance has been described to be characteristic for large melioidosis liver abscesses.
(B) Melioidosis is endemic to northern Australia and the Southeast Asia.
(C) Melioidosis is a serious infection by parasite which is found in wet soils.
(D) The lungs are the most commonly affected organ in melioidosis.
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**（錯誤敘述）。Melioidosis 由**細菌 Burkholderia pseudomallei** 引起，**非 parasite（寄生蟲）**。A（honeycomb肝膿瘍）、B（東南亞/澳洲北部）、D（肺為最常受影響器官）皆正確。
