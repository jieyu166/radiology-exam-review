(A) MRI is the most sensitive imaging tool.
(B) Primary hemochromatosis is hereditary recessive disease.
(C) CT is low specificity.
(D) It can increased rate of occurrence of HCC.
??
**Ans: C**
CT has low sensitivity (63%) and high specificity (96%) for the diagnosis of iron overload. (RadioGraphics 2009;29:1575-89)
