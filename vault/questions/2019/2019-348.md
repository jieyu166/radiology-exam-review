#交換 #2019交換 #NR Which of the following descriptions about posterior reversible encephalopathy syndrome (PRES) is **WRONG**?
(A) Commonly associated with hypertension, eclampsia, and immunosuppressive therapy
(B) Predominantly affects parieto-occipital white matter
(C) Most cases show vasogenic edema
(D) Restricted diffusion (DWI positive) is the typical finding
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**（錯誤敘述）。PRES 絕大多數病例為**血管源性水腫（vasogenic edema）**，DWI 表現為**無擴散受限（DWI 陰性、ADC 升高或正常）**。若出現 restricted diffusion（DWI 陽性、ADC 降低）則提示已進展至細胞毒性水腫，預後較差，但**非典型表現**。
