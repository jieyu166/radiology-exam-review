#交換 #2019交換 #MSK Which of the following diseases is NOT related to osteonecrosis (avascular necrosis)?
(A) Panner disease.
(B) Kienbock disease.
(C) Legg-Calve-Perthes disease.
(D) Blount disease.
??
**Ans: D**
官方原始答案標示為A，惟手寫更正註記「D才對？」。Panner disease（capitellum osteochondrosis）、Kienbock disease（lunate AVN）、Legg-Calve-Perthes（femoral head AVN）皆屬AVN相關疾病；Blount disease為脛骨內翻，病因為機械壓力致生長板障礙，並非AVN。故D為正確答案（NOT related to osteonecrosis）。詳見 [[osteochondroses]]。
