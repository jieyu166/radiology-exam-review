#交換 #2019交換 #ABD Which of the following imaging findings of ascites is the most suggestive of a malignant etiology?
(A) Large volume of ascites.
(B) Loculation of ascitic fluid.
(C) High-attenuation ascites.
(D) Hemorrhagic ascites.
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。腹水呈局限化/分隔化為惡性腹水較特異的影像徵象；大量腹水、高密度腹水、出血性腹水皆可見於良性病因，特異度較低。詳見 [[ascites-malignant-imaging]]。
