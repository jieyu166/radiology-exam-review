#交換 #2019交換 #ABD Which of the following is classically seen with sigmoid volvulus?
(A) U-shaped sigmoid
(B) Dilated sigmoid directed toward right hemidiaphragm
(C) Twisted appearance of ileo-cecal region
(D) Decompressed small bowel
(E) Transverse colon cranial to sigmoid colon
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。Sigmoid volvulus 經典表現：擴張的乙狀結腸朝右側橫膈方向延伸（coffee bean sign / inverted U sign）。
