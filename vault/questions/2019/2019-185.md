#交換 #2019交換 #IR Which one of the following is not long-acting and permanent embolic material?
(A) Gelfoam
(B) Polyvinyl alcohol (Ivalon)
(C) Isobutyl-2-cyanoacrylate (IBC, Bucrylate)
(D) Absolute ethanol
??
**Ans: A**
正確答案為 **A**。Gelfoam屬暫時性材料，數週內被人體吸收，並非長效永久性材料。PVA、cyanoacrylate、絕對酒精皆為永久性栓塞材料。詳見 [[embolic-materials]]。
