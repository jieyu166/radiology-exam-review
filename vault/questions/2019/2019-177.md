#交換 #2019交換 #ABD Each of the following anatomic locations is a watershed area that directs the flow of intraperitoneal fluid except ______.
(A) Transverse mesocolon.
(B) Small bowel mesentery.
(C) Greater omentum.
(D) Sigmoid mesocolon.
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**。大網膜並非引導腹膜液流方向的分水嶺構造；橫結腸繫膜、小腸繫膜、乙狀結腸繫膜皆為分水嶺構造。詳見 [[peritoneal-fluid-watershed-areas]]。
