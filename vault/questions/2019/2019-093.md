#交換 #2019交換 #CV The use of cardiac CT is appropriate in which of the following scenarios?
(A) Asymptomatic patient with prior coronary stent placed <2 years ago
(B) Asymptomatic patient with prior CABG performed <5 years ago
(C) Symptomatic patient with a prior coronary stent with diameter <3mm
(D) Symptomatic patient with prior CABG to evaluate graft patency
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。適當使用準則（appropriate use criteria）認為**有症狀**且曾接受 CABG 之病人，以心臟 CT 評估 **graft patency** 為適當適應症；相對地，**無症狀**病人於 stent <2 年或 CABG <5 年內常規篩檢、以及支架直徑 **<3 mm**（CT 對小支架評估準確度不佳）皆為不適當情境。詳見 [[ccta]]（適應症段落）。
