#交換 #2019交換 #MSK 有關 Lisfranc fracture-dislocation 下列何者有誤?
(A) Dislocation of metatarsocuneiform joint
(B) Associated forced plantar flexion of forefoot on heel
(C) Athletic injury in older population, and Charcot arthropathy in younger diabetic population.
(D) Identify Lisfranc ligament between medial cuneiform and proximal 2nd metatarsal may be the diagnostic clue
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**（錯誤敘述）。運動傷害好發於年輕族群、Charcot arthropathy好發於長期糖尿病患（年齡層通常較高），本選項年齡層對調故錯誤。詳見 [[lisfranc-injury]]。
