#交換 #2019交換 #ABD About the incidence of US findings in patient with acute pancreatitis, which of the following order is correct (from highest to lowest)?
(A) Peripancreatic inflammation > Indistinct ventral margin > Focal mass > Venous thrombosis
(B) Venous thrombosis > Peripancreatic inflammation > Indistinct ventral margin > Focal mass
(C) Peripancreatic inflammation > Venous thrombosis > Indistinct ventral margin > Focal mass
(D) Focal mass > Peripancreatic inflammation > Indistinct ventral margin > Venous thrombosis
??
**Ans: A**
正確答案為 **A**。急性胰臟炎超音波發現頻率排序：Peripancreatic inflammation（最常見）> Indistinct ventral margin > Focal mass > Venous thrombosis（最少見）。
