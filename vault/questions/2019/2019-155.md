#交換 #2019交換 #NR Which of the following description about dura A-V fistula is **wrong**?
(A) Presence of AV shunts within dura
(B) Carotid cavernous fistula is the most common site
(C) Involved dural sinus is often thrombosed
(D) Flow reversal in cortical veins increases risk of hemorrhage
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**（錯誤敘述）。硬腦膜動靜脈瘻管最常見部位為transverse/sigmoid sinus，並非carotid cavernous fistula。硬腦膜內AV shunts、受累靜脈竇常血栓化、皮質靜脈血流逆流增加出血風險等敘述皆正確。詳見 [[dural-avf]]。
