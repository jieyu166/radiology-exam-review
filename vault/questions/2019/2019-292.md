#交換 #2019交換 #ABD Which finding is the clue to rotational abnormalities of the bowel?
(A) Superior mesenteric vein positioned to the right of the SMA
(B) SMA initially courses to the left rather than right
(C) Duodenum crosses midline between the aorta and SMA
(D) Small bowel located in the right abdomen, large bowel on the left
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。小腸位於右側腹部、大腸位於左側腹部是腸道旋轉異常最具診斷價值的線索。A/B/C 為正常解剖配置或非特異性發現。
