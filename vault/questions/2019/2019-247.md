#交換 #2019交換 #US Which of the following is NOT a typical ultrasound finding of pyogenic liver abscess?
(A) Variable echogenicity
(B) Well-defined to irregular border
(C) Vascularity in thick wall portion on color Doppler
(D) Posterior acoustic shadow
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D（Posterior acoustic shadow）**。Posterior acoustic shadow 為 solid lesion/calcification 特徵，非化膿性肝膿瘍典型表現。肝膿瘍典型表現為 variable echogenicity、irregular border、thick wall vascularity。詳見 [[pyogenic-liver-abscess-ultrasound]]。
