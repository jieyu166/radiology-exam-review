#交換 #2019交換 #NR About acute flaccid paralysis in children, which statement is correct?
(A) MR imaging in enterovirus 71 affected patients typically reveals a rhombencephalitis affecting the dorsal pons and medulla, and a radiculomyelitis with a predilection for the anterior horn cells of the spinal cord and ventral nerve roots.
(B) Patients presenting with acute flaccid paralysis and/or cranial nerve dysfunction during the enterovirus D68 outbreak demonstrate unique imaging findings characterized by brain stem and spinal cord gray matter/anterior horn cells lesions.
(C) Brain stem lesions of enterovirus D68 outbreak most commonly involve the pontine tegmentum.
(D) All of above
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**（以上皆正確）。EV71 致 rhombencephalitis（dorsal pons/medulla）+ anterior horn cell radiculomyelitis(A)；EV D68 致 brain stem + spinal cord gray matter lesions(B)；EV D68 腦幹病灶最常累及 pontine tegmentum(C)。
