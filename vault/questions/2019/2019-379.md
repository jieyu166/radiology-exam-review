#交換 #2019交換 #MSK Which of the following about fibrous dysplasia (FD) is **wrong**?
(A) Only 15-20% are polyostotic, mainly at pelvis, femur, tibia
(B) Uncommonly based in cortex; tibia is most common site for cortically based fibrous dysplasia
(C) Commonly involves the metaphysis and occasionally the epiphysis
(D) Most lesions are mildly sclerotic, causing varying degrees of expansion
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**。FD 常見於 diaphysis 或 metaphysis，epiphysis 侵犯極罕見。詳見 [[fibrous-dysplasia]]。
