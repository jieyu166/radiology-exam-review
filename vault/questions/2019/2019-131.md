#交換 #2019交換 #CV About CAD-RADS, which is **wrong**?
(A) CARD-RADS 3 means 50-69% stenosis
(B) S means stent
(C) It was newly published in 2017
(D) 4B means left main > 50% or 3-vessel obstruction > 70%
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**（錯誤敘述）。CAD-RADS 原始版（1.0）**發表於2016年**，並非2017年。CAD-RADS 3 = 50-69%狹窄、S代表stent（支架）、4B = left main >50%或3-vessel obstruction >70%等敘述皆正確。詳見 [[ccta]]。
