#交換 #2019交換 #ABD Which of the following statement for epiploic appendagitis is correct except?
(A) An ischemic infarction of an epiploic appendage
(B) Occurring 2nd to fifth decades
(C) Female predominate.
(D) Obesity may be a risk factor.
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**（錯誤敘述）。Epiploic appendagitis實際上**男性較女性多見**（約4倍），並非女性較多見。腸脂垂缺血性梗塞、好發2-5十年、肥胖為危險因子等敘述皆正確。詳見 [[epiploic-appendagitis]]。
