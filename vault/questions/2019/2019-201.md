#交換 #2019交換 #NR What is **true** about vascular dementia?
(1) WMH (White matter hyperintensity) are best demonstrated on MR imaging and can be measured by using semiquantitative visual rating scales or quantitative voxel-based methods, associated with vascular risk factors, particularly hypertension and impaired glycemic control. It is now widely accepted that WMH are an imaging biomarker of vascular disease.
(2) Lacunar infarcts (or lacunes) occur in the subcortical white matter and basal ganglia and are also an imaging correlate of CVD
(3) Lobar macrohemorrhages having been traditionally associated with cerebral amyloid angiopathy, and macrohemorrhage in deep and infratentorial locations, are associated with vascular risk factors, WMH, and lacunar infarcts
(4) All of above
??
**Ans: 4**
正確答案為 **4（All of above）**。WMH為血管疾病影像生物標記、腔隙性梗塞為CVD影像相關表現、腦葉巨量出血與CAA相關而深部/幕下巨量出血與血管危險因子相關，三者皆正確。詳見 [[dementia-neuroimaging-overview]]。
