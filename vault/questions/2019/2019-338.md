#交換 #2019交換 #PED An 8-month-old male infant with contracted skull base, small foramen magnum, rhizomelic shortening, trident hands, brachydactyly, progressive interpedicular narrowing at lumbar spine, short pedicles, and flat-roofed acetabula. Most likely diagnosis?
(A) Achondrogenesis
(B) Chondroectodermal dysplasia
(C) Thanatophoric dysplasia
(D) Achondroplasia
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**（軟骨發育不全）。Achondroplasia 典型特徵：contracted skull base、small foramen magnum、rhizomelic shortening、trident hand、brachydactyly、腰椎 interpedicular distance 漸縮、short pedicles、flat-roofed acetabula。其他選項（achondrogenesis、thanatophoric dysplasia）多為致死性。
