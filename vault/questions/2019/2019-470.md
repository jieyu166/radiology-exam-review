#交換 #2019交換 #GI 下列關於 Acute Mesenteric Ischemia 之 CT 影像敘述何者錯誤?
(A) Mucosal and serosal enhancement may be completely absent in acute arterial embolism.
(B) Acute thrombosis of SMV is an unusual cause of bowel ischemia.
(C) Mucosal enhancement is usually decreased in acute venous thrombosis.
(D) Mesenteric edema is more characteristic of venous than arterial disease.
??
**Ans: **
急性靜脈血栓時，mucosal enhancement 通常是增加（increased）而非減少，因為靜脈回流受阻導致腸壁充血水腫。
