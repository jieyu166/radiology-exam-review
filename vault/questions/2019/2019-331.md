#交換 #2019交換 #ABD May-Thurner syndrome means:
(A) Left renal vein between SMA and aorta
(B) Third portion of duodenum compressed between SMA and aorta
(C) Compression of celiac artery by median arcuate ligament
(D) Left common iliac vein compressed between right common iliac artery and fifth lumbar vertebra
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。May-Thurner syndrome：左髂總靜脈被夾於右髂總動脈與第五腰椎之間。A = Nutcracker syndrome、B = SMA syndrome、C = Median arcuate ligament syndrome。跨年重複 2018-150。
