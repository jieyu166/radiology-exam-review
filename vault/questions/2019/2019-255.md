#交換 #2019交換 #PE Avulsion fracture of the medial epicondyle of the humerus may be seen in ___?
(A) a young baseball pitcher
(B) a young tennis player
(C) a young runner
(D) a young basketball player
??
**Ans: A**
正確答案為 **A（young baseball pitcher）**。肱骨內上髁撕裂性骨折好發於年輕棒球投手（Little Leaguer's elbow），因反覆投球 valgus stress 造成。詳見 [[medial-epicondyle-avulsion]]。
