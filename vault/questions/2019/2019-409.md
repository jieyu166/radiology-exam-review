#交換 #2019交換 #NR Rapidly progressive dementia can result from a variety of etiologies, including:
(A) prion disease such as Creutzfeldt-Jakob disease
(B) infectious entities such as HIV-associated dementia
(C) autoimmune and inflammatory conditions such as Hashimoto encephalopathy
(D) all of above
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。RPD 的病因涵蓋 prion disease（CJD）、感染（HIV）、自體免疫/發炎（Hashimoto encephalopathy）等。詳見 [[rapidly-progressive-dementia]]。
