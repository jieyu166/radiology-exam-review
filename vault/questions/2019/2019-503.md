#交換 #2019交換 #NR Which of the statements about radiation necrosis (RN) of radiotherapy in the brain is **WRONG**?
(A) RN typically occurs 1 to 2 years after radiation.
(B) The total radiation dose and fraction size are important risk factors.
(C) The RN appearance in conventional imaging is often distinguishable from tumor recurrence.
(D) MRS shows decreased Cho, NAA, and creatine, and elevated lactate in RN.
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**。放射線壞死（RN）在傳統影像上常與腫瘤復發難以區分（indistinguishable），而非容易區分。鑑別需仰賴 MR perfusion（RN 低灌流）、MRS（RN 的 Cho 降低）、及 FDG-PET（RN 低代謝）。選項 A、B、D 皆正確。
