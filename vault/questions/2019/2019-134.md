#交換 #2019交換 #CV Which of the following statement about Bland-White-Garland Syndrome is correct?
(A) Anomalous origin of right main coronary artery from pulmonary artery (ARCAPA)
(B) In contrast to adult type, the infant type is characterized by abundant interarterial collateral vessels between the RCA and the LCA
(C) It results in the "coronary steal" phenomenon, in which a right-to-left shunt leads to abnormal left ventricular perfusion.
(D) Patient may present with mitral insufficiency due to ischemic dysfunction of the papillary muscles and adjacent myocardium.
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。Bland-White-Garland syndrome患者可因乳頭肌與鄰近心肌缺血性功能障礙而出現二尖瓣閉鎖不全。本病為ALCAPA（左冠脈異常起源，非ARCAPA）；嬰兒型側支循環發育不足（非豐富）；分流方向為左向右（非右向左）。詳見 [[alcapa-bland-white-garland]]。
