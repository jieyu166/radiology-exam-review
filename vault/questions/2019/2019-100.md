#交換 #2019交換 #NR What is "not" the imaging features of intracranial solitary fibrous tumor/Hemangiopericytoma?
(A) A narrow base of attachment to the dura
(B) The presence of associated flow voids
(C) Hyperostosis of adjacent bone (meningioma)
(D) A well-circumscribed and lobulated soft tissue mass
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**（非其影像特徵）。顱內 solitary fibrous tumor/hemangiopericytoma（SFT/HPC）之提示徵象為**硬腦膜附著基部窄（narrow dural base）**、**鄰近骨質侵蝕（erosion，而非 hyperostosis）**、**無鈣化**、明顯強化、associated flow voids、以及邊界清楚分葉狀軟組織腫塊；相對地，**硬腦膜廣基附著、鄰近骨質增生硬化（hyperostosis）與瘤內鈣化為腦膜瘤（meningioma）的典型特徵**，可作為鑑別點。詳見 [[solitary-fibrous-tumor-hemangiopericytoma]]。
