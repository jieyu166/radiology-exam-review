#交換 #2019交換 #CH According to the 2017 Fleischner Society guideline for managing incidental pulmonary nodules, what slice thickness is suggested for accurate measurement and characterization?
(A) ≤1.5 mm
(B) 2 mm
(C) 3 mm
(D) 5 mm
??
**Ans: A**
正確答案為 **A**。Fleischner 2017 建議使用 ≤1.5 mm 薄切，以 sharp algorithm 重建，避免 partial volume averaging。詳見 [[fleischner-pulmonary-nodule]]。
