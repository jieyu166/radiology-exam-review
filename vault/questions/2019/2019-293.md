#交換 #2019交換 #ABD Which of the following state describes typical morphological change of cirrhosis?
(A) Left lateral segment hypertrophy, caudate lobe hypertrophy and expanded gallbladder fossa.
(B) Left lateral segment hypotrophy, caudate lobe hypertrophy and expanded gallbladder fossa.
(C) Left lateral segment hypotrophy, caudate lobe hypotrophy and expanded gallbladder fossa.
(D) Left lateral segment hypotrophy, caudate lobe hypotrophy and tight gallbladder fossa.
??
**Ans: A**
正確答案為 **A**。肝硬化典型形態變化：left lateral segment **肥大**（hypertrophy）、caudate lobe **肥大**（hypertrophy）、gallbladder fossa **擴張**（expanded）。Right lobe 則萎縮。
