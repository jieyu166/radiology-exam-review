(A) The lumen of the central venous catheter is compromised between the clavicle and the first rib
(B) Lateral subclavian vein insertion is more vulnerable to pinch-off
(C) The catheter may fragment and embolize to the heart or pulmonary artery
(D) Pinch-off sign can be recognized on chest X-ray
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。**Medial** insertion 更容易被 costoclavicular space 夾擠，非 lateral。詳見 [[pinch-off-syndrome]]。
