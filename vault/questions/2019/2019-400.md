#交換 #2019交換 #MSK Acro-osteolysis can be present in the following conditions EXCEPT...
(A) Hyperparathyroidism
(B) Psoriatic arthritis
(C) Neurofibromatosis type 1
(D) Scleroderma
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**。NF1 不是 acro-osteolysis 的典型病因。Scleroderma、psoriatic arthritis、hyperparathyroidism 皆可造成指端骨溶解。詳見 [[acro-osteolysis]]。
