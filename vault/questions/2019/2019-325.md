#交換 #2019交換 #ABD Which of the following is NOT a cause of small bowel obstruction (SBO)?
(A) Adhesion
(B) Hernia
(C) Crohn disease
(D) Intussusception
(E) All of the above can cause SBO
??
**Ans: E**
正確答案為 **E**（以上皆可造成 SBO）。Adhesion、hernia、Crohn disease、intussusception 皆為 SBO 之已知原因。
