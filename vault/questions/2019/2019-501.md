#交換 #2019交換 #MSK Which of the statements about osteochondritis dissecans is **WRONG**?
(A) Majority of patients are between 10 and 40 years of age.
(B) Knee is most common site to be involved.
(C) Osteochondritis dissecans in the knees is more likely to involve in the lateral condyle than the medial condyle.
(D) MRI T2WI typically shows low signal loose bodies outlined by high signal fluid.
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**。OCD 在膝關節最常侵犯 **medial femoral condyle**（約 70-85%），而非 lateral condyle。選項 C 敘述錯誤。OCD 好發於 10-40 歲，膝關節為最常見部位，MRI T2WI 可見低訊號 loose body 被高訊號關節液包圍。
