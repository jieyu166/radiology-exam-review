#交換 #2019交換 #BR According to ACR BI-RADS 5th edition for mammography, which of the following statements is **WRONG**?
(A) A group of amorphous calcifications: category 4A
(B) A solid mass with indistinct margin: category 4B
(C) A new indistinct, irregular solid mass: category 4C
(D) New fine linear and branching calcifications in segmental distribution: category 5
??
**Ans: A**
正確答案為 **A**（錯誤敘述）。Amorphous calcifications 屬 suspicious morphology，歸類為 category 4B（非 4A）。Indistinct margin mass = 4B，new irregular mass = 4C，fine linear/branching calcifications in segmental distribution = category 5。詳見 [[breast-calcifications]]。
