#交換 #2019交換 #NR Which of the following description about traumatic carotid-cavernous fistula is **wrong**?
(A) Presence of large superior ophthalmic vein
(B) Enlarged extra-ocular muscles
(C) Slow flow fistula between cavernous ICA and sinus
(D) Filling of cortical veins is a sign of danger
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**（錯誤敘述）。外傷性/直接型CCF為頸內動脈與海綿竇直接相通之高流速（非低流速）廔管。大靜脈曲張的上眼靜脈、眼外肌腫大、皮質靜脈填充為危險徵象等敘述皆正確。詳見 [[carotid-cavernous-fistula]]。
