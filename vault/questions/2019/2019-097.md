#交換 #2019交換 #NR About image findings of idiopathic intracranial hypertension, which is incorrect?
(A) Abnormalities of optic nerve sheath complex
(B) Prominent arachnoid granulation
(C) Pachymeningeal enhancement
(D) Empty sella syndrome
(E) Meningoencephalocele
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**（錯誤敘述，非其影像表現）。顱內壓升高（idiopathic intracranial hypertension, IIH）之典型影像徵象包含視神經鞘複合體異常、突出之 arachnoid granulation、dural ectasia、meningoencephalocele（尤好發於顳骨與蝶骨翼）與 empty sella syndrome；**pachymeningeal（硬腦膜）增強並非 IIH 的典型表現**，較常見於顱內低壓（intracranial hypotension）。詳見 [[spontaneous-skull-base-cephalocele]]。
