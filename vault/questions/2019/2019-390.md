#交換 #2019交換 #NR About pediatric low-grade glioma Genetics, which one is correct?
(A) smallest number of genetic alterations, with a median of one mutation per tumor
(B) result in activation of the MAPK (mitogen-activated protein kinase) pathway
(C) most common aberration in this pathway being that of its BRAF component
(D) all above are correct
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。兒童 LGG 基因突變數最少（median 1/tumor）、活化 MAPK pathway、最常見為 BRAF 成分突變。詳見 [[pediatric-lgg-genetics]]。
