#交換 #2019交換 #GU Which of the following is NOT a typical image finding of ovarian mucinous cystadenoma?
(A) Very large
(B) Multilocular cyst
(C) "Spider web" appearance
(D) "Stained glass" appearance
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**。"Spider web" appearance 為**腹膜包裹性囊腫（peritoneal inclusion cyst）**之特徵。黏液性囊腺瘤特徵為體積大(A)、多房(B)、"stained glass" appearance(D)。
