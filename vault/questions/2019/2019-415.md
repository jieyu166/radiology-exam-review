#交換 #2019交換 #PE Which is NOT a common finding of scurvy?
(A) Frankel line
(B) Beaking at metaphysis
(C) Periosteal elevation
(D) Hypoplasia of epiphysis
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。Epiphyseal hypoplasia 不是 scurvy 的典型表現。Scurvy 特徵包括 Frankel line、beaking/corner sign、periosteal elevation（subperiosteal hemorrhage）。詳見 [[scurvy-skeletal]]。
