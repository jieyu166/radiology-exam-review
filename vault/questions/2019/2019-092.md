#交換 #2019交換 #CV In the evaluation of a stent for in-stent restenosis, how does the CT density (HU value) within the stent lumen compare to that of the segment proximal to the stent if the stent is patent and has no stenosis?
(A) It should be higher
(B) It should be equal to the segment proximal to the stent
(C) It should be slightly lower
(D) It should be significantly lower
??
**Ans: A**
正確答案為 **A**。即使支架通暢無狹窄，支架材質（金屬 struts）造成的 **blooming/beam-hardening 偽影**仍會使支架管腔內量測到的 CT 值（HU）較近端血管節段**假性偏高**，此為判讀 in-stent restenosis 時需注意的陷阱，不可單憑管腔內 HU 較高即判定狹窄。詳見 [[ccta]]（支架評估段落）。
