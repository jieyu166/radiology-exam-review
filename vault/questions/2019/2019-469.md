#交換 #2019交換 #Neuro Disorders of myelin metabolism (dysmyelinating disorders), including many inherited leukodystrophies, will generally spare the subcortical U-fibers except which following disease?
(A) Alexander disease
(B) Metachromatic leukodystrophy
(C) X-linked adrenoleukodystrophy
(D) None of above
??
**Ans: **
大多數 dysmyelinating disorders 保留皮質下 U-fibers，但 Alexander disease 和 Canavan disease 是例外，會侵犯 U-fibers。
