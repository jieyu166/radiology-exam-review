#交換 #2019交換 #ABD Which of the statements about differentiating focal autoimmune pancreatitis(AIP) from pancreatic cancer(PC) is **WRONG**?
(A) MRI shows speckled enhancement within a hypointense/isointense lesion on pancreatic phase DCE-T1WI in AIP, and it is less seen in PC.
(B) PET-CT typically shows diffused heterogeneous FDG accumulation in AIP, and focal homogeneous accumulation was frequently seen in PC.
(C) The length of the narrowed portion of the main pancreatic duct (MPD) on endoscopic retrograde pancreatography (ERP) in AIP patients was significantly shorter than in PC patients.
(D) Apparent diffusion coefficient (ADC) values were significantly lower in AIP than in PC.
??
**Ans: C**
Combining markedly decreased apparent diffusion coefficients with the presence of additional signs can be helpful in diagnosis of AIP.
Imaging of Focal Autoimmune Pancreatitis and Differentiating It from Pancreatic Cancer. ISRN Radiology, 2013, 1-8.
