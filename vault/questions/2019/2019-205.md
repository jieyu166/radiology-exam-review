#交換 #2019交換 #MSK 有關 Scaphoid fracture 下列何者為誤?
(A) Transverse fracture line most commonly seen in proximal pole of scaphoid>>waist
(B) Usually difficult to visualize non-displaced fracture line
(C) Union rate is up to 95% in uncomplicated fracture
(D) Scaphoid non-union mostly associated with fracture at proximal third and vertical oblique fracture at middle third
??
**Ans: A**
正確答案為 **A**（錯誤敘述）。舟狀骨骨折最常見於waist（腰部，約70-80%），並非近端極（proximal pole，僅約20%）。詳見 [[scaphoid-fracture]]。
