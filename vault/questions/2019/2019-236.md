#交換 #2019交換 #CV Which of the following findings is suggestive of poor outcome in an adult patient with thalassemia major?
(A) Increase of the myocardial T1 at rest
(B) Increase of the myocardial T1 with vasodilation
(C) Decrease of the T2* of the myocardium at rest
(D) Increase of the T2* of the myocardium at rest
(E) Increase of the T2 of the myocardium at rest
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C（Decrease of T2*）**。T2* 下降代表心肌鐵沉積增加，T2* <20 ms 異常，<10 ms 嚴重且 heart failure 風險顯著增加。詳見 [[cardiac-mri-iron-overload]]。
