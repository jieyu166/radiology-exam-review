#交換 #2019交換 #CV Which of the following CT imaging features is typical of coronary allograft vasculopathy?
(A) Involvement of coronary artery bifurcations.
(B) Diffuse concentric wall thickening.
(C) Collateral vessels.
(D) Calcifications.
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。冠狀動脈同種異體移植血管病變典型CT特徵為瀰漫性向心性血管壁增厚，而非侷限於分叉處、側支血管形成或鈣化。詳見 [[coronary-allograft-vasculopathy]]。
