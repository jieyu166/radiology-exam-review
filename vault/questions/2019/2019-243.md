#交換 #2019交換 #CH What is the most characteristic feature of pulmonary involvement of Kaposi's sarcoma?
(A) Pleural nodules and effusion
(B) Peribronchovascular flame-shaped nodules
(C) Thickening of peribronchovascular interstitium
(D) Lymphadenopathy
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B（Peribronchovascular flame-shaped nodules）**。肺 Kaposi sarcoma 最具特徵性表現為支氣管血管周圍火焰狀（ill-defined）結節，雙側大致對稱分布。詳見 [[pulmonary-kaposi-sarcoma]]。
