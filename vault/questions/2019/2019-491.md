#交換 #2019交換 #CH Which of the following about Mounier-Kuhn syndrome is correct?
(A) Only children can get Mounier-Kuhn syndrome
(B) Trachea collapse while inspiration and dilatation while expiration
(C) It is a genetic disorder caused by point mutation of exon 19
(D) May be associated with some connective tissue disorder, like Ehlers-Danlos syndrome
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。Mounier-Kuhn syndrome 可合併結締組織疾病如 Ehlers-Danlos syndrome。A 錯：非僅兒童，主要發生於成人。B 錯：方向搞反，氣管在吸氣時擴張、呼氣時塌陷。C 錯：非單一基因點突變所致。
