#交換 #2019交換 #H&N Regarding salivary gland presentation of Sjogren's syndrome, which one of the following is incorrect?
(A) Mainly in adult female at post-menopausal age
(B) Diffusely enlarged with normal parenchyma
(C) May exactly mimic benign lymphoepithelial lesion (BLL)
(D) High risk for MALToma
(E) Little to no cystic component
??
**Ans: E**
正確答案為 **E**（錯誤敘述）。Sjögren 症候群唾液腺於中期階段典型表現為腺體瀰漫性腫大，伴**多發、散在囊腫**（<1mm 微囊腫至 >2cm 混合實質-囊性腫塊），並非「幾乎無囊性成分」；慢性期則呈腺體萎縮、回音減低（hypoechoic）。其餘敘述（好發停經後女性、可與 BLL 難以區分、MALToma 高風險）皆正確。詳見 [[sjogren-syndrome-salivary-gland]]。
