#交換 #2019交換 #GU Which one of the follows is not **true** for prostate adenocarcinoma?
(A) Prostate cancer is best seen on T2WI MRI.
(B) Lymphatic drains mostly to pelvic nodes.
(C) Most common on the transitional zone.
(D) CT is not accurate in detection of cancer in prostate.
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**（錯誤敘述）。攝護腺癌最常見於周邊區，並非移行區（移行區主要與BPH相關）。T2WI為最佳偵測序列、淋巴引流主要至骨盆淋巴結、CT偵測準確度不佳等敘述皆正確。詳見 [[prostate-adenocarcinoma-imaging]]。
