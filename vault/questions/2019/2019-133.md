#交換 #2019交換 #CV What should be the main reason to obtain a chest CT in the setting of possible acute traumatic aortic injury?
(A) Abnormal chest radiograph
(B) Mechanism of trauma
(C) Patient request
(D) Chest pain
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。安排胸部CT評估急性外傷性主動脈損傷的主要依據是受傷機轉（mechanism of trauma），而非單純依賴異常胸部X光、胸痛或病人要求。詳見 [[traumatic-aortic-injury]]。
