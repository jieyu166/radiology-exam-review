#交換 #2019交換 #CH What is **false** about the possible UIP (usual interstitial pneumonia) pattern (all Three Features) according to 2011 an Official ATS/ERS/JRS/ALAT Statement: Idiopathic Pulmonary Fibrosis: Evidence-based Guidelines for Diagnosis and Management?
(A) Subpleural, basal predominance
(B) Reticular abnormality
(C) Honeycombing with or without traction bronchiectasis
(D) Absence of features listed as inconsistent with UIP pattern
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**。Honeycombing（合併或不合併 traction bronchiectasis）屬於 **definite UIP pattern** 的特徵，而非 possible UIP pattern。Possible UIP pattern 僅包含三項特徵：(1) subpleural, basal predominance、(2) reticular abnormality、(3) absence of inconsistent features。
