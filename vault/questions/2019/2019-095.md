#交換 #2019交換 #ABD What is the "**wrong**" description about malignant transformation of endometriosis?
(A) Endometriosis have an estimated 1-2% chance of developing ovarian cancer
(B) The human epididymal secretory protein E4 level is not elevated in endometriosis associated ovarian cancer.
(C) Endometriosis associated ovarian cancer affects women 10-20 years earlier than those affected by ovarian cancer without endometriosis.
(D) Enhancing mural nodules within an endometrioma on T1WI is highly suggestive of endometriosis-associated malignancy
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**（錯誤敘述）。**HE4（human epididymal secretory protein E4）與 CA125 血清濃度上升**皆能提高卵巢癌診斷準確度、有助鑑別卵巢癌與卵巢子宮內膜異位囊腫，而非「不會上升」。子宮內膜異位症相關卵巢癌約 1–2% 發生率、發病年齡較早 10–20 年、囊內強化壁結節高度懷疑惡性轉化等敘述皆正確。詳見 [[endometriosis-malignant-transformation]]。
