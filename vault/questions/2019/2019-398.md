#交換 #2019交換 #MSK About musculoskeletal involvement of sarcoidosis, which one is **wrong**?
(A) Soft tissue swelling, and prominent joint effusion are common in acute sarcoidosis arthritis
(B) Tenosynovitis, tendinitis, bursitis, and synovitis can be demonstrated on MR, but findings are nonspecific
(C) Sarcoid muscle nodules usually present a fusiform, longitudinally oriented nodules with central low signal umbilication on TT2WI or T1W C+ FS
(D) Sarcoidosis in digits generally present a lacy trabecular configuration without significant periosteal reaction
??
**Ans: A**
正確答案為 **A**。急性類肉瘤關節炎（Lofgren syndrome）踝關節腫脹為 periarticular soft tissue swelling 及 tenosynovitis，true synovitis 或 joint effusion 少見。詳見 [[sarcoidosis-msk]]。
