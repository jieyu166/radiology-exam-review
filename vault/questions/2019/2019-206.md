#交換 #2019交換 #Breast 有關 Breast hamartoma，下列何者有誤？
(A) Also known as fibroadenolipomas, are benign breast lesions.
(B) Typified by a "breast within a breast" appearance on mammogram.
(C) Hamartomas are hard and not easy to compress.
(D) It shows well-circumscribed, round or oval mass surrounded by a thin capsule.
??
**Ans: C**
