#交換 #2019交換 #Unknown What pulmonary feature is most suggestive of pulmonary infarction in the context of acute pulmonary embolism?
(A) Reverse halo sign
(B) Focal airspace opacity with air-bronchogram
(C) Mosaic lung attenuation
(D) Segmental atelectasis
??
**Ans: A**
引用 [[2013 Reversed halo sign in acute pulmonary embolism and infarction]]認為有PE的病人如果有reverse halo sign代表有infarction
Reverse halo sign: 想到Cryptogenic organizing pneumonia(**COP**) 。但很多疾病也會，如 Wegener's, aspergillosis, acute PE+infarction ...
