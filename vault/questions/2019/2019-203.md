#交換 #2019交換 #2022交換 #MSK 有關 Reflex sympathetic dystrophy 下列何者正確?
(A) Chronic pain disorder of the sympathetic nervous system, usually result of trauma, or complication of surgery, infection or casting
(B) Best diagnostic tool is radiography
(C) Male predominance, and usually occurs in upper extremity
(D) May follow 30% of all injuries
??
**Ans: A**
正確答案為 **A**。CRPS/RSD為交感神經系統之慢性疼痛疾患,通常為外傷、手術、感染或石膏固定之併發症。臨床（clinically）為主要診斷方式（非radiography）、實為女性較多見（非男性優勢）、發生率遠低於30%（約每10萬人年5.46例）。詳見 [[crps]]。
