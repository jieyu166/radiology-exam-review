#交換 #2019交換 #PE Which of the followings is the least common cause of intestinal obstruction in neonates?
(A) Midgut volvulus
(B) Duodenal atresia
(C) Annular pancreas
(D) Acute appendicitis
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D（Acute appendicitis）**。急性闌尾炎極罕見於新生兒。Midgut volvulus、duodenal atresia、annular pancreas 皆為新生兒腸阻塞常見原因。詳見 [[neonatal-intestinal-obstruction]]。
