#交換 #2019交換 #MSK Compared with pyogenic spondylitis, which is NOT a common feature of TB spondylitis?
(A) Panvertebral involvement
(B) Early and severe disc destruction
(C) Subligamentous spread of large paraspinal abscesses
(D) Skip lesions
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。Early and severe disc destruction 是 pyogenic spondylitis 的特徵，非 TB。TB spondylitis 特徵為 relative disc preservation、subligamentous spread、skip lesions、panvertebral involvement。詳見 [[tb-spondylitis]]。
