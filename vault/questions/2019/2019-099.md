#交換 #2019交換 #NR Which cranial nerve is the most common one that cavernous sinus schwannomas arise from?
(A) Oculomotor
(B) Abducens
(C) Trigeminal
(D) Trochlear nerve
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**。海綿竇神經鞘瘤（cavernous sinus schwannoma）**最常起源於三叉神經（trigeminal nerve）**，其次為動眼神經；其他海綿竇內顱神經（外旋、滑車神經）較少見為起源。詳見 [[cavernous-sinus-schwannoma]]。
