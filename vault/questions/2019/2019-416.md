#交換 #2017交換 #2019交換 #ABD Regarding adrenal tumors, which statement is **FALSE**?
(A) Chemical shift MRI can be used to differentiate adenoma from non-adenoma adrenal lesion
(B) Unenhanced CT value less than 10HU is suggestive of adrenal adenoma
(C) Myelolipoma cannot be diagnosed from fat-suppression MRI or non-contrast CT
(D) Fat-containing adrenal metastases can be misleading
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**。Myelolipoma 含大量 macroscopic fat，在 non-contrast CT 可見明顯脂肪密度（<-30 HU），fat-suppression MRI 亦可見 signal drop。因此 C 敘述錯誤。詳見 [[adrenal-adenoma]]。
