#交換 #2019交換 #GU Which is **true** about combined bladder and urethral injury?
(A) Between 10% and 20% of male patients with an injury of the urethra and pelvic fracture have an injury of the bladder.
(B) They may occur os separate injuries.
(C) The bladder injury may be intraperitoneal, extraperitoneal, or rarely, a combined bladder injury.
(D) All of the above are true.
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D（All of the above are true）**。約10-20%合併骨盆骨折之男性尿道損傷病患亦合併膀胱損傷、兩者可各自獨立發生、膀胱損傷可為腹膜內/腹膜外/合併型，三者皆正確。詳見 [[bladder-urethral-trauma-imaging]]。
