#交換 #2019交換 #NR What is the most common form of intracranial tuberculosis?
(A) Tuberculoma
(B) Tuberculous abscess
(C) Miliary TB of the brain
(D) Tuberculous meningitis
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**（結核性腦膜炎）。顱內結核最常見形式為 tuberculous meningitis，好發於顱底蛛網膜下腔 (basal cisterns)，可合併 dystrophic calcification。
