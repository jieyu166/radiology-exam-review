#交換 #2019交換 #CH The Computed tomography approach to diagnosis of pulmonary hypertension begins with identifying an enlarged pulmonary artery diameter which is:
(A) Greater than 29 mm and this diameter must be measured in the axial plane at the bifurcation, orthogonal to the long axis of the pulmonary artery.
(B) Greater than 24 mm and this diameter must be measured in the coronal plane at the pulmonary valve level.
(C) Greater than 29 mm and this diameter must be measured in the axial plane at the pulmonary valve level.
(D) Greater than 24 mm and this diameter must be measured in the sagittal plane, orthogonal to the long axis of the pulmonary artery.
??
**Ans: A**
正確答案為 **A**。CT 診斷肺高壓始於量測主肺動脈徑，擴大閾值為 **>29 mm**，應於**軸位（axial plane）、肺動脈分叉處（bifurcation）、與長軸垂直（orthogonal）**取得，而非於肺動脈瓣位置或冠狀/矢狀面測量。詳見 [[pulmonary-hypertension-classification]]。
