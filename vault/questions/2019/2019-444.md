#交換 #2019交換 #ABD Which of the following imaging finding and description about Sclerosing Angiomatoid Nodular Transformation (SANT) is **wrong**?
(A) SANT is a benign vascular lesion consisting of multiple angiomatoid nodules surrounded by dense fibrous tissue that often coalesces centrally to form a scar
(B) Usually isointense on T1-weighted images and hypointense on T2-weighted images
(C) Most of them have heterogeneous hypo-enhancement during the arterial and portal venous phases of contrast-enhanced CT or MRI, with peripheral enhancing radiating lines
(D) They show progressive enhancement and are isoenhancing or hyperenhancing in the delayed phase. A hyper-enhancing central scar may be present.
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。SANT 的 central scar 在延遲期為 **hypoenhancing**（spoke wheel pattern + central hypoenhancing stellate scar），非 hyperenhancing。詳見 [[sant-spleen]]。
