#交換 #2019交換 #CV Concerning the MR imaging presentation of acute myocardial infarction, which one of the followings is **false**?
(A) T2WI typically shows increased signal intensity in affected region
(B) Late gadolinium enhancement typically shows hyperintensity in epicardial region
(C) First-pass perfusion imaging typically shows decreased wash-in of contrast agent, resulting in subendocardial perfusion defect from affected region
(D) None of the above
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**（false）。急性心肌梗塞的 LGE 呈現**心內膜下（subendocardial）**高訊號，而非心外膜（epicardial）。MI 從心內膜開始，往心外膜方向進展（wavefront phenomenon）。
