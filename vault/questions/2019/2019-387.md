#交換 #2019交換 #BR Which of the following regarding the calcifications of male breast cancer is **true**?
(A) Tends to be more in number than female cancer
(B) Usually smaller than female
(C) More likely linear and less often punctate
(D) Calcifications with a relatively benign appearance in women should be considered suspicious in men
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。男性乳癌鈣化較女性少見、較粗糙，且良性鈣化在男性中罕見（除血管鈣化/脂肪壞死外）。因此在女性看起來良性的鈣化在男性應視為可疑。詳見 [[male-breast-cancer]]。
