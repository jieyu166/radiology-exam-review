#交換 #2019交換 #US Focal fatty infiltration, focal fatty sparing, enlarged caudate lobe and an increase in liver echogenicity are all indicators of what?
(A) Fatty liver disease
(B) Hepatitis
(C) Focal Nodular Hyperplasia
(D) Cirrhosis
??
**Ans: A**
正確答案為 **A**。局灶性脂肪浸潤、局灶性脂肪保留區、尾狀葉增大、肝實質回音增加皆為脂肪肝（hepatic steatosis）的超音波指標。
