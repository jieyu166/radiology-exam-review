#交換 #2019交換 #GI 有關 Evaluation of Acute GI bleeding 之敘述，下列何者為非?
(A) CT angiography is an accurate examination for identifying the source of acute GI bleeding, with an overall sensitivity of 85% and a specificity of 92% for detection of the bleeding site.
(B) CT angiography is more sensitive than fluoroscopic angiography for detection of active extravasation, but less sensitive than RBC scanning, being able to depict bleeding at a rate of 0.3–0.5 mL/min.
(C) Endoscopy is highly useful for diagnosing the cause of upper GI bleeding, which is defined as bleeding originating proximal to the superior mesenteric vessel.
(D) Tc-99m scintigraphy is the most sensitive radiologic technique for the identification of active bleeding, enabling the detection of bleeding at rates as low as 0.1–0.5 mL/min.
??
**Ans: **
Upper GI bleeding 的定義是出血源位於 ligament of Treitz 近端（即 distal duodenum 以上），而非 superior mesenteric vessel 近端。選項 C 將分界錯誤地定義在 superior mesenteric vessel。
