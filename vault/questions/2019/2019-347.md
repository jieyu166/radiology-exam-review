#交換 #2019交換 #NR Which of the following descriptions about Joubert syndrome is **WRONG**?
(A) Molar tooth sign on axial MRI
(B) The cerebellar vermis is enlarged
(C) Elongated and thickened superior cerebellar peduncles
(D) Bat wing appearance of the fourth ventricle
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**（錯誤敘述）。Joubert syndrome 的小腦蚓部（vermis）為**小且發育不全（small and dysmorphic/hypoplastic）**，非「enlarged」。Molar tooth sign (A)、elongated/thickened SCPs (C)、bat wing 4th ventricle (D) 皆為正確特徵。
