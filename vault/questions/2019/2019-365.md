#交換 #2019交換 #CH 下列何者為 most common primary osseous malignancy of the chest wall?
(A) Multiple myeloma
(B) Kaposi sarcoma
(C) Undifferentiated pleomorphic sarcoma
(D) Chondrosarcoma
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**（Chondrosarcoma）。軟骨肉瘤為胸壁最常見原發性骨惡性腫瘤，好發於肋軟骨交界處。
