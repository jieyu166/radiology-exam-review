#交換 #2019交換 #BR 下列何者有誤？關於 accessory breast tissue
(1) Accessory breast tissue is residual breast tissue from normal embryologic development
(2) Accessory breast tissue can be found in up to 36% of the population
(3) The highest incidence occurs in Japanese population, lowest in white individuals
(4) Supernumerary breasts are found along the milk line from anterior axillary folds to inguinal region and medial thigh
(5) Aberrant breast tissue is an island of breast tissue usually located in proximity to the normal breast
??
**Ans: 2**
正確答案為 **2**（錯誤敘述）。副乳組織盛行率約 **2-6%**，非 36%。
