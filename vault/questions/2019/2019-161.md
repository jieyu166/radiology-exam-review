#交換 #2019交換 #CH If air reappears in a postpneumonectomy space that was previously opaque on serial chest radiographs, which of the following complications is most likely present?
(A) Recurrence of lung cancer
(B) Postpneumonectomy syndrome
(C) Cardiac herniation
(D) Bronchopleural fistula
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。肺切除術後空間本應逐漸變混濁；若氣體重新出現，應高度懷疑支氣管肋膜瘻管。詳見 [[postpneumonectomy-complications]]。
