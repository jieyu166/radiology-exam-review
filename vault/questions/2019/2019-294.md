#交換 #2019交換 #ABD Which of the following statement about common collaterals of portal hypertension is incorrect?
(A) Esophageal and paraesophageal varices are typically supplied by left gastric (coronary) vein.
(B) Gastric varices are typically supplied by left gastric (coronary) vein and short gastric veins.
(C) Paraumbilical varices are typically supplied by mesenteric veins.
(D) Left renal vein shunts are typically supplied by gastric (gastrorenal shunt) and splenic (splenorenal shunt) veins
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**（錯誤敘述）。Paraumbilical varices 由 **paraumbilical veins（Inf. Vein of Sappey）** 供應，非 mesenteric veins。
