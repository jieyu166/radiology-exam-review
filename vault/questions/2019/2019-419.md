#交換 #2019交換 #IR Which is NOT **true** about TACE?
(A) Lidocaine added to the chemotherapy mixture decreases the analgesic requirements post-embolization
(B) Portal vein visualization of iodized oil in post-procedure CT implies recurrence of HCC
(C) Grade 0 portal vein visualization is the best prognosis
(D) The blockage of the blood supply at the level of the distal subsegmental artery is important
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**。Portal vein visualization grading：grade 0 = 沒有 lipiodol 進入 portal vein，但這不代表預後最好。Portal vein visualization 反映 TACE 的滲透程度，適度滲透反映較佳的治療效果。詳見 [[tace]]。
