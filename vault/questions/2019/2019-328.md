#交換 #2019交換 #NR Regarding basilar artery occlusion, which of the following is correct?
(A) Proximal segment occlusion is most common
(B) Can cause bilateral thalamic infarction
(C) Associated with intracranial atherosclerotic stenosis (ICAS)
(D) All of the above
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**（以上皆是）。BAO 以近端阻塞最常見(A)、可致雙側丘腦梗塞(B)、與 ICAS 高度相關(C)。
