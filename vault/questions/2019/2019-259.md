#交換 #2019交換 #PE According to grading system for vesicoureteral reflux, ___ is defined by the presence of a very tortuous, dilated ureter.
(A) Grade II reflux
(B) Grade III reflux
(C) Grade IV reflux
(D) Grade V reflux
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D（Grade V reflux）**。Grade V = 嚴重擴張的輸尿管及腎盂腎盞系統 + 輸尿管迂曲（tortuous）+ 乳頭壓痕消失。詳見 [[vesicoureteral-reflux-grading]]。
