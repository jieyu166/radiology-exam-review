#交換 #2019交換 #MSK To evaluate crossover sign of FAI (femoral acetabular impingement) syndrome, we should have
(A) AP view of the pelvis
(B) AP view of the hip joint
(C) Ferguson view
(D) Judet view
??
**Ans: A**
- For initial evaluation of the acetabulum, plain radiographs of the pelvis are recommended.
- An additional view of the femoral neck such as Dunn views, cross-table lateral, frog-leg lateral or Meyer lateral for the assessment of the femoral head-neck junction
