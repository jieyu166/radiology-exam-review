#交換 #2019交換 #NR As compared with primary CNS lymphoma, which is not the MRI characteristics in GBM?
(A) Lower ADC value
(B) More tumor necrosis
(C) Large tumor size
(D) Irregular tumor margin
(E) Higher incidence
??
**Ans: A**
正確答案為 **A**。PCNSL 的 ADC value 比 GBM 更低（cellularity 更高）。Lower ADC 是 PCNSL 特徵而非 GBM。詳見 [[gbm-vs-pcnsl]]。
