#交換 #2019交換 #BR Regarding abnormal enhancement on breast MRI, which of the following statements is suggestive of benign lesion?
(A) Rim enhancement of a solid mass
(B) A circumscribed mass with non-enhancing, dark, internal septation
(C) Non-mass enhancement with clumped internal enhancement pattern
(D) Non-mass enhancement with linear distribution
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。Circumscribed mass with non-enhancing dark internal septation 提示良性（如 fibroadenoma）。Rim enhancement（非典型囊腫惡性率~40%）、enhancing internal septation、clumped/linear non-mass enhancement 均提示惡性。詳見 [[breast-mri-enhancement-patterns]]。
