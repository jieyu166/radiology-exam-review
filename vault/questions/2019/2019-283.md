#交換 #2019交換 #GU Which of the following signs is not a classic finding of renal TB?
(A) Papillary necrosis
(B) Rice bodies
(C) Infundibular strictures with localized caliectasis
(D) End-stage renal parenchymal disease manifesting as a calcified putty kidney
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**（Rice bodies）。Rice bodies 為**關節結核（TB arthritis）**之關節腔內特徵，非泌尿系統結核。腎結核經典表現包括乳頭壞死(A)、漏斗狹窄合併局部腎盞擴張(C)、灰泥腎(D)。
