#交換 #2019交換 #NR Which is NOT a characteristic finding of optic nerve sheath meningioma?
(A) Intratumor calcification
(B) Hypointense pseudocapsule
(C) Tram-tracking enhancement
(D) Perioptic cyst
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。Hypointense pseudocapsule 不是 ONSM 的特徵。ONSM 典型表現包括 intratumor calcification、tram-tracking enhancement、perioptic cyst。詳見 [[optic-nerve-sheath-meningioma]]。
