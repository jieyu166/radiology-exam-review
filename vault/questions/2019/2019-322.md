#交換 #2019交換 #BR Regarding digital breast tomosynthesis (DBT) and architectural distortion (AD), which statement is **WRONG**?
(A) AD is less commonly detected on DBT than on 2D mammography
(B) DBT can reduce false positive recall rates
(C) DBT provides better tissue overlap resolution
(D) DBT is useful for characterizing focal asymmetries
??
**Ans: A**
正確答案為 **A**（錯誤敘述）。Architectural distortion 在 DBT 上偵測率**更高**（非更低），因 DBT 去除組織重疊，使 AD 更容易辨識。
