#交換 #2019交換 #CV For a case of CAD-RADS 4AS**V**, what does it mean?
(A) There is a artery has stenosis > 90%
(B) There is a vulnerable plaque
(C) There is Positive remodeling > 120% as compared to proximal reference
(D) There is a stent, in which there is stenosis > 70%
??
**Ans: B**
[[20211218 CAD-RADS#4 Modifiers]]
A. 4A: 70-99% stenosis
B. V的意思是vulnerable plaque [[#High risk plaque]]
C. vulnerable plaque 有很多種，不一定是positive remodeling
D. S: stent 。敘述是4AS的意思，但題目是問V的意思
