#交換 #2019交換 #OB When chorionic villi invade into the myometrium, it is known as
(A) Placenta previa totalis
(B) Placenta accreta vera
(C) Placenta increta
(D) Placenta percreta
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**。Accreta = 附著 myometrium 表面；Increta = 侵入 myometrium；Percreta = 穿透至 serosa。詳見 [[placenta-accreta-spectrum]]。
