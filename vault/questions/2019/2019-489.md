#交換 #2019交換 #BR Which of the following statement about breast is **true**?
(A) Gynecomastia is not a risk factor of male breast cancer
(B) Fibroadenoma typically shows no enhancement on enhanced T1WI
(C) Adenosis is only found in elder women.
(D) Phyllodes tumor typically shows halo sign on mammography due to compression of surrounding tissue.
??
**Ans: A**
官方答案為 **A**，但此答案有爭議。NIH 研究顯示 gynecomastia（與 Klinefelter syndrome 及肥胖獨立）與男性乳癌風險增加 10 倍有關，因此 A 敘述可能為錯誤。選項 D（Phyllodes tumor 呈 halo sign）可能為較正確的選項。
