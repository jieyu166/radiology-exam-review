#交換 #2019交換 #US Usually the result of ischemic bowel (Crohn's disease, ulcerative colitis) this will appear as echogenic linear structures in the periphery of liver.
(A) Pneumothorax
(B) Hepatic portal vein gas
(C) Pneumobilia
(D) Pneumoperitoneum
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。門靜脈氣體（hepatic portal vein gas）在超音波上表現為肝臟周邊的線狀高回音結構。常見病因包括缺血性腸病、克隆氏症、潰瘍性結腸炎。與氣膽症（pneumobilia）不同，門靜脈氣體分布在肝臟周邊而非中央。
