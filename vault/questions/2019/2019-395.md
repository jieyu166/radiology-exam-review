#交換 #2019交換 #CH Managing incidental findings on thoracic CT, which one is **WRONG**?
(A) Short-axis size threshold of 15 mm guides the decision process
(B) Small, asymptomatic anterior mediastinum pure cystic lesions need no further work-up nor FU
(C) Incidental pericardial cyst, HU < 10, no thickened pericardium, fluid < 50 ml, no calcification, need no further work-up nor FU
(D) Main pulmonary artery >5.5 cm, or equal in diameter to the ascending aorta, should be reported as dilated
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。Main pulmonary artery **≥3.0 cm**（非 5.5 cm）或直徑等於 ascending aorta 即應報告為擴張。詳見 [[incidental-thoracic-ct]]。
