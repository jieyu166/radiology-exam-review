#交換 #2019交換 #ABD Which of the following statement regarding to the echo appearance of bowel wall is correct?
(A) Bowel lumen is well-demonstrated under echo, especially when air is present.
(B) Muscularis mucosae layer is hyperechoic.
(C) Submucosa is hyperechoic.
(D) Under most circumstances, US could identify five bowel wall layers
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**。腸壁超音波五層（gut signature）：superficial mucosa(高)/deep mucosa(低)/**submucosa(高,最亮最厚)**/muscularis propria(低)/serosa(高)。A 錯（空氣干擾回音）、B 錯（muscularis mucosae 為低回音）、D 為部分正確但非最佳答案（大多情況可辨 5 層，但考慮腸氣干擾）。
