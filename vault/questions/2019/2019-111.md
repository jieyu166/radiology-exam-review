#交換 #2019交換 #NR Concerning the normal myelination process in small children, which description is incorrect?
(A) Myelination process starts caudally to cranially.
(B) genu of corpus callosum shows high SI in T1WI by 6 months old
(C) anterior limb of internal capsule shows low SI in T2WI by 8 months old.
(D) splenium of corpus callosum turns dark earlier than genu in T2WI
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**（錯誤敘述）。內囊前肢（anterior limb of internal capsule）於 T1WI 約 3 個月即呈高訊號，**T2WI 約 6 個月**（非 8 個月）才轉為低訊號。髓鞘化由尾側到頭側進行、genu T1 6個月呈高訊號、splenium 較 genu 更早於 T2WI 轉暗等敘述皆正確。詳見 [[normal-myelination-pattern]]。
