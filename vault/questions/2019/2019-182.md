#交換 #2019交換 #GU Concerning retroperitoneal fibrosis, which one of the following is not **true**?
(A) Most commonly extends from renal hilum to pelvic brim.
(B) The IVU or RP showing medially displaced ureters.
(C) The plaque may be asymmetrical.
(D) The MR findings show T1WI lower signal intensity & T2WI high signal intensity of the plaque.
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**（錯誤敘述）。軟組織斑塊於T1與T2皆通常呈低訊號，除非合併活動性發炎才會使T2呈高訊號，並非固定「T1低、T2高」的表現。好發自腎門延伸至骨盆入口、輸尿管內移位移、斑塊可不對稱等敘述皆正確。詳見 [[retroperitoneal-fibrosis]]。
