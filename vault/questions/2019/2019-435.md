#交換 #2019交換 #MSK Which of the following is **wrong** about fibrous dysplasia?
(A) Radiographs depict a well-circumscribed lucent lesion in the metaphysis or diaphysis with a ground-glass appearance
(B) The most common benign rib lesion
(C) MRI is the best technique for characterizing fibrous dysplasia
(D) Periosteal reaction is usually not present unless it is associated with a pathologic fracture
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**。CT 比 MRI 更適合用於 FD 的影像診斷，因為 CT 能更清楚顯示 ground-glass matrix 及骨骼形態。MRI 在 FD 的 signal 變化多樣，難以做出特異性診斷。詳見 [[fibrous-dysplasia]]。
