#交換 #2019交換 #GU Which of the following descriptions about testicular torsion is **WRONG**?
(A) Most common in adolescents
(B) Bell clapper deformity is a predisposing factor
(C) Further ischemia causes hyperechogenicity of the testis, indicating nonviability
(D) Whirlpool sign of the spermatic cord on ultrasound
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**（錯誤敘述）。進一步缺血導致睪丸呈現 **hypoechogenicity（低回音）**而非 hyperechogenicity，表示睪丸組織壞死不可逆。A（青少年好發）、B（bell clapper deformity 為易發因素）、D（精索 whirlpool sign）皆為正確描述。
