#交換 #2019交換 #CH Which of the following is correct about lung cancers associated with cystic airspaces according to the Maschlchi (et al) classification system (which was based on Maki (et al) classification).
(A) Type I is that of cyst wall thickening.
(B) Type II is that of a nodule abutting the external aspect of a lung cyst.
(C) Type III is that of a nodule arising from the cyst wall and projecting into the cystic space.
(D) Type IV is that of multicystic lesion that contains areas of soft tissue attenuation.
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。依 Maschlchi/Maki 分類：Type I 為結節貼附囊壁外側（非壁增厚）；Type II 為結節自囊壁向內突出（非貼附外側）；Type III 為單純囊壁增厚（非結節突入）；**Type IV 為多囊性病灶合併軟組織密度區域**，此選項型別編號與描述正確對應。詳見 [[lung-cystic-airspace-malignancy]]。
