#交換 #2019交換 #NR What is **true** about structural MR imaging studies by using VBM?
(1) Atrophy in DLB (dementia with Lewy bodies) greater in the striatum, midbrain, and hypothalamus, but relative preservation of the hippocampus
(2) Prominent hippocampal atrophy in AD.
(3) Progressive supranuclear palsy results in characteristic atrophy of the midbrain giving rise to the "hummingbird" sign on sagittal images and the "Mickey Mouse" sign on axial images.
(4) All of above
??
**Ans: 4**
正確答案為 **4（All of above）**。DLB萎縮見於紋狀體/中腦/下視丘而海馬迴相對保留、AD顯著海馬迴萎縮、PSP中腦萎縮致hummingbird與Mickey Mouse sign，三者皆正確。詳見 [[dementia-neuroimaging-overview]]。
