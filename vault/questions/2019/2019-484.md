#交換 #2019交換 #Chest Which of the following bronchiectasis is lower lung predominant?
(A) Cystic Fibrosis
(B) Bronchiolitis obliterans
(C) Primary Ciliary Dyskinesia
(D) Allergic bronchopulmonary aspergillosis
(E) Tracheobronchomegaly
??
**Ans: **
Primary Ciliary Dyskinesia（PCD）的支氣管擴張以下肺野為主。CF 與 ABPA 為上肺/中央，Tracheobronchomegaly 為中央。
