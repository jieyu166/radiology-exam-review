#交換 #2019交換 #NR Which of the following is NOT a classic diagnosis of "Hair on ends" appearance of skull bone?
(A) Thalassemia
(B) Osteosarcoma
(C) Sickle cell disease
(D) Leukemic infiltration
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。Osteosarcoma 的 hair-on-end（sunburst）periosteal reaction 出現在長骨，不是頭骨 hair-on-end 外觀的典型診斷。Hair-on-end skull 見於 thalassemia、sickle cell、leukemia 等。詳見 [[hair-on-end-skull]]。
