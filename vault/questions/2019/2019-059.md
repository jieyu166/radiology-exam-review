#交換 #2019交換 #NR All of the following structures are located within the walls or cavity of the cavernous sinus **EXCEPT** for the:
(A) Occulomotor nerve
(B) Internal carotid artery
(C) Ophthalmic division of the trigeminal nerve
(D) Mandibular division of the trigeminal nerve
(E) Abducens nerve
??
**Ans: D**
![ADD cavernous sinus 1](data/images/obsidian/add-cavernous-sinus-1-52c8b653.png)
in the lateral wall from superior to inferior
1. oculomotor nerve (CN III)
2. trochlear nerve (CN IV)
3. trigeminal nerve (CN V)
	 - ophthalmic division
	 - maxillary division: within the very inferolateral aspect of the cavernous sinus wall or even outside the sinus rather than truly within it
