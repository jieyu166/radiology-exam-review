#交換 #2019交換 #NR About otosclerosis, which one is correct?
(A) It seldom involves bilaterally.
(B) CT is not useful for identifying causes of post-stapedectomy failure.
(C) Retrofenestral otosclerosis rarely occurs in isolation and is almost always seen in association with fenestral otosclerosis.
(D) Retrofenestral otosclerosis manifests as a characteristic "halo" around the oval window.
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**。Retrofenestral otosclerosis 幾乎不單獨出現，通常伴隨 fenestral 病變。A 錯（frequently bilateral）。B 錯（CT 可用於評估 post-stapedectomy failure）。D 錯（retrofenestral 表現為 cochlea 周圍 halo，非 oval window）。詳見 [[otosclerosis]]。
