#交換 #2018交換 #2019交換 #Physics Regarding gadolinium deposition in brain, which statement is correct?
(A) Only happens in patients with renal insufficiency
(B) Only associated with linear gadolinium agents
(C) The clinical significance of gadolinium deposition remains unknown
(D) Can only be detected by inductively coupled plasma mass spectrometry (ICPMS), not by in vivo MRI
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**。Gd 沉積的臨床意義仍未知。A 錯（腎功能正常者亦可發生）、B 錯（linear 為主但 macrocyclic 亦有報告）、D 錯（T1WI 上可見 dentate nucleus/globus pallidus 訊號增加）。詳見 [[gadolinium-contrast]]。
