#交換 #2019交換 #Physics Which of the following about Hepatobiliary MRI contrast agents is **false**?
(A) Gd-EOB-DTPA(Primovist) is excretion by renal and liver
(B) Gd-EOB-DTPA(Primovist) is with higher liver parenchyma enhancement than Gd-BOPTA(Multihence)
(C) The delayed time of Gd-BOPTA (Multihence) is shorter than Gd-EOB-DTPA (Primovist)
(D) OATP1 transporter of liver cell is essential for the enhancement of gadolinium-based agents
??
**Ans: C**
- A. 正確 50/50%
- B. 正確。Multihence只有2-4%被肝uptake
- C. 較長。Primovist 20分，Multihence 90-120分太久
- D. 正確。OATP1B1/B3 uptake，之後由MRP2排到膽道。[[20200716 Primovist MRI]]
