#交換 #2019交換 #ABD Which of the following statement for neuroendocrine tumor of pancreas is correct except?
(A) An indolent tumor with malignant potential
(B) Insulinoma is one of the functioning PNET
(C) Hepatic matastasis are always hypovascular.
(D) FDG-PET is not indicated because most PNET are negative
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**（錯誤敘述）。PNET肝轉移典型為動脈期高強化（hypervascular），並非一律乏血管。惰性腫瘤伴惡性潛能、insulinoma為functioning PNET之一、FDG-PET於分化良好PNET敏感度受限等敘述皆正確。詳見 [[pancreatic-neuroendocrine-tumors]]。
