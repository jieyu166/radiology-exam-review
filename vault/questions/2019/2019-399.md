#交換 #2019交換 #MSK Which of the followings about giant cell tumor (GCT) of bone is correct?
(A) Presence of a bulging extraosseous soft tissue mass specifies malignant GCT
(B) Presence of lung metastasis specifies malignant GCT
(C) Presence of multiple bone lesions specifies malignant GCT
(D) None of the above
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。GCT 即使有侵犯性表現仍為 low-grade tumor。Lung metastasis 稱為 benign metastasizing GCT（預後良好）。約 5-10% 為 malignant，sarcomatous transformation 多見於放療後。詳見 [[giant-cell-tumor]]。
