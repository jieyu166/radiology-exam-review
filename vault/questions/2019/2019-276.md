#交換 #2019交換 #GU Which of the following structures makes up the boundary of the perineum?
(A) Puborectalis muscle
(B) Pubococcygeus muscle
(C) Sacrotuberous ligaments
(D) Bulbospongiosus muscle
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**（薦結節韌帶）。會陰邊界：前方 pubic symphysis、前外側 ischiopubic ramus、**後外側 sacrotuberous ligament**、後方 coccyx/sacrum。A/B/D 皆為會陰區域肌肉，非邊界結構。
