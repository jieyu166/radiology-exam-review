#交換 #2019交換 #NR Regarding the WHO 2016 classification of medulloblastoma molecular subtypes, which one is **WRONG**?
(A) WNT-activated: centered in cerebellar peduncle/CPA, best prognosis
(B) SHH-activated: rostral cerebellar hemisphere
(C) Group 4: midline, most common subtype
(D) Group 3: midline, good prognosis with well-defined margins
(E) Group 3: poor prognosis with ill-defined margins and extensive enhancement
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**（錯誤敘述）。Group 3 medulloblastoma 預後**最差**，呈邊界不清 (ill-defined) 且顯著增強，並非 good prognosis with well-defined margins。
