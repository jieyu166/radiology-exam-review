#交換 #2019交換 #ABD Which one is NOT correct about GIST (gastrointestinal stromal tumor)?
(A) Large gastric GIST with central necrosis and ulceration should be differentiated with gastric lymphoma.
(B) The common sites of GIST location are stomach > colon > retroperitoneum > mesentery > small intestine
(C) lymphadenopathy is uncommon for GIST
(D) GIST resoponding to Imatinib therapy shows cystic change with myxoid degeneration and scarring.
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**（錯誤敘述）。GIST好發部位頻率為胃（55.6%）>**小腸（31.8%）**>大腸直腸（6.0%）>其他（5.5%）>食道（0.7%），小腸為第二常見，並非大腸/後腹腔/腸繫膜。詳見 [[gist-imaging]]。
