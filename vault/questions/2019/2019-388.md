#交換 #2019交換 #BR Regarding the following artifacts of digital breast tomosynthesis (DBT), which one of them is rarely appreciated because of the masking effect of out-of-plane blurring?
(A) Blurring-Ripple Artifacts
(B) Truncation Artifacts
(C) Loss of Skin and Superficial Tissue Resolution
(D) Motion Artifacts
(E) None of the above
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。Motion artifact 在 DBT 因 out-of-plane blurring 的遮蔽效應而不易被察覺。詳見 [[dbt-artifact]]。
