#交換 #2019交換 #Breast Which of the following is a descriptor of mass shape in the BIRADS lexicon for ultrasound?
(A) Speculated
(B) Lobulated
(C) Triangular
(D) Oval
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。BI-RADS 超音波 lexicon 中 mass shape 描述詞為 oval／round／irregular；spiculated、（micro）lobulated 屬於 margin 描述詞，triangular 非 lexicon 用詞。詳見 [[bi-rads-mass-lexicon]]。
