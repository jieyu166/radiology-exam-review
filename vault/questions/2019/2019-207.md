#交換 #2019交換 #Breast 有關 Gynaecomastia，下列何者有誤？
(A) It is a benign excess of the male breast tissue, that is usually irreversible.
(B) It may appear as an increased sub-areolar density, which may be flame-shaped.
(C) The differential diagnosis is male breast cancer.
(D) Focal gynaecomastia can variably appear as a retroareolar, triangular or hypoechoic mass.
??
**Ans: A**
正確答案為 **A**（錯誤敘述）。Gynecomastia並非一律不可逆——早期（<1年）nodular/florid phase仍可逆，僅慢性（>1年）dendritic/quiescent phase纖維化後才不可逆。詳見 [[gynecomastia-imaging]]。
