#交換 #2019交換 #PE Which is not **true** in the radiographic features in congenital syphilis?
(A) Bony changes of congenital syphilis predominately involve the bone metaphyses.
(B) Bony involvement includes metaphyseal lucent bands, which may be a nonspecific response to systemic illness or may be due to syphilitic granulation tissue in the metaphyses.
(C) A more specific finding is localized bony destruction of the medial portion of the proximal tibial metaphysis (Wimberger sign).
(D) Other findings include metaphyseal serration ("sawtooth metaphyses"), and diaphyseal involvement with periosteal reaction.
(E) Bony destruction in congenital syphilis may not lead to pathologic fractures, and may mimic battered child syndrome.
??
**Ans: E**
正確答案為 **E**（錯誤敘述）。先天性梅毒之骨破壞「可以」導致病理性骨折（may lead to pathologic fractures），而非「不會」。A-D 皆為正確敘述。詳見 [[congenital-syphilis-skeletal]]。
