#交換 #2019交換 #CV Regarding chronic venous insufficiency (CVI), which one of the following descriptions is **wrong**?
(A) CVI is the diagnosis given to patients with venous dysfunction causing edema, skin change, or ulcerations
(B) The CEAP classification provides insight into the clinical presentation, etiology, anatomy, and pathophysiology
(C) The CVI patients have edema, skin changes like dermatosclerosis pigmentation, corona phlebectatica or eczema, or healed or active venous ulcer
(D) May-Thurner's syndrome is occlusion or stenosis of the right iliac vein due to compression by the overriding left common iliac artery
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**（錯誤敘述）。May-Thurner syndrome 是**左**髂總靜脈受**右**髂總動脈壓迫（夾在動脈與 L5 椎體之間），D 選項把左右方向寫反了。
