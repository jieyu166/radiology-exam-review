#交換 #2019交換 #NR Which of the following paragangliomas OCCURS **RARELY** in the neck?
(A) Carotid body tumor
(B) Glomus vagale tumor
(C) Laryngeal glomus tumor
(D) Glomus jugulare tumor
??
**Ans: C**
- Among head and neck paragangliomas,
  44-48% are carotid body tumors,
  16-24% are glomus jugulare,
  20% are glomus tympanicum, and
  8% are glomus vagale. From [Glomus Jugulare - StatPearls - NCBI Bookshelf (nih.gov)](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK560489/)
- Laryngeal glomus連提都沒提到，pubmed只有一些case report
