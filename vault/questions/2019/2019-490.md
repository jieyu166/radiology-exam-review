#交換 #2019交換 #CH Which description about pulmonary embolism is **wrong**?
(A) Predisposing factors include prolonged immobilization and the frequency of surgical procedures.
(B) CT pulmonary angiography has a very low sensitivity and high specificity and negative predictive value
(C) A peripheral area of more or less wedge-shaped consolidation may indicate associated infarction (Hampton's hump)
(D) The chest radiograph is of limited value. It may be normal or reveal non-specific atelectasis.
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。CTPA 對肺栓塞的敏感度為**高**（83-100%），並非 very low sensitivity。CTPA 確實具有高特異度與高陰性預測值。
