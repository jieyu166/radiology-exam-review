#交換 #2019交換 #GU Which of the following imaging strategy for acute pyelonephritis (APN) is least appropriate?
(A) CT is appropriate in adult patient who are diabetic, immunocompromised or complicated.
(B) Reflux study is appropriate in pediatric patient who had suspected functional disorder of urinary system.
(C) CT or MRI is appropriate in pediatric patient who had repeated pyelonephritis without definite findings in reflux study.
(D) CT is important in patient with simple APN to exam the extend of infection.
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**（最不適當）。單純性 APN 不需常規 CT 評估感染範圍。CT 主要適用於複雜性、糖尿病/免疫低下(A)、或兒童反覆發作(C)。
