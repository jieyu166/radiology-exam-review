#交換 #2019交換 #NR Which one is not the neurovascular contents of superior orbital fissure?
(A) Trigeminal nerve (V1)
(B) Vidian nerve
(C) Oculomotor nerve (III)
(D) Trochlear nerve (IV)
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。Vidian nerve 走 vidian canal（pterygopalatine fossa 後方），不經過 superior orbital fissure。SOF 通過 CN III、IV、V1、VI 及 superior ophthalmic vein。詳見 [[superior-orbital-fissure]]。
