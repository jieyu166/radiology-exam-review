#交換 #2019交換 #MSK Which of the following is NOT a normal variant of glenoid labrum?
(A) Subglenoid recess.
(B) Buford complex.
(C) Sublabral foramen.
(D) Superior recess.
??
**Ans: A**
正確答案為 **A（Subglenoid recess）**。公認之盂唇正常變異包括Buford complex、sublabral foramen、superior sublabral sulcus/recess、pseudo-SLAP lesion等，「subglenoid recess」不在標準正常變異清單中。詳見 [[glenoid-labral-lesions]]。
