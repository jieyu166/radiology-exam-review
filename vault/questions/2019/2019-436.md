#交換 #2019交換 #MSK About elbow ultrasound, which one of the following is **WRONG**?
(A) The lacertus fibrosus extends from the myotendinous junction of the distal biceps to the medial deep fascia of the forearm
(B) The lacertus fibrosus covers the ulnar nerve and the brachial artery and its function is to keep the biceps tendon in the appropriate position
(C) The distal portion of the biceps tendon is covered by an extrasynovial paratenon and by the bicipitoradial bursa
(D) This bicipitoradial bursa is normally not visible at US unless distended with fluid effusion
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。Lacertus fibrosus 覆蓋的是 **median nerve** 與 brachial artery，非 ulnar nerve。詳見 [[elbow-us-anatomy]]。
