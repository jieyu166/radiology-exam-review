#交換 #2019交換 #MSK About hand and wrist imaging, which one of the following is **WRONG**?
(A) Scaphoid fractures represent about 70% of all carpal fractures, dividing into stable and unstable fractures, which is important to define surgical and nonsurgical treatment
(B) Scaphoid tubercle fractures and incomplete waist fractures are stable
(C) Comminuted fractures, fracture-dislocations, oblique distal pole fractures, proximal pole fractures, and complete/displaced waist fractures are all considered unstable
(D) Ligament injuries can lead to progressive instability and further deterioration of the wrist joint; Scapholunate dissociation, resulting from injury of the lunotriquetral ligament is the most common form of carpal instability
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。Scapholunate dissociation 源自 **scapholunate ligament** 損傷，非 lunotriquetral ligament。詳見 [[carpal-instability]]。
