#交換 #2019交換 #GU Which of the following descriptions about urethral injury is **WRONG**?
(A) Posterior urethral injury is more common than anterior urethral injury
(B) Retrograde urethrography is the gold standard for diagnosis
(C) Urethral injury is more common in females than males
(D) Pelvic fracture is the most common cause of posterior urethral injury
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**（錯誤敘述）。尿道損傷在**男性**遠比女性常見，因男性尿道較長且固定於恥骨聯合，更易受骨盆骨折等外傷影響。女性尿道短且可動性大，損傷罕見。
