#交換 #2016交換 #2019交換 #NR Regarding CLIPPERS (chronic lymphocytic inflammation with pontine perivascular enhancement responsive to steroids), which statement is INCORRECT?
(A) It is a brainstem encephalitis centered on the pons
(B) It shows prominent vasogenic edema
(C) It is responsive to corticosteroids
(D) It shows punctate and curvilinear enhancement predominantly in the pons
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。CLIPPERS 的特徵為**無明顯 vasogenic edema**（lack of significant edema），這是與其他 brainstem lesions 鑑別的重要線索。詳見 [[clippers]]。
