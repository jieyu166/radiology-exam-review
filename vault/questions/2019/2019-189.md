#交換 #2019交換 #MSK 關於 chronic peripheral nerve compression 在 ultrasound 的 findings，下列何者錯誤?
(A) A thinner nerve bundle at the site of compression.
(B) Fusiform enlargement is usually 2~4 cm proximal to the compression site.
(C) It is possible to differentiate nerve edema from fibrosis.
(D) The nerve eventually recovers its normal size and fasicular form at a distance from the injury site.
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**（錯誤敘述）。超音波**無法**可靠區分神經水腫（edema）與纖維化（fibrosis）。壓迫處神經變細、近端2-4公分處紡錘狀腫脹、遠離受傷處神經恢復正常大小與束狀結構等敘述皆正確。詳見 [[nerve-entrapment-syndromes]]。
