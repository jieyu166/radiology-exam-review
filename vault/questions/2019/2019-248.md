#交換 #2019交換 #US Which of the following is NOT a typical ultrasound finding of cirrhosis?
(A) Nodular contour
(B) Increased parenchymal echogenicity
(C) Atrophy of caudate lobe
(D) Splenomegaly
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C（Atrophy of caudate lobe）**（錯誤敘述）。肝硬化時 caudate lobe 因獨立靜脈回流而代償性「增大（hypertrophy）」，非萎縮。Nodular contour、increased echogenicity、splenomegaly 皆為典型表現。詳見 [[cirrhosis-ultrasound]]。
