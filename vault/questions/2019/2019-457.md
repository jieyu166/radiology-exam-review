#交換 #2019交換 #CH Which one of the following descriptions about Boerhaave syndrome is **wrong**?
(A) Boerhaave syndrome refers to an esophageal rupture secondary to forceful vomiting and retching
(B) The clinical diagnosis in typical cases is based on Mackler's triad: vomiting, sudden onset of severe chest pain, and pneumothorax
(C) Esophagography typically demonstrates contrast medium extravasation at a supra-diaphragmatic level
(D) CT typically shows periesophageal air collections at a supra-diaphragmatic level
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**（錯誤敘述）。Mackler's triad 為嘔吐、劇烈胸痛、**皮下氣腫（subcutaneous emphysema）**，而非氣胸（pneumothorax）。
