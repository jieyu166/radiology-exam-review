#交換 #2019交換 #GI Which of the following diseases is MOST frequently seen in patients with neutropenia who are undergoing chemotherapy?
(A) Ischemic colitis
(B) Typhlitis
(C) Radiation colitis
(D) Graft-versus-host disease
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。Typhlitis（neutropenic enterocolitis）為化療後嗜中性白血球減少症患者最常見的腸道併發症，主要侵犯盲腸。Ischemic colitis(A) 好發老年人、radiation colitis(C) 需有放射治療史、GVHD(D) 需有移植史。
