#交換 #2019交換 #NR The Spetzler-Martin arteriovenous malformation (AVM) grading system according to the following three features, which of following was **WRONG**?
(A) Size of nidus
(B) Eloquence of adjacent brain
(C) Presence of venous ectasia
(D) Venous drainage
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**（venous ectasia）。Spetzler-Martin AVM 分級依據三項：nidus 大小(A)、鄰近腦 eloquence(B)、靜脈引流型態 superficial vs deep(D)。**Venous ectasia 非 SM 評分項目**。
