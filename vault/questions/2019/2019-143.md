#交換 #2019交換 #PED About the lesions in the presacral region in children, which the following description is CORRECT?
(A) Sacrococcygeal germ cell tumors are malignant germ cell tumors.
(B) Anterior sacral meningocele is most common type of congenital spinal meningocoele. It may occur as part of ASP triad (anorectal malformation, sacrococcygeal osseous defect, and presacral mass).
(C) Tailgut cysts only manifested in childhood.
(D) Cystic lymphatic malformations usually appear as fluid-fluid level and hetrogeneous high signal intensity on T2-weighted images
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。前薦骨腦膜膨出是兒童先天性脊髓膜膨出最常見類型，可作為Currarino（ASP）三徵之一部分。薦尾骨生殖細胞瘤依分化程度可良性或惡性（非皆惡性）、尾腸囊腫可延續至成年期才表現（非僅兒童期）、囊性淋巴管畸形常態呈均勻高T2訊號（液液平面僅見於急性出血時）。詳見 [[pediatric-presacral-masses]]。
