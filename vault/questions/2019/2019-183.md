#交換 #2019交換 #IR Which one of the following is not the indication of percutaneous ethanol injection therapy (PEIT) for hepatic tumors?
(A) Lesions confined to the liver
(B) Tumor is smaller than 3cm
(C) A focal, nodular- type lesion
(D) Poor liver function
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。肝功能不良並非PEIT的適應症。病灶侷限於肝臟、腫瘤小於2公分（非3公分）、局部結節型病灶皆為適應症。詳見 [[percutaneous-ethanol-injection-therapy]]。
