#交換 #2019交換 #ABD Which of the following statements best describes the clinical and pathologic features of pseudomyxoma peritonei?
(A) It is a distinct pathologic diagnosis.
(B) It develops from low-grade mucinous carcinomas of the appendix.
(C) It has a worse prognosis compared with that of peritoneal mucinous carcinomatosis.
(D) It occurs more commonly in men than women.
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。Pseudomyxoma peritonei多源自闌尾低惡性度黏液性腫瘤破裂播種（peritoneal adenomucinosis）。其為臨床-影像症候群而非單一病理診斷、預後優於腹膜黏液性癌病（HAMN/mucinous carcinoma）、女性略多於男性（非男性較常見）。詳見 [[pseudomyxoma-peritonei]]。
