#交換 #2019交換 #Breast Which of the following findings of invasive lobular carcinoma is most commonly seen at ultrasound?
(A) Vague shadowing without a definite mass.
(B) Hypoechoic mass.
(C) Hyperechoic mass.
(D) Circumscribed mass.
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。ILC最常見超音波表現為異質性低回音腫塊，邊緣呈角狀或不規則邊界，合併後方聲影。詳見 [[invasive-lobular-carcinoma]]。
