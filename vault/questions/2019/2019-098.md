#交換 #2019交換 #NR About risk factor of spontaneous lateral sphenoid cephalocele, which is incorrect?
(A) Obese
(B) Idiopathic intracranial hypertension
(C) Poor pneumatization of sphenoid sinus
(D) Middle-aged women
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**（錯誤敘述）。自發性外側蝶骨腦膜腦膨出（spontaneous lateral sphenoid cephalocele）之危險因子包含肥胖、中年女性、以及 idiopathic intracranial hypertension；解剖上與蝶竇**過度氣化（hyperpneumatization，而非氣化不良）**造成骨壁變薄相關，故「poor pneumatization」為錯誤敘述。詳見 [[spontaneous-skull-base-cephalocele]]。
