#交換 #2019交換 #IR Which one of the following is not the suitable embolic material for treatment of duodenal bleeding?
(A) Gelfoam
(B) Polyvinyl alcohol (Ivalon)
(C) Lipiodol
(D) Microcoils
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**。Lipiodol並非栓塞材料，而是化療栓塞用之油性顯影劑/藥物載體，不適用於治療出血。Gelfoam、PVA顆粒、微線圈皆為適用之栓塞材料。詳見 [[embolic-materials]]。
