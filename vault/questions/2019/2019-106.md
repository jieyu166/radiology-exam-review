#交換 #2019交換 #CV Which of the following concerning coronary artery aneurysm (CAA) is not correct?
(A) CAA is now more precisely defined as a dilated vessel segment that measures at least 1.5 times larger in diameter than the diameters of the adjacent normal arterial segments.
(B) Atherosclerotic coronary artery aneurysms (ACAAs) are usually either saccular or fusiform whereas postinflammatory CAAs are usually fusiform.
(C) The term "giant" aneurysm is applied when the size exceeds 5 cm in diameter.
(D) Of all of the different causes of CAAs, atherosclerotic disease is the most common and accounts for at least 50% of CAAs diagnosed in adults.
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**（敘述方向顛倒）。動脈粥樣硬化性冠狀動脈瘤（ACAA）通常呈囊狀或紡錘狀，而**發炎後（postinflammatory，如川崎病）冠狀動脈瘤才是通常呈紡錘狀**——選項將兩者對應關係寫反。CAA 定義（≥1.5倍鄰近正常節段）、giant 定義（成人>5cm）、動脈粥樣硬化為最常見病因（占成人≥50%）等敘述皆正確。詳見 [[coronary-artery-aneurysm]]。
