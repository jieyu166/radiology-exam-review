#交換 #2019交換 #GU Which of the following is NOT a typical MRI finding of endometrial cancer?
(A) Intermediate signal intensity on T2WI relative to normal endometrium
(B) Isointensity on T1WI relative to myometrium
(C) Low signal intensity on ADC map
(D) Hyperenhancement on post-contrast images
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**（非典型表現）。子宮內膜癌在 T1C+(Gd) 增強後**強化程度低於正常子宮內膜**，非高增強。A（T2WI 中等訊號）、B（T1WI 等訊號）、C（ADC 低訊號）皆為典型表現。
