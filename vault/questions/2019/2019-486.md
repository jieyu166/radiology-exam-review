#交換 #2019交換 #GI 關於 paraduodenal hernia 之敘述，何者錯誤?
(A) Left paraduodenal hernia: the fossa of Landzert
(B) Right paraduodenal hernia: the fossa of Waldeyer
(C) Left paraduodenal hernia: dilated small bowel loops lying between the pancreas and stomach to the left of the ligament of Treitz
(D) Right paraduodenal hernia: presence of mesentery between the inferior vena cava and main portal vein (hernia of foramen of Winslow)
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。Right paraduodenal hernia 是小腸經 fossa of Waldeyer 疝出（升結腸系膜與後腹膜先天融合不完全所致），小腸環位於 SMV、SMA 與右結腸血管後方、右下腹腔。選項 D 描述的是 **foramen of Winslow hernia**（網膜孔疝氣），位於 IVC 與 portal vein 之間，並非 right paraduodenal hernia。
