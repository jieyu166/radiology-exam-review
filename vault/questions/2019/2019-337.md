#交換 #2019交換 #ABD The risk of bowel incarceration in hernias, from high to low:
(A) Inguinal hernia (direct) > Inguinal hernia (indirect) > Femoral hernia
(B) Inguinal hernia (indirect) > Inguinal hernia (direct) > Femoral hernia
(C) Femoral hernia > Inguinal hernia (direct) > Inguinal hernia (indirect)
(D) Femoral hernia > Inguinal hernia (indirect) > Inguinal hernia (direct)
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。嵌頓風險由高到低：Femoral > Indirect inguinal > Direct inguinal。股疝股環狹窄堅硬，嵌頓風險最高。
