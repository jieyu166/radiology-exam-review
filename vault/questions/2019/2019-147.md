#交換 #2019交換 #US Which of the following occurs when a sound wave strikes a large, smooth surface at a 90° angle?
(A) refraction
(B) specular (鏡面) reflection
(C) Rayleigh's scatter
(D) nonspecular reflection
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。聲波以90度角撞擊大而平滑的界面時發生鏡面反射（specular reflection）。詳見 [[ultrasound-reflection-scatter]]。
