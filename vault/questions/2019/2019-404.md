#交換 #2019交換 #NR Which of the following description about thyroid radiofrequency ablation is NOT **true**?
(A) The transisthmic approach refers to the insertion of a RF electrode via the isthmus
(B) The electrode tip can be initially positioned in the deepest and most remote portion of the nodule, with subsequent retraction to the most superficial portion of the nodule
(C) The ablation is safe when the electrode moves in a forward direction even toward the "danger triangle" between the trachea and thyroid gland
(D) Ablation is safer when the tip of the electrode is well positioned within a 1-2 mm distance from the target nodule margin
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**。Danger triangle 位於 trachea 與 thyroid 之間，electrode 朝此方向移動是**危險的**，非安全。詳見 [[thyroid-rfa]]。
