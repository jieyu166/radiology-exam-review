#交換 #2019交換 #GU Which is **true** about scrotal trauma?
(A) Scrotal trauma is usually a single injury resulting from a direct forceful compression of the testis or testes against the pubic ramus.
(B) The testis may rupture, fracture, or undergo torsion.
(C) Rupture or fracture occurs when the tunica albuginea is torn.
(D) All of the above are true.
??
**Ans: D**
官方原始答案標示為A，惟詳解手寫更正註記明確指出「答案應該是D（全對）」。A（單一損傷機轉）、B（可能破裂/骨折/扭轉）、C（tunica albuginea撕裂）皆為正確敘述，故採用更正後答案D。詳見 [[scrotal-trauma-imaging]]。
