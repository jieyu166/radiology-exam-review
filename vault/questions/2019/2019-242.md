#交換 #2019交換 #CH The accessory cardiac bronchus most commonly arises from which of the following site?
(A) Medial wall of LLL bronchus
(B) Medial wall of RLL bronchus
(C) Medial wall of bronchus intermedius
(D) Medial wall of RML bronchus
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C（Medial wall of bronchus intermedius）**。副心臟支氣管最常見起源自中間支氣管內側壁。詳見 [[accessory-cardiac-bronchus]]。
