#交換 #2019交換 #NR What is **true** about the common abnormal protein or peptide accumulation in the demented brain?
(1) τ and β amyloid in AD
(2) α synuclein in DLB; and
(3) τ, TDP, or FUS in FTD,
(4) All of above
??
**Ans: 4**
正確答案為 **4（All of above）**。AD為tau與β-amyloid堆積、DLB為α-synuclein堆積、FTD為tau/TDP-43/FUS堆積，三者皆正確。詳見 [[dementia-neuroimaging-overview]]。
