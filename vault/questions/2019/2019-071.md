#交換 #2019交換 #NR Which of the following descriptions about brain tumor is **wrong**?
(A) Oligodendroglioma is an infiltrating glioma that carries both IDH mutation and 1p/19q codeletion.
(B) Astrocytoma is an infiltrating glioma that is subdivided in the classification by the presence of IDH mutation and never contains 1p/19q codeletion.
(C) Primary glioblastoma (GBM) generally occurred in older patients, carried a worse prognosis and most of them were IDH mutant.
(D) IDH mutation leads to accumulation of the oncometabolite 2-hydroxyglutarate (2HG) within tumor cells, which can be detected at MR spectroscopy.
(E) WNT-activated medulloblastoma centered in the cerebellar peduncle or cerebellopontine angle, SHH-activated tumors within the rostral cerebellar hemisphere, and group 3 and 4 tumors located at the midline.
??
**Ans: C**
A. **正確**
B. WHO2016正確。WHO2021錯誤。因為有另一個部分可以繞過1p/19q codeletion(ATRX loss+p53 loss) 。
C. **錯誤**。GBM現在都是IDH-wildtype
D. **正確**。2-HG很愛考。
E. [[medulloblastoma]]等等會在說明
