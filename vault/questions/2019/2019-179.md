#交換 #2019交換 #ABD Each of the following statements accurately characterizes lymphoma of the peritoneum except ______.
(A) Primary peritoneal lymphoma is associated with human herpes virus 8 and Epstein-Barr virus.
(B) At CT, peritoneal lymphoma from a preexisting nodal lymphoma mimics peritoneal carcinomatosis.
(C) At CT, ascites is the principal manifestation of primary peritoneal lymphoma.
(D) At CT, nodular peritoneal thickening is the principal manifestation of primary peritoneal lymphoma.
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**（錯誤敘述）。原發性腹膜淋巴瘤CT上最主要表現為不合併淋巴結病變之惡性腹水，並非結節狀腹膜增厚。與HHV-8/EBV相關、既存結節性淋巴瘤轉移可擬態腹膜癌病、腹水為主要表現等敘述皆正確。詳見 [[peritoneal-lymphoma]]。
