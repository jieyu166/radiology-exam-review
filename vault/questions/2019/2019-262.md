#交換 #2019交換 #NR About temporal bone fracture, which one is incorrect?
(A) The transverse fracture type is more common.
(B) Classification of temporal bone fractures is according to their orientation relative to the long axis of the petrous pyramid.
(C) A transverse fracture is associated with a higher risk of facial nerve injury.
(D) Conductive hearing loss after temporal bone trauma is usually related to hemotympanum or tympanic membrane rupture, rather than ossicular chain disruption.
??
**Ans: A**
正確答案為 **A**（錯誤敘述）。**Longitudinal fracture 才是最常見（70-80%）**，非 transverse。B、C、D 皆為正確敘述。詳見 [[temporal-bone-fracture]]。
