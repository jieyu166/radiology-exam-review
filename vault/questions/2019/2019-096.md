#交換 #2019交換 #ABD What is the "**wrong**" description about ureteral endometriosis (UE)?
(A) The prevalence of UE varies considerably from 0.01% to 1.7% in women with endometriosis.
(B) UE has a peak incidence in patients aged 30-35 years
(C) UE is commonly unilateral, with a left predisposition
(D) MRI is the first-line technique for the assessment of UE.
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**（錯誤敘述）。輸尿管子宮內膜異位症（ureteral endometriosis）評估的**第一線影像檢查為超音波（ultrasonography）**，而非 MRI；經陰道超音波可偵測卵巢子宮內膜異位瘤及直腸陰道中隔結節，腹部超音波可辨識泌尿道阻塞，必要時再以 MRI 進一步評估、並可依 step-by-step 演算法安排檢查順序。盛行率、好發年齡（30–35歲）與左側偏好（left predisposition）之敘述皆正確。詳見 [[ureteral-endometriosis]]。
