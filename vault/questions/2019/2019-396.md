#交換 #2019交換 #GI Regarding chemotherapy-induced sinusoidal injury of liver, which of the followings is NOT correct?
(A) involving non-tumor part of liver
(B) also called sinusoid obstruction syndrome (SOS)
(C) sinusoidal dilatation, peliosis and nodular regenerative hyperplasia are pathologic findings
(D) MRI usually depict well-defined T2 hyperintense nodule with significant diffusion restriction on DWI
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。SOS 結節在 DWI 呈 iso-signal intensity（非 restricted diffusion），ADC 比 metastasis 高。詳見 [[sinusoidal-obstruction-syndrome]]。
概念：[[sinusoidal-obstruction-syndrome]]
