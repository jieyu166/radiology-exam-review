#交換 #2019交換 #PED About Hereditary Renal Cystic Disorders, which the following description is CORRECT?
(A) Multiple and/or bilateral macroscopic renal cysts is never occur in the autosomal dominant polycystic kidney disease (ADPKD) while pediatric period.
(B) The cysts in Autosomal Recessive Polycystic Kidney Disease (ARPKD) are more often complicated by hemorrhage than in ADPKD.
(C) Tuberous Sclerosis Complex represent a heterogeneous group of hereditary neurocutaneous multisystem disorders that commonly affect tissues of ectodermal origin (eg, the CNS, eyes, and skin).
(D) Renal cell carcinoma associated with von Hippel-Lindau syndrome is commonly multifocal, may be bilateral, with a competely cystic appearance.
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**。結節性硬化症（TSC）屬異質性遺傳性神經皮膚多系統疾病，常侵犯外胚層來源組織（CNS、眼睛、皮膚）。ADPKD於兒童期即可出現多發/雙側巨觀囊腫（非絕不發生）、ARPKD囊腫較ADPKD更常合併出血（非相反）、VHL相關RCC呈複雜囊性外觀（非完全囊性）。詳見 [[hereditary-renal-cystic-disorders]]。
