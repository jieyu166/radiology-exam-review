#交換 #2019交換 #CH Which disease can be characterized with air trapping in the appearance of mosaic lung attenuation?
(A) Chronic eosinophilic pneumonia
(B) Pulmonary vascular disease
(C) Small airway disease
(D) Cryptogenic organizing pneumonia
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**（小氣道疾病）。Air trapping 合併 mosaic lung attenuation 為小氣道疾病之典型表現。呼氣相可確認 air trapping。
