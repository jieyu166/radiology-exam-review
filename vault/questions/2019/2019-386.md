#交換 #2019交換 #IR Which of the followings noninvasive techniques can eliminate standing arterial waves on angiography?
(A) Increase of filming rate
(B) Increase of contrast injection rate
(C) Larger volume of contrast
(D) Vasodilator
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。Vasodilator 使血管舒張可消除 standing arterial waves，其餘選項無效。詳見 [[angiography-standing-wave]]。
