#交換 #2019交換 #GU A 75-year-old male with PSA level 8 microgram/L; prostate cancer of Gleason score 3+3; mpMRI revealing no clinically significant tumor in prostate and no metastatic disease. Which of the following strategy of treatment should be suggested?
(A) active surveillance
(B) radical prostatectomy
(C) hormone therapy
(D) radiotherapy
??
**Ans: A**
正確答案為 **A**。Gleason 3+3、PSA<10、mpMRI 無 significant tumor 屬低風險攝護腺癌，標準處置為 active surveillance。詳見 [[prostate-active-surveillance]]。
