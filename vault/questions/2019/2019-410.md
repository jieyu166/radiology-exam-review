#交換 #2019交換 #CH Which is the sign of CT diagnosis of chronic pulmonary thromboembolism?
(A) Calcified thrombus in pulmonary arteries
(B) Transpleural systemic collateral vessels (eg, intercostal arteries) may develop
(C) Multiple peripheral linear opacities in both lower lung lobes
(D) All of above
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。慢性肺栓塞 CT：鈣化血栓、transpleural collateral vessels、bilateral lower lobe peripheral linear opacities 皆為特徵。詳見 [[chronic-pulmonary-thromboembolism]]。
