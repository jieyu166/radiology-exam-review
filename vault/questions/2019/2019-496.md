#交換 #2019交換 #CV Which of the following congenital heart disease is with a large aorta on plain film?
(A) VSD
(B) ASD
(C) AV communis
(D) Aortic stenosis
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。Aortic stenosis 可因 post-stenotic dilatation 導致升主動脈與主動脈弓增大。VSD、ASD 與 AV communis（endocardial cushion defect）因左心輸出量減少，主動脈弓反而偏小。
