#交換 #2019交換 #GU Which is **true** about radiological exam of bladder trauma, regarding the choice between static cystography and CT cystography?
(A) The choice between the two exams is largely dependent on the clinical situation and the preferences of the physicains involved.
(B) If CT of the abdomen is considered to assess potential intra-abdominal injurires, CT cystography should be considered to assess the bladder
(C) If the examination must be performed in the trauma unit with portable equipment, conventional static cystography is a reliable method for establishing the diagnosis of bladder injury.
(D) All of the above are true.
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D（All of the above are true）**。檢查選擇依臨床情境與醫師偏好而定、合併腹部CT時應一併加做CT cystography、創傷單位可攜式設備下static cystography仍可靠，三者皆正確。詳見 [[bladder-urethral-trauma-imaging]]。
