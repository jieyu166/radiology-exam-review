#交換 #2019交換 #BR According to the ACR BI-RADS 5th edition, which morphology of the suspicious calcifications has the highest PPV (70%)?
(A) Fine pleomorphic
(B) Amorphous
(C) Fine-linear or fine-linear branching
(D) Coarse heterogeneous
??
**Ans: C**
根據 ACR BI-RADS 第 5 版，Fine-linear or fine-linear branching calcifications 的 PPV 最高（約 70%），分類為 BI-RADS 4C。
