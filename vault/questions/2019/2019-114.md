#交換 #2019交換 #Breast Which of the following is NOT a descriptor of mass margin in the BIRADS lexicon for ultrasound?
(A) indistinct
(B) angular
(C) microlobulated
(D) obscured
(E) spiculated
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。「Obscured」為乳房攝影（mammography）margin lexicon 專用詞，並非超音波 margin lexicon 用語；超音波 margin 描述詞為 circumscribed／indistinct／angular／microlobulated／spiculated。詳見 [[bi-rads-mass-lexicon]]。
