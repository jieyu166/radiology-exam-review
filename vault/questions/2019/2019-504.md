#交換 #2019交換 #NR Which one of the statements is **WRONG** about expected complications of facial bone fractures?
(A) Superior orbital fissure syndrome is caused by extension of the fracture through the superior orbital fissure with resultant injury to cranial nerves III, IV, V1 and VI.
(B) Orbital apex syndrome is caused by fracture through the superior orbital fissure with only injury to the optic nerve.
(C) Fractures through the mandibular canal may damage the inferior alveolar nerve, a branch of cranial nerve V3, may result in anesthesia of the ipsilateral chin.
(D) Injury to the infraorbital nerve (cranial nerve V2) is a common complication of orbital blowout fracture and can result in hypesthesia of the ipsilateral cheek.
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。Orbital apex syndrome 並非僅損傷 optic nerve，而是累及通過 orbital apex 的所有神經，包括 optic nerve（CN II）加上 CN III、IV、V1、VI。選項 B 將其描述為「only injury to the optic nerve」是錯誤的。
