#交換 #2019交換 #MSK 關於 ultrasound-guided musculoskeletal intervention 下列何者為誤?
(A) The outcomes of emergency physicians performing conventional landmark versus ultrasound-guided knee arthrocentesis revealed no difference in success rates.
(B) Realtime scanning allows simultaneous visualization of the target and needle progression and diminishes the rate of complications.
(C) The major limitation of needle guide ultrasound guidance is that they require the needle to be passed at a specific or fixed angle relative to the transducer and plane of imaging.
(D) Ultrasound-guided injection is not a useful alternative in difficult injection cases, such as post-operative change in anatomy landmark.
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**（錯誤敘述）。US導引注射對於術後解剖地標改變等困難注射情境，仍為有用之替代方案，並非「無用」。傳統地標法與US導引膝關節抽吸成功率無差異、即時掃描降低併發症、針導引器需固定角度進針等敘述皆正確。詳見 [[musculoskeletal-ultrasound-intervention]]。
