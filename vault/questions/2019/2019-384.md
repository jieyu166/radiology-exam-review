#交換 #2019交換 #CV Which is not common drainage site of coronary arteriovenous fistulas (CAVFs)?
(A) right atrium
(B) pulmonary artery
(C) left ventricle
(D) right ventricle
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**。CAVF 最常見 drainage site：right ventricle (41%)、right atrium (26%)、pulmonary artery (17%)。Left ventricle 不常見。詳見 [[coronary-av-fistula]]。
