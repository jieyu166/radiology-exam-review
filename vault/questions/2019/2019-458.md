#交換 #2019交換 #CH About CT presentation of acute interstitial pneumonia, which one of the followings is **true**?
(A) Exudative phase showed architectural distortion and pulmonary fibrosis
(B) Typically demonstrate apico-postero-basal gradient
(C) Characteristic peripheral or peribronchial distribution
(D) Areas of consolidation are also present but are usually less extensive as compared to ground glass opacities and limited to the dependent area of the lung
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**（正確敘述）。AIP 滲出期（exudative phase）中實變區域通常較 GGO 範圍小且局限於依賴側肺區。A 錯：architectural distortion 屬組織化期非滲出期。B 不正確。C 不正確：AIP 呈瀰漫雙側分布而非特定周邊/支氣管周圍分布。
