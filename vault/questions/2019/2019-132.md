#交換 #2019交換 #CV What is the modifier for a vulnerable plaque in CAD-RADS reporting and data system for patients presenting with stable chest pain?
(A) "G"
(B) "P"
(C) "S"
(D) "V"
(E) None of above
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D「V」**。CAD-RADS（1.0版）對高風險/易損斑塊（vulnerable plaque）之modifier為「V」，於2.0版（2022）改稱為HRP。詳見 [[ccta]]。
