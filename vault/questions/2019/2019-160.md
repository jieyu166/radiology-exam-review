#交換 #2019交換 #CH Which of the following symptoms is most common in patients with postpneumonectomy syndrome?
(A) Dyspnea and stridor.
(B) Chest pain.
(C) Fever.
(D) Sudden hypotention
??
**Ans: A**
正確答案為 **A**。肺切除術後症候群因嚴重縱膈移位造成氣管與主支氣管受壓，最常見症狀為呼吸困難與喘鳴。詳見 [[postpneumonectomy-complications]]。
