#交換 #2019交換 #NR For P16+ oropharyngeal cancer, which status is N2 stage?
(A) Unilateral cervical LN(S) ≤ 3cm in greatest dimension, above of caudal border of cricoid cartilage
(B) Bilateral cervical LN(S) ≤ 6cm in greatest dimension, above the caudal border of cricoid cartilage
(C) Unilateral or bilateral retropharyngeal LN ≤ 3cm in greatest dimension (above the caudal border of cricoid cartilage)
(D) Extension below the caudal border of cricoid cartilage
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。P16+ OPC 的 N 分期（AJCC 8th）：N1 = 單側淋巴結 ≤6cm 於環狀軟骨下緣以上；**N2 = 雙側淋巴結 ≤6cm 於環狀軟骨下緣以上**；N3 = >6cm 或延伸至環狀軟骨下緣以下。A 為 N1，D 為 N3。
