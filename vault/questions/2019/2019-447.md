#交換 #2019交換 #CV Which one of the following descriptions is **wrong** about peripheral arterial disease (PAD)?
(A) Atherosclerosis resulting in PAD is the most common cause of chronic limb ischemia
(B) Continued smoking or coexistent diabetes portends worse clinical outcomes for claudicants, including more frequent eventual amputation
(C) Risk factors for PAD include diabetes, hypertension, chronic renal insufficiency, male, hyperlipidemia, but exclude smoking
(D) Patients with critical limb ischemia present with some combination of ischemic rest pain, non-healing ulcers and gangrene
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**（錯誤敘述）。吸菸是 PAD **最重要的可改變危險因子**之一，不應排除。其餘敘述皆正確。
