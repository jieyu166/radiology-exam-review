(A) Tumor cells surrounding cancerous or normal appearing acini or ducts.
(B) Normal cells surrounding cancerous acini or ducts.
(C) Abundant cytoplasm surrounding small nuclei
(D) Abundant cytoplasm surrounding large nuclei.
??
