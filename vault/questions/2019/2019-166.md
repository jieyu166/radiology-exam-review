#交換 #2019交換 #CH Which one is not the common tumor origin of lymphangitic carcinomatosis?
(A) cervical cancer
(B) colon cancer
(C) thyroid cancer
(D) brain cancer
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。原發腦腫瘤極少經淋巴路徑轉移至肺，非肺淋巴管癌病常見來源。子宮頸癌、大腸癌、甲狀腺癌皆為常見原發來源。詳見 [[lymphangitic-carcinomatosis]]。
