#交換 #2019交換 #MSK 下列何者並非 MR imaging characteristics for labral tear?
(A) Irregular labral margins
(B) Signal intensity parallels the glenoid rim
(C) Extension: posterior to the long head of the biceps tendon origin or inferior to the 3-o'clock position
(D) Paralabral cyst
??
**Ans: **
Signal intensity paralleling the glenoid recess（而非 rim）是正常 sublabral recess 的特徵，非 labral tear。
