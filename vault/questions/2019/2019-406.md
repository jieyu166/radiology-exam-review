#交換 #2019交換 #NR About incomplete spinal cord syndromes (ISCS), which one is correct?
(A) Injury to neurons in the ventral horns and peripheral nerves results in LMN deficits
(B) CCS is the most common ISCS. Trauma is the most common cause of CCS
(C) CMS causes mixed UMN and LMN deficits, while CES causes a purely LMN deficit
(D) All above were correct
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。A-C 全部正確。CCS 最常見、外傷為主因；CMS = mixed UMN+LMN；CES = pure LMN。詳見 [[incomplete-spinal-cord-syndrome]]。
