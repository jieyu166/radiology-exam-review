#交換 #2019交換 #Breast Regarding the following artifacts of digital breast tomosynthesis (DBT), which one of them is rarely appreciated because of the **masking effect** of out-of-plane **blurring**?
(A) Blurring-Ripple Artifacts.(B) Truncation Artifacts.
(C) Loss of Skin and Superficial Tissue Resolution.
(D) Motion Artifacts (E) None of the above.
??
**Ans: D**
C有爭議
AB. 根據上述內容，應該都跟out-of-plane有關
C. 雖然有mask，但應該跟out-of-plane無關
D. motion會blur，但跟out-of-plant無關
