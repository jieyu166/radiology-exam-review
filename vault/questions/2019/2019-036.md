#交換 #2019交換 #CV In the diagnosis of arrhythmogenic right ventricular cardiomyopathy (ARVC), RV free wall tether is a possible cause of overdiagnosis. Where is the tether band originated to connect the anterior free wall of RV?
(A) pulmonary ligament
(B) posterior aspect of the sternum
(C) pulmonary trunk
(D) aortic annulus
??
**Ans: B**
 RV free wall tether
- A band of pericardial connective tissue，連接ant. RV free wall －post. sternum
- Cine image這個band不會動，旁邊RV myocardium活動正常。常會被誤診為dyskinetic motion of the RV free wall related to ARVC.
