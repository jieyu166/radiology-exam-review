#交換 #2019交換 #CH Fleischner Society 2017 guidelines for management of incidental pulmonary nodules detected on CT images recommend that risk factors of lung cancer be assigned according to the categories proposed by the American College of Chest Physicians (ACCP). Which of the following characteristics is not associated with a low risk.
(A) Young age
(B) Less smoking
(C) Location in upper lobe
(D) All above are associated with a low risk
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**。依 ACCP 肺癌風險因子分類，**上肺葉位置（upper lobe location）為高風險因子**，並非低風險因子；年輕、較少吸菸皆為低風險因子。詳見 [[fleischner-pulmonary-nodule]]。
