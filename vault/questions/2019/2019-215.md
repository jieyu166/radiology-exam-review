#交換 #2019交換 #MSK Which of the following is NOT associated with distal radioulnar joint instability?
(A) Triangular fibrocartilage complex tear.
(B) Monteggia fracture.
(C) Galleazzi fracture.
(D) Essex-Lopresti fracture.
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B（Monteggia fracture）**。Monteggia fracture為尺骨幹骨折合併橈骨頭脫臼，影響近端橈尺關節（PRUJ），與遠端橈尺關節（DRUJ）不穩定無關。TFCC撕裂、Galeazzi fracture、Essex-Lopresti fracture皆與DRUJ不穩定相關。詳見 [[forearm-fracture-dislocation]]。
