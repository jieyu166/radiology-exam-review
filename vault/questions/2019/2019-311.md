#交換 #2019交換 #CH Which choice is **false** about image findings of mycobacterial infection?
(A) Primary tuberculosis demonstrates radiologic findings that include lymphadenopathy, consolidation, pleural effusion, and miliary nodules.
(B) Postprimary tuberculosis demonstrates consolidations that are predominant in the apical and upper lung zones, nodules, and cavitation.
(C) Lymphadenopathy in tuberculosis typically demonstrates a low-attenuation center with peripheral rim enhancement on contrast material-enhanced CT images.
(D) Classic (cavitary) nontuberculous mycobacterial infection can have an appearance and clinical manifestations indistinguishable from those of postprimary tuberculosis.
(E) Miliary tuberculosis is spread by endobronchial spreading, as demonstrated by the finding of a miliary nodule centered on a small bronchiole.
??
**Ans: E**
正確答案為 **E**（錯誤敘述）。Miliary TB 經由**血行散播（hematogenous dissemination）**，非支氣管內散播（endobronchial）。Miliary nodules 為 random distribution，非 centrilobular。Endobronchial spread 才會產生 tree-in-bud pattern。
