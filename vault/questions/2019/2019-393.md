#交換 #2019交換 #NR Which one is not included in the differential list of diffuse/multiple microbleeds?
(A) Traumatic brain injury
(B) Micrometastasis
(C) Critical medical status
(D) Cerebral amyloid angiopathy
(E) None of above
??
**Ans: E**
正確答案為 **E**。A-D 全部都在 diffuse/multiple microbleeds 的鑑別診斷中。詳見 [[cerebral-microbleeds]]。
