#交換 #2019交換 #MSK Which of the following is **FALSE** regarding developmental dysplasia of the hip?
(A) Increased risk if born in breech position.
(B) Positive Trendelenburg test.
(C) Left hip more commonly involved.
(D) Male predominance.
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D（Male predominance）**（錯誤敘述）。DDH為女性顯著多於男性（M:F ratio約1:8），並非male predominance。臀位產增加風險(A正確)、Trendelenburg test陽性(B正確)、左髖較常見(C正確)。詳見 [[developmental-dysplasia-hip]]。
