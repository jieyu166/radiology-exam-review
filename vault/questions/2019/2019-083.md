#交換 #2019交換 #Physics Exposure to ionizing radiation can be limited to
(A) With the use of shielding
(B) By increasing distance from the source
(C) By limiting the time exposed to the radiation
(D) All of above
??
**Ans: D**
體外暴露的輻防TDS法則：減少暴露時間(time)，增加距離(Distance)，盡量利用屏蔽(Shielding)
