#交換 #2019交換 #CV Which of the following concerning cardiovascular magnetic resonance imaging (CMR) for hypertrophic cardiomyopathy (HCM) is not correct?
(A) It is recommended that CMR studies be performed and interpreted by teams experienced in cardiac imaging and in the evaluation of heart muscle disease.
(B) While phase velocity flow mapping sequences of CMR can be used to determine the peak velocity of blood flow through the left ventricular outflow tract (LVOT) in patients LVOT obstruction, doppler echocardiography is still the modality of choice for quantification of LVOT obstruction.
(C) In an adult, HCM is defined by a wall thickness ≥12 mm in one or more left ventricle myocardial segments
(D) In myocardial hypertrophy in cardiac amyloidosis, there is often global, sub-endocardial or segmental LGE and a highly specific pattern of myocardial and blood-pool gadolinium kinetics caused by similar myocardial and blood T1 signals.
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**（錯誤敘述）。成人 HCM 的定義為任一左心室心肌節段**室壁厚度 ≥15 mm**（非 ≥12 mm），或中隔厚度與中室水平下側壁厚度比 >1.5。Doppler 超音波仍為量化 LVOT 阻塞之首選方式、心臟類澱粉沉積之瀰漫性/心內膜下 LGE 表現等敘述皆正確。詳見 [[hypertrophic-cardiomyopathy]]。
