#交換 #2019交換 #MSK What is the least common joint involved in hemophilia?
(A) Hand
(B) Elbow
(C) Hip
(D) Ankle
??
**Ans: A**
正確答案為 **A**。血友病關節病變（hemophilic arthropathy）好發大關節，頻率排序為 knee > elbow > ankle > hip > shoulder；**手部小關節並非典型好發部位**，於選項中為最少受累關節。詳見 [[hemophilic-arthropathy]]。
