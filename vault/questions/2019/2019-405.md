#交換 #2019交換 #BR Regarding the Synthetic Mammography (SM) reconstructed from digital breast tomosynthesis (DBT), which of the following statements is correct?
(A) Unlike standard full-field digital mammography (FFDM), SM does not require additional radiation exposure
(B) The artifacts of SM include blurring subcutaneous tissue, loss of resolution in the axilla on MLO views, pseudocalcifications, and decreased resolution near foreign bodies
(C) SM's strengths include a reduced radiation dose, shorter acquisition time compared with a combined FFDM/DBT exam, increased conspicuity of calcifications, speculated margins, and architectural distortion
(D) The weakness of SM includes the potential for false positives of pseudocalcifications and the difficulty in assessing from motion artifact
(E) All of the above
??
**Ans: E**
正確答案為 **E**。A-D 全部正確。SM 由 DBT 重建、不需額外輻射、有特定偽影與優缺點。詳見 [[synthetic-mammography]]。
