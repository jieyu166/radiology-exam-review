#交換 #2019交換 #GU Which of the following is NOT a typical image finding of ovarian fibrothecoma?
(A) Solid mass
(B) Low signal intensity on T2WI
(C) Early enhancement
(D) Ascites (Meigs syndrome)
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**。纖維莢膜瘤因富含纖維基質，動態增強呈**延遲弱增強（delayed weak enhancement）**，非早期增強。A/B/D 皆為典型表現。
