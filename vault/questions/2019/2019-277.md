#交換 #2019交換 #GU Which of the following imaging findings allows differentiation of a urethral diverticulum from other periurethral cystic lesions?
(A) Posterolateral location
(B) Round or oval shape
(C) Proteinaceous component
(D) Direct continuity with the urethral lumen
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。尿道憩室與尿道管腔直接相通為其獨特鑑別特徵。Gartner duct cyst（陰道近端前外側）、Bartholin gland cyst（恥骨聯合以下後外側）、Skene duct cyst（外尿道口旁）均不與尿道管腔相通。
