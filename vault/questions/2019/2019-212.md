#交換 #2019交換 #MSK Which of the following bone centers at elbow is the LAST to occur?
(A) Radial head.
(B) Medial humeral epicondyle.
(C) Trochlea.
(D) Capitulum (capitellum).
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C（Trochlea）**。CRITOE口訣：Capitellum(1Y)→Radial head(3Y)→Internal epicondyle(5Y)→Trochlea(7Y)→Olecranon(9Y)→External epicondyle(11Y)。在四個選項中，Trochlea(7Y)出現最晚。詳見 [[elbow-ossification-centers]]。
