#交換 #2019交換 #ABD Which of the following is the ancillary features favoring benignity in liver tumors?
(A) Iron in mass, more than liver.
(B) Nodule-in-nodule architecture.
(C) Corona enhancement.
(D) Non-enhancing capsule.
??
**Ans: A**
正確答案為 **A**。Iron in mass more than liver 為 LI-RADS ancillary feature favoring **benignity**。B（nodule-in-nodule）、C（corona enhancement）、D（non-enhancing capsule）皆為 favoring malignancy/HCC 之特徵。
