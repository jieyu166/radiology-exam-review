#交換 #2019交換 #ABD Which of the following concerning renal lymphoma is **False**?
(A) Non-enhanced CT shows about 30-50 HU.
(B) Can present as bilateral nephromegaly without any focal lesions.
(C) It shows hypoechoic on ultrasound.
(D) Typical hypervascular with vessels invasion.
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**（錯誤敘述）。腎臟淋巴瘤為 **hypovascular**，非 hypervascular。Vessels invasion 亦非其典型特徵。A/B/C 皆正確。
