#交換 #2019交換 #CH Which of the following would be a better action when progressive wall thickening or the emergence of a nodule in a cystic airspace in the lung?
(A) Usually an inflammatory process, conservative treatment and clinical observation.
(B) Must be a collagen vascular disease related change, refer to rheumatologist for further survey.
(C) Usually mycobacterial infection, collect sputum for laboratory examination.
(D) Could be suspicious lung cancer and warrants further surveillance or investigation to rule out early lung cancer.
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。肺囊腔出現進行性壁增厚或新生結節時，應懷疑可疑肺癌並安排進一步監測或檢查以排除早期肺癌，而非一律當作發炎、結締組織病或分枝桿菌感染處理。詳見 [[lung-cystic-airspace-malignancy]]。
