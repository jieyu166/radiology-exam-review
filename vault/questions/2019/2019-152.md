#交換 #2019交換 #NR Which of the following description about central neurocytoma is **wrong**?
(A) Usually mixed solid and cystic
(B) Intraventricular mass attached to septum pellucidum
(C) Calcification is rare
(D) Tumor with extraventricular extension have poorer prognosis
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**（錯誤敘述）。Central neurocytoma常見鈣化，並非罕見。混合囊實質、腦室內附著於透明中隔、合併腦室外延伸預後較差等敘述皆正確。詳見 [[central-neurocytoma]]。
