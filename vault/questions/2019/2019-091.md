#交換 #2019交換 #CV Which of the following statements appropriately describes the value of the coronary artery calcium score (i.e., the "Agatston score")?
(A) It accurately predicts cardiovascular events in a given individual
(B) It accurately assesses the presence or absence of CAD
(C) It does not require additional risk assessment for establishing prognosis
(D) It provides incremental prognostic information over Framingham risk
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。CAC score 的臨床價值在於能對傳統風險因子（如 Framingham risk score）提供**額外（incremental）的預後資訊**、協助重新分層，而非用於個別病人的精準事件預測，也不能取代整體風險評估或排除 CAD 的存在。詳見 [[calcium-score]]。
