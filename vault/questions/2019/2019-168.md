#交換 #2019交換 #CV Which of the following statements about the hemodynamics of cardiac tamponade is **true**?
(A) The intracardiac volumes are decreased and the diastolic filling pressures are decreased.
(B) The intracardiac volumes are decreased and the diastolic filling pressures are increased.
(C) The intracardiac volumes are increased and the diastolic filling pressures are increased.
(D) The intracardiac volumes are increased and the diastolic filling pressures are decreased.
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。心包填塞時心腔內容積減少，但舒張期充盈壓力因外在壓迫而增加。詳見 [[cardiac-tamponade]]。
