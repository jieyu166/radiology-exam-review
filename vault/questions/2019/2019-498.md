#交換 #2019交換 #GU Which of the statements about renal angiomyolipoma (AML) is **WRONG**?
(A) Typically identified in adults, with a strong female predilection.
(B) In the setting of tuberous sclerosis, it is typically associated phakomatoses.
(C) Intensive treatments, such as embolization or partial nephrectomy, are necessary due to its nature of rapid growth.
(D) In MRI, AML typically shows macroscopic fat components with absence of ossification/calcification.
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**。AML 為良性腫瘤，生長緩慢；並非所有 AML 都需要積極治療。僅在腫瘤 >4 cm 或有出血風險時才考慮栓塞或手術。
