#交換 #2019交換 #GU Which subtype of renal cell carcinoma (RCC) is the most common?
(1) Papillary RCC
(2) Chromophobe RCC
(3) Clear cell RCC
(4) Collecting duct carcinoma
??
**Ans: 3**
正確答案為 **3（Clear cell RCC）**。Clear cell RCC 約占 70-80%，為最常見亞型，影像特徵為 hypervascular。Papillary RCC 約 10-15%，Chromophobe 約 5%。詳見 [[renal-cell-carcinoma]]。
