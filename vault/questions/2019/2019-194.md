#交換 #2019交換 #CH On thin section CT of the chest, what is the smallest discrete portion of the lung in normal healthy lungs?
(A) Primary pulmonary lobule
(B) Pulmonary acinus
(C) Pulmonary alveolus
(D) Secondary pulmonary lobule
(E) Terminal bronchiole
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。Secondary pulmonary lobule（次級肺小葉）為薄切面CT下正常健康肺臟可辨識之最小離散單位。詳見 [[secondary-pulmonary-lobule]]。
