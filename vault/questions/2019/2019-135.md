#交換 #2019交換 #ABD What's **WRONG** about the description of pancreatic cystic lesions?
(A) Intraductal papillary mucinous neoplasm usually develops at pancreatic head and uncinate process.
(B) Mucinous cystadenoma has a predilection of location at the tail portion with male predominance.
(C) A microcystic lesion with central fibrous scar and calcifications are typical serous cystadenoma.
(D) Neuroendocrine tumor or ductal adenocarcinoma can also present with central cystic change.
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**（錯誤敘述）。Mucinous cystadenoma好發胰尾，但為**女性優勢（female predominance）**，並非男性。IPMN好發胰頭與鉤突、serous cystadenoma典型微囊合併中央疤痕鈣化、神經內分泌腫瘤或胰管腺癌可表現中央囊性變等敘述皆正確。詳見 [[pancreatic-cystic-lesions]]。
