#交換 #2019交換 #ABD Which of the following best describes Mirizzi syndrome?
(A) CBD stone causing pancreatic duct obstruction
(B) Gallstone impaction in cystic duct causing CBD compression
(C) Gallstone impaction in GB neck compressing adjacent CHD with intrahepatic bile duct dilation
(D) Cholecystoenteric fistula with gallstone ileus
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**。Mirizzi syndrome 為膽囊頸部結石嵌塞，外在壓迫鄰近總肝管 (CHD)，導致肝內膽管擴張。
