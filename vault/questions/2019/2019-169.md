#交換 #2019交換 #CV Which of the following CT findings has been reported in patients with cardiac tamponade?
(A) peri-portal acute hemorrhage.
(B) peri-portal tumor invasion.
(C) peri-portal lymphatic distention.
(D) peri-portal abscess formation.
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**。心包填塞病人可見周邊門脈淋巴管擴張，反映靜脈鬱血與淋巴回流障礙。詳見 [[cardiac-tamponade]]。
