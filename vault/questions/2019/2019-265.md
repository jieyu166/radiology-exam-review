#交換 #2019交換 #NR Which one is contraindication of intra-arterial thrombectomy?
(A) The Alberta stroke programme early CT score = 6
(B) The National Institutes of Health Stroke Scale = 6
(C) The modified Rankin Scale = 6
(D) The Glasgow Coma Scale = 6
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C（mRS = 6）**。Modified Rankin Scale 6 = 死亡，為絕對禁忌症。ASPECTS 6 仍可考慮治療（美國建議 ≥6），NIHSS 6 在治療範圍內，GCS 6 非特定禁忌。詳見 [[intra-arterial-thrombectomy]]。
