#交換 #2019交換 #CV A 23-year-old man presents with fever, pleuritic chest pain, diffuse ST segment elevation, and serum troponin level elevation. A clinical diagnosis of viral myocarditis is made. Which of the following CMR findings would be most consistent with the diagnosis?
(A) A dilated and paper-thin right ventricular free wall with areas of fatty infiltration
(B) A dilated left ventricle with severe aortic regurgitation
(C) A thickened pericardium with normal left ventricular function and no pericardial effusion
(D) A normal size left ventricle with focal mid and subepicardial gadolinium enhancement
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。Viral myocarditis 的 CMR 典型表現為 mid-wall 或 subepicardial delayed enhancement。A = ARVC，B = valvular disease，C = constrictive pericarditis。詳見 [[cardiac-mri-delayed-enhancement]]。
