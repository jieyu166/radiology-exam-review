#交換 #2019交換 #BR Which of the following about invasive lobular carcinoma of breast is correct?
(A) It accounts for 3%-4% of all malignancy of breast
(B) Often bilateral and multi-centricity
(C) The features in mammography are poorly defined opacity and architectural distortion
(D) All of the above
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。ILC 為第二常見的浸潤性乳癌，佔所有乳癌約 5-10%（選項 A 所列 3-4% 偏低但大方向正確）；ILC 較常為雙側及多中心（10-15%）；乳房攝影常表現為邊界不清的不對稱密度或結構扭曲（architectural distortion），亦可表現為 spiculated mass。
