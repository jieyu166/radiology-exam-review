#交換 #2019交換 #BR Which one of the following statements is **wrong**?
(A) Fine pleomorphic calcifications are distinguished from fine-linear and fine-linear branching forms by the absence of fine-linear particles.
(B) Fine pleomorphic calcifications vary in size and shape and are usually less than 0.5 mm in diameter.
(C) Fine pleomorphic calcifications should be placed in BI-RADS assessment category 4C.
(D) Fine pleomorphic calcifications are usually more conspicuous than amorphous forms.
??
**Ans: C**
Fine pleomorphic calcifications 在 BI-RADS 第 5 版中應歸類為 4B（而非 4C）。4C 是 fine-linear/fine-linear branching 的分類。
