#交換 #2019交換 #ABD Which one of the follows is not **true** for adrenal adenoma?
(A) Contrast MRI delay phase showing washout < 50%.
(B) Signal "drop-out" on T1WI out of phase.
(C) Non-contrast CT showing lower attenuation(less than 10 HU).
(D) Dynamic contrast CT showing washout rapidly.
??
**Ans: A**
正確答案為 **A**（錯誤敘述）。MRI診斷腎上腺腺瘤依賴chemical shift（opposed-phase訊號下降），而非以「MRI延遲相washout百分比」判斷；washout百分比概念屬CT技術（RPW>40%或APW>60%）。T1WI out-of-phase訊號下降、非顯影CT<10HU、CT快速washout皆為腺瘤正確表現。詳見 [[adrenal-adenoma]]。
