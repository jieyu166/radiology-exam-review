#交換 #2019交換 #Breast Which one of the following is NOT appropriate for a BI-RADS 3 assessment on ultrasound?
(A) Isolated complicated cyst
(B) Clustered microcysts (with diagnostic uncertainty)
(C) Oval, circumscribed, solid mass
(D) Round, circumscribed solid mass
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。BI-RADS 3 合格超音波表現須為 **oval**（卵圓形）、circumscribed、parallel 之實質腫塊，**round（圓形）並非典型合格形狀描述**，不應直接列入 category 3；孤立複雜囊腫與有診斷不確定性之群聚微囊腫皆為合格表現。詳見 [[bi-rads-categories]]。
