#交換 #2019交換 #CM Which of the following about post-contrast acute kidney injury is **false**?
(A) In CKD patients, hydration reduced the post-contrast AKI risk
(B) Dialysis schedule should be change into the same day of contrast agent administration
(C) Intravenous normal saline and intravenous sodium bicarbonate provide equally effective prophylaxis
(D) No drugs have been consistently shown to reduce the risk of PC-AKI
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。不需要因為注射含碘對比劑而將透析時間提前到注射當天。對比劑會在下一次常規透析時被清除。A 正確：CKD 病患補液可降低 PC-AKI 風險。C 正確：生理食鹽水與碳酸氫鈉效果相當。D 正確：目前無藥物被一致證實能降低 PC-AKI 風險。
