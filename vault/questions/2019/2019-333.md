#交換 #2019交換 #CV Which of the following is **true** regarding complete D-transposition of the great arteries (D-TGA)?
(A) There is atrioventricular concordance and ventriculoarterial discordance
(B) Congenitally corrected transposition
(C) Aorta is anterior and slightly leftward to PA
(D) Newborns are asymptomatic if VSD intact
??
**Ans: A**
正確答案為 **A**。D-TGA = AV concordance (RA→RV, LA→LV) + VA discordance (RV→Aorta, LV→PA)。B 錯（corrected TGA = L-TGA）、C 錯（偏右非偏左）、D 錯（intact septum 嚴重發紺）。
