#交換 #2019交換 #NR Which statement about chronic lymphocytic inflammation with pontine perivascular enhancement responsive to steroids (CLIPPERS) is **wrong**?
(A) punctate and curvilinear enhancement of the pons
(B) diffusion restriction of pontine lesion
(C) clinical and radiological responsiveness to glucocorticosteroid-based immunosuppression
(D) long-term immunosuppressive therapy for sustained improvement
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。CLIPPERS 的 pontine lesion 不會呈現 diffusion restriction。特徵為 punctate/curvilinear enhancement、steroid responsive。詳見 [[clippers]]。
