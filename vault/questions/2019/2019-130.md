#交換 #2019交換 #NR Which sites would have greatest concentration of gadolinium depostion?
(A) Globus pallidus
(B) Dentate nucleus
(C) Thalamus
(D) Pons
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。釓劑腦沉積濃度排序為 dentate nucleus（齒狀核）> globus pallidus（蒼白球）> thalamus > pons，齒狀核為沉積濃度最高部位。詳見 [[gadolinium-contrast]]。
