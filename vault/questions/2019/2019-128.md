#交換 #2019交換 #CM Oral versus intravenous fluid expansion for CIAKI (contrast induced acute kidney injury), which is **wrong**?
(A) Guidelines for prevention of CIAKI: volume expansion by rigid long-term saline infusions
(B) Intravenous infusions should start 6-12 h before the intervention and be maintained for 4 h after the intervention
(C) Out patient: can not undergo such protocols, oral hydration
(D) at least equally effective as IV saline for preventing CIAKI
(E) none
??
**Ans: E**
正確答案為 **E（none，全對）**。CIAKI 預防傳統指引建議長時間大量生理食鹽水靜脈輸液擴容、應於處置前6-12小時開始並維持至處置後4小時；門診病人可改採口服水化，現有證據顯示口服水化至少與靜脈生理食鹽水同樣有效預防CIAKI。以上敘述皆正確。詳見 [[iodinated-contrast-media]]。
