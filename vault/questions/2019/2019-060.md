#交換 #2019交換 #NR Which is INCORRECT for juvenile angiofibroma ?
(A) predisposed to young male
(B) most common site is pterygopalatine foramen
(C) the tumor can demonstrate invasive nature sometimes.
(D) trans-arterial embolization can cure patients all the time.
??
**Ans: D**
- Surgical resection (either open or increasingly endoscopic) is the treatment of choice, usually performed** after pre-operative embolization** to help with hemostasis. The embolization may be performed up to five days before.
  - **Irradiation** may be an option if surgery is not possible or only incomplete resection achieved.
- In cases where there is skull base involvement, a high recurrence rate (up to 50%) has been reported.
