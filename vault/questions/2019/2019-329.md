#交換 #2019交換 #CH 關於結核病傳播途徑，下列何者為非？
(1) Hematogenous spread plays important role in early stages of initial/primary infection
(2) Lymphatic dissemination incidence in postprimary TB is low due to enhanced acquired immunity
(3) Increasing incidence of primary TB in adults and postprimary TB in immunocompromised
(4) Miliary CT findings represent hematogenous dissemination with random pattern micronodules
??
**Ans: 1**
正確答案為 **1**（錯誤敘述）。初期/原發感染早期重要的傳播途徑是**淋巴性傳播 (lymphatic spread)**，非血行性傳播。血行性傳播（粟粒性）較晚發生。
