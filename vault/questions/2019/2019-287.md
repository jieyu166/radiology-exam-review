#交換 #2019交換 #GU Which of the following is NOT correct considering staging of endometrial cancer?
(A) Intact junctional zone is suggestive of absence of deep myometrial invasion
(B) Disruption of low signal intensity cervical stroma is suggestive of cervical invasion
(C) Disruption of outer low signal line of uterus is suggestive of serosal invasion
(D) Bullous edema of urinary bladder wall is suggestive of bladder mucosal invasion > cystitis
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**（不正確敘述）。膀胱壁水泡狀水腫（bullous edema）提示膀胱炎（cystitis），而非膀胱黏膜侵犯。
