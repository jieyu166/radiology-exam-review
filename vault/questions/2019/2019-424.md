#交換 #2016交換 #2019交換 #MSK Regarding McCune-Albright syndrome, which statement is **wrong**?
(A) GNAS1 mutation at 20q13.2
(B) Polyostotic fibrous dysplasia
(C) Endocrine hyperfunction
(D) Precocious puberty is more common in males
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。McCune-Albright syndrome 的 precocious puberty 在男女發生率相當（或女性更多），非男性較多。Triad：polyostotic FD + café-au-lait spots + endocrine hyperfunction。詳見 [[mccune-albright]]。
