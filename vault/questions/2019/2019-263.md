#交換 #2019交換 #NR About large vestibular aqueduct syndrome, which one is correct?
(A) Rarely occurs simultaneously with other inner ear abnormalities.
(B) Large vestibular aqueduct is the most common findings on CT for pediatric patient with sensorineural hearing loss.
(C) No genetic cause has been found.
(D) The vestibular aqueduct was considered enlarged when it measured greater than 0.6 mm at the midpoint at tomography greater than 1.5 mm.
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**。LVA 是兒童 SNHL CT 上最常見的發現。A 錯（常合併其他內耳異常）。C 錯（SLC26A4 基因突變約 27%）。D 的敘述其實是 LVA 的判定標準描述，但選項語句不完整（missing "or"），且 B 為最佳正確答案。詳見 [[large-vestibular-aqueduct]]。
