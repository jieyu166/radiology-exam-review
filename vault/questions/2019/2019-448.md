#交換 #2019交換 #CV In a patient with Buerger disease, which one of the following descriptions is **wrong**?
(A) Also known as thromboangiitis obliterans, is a chronic, non-atherosclerotic, inflammatory, thrombotic arteritis
(B) It predominantly involves the lower limbs in young male smokers
(C) Characteristic angiographic findings include extensive arterial occlusive disease, corkscrew collateral vessels and involving the larger inflow vessels
(D) Endovascular angioplasty is usually ineffective for treatment
??
**Ans: C**
正確答案為 **C**（錯誤敘述）。Buerger disease 侵犯中小型血管，其特徵為**保留（spare）較大 inflow vessels**，而非侵犯。Corkscrew collaterals 正確，但大血管不受累。
