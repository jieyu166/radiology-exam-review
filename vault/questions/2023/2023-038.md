#交換 #2023交換 #NR Which is **wrong** about the strokelike migraine attacks after RT (SMART Syndrome)?
(A) present years after RT with hemiplegia, aphasia, and hemianopia
(B) subacute in onset and resolve after weeks in most cases
(C) driven by cerebral hypoexcitability with impaired autoregulatory mechanisms and vascular epithelial damage
(D) transient, unilateral, gyriform abnormal FLAIR signal intensity with mild mass effect and minimal leptomeningeal enhancement,
(E) all of above are right
??
**Ans: C**
RadioGraphics 2021; 41:224–248
C. 原文是「大腦**過度興奮（hyper­excitability）**+ 自動調節受損 + 遠端放射造成的血管內皮（endothelial，**不是 epithelial**）傷害」機轉上也合邏輯：如果是 hypoexcitability（低興奮性），怎麼會合併癲癇發作？與臨床三聯徵的「seizure」直接矛盾。
