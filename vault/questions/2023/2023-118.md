#交換 #2023交換 #Physics Which of the following statement for ultrasound contrast media is **true**?
(A) Contrast agents available for ultrasound, consisting of macrobubbles, allow for transient improvement in ultrasound contrast resolution, increased conspicuity of vascularity, and detection of blood flow.
(B) The likelihood of bubble rupture can and should be minimized by avoiding small intravenous catheters and using a low mechanical index during imaging.
(C) Intravascular administrated ultrasound contrast agents could enter the interstitium, not only remain in the blood pool.
(D) Ultrasound contrast agents could be used for intra-arterial injection.
(E) All of above.
??
**Ans: B**
A. 可能要說microbubble/微粒球microspheres比較好。
C. 大小平均約 2-5 um，無法穿過內皮細胞。
D. 均嚴禁動脈內注射方式給予
