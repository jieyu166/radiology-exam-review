#交換 #2023交換 #Physics Which one of the following adverse contrast reactions of water-soluble contrast medium is included in the **Nonidiosyncratic (dose-related)** reactions?
(A) Pulmonary edema
(B) Renal failure
(C) Cardiac arrhythmia
(D) Facial laryngeal edema
??
**Ans: 原答案D，但應送分（題目有瑕疵、無單一正解）。** 題問「哪一項屬 Nonidiosyncratic（dose-related，即生理性）反應」,但 (A) Pulmonary edema、(B) Renal failure、(C) Cardiac arrhythmia **三者皆屬生理性/non-idiosyncratic、與劑量相關**——單選題卻有三個正解;而公布答案 (D) Facial laryngeal edema 實屬**類過敏(allergic-like/idiosyncratic)、與劑量無關**（ESUR 2025 hypersensitivity 分類與 ACR 嚴重度分級皆明列 facial/laryngeal oedema 為類過敏）,故 (D) 反而是錯誤選項。題目單選卻有複數正解、且公布答案有誤 → 送分。分類詳見概念。![[ADD contrast ADR.png]]
