#交換 #2023交換 #ABD Ovarian torsion is a surgical emergency and challenging to make an accurate diagnosis, especially in pediatric population. Which of the followings is **NOT** correct?
(A) Torsion of normal adnexa can occur and is more common in the pediatric population than in adults.
(B) The most commonly described finding of ovarian torsion is asymmetrical enlarged ovary.
(C) Peripherally located follicles are a specific sign of torsion in adolescents.
(D) Color Doppler US evaluation is reliable for confirming or excluding ovarian torsion.
(E) Fluid-debris level in the ovary is a specific sign of torsion in perinatal period.
??
**Ans: D**
Color Doppler US evaluation is **not reliable** for confirming or excluding ovarian torsion. from RadioGraphics 2017; 37:1892-1908.
- ovarian torsion在孩童/青少年最常見的 US finding是 an enlarged ovary，早期是 solid，晚期會是 heterogeneous or echoless。
- 可比較兩邊ovary size
- Specific US sign: ovary邊緣有multiple follicles ，且單側ovary edematous enlarged
- A fluid-debris level in an ovarian cyst也是個 specific sign of ovarian torsion in neonates，原因是 cyst fluid separating from a liquefied hematoma.
