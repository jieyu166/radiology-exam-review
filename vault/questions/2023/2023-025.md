**Ans: B**
分布：diffuse, 或peribronchovascular(較少)
### Diffuse Panbronchiolitis
- Rare form of cellular bronchiolitis。常見於東亞中年人。可能和慢性鼻竇炎有關
- CT: nonspecific. Centrilobular nodules, tree-in-bud opacities, bronchiolectasis, mosaic attenuation and/or air trapping. 所有lobe都會看到。
### Constrictive Bronchiolitis
### Organizing Bronchiolitis
![ADD BOOP](data/images/obsidian/add-boop-b533b06b.png)
- OP(Organizing pneumonia): alveolar ducts and alveolar spaces有鬆散結締組織的增生(proliferation of loose connective tissue)
	- 典型的small airway disease的mosaic attenuation, air trapping, centrilobular nodules通常不會有
	- 最常見的表現是：bilateral multifocal airspace opacities, 通常在peripheral/peribronchial distribution
	- 也可能會有perilobular pattern (可看出secondary lobule的樣子)，opacities with reversed halo sign (or atoll sign)代表GGO周圍有consolidation; 還有各種大小的 peribronchovascular nodules
- 可能是不明原因(idiopathic, 稱作cryptogenic OP)或有明確原因(infection最多, 其他還有autoimmune disease, inhalation injury, hypersensitivity pneumonitis, radiation therapy, and aspiration)，有已知原因就叫OP。
- BOOP(bronchiolitis obliterans with organizing pneumonia) ，現在建議用COP(cryptogenic organizing pneumonia)來稱呼。
	- 病理上，有問題的地方在alveolar ducts and alveolar spaces，不影響bronchiole，非Bronchiolitis

- Follicular bronchiolitis: inflammatory process in RA, Sjogren's. Related to lymphoid hyperplasia.
	- Centrilobular GGN, scattered bronchial dilation
- Constrictive bronchiolitis: inflammatory process, in viral illness, transplant, drug, inhalation...
	- DIPNECH
	- secondary to mononuclear cells, form granulation tissue, plug airway
	- Airtrapping, cause of Swyer-James hyperlucent lung
- Aspiration pneumonia
