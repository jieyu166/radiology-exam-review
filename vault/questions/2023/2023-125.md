#交換 #2023交換 #Physics About contrast-associated acute kidney injury (CA-AKI), which of the following statement is **NOT** correct?
(A) CA-AKI may be caused by any nephrotoxic event (including CI-AKI) that is coincident to the intravascular administration of contrast material.
(B) There is evidence of a dose-toxicity relationship following intravenous administration when administered at usual diagnostic doses.
(C) Data from cardiac angiography studies likely over-estimate the risk of CI-AKI for patients undergoing IV contrast-enhanced studies.
(D) There is very little evidence that IV iodinated contrast material is an independent risk factor for AKI in patients with eGFR ≥30 mL / min/1.73m2, and no absolute contraindication to the administration of intravascular iodinated contrast medium in at-risk patients that have AKI or an eGFR <30 mL/min/1.73 m2 and are not undergoing maintenance dialysis.
(E) Prophylaxis is indicated for patients who have AKI or severe CKD with an eGFR <30 mL/min/1.73m2, although the risks of volume expansion (i.e., heart failure or other hypervolemic conditions) should be considered before initiation.
??
**Ans: B**
在腎功能正常受檢者，使用常規診斷劑量（含碘對比劑少於 300 mL 或其含碘量少於90 grams）時，對比劑之劑量與毒性之間並沒有相關連。
A. **正確**。腎功能變差之情形，有時不易區分是含碘對比劑或其他同時存在之因子所造成
D. 對比劑腎病變風險因子並非血管內注射含碘對比劑檢查之絕對禁忌症
