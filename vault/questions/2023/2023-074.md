#交換 #2023交換 #MSK Which of the followings is **NOT** a proposed pathogenesis theory of Mueller-Weiss syndrome?
(A) Osteonecrosis
(B) Trauma
(C) Infection
(D) Osteochondrosis
??
**Ans: C**
原因不明，可能有多種因素。如primary or secondary osteonecrosis, osteochondrosis, trauma or altered biomechanics, congenital causes, dysplasia, and migration of an accessory cuboid等假說都有被提出。沒提到感染。
