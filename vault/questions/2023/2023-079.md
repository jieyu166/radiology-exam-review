#交換 #2023交換 #MSK Which is **NOT** the possible deformity happened in rheumatoid arthritis?
(A) Mallet finger
(B) Dorsal subluxation of distal ulna
(C) Atlantoaxial subluxation
(D) Collar osteophyte at femoral head-neck junction.
??
**Ans: D**
RA原則上不會有osteophyte，除非治療過。
- Collar osteophyte at femoral head-neck junction: 會想到intra-articular osteoid osteoma (Jpn J Radiol. 2013 Sep;31(9):627-32. ), 可能在bone scan上被當成fracture，另外也可能單純是hip OA, FAI...。
