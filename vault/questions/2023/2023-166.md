#交換 #2023交換 #Unknown Enlargement of which cardiac structure would be the most reliable sign of pulmonary valve stenosis?
(A) Right ventricle
(B) Main pulmonary trunk
(C) Main trunk and left pulmonary artery
(D) Right and left pulmonary arteries
??
**Ans: C**
答案給C，但A-C似乎都可以
- 95% congenital, acquired少見，可能因rheumatic heart, metastatic carcinoid
- PA trunk, left PA dilation, RVH都會是表現。Pulmonary trunk到Rt PA要轉90度，所以不太會影響。
- 參考 [[2009 RG CT Angiography of the Cardiac Valves - Normal, Diseased, and Postoperative Appearances.pdf]]
