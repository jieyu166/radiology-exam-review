#交換 #2023交換 #CV Which of the following cardiomyopathy is **not** associated with delayed subendocardial enhancement?
(A) Amyloid cardiomyopathy
(B) Takotsubo cardiomyopathy
(C) Histiocytoid cardiomyopathy
(D) Myocardial infarction
??
**Ans: B**
