(A) Bony collapse of medial aspect of navicular
(B) Subluxation of talonavicular joint
(C) Comma-shaped navicular
(D) Talocuneiform articulation
??
**Ans: A**
comma-shaped deformity due to the collapse of the **lateral** portion of the bone 外側部分collapse造成navicular變成像逗號一樣。
