#交換 #2023交換 #NR Which one is **not** the branch of inferolateral trunk ?
(A) Anastomosis to ophthalmic artery
(B) Artery of the foramen lacerum
(C) Basal tentorial artery
(D) Anastomosis to accessory meningeal artery
??
**Ans: C**
Basal tentorial artery=Basal meningeal a.=Lateral tentorial a. 是MHT的分支 (J Neuroendovasc Ther. 2020; 14(12): 558–564.) 不是ILT。
AD. Inferolateral trunk 的AMB跟眼動脈，PMB跟AMA有Anastomosis
B. Radiopaedia說可能有第五分支，recurrent artery to foramen lacerum 會和APA carotid br.形成anastomosis 比較少在其他文獻看到
