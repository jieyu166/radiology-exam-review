#交換 #2023交換 #Unknown Regarding the 2018 FIGO stage of cervical cancer, if a patient has a tumor sized 3cm, invading the upper vagina, without parametrial invasion, and with an enlarged right obturator lymph node, what is the cancer stage?
(A) IIA1
(B) IIB
(C) IIIB
(D) IIIC1
??
**Ans: D**

IIIA 侵犯lower 1/3 vagina，IIIB侵犯pelvic sidewall/ureter，IIIC侵犯pelvic LNs
