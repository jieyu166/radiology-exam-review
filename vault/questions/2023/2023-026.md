#交換 #2023交換 #NR What is the **most commonly** observed brain MR abnormality pattern (excluding ischemic infarct) in patients with severe coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19)?
(A) non-confluent multifocal white matter hyperintense lesions on FLAIR/diffusion, with variable enhancement, associated with hemorrhagic lesions
(B) extensive and isolated white matter microhemorrhages
(C) extensive and confluent supratentorial white matter FLAIR hyperintensities
(D) unilateral FLAIR and/or diffusion hyperintensities located in mesial temporal lobe
??
**Ans: D**
最常見的8種patterns： (Radiology. 2020 Nov;297(2):E242-E251. )
- **FLAIR/DWI: Unilateral hyperintensities in medial temporal lobe** (43%)
- FLAIR/DWI: nonconfluent multifocal white matter hyperintense lesions,  with variable enhancement, with associated hemorrhagic lesions  (30%)
- Extensive and isolated WM microhemorrhages (24%)
- Extensive and confluent supratentorial WM FLAIR hyperintensities (11%)
- FLAIR/DWI ovoid hyperintense lesion located in central part of splenium of corpus callosum(5%)
- Acute necrotizing encephalopathy(5%)
- FLAIR/DWI hyperintense lesions involving both middle cerebellar peduncles(5%)
- A majority of patients (54%) had intracerebral hemorrhagic lesions with a more severe clinical presentation and a higher admission rate in ICU.
