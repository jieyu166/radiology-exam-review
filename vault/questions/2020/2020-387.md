#交換 #2020交換 #Breast Which one of the following statements is **wrong**?
(A) The detection of more disease at breast MRI has not been translated into improved outcomes.
(B) Abbreviated MRI had been reported to have equivalent diagnostic accuracy compared to full protocols.
(C) DWI are sensitive enough to replace contrast-enhanced breast MRI.
(D) The American Cancer Society and the American College of Radiology categorize women with a lifetime risk of more than 20% as high risk, and recommend annual screening MRI and mammography in this subset of women.
??
**Ans: C**
擴散加權影像(DWI)雖具良好敏感度(如ADC切點0.92×10⁻³mm²/s提供91.5%敏感度、86.5%特異度)，但**尚不足以取代顯影劑加強乳房MRI(DCE-MRI)**（DCE-MRI單獨敏感度達97.9%），故C敘述錯誤。乳房MRI偵測更多病灶未必轉化為更好預後、簡化版MRI已有報告顯示與完整方案診斷準確度相當、終生風險>20%者建議每年篩檢MRI合併乳房攝影，皆為正確敘述。
