#交換 #2020交換 #NR Brain MRI findings in hepatic cirrhosis include bilateral hyperintense areas in the globus pallidus and substantia nigra on T1WI. These findings have been attributed to the deposition of?
(A) Ammonia
(B) Manganese
(C) Gadolinium
(D) Magnesium
??
**Ans: B**
肝硬化病人因肝臟解毒/排泄功能下降，錳（manganese）經膽道排泄受阻而蓄積於腦部，好發沉積於globus pallidus與substantia nigra，於T1WI呈雙側對稱性高訊號，無需顯影劑即可見。此為肝性腦病變（hepatic encephalopathy）相關之經典影像表現，需與其他造成基底核T1高訊號之病因（如高血鈣、肝門靜脈分流、全靜脈營養）鑑別。
