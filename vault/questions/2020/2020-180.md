#交換 #2020交換 #NR Which is **not** the common location of Diffuse axonal injury?
(A) Corpus callosum
(B) External capsule
(C) Superior cerebral peduncle
(D) Brain stem
??
**Ans: B**
Characteristic locations for DAI lesions include the **gray-white matter junction** (especially in the frontal lobes because the axis of rotation of the head is posterior, allowing greater differences in momentum anteriorly), **corpus callosum** (especially the splenium), **internal capsules**, and dorsal midbrain and/or pons.
