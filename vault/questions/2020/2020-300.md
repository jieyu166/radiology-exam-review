#交換 #2020交換 #PE Which description about child abuse is incorrect?
(A) In children less than 2 years of age who are possible victims of abuse, a skeletal survey is mandatory. A "babygram" consisting of single or few images of the entire infant is unsatisfactory.
(B) Follow-up skeletal survey performed 2 weeks later is not necessary.
(C) Radiographic skeletal surveys should be performed in all cases of fatal suspected abuse and unexplained infant death, following the same protocol as used in living infants.
(D) Computed tomography (CT) and magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) are used to evaluate suspected head trauma.
??
**Ans: B**
追加複查骨骼調查(通常2週後)是必要的，可提高診斷率並協助精確判斷損傷時間，故(B)為錯誤敘述。2歲以下完整骨骼調查為必要(babygram不足)、死亡病例應依相同協定調查、CT/MRI用於評估疑似頭部外傷，皆正確。
