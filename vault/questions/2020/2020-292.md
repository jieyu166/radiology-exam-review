#交換 #2020交換 #MS In a Stener lesion, what structure entrapped the ulnar collateral ligament of first metacarpophalangeal joint, which resulted in the need for surgery?
(A) Flexor pollicis longus
(B) Extensor pollicis longus
(C) Adductor aponeurosis
(D) Radial collateral ligament
??
**Ans: C**
完全斷裂之UCL近端斷端回縮並移位至內收肌腱膜(adductor pollicis aponeurosis)淺層，被腱膜阻隔卡住無法回到原止點，故無法自行癒合須手術。
