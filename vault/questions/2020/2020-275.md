#交換 #2020交換 #GI According the 8th version of the American Joint Committee on Cancer (AJCC), which of the following T stage is accurate for hepatocellular carcinoma with tumor involvement of a major branch of the portal vein?
(A) T1b
(B) T2
(C) T3
(D) T4
??
**Ans: D**
AJCC第8版HCC T分期中，單一或多發腫瘤任何大小併門脈或肝靜脈主要分支侵犯即歸為T4，與腫瘤大小無關。
