#交換 #2020交換 #NR Which of the following about CNS pyogenic infection is incorrect?
(A) In early cerebritis, the infection is focal but not localized.
(B) In late cerebritis, intense and irregular rim enhancement can be depicted on post-contrast T1WI.
(C) On DWI, increased signal intensities is noted in abscess but not in cerebritis.
(D) Presence of dual rim sing on SWI helpful in ditingusing abscess from other ring enhancing lesions.
??
**Ans: C**
Cerebritis（早期或晚期）於DWI/ADC上亦可見擴散受限（restricted diffusion），可為patchy分布，並非僅abscess才有訊號增高；故C敘述錯誤。Early cerebritis：病灶局部但邊界不清（focal but not localized），T1等/低訊號、T2/FLAIR高訊號合併血管性水腫、T1顯影後無或輕微不均勻強化、DWI/ADC擴散受限（可呈patchy）。Late cerebritis：漸進性、尤其周邊之強化（邊界不清，與abscess不易區分）、漸進性擴散受限加劇。Dual rim sign於SWI上有助鑑別abscess與其他環狀強化病灶，詳見[[brain-abscess]]。
