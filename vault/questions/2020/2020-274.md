#交換 #2020交換 #GI Regarding the hepatocellular adenoma (HCA), which of the followings is accurate?
(A) HCA is 10 times more common than focal nodular hyperplasia (FNH)
(B) HCA has no risk of bleeding and malignant transformation
(C) HCA has link with use of steroids
(D) CT outperforms MRI in differential diagnosis of different types of HCA
??
**Ans: C**
HCA之發生與口服避孕藥/雄性素類固醇使用有密切關聯，故(C)正確。HCA實際上約比FNH少見10倍(非常見10倍)；HCA具有出血與惡性轉化風險(非無風險)；MRI(非CT)在鑑別HCA不同分子亞型上表現較佳。
