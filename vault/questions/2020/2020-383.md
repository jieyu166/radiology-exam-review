#交換 #2020交換 #US Which of the following descriptions about thyroglossal duct cyst (TDC) is **false**?
(A) The most common location of a TDC is at the level of the hyoid bone.
(B) Suprahyoid TDCs usually have paramedian locations.
(C) At US evaluation, TDCs will be a well-circumscribed hypo- or anechoic cystic structure with posterior acoustic enhancement.
(D) An infected TDC might demonstrate as a thickened and irregular cyst wall with increased peripheral vascularity.
??
**Ans: B**
舌骨上(suprahyoid)與舌骨(hyoid)層級之甲狀舌管囊腫典型呈頸部前方**正中線(midline)**腫塊，並非偏中線(paramedian)；反而是**舌骨下(infrahyoid)**層級之TDC可能呈正中或偏中線位置(可embedding於頸帶肌內)，故B敘述將舌骨上與舌骨下之位置特性對調，錯誤。最常見位置在舌骨層級、超音波呈邊界清楚低回音/無回音囊性構造合併後方回音增強、感染時呈囊壁增厚不規則合併周邊血流增加，皆為正確敘述。
