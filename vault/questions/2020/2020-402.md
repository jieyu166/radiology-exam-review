#交換 #2020交換 #GU 以下關於攝護腺腫瘤的敘述，何者錯誤？
(A) At MR imaging, prostatic cystadenomas appear as multilocular T2-hyperintense cystic lesions with surrounding isointense soft tissue.
(B) MR spectroscopic imaging of prostatic sarcomas demonstrates a markedly decreased choline-to-citrate ratio, a finding that helps to differentiate sarcoma from benign mimics, such as unusually large BPH nodules.
(C) Most prostatic sarcomas appear as well-circumscribed masses that are homogeneously hypointense on T1-weighted MR images and heterogeneous on T2-weighted MR images and enhance avidly after contrast material administration.
(D) At MR imaging, prostatic paragangliomas often appear as mildly T2-hyperintense lobulated masses, with avid enhancement after contrast material administration.
??
**Ans: B**
攝護腺肉瘤之MR頻譜造影應呈**明顯升高(elevated)**之choline-to-citrate ratio（非降低），此有助於與良性病灶(如異常大之BPH結節)鑑別，故B敘述將「升高」誤植為「降低」，錯誤。攝護腺囊腺瘤呈多房T2高訊號囊性病灶合併周邊等訊號軟組織、多數肉瘤呈邊界清楚T1均質低訊號T2異質且明顯強化、副神經節瘤呈輕度T2高訊號分葉狀腫塊合併明顯強化，皆為正確敘述。
