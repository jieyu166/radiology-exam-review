#交換 #2020交換 #GU About prostate zonal anatomy, which of the following is **wrong**?
(A) Central zone is a cone-shaped glandular tissue surrounding the ejaculatory ducts.
(B) The transition zone is well-demarcated from the peripheral zone by a concentric band of fibromuscular tissue and compressed glandular tissue, which has been historically called the "prostate capsule".
(C) Anterior fibromuscular stroma is the main nonglandular tissue of the prostate.
(D) On MR imaging, the normal peripheral zone has a high T2 signal.
(E) The transition zone can grow significantly in size owing to benign prostatic hyperplasia, resulting in significant deformity of the prostate.
??
**Ans: B**
移行區(TZ)與周邊區(PZ)交界處之同心圓纖維肌肉/壓縮腺體組織所形成之薄暗環，於T2WI上稱為「假被膜(pseudocapsule)」或「手術被膜(surgical capsule)」，並非歷史上所稱之「prostate capsule」——後者實際上是指部分包覆整個攝護腺之外層薄暗環（於前方與尖部不完整），與TZ-PZ交界處之假被膜為不同構造，故B將兩者概念混淆，錯誤。中央區為錐狀腺體組織環繞射精管、前纖維肌肉基質為攝護腺主要非腺體組織、周邊區於MR呈T2高訊號、移行區可因BPH顯著增大致攝護腺變形，皆為正確敘述。
