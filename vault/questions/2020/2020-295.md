#交換 #2020交換 #MS Which of the followings is Palmer type 1B injury of triangular fibrocartilage complex (TFCC)?
(A) Ulnar avulsion with or without distal ulnar fracture
(B) Distal avulsion of ulnocarpal ligaments
(C) Radial avulsion with or without sigmoid notch fracture
(D) all of above
??
**Ans: A**
TFCC Palmer type 1B為尺側撕脫，可合併或不合併尺骨遠端骨折。1c為遠端撕脫(ulnotriquetral/ulnolunate韌帶)、1d為橈側撕脫(±sigmoid notch骨折)。
