#交換 #2020交換 #NR For bone changes in glomus jugulare paraganglioma, which one of the following is most likely?
(A) Permeative, destructive
(B) Sharply-marginated, enlarged jugular fossa.
(C) Sclerotic, hyperostosis
(D) None of above
??
**Ans: A**
Glomus jugulare paraganglioma之CT骨窗典型呈permeative, destructive（蟲蝕狀侵蝕性）骨破壞，隨腫瘤增大侵蝕jugular spine並延伸入中耳及顳下窩。相對地，jugular foramen schwannoma呈sharply-marginated smooth骨質擴大(B)，jugular foramen meningioma呈sclerotic/hyperostosis(C)。
