#交換 #2020交換 #CH Bulky mediastinal adenopathy is most characteristic of which one of the following subtypes of lung cancer?
(A) Adenocarcinoma
(B) Squamous cell carcinoma
(C) Small cell carcinoma
(D) Mucoepidermoid carcinoma
??
**Ans: C**
- 90-95% of SCLCs occur centrally, usually arising adjacent to a lobar or main bronchus. 在CT上，侵犯mediastinum的SCLC很難和lymphoma區別。SCLC也是SVC obstruction最常見的原因(可能是compression, thrombosis and/or direct infiltration)
