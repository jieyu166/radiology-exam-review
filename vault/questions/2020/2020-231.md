#交換 #2020交換 #NR According to the 2018 AHA/ASA Guidelines for Management of Acute Ischemic Stroke, for acute large vessel occlusion stroke case of anterior circulation, with the help of MR-DWI or perfusion imaging, it is possible to extend the Intra-arterial thrombectomy (IAT) treatment window up to How Long after symptom onset?
(A) 6 hours
(B) 16~24 hours
(C) 72 hours
(D) 120 hours
??
**Ans: B**
2018 AHA/ASA指引：advanced imaging（CT perfusion或MRI/MR perfusion）可將前循環大血管阻塞中風之機械取栓治療窗延長至最後正常時間後6-24小時，用於篩選具可挽救腦組織之病人（DAWN試驗：good outcome 49% vs 13%；DEFUSE 3試驗：good outcome 45% vs 17%），選項B(16-24小時)為最接近之區間。詳見[[ia-thrombectomy-stroke-window]]。
