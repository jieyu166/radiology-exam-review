#交換 #2020交換 #NR Which location of the dural arteriovenous fistula (dAVF) have higher risk of subarachnoid hemorrhage?
(A) Superior sagittal sinus
(B) Middle fossa
(C) Ethmoid
(D) Cavernous sinus (CCF)
??
**Ans: C**
dAVF位置分布：SSS 8%、Ethmoidal 6%、Middle fossa 4%、Petrosal 9%、CCF 12%、Transverse/Sigmoid 60%。雖然ethmoidal dAVF所占比例不高，但其引流靜脈缺乏靜脈竇保護、直接引流入軟腦膜/皮質靜脈，故蜘蛛網膜下腔出血風險最高。
