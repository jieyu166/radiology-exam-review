#交換 #2020交換 #CH 下列何者不是 inhalational lung injury associated with vaping？
(A) Diffuse alveolar hemorrhage
(B) Acute eosinophilic pneumonia
(C) Giant cell interstitial pneumonia
(D) 以上皆是 inhalational lung injury associated with vaping
??
**Ans: D**
Vaping所致肺部傷害之影像型態包括：organizing pneumonia (OP)、diffuse alveolar damage (DAD)、acute eosinophilic pneumonia、diffuse alveolar hemorrhage、hypersensitivity pneumonitis (HP)、exogenous lipoid pneumonia (ELP)，以及giant cell interstitial pneumonia。四個選項所列皆屬之，故答案為D。
