#交換 #2020交換 #GU Which is not the triad of Meigs syndrome?
(A) malignant ovarian tumor
(B) ascites
(C) pleural effusion
(D) benign ovarian tumor
??
**Ans: A**
Meigs syndrome定義為腹水(ascites)、肋膜積水(pleural effusion)合併良性(benign，非惡性)之實質性卵巢腫瘤(絕大多數80-90%為卵巢纖維瘤)，故「惡性卵巢腫瘤」並非其三聯症之一。
