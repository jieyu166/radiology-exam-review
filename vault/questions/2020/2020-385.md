#交換 #2020交換 #US Which of the following descriptions about the US findings of pediatric lymphangiomas is **false**?
(A) They most often affect the head and neck regions and consist of variably sized cystic spaces.
(B) At US, lymphangiomas generally appear as multilocular cystic masses containing internal septa of varying thickness.
(C) Hyperechoic content might be observed if there is internal hemorrhage, infection, or an elevated lipid content
(D) Lack of Doppler flow on the internal septa.
??
**Ans: D**
都卜勒超音波檢查時，淋巴管瘤之內部分隔**可見動脈或靜脈血流**（並非缺乏都卜勒血流），故D敘述錯誤。淋巴管瘤最常侵犯頭頸部並由大小不一之囊性空間組成、超音波典型呈多房囊性腫塊合併厚薄不一之內部分隔、合併出血/感染/脂質含量增加時可見高回音內容物，皆為正確敘述。
