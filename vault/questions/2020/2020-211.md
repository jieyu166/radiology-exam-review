#交換 #2020交換 #H&N 下面哪些是2015年ATA發佈high suspicious US features for malignancy？①irregular margins②macrocalcifications③taller than wide shape④spongiform nodule
(A) 1,3
(B) 2,3
(C) 2,3,4
(D) 1,2,3,4
??
**Ans: A**
2015 ATA high suspicion pattern之實心低回音結節須合併：microcalcifications（非macrocalcifications）、irregular margins（infiltrative, microlobulated）、extrathyroidal extension、taller than wide、lymphadenopathy，或rim calcifications with an extrusive soft tissue component。②macrocalcifications應為microcalcifications之誤；④spongiform nodule屬very low suspicion pattern（<3%風險），非high suspicion。故僅①irregular margins與③taller than wide屬high suspicious features，答案為A(1,3)。
