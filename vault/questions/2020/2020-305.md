#交換 #2020交換 #Breast Which of the following feature is suggestive of malignancy in breast MRI?
(A) Circumscribed or gently lobulated margin
(B) Nonenhancing internal septations
(C) lesions traversing Cooper's ligaments
(D) Center enhances first
??
**Ans: C**
病灶穿越Cooper's韌帶提示浸潤性生長、破壞正常解剖界線，為惡性徵象。邊緣清楚或輕度分葉、內部無強化之分隔、中央先強化皆為良性特徵。
