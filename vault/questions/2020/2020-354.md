#交換 #2020交換 #CV What will not be found on a pulmonary hypertension patient?
(A) mean pulmonary pressure of 25 mm Hg
(B) pulmonary artery diameter 25 mm
(C) pulmonary artery larger than that of the ascending aorta at the same level
(D) nonspecific cardiovascular and respiratory symptoms including dyspnea, fatigue, chest pain or angina, and syncope
??
**Ans: B**
CT診斷肺高壓始於量測主肺動脈徑擴大，門檻為**>29mm**（通常較同層降主動脈徑為大），非25mm；肺高壓定義為靜息平均肺動脈壓≥25mmHg；臨床表現為非特異性心肺症狀(呼吸困難/疲倦/胸痛/暈厥)，皆為正確之肺高壓相關表現，僅B之25mm門檻數值錯誤。
