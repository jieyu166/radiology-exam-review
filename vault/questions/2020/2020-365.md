#交換 #2020交換 #GU Which is **true** about xanthogranulomatous pyelonephritis?
(A) Chronic renal parenchymal inflammation with replacement of the normal renal parenchyma with lipid-laden histiocytes.
(B) it is secondary to chronic urinary infection (e.g. E coli or Proteus mirabilis ) and obstructing calculus disease.
(C) Renal pelvis initially affected, later corticomedullary areas are affected.
(D) Above of all.
??
**Ans: A**
XGP為慢性腎實質發炎、正常腎實質被充脂質組織球取代，常次發於慢性泌尿道感染(E coli或Proteus mirabilis)合併阻塞性結石，病程始於腎盂發炎後延伸至皮髓質區。⚠️ 官方詳解本身於此頁加註「(D?)」對答案A表達存疑，因A/B/C皆為正確敘述，理論上應選D(以上皆是)；暫依官方標示A為預設值，建議醫師覆核。
