#交換 #2020交換 #MS When necrotizing fasciitis is suspected, which CT finding is important for definitive diagnosis?
(A) Gas tracking with fluid collection along the fascial planes
(B) Subcutaneous fat stranding
(C) Skin thickening
(D) Muscle enlargement with enhancement
??
**Ans: A**
壞死性筋膜炎CT定性診斷之關鍵徵象為「氣體沿筋膜層追蹤合併液體聚積(gas tracking with fluid collection along fascial planes)」；皮下脂肪絮狀變化、皮膚增厚、肌肉腫大強化皆為非特異性軟組織感染徵象，無法確診壞死性筋膜炎。
