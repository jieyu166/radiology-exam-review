#交換 #2020交換 #CH How many percentage of relative reduction in the rate of death due to lung cancer after three rounds of low-dose CT screening in NLST (National Lung Screening Trial)?
(A) 10%
(B) 20%
(C) 30%
(D) 40%
??
**Ans: B**
NLST於55-74歲高風險族群53,454人進行篩檢（低劑量螺旋CT或胸部X光基準攝影加兩次年度追蹤），三輪低劑量CT篩檢使肺癌死亡率相對降低20%。詳見[[lung-cancer-screening-trials]]。
