#交換 #2020交換 #Breast Which of the following statement about ductal carcinoma in situ (DCIS) is **NOT CORRECT**?
(A) A non-invasive malignancy
(B) A proliferation of malignant epithelial cells lining the terminal ductal lobular unit
(C) Evidence of invasive through the basement
(D) Mostly asymptomatic, non-palpable and discovered incidentally
??
**Ans: C**
穿過就變IDC。
A. **正確**，還沒穿過basement membrane。穿過就變IDC。所以C錯誤。
B. **正確**。
