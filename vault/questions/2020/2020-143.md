#交換 #2020交換 #NR 下列 2017 ACR TI-RADS system 應用何者最正確？
(A) If nodules with calcifications that cause strong acoustic shadowing limiting assessment of echogenicity and composition, the grading is at least TR3
(B) If the margin cannot be determined, add 2 points in terms of margin assessment
(C) If there is large comet tail foci, add 1 point in terms of echogenic foci assessment
(D) If there is lymph node with hilum flow rather than peripheral flow, it warrants aspiration
??
**Ans: A**
- A. 如果鈣化的acoustic shadowing造成評估困難，最好 best to assume that the **nodule is solid** and assign 2 points for composition and 1 point for echogenicity. 至少3分
- B. 應該給0分
- C. 應該給0分
	- “Large comet-tail artifacts” are echogenic foci with V-shaped echoes >1 mm deep to them. 在cyst內看到，和 **colloid** 有關，強烈暗示為良性(benignity)  給0分
	-  Small comet-tail artifacts可能在 solid components的Punctate echogenic foci 或spongiform nodules中看到
- D. Hilum有flow是ok的。Suspicious LN需要FNA。
	- Suspicious LN meta包含 globular shape, loss of the normal echogenic hilum, **peripheral** flow rather than hilar flow, heterogeneity with cystic components, and punctate echogenic foci(may represent **microcalcifications**) 此時LN須FNA。
