#交換 #2020交換 #NR According to AJCC 8th edition of buccal cancer, what is the clinical staging of one 3.6cm tumor mass at right tongue with right genioglossus muscle involvement and one 2.5cm metastatic lymph node at left neck level IIA?
(A) T3N2c
(B) T4N2b
(C) T2N2a
(D) T4N2a
(E) T2N2c
??
**Ans: E**
腫瘤3.6cm(2-4cm範圍)且DOI未達>10mm故為T2；轉移淋巴結位於對側(contralateral)頸部(腫瘤右側、淋巴結左側)，即使單一顆對側淋巴結≤6cm仍歸為N2c。
