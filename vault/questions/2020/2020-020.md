#交換 #2020交換 #Breast 下列 Digital Breast Tomosynthesis Blurring-Ripple Artifacts 何者為**錯**?
(A) More prominent with iterative reconstruction than with FBP.
(B) Related to the small number of projections acquired at DBT.
(C) Blurring-ripple artifacts also are present at synthesized mammography as streak or halo artifacts surrounding metallic objects.
(D) It also produces edge blurring and elongation of biopsy or surgical clips.
??
**Ans: A**
用 iterative reconstruction 的 Blurring-Ripple Artifacts會比 FBP(filtered back projection)來的少
### Truncation Artifacts
- 由於掃描角度小，detector size的限制，breast 邊緣的影像可能有異常
- “stair-step” effect, (橘色)區域不是每個projection都有照到，只有比較邊邊的有
- “bright-edge” artifact 影像有經過breast造成attenuation，但機器以為沒有，對X光attenuation估計過高導致。(紅色)
- 改善：更大的detector size，X光tube sweep時對detector加dynamic detector tilt
