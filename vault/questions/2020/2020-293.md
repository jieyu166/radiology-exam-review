#交換 #2020交換 #MS The combination of radial head fracture and dislocation of distal radio-ulnar joint is called?
(A) Monteggia fracture
(B) Galeazzi fracture
(C) Essex-Lopresti fracture
(D) nightstick fracture
??
**Ans: C**
橈骨頭骨折合併遠端橈尺關節脫位稱為Essex-Lopresti fracture。Monteggia為尺骨近端骨折+橈骨頭脫位、Galeazzi為橈骨遠端骨折+遠端橈尺關節脫位。
