#交換 #2020交換 #PE Which of the following descriptions about developmental dysplasia of the hip (DDH) is **wrong**?
(A) Ultrasound is the first imaging modality for evaluation of DDH before femoral epiphysis ossification.
(B) The alpha angle in type II DDH is greater than 60 degrees.
(C) Radiographic evaluation of DDH in infants can be performed after they are 4-6 months of age.
(D) DDH usually occurs during 36-40 weeks gestation.
??
**Ans: B**
Graf分類中，Type I（成熟正常）之alpha角**>60度**；Type II系列(IIa/IIb/IIc)之alpha角則介於43-59度之間（依細分不同），並非>60度，故B敘述錯誤（將Type I之數值誤植於Type II）。股骨骨骺骨化前應以超音波為首選檢查、嬰兒4-6個月大後可行X光評估、DDH通常發生於懷孕36-40週，皆為正確敘述。
