#交換 #2020交換 #GU Which of the following best describes the Epstein inclusion criteria for active surveillance?
(A) Gleason score less than or equal to 7, two or fewer cores with cancer, and less than or equal to 33% cancer involvement of any core.
(B) Gleason score less than or equal to 6, three or fewer cores with cancer, and less than or equal to 50% cancer involvement of any core.
(C) Gleason score less than or equal to 7, three or fewer cores with cancer, and less than or equal to 33% cancer involvement of any core.
(D) Gleason score less than or equal to 6, two or fewer cores with cancer, and less than or equal to 50% cancer involvement of any core.
??
**Ans: D**
Epstein inclusion criteria：Gleason score ≤6、two or fewer cores with cancer、PSA density ≤0.15 ng/mL、cancer involvement ≤50% of any core，符合者可納入active surveillance。與Johns Hopkins、Sunnybrook、PRIAS等其他主動監測納入標準（各系統於clinical stage/PSA level/PSA density/Gleason score/cancer amount之閾值略有不同）併列比較。詳見[[prostate-cancer-mri-treatment]]。
