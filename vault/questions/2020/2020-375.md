#交換 #2020交換 #PE Which one is **false** about the differences of spinal injury between children and adults?
(A) The incidence of spinal injury is lower in children than adults, but the sequelae are more severe.
(B) The cervical spinal injury in children tends to involve the craniocervical junction to C3 spinal level.
(C) The spinal injury that occurs in adults is more likely to be ligamentous.
(D) The spinal injury in adults is rarely associated with delayed spinal cord infarction.
??
**Ans: C**
兒童脊椎損傷較成人更易呈韌帶性/軟組織性損傷(而非骨性)，成人則以骨性損傷為主，故C敘述將兒童與成人之損傷型態對調，錯誤。兒童脊椎損傷發生率較成人低但後遺症較嚴重、兒童頸椎損傷好發顱頸交界至C3節段(成人則為C5-T1)、成人脊椎損傷少合併遲發性脊髓梗塞(兒童因對縱向牽拉之彈性韌性佳、易發生SCIWORA合併遲發性脊髓梗塞)，皆為正確敘述。
