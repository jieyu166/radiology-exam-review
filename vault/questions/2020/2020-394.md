#交換 #2020交換 #CV Which one of the following descriptions is **true** for Scimitar syndrome?
(A) A rare congenital abnormality characterized by a curved, tubular opacity adjacent to the right of the heart and extending toward the diaphragm seen on a frontal radiograph.
(B) Also called Chinese Ring Sword Sign
(C) Mainly occurs in the left lung
(D) Can be total or partial anomalous arterial supply of the lung
??
**Ans: A**
彎刀症候群(Scimitar syndrome)於胸部X光正面呈罕見先天異常，表現為心臟右側、朝橫膈延伸之彎曲管狀陰影(彎刀徵象)，A正確。「Chinese ring sword sign」實為主動脈壁內血腫血池(intramural hematoma blood pools)之徵象，與彎刀症候群無關，B錯；彎刀症候群主要發生於**右肺**（非左肺），C錯；彎刀症候群為肺**靜脈**（非動脈）之全部或部分異常回流（異常靜脈引流至下腔靜脈等體循環靜脈），D敘述將靜脈誤植為動脈，錯誤。
