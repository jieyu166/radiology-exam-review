#交換 #2020交換 #GU A 79-year-old male presents with an ureteroenteric anastomotic stricture of his urinary diversion after radical cystectomy for what ultimately was shown to be a confined tumor. Ureteroenteric anastomotic strictures are most commonly due to which of the following?
(A) Radiation treatment
(B) Recurrent tumor
(C) Improperly fashioned anastomosis
(D) Ureteral ischemia
??
**Ans: D**
以Indiana pouch進行continent diversion後，約8%病人會發生輸尿管狹窄，尤其ureteroenteric anastomotic strictures。術後ureteroenteric anastomotic stricture通常於術後約1年出現，最常見原因為缺血所致之良性狹窄（benign strictures secondary to ischemia），左側輸尿管受累較右側常見。惡性狹窄（因腫瘤復發）亦可能發生，但相對少見。
