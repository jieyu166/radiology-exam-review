#交換 #2020交換 #ABD Impaction of a stone in the gallbladder neck producing inflammation of the adjacent common hepatic duct and intrahepatic bile duct dilation describes which of the following conditions?
(A) Choledocholithiasis
(B) Charcot triad
(C) Mirizzi syndrome
(D) Carcinoid syndrome
??
**Ans: C**
Mirizzi syndrome：膽囊頸或膽囊管結石嵌塞，壓迫/發炎鄰近之總肝管，造成肝內外膽管擴張。
Choledocholithiasis：結石位於膽道系統內造成阻塞。
Charcot triad：黃疸、發燒、右上腹痛，為cholangitis之典型表現。
Carcinoid syndrome：肝臟carcinoid轉移所致之全身症狀，與此題無關。
