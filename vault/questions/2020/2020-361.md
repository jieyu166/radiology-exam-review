#交換 #2020交換 #GU About pathologic processes that mimic pelvic inflammatory disease (PID) on images, which one is **wrong**?
(A) Ovarian torsion : sausage-shaped solid mass or cystic tubular structure with mural nodules, solid components show blood flow on color Doppler images
(B) Ovarian torsion : enlarged ovary (>5 cm) with decreased or absent enhancement, in an abnormal location, twisted pedicle, uterine deviation toward the affected side on CT/MRI
(C) Hemorrhagic ovarian cyst : peripheral "ring of fire" on color Doppler images (corpus luteum cyst)
(D) Isolated fallopian tube torsion fusiform dilatation of fallopian tube with tapered ends ("beak sign") CT/MRI
??
**Ans: A**
「香腸狀實質腫塊或含壁結節之囊性管狀構造、實質成分於彩色都卜勒顯示血流」實為**輸卵管積膿/積水等輸卵管病灶**之描述(壁結節對應增厚之endosalpingeal folds，有助辨識輸卵管)，並非卵巢扭轉之典型表現，故A敘述錯誤地將此歸類為卵巢扭轉。卵巢扭轉典型為卵巢腫大(>5cm)合併顯影減少或消失、位置異常、扭轉之蒂部、子宮偏向患側；出血性卵巢囊腫(黃體囊腫)呈彩色都卜勒周邊「ring of fire」；單純輸卵管扭轉呈紡錘狀擴張合併兩端漸尖(beak sign)，皆為正確敘述。
