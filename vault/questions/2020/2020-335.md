#交換 #2020交換 #NR The "hummingbird sign", also known as "penguin sign" refers to the appearance of sagittal image of the brainstem in patients with __________.
(A) Bickerstaff brainstem encephalitis
(B) CLIPPERS
(C) Wernicke encephalopathy
(D) Progressive supranuclear palsy
??
**Ans: D**
進行性核上性麻痺(PSP)因中腦被蓋萎縮，矢狀切面上腦幹上緣由正常上凸變為扁平/凹陷，形似蜂鳥/企鵝之頭部與身體輪廓，稱為hummingbird sign(penguin sign)；另有軸位mickey mouse appearance與morning glory sign等徵象。中腦/橋腦面積比(midbrain to pons area ratio)降至約0.12(正常約0.24)為最準確之影像特徵，並可與MSA-P（橋腦與中腦皆萎縮）區分。
