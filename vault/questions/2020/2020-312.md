#交換 #2020交換 #CV About anomalous origin of the coronary artery from the opposite sinus (ACAOS), which one is not correct?
(A) ACAOS takes one of the following courses to reach its dependent myocardial territory: interarterial, transseptal (or subpulmonic), retroaortic, and prepulmonic.
(B) An interarterial course is the abnormality most consistently associated with sudden cardiac death.
(C) Right ACAOS is more commonly associated with sudden cardiac death than left ACAOS
(D) Slitlike orifice, acute angle of origin, and intramural segment are anatomic features that confer a higher risk of sudden cardiac death.
??
**Ans: C**
實際上左側ACAOS與猝死關聯性較強，右側雖較常見(約為左側3倍)但關聯性較弱，故(C)不正確。走行路徑分四類、interarterial course最一致相關於SCD、高風險解剖特徵(裂縫狀開口/銳角起源/管壁內走行段)，皆為正確敘述。
