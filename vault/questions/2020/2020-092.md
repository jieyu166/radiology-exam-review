#交換 #2020交換 #CV 下列何種影像發現可能幫助我們懷疑病患有Leriche syndrome？
(A) Dural tear
(B) Aortoiliac occlusion
(C) Spinal stenosis
(D) Deep vein thrombosis
??
**Ans: B**
Aortoiliac occlusive disease: complete occlusion of the aorta distal to renal arteries.
- type I: 只有侵犯 distal abdominal aorta和 common iliac arteries
- type II: type I + external iliac arteries
- type III: aortoiliac segment and femoropopliteal vessels 更長，到大腿都有問題
- DDx: mid-aortic syndrome: occurs at or above renal artery level with the involvement of a longer segment and usually in much younger patients (usually 10-30 years old)
