#交換 #2020交換 #GU Which statement is **false** about adrenal tumors?
(A) Myelolipomas contain gross fat and are characteristically identified at CT based on the presence of macroscopic fat.
(B) A heterogeneous enhancing adrenal mass greater than 4 cm in diameter has a high likelihood of malignancy, with approximately 70% of ACCs measuring >6 cm
(C) Pheochromocytomas typically secrete both norepinephrine and epinephrine.
(D) Adrenal lymphangioma is an exceedingly rare malignant tumor usually discovered incidentally.
??
**Ans: D**
腎上腺淋巴管瘤(adrenal lymphangioma)為極罕見之**良性**(非惡性)腫瘤，盛行率估計約0.06%，通常無症狀、好發第3-6十年、多為偶然發現，故D敘述錯誤。骨髓脂肪瘤含粗大脂肪並可依CT大體脂肪特徵性識別、異質強化腎上腺腫塊>4cm高度提示惡性(約70%ACC>6cm)、嗜鉻細胞瘤通常同時分泌正腎上腺素與腎上腺素，皆為正確敘述。
