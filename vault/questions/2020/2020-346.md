#交換 #2020交換 #Breast Which type of microcalcifications in mammography dose it indicate malignancy?
(A) popcorn
(B) eggshell
(C) milk of calcium
(D) Fine pleomorphic
??
**Ans: D**
Fine pleomorphic(細多形)鈣化屬可疑型態，PPV約29-58%，需歸入BI-RADS 4建議切片；popcorn、eggshell(rim)、milk of calcium皆為典型良性鈣化型態。
