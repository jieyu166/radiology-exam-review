#交換 #2020交換 #NR Which is **wrong** about osmotic demyelination syndrome?
(A) Commonly symmetric lesion involved oligodendrocytes-rich sites, such as the brainstem region, pons
(B) Extra-pontine involvement at bilateral corticospinal tracts is also common
(C) Only extra-pontine involvement can also be found
(D) Classical MRI finding is trident-shaped lesion at central pons
??
**Ans: B**
滲透壓性脫髓鞘症候群(ODS)最常見腦橋外(extra-pontine)侵犯部位為基底核、視丘與大腦半球白質(常對稱)，經典病灶特徵性地保留皮質脊髓徑(corticospinal tract)區域與腦橋周邊，並非侵犯雙側皮質脊髓徑，故B敘述錯誤。經典病灶好發少突膠質細胞豐富部位(如腦橋)呈對稱性；亦可僅見腦橋外侵犯(無腦橋病灶)；經典MRI表現為中央腦橋三叉戟狀(trident-shaped)病灶，A/C/D皆正確。
