#交換 #2020交換 #CV Which of the following description about pulmonary AVM is **wrong**?
(A) Direct anastomosis between pulmonary artery and pulmonary vein
(B) A right to left shunt on physiology
(C) Pulmonary AVM is associated with hereditary hemorrhagic telangiectasia
(D) Surgical resection is the first choice of treatment
??
**Ans: D**
肺動靜脈畸形(pulmonary AVM)之首選治療為**栓塞治療(embolotherapy)**，並非手術切除；手術僅適用於栓塞治療失敗、病灶不適合栓塞、或無栓塞設備之緊急致命出血等情況，故D敘述錯誤。肺動脈與肺靜脈直接吻合、生理上呈右到左分流、與遺傳性出血性微血管擴張症(HHT)相關，皆為正確敘述。
