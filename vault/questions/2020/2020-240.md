#交換 #2020交換 #ABD Which of the following would be the LEAST common cause of an esophageal stricture?
(A) Barrett esophagus
(B) Eosinophilic esophagitis
(C) Drug-induced esophagitis
(D) Reflux esophagitis
??
**Ans: B**
Eosinophilic esophagitis亦可造成食道狹窄，但需長期病程（超過20年約71%會發生），相對於Barrett esophagus、drug-induced esophagitis、reflux esophagitis，是食道狹窄中相對最少見之病因。
