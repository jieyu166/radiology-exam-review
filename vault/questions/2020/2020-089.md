#交換 #2020交換 #CV Multiple bands and bandlike structures can be found within the cardiac chambers. About those cardiac bands, which description is **wrong**?
(A) The moderator band is an important anatomic landmark that is used to characterize the morphologic RV in congenital heart diseases.
(B) Aberrant papillary muscles are anomalies without clinical significance.
(C) Coumadin ridge is a fold of the left atrial wall between the left superior vein and the left atrial appendage. Visualization of the rounded edge of this ridge on echocardiograms used to result in erroneous diagnosis of a thrombus.
(D) At cine imaging, muscular bands become shorter and thicker in systole and the reverse in diastole, whereas fibrous bands become taut and stretched in diastole and relaxed in systole
??
**Ans: B**
- A. 定義，需記憶 RV: defined by moderator band.
- Coumadin ridge  一種anatomical variant。會在LA appendage, Lt sup. PV之間隔開兩者，看起來像棉花棒。要注意不要當成thrombus而誤用Coumadin/Wafarin。From [NEPHROPOCUS: LA ridge](https://nephropocus.com/2022/06/13/left-atrial-ridge/)
