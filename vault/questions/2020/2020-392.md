#交換 #2020交換 #CV A 50 Y/O male patient with abdominal aortic aneurysm (AAA) S/P endovascular aortic repair (EVAR) had received one time of F/U computed tomography (CT). The report of F/U CT revealed no endoleak and stationary aneurysmal sac size. 6 months later, patient was asked to receive F/U CT again. But the renal function was abnormal (Cr: 2.0, eGFR: 40). What is the optimal image modality/method for AAA evaluation?
(1) CT with half dosage of iodinated contrast media (CM) given and adequate hydration before/after CM usage
(2) MRI without IV contrast media
(3) CT without IV contrast media
(4) Angiography with CO2
(5) Sonography
??
**Ans: E**
此病患已有一次追蹤CT確認無內漏(endoleak)且動脈瘤囊袋大小穩定，現因腎功能異常(eGFR 40)不宜再使用含碘顯影劑；於此情境下可選擇：不打顯影劑之MRI(2)、不打顯影劑之CT(3，可評估囊袋大小與鈣化/血栓但無法排除內漏)、超音波(5，可評估囊袋大小)。CO2血管攝影(4)屬侵入性檢查非必要；半劑量含碘顯影劑合併水化(1)仍有腎毒性風險，非本情境optimal選擇。故正確組合為(E) 2,3,5。
