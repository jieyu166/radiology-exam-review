#交換 #2020交換 #NR Which of the following type of intracranial hernia less frequently affects the posterior circulation?
(A) Subfalcine
(B) Transtentorial descending
(C) Transtentorial ascending
(D) Tonsillar
??
**Ans: A**
大腦鐮下疝(subfalcine)主要壓迫前大腦動脈(anterior cerebral artery)，屬前循環，較少侵犯後循環；下行經天幕疝(transtentorial descending)壓迫後大腦動脈與脈絡叢動脈；上行經天幕疝(transtentorial ascending)壓迫後大腦動脈與小腦上動脈；小腦扁桃體疝(tonsillar)壓迫小腦後下動脈；三者皆屬後循環血管壓迫。
