#交換 #2020交換 #Breast According to the ACR BI-RADS 5th edition, which one of the suspicious calcifications has the **lowest** PPV?
(A) Fine pleomorphic
(B) Amorphous
(C) Fine-linear or fine-linear branching
(D) Coarse heterogenous
??
**Ans: B**
根據BI-RADS應該是D。不同paper結果不同。
- 此題引用Korean J Radiol. 2019 Dec;20(12):1646-1652.的結果。Amorphous calcification: PPV 7.2-10.5% 最低。![ADD BI-RADS calcification PPV KJR](data/images/obsidian/add-bi-rads-calcification-ppv-kjr-75a8aeb3.png)
- DI: Breast:
	- Amorphous: grouped 7%惡性，可能是low-grade DCIS, ADH
	- Coarse heterogenous 13%惡性，但>70歲只有3%。
- BI-RADS  ![ADD BI-RADS calcification PPV](data/images/obsidian/add-bi-rads-calcification-ppv-bc1acdb8.png)
		- 雙側多處 coarse heterogeneous/amorphous: benign
	- A single group of coarse heterogeneous: PPV <15% ，算4B
	- Amorphous PPV約20%，應該算4B。
	- Fine pleomorphic PPV約30%，應該算4B。
	- Fine-linearPPV約70%，應該算4C。
