#交換 #2020交換 #CV Which of the following about the Lake Louise consensus criteria is not correct?
(A) T2 myocardium to skeletal muscle ratio >1.5
(B) delayed enhancement in subepicardial and or mid-myocardium in nonischemic distribution
(C) increased global myocardial early gadolinium enhancement ratio between myocardium and skeletal muscle in gadolinium-enhanced T1WI
(D) all of the above is correct
??
**Ans: A**
當局部T2高訊號不明顯無法定位發炎病灶時，可利用心肌相對骨骼肌之整體T2訊號比值輔助偵測，異常門檻為**比值≥2.0**（非>1.5）；心外膜下/壁內延遲強化(非缺血性分布)、心肌相對骨骼肌之整體早期釓增強比值增加，皆為Lake Louise準則之正確描述，僅A之比值門檻數字錯誤。
