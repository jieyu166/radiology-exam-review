### Blurring-Ripple Artifacts
- Blurring-Ripple artifact (模糊-波紋假影)
- 和X光管sweep方向垂直(perpendicular)
- DBT的small number of projections限制了out-of-plane的noise suppression。投影數量增加，Blurring-Ripple artifact 會減少，projection和reconstruction section同樣多時就不會有Blurring-Ripple artifact
- 用 iterative reconstruction 的 Blurring-Ripple Artifacts會比 FBP(filtered back projection)來的少
- reconstructed section和clips, coarse calcification平行時(in-plane)也不會有Blurring-Ripple Artifacts
- 可能造成clip延長，margin模糊，皮膚可能變厚
- reconstructed sections和object的距離越長，artifact也會變得越來越長，會變得像彈簧一樣(vertical train of ripples, “slinky” artifact)
- 後處理軟體可改善，用特製的 DBT-specific markers也可以。
- 重組的synthesized mammography也會有Blurring-Ripple Artifacts
