#交換 #2020交換 #CV About the coronary artery vulnerable plaques, which is **wrong**?
(A) Prone to rupture.
(B) Tend to have a thin fibrous cap and a large lipid core.
(C) Often visible by x-ray angiography.
(D) Acute coronary syndromes often result from the rupture of modestly stenotic plaques.
??
**Ans: C**
易損斑塊(vulnerable plaque)易破裂、具薄纖維帽與大脂質核心、急性冠心症常起因於狹窄程度不高之斑塊破裂，皆正確；但因血管代償性正性重塑(positive remodeling)，管腔常無明顯狹窄，故常規X光血管攝影(僅顯示管腔)常無法顯示此類斑塊，此敘述錯誤。
