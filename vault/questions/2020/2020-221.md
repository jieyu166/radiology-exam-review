#交換 #2020交換 #CH Which of the following is a manifestion of AIDS, especially in children?
(A) Cryptogenic organizing pneumonia
(B) Usual interstitial pneumonia
(C) Despuamative interstitial pneumonia
(D) Lymphoid interstitial pneumonia
(E) Acute interstitial pneumonia
??
**Ans: D**
兒童（<13歲）額外AIDS-defining疾病包括：①多發或反覆性細菌感染、②Lymphoid interstitial pneumonia (LIP)或pulmonary lymphoid hyperplasia complex。LIP是兒童AIDS第二常見之AIDS-defining疾病，好發於CD4+計數相對較高之兒童。
