#交換 #2020交換 #ABD Concerning intrarenal aneurysms, which one is **true**?
(A) they occur commonly in neurofibromatosis
(B) the mycotic type is common
(C) they occur commonly in angiomyolipoma
(D) when calcified they are usually congenital or atherosclerotic
(E) they are associated with aneurysms of the circle of Willis
??
**Ans: C**
**腎內動脈瘤(intrarenal aneurysm)常見於腎血管肌脂瘤(angiomyolipoma)**，故(C)為正解。
(A) 錯：官方訂正為神經纖維瘤病中「罕見報告(rarely reported)」，非常見。
(B) 錯：黴菌性(mycotic)類型並非最常見類型。
(C) **對（正解）**：腎內動脈瘤常見於AML，DSA可見sunburst動脈期表現、onion peel靜脈期表現，並可伴micro-或macro-aneurysm。
(D) 錯：鈣化之腎內動脈瘤通常與結核病、神經纖維瘤病、結節性多動脈炎相關，非單純congenital/atherosclerotic。
(E) 錯：與Willis環動脈瘤之關聯非典型敘述。
