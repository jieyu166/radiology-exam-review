#交換 #2020交換 #US About "the sonographic findings of bile ducts", which of the following statement is not **true**?
(A) The proximal common bile duct normally runs anterior to the portal vein and right hepatic artery.
(B) Dilated intrahepatic ducts produce the parallel channel sign
(C) Bile duct stones are more likely to shadow than gallstones.
(D) The central dot sign is classic for Caroli's disease and is caused by the dilated bile duct surrounding the adjacent hepatic artery and portal vein.
??
**Ans: C**
約20%之總膽管結石(common bile duct stones)不會產生聲影(shadow)；相對地，膽囊結石(gallstones)幾乎皆呈現顯著後方聲影(不論成分/鈣化程度)，故『總膽管結石較膽囊結石更容易產生聲影』為錯誤敘述，實際情況相反。近端總膽管正常走行於門靜脈與右肝動脈前方、擴張之肝內膽管產生parallel channel sign、central dot sign為Caroli's disease之典型徵象(擴張膽管環繞相鄰肝動脈與門靜脈)，皆為正確敘述。
