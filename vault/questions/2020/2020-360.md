#交換 #2020交換 #GI About a typical acute appendicitis on image, which one is **wrong**?
(A) CT, MR imaging: dilated and blind-ending tubular structure arising from the cecum
(B) CT, MR imaging: thickening of the cecal wall ("cecal bar" sign)
(C) US: distended (>5 mm), thick-walled,
(D) US: non-compressible shadowing hyperechoic appendicolith ill-defined microlobulated margins
??
**Ans: C**
超音波診斷急性闌尾炎之管徑擴大門檻為**外徑>6mm**（非5mm），且應為無蠕動、無法壓縮；CT/MR呈起自盲腸之擴張盲端管狀構造、盲腸壁增厚(cecal bar sign)、超音波可見無法壓縮之聲影闌尾結石，皆為正確敘述，僅C之5mm門檻數值錯誤。
