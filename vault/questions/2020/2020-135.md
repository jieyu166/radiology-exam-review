#交換 #2020交換 #NR In the differential diagnosis of a ring-enhancing lesion, which of the following characteristics would make a diagnosis of an organized abscess **less** likely?
(A) Ring enhancement with a smooth inner margin
(B) Filling in of the center on delayed images
(C) Surrounding vasogenic edema
(D) Restricted diffusion in the necrotic center.
??
**Ans: B**
In cerebritis without a collagen capsule, images obtained over 20 – 40 minutes may show “fill-in” of the ring center. This “filling-in” does not occur in a well-organized abscess and suggests cerebritis.
