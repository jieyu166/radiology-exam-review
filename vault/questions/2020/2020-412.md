#交換 #2020交換 #PE Which description about juvenile idiopathic arthritis is **wrong**?
(A) The most common symptoms are joint swelling/effusion, stiffness, pain & tenderness.
(B) TMJs are not often involved.
(C) The definition of polyarticular arthritis is more than 5 joints involvement in first 6 months of disease.
(D) The polyarticular rheumatoid factor (RF)-positive arthritis is more commonly in adolescent girls.
??
**Ans: B**
TMJ可於任何JIA亞型受累，受影響比例達17%-87%，並非「不常受累」；因TMJ形態發生學與解剖複雜，受發炎性關節炎影響時特別容易產生生長障礙。多關節型(polyarticular)定義為發病6個月內≥5個關節受累；RF陽性多關節型較常見於青少女；症狀以關節腫脹/積液、僵硬、疼痛與壓痛最常見，皆為正確敘述。
