#交換 #2020交換 #NR For bone changes in jugular foramen schwannoma, which one of the following is most likely?
(A) Permeative, destructive
(B) Sharply-marginated, enlarged jugular fossa.
(C) Sclerotic, hyperostosis
(D) None of above
??
**Ans: B**
Jugular foramen schwannoma之骨質變化特徵為sharply-marginated smooth（邊緣具皮質之扇貝狀）骨質擴大，內部通常無flow void，血管攝影非高度富血管，無salt-and-pepper徵象，Indium-111 octreotide呈陰性。與glomus jugulare paraganglioma之permeative destructive(A)、meningioma之sclerotic/hyperostosis(C)形成對比，骨緣型態為主要鑑別點。
