#交換 #2020交換 #US Following characteristics help differentiate seminoma from non-seminonatous germ cell tumors (NSGCT), EXCEPT:
(A) Seminomas are often hypoechoic and homogeneous
(B) Seminomas frequently demonstrate cystic spaces
(C) NSGCTs are more commonly heterogeneous in echotexture
(D) NSGCTs is more likely to demonstrate calcifications
??
**Ans: B**
- Seminomas are often hypoechoic and homogeneous and less demonstrate calcifications or cystic spaces
- NSGCTs are more commonly heterogeneous in echotexture, with most demonstrating cystic spaces.
	- Calcifications are also seen much more frequently in NSGCTs than in seminomas.
