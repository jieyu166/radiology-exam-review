#交換 #2020交換 #NR 關於thyroid nodules 的US 特徵，以下何者非suggesting benignity?
(A) Uniform halo around nodule
(B) Predominantly cystic
(C) Avascular
(D) Markedly hypoechoic
(E) Enlarged thyroid with multiple nodules
??
**Ans: D**
為3分feature。US Features of Thyroid Nodules Ref [[2014 RG The Thyroid - Review of Imaging Features and Biopsy Techniques with Radiologic-Pathologic Correlation.pdf]]
- Features suggesting benignity: Uniform halo around nodule, Predominantly cystic, Avascular, Enlarged thyroid with multiple nodules
- Features suspicious for malignancy
	- Specific: Microcalcifications, Extension beyond thyroid margin, Cervical lymph node metastasis, Taller than wide in transverse plane, Markedly hypoechoic
	- Less specific features: No halo around nodule, Ill-defined or irregular margin, Solid, Increased central vascularity
