#交換 #2020交換 #ABD Which of the following is **WRONG** about adenomyosis?
(A) "Swiss cheese" appearance of myometrium in cystic adenomyosis
(B) HSG shows multiple small linear / saccular diverticula extending into myometrium
(C) Focal widening of junctional zone ≥12 mm hypointense on T2WI
(D) None of above
??
**Ans: D**
A/B/C三項描述皆正確：囊性adenomyosis呈Swiss cheese外觀、HSG可見多發線狀/囊袋狀憩室延伸入肌層、局部junctional zone增寬≥12mm於T2WI呈低訊號，皆為典型表現，故答案為D(none of above，即無錯誤選項)。
