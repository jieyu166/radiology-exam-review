#交換 #2020交換 #GI About a typical heterotopic pancreas on CT image, which one is **wrong**?
(A) Homogeneous avid enhancement that is greater than or equal to that of the orthotopic pancreas
(B) Endoluminal growth pattern
(C) Mostly found at gastic fundus
(D) Ill-defined microlobulated margins
??
**Ans: C**
異位胰臟最常見位置為**胃竇(gastric antrum)大彎側，幽門6公分範圍內**，並非胃底(fundus)，故C敘述錯誤。腺泡優勢病灶呈均質強烈強化(可等於或大於原位胰臟)、腔內生長型態、邊界不清微分葉狀，皆為典型胃/十二指腸異位胰臟之正確CT特徵。
