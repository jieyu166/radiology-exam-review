#交換 #2020交換 #NR Which of the following descriptions about carbon monoxide poisoning is **false**?
(A) Hyperintensities on T2-weighted and FLAIR images with a thin hypointense rim around the lesion caused by hemorrhage.
(B) Also symmetric diffusion restriction in bilateral globi pallidi.
(C) The second most common site is cerebral white matter.
(D) The white matter signal change is irreversible.
??
**Ans: D**
一氧化碳中毒次發之遲發性白質病變(delayed leukoencephalopathy)於亞急性期(初次中毒後數週)出現，呈瀰漫雙側對稱融合性T2/FLAIR白質高訊號，數月後可完全或部分消退(可逆)，並非不可逆，故D敘述錯誤。病灶周邊因出血呈薄層T2低訊號邊緣、雙側蒼白球對稱性擴散受限、第二常見侵犯部位為大腦白質，皆為正確敘述。
