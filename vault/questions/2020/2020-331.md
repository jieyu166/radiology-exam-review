#交換 #2020交換 #GU Which is **true** about radiological exam of bladder trauma, regarding the choice between static cystography and CT cystography?
(A) The choice between the two exams is largely dependent on the clinical situation and the preferences of the physicians involved.
(B) If CT of the abdomen is considered to assess potential intra-abdominal injuries, CT cystography should be considered to assess the bladder
(C) If the examination must be performed in the trauma unit with portable equipment, conventional static cystography is a reliable method for establishing the diagnosis of bladder injury.
(D) All of the above are true.
??
**Ans: D**
傳統膀胱攝影與CT膀胱攝影之選擇取決於臨床情境與醫師偏好；若已排CT腹部評估腹內損傷應一併做CT膀胱攝影；若須於創傷單位以可攜式設備檢查，傳統靜態膀胱攝影仍為可靠方法，三者皆正確。
