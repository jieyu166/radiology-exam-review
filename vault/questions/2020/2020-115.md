#交換 #2020交換 #NR Metopic craniosynostosis may result in which of the following imaging features?
(A) Scaphocephaly
(B) Harlequin eye
(C) M sign
(D) Omega sign
??
**Ans: D**
- Harlequin eye deformity: 像丹鳳眼 --> 單雙側 **coronal** synostosis 過早癒合  ![ADD harlequin eye deformity](data/images/obsidian/add-harlequin-eye-deformity-718e9fb1.png)
- ![ADD omega sign and M sign](data/images/obsidian/add-omega-sign-and-m-sign-42334636.png)
	- M sign --> Metopic ridging.
	- Omega sign --> Metopic craniosynostosis有70%會有。
	- Ridging可保守治療，craniosynostosis要開刀。但根據[Metopic Craniosynostosis from Metopic Ridging](https://doi.org/10.1097/gox.0000000000001944) 沒有signs可明確區分兩者。
