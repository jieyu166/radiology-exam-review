#交換 #2020交換 #MS Which statement is **true** as following?
(A) Monteggia fracture-dislocation is a combination of a fracture of the proximal ulna shaft and subluxation or dislocation of the radial head.
(B) Galeazzi Fracture-Dislocation is a fracture of the distal third of the radius with dislocation of the distal radioulnar joint.
(C) Colles fracture is a transverse extraarticular fracture of the distal inch of the radius with dorsal displacement of the distal fragment together with the bones of the wrist and hand.
(D) All of above.
??
**Ans: D**
Monteggia骨折脫位(尺骨近端骨折+橈骨頭半脫位或脫位)、Galeazzi骨折脫位(橈骨遠1/3骨折+遠端橈尺關節脫位)、Colles骨折(橈骨遠端一吋處橫向關節外骨折，遠端骨片連同腕骨與手部向背側移位)之描述皆正確，故答案為D。
