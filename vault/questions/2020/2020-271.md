#交換 #2020交換 #US Which step will most improve image resolution? Please select the CORRECT response.
(A) Increasing the depth
(B) Reducing the frequency
(C) Moving your point of interest to the lower part of the screen
(D) Increasing the frequency
??
**Ans: D**
提高探頭頻率可縮短波長、提升軸向與側向解析度，是提升解析度最有效方法，惟需犧牲穿透深度。增加深度、降低頻率、將目標移至螢幕下方皆不會改善解析度。
