(A) Increasing age is not associated with poorer outcome in patients with closed traumatic brain injury.
(B) Early clinical and radiologic findings in child abuse are of no prognostic value for neurodevelopmental outcome.
(C) DWI has proven to be highly sensitive in the early detection of acute cerebral ischemia and seems promising in the evaluation of traumatic brain injury
