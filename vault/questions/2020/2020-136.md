#交換 #2020交換 #NR Most likely a pituicytoma ?
(A) Two types: adamantinomatous and papillary.
(B) An intrasellar mass is clearly separate from anterior pituitary gland and enhances homogeneously.
(C) almost present with diabetes insipidus. (D)Arise from adenophypophysis. (E)Never absent posterior pituitary “ bright spot”
??
**Ans: B**
A. craniopharyngioma（顱咽管瘤）才分這兩種
BD. 從neurohypophysis長出，，非 adenohypophysis（腺垂體）。只有 pituicytoma 會呈現純粹蝶鞍內病灶，且有些與垂體明顯分離，74% 呈均勻顯影
C. Rare DI. 幾乎從不出現尿崩症 (Essentials of Osborn's brain)
E. 後葉長tumor或前葉壓到stalk T1高訊號會不見。
