#交換 #2020交換 #ABD Regarding CT cystography for evaluation of bladder rupture, which of the following is **wrong**?
(A) CT cystography requires retrograde bladder distension with diluted contrast medium.
(B) At least 450ml of diluted contrast should be injected into bladder lumen for optimal distension and image quality.
(C) The diagnostic accuracy of CT cystography approaches 100%.
(D) CT cystography is superior to conventional cystography because it also provides information on possible pelvic fracture.
(E) Delay in diagnosis and treatment of bladder trauma may increase patient morbidity and mortality.
??
**Ans: B**
AB. ![ADD CT Cystography RG](data/images/obsidian/add-ct-cystography-rg-98596134.png) 顯影劑須稀釋，不然會太亮，paper說是3-5%。總量250-350cc。而2000 RG的文章說至少要有250-300cc才好看extravasation。A正確。B錯誤。
C. 根據2000 RG，retrograde bladder distention灌顯影劑，sensitivity可達100%。正確。但如果膀胱沒顯影劑，sensitivity遜於傳統cystoscopy。
DE. 正確。
