#交換 #2020交換 #Breast Ductal carcinoma in situ (DCIS) evaluates by MR imaging, which is **NOT CORRECT** in the below statement?
(A) Locally advanced lesions (>5cm in long-axis diameter, or multi-centric disease, total mastectomy should be recommended
(B) Localized lesions, breast-conserving therapy +/- radiation therapy are considered
(C) Tamoxifen therapy + lumpectomy and radiation therapy reduce the recurrence rate of 5 years, but no survival benefit
(D) Post excision positive margins is not the risk for disease recurrence
??
**Ans: D**
The most important risk factors for disease recurrence are postexcision-positive margins and synchronous foci that were not removed
