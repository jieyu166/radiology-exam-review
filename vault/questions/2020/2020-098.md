#交換 #2020交換 #CV 有關 myocardial calcification 的常見原因，何者為**非**?
(A) Infarction,
(B) rheumatic fever,
(C) Pulmonary arterial hypertension,
(D) aneurysm
??
**Ans: C**
- Myocardial calcification cause: infarction, aneurysm, rheumatic fever, myocarditis 心肌鈣化 MI RF AM
- Pulmonary arterial calcification 肺動脈鈣化 cause: severe precapillary arterial pulmonary hypertension (pHTN), syphilis
