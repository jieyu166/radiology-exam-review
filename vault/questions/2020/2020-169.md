#交換 #2020交換 #Unknown 關於 retroperitoneal tumor,何者為**非**?
(A) Of the primary retroperitoneal neoplasms, 70%–80% are malignant in nature.
(B) Leiomyosarcoma is the most common primary retroperitoneal sarcoma.
(C) Tumors that arise from the chromaffin cells of the adrenal medulla are called pheochromocytomas, and those that arise in an extraadrenal location (10%) are referred to as paragangliomas.
(D) Lymphoma is the most common retroperitoneal malignancy.
??
**Ans: B**
Ref: Imaging of uncommon retroperitoneal masses. Radiographics. 2011 Jul-Aug;31(4):949-76.
- **Lymphoma** 是最常見的 retroperitoneal malignancy (33%)
- **Liposarcoma** 是最常見的 primary retroperitoneal sarcoma (33%)，第二名是 Leiomyosarcoma (28%)。第三名是 Malignant fibrous histiocytoma(UPS = undifferentiated pleomorphic sarcoma) (19%)
