(A) A solid nodule with diameter of 10 mm at baseline scan.
(B) A part solid nodule with diameter of 7 mm with 3mm solid component at baseline scan.
(C) A solid nodule with diameter of 20mm at baseline scan.
(D) A 6 mm solid nodule with spiculation at its margin
??
**Ans: A**

B. Lung-RADS 3
