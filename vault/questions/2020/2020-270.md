#交換 #2020交換 #US Which of the following is least likely to improve a suboptimal US image?
(A) Changing the focal point of the transducer
(B) Altering the angle of the transducer
(C) Turning the overall gain up to maximum
(D) Moving the patient
??
**Ans: C**
單純將整體增益(overall gain)調至最大最不可能改善影像品質，過度增益反使影像過曝、雜訊增加、對比下降。調整焦點位置、改變探頭角度、移動病人改善聲窗皆為有效方法。
