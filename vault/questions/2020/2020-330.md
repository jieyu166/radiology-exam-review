#交換 #2020交換 #CV About Tetralogy of Fallot, which statement is **wrong**?
(A) Cyanotic congenital heart disease.
(B) Included VSD, pulmonary stenosis, right ventricular hypertrophy and an overriding aorta.
(C) The heart is usually not enlarged at birth but it may later become "Boot-shaped heart".
(D) Left-to-right shunt.
??
**Ans: D**
法洛氏四重症為發紺型先心病(A對)，含VSD/肺動脈狹窄/RVH/主動脈跨騎(B對)，出生時心臟通常不大、日後可呈靴狀心(C對)；但因右心室流出道阻塞造成經VSD之**右到左分流**（非左到右分流），故D敘述錯誤。
