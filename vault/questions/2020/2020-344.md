#交換 #2020交換 #NR Which condition would most likely find unilateral basal ganglions lesion in brain MRI?
(A) 57 years old woman with chronic kidney disease and uremia
(B) 50 years old woman with cirrhosis, presented with acute hyperammonemia and conscious change
(C) 70 years old man with poor controlled type 2 diabetes mellitus, presented with involuntary movements
(D) 40 years man with alcohol abuse, presented with ocular dysfunction and ataxia
??
**Ans: C**
第二型糖尿病控制不良合併非自主運動(舞蹈症)之病患，MRI呈現**單側紋狀體(unilateral striatum)T1高訊號**，為糖尿病性紋狀體病變(diabetic striatopathy)之特徵表現。尿毒症/慢性腎病(A)、急性高血氨腦病(B)、酒精濫用合併眼球運動障礙與共濟失調(D，Wernicke encephalopathy)皆非典型單側基底核病灶表現。
