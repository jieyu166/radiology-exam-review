#交換 #2020交換 #NR For bone changes in jugular foramen meningioma, which one of the following is most likely?
(A) Permeative, destructive
(B) Sharply-marginated, enlarged jugular fossa.
(C) Sclerotic, hyperostosis
(D) None of above
??
**Ans: C**
Jugular foramen meningioma典型呈sclerotic骨質變化，伴hyperostosis（約5%），為meningioma緊貼顱底骨質時之常見表現，可合併鈣化與板障浸潤。與glomus jugulare paraganglioma之permeative destructive(A)、schwannoma之sharply-marginated smooth擴大(B)不同。
