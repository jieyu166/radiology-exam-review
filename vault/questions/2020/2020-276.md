#交換 #2020交換 #IR Which of the followings is NOT **true** while talking about air emboli?
(A) A pressure gradient is not required to drive air into the vascular system; a central line or its tract (post-removal) is an example of such a connection.
(B) As CVP may be sub-atmospheric at baseline in up to 40% of patients, those patients in an upright position or those undergoing IR procedures such as hemodialysis catheter placements are particularly susceptible.
(C) Animal studies have been performed to estimate the volume of air required to produce lethal circulatory arrest, with case reported suggesting the lethal dose of air in adults to be between 200 and 300c.c., or 3-5 mL/kg
(D) A 15 French (5 mm diameter) peel-away sheath used during placement of a tunneled hemodialysis catheter can allow 300 c.c. of air to enter the vascular system in just 0.5 s
??
**Ans: A**
空氣進入血管系統需要壓力梯度驅動，故(A)敘述之「不需要壓力梯度」為非真。中心靜脈導管或其拔除後之管道本身可構成連通路徑。CVP於40%病人可呈次大氣壓、成人致死量約200-300cc(3-5 mL/kg)、15Fr peel-away鞘管可於0.5秒內使300cc空氣進入血管系統，皆為正確敘述。
