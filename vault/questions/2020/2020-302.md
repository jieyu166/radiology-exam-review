#交換 #2020交換 #Breast Which of the following description about breast calcification is **WRONG**?
(A) The morphology of suspicious calcification includes amorphous, coarse heterogeneous, dystrophic, fine pleomorphic, and fine-linear or fine linear–branching
(B) Fine-linear or fine linear–branching calcification account for the highest positive predictive value among all suspicious calcifications
(C) rim calcification is the typical finding of oil cyst
(D) Large, dense, sharply marginated mass with popcorn like calcifications is the typical finding of involuting fibroadenoma
??
**Ans: A**
可疑鈣化型態實際上僅四類(amorphous、coarse heterogeneous、fine pleomorphic、fine-linear/branching)，dystrophic實屬典型良性型態，不應列入可疑清單，故(A)為錯誤敘述。Fine-linear/branching PPV最高、rim鈣化為oil cyst典型表現、大而邊界銳利合併爆米花狀鈣化為involuting fibroadenoma典型表現，皆正確。
