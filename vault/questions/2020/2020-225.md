#交換 #2020交換 #CH 關於Silhouette Sign在肺葉病灶的運用下列何者為誤?
(A) RML(右肺中葉) lesion with obliteration of right heart border
(B) RLL(右肺下葉) lesion with obliteration of right heart border
(C) Lingular lobe of left upper lobe(左上肺舌葉) lesion with obliteration of left heart border
(D) RLL(右肺下葉) lesion with obliteration of diaphragm
(E) None of above
??
**Ans: B**
右心緣消失對應之病灶為RML（或內側RLL），非單純RLL；RLL病灶典型遮蓋右橫膈緣（選項D正確）而非右心緣。正面胸部X光silhouette sign對應：right paratracheal stripe消失→RUL前段；right heart border消失→RML或medial RLL；right hemidiaphragm消失→RLL；aortic knuckle消失→LUL尖後段；left heart border消失→lingular segments of LUL；left hemidiaphragm/descending aorta消失→LLL。詳見[[silhouette-sign]]。
