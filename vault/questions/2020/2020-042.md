#交換 #2020交換 #ABD Which of the following favors the diagnosis of a hemorrhagic cyst rather than an endometrioma at MR imaging?
(A) Hyperintense signal on T2W images
(B) Angled and distorted margins
(C) Uniform homogeneous low signal on T2W images
(D) Very bright ("light bulb") appearance on T1W images
??
**Ans: A**
Hemorrhagic ovarian cyst:
- brighter on T2-weighted images
C. 可能指 T2 shading sign
### [[endometrioma]]
### Cystic teratoma, desmoids
- 通常發生在年輕女性，<20歲女性最常見的卵巢腫瘤。
- **Tip of the Iceberg Sign** US在mass的頂部就被吸收掉，如冰山一角。
- MRI：T1亮，FS**可抑制**。CT可看到含fat的ovarian mass。
- 1% 可能malignant transformation變成SCC。小心：年齡>05歲，或直徑大於10cm。
