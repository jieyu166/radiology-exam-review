#交換 #2020交換 #CH Which one of the following is not recommended for using Fleischner Society Guidelines for Managing Incidental Pulmonary Nodules?
(A) A 40-Years-old patient
(B) Patient without known malignancy
(C) Lung Cancer Screening Population
(D) A 50-Years-old patient
??
**Ans: C**
Fleischner偶見肺結節指引適用於35歲以上、無已知惡性病史之偶見結節病患；不適用族群包括：35歲以下病患、已知惡性病史者、免疫低下病患、肺癌篩檢族群(此族群另有專屬篩檢指引LungRADS)。40歲及50歲病患皆在適用範圍內。
