#交換 #2020交換 #2021交換 #Physics 關於心肌灌注顯影(Myocardial Perfusion Imaging，MPI)之應用，心臟核磁共振造影(Cardiovascular Magnetic Resonance，CMR)與單光子電腦斷層掃描(Single-photon Emission Tomography，SPECT)之比較，何者為誤？
(A) 相較於SPECT，CMRMPI可以避免產生游離輻射(Ionizing Radiation)。
(B) SPECT和CMR均可能造成衰減假影(Attenuation Artifact)。
(C) CMR可提供較SPECT高三到四倍的影像解析度。
(D) 針對偵測冠狀動脈疾病(Multivessel Coronary Artery Disease)的準確度，CMR較SPECT為高
(E) SPECT和CMR皆可用以評估心肌存活性(MyocardialViability)。
??
**Ans: B**
“Attenuation artifact” observed in a nuclear stress test is due to the reduction in the intensity / strength of signal when it travels through various body tissues of different densities, such as breast tissues, chest wall, and organs under the diaphragm
