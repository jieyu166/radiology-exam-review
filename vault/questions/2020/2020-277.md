#交換 #2020交換 #ABD Which of the following descriptions about small bowel is **wrong**?
(A) Small bowel neoplasms are rare lesions that account for less than 5% of all gastrointestinal neoplasms.
(B) Small bowel lymphoma is characteristically associated with marked lumen dilation.
(C) Mesenteric mass with desmoplastic reaction and calcification may suggest the diagnosis of carcinoid tumor.
(D) Gastrointestinal stromal tumors are intramural or subserosal tumors, which cannot be intraluminal location.
??
**Ans: D**
GIST傾向壁內生長，但腫瘤夠大時可向腔內凸出(bulging intraluminal)或向外突出(extramural)，並非不可能腔內生長，故(D)為錯誤敘述。小腸腫瘤<5%佔所有GI腫瘤、小腸淋巴瘤伴明顯腔擴張、類癌腸繫膜腫塊合併促結締組織增生反應與鈣化皆為正確敘述。
