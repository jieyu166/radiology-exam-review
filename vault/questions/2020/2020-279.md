#交換 #2020交換 #GI Which of the following descriptions is **wrong**?
(A) Pancreaticobiliary maljunction (PBM) usually forms a long common channel.
(B) Prevalence of PBM is much higher in Asian people than Western people.
(C) MR cholangiopancreatography is an important modality in diagnosis of PBM, featuring pancreaticobiliary junction located outside the duodenal wall.
(D) PBM cannot be diagnosed without the presence of biliary dilatation.
(E) PBM is associated with higher risk of biliary cancer.
??
**Ans: D**
PBM即使不伴膽道擴張仍可診斷，惟此類無擴張型較難診斷，多數成人病例經由膽道癌臨床徵象發現，故(D)為錯誤敘述。PBM通常形成過長共同管、亞洲族群盛行率遠高於西方、MRCP特徵為膽胰管匯合位於十二指腸壁外、PBM與較高膽道癌風險相關，皆為正確敘述。
