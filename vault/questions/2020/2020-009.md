## Mass
Mass internal enhancement pattern

- **Homogeneous** internal enhancement is uniform and can be seen in both benign and malignant lesions.
- **Heterogeneous** internal enhancement describes non-uniform enhancement within the lesion and is suspicious, especially in the presence of rim enhancement.
- **Rim enhancement** is a highly suspicious finding for cancer, representing malignancy in upto 84% of cases, although this finding is only seen in 16% of cancers. Potential pitfalls are a peripherally enhancing inflammatory cyst or fat necrosis, both of which can demonstrate rim enhancement and are discussed later in the chapter.
- **Dark internal septations** are highly specific for a benign fibroadenoma (>95% positive predictive value). A fibroadenoma will also typically be circumscribed and hyperintense on T2-weighted images. A hyalinizing fibroadenoma, typically seen in older women, rarely enhances.

## BPE
[[BI-RADS 2013.pdf]]
- BPE指的是正常fibroglandular tissue 的顯影。
- BPE可以被描述為minimal, mild, moderate, or marked.
- 評估BPE在第一張術後影像(90s)，之後會progressive enhancement，顯影的gland也會變多。
- 影響BPE的因素：
	- 不論有月經或更年期，無論月經週期為何，都會有BPE。
	- BPE與腺體組織的量並不直接相關。Extreme dense breast的BPE可能幾乎沒有，但年輕女性dense breast會有BPE。Scattered fibroglandular可能有marked BPE。
	- BPE是基於腺體組織的量進行評估的，而不是整個乳房的體積。
	- BPE在月經週期的黃體期通常更加明顯。對於elective MRI，建議在**月經週期早期(7-14天/follicular phase)**安排，以減少BPE。
- 臨床意義：
	- 起初人們認為BPE可能會干擾判讀。但研究已經顯示，BPE增加可能有更多call back，但不會減少cancer detection rate。
	- BPE的描述應該是乳房MRI報告的一部分。
	- 對於已經被診斷出癌症的患者，無論月經週期或有沒有月經，都應該進行MRI以進行分期。
- 增強模式：
	- Symmetric 對稱增強：良性BPE，通常看起來像鏡像增強mirror-image enhancement。
	- Preferential enhancement：靠近血管會先顯影。如UOQ和乳房下半部，會有sheets of enhancement。
	- Asymmetric 非對稱增強：表示一側乳房的顯影比另一側更明顯，可能是由於良性或惡性原因。
