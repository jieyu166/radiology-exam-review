#交換 #2020交換 #GU Which statement is **true** about Weigert-Meyer law?
(A) Upper renal moiety has ectopic insertion medial and inferior to the lower pole moiety ureter, and frequently ends in a ureterocoele.
(B) Lower renal moiety has orthotopic insertion lateral and superior to the ureter draining the upper pole, and vesiculoureteral reflux typically occurs.
(C) Both (A)and(B) are false.
(D) Both (A)and(B) are true.
??
**Ans: D**
Weigert-Meyer法則：上極腎異位開口偏內下（medial/inferior）於下極輸尿管開口，且常end於輸尿管囊腫(ureterocele)；下極腎則呈正位開口(orthotopic)，位置偏外上（lateral/superior）於引流上極之輸尿管，因開口位置異常(短壁內段)常致膀胱輸尿管逆流(VUR)。A、B皆為正確敘述，故答案為D。
