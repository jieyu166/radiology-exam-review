#交換 #2020交換 #ABD Which of the following is **NOT** correct about ovarian metastases?
(A) The same as Krukenberg tumor
(B) Commonly involve both ovaries
(C) Predominantly solid appearance of the mass
(D) Common primary tumors include colon cancer and gastric cancer
??
**Ans: A**
- Krukenberg tumor is **a metastatic disease to the ovaries composed of mucin-rich signet-ring cells**. The most common primary site for this tumor is the stomach. These tumors spread most likely through the lymphatic channels. (StarPearls)
