#交換 #2020交換 #Breast 下列 Digital Breast Tomosynthesis Truncation Artifacts 何者為**錯**?
(A) Because of the small (less than 180°) sweep angle and limited detector size.
(B) Breast tissue captured only by the peripheral-most acquisitions does not fall within the reconstructed volume.
(C) Truncation artifacts can be minimized with the use of a larger detector size or with a dynamic detector tilt that is concentric with the x-ray tube sweep angle..
(D) A “bright-edge” artifact with overestimation of x-ray attenuation at the margins is not a truncation artifact.
??
**Ans: D**
也是truncation artifact的一種。
### Loss of Skin and Superficial Tissue Resolution
- Desne breast機器要用較高能量的輻射，對只穿過skin, 皮下組織，沒穿過breast tissue的X光來說能量太高，造成burn-out effect。
(A) DBT: 有一些鋸齒狀很白的東西(jagged)，皮膚，皮下評估受限。(B) 重組2D也還是有 (C) MLO view FFDM 沒有artifact。
