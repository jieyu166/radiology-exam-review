#交換 #2019交換 #2020交換 #2024交換 #NR Select the one **wrong** statement about Sturge-Weber syndrome from the list below:
(A) Blood is shunted through deep collateral to.the superficial venous system.
(B) It belongs to venous abnomralities.
(C) It is characterized by thickened dipole
(D) It has hypointense subcortical zone on T2-weighted imaging.
??
**Ans: A**
正確答案為 **A**（錯誤敘述，方向顛倒）。Sturge-Weber症候群屬於靜脈系統異常，因表淺皮質靜脈引流受阻，血流經深部側支**轉而引流至深部靜脈系統**，並非「引流至表淺靜脈系統」。增厚脈絡叢、T2WI皮質下低訊號區等敘述皆正確。詳見 [[sturge-weber-syndrome]]。
