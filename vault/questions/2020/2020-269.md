#交換 #2020交換 #US The time gain compensation (TGC) control, compensates for? Please select the CORRECT response.
(A) The time take to undertake the scan
(B) Attenuation deficits
(C) The difference in ultrasound speed in different body tissues
(D) The video image display lag time
??
**Ans: B**
TGC用以補償聲波隨深度增加而衰減(attenuation)之效應，使等回音組織於不同深度呈現相同亮度。非補償掃描所需時間、不同組織間聲速差異、或影像顯示延遲時間。
