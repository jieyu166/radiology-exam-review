#交換 #2020交換 #NR Concerning lymphoma of the kidney, which one is **true**?
(A) It is a vascular lesion
(B) it has a characteristic mixed (echoic and hypoechoic) sonographic pattern
(C) it occurs more commonly in nonHodgkins than in Hodgkins cell types
(D) it is primarily an expansive growth process
(E) it is rarely bilateral in clinical presentation
??
**Ans: C**
Typical imaging findings are multiple bilateral hypodense or infiltrative renal masses.
1. multiple masses (up to 60%: most common pattern)
2. invasion from retroperitoneal nodal mass (over 30% of cases)
3. single mass (over 20% of cases)
4. diffuse infiltration (up to 20% of cases)
5. perirenal mass (less than 10% of cases)
US: Hypoechoic lesions within renal parenchyma with very little internal vascularity.
