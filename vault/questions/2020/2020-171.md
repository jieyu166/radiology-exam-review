#交換 #2020交換 #Unknown HSG reveals a distorted and enlarged uterine cavity with a smooth ﬁlling defect. Which of the following is the most likely diagnosis?
(A) Polyp
(B) Synechiae
(C) Leiomyomas
(D) DES exposure
??
**Ans: C**
- Leiomyoma: **Enlarged, elongated and distorted uterine cavity** due to intramural leiomyoma
- Synechiae: Irregular cavity with a linear **filling defect.**
- DES exposure (**T-shape**): narrowed irregular endocervical canal and small endometrial cavity, with a shortened upper uterine segment.
- Endometrial polyp: **pedunculated** or sessile **filling defects** within the cavity.
