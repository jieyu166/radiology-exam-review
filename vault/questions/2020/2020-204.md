#交換 #2020交換 #ABD Hepatic pseudolesion 描述何者錯誤?
(A) Focal low attenuation on CT images or low signal intensity on contrast-enhanced MR images adjacent to the falciform ligament is probably related to anomalous venous drainage.
(B) A focal liver lesion located in the posterior part of segment 4 initially suggests focal fatty sparing or focal steatosis.
(C) In patients with steatosis, focal fatty sparing may be seen around the gallbladder fossa in segments 4 and 5.
(D) 以上描述皆正確
??
**Ans: D**
四個選項皆正確，無錯誤選項。
Pseudolesions around the falciform ligament：probably related to anomalous venous drainage (inferior vein of Sappey)，因多數病人於OOP-images無訊號掉落。
Pseudolesion in the posterior part of segment 4（insulin theory）：aberrant right gastric veins（低insulin濃度）直接引流入segment 4造成focal fatty sparing；aberrant duodenopancreatic arcades（高insulin濃度）直接引流入segment 4造成focal fatty steatosis。
