#交換 #2020交換 #NR About posterior reversible encephalopathy syndrome(PRES), which is less likely description?
(A) Common caused by hypoxia or toxic induced cellular edema, mainly involving gray matter
(B) Common involving parieto-occipital lobes
(C) Superior frontal sulcus can also be involvement
(D) Possible progression to subarachnoid hemorrhage
??
**Ans: A**
PRES主要因血壓調節失能或毒性作用致血管源性水腫(vasogenic edema)，主要侵犯**白質**（非灰質），皮質與皮質下區域皆可受累，故A敘述錯誤。常見侵犯頂枕葉(parieto-occipital，最典型，約90%)、額上溝型(superior frontal sulcus pattern，約70%)，並可能進展為蜘蛛膜下腔出血，B/C/D皆正確。
