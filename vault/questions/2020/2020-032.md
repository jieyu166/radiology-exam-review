#交換 #2020交換 #ABD A 2-year-old boy with a left lower quadrant abdominal mass undergoes an abdominal ultrasound. A well-circumscribed cystic mass is found. Which sonographic feature is most suggestive of a gastrointestinal duplication cyst?
(A) Trilaminar wall with alternating hyper-, hypo-, and hyperechoic layers.
(B) Internal calcifications
(C) Central vascularity
(D) Internal fluid-debris level
??
**Ans: A**
- Enteric duplication cysts：少見。依發生頻率 ileum (33%), esophagus (20%), colon (13%), jejunum (10%), stomach (7%) and duodenum (5%).
- Cyst wall=腸壁，可看到典型的 multilaminar or “gut” signature appearance。有時會是 bilaminar，mucosa, submucosa會是hyperechoic ，而muscularis propria是hypoechoic。
	- DDx: mesenteric and omental cysts, lymphangiomas, nonpancreatic pseudocysts, mesothelial cysts, Meckel diverticulum, exophytic liver, choledochal or renal cysts, 甚至 artifacts。
- AJR. 2015 Feb;204(2):W222-3.建議還要看到下列情況
	- “Y”  configuration of the muscle layer：duplication cyst外部的muscularis propria會和腸壁相連，在Meckel diverticulum沒人報導過。 \* Y configuration, D: duplication cyst, I: ileum. 箭頭 LN
	- cyst壁因有腸壁結構，所以會收縮。其他cyst不會，Meckel diverticulum也沒人報導過。探頭要保持穩定約15秒才能記錄到。
	- Duplication cyst在mesentery side，Meckel diverticulum在anti-mesentery side
