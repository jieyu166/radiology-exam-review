#交換 #2020交換 #US Comparison of color Doppler with power Doppler, which of the following statement is **wrong**?
(A) Power Doppler encodes the Doppler signal base on the power of the signal rather than on the frequency shift
(B) The advantage of color Doppler is its capability to determine flow direction and its relative lack of sensitivity to tissue and transducer motion
(C) The advantage of power Doppler is its increased sensitivity to slow flow and increased dependence on the Doppler angle.
(D) Color Doppler codes based on mean frequency shift which is dependent on flow velocity.
??
**Ans: C**
Power Doppler以訊號振幅(power/amplitude)而非頻移編碼，其優勢為對慢血流敏感度較高，且『不依賴』都卜勒角度(independent of angle)——因不受流速方向影響，故無aliasing問題，可偵測較小流速；題目敘述『increased dependence on Doppler angle』為錯誤，應為『independent of angle』。Power Doppler較Color Doppler敏感度高，但以flash artifact為代價。Color Doppler優勢為可判斷流向且較不受組織/探頭移動影響、依平均頻移編碼(與流速相關)，皆為正確敘述。
