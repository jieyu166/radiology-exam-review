#交換 #2020交換 #US Which of the following descriptions is **false**?
(A) At US, acute appendicitis is larger than 8 mm in cross-sectional diameter.
(B) Wall thickening and discontinuity of the echogenic submucosal layer with periappendiceal fluid might suggest appendiceal perforation.
(C) At US, an appendiceal mucocele appears as a well-defined thin-walled cystic mass of variable echogenicity.
(D) Concentric echogenic layers within a cystic demonstrating "onion skin" pattern is fairly specific for appendiceal mucocele.
??
**Ans: A**
經陰道超音波評估急性發炎之骨盆位闌尾，其橫切面外徑門檻為**大於6mm**（非8mm），故A敘述錯誤。管壁增厚合併高回音黏膜下層不連續與闌尾周圍積液提示穿孔、闌尾黏液囊腫呈邊界清楚薄壁囊性腫塊(回音強度不一)、洋蔥皮徵象(同心圓高回音層)高度提示闌尾黏液囊腫，皆為正確敘述。
