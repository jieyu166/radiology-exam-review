#交換 #2020交換 #MS What is Buford Complex?
(1) Congenital absence of anterosuperior labrum
(2) Cordlike middle glenohumeral ligament
(3) Congenital absence of anterior labrum
(4) Congenital absence of posterior labrum
??
**Ans: C**
Buford complex為肩關節正常解剖變異，組合為：先天性前上方盂唇缺失(congenital absence of anterosuperior labrum，選項1)合併索狀增厚之中盂肱韌帶(cordlike middle glenohumeral ligament，選項2)，即1+2(答案C)，不應誤認為病理性盂唇撕裂。
