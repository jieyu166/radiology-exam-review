#交換 #2020交換 #MSK 有關Lisfranc fracture-dislocation下列何者有誤?
(A) Dislocation of metatarso-cuneiform joint
(B) Associated forced plantar flexion of forefoot on heel
(C) Athletic injury in older population, and Charcot arthropathy in younger diabetic population.
(D) Identify Lisfranc ligament between medial cuneiform and proximal 2nd metatarsal may be the diagnostic clue
(E) None of above
??
**Ans: C**
官方詳解標示答案C（選項C年齡族群與病因描述反了——athletic injury好發於較年輕族群、Charcot arthropathy好發於較年長糖尿病族群，與選項所述相反）。官方詳解對選項B亦加註「描述也不很正確?」表達存疑，因受傷機轉原文為軸向負荷合併forefoot過度蹠屈（axial load through hyperplantar flexed forefoot），與選項B「forced plantar flexion of forefoot on heel」用詞略有差異，暫依官方標示C為預設值。Lisfranc ligament連接medial cuneiform與2nd metatarsal base，是唯一跨越第1、2列之骨間韌帶，斷裂為診斷關鍵線索，詳見[[lisfranc-injury]]。好發第三個十年、男性多於女性。
