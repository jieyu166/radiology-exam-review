#交換 #2020交換 #GU Which of the following ovarian lesions is typically hypointense (relative to skeletal muscles) at T2W MR imaging?
(A) Krukenberg tumor
(B) Yolk sac tumor
(C) Abscess
(D) Subacute hemorrhage
??
**Ans: D**
T2低訊號（相對於骨骼肌）之卵巢病灶包括：endometrioma、cystic adenomyosis、hematosalpinx、leiomyoma、fibroma、fibrothecoma、cystadenofibroma、struma ovarii。T2高訊號病灶則包括：hemorrhagic cyst、malignant transformation of endometrioma、mucinous cystic neoplasm、Krukenberg tumor。本題答案D「subacute hemorrhage」須留意：早期subacute（intracellular methemoglobin，3-7天）T1高、T2低；但late subacute（extracellular methemoglobin，1週至數月）則T1與T2皆呈高訊號，官方詳解對此已加註提醒。
