#交換 #2020交換 #ABD Which of the following regarding renal cell carcinoma is not **true**?
(A) Clear cell type is the most common subtype
(B) Clear cell type typically exhibits exophytic growth and tends to be heterogeneous due to necrosis, hemorrhage or cystic change
(C) Calcification is more common in the papillary and chromophone subtype
(D) Cleat cell RCC have a better prognosis and less likelihood of metastasus than the other subtypes of RCC
??
**Ans: D**
實際上clear cell RCC預後較差、轉移傾向較高（相對papillary/chromophobe），故(D)為非真敘述。Clear cell最常見亞型且呈exophytic異質性生長(壞死/出血/囊變)、鈣化於papillary/chromophobe較常見，皆為正確敘述。
