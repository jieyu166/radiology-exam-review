#交換 #2020交換 #GI When a hypervascular intramural subepithelial mass larger than 3 cm is encountered at contrast-enhanced CT involving the GI tract, which one is the most common and should be considered first in the differential diagnosis?
(A) Angiosarcoma
(B) Gastrointestinal Stromal Tumor
(C) Kaposi Sarcoma
(D) Nerve Sheath Tumors
??
**Ans: B**
當顯影CT上遇到>3cm之富血管性壁內黏膜下腫瘤，GIST為最常見且應優先列入鑑別診斷之腫瘤；其他富血管性黏膜下病灶(類癌、腎絲球瘤、異位胰臟)多呈腔內病灶、<3cm、且較GIST強化更明顯，惟<3cm之GIST影像特徵與上述病灶有重疊。
