#交換 #2020交換 #Breast 下列何者為非？（關於Pseudoangiomatous Stromal Hyperplasia, PASH）
(A) Pseudoangiomatous Stromal Hyperplasia (PASH) is a benign proliferative lesion of the breast stroma that is seen more commonly in premenopausal and perimenopausal patients
(B) PASH can appear as a mass or asymmetry but may be occult at mammography
(C) PASH is more often seen as a hypoechoic or heterogeneous circumscribed or irregular mass but may have echogenic components and rarely can be predominantly hyperechoic at US.
(D) PASH is not a premalignant condition or a high-risk marker for malignancy, 4%–10% of biopsy specimens have been reported to have concurrent DCIS or invasive carcinoma in the surrounding tissues.
??
**Ans: D**
官方詳解原始標註「ANS: 4」，並附註「看起來應該都對?」，表達對此答案之自我存疑；詳見vault/concepts/pseudoangiomatous-stromal-hyperplasia.md之[!warning]標記，建議醫師覆核。暫依官方標示答案D為預設值。
PASH好發於停經前及停經前後婦女之乳房間質良性增生性病灶。可表現為腫塊或不對稱，於乳房攝影上可能無法顯示（occult）。超音波多呈低回音或異質性、邊緣清楚或不規則之腫塊，少數可主要呈高回音。PASH並非癌前病變或惡性高風險標記，但4-10%切片檢體於周邊組織可見合併DCIS或侵襲性癌，故仍建議密切臨床與影像追蹤。
