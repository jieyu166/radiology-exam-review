#交換 #2020交換 #GU About renal cell carcinoma staging, which is Stage T3c?
(A) Tumor invades beyond gerota fascia
(B) Tumor extends into ipsilateral adrenal gland
(C) Tumor grossly extends into the vena cava above diaphragm
(D) Tumor grossly extends into the renal vein or its segmental branches
??
**Ans: C**
T3c定義為侵犯橫膈以上下腔靜脈或下腔靜脈管壁。侵犯超過Gerota筋膜為T4；侵犯同側腎上腺亦屬T4；侵犯腎靜脈或其節段分支為T3a。
