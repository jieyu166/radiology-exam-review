#交換 #2020交換 #PE Which of the following descriptions is **false**?
(A) Pseudosubluxation shows mild anterior positioning of C2 in relation to C3.
(B) Pseudosubluxation is rarely seen in the 2nd decade of life.
(C) The margin of a fracture is irregular with a cortical border.
(D) Synchondroses are located in predictable areas with relatively smooth border.
??
**Ans: C**
骨折邊緣特徵為不規則且「不具(without)」皮質緣(cortical border)，而非「具有(with)」皮質緣，C敘述錯誤（應為irregular WITHOUT cortical border）。假性半脫位(pseudosubluxation)呈C2相對C3輕度前移，於兒童常見但青春期後(第二個十年)少見；軟骨聯合(synchondroses)位於可預測部位且邊緣相對平滑，皆為正確敘述。
