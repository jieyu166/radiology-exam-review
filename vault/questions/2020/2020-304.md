#交換 #2020交換 #ABD Which of the following feature is least likely seen in benign ovarian mass?
(A) absence of papillary projection
(B) thin wall <3 mm
(C) increased echogenicity within a cyst
(D) unilocularity of cyst
??
**Ans: C**
良性單純囊腫內部應為無回音，回音增加提示出血/感染/複雜成分，非典型良性特徵，故(C)最不可能見於良性卵巢腫塊。無乳突狀突起、壁薄<3mm、單房性皆為典型良性特徵。
