#交換 #2020交換 #GU The ideal renal entry and puncture site for a simple draining percutaneous nephrostomy includes the following:
A. subcostal, B. intercostal, C. upper pole calyx, D. lower pole calyx, E. posterior calyx, F. anterior calyx, G. Brodel bloodless line, H. Treves bloodless line.
(A) A+C+E+G
(B) A+D+E+G
(C) A+D+F+H
(D) B+D+E+H
(E) B+C+E+G
??
**Ans: B**
理想之經皮腎造廔穿刺進針點為：subcostal(肋骨下，避開肋間神經血管及胸膜)＋lower pole calyx(下極腎盞，較安全)＋posterior calyx(後側腎盞，經Brodel無血管平面進入)＋Brodel bloodless line(腎臟腹側2/3與背側1/3交界處之相對無血管平面)。故正確組合為A+D+E+G(B)。Brodel無血管平面約位於腎臟腹側2/3與背側1/3交界；下側後方腎盞方向朝向此無血管平面。
