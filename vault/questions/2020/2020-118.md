#交換 #2019交換 #2020交換 #NR Which of the following descriptions of spontaneous intracranial hypotension is **INCORRECT**?
(A) Diffuse pachymeningeal enhancement is common in patients with SIH.
(B) In patients with SIH, the angle between midbrain, pons increases.
(C) Narrowing of midbrain-pons angle predicts poor response to blood patch treatment.
(D) According to ICHD-3, SIH diagnosis requires measurement of low CSF pressure and/or evidence of CSF leak on neuroimaging (CT myelography or MR myelography)
??
**Ans: B**
正確答案為 **B**（錯誤敘述）。SIH 病人因腦幹下沉（sagging brain），中腦-橋腦夾角（midbrain-pons angle）應**縮小（narrowing，<50° 提示顱內低壓）**，而非增大。夾角縮小可預測對 blood patch 治療反應不佳；瀰漫性硬腦膜強化為常見表現；ICHD-3 診斷需低 CSF 壓力測量值及/或影像 CSF 滲漏證據。詳見 [[spontaneous-intracranial-hypotension]]。
