#交換 #2020交換 #US About "the pulse-echo display modes", which of the following statement is **wrong**?
(A) A-mode produces a one-dimensional image that displays the amplitude strength of the returning echo signals along the horizontal axis and the time along the vertical axis
(B) The B-mode method displays the intensity of an echo by varying the brightness of a dot to correspond to echo strength.
(C) The M-mode displays time along the horizontal axis and the depth along the vertical axis to depict movement, especially in cardiac structures
(D) Real-time imaging provides a dynamic presentation of multiple image frames per second over selected areas of the body
??
**Ans: A**
A-mode產生一維影像，應為振幅(amplitude)沿縱軸(vertical axis)、時間沿橫軸(horizontal axis)呈現，題目敘述橫縱軸相反，為錯誤敘述。B-mode以亮度對應回音強度、M-mode以橫軸為時間縱軸為深度呈現運動(如心臟結構)、即時成像(real-time)提供每秒多張動態影像，皆為正確敘述。
