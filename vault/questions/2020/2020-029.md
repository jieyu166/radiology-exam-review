#交換 #2020交換 #ABD Which one is **wrong** about hepatic cavernous hemangioma?
(A) Most common solid benign liver tumor
(B) 3rd most common liver tumor
(C) Rarely seen in young children
(D) May enlarge during pregnancy
??
**Ans: B**
- 最常見的肝良性腫瘤，4%人有。成人較多，女性較多(5:1)
- 第二常見的liver tumor，最多的是metastases
### Kasabach-Merritt syndrome
- **Thrombocytopenia**, caused by sequestration and destruction of platelets within a large cavernous hemangioma, occasionally occurs in **infants** .
- Hemangioma thrombocytopenia syndrome: Rapidly growing vascular tumor ==> thrombocytopenia, microangiopathic hemolytic anemia and consumptive coagulopathy
## 影像表現
### 超音波的表現很多樣化
* 典型表現：[A-C] 典型的小的跟大的 hemangioma
  * 很多很小亮亮的 small (<3 cm in diameter), well defined, homogeneous, and hyperechoic[A]
    * 因內有很多cavernous sinuses，wall造成反射會hyperechoic。
  * 也可能是一個很大的lobulated echogenic mass。大的hemangioma通常是 heterogeneous中間會有 hypoechoic的部分，可能是 fibrous collagen scars, large vascular spaces, thrombosis, ... [C]，而邊緣可能像貝殼一樣 (a tendency toward scalloping margin) [B]  ![ADD Scalopped](data/images/obsidian/add-scalopped-d28973d4.jpg)
* 67 ~ 79% hemangiomas是 hyperechoic，其中58 ~ 73%是 homogeneous。
* Atypical findings: [D-F]
  * 中間較不均質(a nonhomogeneous central area)，會有 hypoechoic portions，可能是 uniformly granular 顆粒狀 or 花邊 lacelike  [D-F]
  * 邊緣是 echogenic的，粗細都可以 [E-G]
  * (D) hypoechoic的 hemangioma。有很薄的 echogenic border.
  * (E) 左邊典型，右邊 atypical，有很厚的echogenic border，中間hypoechoic.
  * (F) 中間 hypoechoic 且rim irregular echogenic.
* 少見的 findings: [G-I]
  * 如果病人有fatty liver，那hemangioma可以是 hypoechoic
  * (G) Exophytic hemangioma.
  * (H) Hypoechoic hemangioma with increased through transmission。Posterior acoustic enhancement，做angiography可能會是 hypervascularity。算典型但很少看到(suggestive but infrequently encountered sign)
  * (I)有鈣化的也很罕見
![ADD Hemangioma 1](data/images/obsidian/add-hemangioma-1-18308360.png)
Hemangiomas的特點是** extremely slow blood flow**，慢到 color/duplex Doppler 也看不到。至於power Doppler能不能看到則有爭議。Occasional lesions may show a low to midrange kilohertz (kHz) shift from both peripheral and central blood vessels.
### CEUS
Arterial phase：
* hemangiomas show **peripheral puddles and pools** that are brighter than the adjacent enhanced liver parenchyma.
* no linear vessels.
Portal venous phase:
* 會有 **centripetal progression** of the enhancement leads to complete globular fill-in, with **sustained enhancement** equal to or better than the liver in the portal venous phase, which may last for several minutes
* 顯影可能快或慢，也可能過很久都不會全部enhancement (may be incomplete) [Video 4.6]
* Rumack 幾乎所有hemangioma都用CEUS診斷，就不需要再排其他檢查了
## 追蹤
腹超常看到 hemangioma，如果每個要再排其他檢查確認會花太多錢。
* 典型的lesion，一般都是保守處理，甚至不追蹤，頂多3-6月追蹤看有沒有變化。It is considered acceptable practice to manage some patients conservatively without confirmation of the diagnosis.
* 病人如果 (1) 有已知的惡性腫瘤 (2) HCC高風險族群 (3) 肝功能異常 (4) 有些和肝臟有關的 symptoms (5) Echo是 atypical pattern，那還是要加做 CEUS, CT, or MRI 來確認是不是是hemangioma
	* 有些lesion，有可能會被誤認為hemangioma。如colon cancer metastases，vascular primary tumor (neuroendocrine tumor,  small HCCs)
  *  在Caturelli et al.的一篇前瞻性研究中，收錄了1982位有新診斷肝硬化病人，這些echogenic lesions，50%是hemangioma，50%是 HCC！！其中1648位肝硬化病人，有新出現的 echogenic tumor，雖然看起來像hemangioma，但全都被確診為 HCC！！==所以在high risk病人中做進一步檢查確認診斷是必要的。==

# Spleen
- US: Homogeneous echotexture that is predominantly hyperechoic
- MRI: similar to hepatic hemangioma
- 99mTc RBC scan: slow accumulation of activity in the lesion followed by slow washout.
- Nuclear scans with 99mTc labeled sulfur colloid usually show a photopenic defect due to the radionuclide accumulation being restricted to functional splenic tissue.
