#交換 #2020交換 #MS Which is **TRUE** regarding secondary osteosarcomas?
(A) Secondary osteosarcoma is associated with underlying Paget's disease
(B) Secondary osteosarcoma occurs exclusively after radiation therapy
(C) Secondary osteosarcoma has a better prognosis than primary osteosarcoma
(D) Secondary osteosarcoma most commonly affects children
??
**Ans: A**
續發性骨肉瘤(secondary osteosarcoma)與潛在Paget氏病相關(Paget肉瘤變)，並非僅發生於放射治療後；多見於中老年人(非兒童為主)，預後較原發性骨肉瘤差。
