AJR. 2019 Apr 11; 1-11 Microscopic polyangiitis的表現可能太細了
- Definie/Possible UIP pattern: Reticulation (93%), Traction bronchiectasis(73%), GGO (66%), Honeycombing (58%), emphysema (35%), septal thickening (22%)
- Inconsistent with the UIP pattern, GGO (97%), honeycombing (45%), consolidation (17%), mosaic attenuation pattern (7%), micronodules or nodules (10%),
- In Europe, the most manifestation is diffuse alveolar hemorrhage (DAH) (20.4%).
- Japan were interstitial pneumonia (IP) in 47.5% of patients and DAH in 11.5%.
