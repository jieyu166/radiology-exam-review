(A) Hypo- to isointense on precontrast T1W-weighted and fat-saturated T2-weighted images
(B) Early contrast enhancement
(C) Clumped nonmass-like enhancement in a ductal, linear, segmental or regional distribution
(D) Other enhancement, such as segmental, diffuse enhancement; or an enhancing mass would not be happened

**Ans: D**
The most commonly reported MR imaging manifestation of DCIS is **clumped nonmasslike** enhancement in a ductal (Fig 3), linear (Fig 4), segmental, or regional distribution.
