#交換 #2020交換 #Breast 下列 Digital Breast Tomosynthesis 何者為**錯**?
(A) Most DBT units use a tungsten (W) anode target with a rhodium (Rh) or aluminum (Al) filter.
(B) Increased breast compression thickness leads to increased average glandular dose for both FFDM and DBT.
(C) Anode target and filter combinations produce x-rays with lower mean energy, which leads to a lower average glandular dose compared with that of analog-film mammography.
(D) If FFDM is replaced with synthesized 2D mammography, the radiation dose can be reduced by 45%.
??
**Ans: C**
All of these anode target and filter combinations produce x-rays with higher mean energy, which leads to a lower average glandular dose compared with that of analog-film mammography, but an equivocally to slightly higher dose compared with that of FFDM.
Half-value layers (HVLs) of an X-ray beam, defined as the thickness of a reference material (aluminum is most often used) needed to attenuate the beam intensity by half, are commonly used to characterize its beam quality. The higher the HVL, the higher mean energy the X-ray beam has.

D. **正確**
