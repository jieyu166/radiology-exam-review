#交換 #2020交換 #NR According to 8th AJCC Cancer staging system, a 3 cm ipsilateral metastatic lymph node with extranodal extension in hypopharyngeal carcinoma denotes:
(A) N1
(B) N2a
(C) N3a
(D) N3b
??
**Ans: D**
AJCC第8版頭頸癌症分期中，**N3b定義為任何淋巴結轉移合併臨床明顯之腺外擴散(clinically overt extranodal extension, ENE(+))**——不論淋巴結大小或側別，只要ENE陽性即歸為N3b，故本題3cm同側轉移淋巴結合併腺外擴散應歸類為N3b(D)。
