#交換 #2020交換 #ABD All of the following may cause or simulate a hypervascular mass within the pancreas. Which is the MOST common etiology for such a lesion within the pancreatic body?
(A) Pancreatic neuroendocrine tumor
(B) Accessory spleen
(C) Pancreatic serous cystadenoma
(D) Metastases to the pancreas
??
**Ans: A**
胰臟體部富血管腫塊，最常見之病因為pancreatic neuroendocrine tumor（PNET）。Accessory spleen（副脾）好發於胰尾（非胰體），且與脾臟訊號/顯影行為同步可資鑑別；serous cystadenoma多呈微囊蜂窩狀伴中央瘢痕；胰臟轉移癌相對少見。詳見[[pancreatic-neuroendocrine-tumors]]。
