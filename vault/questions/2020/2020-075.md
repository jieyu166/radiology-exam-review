#交換 #2020交換 #CH Which one of the followings only involves one hemithorax and does **not** involve the other side?
(A) Malignant mesothelioma
(B) Pulmonary tuberculosis
(C) Pneumoconiosis
(D) idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis
??
**Ans: A**
Malignant mesothelioma: A unilateral pleural effusion is the commonest feature, reported in 94% of cases.
