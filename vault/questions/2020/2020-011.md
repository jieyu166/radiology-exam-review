#交換 #2020交換 #Breast 下列關於結構扭曲Architectural distortion，在Digital 2D 和 Digital breast Tomosynthesis 的比較，何者敘述**錯誤**?
(A) Digital breast Tomosynthesis 可以發現更多的結構扭曲
(B) 針對結構扭曲，Digital breast Tomosynthesis 發現乳癌的 positive predictive value 比 Digital 2D mammography 較高(73.6% vs 50.7%; p < 0.001)
(C) 當 Digital breast Tomosynthesis 發現結構扭曲，而超音波沒有發現時，惡性的機率較低
(D) 以上皆正確
??
**Ans: B**
AJR 2017; 209:1162–1167.
- A. Architectural distortion was more commonly detected in the DBT group than the DM group (0.14% vs 0.07%).
- B. **對於Architectural distortion，DBT PPV 較低(和其他研究較不同)**。The PPV(positive predictive value) of architectural distortion for malignancy was significantly lower in the DBT group than the DM group (50.7% vs 73.6%).
- C. In the DBT group, architectural distortion without correlative findings on ultrasound was **less likely to represent** malignancy than was architectural distortion with correlative findings on ultrasound (29.2% vs 66.5%.)
	- 但還是有接近30%可能是惡性，所以還是建議biopsy。
