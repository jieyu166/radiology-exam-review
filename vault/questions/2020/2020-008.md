#交換 #2020交換 #Breast 根據美國放射線醫學會(ACR) BI-RADS 2013 版，screening mammography 建議之 audit performance, 以下何者為非？
(A) PPV1(abnormal interpretation)要>=12%;
(B) cancer detection rate >=2.5/1000;
(C) recall rate 5~12%;
(D) sensitivity >=75%.
??
**Ans: A**
Table 7 Acceptable ranges of screening mammography performance
- Cancer detection rate >= 2.5/1000
- recall rate 5-12%
- **PPV1 3-8%**
- PPV2 20-40%
- Sensitivity >= 75%
- Specificity 88-95%
- *PPV3 20-45%*
