#交換 #2020交換 #NR Which of the following is not a typical imaging finding of chronic lymphocytic inflammation with pontine perivascular enhancement responsive to steroids (CLIPPERS)?
(A) Puncate and curvilinear enhancement in pons
(B) Minimal vasogenic edema
(C) No mass effect
(D) Hemorrhage
??
**Ans: D**
CLIPPERS典型影像特徵包含：橋腦點狀與曲線狀強化病灶(puncate and curvilinear enhancement)、極少血管源性水腫(minimal vasogenic edema)、缺乏佔位效應(no mass effect)（儘管顯影後表現顯著但幾乎無水腫或明顯佔位效應）、對類固醇治療後病灶數目與大小放射學上減少。**出血(hemorrhage)並非**CLIPPERS之典型影像表現。
