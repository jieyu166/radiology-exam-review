#交換 #2020交換 #NR What is the scope of benefits (給付標準) of IA thrombectomy in National Health Insurance of Taiwan(全民健保)？
(A) Anterior circulation, onset within 8 hours.
(B) Posterior circulation, onset within 24 hours.
(C) Large vessel occlusion, including internal carotid artery、M1, M2 segment of middle cerebral artery, anterior cerebral artery, basilar and vertebral arteries.
(D) NIHSS ≧8 and lower than ≦30
(E) All of the above
??
**Ans: E**
台灣全民健保顱內血管支架取栓裝置給付需同時符合：①前循環發作內8小時、後循環發作後24小時內；②NIH Stroke Scale評分≧8 and ≦30；③影像診斷為顱內大動脈阻塞，包括內頸動脈、大腦中動脈第1及第2段、大腦前動脈、基底動脈和脊椎動脈。四個選項皆為正確之給付條件組成部分，故答案為E(all of the above)。詳見[[ia-thrombectomy-stroke-window]]。
