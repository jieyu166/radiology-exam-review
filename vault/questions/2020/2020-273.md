#交換 #2020交換 #IR Which of the followings is NOT a recommended technique forming a suitable lipiodol-epirubicin emulsion for conventional TACE(cTACE) of hepatoma?
(A) water-in-oil [W/O]
(B) at least 20 pumping exchanges through the stopcock
(C) the volume ratio of epirubicin solution and Lipiodol of 1:2
(D) normal saline is recommended for preparation of epirubicin aqueous solution
??
**Ans: D**
Epirubicin應溶於顯影劑（比重與Lipiodol相近約1.27）配製，而非生理食鹽水，故(D)非建議技術。W/O型態、至少20次stopcock抽吸交換、epirubicin溶液與Lipiodol體積比1:2皆為建議技術。
