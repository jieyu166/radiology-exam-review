#交換 #2020交換 #CV Which one of the following statements about pulmonary embolism is **false**?
(A) Most pulmonary emboli resolve without sequelae.
(B) Acute pulmonary embolism cause more severe pulmonary hypertension and cor pulmonale than chronic thromboembolism.
(C) Patients with underlying malignancy, cardiovascular, or pulmonary disease are at increased risk of pulmonary embolism.
(D) Mosaic perfusion pattern may be seen in chronic pulmonary thromboembolism.
??
**Ans: B**
實際上是慢性血栓栓塞才因血管長期重塑導致更嚴重之肺動脈高壓與肺心症，非急性肺栓塞，故(B)為假。多數肺栓塞自行消退不留後遺症、惡性腫瘤/心血管/肺部疾病增加風險、mosaic perfusion pattern見於慢性肺血栓栓塞症，皆正確。
