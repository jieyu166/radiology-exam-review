#交換 #2020交換 #MS Which is **wrong** concerning the posttreatment imaging features of pediatric musculoskeletal tumors?
(A) The goal of treatment in benign bone tumors is removal of the tumor followed by the reestablishment of bone morphology and structural support without compromising axial growth, joint function, or overall morbidity.
(B) Ceramic material appears denser than adjacent native bone, and a thin, well-defined radiolucent zone at the host-graft junction is an expected finding early after placement, but this radiolucent zone should disappear as osseous ingrowth occurs.
(C) Persistent or developing radiolucency—often at the center of the graft—following graft placement may indicate tumor recurrence or graft resorption without incorporation.
(D) Nonunion is arbitrarily defined as lack of union by 1 year and is the most frequently encountered complication in biologic reconstruction.
??
**Ans: C**
移植物置放後持續性或新發之透亮區(radiolucency)常見於**移植物邊緣(margin)**（非中心center），可提示腫瘤復發或移植物未整合之吸收，故C敘述之「center」有誤。良性骨腫瘤治療目標為切除並重建骨形態與結構支撐、不影響生長板/關節功能；陶瓷材料植入早期於host-graft交界處可見薄而清楚之透亮帶並隨骨整合消失；不癒合(nonunion)定義為1年內未癒合、為生物重建最常見併發症，A/B/D皆正確。
