#交換 #2020交換 #CV Which of the following is **correct** about cardiovascular magnetic resonance?
(A) Narrowing velocity range when there is aliasing in phase contrast acquisition images could help in obtaining accurate flow velocity.
(B) Placing location of interest for imaging away from isocenter of the scanner would result in more accurate blood flow measurement.
(C) Coronary MR angiography could be performed without contrast agent injection.
(D) Late gadolinium enhancement imaging is usually performed after 30 minutes following contrast agent injection.
??
**Ans: C**
正確。
A. (Core Rad) Phase contrast 就是velocity encoded GRE。血流和image plane垂直(perpendicular)可被測量，順向白色，反向黑色。必須先定義速度的範圍，不然超過會有aliasing。所以應該要加大。
B. 一般而言應該要把心臟放在中間。
D. **錯誤**。LGE要多久之後 [[2020 RG Cardiac MRI in Patients with Acute Chest Pain.pdf]]說要7-12分後，Crack the core是說5-20分的區間。
