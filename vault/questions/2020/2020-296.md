#交換 #2020交換 #CH Which of the following is not one of seat belt-related injuries?
(A) laryngotracheal injury
(B) bowel and mesenteric injuries
(C) C7 and T1 transverse process fractures
(D) hip dislocation
??
**Ans: D**
髖關節脫位屬儀表板相關損傷(dashboard-related injuries)，非安全帶相關損傷。喉氣管損傷、腸道與腸繫膜損傷、C7/T1橫突骨折皆為安全帶相關損傷之典型表現。
