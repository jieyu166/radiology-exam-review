#交換 #2020交換 #CH Regarding HRCT findings of pulmonary lymphangitic carcinomatosis, which of the followings is INCORRECT?
(A) Distorted lung architecture
(B) Smooth or nodular peribronchial interstitial thickness.
(C) Presence of polygonal structures 1-2 cm in diameter.
(D) Diffuse, patchy or unilateral distribution.
??
**Ans: A**
Relatively little destruction of overall lung architecture.
