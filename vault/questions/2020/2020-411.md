#交換 #2020交換 #PE Which of the following imaging features is not typically seen in hepatoblastoma?
(A) Well-defined heterogeneous mass
(B) Cystic
(C) Chunky dense calcification
(D) Hypoattenuating
??
**Ans: B**
肝母細胞瘤(hepatoblastoma)典型影像為邊界清楚之異質性(heterogeneous)腫塊，低密度(hypoattenuating)，可見壞死/出血，約40%可見緻密塊狀鈣化(chunky dense calcification)，並非典型囊性(cystic)病灶。轉移最常至肺(10-20%)，亦可轉移骨/腦/淋巴結/眼/卵巢；可侵犯或壓迫肝內血管及下腔靜脈。
