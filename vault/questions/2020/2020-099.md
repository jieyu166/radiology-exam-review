#交換 #2020交換 #CV 有關 coarse amorphous myocardial calcification 的常見原因，何者為**非**?
(A) Trauma
(B) Cardioversion
(C) Endocardial fibrosis
(D) Mural thrombus
??
**Ans: D**
Coarse amorphous calcifications caused by trauma, cardioversion, infection, endocardial fibrosis.
Mural thrombus(即MI) 造成shaggy laminated calcification
