#交換 #2020交換 #NR 關於 VOGT-KOYANAGI-HARADA syndrome，下列何者並非其診斷的 criteria?
(A) Uveitis without a history of ocular trauma or surgery.
(B) Uveitis without clinical or laboratory evidence of other ocular disease
(C) Bilateral uveitis without retinal detachment
(D) Neurological/auditory signs/symptoms. Meningismus, tinnitus or CSF pleocytosis.
(E) 以上皆是診斷 criteria
??
**Ans: C**
- Vogt-Koyanagi-Harada syndrome is a multisystem disorder characterized by **granulomatous panuveitis** with exudative **retinal detachments** that is often associated with neurologic and cutaneous manifestations.
- Usually initially present to an ophthalmologist for ocular problems, including sudden loss of vision, ocular pain and photophobia. Hearing disturbances and dizziness may be present. After weeks or months, most patients notice cutaneous signs
- MR imaging:
	- bilateral contrast enhancement of the choroid is seen along diffuse choroidal thickening with scleral sparing and **retinal detachment**
	- T2 scattered **periventricular** white matter lesions
	- **pachymeningeal enhancement**
Am J Ophthalmol. 2001 May;131(5):647-52.
