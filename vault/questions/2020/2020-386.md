#交換 #2020交換 #Breast According to the ACR BI-RADS 5th edition, which one of the suspicious calcifications has the lowest PPV?
(A) Fine pleomorphic
(B) Amorphous
(C) Fine-linear or fine-linear branching
(D) Coarse heterogenous
??
**Ans: B**
ACR BI-RADS第5版中，三種可疑鈣化型態(amorphous、coarse heterogeneous、fine pleomorphic)皆列為4B類，PPV介於13-26%(平均20%)；其中amorphous(無定形)鈣化之PPV最低，文獻報告約7.2-10.5%，低於coarse heterogeneous(約13%平均)。
