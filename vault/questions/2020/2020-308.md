#交換 #2020交換 #CV Current gadolinium chelates (e.g., Gd-DTPA) that are commonly used as CMR contrast agents are:
(A) Intracellular agents
(B) Extracellular agents
(C) 60% extracellular and 40% intracellular
(D) Extracellular for the first 5 minutes after administration and then intracellular
??
**Ans: B**
多數含釓對比劑(GBCA)為細胞外對比劑，分布於血管與組織間質，非細胞內攝取(Primovist等肝細胞特異性製劑為例外)。
