#交換 #2020交換 #NR Which is associated with retropharyngeal edema?
(A) Calcific tendinitis
(B) Internal jugular vein thrombosis
(C) Radiation
(D) All of the above
??
**Ans: D**
咽後間隙水腫(retropharyngeal edema)可見於鄰近發炎(如頸長肌鈣化性肌腱炎)、內頸靜脈栓塞、放射治療後等多種情形，故A/B/C皆正確。
