#交換 #2020交換 #MSK 有關Scaphoid fracture下列何者為誤?
(A) Transverse fracture line most commonly seen in middle third of scaphoid
(B) Usually difficult to visualize non-displaced fracture line
(C) Union rate is up to 50% in uncomplicated fracture
(D) Scaphoid non-union mostly associated with fracture at proximal third and vertical oblique fracture at middle third
??
**Ans: C**
未併發症之scaphoid fracture癒合率應遠高於50%（非unproblematic fracture本身癒合率僅50%這麼低）；非癒合(nonunion)發生率僅5-10%（固定後），意即多數uncomplicated fracture癒合率應達90%以上，故C敘述錯誤。骨折部位分布：waist 65%、proximal third 25%、distal third 10%（較常見於兒童）；男女比2:1，好發第三個十年。Nonunion危險因子：vertical oblique骨折型態、位移>1mm、年齡增長、吸菸；transverse型態較vertical/oblique型態穩定。AVN發生率與骨折距近端極之距離直接相關：proximal 5th AVN率達100%，proximal 3rd AVN率33%。X光初期可能為陰性(27%)，需於14-21天後複查。
