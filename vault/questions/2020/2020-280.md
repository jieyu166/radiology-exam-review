#交換 #2020交換 #ABD Which of the followings is not a main risk factor for local recurrence of rectal cancer?
(A) Circumferential resection margin(CRM) positivity
(B) Close proximity of the tumor to the anal verge
(C) Higher tumor differentiation
(D) Extramural vascular invasion(EMVI)
(E) Anastomotic leak
??
**Ans: C**
實際上是較差分化(lower differentiation)才是局部復發危險因子，非較高分化，故(C)非危險因子。CRM陽性、腫瘤靠近肛緣、EMVI、吻合口滲漏皆為確立之局部復發危險因子。
