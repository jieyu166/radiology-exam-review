#交換 #2020交換 #US About normal sonographic appearance of adult kidney, which of the following statement is not **true**?
(A) The normal adult kidney is approximately 11±2 cm
(B) The normal liver is usually similar or slightly more echogenic than the right kidney.
(C) The central renal sinus contains a combination of fat and soft tissue and appears echogenic.
(D) It is possible to visualize the renal pyramids as structures even slightly more echogenic than the renal cortex
??
**Ans: D**
正常腎錐體(renal pyramid/medulla)於超音波應呈現較腎皮質(renal cortex)『低回音(hypoechoic)』，而非『稍高回音』，故題目敘述為錯誤。正常成人腎臟長度約11±2cm、正常肝臟回音通常與右腎相似或稍高於右腎、腎竇(renal sinus)含脂肪與軟組織混合呈高回音，皆為正確敘述。
