#交換 #2020交換 #US Which of the following is NOT a typical ultrasound finding of hepatocellular carcinoma?
(A) May be hyperechoic
(B) May be surrounded by a thin hyperechoic halo
(C) Calcification is rare
(D) May show invasion of portal vein
??
**Ans: B**
HCC周邊暈環典型為低迴聲(hypoechoic halo)（對應局部脂肪缺失），非高迴聲，故(B)為非典型。可呈高迴聲腫塊、鈣化罕見、可侵犯門脈皆為典型/正確敘述。
