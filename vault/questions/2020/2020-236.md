#交換 #2020交換 #CH Which data is the first randomized controlled trial to report a significant reduction in disease-specific lung cancer mortality due to screening?
(A) NELSON (Dutch-Belgian Randomized Lung Cancer Screening Trial)
(B) DLCST (Danish Lung Cancer Screening Trial)
(C) NLST (National Lung Screening Trial)
(D) UKLS (U.K. Lung Cancer Screening Trial)
??
**Ans: C**
NLST（National Lung Screening Trial）於2011年首度公布結果，是第一個報告低劑量CT肺癌篩檢可顯著降低肺癌特異性死亡率之隨機對照試驗，成為2013年USPSTF公共衛生建議之實證基礎。詳見[[lung-cancer-screening-trials]]。
