#交換 #2020交換 #GI Which of the following statement about multi-phasic MDCT for assessment of pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma is **true**
(A) Pancreatic phase is best for tumor detection
(B) Arterial phase is best for tumor detection
(C) The portal phase is acquired at 35-50 seconds after contrast injection
(D) Hyperattenuation of the tumor versus the healthy pancreas is characteristic
??
**Ans: A**
胰臟期(pancreatic phase，又稱early-portal phase，掃描延遲約40-50秒)為偵測與分期胰臟腫瘤最重要之時相，A正確。動脈期(arterial phase，掃描延遲約35-40秒)對供血自動脈之構造顯示最佳，但非腫瘤偵測最佳時相；門脈期(portal phase，或稱hepatic/late portal phase)實際掃描延遲約70-80秒(非35-50秒，該數值實為胰臟期範圍)；胰臟管腺癌典型於胰臟期呈**低血流(hypoattenuation)**（非高血流），與正常胰臟實質相比呈相對低密度。
