#交換 #2020交換 #US About hepatic abscess, which of the following statement is not **true**?
(A) It may appear as a complex solid and cystic liver lesion.
(B) Target lesions are usually malignant tumors, but abscesses may also produce a target appearance
(C) Abscess can appear solid on ultrasound.
(D) Liver abscesses often appear more characteristic on ultrasound than on CT
??
**Ans: D**
非顯影超音波(nonenhanced US)鑑別膿瘍與腫瘤(neoplasm)之能力，相較CT或MR影像有限——肝膿瘍於超音波上之特徵性表現『不如』CT明確，題目敘述相反，為錯誤。肝膿瘍可表現為複雜實性合併囊性病灶、可產生target appearance(靶樣病灶，通常見於惡性腫瘤但膿瘍亦可產生)、超音波上亦可呈實性(solid)表現，皆為正確敘述。
