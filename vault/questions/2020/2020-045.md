#交換 #2020交換 #2022交換 #ABD According to the 2019 PI-RADS Version2.1 (Prostate Imaging – Reporting & Data System), which of the following is **incorrect**?
(A) ADC map calculation should be performed using a low b-value set at 0-100 sec/mm2 and intermediate b-value set at 800-1000 sec/mm2.
(B) A high b-value of >1,400 sec/mm2 image set is mandatory.
(C) Biparametric MRI for prostate is that performed without DCE image. It’s has a growing role in the follow up of patients under active surveillance.
(D) The prostate is divided into three planes. The peripheral zone is further divided into anterior, medial posterior and lateral posterior sections on each side.
(E) PI-RADS system is a living document with adaptions being made as evidence accumulates in its use in practice (F) Dynamic contrast enhancement may assist in detection of prostate cancer, as a ‘safety-net’ or ‘back-up’ sequence, especially when DWI is degraded by artifacts or inadequate signal-to-noise ratio.
??
**Ans: C**
bpMRI在臨床顯著癌症 csPCa 的誤診頻率可能增加；另外在看 local recurrence，DCE 仍是必需的。
- “High b-value” images utilize a b-value of at least 1400 sec/mm2. 當 b-value增加，SNR 會下降。也要看磁場強度，軟體，廠商等。沒有最好的值只說要>1400。
- bpMRI跟mpMRI仍有爭議。目前PI-RADS仍建議用mpMRI。

| 消除了一些釓造影劑的副作用和釓滯留問題                          | 某些研究表明，DCE-MRI 的敏感性較高。 **臨床顯著癌症 csPCa 誤診頻率可能增加**                                   |
| 縮短檢查時間                                                    | bpMRI 的表現在多中心臨床試驗會中降低，尤其是在有多個判讀醫師的情況下。                                       |
| 可能降低成本，提高未經活檢的疑似前列腺癌患者接受 MRI 的可及性。 | 看 local recurrence，DCE 仍是必需的                                                                          |
