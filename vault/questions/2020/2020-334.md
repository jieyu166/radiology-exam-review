#交換 #2020交換 #NR Which one of following entities has a key imaging feature of T1 hyperintensity in the pulvinar?
(A) Japan encephalitis
(B) Fabry disease
(C) Susac syndrome
(D) Proteus syndrome
??
**Ans: B**
法布瑞氏症(Fabry disease)因醣脂沉積於深部灰質(尤其視丘枕核pulvinar)導致鈣化/礦化，於T1WI呈高訊號，即pulvinar sign，見於少數(約3%)重度腎功能受損之男性病患。
