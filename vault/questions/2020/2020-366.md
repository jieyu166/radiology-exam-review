#交換 #2020交換 #MS Which statement is **true** fracture terminology?
(A) Open fracture = a fracture that communicates with the outside environment such as ambient air.
(B) Fatigue fracture = fracture of an abnormal bone due to a normal stress
(C) Insufficiency fracture = fracture of a normal bone due to abnormal stress.
(D) Butterfly fragment = fracture entailing a fragment that is pulled away from the bone at the ligamentous or tendinous insertion (avulsion fracture)
??
**Ans: A**
開放性骨折定義為骨折處與外界環境(如空氣)相通，A正確。疲勞性骨折(fatigue fracture)實為正常骨骼承受異常/過度應力所致(非異常骨骼承受正常應力)，B敘述將定義對調；不足性骨折(insufficiency fracture)實為異常骨骼承受正常應力所致(非正常骨骼承受異常應力)，C敘述亦將定義對調；蝴蝶狀骨片(butterfly fragment)實為位於近端與遠端骨折片之間之大型三角形/楔形骨片(酷似蝴蝶)，並非韌帶/肌腱附著處撕脫之骨片(該描述屬avulsion fracture定義)，D敘述錯誤。
