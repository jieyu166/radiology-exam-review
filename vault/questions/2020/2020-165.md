#交換 #2020交換 #Unknown 根據 AJCC cancer staging manual 8th，Classification of lung cancers with multiple lesions 下列何者不包括?
(A) Second primary tumors
(B) Multifocal lung adenocarcinomas with ground-glass/lepidic features
(C) Multiple lung nodules
(D) Diffuse pneumonic-type adenocarcinoma
??
**Ans: C**
multiple lung nodules 包含了benign 跟malignant solid nodules，但是只有確定malignant solid nodule 才被包含在AJCC 8th staging 中 (either second primary or intrapulmonary metastasis)
[AJCC 8th](https://acsjournals.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.3322/caac.21390) 包括
- SECOND PRIMARY LUNG CANCER
- SEPARATE TUMOR NODULE (INTRAPULMONARY METASTASIS)
- MULTIFOCAL GG/L NODULES
- PNEUMONIC TYPE OF ADENOCARCINOMA
