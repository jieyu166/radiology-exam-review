#交換 #2020交換 #PE Which of the following descriptions about slipped capital femoral epiphysis (SCFE) is **wrong**?
(A) About 20% of femoral slips are bilateral at initial presentation.
(B) Radiographs for evaluation of SCFE must be performed for bilateral hips.
(C) CT is superior to MRI for evaluation of early physeal change before slippage.
(D) Predisposing factors include hypothyroidism, growth hormone deficiency and renal osteodystrophy.
??
**Ans: C**
評估滑脫前早期生長板變化，**MRI優於CT**（非CT優於MRI），故C敘述錯誤。約20%股骨滑脫於初診時已呈雙側、SCFE評估X光須雙側髖關節攝影、致病因子含甲狀腺低下/生長激素缺乏/腎性骨病變，皆為正確敘述。
