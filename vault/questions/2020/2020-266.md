#交換 #2020交換 #US Which of the following is NOT a typical ultrasound finding of hepatic hemangioma?
(A) Hyperechoic mass
(B) Hypoechoic in a fatty liver
(C) Calcification is rare
(D) Color Doppler flow in center of lesion
??
**Ans: D**
血管瘤中央典型為乏血流(慢流)，非中央可見血流訊號，故(D)為非典型。高回音腫塊、脂肪肝背景下呈低回音、鈣化罕見皆為典型表現。
