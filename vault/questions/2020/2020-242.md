#交換 #2020交換 #ABD The least common etiology or type of diverticulum in the esophagus/hypopharynx is which of the following?
(A) Killian-Jamieson diverticulum
(B) Zenker diverticulum
(C) Traction diverticulum
(D) Epiphrenic diverticulum
??
**Ans: A**
Killian-Jamieson diverticulum相對於Zenker diverticulum較少見（infrequently）。上食道憩室：Zenker diverticulum（pulsion，起源於下咽後壁正中，near pharyngoesophageal junction）與Killian-Jamieson diverticulum（pulsion，位於cricopharyngeus肌下方，前外側，多為左側，少數雙側）。中段食道憩室：traction diverticula（true diverticula，繼發於縱膈疤痕/纖維化/發炎如結核性淋巴結炎）較pulsion diverticula常見。下段食道憩室：epiphrenic diverticula（pulsion，位於下食道括約肌上方，多見右後外側壁）。Traction與epiphrenic diverticula多無症狀；症狀通常於憩室>5cm時出現（多為pulsion型），包含吸入、逆流、食道腔壓迫。
