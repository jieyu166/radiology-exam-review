#交換 #2020交換 #CH Patients with influenza pneumonia show reticulonodular areas of opacity with or without focal areas of consolidation, usually in which lobe of lung?
(A) upper lobe, unilateral
(B) upper lobes, bilateral
(C) lower lobes, unilateral
(D) lower lobes, bilateral
??
**Ans: D**
流感肺炎(influenza pneumonia)胸部X光典型呈雙側下肺葉(bilateral lower lobes)網狀結節狀混濁，可合併局部實質化；邊界不清之斑片狀或結節狀實質化可迅速融合，代表瀰漫性肺泡損傷或次發感染，多於3週內消退。
