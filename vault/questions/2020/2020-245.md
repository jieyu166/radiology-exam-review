#交換 #2020交換 #GU Which of the following is NOT a typical image finding of adnexal torsion?
(A) Hemorrhage
(B) Uterine deviation towards torsed ovary
(C) "Twisted vascular pedicle" sign
(D) More commonly related to a malignant ovarian tumor
??
**Ans: D**
卵巢/附件扭轉大多與良性病灶（most commonly dermoid cysts或paraovarian cysts）相關，而非惡性卵巢腫瘤；5-10cm大小之腫塊風險最高。扭轉主要特徵為卵巢腫大（因靜脈/淋巴回流受阻、水腫、出血），次要徵象包括骨盆腔游離液體、underlying ovarian lesion、血流減少或消失、及twisted dilated tubular structure對應vascular pedicle（twisted vascular pedicle sign）。附件扭轉常見單側，右側略多見(3:2)。
