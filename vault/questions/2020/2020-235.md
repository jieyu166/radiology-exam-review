#交換 #2020交換 #PED A newborn with microcephaly has a "blueberry muffin" rash. Head ultrasound shows lateral ventriculomegaly with numerous periventricular calcifications, abnormal sulcation, and cerebellar hypoplasia. What is the most likely diagnosis?
(A) Dandy-Walker malformation
(B) Tuberous sclerosis
(C) TORCH (toxoplasmosis, other agents, rubella, cytomegalovirus, herpes simplex) infection
(D) Alobar holoprosencephaly
??
**Ans: C**
Blueberry muffin syndrome（藍莓鬆餅疹）指皮膚多發性隆起、典型藍紫色病灶，因真皮層髓外造血所致，病因包括先天性感染（CMV、rubella）、血液疾病、惡性腫瘤（neuroblastoma、AML）、先天性血管疾病。合併microcephaly、腦室擴大、periventricular calcification、異常腦溝形成、小腦發育不全，符合TORCH（toxoplasmosis、other agents、rubella、CMV、herpes simplex）先天性感染之典型神經影像表現。不同病原體鈣化位置略有差異：CMV為thick chunky periventricular calcification；toxoplasmosis為nodular periventricular/cortical calcification；rubella為basal ganglia/periventricular；Zika則為皮質-皮質下白質交界處點狀鈣化，且microcephaly/micrencephaly程度通常較其他先天感染更嚴重。
