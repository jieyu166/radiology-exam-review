#交換 #2020交換 #NR Which kind of Gd agent has higher risk for brain deposition after repeated used?
(A) Cyclic ionic
(B) Cyclic non-ionic
(C) Linear ionic
(D) Linear non-ionic
??
**Ans: D**
Macrocyclic（環狀）製劑因籠狀結構較linear（線狀）穩定；ionic（離子性）製劑因gadolinium與chelate間之靜電作用較non-ionic穩定。故穩定度排序：cyclic ionic最穩定，linear non-ionic最不穩定、腦沉積風險最高。
