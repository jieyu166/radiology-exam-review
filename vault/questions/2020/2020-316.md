#交換 #2020交換 #GU About CT cystography for suspicion of traumatic UB injury, which one is **wrong**?
(A) Understanding the indications and optimal protocol for CT cystography in the acute traumatic setting is critical for efficient and accurate diagnosis .
(B) Prior to performing CT cystography, first check urethral injury prior to Foley catheter placement.
(C) Type 3 UB injury shows focal contrast extravasation near UB, but without extension into the intra- or extraperitoneal spaces.
(D) None of the above
??
**Ans: D**
了解適應症與最佳規範至關重要、應先排除尿道損傷再置Foley導尿管、Type 3損傷為局部對比劑貫穿但未延伸腹膜腔內外空間，A/B/C皆正確，故答案為D(none of the above)。
