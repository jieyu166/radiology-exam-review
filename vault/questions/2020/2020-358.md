#交換 #2020交換 #GI About the clinical and CT features of an hypervascular subepithelial masses and mass-like lesion, which one is **wrong**?
(A) Neuroendocrine carcinoma : Ulcerative tumor similar to adenocarcinoma; has metastases
(B) Glomus tumor : Relatively homogeneous; minimal enhancement in the arterial phase and delayed enhancement in the delayed phase
(C) Hemangioma : Phleboliths
(D) Heterotopic pancreas : Typically in the gastric antrum; shows intraluminal growth, an irregular border, and enhancement similar to that of the pancreas
??
**Ans: B**
腎絲球瘤(glomus tumor)於動態顯影CT呈動脈期與門脈期強烈周邊結節狀或均質強化、延遲期持續強化(非動脈期微弱強化)，強化型態類似門靜脈、下腔靜脈甚至降主動脈，反映其血管組織成分，故B敘述錯誤。神經內分泌癌似腺癌之潰瘍性腫瘤合併轉移、血管瘤合併靜脈石、異位胰臟典型位於胃竇合併腔內生長不規則邊界及類胰臟強化，皆為正確敘述。
