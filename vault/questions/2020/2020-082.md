#交換 #2020交換 #CV 有關 exudate pericardial effusion 的常見原因，何者為非?
(A) Tuberculous infection
(B) Uremia
(C) SLE
(D) Dressler syndrome
??
**Ans: D**
Rare. A-C是pericardial effusion常見原因。
- Uremia造成的pericardial effusion應該是transudate而非exudate。B不恰當。
- Pericardium正常有50cc的液體，超過叫pericardial effusion
	- 原因很多，最常見的是uremia。考試常考的是lupus, Dressler syndrome
- Dressler syndrome (DS): **RARE**. Inflammatory effusion post MI(weeks to months later). Delayed immune-mediated or secondary pericarditis.
	- DDx:
		- pericarditis epistenocardica (seen earlier in the post-MI period)
		- postcardiotomy syndrome
		- posttraumatic pericarditis
