#交換 #2020交換 #Breast On breast MRI, a focus is a dot of enhancement which is too small to be otherwise characterized. Which one of the following features does not make a focus more likely to be benign?
(A) High signal intensity on T2WI.
(B) No fatty hilum.
(C) Persistent kinetics.
(D) Stable since the prior examination.
??
**Ans: B**
Focus(直徑<5mm、無法進一步定性之點狀強化病灶)為良性之特徵包含：T2WI高訊號(如囊腫/淋巴結/脂肪壞死，但膠樣癌為例外)、持續型(Type I)強化動力學、與前次檢查相比穩定不變。「無脂肪門(no fatty hilum)」為淋巴結相關描述(正常淋巴結應具脂肪門)，並非focus良性與否之判斷依據，故B為不使focus更可能良性之選項。
