#交換 #2020交換 #CH Which one is **WRONG** for imaging features of COVID-19.
(A) The typical findings on CT is ground-glass opacities ( bilateral, subpleural, peripheral) crazy paving appearance, air space consolidation, bronchovascular thickening, traction bronchiectasis.
(B) Contrast chest CT is the first-line screening imaging modality for COVID-19 in daily practice.
(C) The primary findings of COVID-19 on chest radiograph and CT are those of atypical pneumonia or organizing pneumonia in peak stage (9-13 days)
(D) Mediastinal lymphadenopathy, pleural effusion, multiple tiny pulmonary nodules, cavitation are relative atypical.
??
**Ans: B**
常規篩檢CT並非第一線診斷工具，多數放射學會不建議以CT作為COVID-19常規篩檢方法，故(B)為錯誤敘述。典型CT表現、高峰期呈非典型/機化性肺炎、縱膈淋巴結病變等相對非典型徵象之描述，皆為正確敘述。
