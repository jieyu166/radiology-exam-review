#交換 #2020交換 #CV For coronary magnetic resonance (MR) angiography, data are typically obtained over several cardiac cycles. To freeze cardiac motion, it is desirable to obtain image data during a very short time period within each cycle. What would the optimal time for data collection be?
(A) 75 ms after the QRS
(B) Early systole
(C) Early diastole
(D) Mid diastole
??
**Ans: D**
多數低至中等心率病人，舒張中期(mid diastole)為心臟週期最平靜之時相，最適合凍結心臟運動、獲取影像資料。
