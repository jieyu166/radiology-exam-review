#交換 #2020交換 #NR Which is **wrong** about "persistent stapedial artery (PSA)" ?
(A) It can cause conductive hearing loss or pulsatile tinnitus
(B) It may be an isolated anomaly or associated with an aberrant ICA.
(C) There is no vidian canal in cases of PSA.
(D) None of above.
??
**Ans: C**
PSA之影像關鍵徵象是無foramen spinosum(因中腦膜動脈血流改由PSA供應)，與vidian canal無關，PSA不影響vidian canal之存在，故(C)為錯誤敘述。可致傳導性聽損/搏動性耳鳴、可單獨或合併aberrant ICA，皆為正確敘述。
