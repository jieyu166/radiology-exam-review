#交換 #2020交換 #CV About "intramural blood pool" in aortic intramural hematoma, which is CORRECT
(A) it is hypothesized to be result from tearing of intima
(B) its prognosis is similar to the ulcer-like projection
(C) it rarely presents as multiple lesions
(D) this term is relatively new, recently defined in 2011.
??
**Ans: D**
IBP一詞於2011年才正式定義，故(D)正確。IBP假說為主動脈壁剪力撕裂所致(非單純內膜撕裂)、與ULP預後不同(非相似)、可多發並非罕見。
