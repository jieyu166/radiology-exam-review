#交換 #2020交換 #PED A 1-month-old girl is brought to her pediatrician for a unilateral neck mass associated with torticollis. The mass was not present at birth. The patient's mother reports that forceps were used during the delivery. An ultrasound of the mass shows a heterogeneous solid lesion in the midportion of the sternocleidomastoid muscle. What is the most likely diagnosis?
(A) Fibromatosis colli
(B) Rhabdomyosarcoma
(C) Lymphatic malformation
(D) Infantile hemangioma
??
**Ans: A**
Fibromatosis colli為罕見之嬰兒纖維瘤病，發生於胸鎖乳突肌(SCM)內。臨床表現常為斜頸(torticollis)，最常與生產創傷（如forceps delivery）或胎位不正（如breech presentation）有關；可能略好發於男性。屬自限性疾病，通常於4-8個月內自行緩解，僅需物理治療。超音波典型呈SCM肌肉中段之異質性實質病灶。
