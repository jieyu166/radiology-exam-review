#交換 #2020交換 #US Which of the following is NOT a typical ultrasound finding of urolithiasis?
(A) Echogenic foci
(B) Posterior acoustic shadowing
(C) Twinkling artifacts on color Doppler ultrasound
(D) Ureteral jet into the urinary bladder
??
**Ans: D**
輸尿管噴流為正常生理現象，非結石本身之直接超音波表現，故(D)為非典型。Echogenic foci、後方聲影、color Doppler twinkling artifact皆為結石之典型直接徵象。
