#交換 #2020交換 #MS About "seronegative spondyloarthropathies", which one is **wrong**?
(A) These disorders are all linked to the HLA-B27 histocompatibility antigen.
(B) Ankylosing spondylitis classically has marginal, symmetric syndesmophytes
(C) Psoriatic arthritis and Reiter syndrome classically have Nonmarginal, asymmetric syndesmophytes.
(D) Involvement of the SI joints is not common.
??
**Ans: D**
實際上薦髂關節侵犯在此疾病家族中常見，並非罕見，故(D)為錯誤敘述。皆與HLA-B27相關、僵直性脊椎炎邊緣性對稱性syndesmophytes、乾癬性關節炎與萊特氏症候群非邊緣性不對稱性syndesmophytes，皆正確。
