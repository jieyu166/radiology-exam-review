#交換 #2020交換 #US Which of the following descriptions is **false**?
(A) Transabdominal ultrasound is superior to endovaginal ultrasound for evaluation of bowel lesions.
(B) At US, the cross section of the normal bowel wall appears as concentric echogenic rings.
(C) The normal thickness of the colon wall is 4 mm or less.
(D) Floating echogenicity might be seen in the abscess.
??
**Ans: B**
超音波上正常腸壁橫切面呈**交替之高回音與低回音同心圓環**(alternating concentric echogenic and hypoechoic rings)，對應腸壁各層組織結構，稱為「gut signature」，並非單純高回音環，故B敘述錯誤。經腹超音波因視野較廣，對腸道病灶評估優於經陰道超音波、大腸壁正常厚度≤4mm(小腸壁≤3mm)、膿瘍內可見漂浮回音，皆為正確敘述。
