#交換 #2020交換 #NR About oropharyngeal squamous cell carcinoma (OPSCC), which one is "**WRONG**"?
(A) Smoking-related OPSCC tends to occur in younger patients.
(B) Nonsmokers with HPV-positive OPSCC have the best prognosis with 3-year overall survival of 93%.
(C) Despite multiple metastatic nodes, the prognosis is not poor for these p16-positive tumors, resulting in reevaluation of the nodal tables.
(D) The most common source of a metastatic cervical LN in level II is a p16-positive OPSCC arising from the tonsil-like tissue of the palatine and lingual tonsils
??
**Ans: A**
實際上HPV相關(非抽菸相關)OPSCC才好發較年輕病人，故(A)為錯誤敘述。不吸菸HPV陽性OPSCC三年存活率93%預後最佳、p16陽性腫瘤多發轉移淋巴結預後仍不差(促使N分期表格重新評估)、level II轉移淋巴結最常見來源為顎/舌扁桃體之p16陽性OPSCC，皆為正確敘述。
