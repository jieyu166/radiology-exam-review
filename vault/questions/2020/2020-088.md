#交換 #2020交換 #CV 根據心臟核磁共振掃瞄技術 Late Gadolinium Enhancement, 如果你看見左心室 free wall 厚度正常但是出現 subepicardial and midwall enhancement, 下列疾病中哪一個是最可能的原因?
(A) ischemic infarct
(B) acute myocarditis
(C) sarcoidosis
(D) hypertrophic cardiomyopathy
??
**Ans: B**
C. Sarcoidosis: frequently nodular in appearance.
- Acute inflammation: myocardial thickening and increased T2 signal indicative of edema can be seen , with a subepicardial or midmyocardial enhancement pattern
- Chronic setting: granulomas cause focal areas of myocardial thinning resulting from scar formation. Delayed contrast enhancement can be seen in areas of granuloma and scar formation and is frequently patchy and nodular
Ref: [[2009 RG A Pattern-based Approach to Assessment of Delayed Enhancement in Nonischemic Cardiomyopathy at MR Imaging.pdf]]
