#交換 #2020交換 #US Which of the following descriptions about the US findings of pediatric thyroid papillary carcinoma is **false**?
(A) Hyperchoic solid nodule compared with the adjacent normal thyroid parenchyma.
(B) Prominent perinodular and/or intranodular vascular flow.
(C) Irregular border.
(D) Enlarged glands with diffuse microcalcifications.
??
**Ans: A**
兒童甲狀腺乳突癌超音波典型呈**低回音(hypoechoic)**（非高回音）之實質結節，相對於鄰近正常甲狀腺實質；結節周邊/內部血流增加、邊界不規則、彌漫性甲狀腺腫大合併微鈣化，皆為正確之惡性特徵。故A敘述錯誤。
