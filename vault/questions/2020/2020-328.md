#交換 #2020交換 #CV The diagnosis of ARVC by MRI is presently based on
(A) The morphology of the right ventricle.
(B) Global and regional function of right ventricle.
(C) To differentiate fatty tissue from myocardium.
(D) All of the above.
??
**Ans: D**
ARVC之MRI診斷需綜合右心室形態、整體與局部功能評估，以及脂肪組織與心肌之區別，三者皆為診斷依據，故答案為D(以上皆是)。
