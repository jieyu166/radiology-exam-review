#交換 #2020交換 #CV Which of the following statements is correct regarding the usefulness of CMR coronary angiography?
(A) It is indicated to assess for significant CAD
(B) It is indicated for screening of subclinical atherosclerosis
(C) It has high sensitivity but low–moderate specificity for detection of proximal and mid-significant CAD in selected individuals
(D) It is not useful for assessment of coronary anomalies
??
**Ans: C**
冠狀動脈MRA對偵測近端/中段顯著CAD於選定族群中呈現高敏感度但中低特異度(約94%/57%)。並非適用於評估顯著CAD之常規指標(CT較適用)、亦非次臨床動脈粥狀硬化篩檢工具；對冠狀動脈異常評估則有其角色(非無用)。
