#交換 #2020交換 #CV The description about Agatston calcium scoring, which is **wrong**?
(A) CT image greater than 120HU in density is identified as calcification.
(B) The lesion must > 1mm square in area.
(C) The sum of all lesions in all four coronary vessels(LM, LAD, LCx and RCA) corresponds to the total Agatston score.
(D) The Agatston quantification method is of limited value for MDCT.
??
**Ans: A**
Agatston法鈣化定義門檻為**>130 HU**(非120HU)且面積>1mm²；四條冠狀動脈(LM/LAD/LCx/RCA)各病灶總和即為總Agatston分數，此法於MDCT仍廣泛適用(非限制其價值)，故A敘述錯誤。
