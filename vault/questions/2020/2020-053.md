#交換 #2020交換 #CH Which type of bronchiolitis is associated with common ancillary findings, include post-treatment changes of head and neck cancer, esophageal dilation, Zenker diverticulum and large hiatal hernia?
(A) Infectious bronchiolitis
(B) Aspiration bronchiolitis
(C) Respiratory bronchiolitis
(D) Hypersensitivity pneumonitis
??
**Ans: B**
- Infectious bronchiolitis跟感染有關
- Respiratory bronchiolitis 跟抽菸有關
- Hypersensitivity pneumonitis吸入過敏原，看過敏原是啥
- Aspiration bronchiolitis, or diffuse aspiration bronchiolitis, is a condition characterized by a chronic inflammation of bronchioles caused by recurrent aspiration of foreign particles.
- Patients with esophageal conditions such as achalasia, Zenker diverticulum, or esophageal carcinoma are at risk for aspiration bronchiolitis.
- Associations
	- neurologic disorders: ~ 50%
	- dementia: ~50%
