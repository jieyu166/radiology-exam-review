#交換 #2020交換 #2022交換 #Breast 關於 Mammography 擺位評估的描述下列何者**錯誤**?
(A) Inferior extent of pectoral muscle at least to the level of nipple on MLO view.
(B) Nipple in midline on CC view.
(C) Margin of pectoral muscle convex toward nipple.
(D) Pectoral muscle should always be seen in CC view.
??
**Ans: D**
A correctly performed CC projection may show the pectoral muscle on the posterior edge of the breast, indicating that the breast has been positioned as far forward as possible, although this is not always achievable.
