#交換 #2020交換 #NR About Temporal Bone Trauma, which statement is Correct ?
(A) The most common site of ossicular dislocation is the incudostapedial joint.
(B) Incudomalleolar junction dislocations are the second most common, often occur with longitudinal temporal bone fractures.
(C) The incudomalleolar joint normally forms the so-called "ice-cream cone" sign on axial CT images
(D) all of above
??
**Ans: D**
四項描述皆正確：incudostapedial joint為聽小骨脫位最常見部位；incudomalleolar junction脫位第二常見且常合併縱向顳骨骨折；正常incudomalleolar joint軸位CT呈ice-cream cone徵象。
