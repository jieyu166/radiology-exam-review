#交換 #2020交換 #Physics About CT attenuation, which one is CORRECT
(A) The unit is after the name of a sole CT inventor
(B) It linearly correlates with the physical density of the human body
(C) The optimal HU of contrast enhanced artery is about 800 HU
(D) normal lung parenchyma is about -700 to -950 HU
??
**Ans: D**
正常肺實質約-700至-950 HU，故(D)正確。HU以Hounsfield命名但非唯一CT發明者(Cormack共同獲諾貝爾獎)、HU非直接與物理密度線性相關(實為衰減係數之線性轉換)、冠狀動脈CTA最佳血管內強化約250-300 HU非800 HU，其餘選項皆錯誤。
