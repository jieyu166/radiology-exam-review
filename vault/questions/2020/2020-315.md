#交換 #2020交換 #GU About locally advanced and metastatic RCC, which one is correct?
(A) RCC has been reported to metastasize to any part of the human body, including scalp, oral cavity, thyroid, and testis, etc..
(B) RCC is characterized by tumor heterogeneity, a phenomenon in which different clones of cells with specific genomic alterations coexist in primary tumor and possibly even metastases.
(C) As with most malignancies, the time of greatest risk for recurrence of RCC is early after treatment, with the midian time to relapse within 1-2 years after surgery..
(D) All of the above
??
**Ans: D**
RCC可轉移至任何部位(含頭皮/口腔/甲狀腺/睪丸)、具高度腫瘤異質性(不同基因變異細胞株可共存於原發與轉移病灶)、復發風險最高於治療後早期(中位復發時間術後1-2年內)，皆正確。
