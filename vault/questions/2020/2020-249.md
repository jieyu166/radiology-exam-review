#交換 #2020交換 #GU What are the characteristics of a typical transitional zone prostate cancer?
(A) Well-defined, low T2 signal intensity, early enhancement, and low apparent diffusion coefficient (ADC).
(B) Ill-defined, low T2 signal intensity, late enhancement, and low ADC.
(C) Ill-defined, low T2 signal intensity, early enhancement, and low ADC.
(D) Well-defined, low T2 signal intensity, early enhancement, and high ADC.
??
**Ans: C**
移行區前列腺癌（transition zone cancer）於T2WI呈均質低訊號（「erased charcoal」sign），邊界不清（ill-defined margins）、缺乏包膜（lack of capsule）、呈lenticular形狀，可合併或不合併anterior fibromuscular stroma侵犯，並呈低ADC值、早期顯影(early enhancement)。詳見[[prostate-cancer-mri-treatment]]。
