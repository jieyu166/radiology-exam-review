#交換 #2023交換 #2024交換 #NR The following description about feature of radiation-induced changes, which one is **wrong**?
(A) Radiation-induced leukoencephalopathy is characterized by cerebral white matter high signal intensity on T2-weighted or fluid-attenuated inversion-recovery (FLAIR) images
(B) Radiation-induced injury can be divided into three phases: acute, early delayed, and late delayed
(C) Keys to recognizing radiation-induced changes at follow-up imaging are knowledge of the amount of time during RT
(D) Radiation-induced leukoencephalopathy exhibits diffuse and symmetric involvement after whole-brain RT, with relative sparing of the subcortical U-fiber, corpus callosum, and gray matter
??
**Ans: C**
Ref. RadioGraphics 2021; 41:224–248
A. 正確（原文：「T2 或 FLAIR 上大腦白質高訊號」）
B. 正確（原文：「依表現時間，放射線誘發的傷害可分為三期：acute、early delayed、late delayed」）
C 的錯誤：原文說的是「RT 後（after RT）經過的時間」，不是「during RT（RT 期間）」。這個時序是用來判讀追蹤影像，指的是 RT 完成之後到目前掃描的時間間隔，因為：
Acute = 治療中～治療後數天至數週
Early delayed = RT 後 1–6 個月
Late delayed = RT 後 > 6 個月
D. **正確**
