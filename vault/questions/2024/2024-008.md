#交換 #2019交換 #2024交換 #NR Which of the following venous pathways is **not** a common outflow connection for arterialized flow in patients with carotid-cavernous fistula?
(A) Inferior petrosal sinus
(B) Superficial sylvian cortical veins
(C) Pterygoid venous plexus
(D) Thalamostriate vein
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。頸動脈海綿竇瘻管動脈化血流常見流出路徑為inferior petrosal sinus、superficial sylvian cortical veins、pterygoid venous plexus等；**thalamostriate vein並非常見流出路徑**。詳見 [[carotid-cavernous-fistula]]。
