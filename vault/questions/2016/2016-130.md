#交換 #2016交換 #PED About the characteristics of congenital cystic adenoid malformation, which of the following statement is **wrong** one ?
(A) Hamartomatous malformation of the lung.
(B) Usually unilateral, involving one lobe.
(C) Microscopic type: small cysts with solid US appearance.
(D) Type I CCAM cysts usually less than 2 cm, overall good prognosis.
??
**Ans: D**
> [!todo] 待補詳解
> 正解 **D**（錯誤敘述）。
> - A 對：肺的錯構瘤樣畸形。
> - B 對：多單側、侵犯單一肺葉。
> - C 對：microcystic 型（III）小囊、超音波呈實質樣。
> - **D（正解，錯）**：**Type I 為大囊（2–10 cm）、最常見（70%）、預後佳**，非「<2 cm」（<2cm 是 type II）。
