#交換 #2016交換 #CV during exercise. Popliteal artery entrapment is suspected clinically. Which of the following statements regarding imaging of popliteal artery entrapment syndrome (PAES) is **true**?
(A) In PAES, the popliteal artery is compressed with the ankle in the neutral position
(B) A normal Doppler ultrasound of the popliteal artery excludes the diagnosis
(C) In the normal popliteal fossa, the popliteal artery and vein pass lateral to the medial head of the gastrocnemius and are surrounded by fat
(D) Catheter angiography is the gold standard for the diagnosis
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **D**。
- **D（正解）**：**導管血管攝影（DSA）配合誘發姿勢為（傳統）金標準**。
- A 錯：壓迫需蹠屈/背屈誘發，非中立位。
- B 錯：靜息 Doppler 可正常，正常 Doppler **不能**排除診斷。
- C 錯：解剖敘述不正確。
