#交換 #2016交換 #Physics If the propagation speed of a medium is less than soft tissue, where will the resulting echo be placed?
(A) More superficial than it actually is.
(B) Lateral to the true location
(C) Deeper than it actually is
(D) There is no change.
??
**Ans: C**
機器固定假設聲速 **1540 m/s** 換算深度（深度＝假設聲速×來回時間÷2）。介質聲速**較慢** → 回波較晚返回 → 被誤判為**較深**。
(A) More superficial：較淺是「較快」介質的情形，方向相反。
(B) Lateral：此為**軸向（深度方向）**位移，非側向。
(C) **Deeper than it actually is（正解）**：較慢→回波晚到→機器當成更深。
(D) No change：聲速顯著偏離 1540 即會錯位，並非不變。
