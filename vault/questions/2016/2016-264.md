#交換 #2016交換 #MSK Which ONE PAIR of the following bones are connected by the Lisfranc ligament?
(A) The 1st metatarsal base and the 2nd metatarsal base
(B) The 1st cuneiform and the 2nd cuneiform
(C) The 1st metatarsal base and the 2nd cuneiform
(D) The 1st cuneiform and 2nd metatarsal base
??
**Ans: D**
**Lisfranc韌帶連接第1楔狀骨（1st cuneiform）與第2蹠骨基部（2nd metatarsal base）**，是穩定跗蹠關節（tarsometatarsal joint）內側柱的關鍵韌帶結構，此韌帶斷裂為Lisfranc損傷的核心病理。
(A)(B)(C) 錯：非Lisfranc韌帶連接的骨頭配對。
(D) **正解**：第1楔狀骨至第2蹠骨基部。
