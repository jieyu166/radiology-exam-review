#交換 #2016交換 #MSK Which ONE of the following imaging features is NOT usually associated with malignant transformation of osteochondromas?
(A) A bulky cartilaginous cap 2-3 cm thick.
(B) Dispersed calcification in the cartilaginous cap.
(C) Development of a bursa over the cartilaginous cap.
(D) Rapid enlargement of the lesion.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**（非惡性徵象）。惡性變（→軟骨肉瘤）徵象＝**軟骨帽增厚（成人 >1.5 cm）、散在/不規則鈣化、快速增大**。
- **C（正解）**：**軟骨帽上形成滑囊（bursa）為良性反應，非惡性徵象**。
