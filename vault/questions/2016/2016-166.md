#交換 #2016交換 #H&N Which statement is **wrong**?
(A) Hemangioblastoma is the most common retinal tumor in the children and associated with von Hippel Lindau disease.
(B) Many benign and malignant entities share clinical and imaging features with rhabdomyosarcoma, but the presence of unilateral, rapidly progressive proptosis must always raise concern for rhabdomyosarcoma.
(C) Children calcifications in the globe mean retinoblastoma until proven otherwise even if it is bilateral.
(D) Retinoblastoma is usually detected through leukocoria as it occurs in two third of patients with retinoblastoma.
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**（錯誤）。
- **A（正解，錯）**：「hemangioblastoma 是兒童最常見視網膜腫瘤」錯——**retinoblastoma 才是兒童最常見眼內腫瘤**。
- B 鑑別多、C 兒童眼球鈣化→retinoblastoma、D leukocoria 表現皆對。
