#交換 #2016交換 #CV Which statement about the coronary artery fistula is **false**?
(A) Single or multiple fistulous connections between a coronary artery and other heart structures
(B) Connection with : RV > RA > pulmonary trunk> coronary sinus
(C) Left coronary artery more than right coronary artery RCA (50%) and LAD (42%)
(D) May with left to right shunt
??
**Ans: C**
冠狀動脈瘻管起源以 RCA(~50%)>LAD(~42%),故 (C)「左>右」為假。
(A) 單/多發瘻管:正確。
(B) 引流 RV>RA>肺動脈幹>冠狀竇:正確。
(C) **左冠 > 右冠(正解＝為假)**:實為 RCA>LAD。
(D) 可有左到右分流:正確。
