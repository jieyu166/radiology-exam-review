#交換 #2016交換 #CV Regarding pediatric anomalous pulmonary venous return (CT/MRI), which description is INCORRECT?
(A) It has supracardiac, cardiac, infracardiac and mixed types.
(B) Always check for left 2 and right 3 pulmonary venous orifices on the left atrium to exclude the diagnosis.
(C) Associated pulmonary venous obstruction most frequently occurs in the infracardiac type.
(D) Obstruction in the supracardiac type occurs where the ascending vein crosses the left pulmonary artery.
(E) Cardiac-type TAPVR may mimic cor triatriatum.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**（不對）。正常為**左2右2（共4條）肺靜脈**，非右3。四型分類(A)、infracardiac 最易阻塞(C)、supracardiac 阻塞於垂直靜脈跨左肺動脈(D)、cardiac 型似 cor triatriatum(E)皆正確。
