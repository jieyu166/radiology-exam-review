#交換 #2016交換 #NR About the imaging features of multifocal angioinvasive aspergillosis in an immunocompromised patient, which of the followings is **false**?
(A) Multiple nodules with strong ring enhancement
(B) Reduced diffusion pattern is frequently heterogeneous
(C) Diffusion restriction could be ring-like and peripheral
(D) On T2WI, the areas of low signal intensity at the periphery of the lesions may correspond to hemorrhages
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**（為假）。免疫低下腦部麴菌症：B 異質擴散受限、C 周邊 ring-like 擴散受限、D 周邊 T2 低訊號皆對。
- **A（正解，為假）**：「多發結節合併強烈環狀強化」假——免疫低下宿主**強化微弱/不規則**（強環較像化膿性膿瘍）。
