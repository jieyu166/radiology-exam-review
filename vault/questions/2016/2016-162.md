#交換 #2016交換 #CH About the imaging features of multifocal angioinvasive aspergillosis in an immunocompromised patient, which of the followings is **false**?
(A) Multiple nodules with strong ring enhancement
(B) Reduced diffusion pattern is frequently heterogeneous
(C) Diffusion restriction could be ring-like and peripheral
(D) On T2WI, the areas of low signal intensity at the periphery of the lesions may correspond to hemorrhages
??
**Ans: A**
> [!todo] 待補詳解
> MRI: reduced diffusion and weak ring enhancement.
> - Angioinvasive aspergillosis: multifocal hemorrhagic lesions with reduced diffusion.
> - Cryptococcosi: gelatinous pseudocyst formation in the basal ganglia.
> - Mucormycosis: frontal lesions with markedly reduced diffusion.
> - Candidiasis: microabscesses of less than 3 mm
> - Coccidioidomycosis: meningitis with contrast enhancement of the basal cisterns.
> - Blastomycosis and histoplasmosis: parenchymal abscesses or meningitis
