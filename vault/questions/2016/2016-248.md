#交換 #2016交換 #Physics Which one is NOT a risk factor of nephrogenic systemic fibrosis for patients receiving gadolinium-based contrast medium?
(A) Patients with chronic kidney disease 4 and 5 (GFR < 30 ml/min).
(B) Pregnant patients.
(C) Patients on dialysis.
(D) Patients with acute kidney insufficiency.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**（非風險因子）。**懷孕本身非 NSF 風險因子**（孕婦避用釓是為胎兒考量）。CKD 4–5/GFR<30(A)、透析(C)、acute kidney insufficiency(D)皆為風險因子。
