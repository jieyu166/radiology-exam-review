#交換 #2016交換 #MSK Concerning quadrilateral space, which of the following is **wrong**
(A) Surrounded by teres major, teres minor, triceps and humerus
(B) Suprascapular nerve passing through this space axillary nerve
(C) Fracture at humeral surgical neck may result in nerve compression
(D) None of the above
??
**Ans: B**
通過四邊孔的是**腋神經**＋後旋肱動脈,非 suprascapular nerve,故 (B) 錯。
(A) 邊界 teres major/minor、三頭肌、肱骨:正確。
(B) **Suprascapular nerve 通過(正解＝錯)**:應為 axillary nerve。
(C) 外科頸骨折可壓迫:正確。
(D) None:不成立。
