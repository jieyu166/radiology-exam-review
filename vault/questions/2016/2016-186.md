#交換 #2016交換 #NR Which is **WRONG** about capillary telangiectasia?
(A) Most commonly found in pons.
(B) Dilated capillaries without intervening brain parenchyma.
(C) Most lesions are clinically silent
(D) Lacelike re of stippled enhancement with no more subtle signal change on non-contrast spin-echo sequences.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**（錯）。A 好發橋腦、C 多無症狀、D lacelike/stippled 強化皆對。
- **B（正解，錯）**：「dilated capillaries **without** intervening brain parenchyma」錯——capillary telangiectasia **間夾正常腦實質**（無間質腦組織者是 cavernoma）。
