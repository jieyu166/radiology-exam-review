#交換 #2016交換 #PED About the following description of CROUP, which one is **wrong**?
(A) supraglottic laryngotracheobronchitis
(B) Most commonly caused by parainfluenza virus.
(C) Age: 6 months-3 years (younger age group than epiglottitis).
(D) Radiographic features including inverted "V" or "steeple sign".
??
**Ans: A**
**Croup（急性喉氣管支氣管炎）為聲門下(subglottic)病變，非supraglottic**——supraglottic病變典型為epiglottitis（會厭炎），故(A)為非。
(A) **錯（正解）**：croup應為laryngotracheobronchitis，非supraglottic（supraglottic為epiglottitis特徵）。
(B) 對：最常見致病病毒為parainfluenza virus。
(C) 對：好發6個月-3歲（較epiglottitis年齡層小）。
(D) 對：影像可見inverted V/steeple sign。
