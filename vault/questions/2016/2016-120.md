#交換 #2016交換 #ABD Which one is the most correct among all of the following descriptions for acute pyelonephritis?
(A) About three fourths of acute pyelonephritis are abnormal on imaging studies
(B) Ultrasound is more informative than CT regarding the extent of acute pyelonephritis
(C) Acute pyelonephritis shows normal or only renal enlargement on unenhanced CT.
(D) When acute pyelonephritis shows normal appearance on urography; similarly, normal appearances are depicted on contrast enhanced CT.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**（最正確）。
- A 錯：影像多正常，**僅約 25% 異常**（非 3/4 異常）。
- B 錯：CT 評估範圍/併發症**優於** US。
- **C（正解）**：**非顯影 CT 呈正常或僅腎腫大**；診斷性所見（striated nephrogram、楔形低強化）需顯影。
