#交換 #2016交換 #NR Which statement about congenital spinal intramedullary tumor is **false**:
(A) Mature teratomas show evidence of fat and calcification; bone and cartilage may be present.
(B) Hamartomas usually show isointense with spinal cord on both T1/T2WI. Rarely, may contain functioning choroid plexus and present with syringohydromyelia.
(C) Dermoids contain skin appendages with variable on T1WI and high intensity on T2WI.
(D) Epidermoids show most commonly isointense to CSF, but low signal intensity on DWI. high
??
**Ans: D**
Epidermoid 在 CT/MRI 似 CSF,但 **DWI 為 restricted diffusion(高訊號)**,故 (D)「DWI 低訊號」為假。
(A) Mature teratoma 含脂肪/鈣化、可有骨軟骨：正確。
(B) Hamartoma T1/T2 與脊髓等訊號、罕含功能性脈絡叢致 syringohydromyelia：正確。
(C) Dermoid 含皮膚附屬器、T1 可變、T2 高訊號：正確。
(D) **Epidermoid isointense to CSF but LOW DWI（正解＝假）**：epidermoid DWI 為 restricted(高訊號),非低訊號。
