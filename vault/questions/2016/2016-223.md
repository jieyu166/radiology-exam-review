#交換 #2016交換 #US A 64-year-old man presents with a 5-month history of painless enlargement of the right testis. Scrotal echo demonstrates a uniformly hypoechoic lesion almost replacing all the parenchyma of the right testis, with enlargement of the right epididymis and adjacent spermatic cord. The left testis shows a small hypoechoic lesion. Most likely diagnosis?
(A) Lymphoma.
(B) Leukemia.
(C) Bilateral seminoma.
(D) Testicular metastases.
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**。**Testicular lymphoma**＝>60 歲男性最常見睪丸腫瘤、最常見雙側睪丸內腫瘤；瀰漫低回聲取代實質、常侵犯附睪/精索、可雙側（~40%）。bilateral seminoma（罕見、年輕）、leukemia、metastases 較不符。
