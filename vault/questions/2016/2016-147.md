#交換 #2016交換 #CV What is the correct explanation for why a particular type of endoleak may occur after endovascular aortic repair (EVAR)? • (A) A type I endoleak occurs when patent branch vessels continue to perfuse the aneurysm sac
(B) A type IV endoleak occurs when there is a tear in the • endograft fabric
(C) A type III endoleak occurs when there is separation of • overlapping endograft components
(D) A type II endoleak occurs when there is poor apposition of the • endograft to the vessel wall
??
**Ans: C**
> [!todo] 待補詳解
> 正解 **C**。
> - **C（正解）**：**type III＝重疊移植物組件分離（junctional separation of modular components）或織物破孔**。
> - B 錯：type IV 才是 fabric porosity（非 tear）。
> - D 錯：附著處封合不良/poor apposition 是 type I（非 II；type II 為分枝血管回填）。
