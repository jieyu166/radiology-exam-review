#交換 #2016交換 #CH Regarding lung atelectasis, which of the following statements is NOT correct?
(A) In lateral view, RUL atelectasis appears as a triangular opacity.
(B) In lateral view, LUL atelectasis appears as an opacity extending from apex to diaphragm.
(C) Luftsichel sign is para-aortic lucency on PA view due to hyper-aeration of the left lower lobe.
(D) Right middle lobe syndrome is associated with endobronchial tumor.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**（不正確）。經典**右中葉症候群為非阻塞型**（差的側枝通氣＋窄 ostium＋感染/發炎）,非內支氣管腫瘤。A 對（RUL 側位三角形）、B 對（LUL 側位自尖延伸至橫膈）、C 對（Luftsichel＝LUL 塌陷時 LLL 上節段疝入致主動脈旁新月透亮）。
