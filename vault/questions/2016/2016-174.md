#交換 #2016交換 #NR Which of the following statement is **wrong**:
(A) Lenticulostriate arteries arise from the proximal MCA.
(B) Lenticulostriate arteries enter the brain through the medial 2/3 of the anterior perforated substance.
(C) Lenticulostriate arteries average 9-10.4 (range 1-21) in number.
(D) Lentioculostriate arteries measured about 100-2,200 micrometer in diameter at origin.
??
**Ans: B**
> [!todo] 待補詳解
> 正解 **B**（錯）。A 起自近端 MCA、C 數目 9-10.4(1-21)、D 管徑 100-2200μm 皆對。
> - **B（正解，錯）**：「經前穿質 **medial 2/3** 進入」錯——MCA 來源者經前穿質**外側**進入。
