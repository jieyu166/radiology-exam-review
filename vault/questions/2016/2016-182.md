#交換 #2016交換 #NR Among the so called carotid-vertebrobasilar anastomoses, which of the following is the one less reported or documented:
(A) proatlantal artery type 1
(B) persistent hypoglossal artery
(C) persistent otic artery
(D) persistent trigeminal artery
??
**Ans: C**
> [!todo] 待補詳解
> 正解 **C（persistent otic artery）**。
> - **C（正解）**：**persistent otic artery 最少被記載、其存在仍具相當爭議**。
> - A proatlantal type 1、B persistent hypoglossal、D persistent trigeminal（最常見）皆較確立。
