#交換 #2016交換 #IR Concerning percutaneous tumor ablation, which of the following is **wrong**?
(A) The organ involved, tumor type, and underlying comorbid conditions can affect the safety and effectiveness of different ablation modalities.
(B) The precision that is possible with cryoablation can be important for treatment of tumors adjacent to vulnerable structures.
(C) A direct approach for a lung nodule that leaves as less as possible tract of aerated lung is preferred.
(D) Vascular mediated cooling can dramatically affect the size and shape of the ablation zone.
??
**Ans: C**
肺結節消融偏好**保留一段(約≥2cm)未消融的充氣肺路徑**以防氣漏與支氣管肋膜瘻,故 (C)「盡量不留充氣肺路徑」錯誤。
(A) 器官/腫瘤型態/共病影響各消融模式安全與效果:正確。
(B) Cryoablation 對鄰近脆弱結構精準度佳:正確。
(C) **Direct approach leaving as little aerated lung tract as possible preferred（正解＝錯誤）**:應保留≥2cm未消融充氣肺路徑防氣漏/BPF。
(D) Vascular-mediated cooling(heat sink)顯著影響消融帶大小形狀:正確。
