#交換 #2016交換 #CH Which statement is **not** **true** in the diagnosis of primary pulmonary tuberculosis?
(A) Radiographic abnormalities in primary tuberculosis infections often resolve quickly under prompt treatment.
(B) Lymphadenopathy commonly occurs in children with primary tuberculosis infection. Unilateral lymphadenopathy is more often seen than bilateral disease.
(C) Pleural effusion may be seen without lung disease.
(D) Lymphadenopathy is uncommon in adults unless they are immunocompromised.
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**（非真）。
- **A（正解，非真）**：primary TB 影像異常**消退緩慢（數月至數年），常留鈣化/疤痕（Ghon/Ranke）**，並非快速消退。
- B 真：兒童淋巴結腫常見、右側/單側 >雙側（childhood >90%）。
- C 真：胸水可單獨出現（無肺病灶）。
- D 真：成人淋巴結腫少見（除非免疫低下）。
