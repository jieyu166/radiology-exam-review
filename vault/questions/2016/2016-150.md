#交換 #2016交換 #CV The diagnostic quality of a cardiac CTA with dense calcification can be improved by the following reconstruction and post-processing techniques: 
(A) Smooth kernel and narrow window
(B) Sharp kernel and narrow window
(C) Sharp kernel and wider window
(D) Smooth kernel and wider window 
??
**Ans: C**
Ref: Cardiac Computed Tomography Problem-Based Learning Page 28
正解 **C（Sharp kernel + wider window）**。評估鈣化斑塊/支架內管腔：**銳利（高解析）kernel** 減少鈣/支架部分容積、**較寬 window** 縮小高密度 blooming → 利於看管腔。
