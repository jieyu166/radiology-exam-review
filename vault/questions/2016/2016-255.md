#交換 #2016交換 #CH Which of the following is NOT a feature of an extrapulmonary lesion?
(A) Engulf pulmonary vessels.
(B) Incomplete border sign.
(C) Obtuse angle with the chest wall.
(D) Displacement of pulmonary vessels.
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**。**包覆肺血管(engulf)是『肺內』病灶特徵**;肺外病灶為推擠(displacement)肺血管、incomplete border sign、與胸壁成鈍角。
