#交換 #2016交換 #ABD What is the most common category of causes for splenomegaly? • (A) Neoplastic (e.g., lymphoma)
(B) Inflammatory/infectious (e.g., mononucleosis) •
(C) Congestive (e.g., portal hypertension, heart failure) •
(D) Hematologic (e.g., hemoglobinopathy) •
??
**Ans: C**
