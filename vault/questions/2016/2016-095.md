#交換 #2016交換 #ABD What is the “**wrong**” statement about renal oncocytoma?
(A) A benign tumor composed of eosinophilic epithelial cells from collecting duct.
(B) The tumor appears less dense than renal parenchyma in nephrographic phase of CT scan.
(C) The tumor shows central scar which is radiating hypodense area in nephrographic phase of CT scan.
(D) The angiographic finding usually shows bizarre neoplastic vessels and arteriovenous shunting. RCC; oncocytoma: spoke wheel
??
**Ans: D**
Bizarre neoplastic vessels＋AV shunting 是 RCC 特徵(oncocytoma 為 spoke-wheel),故 (D) 錯。
(A) 集尿管嗜酸上皮良性瘤:正確。
(B) Nephrographic 期較腎實質低密度:正確。
(C) 中央放射狀低密度疤痕:正確。
(D) **血管攝影 bizarre vessels＋AV shunting(正解＝錯)**:屬 RCC;oncocytoma 為 spoke-wheel。
