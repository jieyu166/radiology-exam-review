#交換 #2016交換 #CH Which of the following interstitial pnumonias is smoking-related?
(A) Lymphoid interstitial pneumonia
(B) Desquamative interstitial pneumonia
(C) Nonspecific interstitial pneumonia
(D) Usual interstitial pneumonia
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B（DIP）**。**Desquamative interstitial pneumonia（DIP，現稱 AMP）為直接吸菸相關**。A LIP（Sjögren/自體免疫）、C NSIP（CTD/特發）、D UIP（IPF/特發）非吸菸專屬。
