#交換 #2016交換 #CH Which of the following diseases causes confluent areas of ground-glass opacity that tends to be mid and lower lung predominant?
(A) Chronic hypersensitivity pneumonitis.
(B) Respiratory bronchiolitis.
(C) Desquamative interstitial pneumonia.
(D) Chronic eosinophilic pneumonia.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C（DIP）**。**Desquamative interstitial pneumonia（DIP，現稱 AMP）為吸菸相關，造成下肺/周邊為主的瀰漫融合 GGO**。
- A chronic HP（上中肺、馬賽克/air trapping）、B respiratory bronchiolitis（上肺 centrilobular 結節）、D chronic eosinophilic pneumonia（上肺周邊 consolidation）分布不同。
