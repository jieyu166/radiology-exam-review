#交換 #2016交換 #ABD Which description about congestive liver in cardiac disease is NOT **true**?
(A) Stasis of blood within liver parenchyma as a result of impaired venous drainage.
(B) Dilated hepatic veins with to-and-fro blood flow on color Doppler.
(C) CT shows delayed enhancement of dilated IVC and hepatic veins.
(D) Differential diagnosis includes Budd-Chiari syndrome and hepatic cirrhosis.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**（不真）。congestive liver 的 CT 為**對比自右房『早期逆流(early reflux)』入擴張 IVC/肝靜脈**,非 delayed。定義(A)、to-and-fro 血流(B)、DDx(D)皆正確。
