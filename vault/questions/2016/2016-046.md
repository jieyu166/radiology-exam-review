#交換 #2016交換 #CH Which statement is **wrong** about septic pulmonary emboli?
(A) cavitation happens frequently
(B) migratory infiltrates is typical
(C) uncommon with feeding vessel sign
(D) predilection for lung bases
??
**Ans: C**
敗血性肺栓塞的 feeding vessel sign 是其**特徵**，故 (C)「uncommon with feeding vessel sign」為錯誤敘述。
(A) Cavitation happens frequently：真（尤 gram-positive）。
(B) Migratory infiltrates typical：真（反覆栓子陣雨、多階段並存）。
(C) **Uncommon with feeding vessel sign（正解＝錯誤）**：feeding vessel sign 為其特徵，非少見。
(D) Predilection for lung bases：真（周邊、下肺基底為主）。
