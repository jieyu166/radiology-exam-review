#交換 #2016交換 #NR Concerning cerebral amyloid angiopathy, which one of the following is NOT correct?
(A) Common cause of spontaneous lobar hemorrhage in the elderly.
(B) Multifocal black dots of susceptibility on gradient echo or SWI.
(C) Mainly involving deep gray matter and brainstem.
(D) Signs/symptoms include dementia.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**（不正確）。**CAA 為皮質-皮質下(lobar)、保留深部灰質與腦幹**；深部灰質/腦幹出血屬高血壓性。老年 lobar 出血(A)、GRE/SWI 多發黑點(B)、合併失智(D)皆正確。
