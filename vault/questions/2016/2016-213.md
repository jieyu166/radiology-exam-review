#交換 #2016交換 #MSK The following radiographic abnormalities are NOT related to anterior shoulder dislocation:
(A) Hill-Sachs lesion.
(B) Bankart lesion.
(C) Trough-like impaction fracture of the humeral head.
(D) Subcoracoid position of the humeral head.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**（非前脫位）。前脫位＝Hill-Sachs（肱骨頭後外側壓迫）、Bankart（前下盂唇）、肱骨頭 subcoracoid 位。
- **C（正解）**：**trough-like impaction（trough sign／reverse Hill-Sachs）屬『後』脫位**。
