#交換 #2016交換 #ABD What is the “**wrong**” statement about renal angiomyolipoma?
(A) Benign renal tumor composes of abnormal blood vessels, smooth muscle, and fatty components.
(B) Calcification is often seen in renal angiomyolipoma. rare
(C) Multiple renal angiomyolipoma usually indicate tuberous sclerosis.
(D) If the tumor size > 4 cm, partial nephrectomy is often recommended.
??
**Ans: B**
腎 AML 鈣化**罕見**(有鈣化想 RCC),故 (B) 錯。
(A) 含異常血管+平滑肌+脂肪之良性瘤:正確。
(B) **常見鈣化(正解＝錯)**:罕見,有鈣化想 RCC。
(C) 多發想 tuberous sclerosis:正確。
(D) >4cm 常建議部分腎切除:正確。
