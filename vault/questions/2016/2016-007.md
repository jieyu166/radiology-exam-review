#交換 #2016交換 #CH Which one of the following statement is **correct** while talking about pulmonary sarcoidosis?
(A) Sarcoidosis is characterized by caseous epithelioid cell granulomas.
(B) Siltzbach classification system defines stages of sarcoidosis based on chest computed tomography (CT) appearance.
(C) Lung parenchymal abnormalities of sarcoidosis are predominant in the lower lung zones.
(D) Nodules and ground-glass opacities are suggestive of granulomatous inflammation that may be reversed with therapy.
??
**Ans: D**
(A) 錯：sarcoidosis 為 noncaseating（非乾酪性）epithelioid granuloma，非 caseating（乾酪性是 TB）。
(B) 錯：Siltzbach / Scadding 分期是基於 chest radiograph（胸部 X 光），非 CT。
(C) 錯：肺實質病灶以上肺與中肺野為主，非下肺。
(D) 正確：nodules 與 GGO 代表 active 肉芽腫發炎（perilymphatic、peribronchovascular/subpleural 分布），治療後可能消退；相對地 fibrosis（traction bronchiectasis、結構扭曲、囊狀）為不可逆。
