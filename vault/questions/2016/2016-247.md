#交換 #2016交換 #Breast Which of the following is NOT a common pattern of normal background parenchymal enhancement (BPE) in breast MRI?
(A) Picture framing.
(B) Scattered or innumerable similar foci of enhancement.
(C) Rapid early contrast uptake.
(D) Geographic areas of symmetric regional enhancement.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**（非正常 BPE）。**Rapid early contrast uptake（快速早期攝取）屬可疑/惡性 kinetics，非正常 BPE 型態**。picture framing(A)、scattered/innumerable similar foci(B)、geographic symmetric regional enhancement(D)皆為正常 BPE 型態。
