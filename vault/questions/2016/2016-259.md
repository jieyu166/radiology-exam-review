#交換 #2016交換 #Physics If the MRI receiver bandwidth (BW) is changed to half its original value, what is the effect on signal-to-noise ratio (SNR)?
(A) Becomes 2 times the original.
(B) Becomes √2 times the original.
(C) Becomes 1/2 of the original.
(D) Unchanged.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**。**SNR 與接收頻寬的平方根成反比（SNR ∝ 1/√BW）**；頻寬減半 → SNR 變為原來的 **√2 倍**。
