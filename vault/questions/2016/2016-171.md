#交換 #2016交換 #Unknown

一位三歲男孩的 Brain MRI 顯示 cerebellar tonsil 位於 foramen magnum 之下，請問以下何種表現支持 Chiari I malformation 的診斷？
(A) callosal dysgenesis
(B) myelomeningocele
(C) medullary spur
(D) craniosynostosis
??
**Ans: D**
> [!todo] 待補詳解
> Chiari I可能與craniosynostosis有關，特別是當這導致posterior fossa後顱窩空間不足的時候。
> - Essential of Osborn's brain: Many patients with CM1 demonstrate abnormal geometry of the bony posterior fossa (“normal-sized hindbrain housed in a too-small bony envelope”).
> - Osborn's brain 2/e: 10% of patients with nonsyndromic single-suture craniosynostosis have CM1.
> - A. Chiari II malformation可能合併：corpus callosal dysgenesis, heterotopias, and sulcation abnormalities.
> - B.  Chiari II malformation ：特別指和 Myelomeningocele 脊髓脊膜膨出一起發生的腦部畸形
