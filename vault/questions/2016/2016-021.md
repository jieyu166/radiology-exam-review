#交換 #2016交換 #ABD Which of the following statements best describes portal vein thrombosis in infiltrative HCC?
(A) Portal vein thrombosis occurs as frequently as does hepatic vein thrombosis.
(B) Portal vein thrombosis is less common in infiltrative HCC than in nodular or multifocal HCC.
(C) Portal vein thrombosis, when present, involves only intrahepatic branches.
(D) Portal vein thrombosis is a common finding, occurring in nearly two-thirds of patients.
??
**Ans: D**
門脈瘤栓在 infiltrative HCC **常見/具特徵性**，常為主要甚至唯一影像線索（題目依其 RG 來源量化為近三分之二病人）。
(A) As frequent as hepatic vein thrombosis：否，門脈侵犯遠多於肝靜脈。
(B) Less common than in nodular/multifocal HCC：相反，浸潤型更常見/更具特徵性。
(C) Only intrahepatic branches：可延伸至主門脈乃至肝外，不限肝內分支。
(D) **Common finding, occurring in nearly two-thirds of patients（正解）**：常見、依來源約近三分之二。
