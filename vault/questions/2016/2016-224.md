#交換 #2016交換 #US A 73-year-old male with poorly controlled hypertension referred for renal Doppler US. The US shows bilateral small kidneys about 5-6 cm in bipolar diameters. The resistive indices are about 0.9 on both sides. The peak systolic velocity is 130 cm/s on the left and 160 cm/s on the right. What intervention is indicated?
(A) Renal artery angioplasty on the right.
(B) Renal artery angioplasty on the left.
(C) Bilateral renal artery stenting.
(D) No intervention.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**。PSV 130/160 cm/s **未達 >180 cm/s 狹窄閾值**（非血流動力學顯著狹窄）；加上**雙側萎縮小腎（5–6 cm）＋ 實質高阻力（RI 0.9）**＝末期腎硬化，血管重建無益 → **不介入**。
