#交換 #2016交換 #ABD Which of the following statement about fatty liver is incorrect?
(A) Severe fatty liver may lead to cirrhosis.
(B) Fatty liver can be diagnosed if the attenuation of the liver is at least 10 HU less than that of the spleen or if the attenuation of the liver is less than 40 HU.
(C) Focal fat deposition or sparing characteristically occurs in specific areas such as adjacent to the falciform ligament or ligamentum venosum, porta hepatis, and gallbladder fossa.
(D) Multifocal fat deposition can be differentiated from hypovascular metastases and lymphoma without difficulty.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**（錯誤敘述）。
- A 對：嚴重脂肪肝可致肝硬化。
- B 對：肝衰減較脾低 ≥10 HU（或 <40 HU）可診斷。
- C 對：局部脂肪沉積/spared 好發特定區（膽囊旁、葉裂旁、門脈周圍）。
- **D（正解，錯）**：**多灶性脂肪沉積可被「誤認為」低血管轉移**（mimic、不易區別），並非「可區別」。
