#交換 #2016交換 #MSK Concerning pars interarticularis defects, which of the following is **TRUE**?
(A) Associated spondylolisthesis is most common at L5-S1.
(B) Symptomatic spondylolisthesis is always associated.
(C) They usually represent an acute injury.
(D) Magnetic resonance imaging is the best imaging modality for depicting the extent of an established spondylolytic defect.
??
**Ans: A**
Pars defect：~90% 在 L5，伴隨的 spondylolisthesis 最常見於 **L5-S1**。
(A) **Associated spondylolisthesis most common at L5-S1（正解＝真）**：好發 L5，故伴隨滑脫最常見於 L5-S1。
(B) Symptomatic spondylolisthesis always associated：假（常無症狀）。
(C) Usually an acute injury：假（多為慢性重複微創傷/疲勞骨折）。
(D) MRI best for depicting extent of established defect：假（既成缺損範圍以 CT 最佳；MRI/SPECT 用於早期 stress response）。
