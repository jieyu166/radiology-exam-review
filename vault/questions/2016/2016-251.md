#交換 #2016交換 #CV Regarding the classification of VSD on CT/MRI, which description is **WRONG**?
(A) Defect under the aortic valve is called the subarterial type.
(B) Defect involving the perimembranous septum is called the perimembranous type.
(C) "Swiss cheese" VSDs occur in the muscular septum (muscular type).
(D) Defect extending to the atrioventricular valve insertion is called the inlet type.
(E) Types are named from the viewpoint of the right ventricle.
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**（錯誤）。**subarterial（supracristal）VSD 位於『肺動脈瓣下(subpulmonary)』,非主動脈瓣下**。perimembranous、Swiss-cheese muscular、inlet、以右心室面命名皆正確。
