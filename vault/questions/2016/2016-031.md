#交換 #2016交換 #NR Which statement is **false** for anti-NMDA (N-methyl-D-aspartic acid) receptor encephalitis?
(A) Old female patients are commonly affected.
(B) Clinical presentation as prominent psychiatric symptoms and/or seizures.
(C) Ovarian teratoma may be found in more than half of patients. Early resection of tumor relates to better prognosis.
(D) MRI may show non-specific increased T2 FLAIR signals without significant correlation with patients’ symptoms
??
**Ans: A**
抗 NMDAR 腦炎好發**年輕女性**（F:M≈4:1），故「老年女性好發」為假。
(A) **Old female patients commonly affected（正解＝假）**：好發年輕女性，非老年。
(B) Prominent psychiatric symptoms and/or seizures：真。
(C) Ovarian teratoma in >half; early resection better prognosis：真（畸胎瘤 40–60%，早切預後較佳）。
(D) MRI nonspecific T2/FLAIR without symptom correlation：真（影像常正常/非特異）。
