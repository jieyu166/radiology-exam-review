#交換 #2016交換 #CV A 44-year-old female with a bicuspid aortic valve and mild aortic regurgitation; on metoprolol; smoker; brother died of ascending aortic dissection at 48. CT shows ascending aorta 4.7 cm. You recommend:
(A) Yearly imaging; repair when AR severe or symptoms develop.
(B) Biannual imaging; elective repair when aortic diameter >5.5 cm.
(C) Elective repair when aortic diameter >5 cm.
(D) Proceed with elective ascending aortic aneurysm surgical repair now.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**。**BAV ＋ 一等親主動脈剝離家族史**為高風險特徵,使手術門檻自 5.5 cm 下修（約 5.0 cm,合併高風險家族史更低至 ~4.5 cm）;4.7 cm 已達介入指徵 → 立即擇期手術。等 >5.5/>5.0 cm 或症狀(A/B/C)不符高風險族群。
