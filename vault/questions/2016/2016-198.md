#交換 #2016交換 #ABD Which of the following is **TRUE** concerning papillary necrosis?
(A) Common etiologies are analgesics, diabetes mellitus, and sickle cell anemia.
(B) Conventional radiographs usually show characteristic calcification.
(C) Approximately 90% of patients with sickle cell disease develop papillary necrosis.
(D) Medullary type papillary necrosis is associated with an absence of the entire papilla.
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**（為真）。
- **A（正解，為真）**：papillary necrosis 病因記憶法 **NSAID**——NSAIDs、Sickle cell anemia、Analgesic nephropathy、Infection（含 TB）、Diabetes mellitus。
- B 錯：平片**通常正常**；壞死時可有不規則鈣化（2-6mm）或滯留脫落乳頭之環狀鈣化，但非「特徵性鈣化」。
- C 錯：約 **up to 50%**（非 90%）鐮型球患者發生乳頭壞死。
- D 錯：**medullary 型＝乳頭『中央』侵蝕**；整顆乳頭脫落是 papillary（total）型。
