#交換 #2016交換 #ABD Generally, a constant relationship between external liver anatomy, haptic veins, and the intrahepatic branches of the portal veins is presumed, but there are still variants. About the right inferior hepatic vein, which of the following statement is incorrect?
(A) A large right inferior hepatic vein draining segment 6 is found in 15-20% of normal subjects.
(B) When a right inferior hepatic vein is present, the right hepatic vein is usually smaller, as it does not drain segment 6.
(C) when a small inferior right hepatic vein is associate with a predominant middle hepatic vein, the right hepatic vein could be absent. 應該還在
(D) A multiplanar reconstructed CT is helpful in demonstrating a right inferior hepatic vein.
??
**Ans: C**
RHV「缺如」需要的是**大的** RIHV(＋predominant MHV)接管引流；**小的** RIHV 時 RHV 應仍在，故 (C) 錯誤。
(A) Large RIHV draining segment 6 in 15-20% of normal：正確。
(B) When RIHV present, RHV usually smaller (does not drain seg 6)：正確。
(C) **Small RIHV + predominant MHV → RHV could be absent（正解＝錯誤）**：需大 RIHV 才可能 RHV 缺如；小 RIHV 時 RHV 應仍存在。
(D) MPR CT helpful in demonstrating RIHV：正確。
