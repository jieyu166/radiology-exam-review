#交換 #2016交換 #ABD About gastric target ("bull's eye") lesions, which of the following statement is correct?
(A) Most are highly suggestive of benign lesions such as ectopic pancreas.
(B) Multiple lesions are almost always benign.
(C) Most common cause of bull's-eye metastases is lymphoma.
(D) Gastric Kaposi sarcoma can also result in a "bull's-eye" appearance.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**。**Gastric Kaposi sarcoma 也可呈 bull's-eye 外觀**。多發病灶多為惡性（血行轉移，B 誤）；最常見 bull's-eye 轉移原發為 **melanoma**（非 lymphoma，C 誤）；多數非良性（A 誤）。
