#交換 #2016交換 #IR Which statement is incorrect about BRTO (Balloon-occluded retrograde transvenous obliteration)?
(A) Retrograde filling of sclerosing agent into varix under assistance of occlusion balloon.
(B) Sclerosing agent for BRTO of choice is ethanolamine oleate iopamidol.
(C) Hepatoencephalopathy will be more severe after BRTO. relief
(D) Complicated varix can be treated with combination of BRTO and transhepatic antegrade obliteration.
??
**Ans: C**
BRTO 關閉門體分流 → **改善**肝性腦病(甚至用於 TIPS 禁忌之 HE 者),故 (C) 說 HE 更嚴重為錯誤。
(A) 球囊阻塞下逆行注入硬化劑至曲張:正確。
(B) 首選硬化劑 ethanolamine oleate(混 iopamidol):正確。
(C) **Hepatoencephalopathy more severe after BRTO（正解＝錯誤）**:相反,BRTO 改善 HE;惡化的是食道靜脈曲張/腹水(門脈壓↑)。
(D) 複雜曲張可 BRTO + 經肝順行 obliteration 合併:正確。
