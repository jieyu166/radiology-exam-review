#交換 #2016交換 #ABD shows a 6 cm right adrenal mass, which shows heterogeneous but peripheral enhancement, necrosis, and some calcifications. There is an early invasion of the IVC. Left adrenal gland is atrophied. What is the most likely diagnosis?
(A) Adrenal cortical carcinoma
(B) Pheochromocytoma
(C) Adrenal neuroblastoma
(D) Adrenal adenoma
??
**Ans: A**
> [!todo] 待補詳解
> 正解 **A（Adrenal cortical carcinoma）**。6 cm 腎上腺腫塊、異質性周邊強化、壞死、鈣化、早期侵犯 → **ACC**（大、異質、壞死、~30% 鈣化、侵犯腎靜脈/IVC/肝）。
> - B pheo（顯著強化、T2 light-bulb）、C neuroblastoma（兒童、跨中線）、D adenoma（小、富脂、快 washout）較不符。
