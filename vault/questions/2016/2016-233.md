#交換 #2016交換 #CV What is NOT the clue to differentiate chronic myocardial infarct from acute myocardial infarction?
(A) Linear myocardial calcification or subendocardial fatty metamorphosis on CT.
(B) Findings follow coronary vascular distribution.
(C) Myocardial thinning with akinesis or dyskinesis.
(D) Delayed hyperenhancement without increased T2 signal.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**。**急、慢性梗塞皆循冠狀動脈分布**,故「follows coronary vascular distribution」非鑑別線索。線狀鈣化/脂肪化生(A)、變薄+akinesis(C)、LGE 無 T2 升高(D)皆為慢性線索。
