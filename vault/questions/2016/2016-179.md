#交換 #2016交換 #NR Which of the following statements is not characterizing Lemierre Syndrome?
(A) Also known as suppurative thrombophlebitis of the internal jugular vein.
(B) Typically necrobacillosis, an anaerobic Gram-positive cocci, Fusobacterium necrophorum, is responsible for a majority of cases. Gram-negative cocci
(C) Infection spreads from the peritonsillar space into the internal jugular vein, causing thrombus formation.
(D) The rates of morbidity and mortality are higher than with other head and neck infections.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**（非特徵）。Lemierre＝IJV 化膿性血栓性靜脈炎、Fusobacterium necrophorum(Gram-陰性 bacillus)、源自口咽感染。
- **C（正解）**：「感染由 peritonsillar space 直接入 IJV」非準確描述——實為口咽感染經**側咽/頸動脈間隙**入 IJV。
（註：該題另一選項稱 Fusobacterium 為「Gram-positive cocci」亦錯,實為 Gram-陰性 bacillus。）
