#交換 #2016交換 #US Regarding testicular torsion, which one is **TRUE**?
(A) It is ruled in when there is asymmetric flow on color Doppler between the testes.
(B) A high resistance arterial waveform in the spermatic artery may be the only imaging finding in incomplete torsion.
(C) Doppler has equal sensitivity in the pediatric and adult populations.
(D) The bell clapper deformity does not predispose to testicular torsion.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**。不完全扭轉時靜脈先受阻，**精索動脈呈高阻力波形（RI >0.75、舒張期血流喪失/逆轉），可為唯一影像所見**。bell clapper deformity 會增加風險（D 錯）、兩側不對稱血流非確診（A 錯）、Doppler 兒童敏感度低於成人（C 錯）。
