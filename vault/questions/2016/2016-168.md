#交換 #2016交換 #MSK Which ONE of the following findings is not usually identified in the cervical spine radiography of rheumatoid patients?
(A) Caudal migration of C2
(B) Fusion of apophyseal joints
(C) Atlantoaxial subluxation
(D) Erosion of odontoid
??
**Ans: A**
> [!todo] 待補詳解
> 正解 **A**（非常見）。B apophyseal 融合、C 寰樞前脫位、D 齒突侵蝕皆常見。
> - **A（正解，非常見）**：「C2 向尾側(caudal)移位」非——RA 為 **C2『頭側(cranial)』移位（cranial settling/basilar invagination）**。
