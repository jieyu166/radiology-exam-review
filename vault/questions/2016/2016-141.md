#交換 #2016交換 #CH Calcified pulmonary metastatic nodules occurs commonly in the following except?
(A) Osteogenic sarcoma
(B) Mucinous adenocarcinoma of colon
(C) Thyroid carcinoma
(D) Hepatocellular carcinoma
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D（除外 HCC）**。會鈣化的肺轉移：A osteosarcoma（成骨）、B 大腸黏液腺癌、C 甲狀腺癌皆會鈣化；**D 肝細胞癌（HCC）不屬常見鈣化肺轉移**。
