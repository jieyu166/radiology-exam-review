#交換 #2016交換 #CV How is endocardial cushion defect (ECD) confirmed on CT or MRI?
(A) A large VSD (ventricular septal defect).
(B) ASD located anteriorly.
(C) Parallel arranged bilateral atrioventricular valves.
(D) Short left ventricular outflow tract.
(E) Long right ventricular outflow tract.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**。**雙側房室瓣平行排列、位於同一水平**（正常 offset 消失）為 ECD 診斷關鍵。LVOT 實為拉長(goose-neck)非縮短。
