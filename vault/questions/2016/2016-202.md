#交換 #2016交換 #CH Which of the following statements best describes **air trapping**?
(A) Lobules of air trapping are a common finding in healthy patients.
(B) The presence of any degree of air trapping signifies clinically significant disease.
(C) Air trapping is less common in smokers.
(D) Air trapping tends to decrease with age.
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**。
- **A（正解）**：**小葉狀 air trapping 在健康人常見**（limited extent ~50% 胸部 CT、隨年齡增加、吸菸者更多）。
- B 錯：輕度（<25% 肺實質）可無臨床意義。
- C 錯：吸菸者**更常見**（非更少）。
- D 錯：**隨年齡增加**（非減少）。
