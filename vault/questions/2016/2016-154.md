#交換 #2016交換 #Breast 有關 Phyllodes tumor of breast 的敘述，下列何者為**非**?
(A) Phyllodes tumor generally presents as larger mass. The median size of the tumor is 4~5 cm.
(B) Local recurrence following surgical excision is very rare.
(C) On mammography, phyllodes tumor is typically revealed as well-circumscribed, lobulated mass.
(D) General sonographic features are non specific and can mimic that of a fibroadenoma
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**（為非）。A 典型大而快速生長、C well-circumscribed、D 似 fibroadenoma 皆對。
- **B（正解，為非）**：「局部復發很罕見」錯——phyllodes **未廣泛切除易復發**,5-25% 惡性變。
