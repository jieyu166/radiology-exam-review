#交換 #2016交換 #ABD Which is incorrect about focal nodular hyperplasia (FNH)?
(A) FNH is 2nd most common benign tumor of liver after hemangioma.
(B) NECT shows iso/slight hyperattenuating homogenous mass
(C) Age peak at 5th-6th decade young to middle-aged adults, with a strong female predilection
(D) MR image shows central scar which is more often detected than on US/CT.
??
**Ans: C**
> [!todo] 待補詳解
> 正解 **C**（敘述錯誤）。
> - A 對：FNH 為肝臟**第二常見良性腫瘤**（次於 hemangioma）。
> - B 對：NECT 呈**等/略高密度均質腫塊**。
> - **C 錯（正解）**：FNH 年齡高峰在**年輕至中年（約 20–50 歲、3rd–5th decade）女性**，非「5th–6th decade」。
> - D 對：MR 比 US/CT **更易顯示中央疤痕**。
