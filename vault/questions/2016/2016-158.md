#交換 #2016交換 #ABD The CT severity index (CTSI) for acute pancreatitis is based on the Balthazar score, and is probably the most used CT score. Which of the following is **not** included in the parameters of the scoring systems?
(A) Peri-pancreatic stranding
(B) The percentage of pancreatic necrosis
(C) Effacement of the pancreatic lobular contours
(D) Pleural effusion
??
**Ans: D**
> [!todo] 待補詳解
> The original CT severity index has a number of limitations:
> - complications like organ failure, do not correlate well with the score given by original CTSI
> - interobserver variability with original CTSI can result in different scores for the same patient
> - patients with >30% necrosis have similar morbidity and mortality
