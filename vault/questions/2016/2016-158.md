#交換 #2016交換 #ABD The CT severity index (CTSI) for acute pancreatitis is based on the Balthazar score, and is probably the most used CT score. Which of the following is **not** included in the parameters of the scoring systems?
(A) Peri-pancreatic stranding
(B) The percentage of pancreatic necrosis
(C) Effacement of the pancreatic lobular contours
(D) Pleural effusion
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D（pleural effusion）**。原始 CTSI＝**Balthazar 分級(A-E)＋壞死%**；peripancreatic stranding(A)、necrosis%(B)、胰腫大/輪廓(C) 皆屬之。
- **D（正解）**：**pleural effusion 不在原始 CTSI**（屬 modified CTSI 2004 的 extrapancreatic complications）。
