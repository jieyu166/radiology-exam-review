#交換 #2016交換 #US About ultrasonogrpahy artifacts, which description is **incorrect**?
(A) Solid masses that attenuate sound less than adjacent soft tissues may cause **posterior enhancement**.
(B) Bone and the back wall of the carotid can act as **mirrors** for color Doppler imaging due to marked contrast between soft tissue and vascular structure.
(C) The **ring down** artifact occurs posterior to air-bubbles, because ultrasound cause resonate vibration of the fluid trapped between bubbles.
(D) The most widely recognized **refraction** artifact occurs at the junction of the rectus abdominis muscle and abdominal wall fat.
(E) None of above
??
**Ans: E**
> [!todo] 待補詳解
> 正解 **E（none of above）**。A 後方增強、B mirror（骨/頸動脈後壁）、C ring-down（氣泡共振）、D refraction（腹直肌交界）**描述皆正確** → 無錯誤者,答 E。
