#交換 #2016交換 #PED Concerning congenital ureteropelvic junction (UPJ) obstruction, which one of the following is **TRUE**?
(A) It is an uncommon cause of hydronephrosis in children.
(B) Urinary tract infection is the most common presentation.
(C) Females and males are affected equally.
(D) The presence of crossing vessels decreases the success rate of pyeloplasty.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**（為真）。
- **D（正解，真）**：**crossing vessel 存在會降低 pyeloplasty/endopyelotomy 的成功率**。
- A 錯（UPJO 是兒童 hydronephrosis 最常見原因）、B 錯（現多以產前 hydronephrosis 表現,非 UTI）、C 錯（男>女,非相等）。
