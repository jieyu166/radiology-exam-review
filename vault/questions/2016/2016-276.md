#交換 #2016交換 #Breast Which is the "**wrong**" statement of normal breast anatomy?
(A) The fibrous strands that extend from the deep dermis into the underlying breast tissue is called suspensory ligaments of Cooper.
(B) The breast consists of 5 to 10 lobes, each emptying into a separate major duct terminating in the nipple.
(C) The lobule is the functional unit of the breast. Historically, it is composed of terminal ducts and acini.
(D) The tissue composition is important because is correlates with the ability of mammography to detect lesions in the breast.
??
**Ans: B**
**乳房由15-25個腺葉(lobes)組成**（非5-10個），各自匯入獨立的主乳管終於乳頭，故(B)為非。
(A) 對：Cooper氏韌帶，深真皮延伸入乳房組織之纖維束。
(B) **錯（正解）**：官方訂正為15-25個腺葉（非5-10個）。
(C) 對：小葉為乳房功能單位，由終末導管與腺泡組成。
(D) 對：組織組成影響乳房攝影偵測病灶之能力。
