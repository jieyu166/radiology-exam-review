#交換 #2016交換 #US Which ultrasound feature is **not** suspicious for thyroid malignancy?
(A) Markedly hypoechoic
(B) Taller than wide in transverse plane
(C) Enlarged thyroid with multiple nodules
(D) Microcalcifications
??
**Ans: C**
> [!todo] 待補詳解
> 正解 **C**（非可疑）。A markedly hypoechoic、B taller-than-wide、D microcalcifications 皆為惡性可疑特徵。
> - **C（正解，非可疑）**：**甲狀腺腫大合併多發結節（多結節甲狀腺腫）本身非惡性可疑特徵**。
