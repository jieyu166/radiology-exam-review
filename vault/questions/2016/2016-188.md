#交換 #2016交換 #PED Which of the following statements is **NOT** included in the triad of prune belly syndrome?
(A) Widely separated abdominal rectus muscles
(B) Spinal bifida
(C) Hydroureteronephrosis
(D) Cryptorchidism
??
**Ans: B**
