#交換 #2016交換 #CV Which feature can be observed in the first stage of mitral stenosis (pulmonary venous hypertension)?
(A) Loss of hilar angle, redistribution (stage I).
(B) Interstitial edema (stage II).
(C) Alveolar edema (stage III).
(D) Hemosiderin deposit, ossification (stage IV).
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**。**Stage I＝血流重分布(cephalization)、loss of hilar angle**。interstitial edema 屬 II、alveolar edema 屬 III、hemosiderin/ossification 屬 IV。
