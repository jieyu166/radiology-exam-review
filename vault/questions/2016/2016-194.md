#交換 #2016交換 #2017交換 #CH Regarding lung cancer, which of the following statement is **NOT** correct?
(A) In small cell lung cancer, when tumor and metastatic lesion cannot be encompassed in the same radiation port, it is called extensive disease.
(B) Adenocarcinoma is the most common cell type of lung cancer in women and nonsmokers.
(C) Atypical adenomatous hyperplasia, adenocarcinoma in situ, and minimally invasive adenocarcinoma are staged in the 8th edition of TNM system.
(D) Pleural thickening adjacent to the tumor seen in CT is not very accurate for chest wall invasion.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**（不正確）。A SCLC extensive stage、B adeno 為女性/非吸菸者最常見、D 腫瘤旁肋膜增厚對胸壁侵犯準確度不高 皆對。
- **C（正解，不正確）**：「AAH、AIS、MIA 被歸於『同一』分期」不正確——三者分期不同（AAH preinvasive、**AIS=Tis、MIA=T1mi**）。
