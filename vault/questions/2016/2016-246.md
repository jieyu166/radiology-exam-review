#交換 #2016交換 #IR Which one is the **wrong** description about endoleak of aortic graft?
(A) Type I endoleak indicates a persistent perigraft channel of blood flow caused by attachment site.
(B) Type II endoleak is attributed to retrograde flow from IMA, lumbar arteries, or other branches.
(C) Type III endoleak should be treated immediately.
(D) Endotension is not an endoleak (Type V).
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**（描述錯誤）。**Endotension 即歸類為 Type V endoleak**（無可見漏液但瘤囊持續擴大），故「不是 endoleak」與「Type V」自相矛盾。Type I＝附著處持續血流(A)、Type II＝IMA/腰動脈逆流(B)、Type III＝須立即處理(C)皆正確。
