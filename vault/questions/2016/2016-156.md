#交換 #2016交換 #ABD Which of the following is NOT a typical ultrasound finding of adenomyomatosis of gallbladder?
(A) Diffuse gallbladder wall thickening
(B) Focal gallbladder wall thickening
(C) Comet-tail artifacts
(D) All of the above are typical image findings
??
**Ans: D**
> [!todo] 待補詳解
> 正解 **D**。A 瀰漫壁增厚、B 局部壁增厚、C comet-tail（RAS 內結晶）**皆為典型**超音波所見 → 故「何者『非』典型」答 **D（以上皆典型）**。
