#交換 #2016交換 #CV #記 Which statement about the May-Thurner syndrome is **right**?
(A) Physical entrapment with compression of left common iliac artery by right common iliac vein
(B) Physical entrapment with compression of left common iliac vein by right common iliac artery
(C) Physical entrapment with compression of right common iliac vein by left common iliac vein
(D) Physical entrapment with compression of right common iliac artery by left common iliac vein
??
**Ans: B**
May-Thurner＝**右髂總動脈壓迫左髂總靜脈**,故 (B) 正確。
(A) 右動脈壓左『動脈』:錯。
(B) **右髂總動脈壓迫左髂總靜脈(正解)**。
(C) 左靜脈壓右靜脈:錯。
(D) 左靜脈壓右動脈:錯。
