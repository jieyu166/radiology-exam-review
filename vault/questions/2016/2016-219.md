#交換 #2016交換 #ABD What is the "**wrong**" statement about pyelonephritis?
(A) It presents wedge-shaped, striated area of decreased enhancement in enhanced CT scan.
(B) It presents cortical rim sign in delayed phase of enhanced CT scan.
(C) E. coli is the most common pathogen found in urine culture.
(D) The complications include perirenal abscess, necrotizing papillitis, and pyonephrosis.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**（錯誤敘述）。**Cortical rim sign 屬腎梗塞**（包膜側支保留薄層皮質強化、數天後最明顯），非腎盂腎炎。腎盂腎炎為楔形 striated reduced enhancement（A 對）、E. coli 最常見（C 對）、併發症含腎周膿瘍/壞死性乳頭炎/膿腎（D 對）。
