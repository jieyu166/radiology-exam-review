#交換 #2016交換 #CV Concerning deep venous thrombosis (DVT), which one is **TRUE**
(A) 60% of patients with lower extremity DVT have thrombus in the iliofemoral region Thrombosis of an iliofemoral vein accounts for ~25% of all lower extremity DVTs
(B) Venous compression is performed in longitudinal orientation of the vein.
(C) The diameter of the affected vein is decreased in acute deep venous thrombosis. increase
(D) Inability to obliterate the vein lumen completely is the diagnostic criterion.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**（敘述正確者）。
- A 錯：髂股段 DVT 約佔下肢 DVT **~25%**（非 60%）。
- B 錯：加壓應在**橫切面（transverse）**進行，非縱切。
- C 錯：急性 DVT 受累靜脈管徑**增大（擴張）**，非縮小。
- **D 對（正解）**：**無法以加壓完全閉合靜脈管腔＝診斷標準**徵象。
→ 概念詳解見 [[dvt-ultrasound]]。
