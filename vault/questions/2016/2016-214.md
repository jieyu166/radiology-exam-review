#交換 #2016交換 #MSK A 14 y/o male gets an eccentric geographic osteolytic lesion with a very dense sclerotic border, endosteal scalloping, and sharp zone of transition in his distal metaphysis of the tibia. Which is the most likely impression?
(A) Nonossifying fibroma.
(B) Giant cell tumor.
(C) Enchondroma.
(D) Fibrous dysplasia.
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**。**Nonossifying fibroma（NOF）**＝青少年、長骨幹骺端偏心、地圖狀透亮、薄/緻密硬化緣、清楚移行帶。GCT（骨閉合後鄰關節）、enchondroma（手足、軟骨鈣化）、fibrous dysplasia（毛玻璃）皆不符。
