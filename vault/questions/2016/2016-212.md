#交換 #2016交換 #MSK Which ONE of the following locations is NOT the common site of brown tumors developed in patients with hyperparathyroidism?
(A) Jaw.
(B) Shoulder.
(C) Pelvis.
(D) Femur.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**（非常見部位）。棕色瘤好發**下頷/顎（jaw）、骨盆、肋骨、長骨（股骨）**。
- **B（正解）**：**肩部非棕色瘤常見部位**。
