#交換 #2016交換 #ABD Which of the following imaging findings is highly suggestive of maglignant portal vein thrombosis?
(A) Arterialization of the thrombus is found in nearly all cases of infiltrative HCC.
(B) The thrombus expands the portal vein and has an enhancement pattern similar to adjacent tumor.
(C) The thrombus has low signal intensity on T2-weighted MR images.
(D) The thrombus involves both the intra- and extrahepatic portal veins.
??
**Ans: B**
高度提示**惡性（腫瘤）**門脈瘤栓的徵象是：**瘤栓擴張門脈管腔、且強化型態與鄰近腫瘤相同**（腫瘤樣 arterialization）。
(A) Arterialization in nearly all cases：過度概括，並非「幾乎所有」病例皆有。
(B) **Thrombus expands the portal vein and enhances like adjacent tumor（正解）**：擴張＋腫瘤樣強化＝惡性瘤栓標誌。
(C) Low signal on T2：相反，腫瘤瘤栓多為 T2 高訊號、DWI 受限。
(D) Involves both intra- and extrahepatic portal veins：僅描述範圍，非惡性的特異徵象。
