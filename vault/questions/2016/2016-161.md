#交換 #2016交換 #ABD _E. coli_ is the most common pathogen behind many urological conditions. All of the following conditions are most commonly due to _E. coli_ infections **excepts**?
(A) Xanthogranulomatous pyelonephritis
(B) Emphysematous pyelonephritis
(C) Pyeloureteritis cystica
(D) Malakoplakia
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A（XGP）**。問「皆 E. coli 除外」。
- **A（正解）**：**XGP 經典為 Proteus mirabilis**（產 urease→struvite 結石）。
- B emphysematous pyelonephritis、C pyeloureteritis cystica、D malakoplakia 多為 E. coli。
