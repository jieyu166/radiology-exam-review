_E. coli_ is the most common pathogen behind many urological conditions. All of the following conditions are most commonly due to _E. coli_ infections **excepts**?
(A) Xanthogranulomatous pyelonephritis
(B) Emphysematous pyelonephritis
(C) Pyeloureteritis cystica
(D) Malakoplakia
??
**Ans: A**
最常見的致病菌是Proteus和 E. coli. Proteus可能略多，考這可能太細。
B.  E coli, Klebsiella pneumonia, and Proteus mirabilis 最常見(Radiopaedia)
C. 最常見是E. coli 其他還有TB, Enterococcus, Proteus spp., Schistosomiasis等等(Radiopaedia)
D. 在Urotract常見致病菌是E. coli or Proteus (Radiopaedia)
