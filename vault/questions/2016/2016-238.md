#交換 #2016交換 #H&N Subperiosteal abscess of the orbit most commonly involves which compartment of the orbit?
(A) Inferior extraconal space.
(B) Medial extraconal space.
(C) Preseptal space.
(D) Intraconal space.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**。眼眶骨膜下膿瘍**最常見於 medial extraconal space**（沿 lamina papyracea），多繼發於 ethmoid sinusitis。
