#交換 #2016交換 #Physics In mammography, changing the anode/filter from Mo/Mo to Rh/Rh affects mean glandular dose and image contrast how?
(A) Mean glandular dose decreases, image contrast increases.
(B) Both mean glandular dose and image contrast decrease.
(C) Mean glandular dose decreases, image contrast unchanged.
(D) Mean glandular dose unchanged, image contrast increases.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**。Rh/Rh 較具穿透性（用於較厚/緻密乳房），**平均乳腺劑量最低、影像對比也較 Mo/Mo 低 → 兩者皆降低**。
