#交換 #2016交換 #ABD Which of the following statements about splenic trauma is **incorrect**?
(A) Perisplenic clotted blood may be the only sign of splenic injury
(B) A diffusely low-attenuation spleen may be due to prior shock
(C) Delayed rupture may not occurred even if CT is initially normal
(D) CT grading systems are of limited valve in predicting the clinical outcome of splenic injuries 
??
**Ans: C**
> [!note] 詳解
> 正解 **C**（敘述錯誤者）。
> - A 對：perisplenic（sentinel）clot 可為脾損傷的**唯一徵象**。
> - B 對：瀰漫低密度脾可因**先前休克（低灌流）**。
> - **C 為非（正解）**：**延遲破裂即使初始 CT 正常仍可能發生**（題述「may not occur」錯誤）。
> - D 對：**CT 分級對臨床結果的預測價值有限**。
>
