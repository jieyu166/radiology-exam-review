#交換 #2016交換 #Breast Which is **wrong** about the following descriptions of Digital Breast Tomosynthesis (DBT)?
(A) In screening population, DBT have significantly reduced callback rate than 2D mammography.
(B) Better conspicuity of microcalcifications in DBT, in both morphology and distribution.
(C) Better conspicuity of lesions in DBT, especially the architectural distortion and lesion margins.
(D) Problem solve with DBT images may alleviate the need for additional diagnostic images.
??
