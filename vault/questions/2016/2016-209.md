#交換 #2016交換 #MSK A spiral proximal fibular fracture associated with an ankle joint injury is known as a Maisonneuve fracture, which one of the following is **TRUE**?
(A) Fracture of the sustentaculum tali.
(B) Dislocation of the subtalar joint.
(C) Tear of the spring ligament.
(D) Tear of the tibiofibular syndesmosis.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**。Maisonneuve fracture＝近端腓骨螺旋骨折＋踝損傷，合併**脛腓骨聯合（tibiofibular syndesmosis）撕裂**＋內踝/三角韌帶損傷（旋前-外旋機轉、不穩定）。
