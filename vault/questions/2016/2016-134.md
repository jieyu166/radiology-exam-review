#交換 #2016交換 #US Which of the following statement about US elastography is incorrect?
(A) US elastography quantify the tissue stiffness, which was previously judged only subjectively by physical examination.
(B) Stain elastography determine the relative strain or elasticity of tissue within an FOV.
(C) Applying precompression can largely affect the result on both strain and shear wave elastography.
(D) Strain elastography using ARFI as compressing force can serve as a quantification tool of tissue stiffness.
??
**Ans: D**
> [!todo] 待補詳解
> 正解 **D**（incorrect）。
> - A 對：elastography 量組織硬度（取代觸診）。
> - B 對：strain elastography 測相對應變/彈性。
> - C 對：precompression 會影響 strain 與 shear wave 結果。
> - **D（正解，錯）**：**ARFI 屬 shear wave（剪力波）技術、為定量**；strain 用（手動/生理）壓縮、為相對/半定量。「strain elastography 用 ARFI 當壓縮力做定量」混淆兩者。
