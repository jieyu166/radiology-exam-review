#交換 #2016交換 #ABD Which of the following statesments best describes the pathologic appearance of infiltrative HCC?
(A) Infiltrative HCC is characterized by the spread of minute tumor nodules throughout an entire lobe or the entire liver.
(B) Infiltrative HCC is rarely associated with microscopic vascular tumor thrombi.
(C) Many of the histologic features of infiltrative HCC are unique to this subtype.
(D) Infiltrative HCC is a large dominant hepatic mass with well-defined borders.
??
**Ans: A**
Infiltrative HCC 的病理特徵是**微小腫瘤結節瀰漫散布整葉或整肝、無主導結節**、邊界不清（cirrhotomimetic）。
(A) **Spread of minute tumor nodules throughout an entire lobe/liver（正解）**：正是浸潤型的定義。
(B) Rarely associated with microscopic vascular tumor thrombi：相反，常合併（微）血管瘤栓。
(C) Histologic features unique to this subtype：組織學特徵非此型獨有，差別在生長型態。
(D) Large dominant mass with well-defined borders：那是 nodular/massive 型，與浸潤型相反。
