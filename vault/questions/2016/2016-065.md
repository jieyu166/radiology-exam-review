#交換 #2016交換 #IR Which of the following description about hemodialysis is NOT **true**?
(A) Central venous hemodialysis catheters should be avoided due to highest rates of thrombosis and infection.
(B) Arteriovenous fistulas are preferred over graft.
(C) Infection rates, rates of steal syndrome, and rates of symptomatic central venous stenosis are lower in patients with grafts than in those with fistulas.
(D) The most common stenosis site of radiocephalic fistula is juxtaanastomotic segment.
??
**Ans: C**
AVF 的併發症(感染/steal/中央靜脈狹窄)『低於』graft,故 (C) 說 graft 低於 fistula 為非真。
(A) 中央靜脈導管血栓/感染率最高應避免：真。
(B) AVF 優於 graft：真(fistula first、預後較佳)。
(C) **Infection/steal/central stenosis lower in grafts than fistulas（正解＝非真）**：相反,fistula 較低。
(D) Radiocephalic fistula 最常見狹窄為 juxta-anastomotic 段：真。
