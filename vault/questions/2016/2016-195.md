#交換 #2016交換 #ABD Which of the following image finding of uterine cervical cancer on MRI does not affect cancer TNM staging?
(A) Tumor invasion to uterine body
(B) Tumor invasion to parametrium
(C) Hydronephrosis
(D) Pelvic lymphadenopathy
??
**Ans: A**
> [!todo] 待補詳解
> 正解 **A**（不影響分期）。B parametrium 侵犯(→IIB)、C hydronephrosis(→IIIB)、D 骨盆淋巴結 皆影響分期。
> - **A（正解，不影響）**：**腫瘤侵犯子宮體(uterine corpus)不改變 FIGO 分期**。
