#交換 #2016交換 #NR Three typical pattern of drainage of sphenoparietal sinus have been described except for Deep drainage
(A) into the cavernous sinus.
(B) into the pterygoid plexus via basal emissary veins.
(C) posteriorly along the floor of the middle cranial fossa, then passing over the petrous pyramid to drain into the inferior petrosal vein or transverse sinus.
(D) Directly go upward to the middle cerebral vein and than drain to the superior sagittal sinus.
??
**Ans: D**
蝶頂竇三種典型引流型為：①海綿竇 ②翼叢（basal emissary）③沿中顱窩底向後越岩錐入岩下/橫竇。故「向上至 MCV 再入上矢狀竇」非其引流型（MCV 為其屬支、方向相反）。
(A) Into the cavernous sinus：屬三型之一。
(B) Into the pterygoid plexus via basal emissary veins：屬三型之一。
(C) Posteriorly along middle cranial fossa floor over petrous to inferior petrosal/transverse sinus：屬三型之一。
(D) **Directly upward to MCV then superior sagittal sinus（正解＝EXCEPT）**：非其引流型，MCV 為其屬支、流向相反。
