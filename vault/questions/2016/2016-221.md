#交換 #2016交換 #ABD Which one of the following ureteral calculi has the highest spontaneous passage rate?
(A) Proximal ureter: 9 mm.
(B) Middle ureter: 7 mm.
(C) Distal ureter: 5 mm.
(D) Ureterovesical junction: 3 mm.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**。自發排出率主要取決於**大小**：0–3 mm 達 98%、4 mm 81%、5 mm 65%、6 mm 33%。故 **UVJ 的 3 mm 結石排出率最高**。
