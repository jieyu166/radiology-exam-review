#交換 #2016交換 #CV According to the appropriateness criteria, what is the stent size below which it is inappropriate to perform CT for assessment of stent patency?
(A) 2 mm
(B) 2.5 mm
(C) 2.75 mm
(D) 3 mm
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D（3 mm）**。依 appropriateness criteria，**支架直徑小於 3 mm 時以 CT 評估支架通暢『不適當』**（<3mm CT 對 in-stent restenosis 敏感度顯著下降，≤3mm ~54% vs >3mm ~86%）。與 2016-200 同一閾值（3 mm），僅選項表述不同。
