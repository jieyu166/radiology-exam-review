#交換 #2016交換 #Physics Which statement about a direct digital detector is correct? • (A) Direct exposure of a charge coupled device (CCD) chip by x-ray photons
(B) X-ray photons are directly converted into visible light, which is • read with a digital detector
(C) X-ray photons are directly read as they strike the detector while • the exposure is taking place
(D) X-ray photons are directly converted into an electric charge, • which is read out after the exposure
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**。直接（direct）數位偵測器＝**X 光光子直接轉換成電荷**（非晶硒 a-Se 光導體）後讀出。
- A 錯（CCD 屬間接）、B 錯（X 光轉可見光是『間接』）、C 不精確。
