#交換 #2016交換 #ABD Which of the following is NOT a typical image finding of polycystic ovary syndrome on MRI?
(A) Enlarged ovaries
(B) Multiple follicles at the central part of the ovary
(C) Usually bilateral
(D) All of the above are typical image findings
??
**Ans: B**
PCOS 的濾泡呈**周邊**排列（string of pearls），故 (B)「濾泡位於卵巢中央」**非**典型。
(A) Enlarged ovaries：典型。
(B) **Multiple follicles at the central part of the ovary（正解＝非典型）**：濾泡應在**周邊**（中央為高回音 stroma）。
(C) Usually bilateral：典型。
(D) All of the above are typical：因 (B) 非典型，故此項不成立。
