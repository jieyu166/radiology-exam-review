#交換 #2016交換 #CV Which statement is **wrong** about papillary fibroelastoma?
(A) mostly asymptomatic
(B) most common vavular tumor
(C) not related to embolic events related
(D) second most common benign cardiac neoplasm after myxoma
??
**Ans: C**
Papillary fibroelastoma **可引起栓塞**（腫瘤/血栓碎屑→中風/TIA），故 (C)「與栓塞無關」為錯誤敘述。
(A) Mostly asymptomatic：真（常偶然發現）。
(B) Most common valvular tumor：真（約占瓣膜腫瘤 75%）。
(C) **Not related to embolic events（正解＝錯誤）**：可使腫瘤/血栓碎屑栓塞致中風/TIA。
(D) Second most common benign cardiac neoplasm after myxoma：真。
