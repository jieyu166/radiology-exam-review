#交換 #2016交換 #Breast Which of the following is not **true** for sternalis muscle on mammography?
(A) Visible on MLO view only (also CC, mistake as mass)
(B) Small free slip of pectoralis muscle adjacent to sternum
(C) Present in 1% of women
(D) Can be round, triangular, flame-shaped
??
**Ans: A**
Sternalis 見於乳房攝影 **CC view 內側**(非僅 MLO),故 (A) 為非真。
(A) **僅見於 MLO view(正解＝非真)**:見於 CC view 內側、易誤為腫塊。
(B) 胸骨旁 pectoralis 內緣小肌:真。
(C) 約 1%:真。
(D) 圓/三角/火焰狀:真。
