#交換 #2016交換 #ABD Which of the following regarding bile duct hemartomas is **NOT TRUE**?
(A) also known as von Meyenburg complexes.
(B) present as multiple small hyperechoic and hypoechoic nodules, and multiple spotty echogenic foci with comet tail artifacts in ultrasound.
(C) due to developmental malformations composed of cystic dilated bile ducts filled with a collagenous stroma.
(D) cholesterol crystals are rarely found associated with the dilated ductules.
??
**Ans: D**
膽管錯構瘤管腔內**膽固醇結晶/濃稠物常見**(comet-tail 偽影來源),故 (D)「罕見」為非真。
(A) 別名 von Meyenburg complexes:真。
(B) US 多發高/低回音結節＋彗星尾:真。
(C) 囊狀擴張小膽管＋膠原基質之發育畸形:真。
(D)膽固醇結晶 **~~罕~~**與擴張小管相伴: 實為常見、即 comet-tail 來源。
