#交換 #2016交換 #Breast 以下有關 Digital Breast Tomosynthesis (DBT)在乳房診斷的應用，以下何者有誤?
(A) Architectural distortion is better visualized in DBT, which is important in diagnostic examination due to high PPV for cancer in approximately 60% of cases.
(B) In focal asymmetry, DBT can be used to confirm a finding as a true asymmetry, or reclassify it as a mass. DBT has higher sensitivity than spot compression views.
(C) DBT is often effective in localizing one-view suspicious abnormalities, may help us to target the lesion location.
(D) Due to thin slices provided, DBT is usually better in visualizing the individual morphology of microcalcifications.
??
**Ans: D**
**DBT薄切片雖提升微鈣化的「可見度」，但因鈣化分散於多層切面，並未提升對微鈣化個別形態（morphology）的評估能力**，微鈣化型態判讀仍需依賴傳統2D/放大攝影。
(A) 對：DBT對architectural distortion顯示較佳，診斷檢查中此徵象惡性率高。
(B) 對：DBT可確認focal asymmetry真偽，敏感度優於spot compression view。
(C) 對：DBT有助於定位單一視野可疑病灶。
(D) **錯（正解）**：薄切片並未使微鈣化個別形態評估更佳，反而因分散於多層而較不利成群判讀。
