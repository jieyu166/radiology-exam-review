#交換 #2016交換 #IR Concerning the hepatic falciform ligament artery, which one of the following is correct?
(A) Running within the falciform ligament.
(B) Arising from the middle or left hepatic artery.
(C) Anastomosis with branches of the internal thoracic artery.
(D) Non-target embolization may result in supraumbilical skin complications.
(E) All of the above are correct.
??
**Ans: E**
正解 **E**（以上皆正確）。HFLA 走行於鐮狀韌帶(A)、源自肝中/左動脈(B)、與內胸動脈分支吻合(C)、非目標栓塞致臍上皮膚紅疹/壞死(D)。
