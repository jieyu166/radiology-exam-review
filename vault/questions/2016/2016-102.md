#交換 #2016交換 #ABD Which is incorrect about MEN 1 Syndrome?
(A) MEN 1 Syndrome is also called WERMER SYNDROME
(B) If pancreatic islet cell tumor is seen, gastrinoma is the most common type.
(C) Parathyroid hyperplasia is rare in MEN 1 syndrome
(D) May be associated with thyroid tumor, thymoma, buccal mucosal tumor, and colonic polyposis
??
**Ans: C**
> [!todo] 待補詳解
> 正解 **C**（敘述錯誤）。
> - A 對：MEN 1 ＝ **Wermer 症候群**。
> - B 對：胰島細胞瘤以 **gastrinoma**（Zollinger-Ellison）最常見。
> - **C 錯（正解）**：**原發性副甲狀腺機能亢進/增生其實是 MEN1 最常見表現（~95%，多腺體增生）**，並非罕見。
> - D 對：可合併甲狀腺腫瘤、胸腺瘤、頰黏膜腫瘤、結腸息肉等。
