#交換 #2016交換 #NR About Vertebrobasilar Dolichoectasia (VBD), which statement was correct?
(A) Most patients with VBD are asymptomatic.
(B) Clinical manifestations can be divided into compressive symptoms and vascular events, including TIA, ischemic stroke, and intraparenchymal or subarachnoid hemorrhage.
(C) The most frequently described ischemic lesion is in the brainstem, accounting for 41% of cases, and is most often in the pons.
(D) All above statements were correct.
??
**Ans: D**
**VBD（椎基底動脈延長擴張症）大多數病人無症狀；臨床表現可分為壓迫性症狀與血管事件（TIA、缺血性中風、腦實質內或蜘蛛膜下出血）；最常見缺血病灶位於腦幹（約41%），又以橋腦最常見**。四個敘述皆正確。
(A)(B)(C) 皆正確描述VBD之流行病學、臨床表現分類與最常見缺血病灶部位。
(D) **正解**：以上皆正確。
