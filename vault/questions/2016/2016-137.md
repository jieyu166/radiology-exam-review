#交換 #2016交換 #MSK patella. Which following MRI findings is consistent with the clinical presentations?
(A) Bone edema involving medial facet of patella and medial femoral condyle
(B) Bone edema involving posterior patella and anterior aspect of the tibial plateau
(C) Bone edema involving medial facet of patella and lateral femoral condyle
(D) Bone edema involving lateral facet of patella and lateral femoral condyle
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**。暫時性外側髕骨脫位的 kissing contusion＝**髕骨內側面（medial facet）＋ 外側股骨髁（lateral femoral condyle）** 骨髓水腫，伴 MPFL/內側支持帶撕裂、關節積液。
