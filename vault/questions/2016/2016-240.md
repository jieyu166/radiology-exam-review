#交換 #2016交換 #NR Which is **wrong** about pituicytoma?
(A) Rare primary tumor of the adult adenohypophysis.
(B) WHO grade I CNS tumor.
(C) Sellar or suprasellar tumor.
(D) Immunoreactive for vimentin, S-100 protein, EMA, and GFAP.
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**（錯誤）。**Pituicytoma 源自神經垂體(neurohypophysis)/漏斗部的 pituicytes**，非腺垂體(adenohypophysis)。WHO grade I(B)、鞍區/鞍上(C)、vimentin/S-100/EMA/GFAP 陽性(D)皆正確。
