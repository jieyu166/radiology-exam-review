#交換 #2016交換 #ABD What is the “**wrong**” statement about retroperitoneal fibrosis?
(A) Mantle of soft tissue that encases aorta, inferior vena cava and ureters.
(B) IVP study shows triad of upper ureteraal hydronephrosis, lateral deviation of ureters in middle 1/3, and gradual tapering of ureters. Medial deviation (C). Fibrous plaque might extend from crus of diaphragm to common iliac vessels.
(D) Contrast enhancement varies depending on the different stage of fibrous tissue in CT study.
??
**Ans: B**
後腹膜纖維化使輸尿管**內側(medial)偏移**(非外側),故 (B) 錯。
(A) 包繞主動脈/IVC/輸尿管:正確。
(B) **輸尿管中段外側(lateral)偏移(正解＝錯)**:應為內側(medial)偏移。
(C) 斑塊由橫膈腳延伸至 common iliac:正確。
(D) CT 強化隨纖維分期變化:正確。
