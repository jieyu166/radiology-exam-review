#交換 #2016交換 #NR The advantages of CT venography (CTV) includes the followings:
(A) Better spatial resolution compared with MRV.
(B) No flow-related artifacts.
(C) CTV better depicts venous anatomy than time-of-flight MRV.
(D) All of the above
??
**Ans: D**
CTV 相對 MRV 的三項優勢同時成立，故選「以上皆是」。
(A) Better spatial resolution compared with MRV：正確。
(B) No flow-related artifacts：正確（TOF-MRV 才有 flow-related 假影）。
(C) CTV better depicts venous anatomy than time-of-flight MRV：正確。
(D) **All of the above（正解）**：三項皆為 CTV 優勢。
