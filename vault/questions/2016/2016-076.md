#交換 #2016交換 #ABD Regarding intrascrotal calculus, which of the following is **FALSE**?
(A) also known as “fibrinoid loose body” or “scrotal pearl.”
(B) thought to represent freely mobile calcified bodies lying between the layers of the tunica albuginea of the testes. tunica vaginalis extra testicular macro-calcifications within the scrotum
(C) etiology is unclear. They may develop as a sequela to hematomas or inflammatory changes within the scrotum or loose bodies from torsion and infarction of the appendix testis or epididymis.
(D) The associated hydrocele is an exudate that accumulates between the membranes of the tunica.
??
**Ans: B**
Scrotal pearl 位於 **tunica vaginalis** 兩層之間(睪丸外),故 (B)「tunica albuginea」為假。
(A) 別名 fibrinoid loose body/scrotal pearl:真。
(B) **位於 tunica albuginea 兩層之間(正解＝假)**:應為 tunica vaginalis、extratesticular、可游離。
(C) 病因不明(血腫/發炎/appendix 扭轉脫落):真。
(D) 伴隨 hydrocele(膜間滲出液):真。
