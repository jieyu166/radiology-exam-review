#交換 #2016交換 #MSK Which of the following is **NOT** a radiographic feature of ankylosing spondylitis? 
(A) Male predominance.
(B) Syndesmophytes.
(C) Arthritis mutilans. Severe arthritis with deformity
(D) Enthesopathy.
??
**Ans: C**
- **C（正解，非 AS 特徵）**：**Arthritis mutilans** 是**乾癬性關節炎**（及類風濕）的嚴重破壞性表現，非 AS。
- A/B/D 皆為 AS 特徵：男性為主、（細直）syndesmophytes（→bamboo spine）、enthesopathy。
