#交換 #2016交換 #MSK Concerning osteopetrosis, which one of the following is **TRUE**?
(A) Osteoblastic activity results in excessive bone production.
(B) The radiographic hallmark is diffuse cortical thickening.
(C) Alternating bands of sclerosis indicate the fluctuating course of the disease.
(D) In the adult, bone is more resistant to fracture and undergoes bone marrow suppression.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**。
- **C（正解）**：交替的硬化帶反映疾病**活性起伏（fluctuating course）**。
- A 錯：機轉是**破骨細胞缺陷**（非成骨過度）。
- D 錯：骨雖硬化但**脆弱易骨折**（非更耐折）。
