#交換 #2016交換 #MSK A 40-year-old female with a history of low back pain. What MRI feature is useful in differentiating discitis from Modic type 1 endplate change?
(A) Reduction in disc height.
(B) High signal change in the endplate on T2WI.
(C) High signal change within the disc on T2WI.
(D) Mild irregularity of the endplates.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**。**椎間盤內 T2 高訊號（含椎間盤強化、核間裂消失、終板破壞）為感染特徵**;Modic type 1 僅終板/骨髓變化、椎間盤不呈高訊號。椎間盤高度降低（A）、終板 T2 高訊號（B）、終板輕度不規則（D）兩者皆可見、鑑別力低。
