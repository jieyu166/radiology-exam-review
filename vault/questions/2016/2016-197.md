#交換 #2016交換 #Physics 關於 Proton density-weighted images，下列何項敘述為**非**？
(A) PD images have a signal-to-noise ratio (SNR) that is higher than any comparable T1-weighted or T2W image.
(B) In some musculoskeletal applications, differences in PD within tissue elements is inherently high so that PDW imaging provides both excellent contrast and superior SNR.
(C) Short TR and long TE produces proton density-weighted images.
(D) In detection of multiple sclerosis plaques in posterior fossa, lesions could be more conspicuous on PD than on FLAIR due to better SNR.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**（為非）。A PD 的 SNR 高、B MSK 質子密度差敏感、D 後顱窩 MS 較 T2 明顯 皆對。
- **C（正解，為非）**：「**短 TR + 長 TE 產生 PD**」非——PD 為 **長 TR + 短 TE**（參數相反）。
