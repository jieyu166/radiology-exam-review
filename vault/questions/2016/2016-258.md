#交換 #2016交換 #Physics When using a C-arm for vascular interventional procedures, which method may reduce staff radiation dose?
(A) When the C-arm is inverted, staff should stand on the X-ray tube side, not the image receptor side.
(B) Maximize the source-to-detector distance (air gap).
(C) Reduce the frame rate of fluoroscopy.
(D) Use a larger field of view.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**。**降低透視 frame rate（脈衝透視）直接減少劑量**。C 臂倒置時應站影像接收器側（非 X 光管側，A 誤）、勿拉長 air gap(B 誤)、勿用過大視野(D 誤)。
