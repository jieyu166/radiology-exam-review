#交換 #2016交換 #CH About the characteristics of extralobar pulmonary sequestration, which of the following statement is **wrong** one ?
(A) 90% are left-sided.
(B) Males are more common.
(C) The arterial supply is usually systemic.
(D) The venous drainage usually flows into pulmonary vein.
??
**Ans: D**
> [!todo] 待補詳解
> 正解 **D**（為錯）。extralobar：A 左下葉~90%、B 男性多、C 體循環動脈供血皆對。
> - **D（正解，為錯）**：「靜脈引流入肺靜脈」錯——extralobar 經典**引流入體循環靜脈（azygos/IVC 等）**；intralobar 才入肺靜脈。
