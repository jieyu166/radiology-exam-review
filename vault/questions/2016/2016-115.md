#交換 #2016交換 #Breast Which one of the following descriptions about benign cystic lesions of the breast is not **true**?
(A) Epidermal inclusion cysts usually arise from obstruction of the infundibular portion of the hair follicle
(B) The mammographic finding of a circumscribed mass with a fat-fluid level on CC view is diagnostic for galactocele ML/MLO
(C) Duct ectasia is a nonspecific dilation of the major subareolar ducts, with occasional involvement of smaller ducts
(D) There is no increased risk for the subsequent development of breast cancer in women with nonproliferative fibrocystic breast changes.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**（非真）。
- A 對：表皮包涵囊腫源自毛囊**漏斗部**阻塞。
- **B（正解，非真）**：galactocele 的 **fat-fluid level 需水平射束的側位（ML/MLO）才顯現**，非 **CC view**。
- C 對：乳管擴張為主乳管非特異擴張。
- D 對：非增生性纖維囊性變化不增乳癌風險。
