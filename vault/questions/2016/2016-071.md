#交換 #2016交換 #IR risks of transarterial chemoembolization for hepatocellular carcinomas?
(A) Main portal vein occlusion is contraindicated. 選項
(B) Serum bilirubin level greater than 3.0mg/dL is contraindicated.
(C) The presence of a transjugular intrahepatic portosystemic shunt is associated with low risk of liver failure after chemoembolization.
(D) Bile duct dilatation or history of endoscopic papillotomy or bilioenteric anastomosis will increase the risk of liver abscess formation Development of Conventional Transarterial Chemoembolization for
??
**Ans: C**
TIPS 存在會使 TACE 後**肝衰竭風險升高**(門脈血流被分流),故 (C)「TIPS 與低風險相關」錯誤。
(A) 主門脈阻塞為(相對)禁忌:正確。
(B) 膽紅素>3.0 為禁忌:正確。
(C) **TIPS 與低肝衰竭風險相關(正解＝錯誤)**:TIPS 反而增加肝衰竭風險。
(D) 膽道擴張/乳頭切開/膽腸吻合增加肝膿瘍風險:正確。
