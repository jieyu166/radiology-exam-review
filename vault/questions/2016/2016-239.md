#交換 #2016交換 #NR Which of the following CANNOT cause multiple hypodense parenchymal lesions seen on NECT scans?
(A) Low-grade diffuse astrocytoma.
(B) Demyelinating disease.
(C) Multiple metastases.
(D) Multiple embolic infarcts.
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**。**Low-grade diffuse astrocytoma 為單一浸潤性腫瘤**，非多發離散低密度灶。demyelinating disease、multiple metastases、multiple embolic infarcts 皆可造成多發低密度灶。
