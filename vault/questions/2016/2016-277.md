#交換 #2016交換 #Breast Which one is the "**wrong**" statement of the invasive lobular carcinoma?
(A) Invasive lobular carcinoma is the most common type of breast malignancy.
(B) Invasive lobular carcinoma most commonly presents as a spiculated or ill-defined mass on mammography, ultrasonography, and MRI.
(C) There are mainly four variants of invasive lobular carcinoma: classic, signet ring, alveolar and pelomorphic.
(D) The classic type invasive lobular carcinoma shows two types of histologic patterns; the linear and targetoid pattern.
??
**Ans: A**
**ILC僅佔所有乳癌約5-10%**，最常見乳癌型態為invasive ductal carcinoma（IDC），非ILC，故(A)為非。
(A) **錯（正解）**：ILC非最常見乳癌型態（IDC才是），ILC僅佔5-10%。
(B) 對：最常見表現為spiculated或ill-defined mass。
(C) 對：classic、signet ring、alveolar、pleomorphic為ILC主要型態與變異型。
(D) 對：classic type呈linear與targetoid兩種組織型態，linear infiltrates環繞乳管周圍分布。
