#交換 #2016交換 #MSK Which one of the followings differentiates aneurysmal bone cyst from simple bone cyst?
(A) Shepherd's crook deformity.
(B) Significant limb length discrepancy.
(C) A very dense sclerotic border.
(D) Buttressing effect.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**。**Buttressing effect（皮質支撐/扶壁）**＝ABC 偏心、明顯膨脹重塑的表現;SBC 為中央性、膨脹不超骨幹寬。Shepherd's crook（fibrous dysplasia）、明顯肢長不等、緻密硬化緣（NOF）非鑑別點。
