#交換 #2016交換 #NR Which vein does **not** belong to the deep venous system?
(A) The internal cerebral vein
(B) The basal vein of Rosenthal
(C) The vein of Galen.
(D) The superior anastomotic vein of Trolard.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**（不屬深部）。深部靜脈系統＝A internal cerebral vein、B basal vein of Rosenthal、C vein of Galen。
- **D（正解，不屬）**：**superior anastomotic vein of Trolard** 是淺表(cortical)吻合靜脈。
