#交換 #2016交換 #CH The tracheal bronchus most commonly arises from which of the following site?
(A) Right tracheal wall, >2cm from tracheal bifurcation
(B) Right tracheal wall, <2cm from tracheal bifurcation
(C) Left tracheal wall, >2cm from tracheal bifurcation
(D) Left tracheal wall, <2cm from tracheal bifurcation
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**。tracheal bronchus（pig bronchus/bronchus suis）最常起自**氣管右側壁、距分叉（隆突）<2 cm**。
