#交換 #2016交換 #US Which of the following statements about splenic pyogenic abscess is **true**?
(A) The imaging appearance is specific
(B) Guided needle aspiration may be both diagnostic and curative
(C) Abscess most commonly occur secondary to prior infarction
(D) Abscess can be differentiated confidently from true cyst
??
**Ans: B**
**超音波/CT導引針吸引流對脾臟化膿性膿瘍兼具診斷與治療效果**，故(B)為正解。
(A) 錯：影像表現不具特異性。
(B) **對（正解）**：導引針吸引流可兼具診斷與治療效果。
(C) 錯：官方訂正為典型繼發於**心內膜炎或其他血行性播種來源**，非「先前梗塞」。
(D) 錯：無法自信地與真性囊腫作影像鑑別。
