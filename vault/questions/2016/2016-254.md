#交換 #2016交換 #PED Regarding Wilms tumor, which statement is **FALSE**?
(A) Accounts for ~90% of pediatric renal tumors.
(B) Peak age is less than 1 year.
(C) May invade the renal vein.
(D) Bilateral masses suggest nephroblastomatosis and genetic predisposition.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**（為非）。**Wilms tumor peak age 為 3–4 歲（<5 歲）**，非 <1 歲（<1 歲想 mesoblastic nephroma）。占兒童腎腫瘤 90%(A)、可侵腎靜脈(C)、雙側提示 nephroblastomatosis/遺傳(D)皆正確。
