#交換 #2016交換 #CH Regarding plain chest radiograph technique, which of the following statements is NOT correct?
(A) High kVp permits better visualization of the lung parenchyma and mediastinum as compared with low kVp.
(B) Expiration radiography can be used to detect air trapping and confirm small pneumothorax.
(C) Lordotic radiograph can be used to improve visualization of right middle lobe atelectasis.
(D) At a target film distance of 6 feet, grid is not required to reduce scatter radiation.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**（不正確）。6 呎（180 cm）SID 主要降低**幾何放大率**,而非散射;降散射靠 **grid**（胸片高 kVp 散射多、常規使用 grid）。A 對（高 kVp 顯示心後/縱膈較佳）、B 對（呼氣片查 air trapping/小氣胸）、C 對（前凸位改善 RML 塌陷）。
