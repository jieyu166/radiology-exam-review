#交換 #2016交換 #ABD Which one of the following pulse sequences is **not** included in so called multiparametric prostate magnetic resonance imaging (MRI)?
(A) Diffusion weighted image
(B) Apparent diffusion coefficient (ADC) map
(C) Opposed phased image
(D) Dynamic contrast enhanced (DCE) image
??
**Ans: C**
> [!todo] 待補詳解
> 正解 **C**（不含）。mpMRI＝**T2W ＋ DWI(含 ADC) ＋ DCE**（A/B/D 皆屬之）。
> - **C（正解）**：**opposed-phase（化學位移）image 不屬 mpMRI**。
