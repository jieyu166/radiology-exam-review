#交換 #2016交換 #ABD According to the revised Atlanta classification system, which of the following terms describes a necrotic collection that persists 4 weeks after the onset of necrotizing pancreatitis and develops a discrete wall?
(A) Fluid collection.
(B) Pancreatic abscess.
(C) Pseudocyst.
(D) WON (Walled-off necrosis).
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D（WON）**。Revised Atlanta：壞死型積液 **>4 週包覆＝WON（walled-off necrosis）**。pseudocyst(C) 是間質水腫型的非壞死積液 >4 週才稱。
