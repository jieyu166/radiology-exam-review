#交換 #2016交換 #ABD Which of the following is **NOT** a typical image finding of endometrioma on MRI?
(A) Hyperintensity on T1WI
(B) Hypointensity on T2WI
(C) Hypervascular on post-contrast images
(D) All of the above are typical image findings
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**（非典型）。endometrioma＝T1 高(A)、T2 低 shading(B)。
- **C（正解，非典型）**：「post-contrast hypervascular」非典型——endometrioma **無內部強化**；強化壁結節要警惕惡性變。
