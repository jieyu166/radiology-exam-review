#交換 #2016交換 #NR What is the commonest location for spontaneous hypertensive intracranial hemorrhage?
(A) Putamen/external capsule
(B) Cerebellum
(C) Thalamus
(D) Pons •
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**。自發性高血壓腦出血最常見位置＝**putamen/external capsule（基底節，lenticulostriate ~80%）**；其次 pons、cerebellum 各約 10%。
