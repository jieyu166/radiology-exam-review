#交換 #2016交換 #2017交換 #CH Which is the most common complication of tuberculous cavitation?
(A) Rasmussen aneurysm uncommon complication of pulmonary tuberculosis and represents a pulmonary artery aneurysm adjacent or within a tuberculous cavity
(B) endobronchial (acinar) tuberculosis Centrilobular nodules with a linear branching pattern are consistent with tree-in-bud appearance in a patient with endobronchial spreading of post-primary tuberculosis.
(C) aspergilloma
(D) miliary tuberculosis
??
**Ans: B**
結核空洞最常見的併發症是**經氣道（endobronchial / bronchogenic, acinar）播散**——空洞與支氣管相通，CT 呈 centrilobular nodules + tree-in-bud。
(A) Rasmussen aneurysm：少見併發症（空洞鄰近肺動脈假性動脈瘤）。
(B) **Endobronchial (acinar) spread（正解）**：最常見；tree-in-bud＝postprimary 經氣道播散。
(C) Aspergilloma：慢性期既存空洞內真菌球，非最常見。
(D) Miliary TB：屬血行播散，非空洞之經氣道併發症。
