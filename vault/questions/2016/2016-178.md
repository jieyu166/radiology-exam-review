#交換 #2016交換 #NR Which structure is **not** included in the masticator space?
(A) Temporalis muscle
(B) Pterygoid plate
(C) Masseter muscle
(D) Mandibular division of trigeminal nerve
(E) Mandible ramus
??
**Ans: B**
> [!todo] 待補詳解
> 正解 **B**（不含）。A temporalis、C masseter、D V3、E mandible ramus 皆為內容物。
> - **B（正解，不含）**：**pterygoid plate（翼板,骨性結構）** 為骨界,非咀嚼肌間隙內容物。
