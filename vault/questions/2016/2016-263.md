#交換 #2016交換 #NR Which statement is **false** for CLIPPERS (chronic lymphocytic imflammation with pontine perivascular enhancement responsive to steroid)?
(A) The disorder predominantly involves pons, however, supratentorial involvement is also possible.
(B) MRI reveals “punctate and curvilinear” or “peppering” enhancement of the lesions.
(C) Definite diagnosis is made by specific serum/CSF marker currently. Image findings are not specific.
(D) Long-term immunosuppressive therapy is mandatory, due to disease exacerbation after withdrawal of glucocorticosteroid.
??
**Ans: C**
**CLIPPERS目前並無特異性血清/CSF標記可供確診**——診斷依賴臨床、影像、實驗室與CSF檢查綜合判斷，並需審慎排除其他鑑別診斷（必要時腦切片）。
(A) 對：病灶以橋腦為主，但幕上侵犯亦可見。
(B) 對：MRI典型呈「點狀與曲線狀（punctate and curvilinear）」或「胡椒粉狀（peppering）」強化。
(C) **錯（正解）**：目前並無特異性血清/CSF標記可確診，需綜合臨床/影像/實驗室/CSF、審慎排除其他診斷。
(D) 對：停用類固醇後常復發，故需長期免疫抑制治療。
