#交換 #2016交換 #Breast According to the AJCC’s 7th edition TNM staging system of breast cancer, which of the following statement is **wrong**?
(A) Tis – carcinoma in situ (DCIS, LCIS, Paget disease of the nipple not associated with invasive carcinoma)
(B) T1mi – Invasive carcinoma with tumor equal or less than 1mm
(C) T2 – tumor > 20 mm but equal or less than 50 mm
(D) T4a – tumor extension to pectoralis major or minor
(E) T4b – uncertain, ipsilateral satellite nodules, and/or edema of the skin (including peau d’orange)
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**（錯）。A Tis 原位癌、B T1mi ≤1mm、C T2 20-50mm、E T4b 皮膚 皆對。
- **D（正解，錯）**：「T4a＝延伸至 **pectoralis major or minor**」錯——**T4a 是延伸至『胸壁』（肋骨/肋間肌/前鋸肌）**;單純胸肌侵犯不算 T4。
