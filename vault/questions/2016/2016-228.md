#交換 #2016交換 #US When performing a longitudinal scan in abdominal sonography, which direction is displayed on the left side of the screen (the left-hand side of the operator)?
(A) Cranial.
(B) Caudal.
(C) Left.
(D) Right.
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**。縱切（longitudinal）時探頭 marker 朝病人**頭側**、對應**螢幕左側**,故螢幕左＝**cranial（頭側）**、右＝caudal。
