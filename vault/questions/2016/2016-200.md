#交換 #2016交換 #CV Based on current recommendations, what is the stent size below which CT should not be considered for stent patency assessment?
(A) < 2 mm
(B) < 2.5 mm
(C) < 3 mm
(D) < 3.5 mm
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C（< 3 mm）**。SCCT 共識與文獻：**支架直徑 <3 mm 顯著降低 CT 對 in-stent restenosis 的準確度/敏感度**（≤3mm ~54% vs >3mm ~86%），小支架臨床評估不建議；CT 評估理想對象為**直徑 ≥3 mm 與近端節段**支架。
