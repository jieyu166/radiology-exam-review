#交換 #2016交換 #PED Schwachman-Diamond syndrome has
(A) Fatty replacement of the liver
(B) Fatty replacement of the pancreas
(C) Fatty replacement of the spleen
(D) Fatty replacement of the kidneys
??
**Ans: B**
Shwachman-Diamond syndrome 的影像招牌是**胰臟脂肪化（pancreatic lipomatosis）**。
(A) Fatty replacement of the liver：非。
(B) **Fatty replacement of the pancreas（正解）**：外分泌胰臟功能不全＋胰臟脂肪取代，伴骨髓低下、幹骺端發育不良。
(C) Fatty replacement of the spleen：非。
(D) Fatty replacement of the kidneys：非。
概念：[[shwachman-diamond]]
