#交換 #2016交換 #ABD In ureters of duplicated upper urinary tracts, where is the typical abnormally location of upper moiety ureteric insertions?
(A) Inferior and medial in location
(B) Superior and lateral in location
(C) Anterior in location
(D) Posterior in location
??
**Ans: A**
完全重複系統中,引流**上極**的輸尿管異位插入點為**下方且內側(inferior and medial)**——Weigert-Meyer。
(A) **Inferior and medial(正解)**。
(B) Superior and lateral:為下極輸尿管。
(C) Anterior、(D) Posterior:非。
