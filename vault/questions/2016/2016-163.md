#交換 #2016交換 #CH Which one of the following statement is correct?
(A) Cigarette smoking is related to the development of lymphangioleiomyomatosis (LAM), pulmonary Langerhans cell histiocytosis (PLCH), and idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis (IPF)
(B) Thin walled cysts throughout the lung with subpleural sparing is the most common finding of LAM.
(C) Nodules and cysts in upper and mid lungs with subpleural sparing are characteristic findings of PLCH.
(D) Honeycombing in lower lungs with subpleural sparing is predominant in IPF.
??
**Ans: C**
> [!todo] 待補詳解
> 正解 **C**。
> - **C（正解）**：「上中肺野結節＋囊腫、subpleural sparing」是 **PLCH** 的特徵。
> - A 吸菸與 LAM 無關（PLCH 才吸菸相關）；B LAM 為瀰漫均勻囊腫、無 subpleural sparing；D 下肺蜂窩是 UIP/IPF。
