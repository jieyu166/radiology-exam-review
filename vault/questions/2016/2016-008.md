#交換 #2016交換 #CH Which statement is **not** true in the diagnosis of pulmonary embolism?
(A) If the vessel showing the potential filling defect is accompanied by a bronchus, it is likely a pulmonary vein and pulmonary embolism should not be diagnosed.
(B) Pulmonary arterial sarcoma probably is the rarest pitfall in the diagnosis of pulmonary embolism.
(C) Nodular hilar lymph nodes commonly simulate pulmonary embolism on CTA imaging.
(D) Vessels oriented in the transverse plane are the most difficult to image.
??
**Ans: A**
(A) 不正確（答案）：bronchoarterial pairing —— 伴隨支氣管的血管是肺動脈（非肺靜脈）；其內填充缺損可診斷 PE。肺靜脈不伴支氣管、走向左心房。
(B) 正確：pulmonary artery sarcoma 可能是最罕見的 PE pitfall。
(C) 正確：肺門淋巴結（鄰 interlobar arteries）常可模擬 CTA 上的填充缺損。
(D) 正確：走向與掃描面平行（transverse）之血管最難成像/評估。
三類 pitfalls 與來源見概念 [[pe-cta-pitfalls]]。
