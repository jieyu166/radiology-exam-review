#交換 #2016交換 #MSK with pain in both hands. Which of the following findings on plain radiograph of the hands is atypical of skeletal sarcoidosis?
(A) Joint space narrowing
(B) Bone erosions
(C) Subcutaneous soft tissue nodules/mass
(D) Lace like pattern of bone destruction
??
**Ans: A**
> [!todo] 待補詳解
> 正解 **A**（非典型）。
> - **A（正解，非典型）**：骨骼類肉瘤病**關節/椎間隙通常保留**，**關節間隙狹窄非典型**。
> - B（bone erosion）、C（皮下軟組織結節）、D（lace-like 骨破壞）皆典型。
