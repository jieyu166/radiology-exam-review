#交換 #2016交換 #NR Which statement of artery of Adamkiewicz is **incorrect**?
(A) A dominant, great anterior medullary artery usually in the thoracolumbar region
(B) Typically with the “coathook” configuration
(C) Usually with ascending and descending branches to the anterior spinal artery
(D) Most commonly arises from the left side between T9 and T12
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**（錯誤）。A 主要前段髓質動脈、C 升降支至前脊髓動脈、D 左側 T9-T12 為主皆對。
- **B（正解，錯）**：「coathook configuration」錯——特徵為 **hairpin（髮夾）轉折**。
