#交換 #2016交換 #ABD About rectal MR imaging for T staging, which of the following statement is correct?
(A) MRI is very accurate in distinguishing between an adenoma and a T1 adenocarcinoma.
(B) MRI is very accurate in identifying tumors limited to the bowel wall (intact muscularis propria).
(C) MRI is accurate in identifying penetrating tumor when the intact bowel wall sign is absent.
(D) T2/T3 tumors are often understaged because MRI cannot differentiate desmoplastic reaction from tumor.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**。MRI 能準確判斷腫瘤是否**侷限於腸壁**（完整 muscularis propria 黑線）。MRI 不擅長 adenoma vs T1(A 誤)；desmoplastic reaction 致 T2 被『高估(overstage)』為 T3（非 understage，D 誤）。
