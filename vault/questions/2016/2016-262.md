#交換 #2016交換 #NR Regarding dissecting/dissection artery, which of the following statement is **wrong**:
(A) They usually shows various signal changes on MR images.
(B) On spin-echo sequences, intramural hematoma has a striking appearance as an eccentric or occasionally circumferential crescentic, linear, or bank-like focus of high intensity.
(C) Acute hematoma usually shows high signal intensity.
(D) The residual lumen narrows with time, which reflects absent or diminished flow void.
??
**Ans: C**
**急性期血腫（acute hematoma）通常呈低訊號（非高訊號）**，需隨時間演變（deoxyhemoglobin→methemoglobin）才轉為高訊號。
(A) 對：動脈剝離之壁內血腫在MR上隨時期呈多樣訊號變化（依血紅素分解階段）。
(B) 對：spin-echo序列上壁內血腫可呈偏心或環周之新月形／線狀／帶狀高訊號。
(C) **錯（正解）**：急性血腫（去氧血紅素為主）通常呈低訊號，而非高訊號；訊號隨時間演變才轉為高訊號（methemoglobin期）。
(D) 對：殘餘管腔隨時間縮窄，反映流空訊號減少/消失（血栓化）。
