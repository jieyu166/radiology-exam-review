#交換 #2016交換 #CV In a patient after total cavopulmonary connection (TCPC, Fontan), what should be carefully evaluated on CT or MRI?
(A) Any junctional stenosis between SVC/IVC and the pulmonary artery.
(B) Presence of IVC conduit fenestration.
(C) Thrombosis or intimal hyperplasia in the IVC conduit.
(D) Prolapse of the atrioventricular valve on 4-chamber CINE images.
(E) All of the above.
??
**Ans: E**
正解 **E**（以上皆是）。TCPC/Fontan 術後 CT/MRI 須評估 SVC/IVC-PA 吻合處狹窄(A)、conduit fenestration(B)、conduit 血栓/內膜增生(C)、房室瓣脫垂(D)。
