#交換 #2016交換 #CH Regarding pulmonary calcifications, which of the following statements is NOT correct?
(A) Interstitial ossification in idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis appears as branching opacities along bronchovascular bundles.
(B) Metastatic calcification due to renal failure usually occurs in the apical and subapical lung zones.
(C) End-stage mitral stenosis is related to diffuse pulmonary calcification.
(D) Laminated pattern in a pulmonary nodule is associated with malignancy.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**（不正確）。**層狀（laminated/concentric）鈣化屬良性型態**（如肉芽腫）,非惡性。A 對（IPF 樹枝狀骨化沿支氣管血管束）、B 對（腎衰轉移性鈣化好發上肺、V/Q 高較鹼性）、C 對（末期二尖瓣狹窄致瀰漫肺鈣化/骨化）。
