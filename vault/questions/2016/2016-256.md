#交換 #2016交換 #ABD Which of the following is NOT a sonographic finding of hepatic cavernous hemangioma?
(A) Ill-defined, heterogeneous, hyperechoic mass.
(B) Doppler shows no internal blood flow or slow flow.
(C) Most lesions remain stable in size over time.
(D) Large lesions may contain hypoechoic thrombosis, fibrosis and calcification.
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**。肝海綿狀血管瘤典型為**well-defined、homogeneous、hyperechoic**（約 10% 因脂肪肝背景呈 hypoechoic），非 ill-defined/heterogeneous。Doppler 無/慢血流(B)、多年穩定(C)、大者可含血栓/纖維化/鈣化(D)皆為其表現。
