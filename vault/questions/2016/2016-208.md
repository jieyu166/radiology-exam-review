#交換 #2016交換 #MSK The "rotator cuff interval" is defined as the anatomic space between which tendons?
(A) Supraspinatus and infraspinatus.
(B) Supraspinatus and subscapularis.
(C) Long head of biceps and subscapularis.
(D) Infraspinatus and teres minor.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**。旋轉肌間隙＝**supraspinatus 與 subscapularis 肌腱之間**的三角空間，內含肱二頭肌長頭腱、喙肱韌帶、上盂肱韌帶。
