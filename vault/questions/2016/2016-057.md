#交換 #2016交換 #ABD About splenic tumors, which of the following statement is **incorrect**?
(A) The most common benign splenic tumor is hemangioma.
(B) The most common malignancy of the spleen is metastasis.
(C) Splenic epidermoid cyst is true cyst with epithelial lining, while false cysts arising from trauma, infection, and infarction.
(D) Splenic hamartoma may associate with anemia and thrombocytopenia.
??
**Ans: B**
脾臟最常見惡性是 **lymphoma**(多為續發)，不是轉移，故 (B) 錯誤。
(A) Most common benign = hemangioma：正確。
(B) **Most common malignancy = metastasis（正解＝錯誤）**：應為 lymphoma；脾轉移相對少見。
(C) Epidermoid cyst = true cyst with epithelial lining; false cysts from trauma/infection/infarction：正確。
(D) Splenic hamartoma may associate with anemia and thrombocytopenia：正確。
