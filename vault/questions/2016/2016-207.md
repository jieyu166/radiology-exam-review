#交換 #2016交換 #ABD Which of the following arteries is most frequently involved by pseudoaneurysm formation in necrotizing pancreatitis?
(A) Gastroduodenal.
(B) Splenic.
(C) Gastric.
(D) Hepatic.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B（Splenic）**。壞死性胰臟炎中,胰酶侵蝕鄰近血管形成假性動脈瘤,**最常侵犯脾動脈（splenic artery）**,其次 gastroduodenal artery。可致大出血、hemosuccus pancreaticus,需血管栓塞處置。
