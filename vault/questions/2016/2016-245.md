#交換 #2016交換 #Breast Which one is the "**wrong**" statement about Paget's disease of the nipple?
(A) Grossly, Paget's disease has the appearance of eczema with scaly inflamed areas.
(B) A wedge biopsy of the nipple can successfully establish the diagnosis.
(C) An associated carcinoma is rare when Paget's disease is present in the nipple.
(D) Paget cells in the epidermis within a biopsy specimen confirm the diagnosis.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**（錯誤）。**乳頭 Paget disease 絕大多數(>85–95%)合併下方乳癌（DCIS 或浸潤癌）**，並非罕見。濕疹樣外觀(A)、wedge biopsy 可診斷(B)、表皮 Paget cells 確診(D)皆正確。
