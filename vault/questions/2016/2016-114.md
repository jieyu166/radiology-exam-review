#交換 #2016交換 #CV Which of the following pulse sequence is most suitable when imaging the presence of myocardial scars during cardiac MR?
(A) Late gadolinium enhancement (LGE) imaging
(B) Resting perfusion imaging
(C) Cine imaging
(D) Phase contrast imaging
??
**Ans: A**
> [!todo] 待補詳解
> 正解 **A（LGE）**。
> - **A（正解）**：**Late gadolinium enhancement** 評估心肌**疤痕/壞死（viability）**，疤痕延遲滯留釓呈高訊號。
> - B resting perfusion＝灌流；C cine＝室壁運動/功能；D phase contrast＝流速。
