#交換 #2016交換 #NR According to the 2010 McDonald MRI criteria for multiple sclerosis, DIS (dissemination in space) can be demonstrated with at least one T2 lesion in at least 2 of 4 following locations characteristic for MS **EXCEPT**…
(A) Periventricular
(B) Juxtacortical
(C) Optic nerves
(D) Infratentorial
(E) Spinal cord
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C（optic nerves）**。2010 McDonald 的 DIS 四部位＝periventricular(A)、juxtacortical(B)、infratentorial(D)、spinal cord(E)。
- **C（正解）**：**optic nerve（視神經）不在 2010 DIS 部位之列**。
