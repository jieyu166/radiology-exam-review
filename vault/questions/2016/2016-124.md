#交換 #2016交換 #ABD Which of the following description about colon volvulus is incorrect?
(A) Cecal volvulus is the most common type sigmoid(60%)
(B) Typical cecal volvulus has kidney or coffee bean-shaped gas-filled cecum
(C) In sigmoid volvulus, the dilated twisted sigmoid colon projects above transverse colon called “Northern exposure” sign
(D) Colon volvulus is rare in children
??
**Ans: A**
> [!todo] 待補詳解
> 正解 **A**（錯誤敘述）。
> - **A（正解，錯）**：**sigmoid（乙狀結腸）才是最常見（~60%）**，非 cecal。
> - B 對：cecal volvulus 呈腎形/coffee-bean 充氣盲腸。
> - C 對：sigmoid 巨大迴圈投射過橫結腸（northern exposure）。
> - D 對：結腸扭轉好發老年，兒童罕見。
