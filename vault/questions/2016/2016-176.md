#交換 #2016交換 #NR Usually, the inferior sagittal sinus will join the:
(A) superior sagittal sinus
(B) straight sinus
(C) vein of Galen
(D) confluence of sinsues
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C（vein of Galen）**。下矢狀竇(ISS)與 **vein of Galen(大腦大靜脈)** 匯合形成 straight sinus。
