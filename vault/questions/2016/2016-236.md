#交換 #2016交換 #NR In moyamoya disease, maximal basal collaterals with small ACA/MCA corresponds to which Suzuki stage?
(A) Stage I.
(B) Stage II.
(C) Stage III.
(D) Stage IV.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**。**Stage III＝煙霧側支最盛(maximal basal collaterals)、ACA/MCA 顯影缺損**。之後 IV–VI 煙霧反而減少、轉 ECA 側支代償。
