#交換 #2016交換 #CH Which of the following diseases tends to cause large regional areas of mosaic attenuation that conform to vascular territories?
(A) Chronic thromboembolic pulmonary hypertension (CTEPH).
(B) Idiopathic PAH.
(C) PVOD.
(D) Mitral stenosis.
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A（CTEPH）**。**CTEPH 因大（節段/葉）肺動脈慢性栓塞，造成大範圍、區域性、符合血管領域（vascular territory）的 mosaic perfusion**。
- B idiopathic PAH、C PVOD、D mitral stenosis 屬小血管/後微血管，較均勻/瀰漫，非territory mosaic。
