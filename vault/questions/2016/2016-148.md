#交換 #2016交換 #Physics What is the threshold for permanent sterility in males from an acute exposure, approximately? • (A) 1 Gy
(B) 10 Gy •
(C) 1 Gy •
(D) 6 Gy •
??
**Ans: D**
> [!todo] 待補詳解
