#交換 #2016交換 #ABD Which of the following findings would make the diagnosis of large renal angiomyolipoma more likely than a retroperitoneal liposarcoma?
(A) Presence of mass effect with displacement of kidney.
(B) Presence of aneurysmal blood vessels within the lesion.
(C) Presence of ill-defined margin.
(D) Presence of soft tissue density areas within the lesion.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**。**病灶內動脈瘤樣血管 → AML（富血管）**；retroperitoneal liposarcoma 為 hypovascular。腫塊外推腎臟（A）、邊界不清（C）、軟組織密度（D）在兩者皆可見、非鑑別點。
