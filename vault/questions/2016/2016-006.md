#交換 #2016交換 #CH Which one of the following statement is **incorrect** while talking about primary tuberculosis (TB) and postprimary TB?
(A) Cavitation is more common in postprimary TB, typically with thick and irregular walls.
(B) Parenchymal consolidation in upper lobe is the predominant presentation of primary TB.
(C) Pleural effusion is more common in primary TB than in post-primary TB, usually unilaterally.
(D) Necrotic hilar and paratracheal lymphadenopathy can be the sole radiographic finding of primary TB.
??
**Ans: B**
題目問「不正確」者 → 答案 B。
(A) 正確：cavitation 在 postprimary（reactivation）TB 較常見，典型為厚/不規則壁。
(B) 不正確（答案）：primary TB 的 consolidation 無上肺偏好（任何肺葉、常下/中葉）；上肺 apical/posterior 的 consolidation 是 postprimary 的特徵，非 primary。
(C) 正確：pleural effusion 在 primary TB 較常見，通常單側。
(D) 正確：低密度（壞死）hilar/paratracheal lymphadenopathy 可為 primary TB 的唯一影像所見（尤其兒童）。
Primary vs postprimary 對照與來源見概念 [[pulmonary-tb]]。
