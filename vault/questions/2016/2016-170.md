#交換 #2016交換 #NR Which is **WRONG** about typical image appearance of brain cavernous hemangioma (cavernous angioma/cavernoma)?
(A) Mixed internal signal intensities with peripheral hypointensity
(B) Rarely showing perifocal edema unless recent hemorrhage
(C) May associated with developmental venous anomaly
(D) Never shows enhancement
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**（為錯）。A 混雜訊號+周邊含鐵血黃素低訊號、B 少水腫(除非近期出血)、C 合併 DVA 皆對。
- **D（正解，為錯）**：「Never shows enhancement（從不強化）」錯——cavernoma 為 **minimal or no enhancement（微弱/無）**，並非絕對不強化。
