#交換 #2016交換 #CV Which is NOT a sign of impending aneurysm rupture?
(A) Increasing size of aneurysm.
(B) Focal discontinuity in circumferential wall calcifications.
(C) Crescent sign.
(D) Thickening of mural thrombus.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**。**單純血栓增厚非即將破裂徵象**。increasing size(A)、wall calcification 局部不連續(B)、crescent sign（壁內出血，C）皆為警訊。
