#交換 #2016交換 #Unknown About rhinocerebral mucormycosis, which of the following descriptions is **false**?
(A) Typically affecting diabetic or immunocompromised patients
(B) A necrotizing vasculitis that extends rapidly into deep face, orbits, cranial cavity, and brain
(C) Enhancement patterns are variable, ranging from homogeneous to heterogenous or non-enhancing at all.
(D) Signal intensity of mucormycosis lesions tends to be hyperintense on T2WI and hypointensity on T1WI
(E) May result in cerebral infarction
??
**Ans: D**
> [!todo] 待補詳解
> : Variable T2(20% high); iso T1
> T1: isointense lesions relative to brain in most cases (~80%)
> T2: fungal elements themselves tend to have low signal on T2
>        variable with around 20% of patients showing high T2 signal
> T1 C+ (Gd): the devitalised mucosa appears non-enhancing tissue, leading to he black turbinate sign
> - **A) TRUE** — 搜尋資料明確指出 ROCM 好發於糖尿病控制不佳及免疫抑制患者。
> - **B) TRUE** — PMC 文獻描述 Mucorales 造成 necrotizing vasculitis，經顱底孔道及裂隙迅速擴散至深部面部、眼眶及腦部。
> - **C) TRUE** — 影像文獻記載顯影模式多變，從均勻顯影、不均勻顯影到完全不顯影（壞死組織）皆可出現。
> - **D) ❌ FALSE** — 這是本題的錯誤選項。根據搜尋資料，ROCM 病灶在 **T1WI 多呈等訊號（80%）**，而 **T2WI 傾向等訊號至低訊號**（僅 20% 呈高訊號）。T2 低訊號是因為真菌菌絲含有順磁性物質（鐵、錳），這恰好與一般感染性病灶的 T2 高訊號相反，是重要的鑑別線索。選項描述的「T2 hyperintense, T1 hypointense」是典型發炎/水腫的訊號特徵，並非 mucormycosis 的特點。
> - **E) TRUE** — 真菌的血管侵襲性造成血管血栓，可導致腦梗塞，文獻中有明確記載。
