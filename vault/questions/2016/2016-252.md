#交換 #2016交換 #CV In tetralogy of Fallot, which associated anomalies should be carefully evaluated on CT/MRI?
(A) Anomalous LAD from the right coronary artery crossing the RVOT.
(B) Persistent left-sided superior vena cava.
(C) Right upper lobe tracheobronchus.
(D) Major aortopulmonary collateral arteries (MAPCA).
(E) All of the above.
??
**Ans: E**
正解 **E**（以上皆是）。TOF 常合併異常 LAD 自 RCA 跨 RVOT(A)、persistent left SVC(B)、RUL tracheobronchus(C)、MAPCA(D)，術前皆須評估。
