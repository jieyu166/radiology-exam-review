#交換 #2016交換 #ABD Which of the following statements **best** describes adrenal cortical nodular hyperplasia?
(A) It does not occur with adrenal cortical adenomas.
(B) It increases in prevalence with age.
(C) It is most commonly focal.
(D) It is most commonly unilateral.
??
**Ans: B**
腎上腺皮質結節性增生**隨年齡增加**而盛行率上升。
(A) Does not occur with adrenal cortical adenomas：錯（可與腺瘤並存）。
(B) **Increases in prevalence with age（正解）**。
(C) Most commonly focal：錯（多為瀰漫/多結節，非單一焦點）。
(D) Most commonly unilateral：錯（多為雙側）。
