#交換 #2016交換 #CH Which of the following statements best characterizes the pathophysiology and imaging findings of amyloidosis?
(A) Lung lesions in nodular parenchymal amyloidosis commonly contain light chain (AL) amyloid.
(B) Amyloid deposits contain starch.
(C) The most common forms of amyloidosis are genetically inherited.
(D) Tracheobronchial involvement usually spares the posterior membrane of the trachea.
??
**Ans: A**
(A) 正確（答案）：nodular parenchymal amyloidosis 通常為局部 AL（amyloid light chain）。
(B) 錯：amyloid 是錯誤摺疊蛋白（Congo red 染色、偏光下 apple-green birefringence），非澱粉（starch）。
(C) 錯：最常見型為後天（AL 最常見、AA 次發），非遺傳；hereditary 型較少見。
(D) 錯：tracheobronchial amyloidosis 環狀增厚、累及氣管後壁膜；保留後壁的是 relapsing polychondritis / TBO。
