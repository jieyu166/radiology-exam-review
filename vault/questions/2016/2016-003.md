#交換 #2016交換 #CV

What was the principal finding of the ACCURACY (Assessment by Coronary Computed Tomographic Angiography of Individuals Undergoning Invasive Coronary Angiography) trial in terms of detection of > 70% stenosis?
(A) Sensitivity and NPV > 90%, PPV < 75%
(B) Sensitivity 80%, NPV > 90%, PPV< 75%
(C) Sensitivity >90%, PPV> 90%, NPV > 90%
(D) 
??
**Ans: A**
(A) 正確：ACCURACY trial（Budoff 2008，64-MDCT，patient-based）對 ≥70% 狹窄：Sensitivity 94%、NPV 99%（皆 >90%）、PPV 48%（<75%）、Specificity 83%。
(B) 錯：Sensitivity 並非 80%，實測為 94%（>90%）。
(C) 錯：PPV 並非 >90%，實為 48%——重度鈣化 blooming 與假影致偽陽性偏多，故 PPV 低；CCTA 強在高 NPV 的 rule-out。
試驗數據、calcium >400 降低 specificity、CAD-RADS 等細節與來源見概念 [[ccta]]。
