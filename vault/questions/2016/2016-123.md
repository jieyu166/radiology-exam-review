#交換 #2016交換 #ABD The relative liver/gallbladder bile signal intensity ratio may be significantly ___ on T1-weighted sequences in patients with chronic choleystitis or normal status.
(A) increased
(B) decreased
(C) same
(D) indetermined
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B（decreased）**。膽汁 T1 訊號 正比於 濃度；慢性膽囊炎/膽石症膽汁**滯留、濃縮、sludge↑** → 膽囊膽汁 T1 訊號**增高（變亮）** → **肝/膽囊膽汁訊號比值顯著『下降』**。
