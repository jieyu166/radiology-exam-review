#交換 #2016交換 #2017交換 #ABD Which of the following is NOT a typical ultrasound finding in patients with portal hypertension?
(A) Small diameter of portal vein
(B) Splenomegaly
(C) Hepatofugal flow in portal vein It is the opposite of hepatopetal.
(D) Recanalization of umbilical vein
??
**Ans: A**
門脈高壓使門脈**擴張**，故 (A)「門脈直徑變小」非典型。
(A) **Small diameter of portal vein（正解＝非典型）**：門脈高壓→門脈擴張(>13mm)，非變小。
(B) Splenomegaly：典型。
(C) Hepatofugal flow in portal vein：典型(離肝/逆流)。
(D) Recanalization of umbilical vein：典型(臍靜脈再通，近乎診斷性)。
