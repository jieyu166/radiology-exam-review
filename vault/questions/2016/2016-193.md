#交換 #2016交換 #Unknown Which one is **incorrect** about ultrasound artifact
(A) **Ring down** occurs most frequently due to gas, and metal is also capable of producing a ring-down artifact.
(B) A high resistance arterial waveform in spermatic artery may be the only imaging finding in partial torsion.
(C) **Aliasing** is a well-known artifact that occurs when the Doppler sampling rate is more than twice the Doppler frequency shift
(D) None of above. (All of above are correct)
??
**Ans: C**
> [!todo] 待補詳解
> Nyquist limit=PRF/2。超過會產生 aliasing artifact。主要是sampling rate不夠快。所以應該是less than。![ADD US Aliasing artifact](data/images/obsidian/add-us-aliasing-artifact-f5875b50.png) Doppler中 upper margin of the velocity scale調太低也會導致(波峰被截斷，出現在基線對側（wrap-around）)。
> - A. Ring-Down appears mostly due to gas (rather than metal, as in the case of comet tail artifact)
> - B. intratesticular arteries 正常是低阻力，阻力變大要懷疑partial torsion。
