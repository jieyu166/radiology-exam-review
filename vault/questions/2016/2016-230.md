#交換 #2016交換 #ABD Which feature best distinguishes CT urography from contrast-enhanced CT with an excretory delayed phase?
(A) They are synonymous descriptions of the same CT technique.
(B) Contrast-enhanced CT with an excretory delayed phase does not allow evaluation of the ureters.
(C) CT urography does not include a contrast-enhanced phase.
(D) CT urography includes a nonenhanced phase.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**。**CTU 含專屬未顯影（nonenhanced）期**（KUB 查結石）＋ nephrogram ＋ excretory 期。兩者皆能評估輸尿管（B 錯）、CTU 含顯影期（C 錯）、非同義詞（A 錯）。
