#交換 #2016交換 #NR Which is **wrong** about “pituicytoma”
(A) Rare primary tumor of the adult adenohypophysis.
(B) WHO grade I of CNS tumor
(C) Sellar or suprasellar tumor
(D) Immunoreactive for vimentin, S-100 protein, epithelial membrane antigen (EMA) and glial fibrillary acidic protein (GFAP).
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**（錯）。B WHO grade 1、C 鞍/鞍上、D S100/vimentin/EMA 皆對。
- **A（正解，錯）**：「成人**腺垂體(adenohypophysis)**的罕見原發腫瘤」錯——pituicytoma 源自**神經垂體(neurohypophysis)/漏斗部**。
