#交換 #2018交換 #CH Which choice is **false** about immune-related adverse events (irAEs)?
(A) irAE is a unique set of complications related to immune checkpoint inhibitors.
(B) Immune checkpoint inhibitor–related pneumonitis indicated a wide spectrum of clinical and imaging manifestations; the radiologic patterns reflect disease severity.
(C) Sarcoid-like granulomatosis and lymphadenopathy is another important irAE that involves the thorax.
(D) Treatment of immune checkpoint inhibitor–related pneumonitis includes withholding the immune checkpoint inhibitors and administering corticosteroid therapy.
(E) Patients with immune checkpoint inhibitor–related pneumonitis cannot restart immune checkpoint inhibitor therapy after pneumonitis is successfully treated.
??
**Ans: E**
根據文章，治療ICI-related pneumonitis包含停藥，steroid治療。嚴重患者還需要其他免疫抑制劑治療。約三分之一的患者在肺炎成功治療後，可以重新使用ICI治療，但25-28% 患者可能復發。
