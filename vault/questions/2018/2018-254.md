#交換 #2018交換 #Breast Which statement below is incorrect about lobular cancer in situ (LCIS) and Atypical lobular hyperplasia (ALH)?
(A) LCIS is generally diagnosed in pathologically when there is involvement of more than 50% of lobular acini, less than 50% called ALH.
(B) A pleomorphic variant (PLCIS) strong resembles DCIS in histologically.
(C) LCIS is symptomatic; frequently multifocal, multicentric and can be bilateral in up to 1/3 cases.
(D) Lobular neoplasia can be found associated with DCIS, IDC and ILC.
??
**Ans: C**
LCIS通常「無症狀(asymptomatic)」，多為偶然發現（非有症狀），此為本題錯誤敘述。LCIS病理診斷以侵犯>50%小葉腺泡為準(<50%稱ALH)、多形性變異型(PLCIS)組織學上近似DCIS、常多發灶/多中心且可達1/3雙側、小葉贅生可與DCIS/IDC/ILC並存，皆為正確描述。
