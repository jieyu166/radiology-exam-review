#交換 #2018交換 #Unknown Which of the following is NOT correct considering the AJCC 8th staging system of cervical cancer?
(A) Tumor with parametrial invasion is considered T2b
(B) N1 removed from FIGO stage IIIB
(C) Paraaortic lymphadenopathy is considered N1
(D) All of the above are correct
??
**Ans: D**

WHO T3c / FIGO IIIC
- Involvement of pelvic and/or para-aortic lymph nodes, irrespective of tumor size and extent
WHO T3c1/ FIGO IIIC1
- Pelvic lymph node metastasis only
WHO T3c2 / FIGO IIIC2
- Para-aortic lymph node metastasis
N0: No regional lymph node metastasis
N0 (i+): Isolated tumor cells in regional lymph node(s) ≤ 0.2 mm
N1: Regional LN metastasis (para-aortic, pelvic(para-metrial/cervical), inguinal)
