#交換 #2018交換 #IR Which of the following arteries is most likely to be associated with hiccups as the complication during TACE?
(A) Intercostal artery
(B) Inferior phrenic artery
(C) Internal mammary artery
(D) Cystic artery
??
**Ans: B**
膈下動脈(inferior phrenic artery)緊鄰橫膈，栓塞化療藥物灌注時易刺激橫膈神經而誘發呃逆(hiccups)，為TACE經膈下動脈供血栓塞時常見之併發症。
