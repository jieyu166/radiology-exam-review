#交換 #2018交換 #PED Which one of the followings related to abusive children with head injury is correct?
(A) MRI is recommended if acute neurologic symptoms or signs, even nonspecific, are present.
(B) Subdural hemorrhages frequently account for severe neurologic sequelae of head trauma
(C) A short fall may still frequently cause child’s severe intracranial injury.
(D) Retinal hemorrhages are reported in approximately 85% of children with abusive head trauma.
??
**Ans: D**
視網膜出血於受虐性頭部創傷兒童中約85%可見，為正確敘述。急性神經學症狀/徵象出現時應優先安排CT（非MRI，A錯）；硬腦膜下出血雖可見但「通常不」是造成嚴重神經學後遺症的主因(B錯)；低處跌落極少造成兒童嚴重顱內損傷，非常見機轉(C錯)。
