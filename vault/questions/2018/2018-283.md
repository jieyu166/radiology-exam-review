#交換 #2018交換 #NR Which one is **wrong** about second impact syndrome?
(A) Disproportionate mass effect of the acute subdural hemorrhage.
(B) Repeated head trauma within “window of vulnerability”.
(C) Can result catastrophic brain swelling with permanent disability or death.
(D) Acute subdural hemorrhage usually > 0.5cm.
??
**Ans: D**
二次撞擊症候群(second impact syndrome)之顱內急性硬腦膜下出血通常「薄(thin，<0.5cm)」，但可合併與其血腫大小不成比例(disproportionate)之嚴重中線偏移與腫塊效應，故「通常>0.5cm」為錯誤敘述。反覆頭部創傷發生於「易感窗期(window of vulnerability)」內、可致災難性腦腫脹合併永久失能或死亡、硬腦膜下出血合併不成比例腫塊效應，皆為正確描述。
