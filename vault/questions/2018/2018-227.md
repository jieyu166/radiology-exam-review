#交換 #2018交換 #NR Which one of the following is most likely to cause a jugular foramen mass
(A) Paraganglioma
(B) Meningioma
(C) Medulloblastoma
(D) Astrocytoma
??
**Ans: A**
副神經節瘤(glomus jugulare/paraganglioma)為頸靜脈孔(jugular foramen)最常見之腫瘤，具富血管特徵與T2 salt-and-pepper徵象。Meningioma可侵犯頸靜脈孔但非最常見原發部位；medulloblastoma、astrocytoma非典型頸靜脈孔腫瘤。
