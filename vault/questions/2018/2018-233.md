#交換 #2018交換 #PED About Kawasaki disease, which statement is **wrong**?
(A) First described in Japan in 1967
(B) Is an chronic self-limited multisystemic panarteritis
(C) Cardiac sequelae include coronary artery dilatation, premature atherosclerosis and stenosis, thrombosis, or occlusion with myocardial infarction
(D) Most affect left main coronary artery
??
**Ans: B**
川崎病為「急性(acute)」而非「慢性(chronic)」自限性多系統性全血管炎，此為本題錯誤敘述。1967年於日本首次描述、心臟後遺症包含冠狀動脈擴張/早發性動脈粥樣硬化與狹窄/血栓/併發心肌梗塞、最常侵犯左主冠狀動脈，皆為正確描述。
