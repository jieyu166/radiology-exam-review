#交換 #2018交換 #CV Which statement about cardiac MR sequence was **wrong**?
(A) Triple IR sequence is also fat suppressed images.
(B) The ratio of perfusion at stress to that at rest is defined as myocardial perfusion reserve. In normal myocardium, MPR is above 2
(C) Myocardial ECV is defined as the ratio of the change in the myocardial T1 to the change in the blood T1. in pre-contrast vs. post-contrast.
(D) Decreased ECV is associated with the severity of diffuse myocardial fibrosis in histology, and with diastolic dysfunction in patients with heart failure and preserved ejection fraction.
??
**Ans: D**
錯誤。通常和iron, fatty overload有關。
A. **正確**。(Core Rad) Double IR是FSE加上dark-blood technique，會讓血液更黑。Triple IR則是再加上IR抑制fat。
B. 可參考 J Cardiovasc Magn Reson. 2014 Mar 27;16(1):24. MPR定義如題目所述。其Impaired MPR 定義是**<2** (peak CS flow/pre CS flow<2) 且和 advanced age, diabetes, current smoking and higher Framingham risk score 有關。
C. 大致上是正確的。很難用文字描述。 ![ADD T1 mapping](data/images/obsidian/add-t1-mapping-c9633e41.png)
