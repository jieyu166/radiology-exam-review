#交換 #2018交換 #Unknown Which one of the following radiographic feature is most likely to see in Tetralogy of the fallot ?
(A) Boot shaped heart
(B) Rib notching
(C) Increased pulmonary vascular markings
(D) Enlargement of hilum
??
**Ans: A**
法洛氏四合症(Tetralogy of Fallot)胸部X光典型呈「靴狀心(boot-shaped heart)」——右心室肥厚合併肺動脈幹凹陷、心尖上翹，同時因肺動脈狹窄致肺血流減少而肺紋理減少(非增加)。Rib notching為主動脈狹窄(coarctation)特徵；hilum腫大非本病典型特徵。
