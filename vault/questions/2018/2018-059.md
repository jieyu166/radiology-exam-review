#交換 #2018交換 #CV Which of the following statements applies to T1 mapping in myocardial fibrosis?
(A) Diffuse myocardial fibrosis usually causes an increase in both native T1 and ECV.
(B) ECV is regarded as the more robust parameter for the assessment of myocardial fibrosis and shows better correlation with histologic findings than native T1.
(C) An increase in ECV can reflect other processes associated with extracellular matrix expansion than diffuse myocardial fibrosis.
(D) All of the above
??
**Ans: D**
全對
A. **正確**。 [[2016 Cardiac T1 Mapping and Extracellular Volume (ECV) in clinical practice - a comprehensive review.pdf]] 在myocarditis有提到。[[2016 R State of the Art - Clinical Applications of Cardiac T1 Mapping.pdf]]提到很多diffuse fibrosis都會造成native T1值增加，但和正常人有overlapping所以不好用。
B. **正確**。 [[2016 Cardiac T1 Mapping and Extracellular Volume (ECV) in clinical practice - a comprehensive review.pdf]] 有提到前半，且比post-contrast T1好。[[2016 R State of the Art - Clinical Applications of Cardiac T1 Mapping.pdf]]則提到後半，ECV和collagen volume fraction的一致性比native T1更好。
C. **正確**
