#交換 #2018交換 #Breast Which the following one is **incorrect** in description of phyllodes tumor of breast?
(A) Malignant phyllodes tumor is a rare tumor of the breast occurring in females usually between the ages of 35 and 55 years.
(B) Pseudoangiomatous stromal hyperplasia, fibroadenoma, intraductal papilloma, and complicated cyst could also be included in the differential diagnosis
(C) Cystic areas at sonography were more common in malignant than benign tumors
(D) All of the above are correct
??
**Ans: D**
C. 敘述正確，其引用Radiol Case Rep. 2017 Jul 29;12(4):645-647. ，但良惡性是無否用影像來區分的。Cystic areas at sonography were more common in malignant than benign tumors, but **the difference was not statistically significant.**
