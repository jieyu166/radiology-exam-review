#交換 #2018交換 #MSK Which ONE of the following image findings is NOT commonly seen in a Brodie abscess on the radiograph?
(A) Ill-defined margin
(B) Surrounding sclerosis
(C) Center in the metaphysis
(D) Extending into the growth plate
??
**Ans: A**
Brodie abscess(亞急性骨髓炎)典型呈「界線清楚(well-demarcated)」病灶，而非邊界不清(ill-defined)，故本敘述錯誤。周圍反應性硬化緣、中心位於幹骺端、可延伸跨越生長板，皆為正確典型描述。
