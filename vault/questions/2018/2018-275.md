#交換 #2018交換 #Physics If the transducer diameter increases, the lateral resolution at its smallest dimension is:
(A) Increased
(B) Decreased
(C) Unchanged
??
**Ans: A**
探頭直徑(孔徑)增加時，聚焦區(focal zone)波束最窄處之側向解析度(lateral resolution)會提升(改善)，故答案為increased。
