#交換 #2018交換 #CV About manifestations of cardiac amyloidosis, which is **wrong**?
(A) Transmural delayed enhancement of myocardium.
(B) Subendocardial delayed enhancement of myocardium.
(C) Ventricular wall akinesia.
(D) Ventricular wall thickening.
??
**Ans: C**
心臟類澱粉沉積(cardiac amyloidosis)典型MRI表現為心內膜下或透壁性延遲強化、心室壁增厚；「心室壁運動不能(akinesia)」並非典型描述特徵(心肌收縮力early期常仍保留，屬非特異性表現)，故為錯誤敘述。
