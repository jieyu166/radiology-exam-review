#交換 #2018交換 #IR Which of the following statement regarding to TIPS(Transjugular intrahepatic portosystemic shunt) is **false**?
(A) The main purpose of performing TIPS is to prolong the survival of patient with end stage cirrhosis.
(B) Patient with severe hepatic encephalopathy, decompensated hepatic failure or severe heart failure is contraindicated for TIPS.
(C) After performing TIPS, the porto-systemic pressure gradient decreases.
(D) The most common course of shunting is from right hepatic vein to right portal vein.
??
**Ans: A**
TIPS置放之主要目的為降低與門脈高壓相關併發症之門脈壓力(如控制靜脈瘤出血、頑固性腹水)，並非以「延長末期肝硬化病人存活期」為主要目的，故本敘述錯誤。重度肝性腦病變、失代償肝衰竭或嚴重心衰竭為TIPS之禁忌症、術後門體壓力梯度下降(目標<12 mmHg)、最常見分流路徑為右肝靜脈至右門脈，皆為正確描述。
