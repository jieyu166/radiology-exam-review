#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Which of the following statement about the kidney is **false**?
A. The columns of Bertin extend medially within the substance of the kidney separating the medulla into pyramids.
B. The renal arteries arise from the aorta at the superior margin of L2.
C. Compound calyces are less efficient at preventing intrarenal reflux of urine.
D. The right kidney is usually larger by about 1.5cm than the left kidney.
E. The right renal artery is posterior to the inferior vena cava.
(A) The columns of Bertin extend medially within the substance of the kidney separating the medulla into pyramids.
(B) The renal arteries arise from the aorta at the superior margin of L2.
(C) Compound calyces are less efficient at preventing intrarenal reflux of urine.
(D) The right kidney is usually larger by about 1.5cm than the left kidney.
(E) The right renal artery is posterior to the inferior vena cava.
??
**Ans: D**
正常腎臟頭尾徑約9-13公分，「左」腎本質上較右腎大約0.5公分（非右腎較左腎大1.5公分），男性腎臟略大於女性，故本敘述錯誤。Columns of Bertin向內延伸分隔髓質成錐體、腎動脈於L2上緣起源於主動脈、複合腎盞較不易防止腎內尿液逆流、右腎動脈位於下腔靜脈後方，皆為正確描述。
