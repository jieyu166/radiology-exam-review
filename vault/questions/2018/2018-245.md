#交換 #2018交換 #NR Which one of the following is **wrong**?
(A) Cocaine is the hallmark drug well known for producing both ischemic stroke and intracranial hemorrhage.
(B) The practice of cocaine inhalation is called "chasing the dragon."
(C) Heroin is the hallmark drug associated with leukoencephalopathy.
(D) The key to differentiating heroin-induced leukoencephalopathy from other mimics is involvement of the cerebellum in a clinically nonhypertensive patient.
(E) Alcohol can cause loss of both grey and white matter of brain.
??
**Ans: B**
「Chasing the dragon」一詞指的是「海洛因(heroin)」吸入之施用方式，並非古柯鹼(cocaine)，故本敘述錯誤。古柯鹼可致缺血性中風與腦內出血、海洛因為leukoencephalopathy之代表藥物、海洛因白質病變鑑別關鍵為非高血壓病人合併小腦侵犯、酒精可致灰質與白質皆流失，皆為正確描述。
