#交換 #2018交換 #CH A proved non-small cell lung cancer which is 5.1cm in size with visceral pleural involvement and ipsilateral mediastinal lymphadenopathy. What is the TNM staging? (No definite distant metastasis)
(A) T1N3
(B) T2N2
(C) T3N2
(D) T3N3
??
**Ans: B**
官方依AJCC 7th答B(T2N2)：5.1cm於7th為T2b、同側縱膈LN為N2。AJCC 8th中5.1cm(>5cm)為T3→T3N2(答案C)。時效雙層，flag。
