#交換 #2018交換 #CH In patients with cancer treated with VEGF inhibitors (Bevacizumab, sorafenib, sunitinib), which major complications are included?
(A) Bleeding or hemorrhage due to the decreased renewal capacity of endothelial cells.
(B) Thrombosis due to tissue factor activation secondary to exposure to subendothelial collagen.
(C) Both of the aboved.
(D) None of the aboved.
??
**Ans: C**
VEGF抑制劑(Bevacizumab、sorafenib、sunitinib)可同時造成出血(因血管內皮細胞更新能力下降)與血栓(因暴露內皮下膠原致組織因子活化)兩種看似矛盾的併發症，兩者皆為主要併發症。VEGF抑制劑治療其他常見胸腔併發症尚包括肺栓塞與氣胸。
