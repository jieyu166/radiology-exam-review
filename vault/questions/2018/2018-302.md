#交換 #2018交換 #MSK Which one of the following radiographic features on radiography is NOT usually seen in patients with hyperthyroidism? (應該是問hyperparathyrodism)
(A) Subperiosteal resorption of phalanges
(B) Intracortical resorption of long bones
(C) Cystic-like radiolucent lesions in bones
(D) Button sequestrum in skull vault
??
**Ans: D**
鈕扣狀死骨(button sequestrum)——溶骨性病灶內殘存之中央骨密度區，周圍環繞透亮帶——典型見於骨髓炎(osteomyelitis)與嗜酸性肉芽腫(eosinophilic granuloma)，偶見於纖維肉瘤與淋巴瘤，並非副甲狀腺機能亢進(hyperparathyroidism)之典型表現，故本敘述錯誤。指骨骨膜下骨吸收、長骨皮質內骨吸收、骨內囊狀透亮病灶(棕色瘤)，皆為副甲狀腺機能亢進之正確典型表現。
