#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Features of abdominal wall hernia, which one is **wrong**?
(A) Inguinal hernia is the most common type
(B) Femoral hernias are more common than inguinal hernia
(C) Umbilical hernia are particularly common in women
(D) Parastomal hernias are subtype of incisional hernia
??
**Ans: B**
腹股溝疝為最常見的腹壁疝型別，股疝反而遠少於腹股溝疝，故「股疝較腹股溝疝常見」為錯誤敘述。臍疝好發女性、造口旁疝屬 incisional hernia 亞型皆為正確敘述。
