#交換 #2018交換 #Unknown 下列關於 Rheumatoid arthritis 及 Seronegative spondyloarthropathy (SPA)的影像學比較，SPA 較常發生的是：
(A) Osteoporosis
(B) Subcutaneous nodule
(C) Articular pannus formation
(D) Enthesitis
??
**Ans: D**
An enthesis is the point where a ligament or tendon attaches to bone. (The plural form is entheses.)
Enthesopathy (en-THEE-sawp-a-thee) is the medical name for any disease or disorder affecting an enthesis.
