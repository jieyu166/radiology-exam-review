#交換 #2018交換 #ABD During MRCP, which substance may be administered to improve visualization of the pancreatic ducts?
(A) glucagon
(B) secretin
(C) cholecystokinin
(D) gastrin
(E) Buscopan
??
**Ans: B**
Secretin刺激胰管細胞分泌碳酸氫鹽液體使胰管充盈顯影。glucagon/Buscopan為抗蠕動劑、cholecystokinin刺激膽囊收縮、gastrin刺激胃酸，皆非使胰管顯影。
