#交換 #2018交換 #NR Which one of the following intracranial tumor is the most likely to see in Von Hippel–Lindau Disease?
(A) Hemangioblastoma
(B) Medulloblastoma
(C) Lymphoma
(D) Ependymoma
??
**Ans: A**
血管母細胞瘤(hemangioblastoma)為VHL疾病最典型之中樞神經系統腫瘤，好發於小腦、腦幹、脊髓；可為多發性。Medulloblastoma、lymphoma、ependymoma皆非VHL之特徵性顱內腫瘤。
