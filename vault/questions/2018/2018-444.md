#交換 #2018交換 #Physics Which sites would have greatest concentration of gadolinium deposition?
(A) Globus pallidus
(B) Dentate nucleus
(C) Thalamus
(D) Pons
??
**Ans: B**
**Dentate nucleus（B）**——釓腦沉積程度依序為 dentate nucleus > globus pallidus > thalamus > pons，以齒狀核濃度最高。
