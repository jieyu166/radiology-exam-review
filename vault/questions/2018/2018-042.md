#交換 #2018交換 #CH 關於 lymphangioleiomyomatosis,下列何者正確？
(A) 好發於育齡婦女
(B) Will not involve bone
(C) numerous randomly scattered thin-walled lung cysts surrounded by normal lung parenchyma為主要的CT影像表現
(D) 可能會反覆發生pneumothorax或chylothorax
(E) 可能與tuberous sclerosis有關
??
**Ans: B**
May show massive osteolysis with little or no periosteal reaction. Often multifocal disease. Ref: Radiology review manual 6th ed. P.508.  [Lymphangioleiomyomatosis](https://radiopaedia.org/articles/lymphangioleiomyomatosis-1)
