#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Which of the followings about hepatic carcinoid tumors is **wrong**?
(A) Primary hepatic carcinoid tumors are rare.
(B) Liver is the most common site of carcinoid metastasis
(C) Female is more common than male for acquiring primary carcinoid tumors
(D) Primary carcinoid tumors have poorer prognosis than hepatocellular carcinoma.
??
**Ans: D**
原發性肝臟類癌腫瘤(primary hepatic carcinoid tumor)之預後「較」肝細胞癌(HCC)為佳(非較差)，此為本題錯誤敘述。原發性肝臟類癌極罕見、肝臟為類癌轉移最常見部位、女性好發皆為正確描述。
