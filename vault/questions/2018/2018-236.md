#交換 #2018交換 #CH Regarding scimitar syndrome, which of the following is **false**?
(A) A subtype of partial anomalous pulmonary venous return
(B) Scimitar vein joins inferior vena cava
(C) Prominent right atrium.
(D) Scimitar vein in left medial costophrenic sulcus
??
**Ans: D**
彎刀症候群(scimitar syndrome)之彎刀靜脈位於「右側」內側肋膈角(right medial costophrenic sulcus)，非左側，故本敘述錯誤。本病為部分肺靜脈異位回流之亞型、彎刀靜脈注入下腔靜脈、常合併右心房突出，皆為正確描述。
