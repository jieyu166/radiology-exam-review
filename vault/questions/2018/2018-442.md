#交換 #2018交換 #Physics Oral versus intravenous fluid expansion for CIAKI (contrast induced acute kidney injury), which is **wrong**?
(A) Guidelines for prevention of CIAKI: volume expansion by rigid long-term saline infusions.
(B) Intravenous infusions should start 6–12 h before the intervention and be maintained for 4 h after the intervention.
(C) Out patient: can not undergo such protocols → oral hydration.
(D) Oral hydration is at least equally effective as IV saline for preventing CIAKI.
(E) None
??
**Ans: E**
**None（E）**——四項敘述皆正確：CIAKI 預防以生理食鹽水靜脈擴容(A)、靜脈輸液術前 6–12 h 開始並維持術後 4 h(B)、門診病人可改口服水化(C)、口服水化在預防 CIAKI 上至少與靜脈生理食鹽水同等有效(D)。故「何者錯誤」答案為「以上皆非（none）」。
