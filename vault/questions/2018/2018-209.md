#交換 #2018交換 #Physics Which the following one is incorrect in description of AKIN definition of Acute Kidney Injury within 48 hours after a nephrotoxic event?
(A) Absolute serum creatinine increase ≥0.3 mg/dL (>26.4 μmol/L)
(B) A percentage increase in serum creatinine ≥50% (≥1.5-fold above baseline).
(C) Urine output reduced to ≤0.5 mL/kg/hour for at least 6 hours.
(D) All of the above are correct
??
**Ans: D**
AKIN(Acute Kidney Injury Network)診斷標準為48小時內符合下列任一項：肌酸酐絕對值上升≥0.3 mg/dL、或相對上升≥50%(≥1.5倍基礎值)、或尿量≤0.5 mL/kg/hr達6小時以上，三者皆為AKIN定義之診斷條件，故皆正確。
