#交換 #2018交換 #CH A CT scan with findings of a 4.3 cm nodule at right upper lobe with a contralateral 2.0 cm hilar lymphadenopathy and multiple metastatic bilateral pleural nodules with bilateral pleural effusion and one metastatic nodule at left adrenal gland. Which of the following clinical TNM classification according to AJCC 8th edition is correct for the above description?
(A) T2bN2M1b
(B) T2aN2M1a
(C) T2bN3M1b
(D) T3N2M1b
??
**Ans: C**
4.3 cm 結節落於 >4–5cm 區間為 T2b；對側肺門淋巴結腫大依規則一律為 N3（非 N2）；雙側肋膜多發轉移結節/積液符合 M1a，另有左腎上腺單一轉移符合 M1b。
> [!warning] 官方詳解本題答案欄僅標示「A C?」（存疑未定案）。依 N 分期邏輯（對側肺門淋巴結應為 N3）僅 C 選項符合；惟同時具 M1a 與 M1b 特徵時分期表未列對應單一類別,暫依此邏輯記為 C,待醫師對照原始 AJCC 8th 規則與原始考卷確認。
