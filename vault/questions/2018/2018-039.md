#交換 #2018交換 #CH Which of the following description about interstitial pneumonitis is **WRONG**?
(A) LIP presents as ground-glass opacity, poorly defined centrilobular nodules, thin-walled cysts and air trapping. More common in younger patients.
(B) UIP presents as peripheral, basal, subpleural reticulation and honeycombing and ground-glass opacity.
(C) RB-ILD are more common in non-smoling middle aged females.
(D) DIP is usually characterised by diffuse ground-glass opacities, which correlate histologically with the spatially homogeneous intra-alveolar accumulation of macrophages and thickening of alveolar septa.
??
**Ans: C**
RB-ILD: 幾乎都有抽菸，且男性較多。參考 [[bronchiolitis]]
A. 年輕人要想到AIDS，Sjögren's syndrome
B. 診斷criteria Prominent GGO會被放在alternative Dx, GGO只能mild
D. DIP分布 雙側對稱(86%), basal and peripheral (60%)。而patchy, diffuse 各佔20%。典型表現是diffuse GGO。
