(A) Abduction injury to the first metacarpophalangeal joint
(B) May injure the ulnar collateral ligament
(C) Stener lesion means ulnar collateral ligament is retracted proximally and displaced superficial to the abductor aponeurosis
(D) Stener lesion prevents healing and lead to chronic laxity, loss of grip, and degenerative joint disease.
??
**Ans: C**
