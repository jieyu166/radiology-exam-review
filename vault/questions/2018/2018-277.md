#交換 #2018交換 #PED Which one of the followings related to abusive children is correct?
(A) Physically abused children, particularly infants, may present with nonspecific symptoms and signs, such as vomiting or apnea.
(B) Physical findings, such as bruising of the face, neck, or torso, or intraoral lesions, such as torn frenula, in infants should not arouse suspicion of non-accidental trauma.
(C) The evaluation of infants and young children for suspected inflicted trauma should include imaging of the brain and regional radiographs of symptomatic extremities.
(D) the shaken-baby syndrome are attributable to shaking alone
??
**Ans: A**
受虐兒童(尤其嬰兒)可能以嘔吐、呼吸暫停等「非特異性」症狀表現，此為正確敘述。臉部/頸部/軀幹瘀傷或口腔內損傷(如舌繫帶撕裂)於嬰兒應「高度」懷疑非意外傷害(B錯，非「不應懷疑」)；評估應包含腦部影像與「全身」骨骼調查(skeletal survey)而非僅症狀肢體之局部攝影(C錯)；嬰兒搖晃症候群並非僅歸因於搖晃本身，常合併撞擊等其他機轉(D錯)。
