#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Which of the following regarding struma ovarii is incorrect?
(A) a kind of teratoma
(B) may associate with hyperthyroidism or carcinoid syndrome.
(C) all are benign
(D) mostly are asymtomatic
??
**Ans: C**
The vast majority of strumae ovarii are benign, but malignant disease is found in a small percentage of cases, the most common being papillary thyroid carcinoma.
