#交換 #2018交換 #CV Which of the followings is **not** one of the established risk factors for sudden cardiac death in patients with hypertrophic cardiomyopathy?
(A) Unexplained syncope
(B) Delayed myocardial enhancement
(C) Family history of sudden cardiac death
(D) Nonsustained ventricular tachycardia
??
**Ans: B**

- HCM發生SCM的risk factor:
	- 曾經SCD過，每年猝死機率達10%
	- LV wall>3cm, 有SCD一等親家族史, 非神經性syncope等也很危險
	- 年輕病人，nonsustained VT, LA dilation, LVOT obstruction, 運動時血壓變化異常(如SBP↑不足20mmHg)...
