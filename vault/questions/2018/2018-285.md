#交換 #2018交換 #NR Which one is **WRONG** about idiopathic intracranial hypertension?
(A) Empty or partially empty sella.
(B) Often shows transverse sinus enlargement.
(C) Intraocular protrusion of optic nerve head.
(D) Posterior eye globe flattening.
??
**Ans: B**
原發性顱內壓升高(IIH)常見表現為橫竇「狹窄/血栓/web」(stenosis/thrombosis/web)，而非橫竇「擴大」——硬腦膜靜脈竇擴張反而見於「低顱內壓」情境，兩者方向相反，故本敘述錯誤。空蝶鞍或部分空蝶鞍、視神經頭眼球內突出、後眼球扁平化，皆為IIH正確之影像表現。
