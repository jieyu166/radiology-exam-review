#交換 #2018交換 #Unknown Which one is cerebrovascular malformation without AV shunting?
(A) Dural AV fistula.
(B) Sinus pericranii.
(C) Arteriovenous malformation.
(D) Vein of Galen aneurysmal malformation.
??
**Ans: B**
![ADD Vascular malformation 分類 1](data/images/obsidian/add-vascular-malformation-1-3ca865cf.png)[[Sinus pericranii]] is a cranial venous anomaly in which there is an abnormal communication between **intracranial dural sinuses** and **extracranial venous** structures, usually via an **emissary transosseous vein**.
