#交換 #2018交換 #CH Which of the following is NOT the feature of pulmonary Langerhans cell histiocytosis?
(A) Starfish –shaped nodules are common
(B) Associated with bizarre-shaped cysts
(C) Usually with very large lymphadenopathy
(D) Typically present in young smokers
??
**Ans: C**
HRCT of the Lung, page 3:264-267
- Pulmonary Langerhans cell histiocytosis (PLCH): absence of mediastinal and hilar LAPs
- Pneumocystis pneumonia (PCP): LAP rare (10%), pleural effusions are rare
- lymphangioleiomyomatosis (LAM):  mediastinal /abdominal LAPs
- Lymphocytic interstitial pneumonitis (LIP): mediastinal LAPs
