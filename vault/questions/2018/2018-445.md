#交換 #2018交換 #CH Fleischner Society 2017 guidelines assign lung cancer risk by ACCP categories. Which of the following characteristics is NOT associated with a low risk?
(A) Young age
(B) Less smoking
(C) Location in upper lobe
(D) All above are associated with a low risk
??
**Ans: C**
**Location in upper lobe（C）**——依 ACCP 分類，上肺葉位置為惡性**高風險**因子，非低風險。Young age(A)、Less smoking(B)皆為低風險因子，故 D 不成立。
