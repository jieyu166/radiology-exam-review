#交換 #2018交換 #CV Which of the imaging characteristics of amyloidosis is not **true**?
(A) Nodular parenchymal amyloidosis may manifest as solitary or multiple pulmonary nodules.
(B) The predominant late gadolinium enhancement pattern in amyloidosis is diffuse, subendocardial or transmural delayed enhancement.
(C) Basal and peripheral lung predominance.
(D) Usually, long segment tracheal widening is encountered at imaging.
??
**Ans: D**
長節段氣管狹窄（非增寬）並非典型必見表現；其餘（結節型肺侵犯、心臟瀰漫性/心內膜下或跨壁延遲增強、基底周邊肺分布）皆為正確敘述。
