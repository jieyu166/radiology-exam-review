#交換 #2018交換 #Physics If the frequency of an US wave is doubled, what happens to the period?
(A) Doubles
(B) Halved
(C) Remains the same
(D) 4 times greater
??
**Ans: B**
週期(period)與頻率(frequency)互為倒數關係(T=1/f)，故頻率加倍時週期減半。
