#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Which of the following is **wrong** about retroperitoneal fibrosis?
(A) It's a chronic inflammatory process.
(B) At presentation, about 50% of patients with autoimmune pancreatitis have retroperitoneal fibrosis.
(C) It can cause abdominal aortic aneurysm.
(D) It can cause hydronephrosis.
??
**Ans: B**
自體免疫胰臟炎(autoimmune pancreatitis)患者於診斷時合併後腹膜纖維化之比例約為「20%」(非50%)，故本敘述錯誤。後腹膜纖維化為慢性發炎過程、可導致腹主動脈瘤(炎性腹主動脈瘤IAAA)、可導致腎積水，皆為正確描述。
