#交換 #2018交換 #IR Which of the following about renal trauma is **wrong**?
(A) Renal laceration involved the collecting system is graded at least IV in AAST renal injury scale.
(B) Most urinomas is located within the Gerota fascia.
(C) The term “page kidney” refers to mass effect caused by large subcapsular urinoma.
(D) Posttraumatic renovascular hypertension may occur from a few weeks to decades following the injury.
??
**Ans: C**
Page kidney指大型慢性被膜下血腫(hematoma)壓迫致高血壓，非urinoma，故C錯。腎撕裂入集尿系統≥grade IV、多數urinoma位於Gerota筋膜內、創傷後腎血管性高血壓可數週至數十年後發生皆正確。
