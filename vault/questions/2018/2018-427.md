#交換 #2018交換 #NR Which of the following venous pathways is not a common outflow connection for arterialized flow in patients with carotid-cavernous fistula?
(A) Inferior petrosal sinus
(B) Superficial sylvian cortical veins
(C) Pterygoid venous plexus
(D) Thalamostriate vein
??
**Ans: D**
Thalamostriate vein屬深部腦靜脈系統，非CCF常見外流路徑。inferior petrosal sinus/superficial sylvian cortical veins/pterygoid venous plexus皆為常見外流路徑。
