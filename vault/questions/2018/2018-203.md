#交換 #2018交換 #CH Which the following one is incorrect in description of Marfan syndrome?
(A) Diagnosis of Marfan syndrome based on Ghent criteria which includes cardiovascular (major criteria), ocular (major criteria) and pulmonary (minor criteria) abnormalities
(B) Annuloaortic ectasia and ascending aortic dissection are the two major criteria.
(C) Mitral valve prolapse is the most prevalent valvular abnormality seen in 35-100% of patients
(D) All of the above are correct
??
**Ans: D**
Ghent分類心血管/眼科為主要標準、肺部為次要標準（無獨立肺部主要標準）；環主動脈擴張與升主動脈剝離為兩大主要標準；MVP為最常見瓣膜異常(35-100%)，皆正確。
