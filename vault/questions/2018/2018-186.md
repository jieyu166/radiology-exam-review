#交換 #2018交換 #ABD When using contrast-enhanced US to approach focal liver mass, which of the following will have "portal venous or late phase washout"?
(A) Hemangioma
(B) Focal nodular hyperplasia
(C) Cholangiocarcinoma
(D) Abscess
??
**Ans: C**
Cholangiocarcinoma 屬非肝細胞性惡性病灶，呈快速且強烈的門脈期/延遲期 washout；hemangioma 與 FNH 為良性持續強化。
