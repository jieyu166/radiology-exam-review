#交換 #2018交換 #ABD 關於 Normal pregnancy 與 ectopic pregnancy 的描述下列何者錯誤?
(A) In normal pregnancy, β-HCG level doubles every 48-72 hours until it reaches 10,000-20,000mIU/mL.
(B) If serum β-HCG level was 1600 mIU/mL and no gestational sac within the uterus seen under abdominal ultrasonography, normal pregnancy could be excluded.
(C) Direct sign of ectopic pregnancy on CT or MR is ectopic gestational sac.
(D) Indirect signs of ectopic pregnancy on CT or MR include adnexal hematoma, hemosalpinx and hemoperitoneum.
??
**Ans: B**
腹部超音波判別區約6000-6500 mIU/mL，β-HCG 1600遠低於此門檻，未見妊娠囊不能排除正常懷孕，故B錯。(transvaginal判別區1500-1800)
