#交換 #2018交換 #NR Which one is **wrong** about fibromuscular dysplasia?
(A) ECA(30-50%) > ICA > vertebral arteries(10-20%)
(B) Type I (85%): Classic "string of beads" = medial fibroplasia.
(C) Type II (10%): Long, tubular stenosis = intimal fibroplasia.
(D) Type III (5%): Asymmetric outpouching from 1 side of artery = periadventitial fibroplasia.
??
**Ans: A**
依Olin et al. 2012年447例病人資料，纖維肌肉發育不良(FMD)最常侵犯動脈依序為腎動脈(79.7%)、肋外頸動脈(74.4%)與椎動脈(36.6%)、腸繫膜動脈(26%)、顱內頸動脈(17%)——並非「外頸動脈>內頸動脈>椎動脈」之排序(內頸動脈非典型好發部位)，故本敘述錯誤。Type I(85%，classic string of beads=medial fibroplasia)、Type II(10%，長管狀狹窄=intimal fibroplasia)、Type III(5%，單側不對稱外突=periadventitial fibroplasia)皆為正確描述。
