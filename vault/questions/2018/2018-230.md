#交換 #2018交換 #Physics About contrast media extravasation, which statement is **wrong**?
(A) The use of deep brachial intravenous catheters seems much more safely
(B) May have swelling, tightness, stinging, or burning pain
(C) There is no agreement among physicians on the best treatment of intravenous contrast agent extravasation
(D) Few patients require surgical intervention
??
**Ans: A**
深部肱靜脈導管(deep brachial intravenous catheters)因位置較深、外滲時較不易早期察覺，相對於「較安全」，其外滲風險反而「顯著較高(markedly higher relative risk)」，故本敘述錯誤。外滲可能出現腫脹/緊繃/刺痛/灼熱感、醫師對最佳處置尚無共識、僅少數病人需外科介入，皆為正確描述。
