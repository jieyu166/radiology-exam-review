#交換 #2018交換 #Physics Which one of the followings related to use of contrast medium in breastfeeding women is correct?
(A) ought to discontinue breastfeeding after using all contrast media.
(B) clear encouragement to continue breastfeeding after imaging with the iodine-containging contrast media.
(C) no need to discontinue breastfeeding after imaging with MRI contrast media.
(D) all breastfeeding mothers should temporarily discontinue breastfeeding after contrast media imaging.
??
**Ans: B**
靜脈注射含碘顯影劑後，血液中僅約1%進入母親乳汁，嬰兒消化道中乳汁內含碘對比劑再約1%進入嬰兒血液，故嬰兒實際吸收量僅約母親所接受劑量的0.01%，因此應明確鼓勵哺乳期婦女於注射含碘對比劑後「繼續」哺乳，不需暫停。並非所有顯影劑皆需停止哺乳(A、D錯)；含釓MRI對比劑則有微量(<0.04%)進入乳汁，為避免嬰兒吸入微量含釓對比劑，仍可建議暫停哺乳24小時(C錯，並非完全不需暫停)。
