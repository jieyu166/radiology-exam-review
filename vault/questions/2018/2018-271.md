#交換 #2018交換 #PED Which of the following is NOT a typical ultrasound finding of intussusception?
(A) Coil spring appearance
(B) Target sign
(C) Pseudokidney sign
(D) Mural vascularity in intussusceptum
??
**Ans: A**
「Coil spring appearance（彈簧狀外觀）」為鋇劑灌腸(barium enema)之典型影像描述，非超音波典型表現。超音波典型表現為靶徵(target sign)、假腎徵(pseudokidney sign)、套入腸段壁血流(mural vascularity in intussusceptum，反映腸段是否仍存活)。
