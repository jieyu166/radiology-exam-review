#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Which of the following is NOT correct about Krukenberg tumor?
(A) Metastases to ovary from signet ring cell adenocarcinoma
(B) Most common from stomach
(C) Often involve bilateral ovaries
(D) Predominantly cystic ovarian tumors
??
**Ans: D**
Krukenberg腫瘤之多數影像表現非特異性，主要呈「實質為主」或「囊實混合」腫塊，並非「以囊性為主」，故本敘述錯誤。轉移自印戒細胞腺癌至卵巢、最常見原發部位為胃、常侵犯雙側卵巢，皆為正確描述。
