#交換 #2018交換 #Unknown Which of the following findings suggests colon cancer rather than diverticulitis?
(A) Regional lymphadenopathy
(B) Halo sign
(C) Pericolonic inflammatory change
(D) Ascites
(E) Mural thickening
??
**Ans: A**
Regional lymphadenopathy is more commonly seen with cancer than with diverticulitis. Halo sign is indicative of diverticulitis, and mural thickening and pericolonic inflammatory change can be seen in both cancer and diverticulitis.
Ascites is not a discriminator.
