#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Which of the following descriptions of Meckel diverticulum is **wrong**?
(A) approximately 100 cm from the ileocecal valve
(B) at the mesenteric side of ileum
(C) is the remnant of embryologic omphalomesenteric duct
??
**Ans: B**
antimesenteric.
A memory aid is the rule of 2s:
- 2% (of the population)
- 2 feet (proximal to the ileocecal valve) 60-100cm
- 2 inches (in length) 3-6cm
- 2 types of common ectopic tissue (gastric and pancreatic)
- 2 years is the most common age at clinical presentation
- 2:1 male:female ratio
