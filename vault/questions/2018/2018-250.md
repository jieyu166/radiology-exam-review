#交換 #2018交換 #NR About pituitary gland, which is incorrect?
(A) Physiologic pituitary enlargement may be caused by puberty or pregnancy/postpartum state.
(B) Pituitary gland consists of ventral adenohypophysis and dorsal neurohypophysis.
(C) In T2-weighted image, hyperintense signal is usually observed at neurohypophysis.
(D) In T1-weighted image, the signal of the normal anterior pituitary lobe appears isointense to pons on sagittal images.
??
**Ans: C**
神經下垂體(neurohypophysis)之高訊號「亮點(posterior pituitary bright spot)」典型出現於「T1加權」影像（非T2），反映後葉神經分泌顆粒(vasopressin相關)，故本敘述錯誤。生理性腦垂體增大可見於青春期或懷孕/產後、腦垂體由腹側腺垂體與背側神經垂體組成、T1上正常前葉訊號與橋腦等訊號，皆為正確描述。
