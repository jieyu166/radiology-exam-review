#交換 #2018交換 #Unknown Which of the following statements about sonography artifact is **wrong**.
(A) **Tissue harmonic imaging** is helpful with large body habitus or “noisy” tissues such as an echogenic liver.
(B) **Reverberation** is useful in creating the “comet tail” artifact characteristic of adenomyomatosis of the gallbladder wall.
(C) Color Doppler can be used to accentuate the **reverberation** effects of adenomyomatosis.
(D) **Acoustic shadowing** is the result of nonuniformity of the ultrasound beam at the periphery of the transducer.
(E) **Mirror image** artifact is the result of the ultrasound beam “bouncing” off two strong specular reflectors before returning to the transducer.
??
**Ans: D**
A. Yes. 胖的人要開harmonic
BC. Yes. ![ADD GB adenomyomatosis 7](data/images/obsidian/add-gb-adenomyomatosis-7-b9f93a01.png)
D. **錯誤**.
- Side lobes: result of **nonuniformity** of the ultrasound beam at the **periphery** of the transducer. ![ADD Side lobe artifact 2](data/images/obsidian/add-side-lobe-artifact-2-2946608f.png)
- Acoustic shadowing: A signal void behind structures that strongly absorb or reflect ultrasonic waves. This happens most frequently with solid structures, such as in bone or stones.  通常是物質有強烈吸收/反射，以致後方無訊號
