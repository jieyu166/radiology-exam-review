#交換 #2018交換 #MSK Which of the following statement regarding "Bizarre Parosteal Osteochondromatous Proliferation" is incorrect?
(A) Is a malignant tumor
(B) Also known as Nora lesion.
(C) Medullary continuity between the normal bone is uncommon.
(D) Most lesions are asymptomatic, but may progressively enlarge.
??
**Ans: A**
BPOP(Bizarre Parosteal Osteochondromatous Proliferation)為罕見「良性」病灶(非惡性)，屬局部侵襲性且常復發之骨膜性骨軟骨性外生骨疣，源自皮質，故本敘述錯誤。又稱Nora lesion、與正常骨之髓腔連續性不常見、多數病灶無症狀但可漸進性增大，皆為正確描述。
