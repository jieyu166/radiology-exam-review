#交換 #2018交換 #Unknown A 50 years old man presents with right side **hearing loss**. The head MRI shows a **mix** solid and cyst component mass at cerebellopontine angle and extending into the internal acoustic canal. Enhancement of solid component is noted after contrast administration. Which one of the following is the most likely diagnosis?
(A) Vestibular schwannoma
(B) Arachnoid cyst
(C) Meningioma
(D) Hemagioma
??
**Ans: A**
