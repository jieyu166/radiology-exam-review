(A) Calcineurin inhibitors can cause posterior reversible encephalopathy syndrome (PRES)
(B) Cocaine and amphetamines can cause PRES as well
(C) The side effects of phenytoin include skull bone thinning and cerebellar atrophy
(D) Subacute combined degeneration of spinal cord can be caused by nitrous oxide during dental procedures
(E) The complications of long-term total parenteral nutrition mainly are due to deposition of manganese
??
**Ans: C**
Both cerebellar atrophy and hyperostosis of the skull can be seen with phenytoin use.
