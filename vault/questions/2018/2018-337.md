#交換 #2018交換 #MSK Which one is not for evaluation and measurement for developmental dysplasia of hip?
(A) Meary's angle
(B) Hilgenreiner's line
(C) Center-edge angle
(D) Slope of lateral edge of acetabulum
??
**Ans: A**
Meary's angle為足部(1st metatarsal與talus軸線)測量方法，非DDH評估項目。Hilgenreiner's line、center-edge角、acetabulum外側緣斜率皆為DDH標準評估測量項目。
