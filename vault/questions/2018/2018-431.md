#交換 #2018交換 #GU About testicular cancer, which one is **correct**?
(A) Testicular seminoma is the most common subtype of testicular cancer.
(B) Testicular seminoma is highly curable even at advanced stages. The most common stage at presentation is stage I disease.
(C) At Doppler US, seminoma demonstrates increased vascularity compared with adjacent normal testis.
(D) All of the above.
??
**Ans: D**
**All of the above（D）**——精細胞瘤（seminoma）為睪丸癌最常見亞型（A對）；即使晚期仍高度可治癒，最常見表現期別為第一期疾病（B對）；杜卜勒超音波上相對於鄰近正常睪丸組織呈血流增加（C對），三者皆正確。
