#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Which of the following statements about ultrasound of the gallbladder is incorrect.
(A) Thickening of the gallbladder wall alone should be the specific diagnosis of acute cholecystitis
(B) Congestive heart failure can cause gallbladder wall thickening.
(C) Flow can be absent on color doppler sonography of gallbladder cancer.
(D) Choledocholithiasis is the most common cause of bile duct dilatation in patients with gallstones..
(E) The ultrasound finding of adenomyomatosis is comet tail artifact caused by reverberation from crystals trapped within hypertrophied Rokitansky-Aschoff sinuses.
??
**Ans: A**
單獨膽囊壁增厚並「非」急性膽囊炎之特異性診斷依據——瀰漫性膽囊壁增厚可見於肝炎、肝硬化/門脈高壓、腹水、低白蛋白血症、鬱血性心衰竭等多種非膽囊本身病因，故本敘述錯誤。鬱血性心衰竭可致膽囊壁增厚、膽囊癌之彩色都卜勒可無血流訊號、總膽管結石為膽結石病人膽管擴張最常見原因、腺肌症之comet tail偽影源自Rokitansky-Aschoff竇內結晶之殘響，皆為正確描述。
