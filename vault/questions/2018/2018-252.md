#交換 #2018交換 #PED A 6-day newborn presents with abdominal distention, bilous emesis and blood streaked stools. CT scan shows small bowel and colon distention, bowel wall thickening and "thumbprinting", pneumatosis intestinalis also noted, what's the most likely diagnosis?
(A) Hirschsprung disease
(B) Familial adenomatous polyposis syndrome
(C) Intussusception
(D) Necrotizing enterocolitis
(E) Peutz-Jehger syndrome
??
**Ans: D**
新生兒腹脹合併膽汁性嘔吐、血絲便，CT見小腸與結腸擴張、腸壁增厚合併「thumbprinting」及腸壁積氣(pneumatosis intestinalis)，為壞死性腸炎(necrotizing enterocolitis, NEC)之典型表現。Hirschsprung disease好發便秘非血便；FAP、Peutz-Jeghers為息肉症候群非新生兒急症；腸套疊雖可有血便但通常無廣泛腸壁積氣。
