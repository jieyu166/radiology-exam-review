#交換 #2018交換 #CV About coronary artery aneurysm, which statement is **true**?
(A) True aneurysm: vessel wall composed of three layers (adventitia, media, and intima)
(B) Saccular aneurysm: transverse > longitudinal diameter
(C) Giant aneurysm: > 20mm-150mm in diameter of adults
(D) All of above
??
**Ans: D**
真性動脈瘤血管壁由外膜/中膜/內膜三層構成、囊狀動脈瘤橫徑大於縱徑、成人巨大型冠狀動脈瘤定義為直徑>20mm(2公分)，皆為正確描述。
