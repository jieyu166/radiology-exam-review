#交換 #2018交換 #NR Which one is **WRONG** about paraneoplastic syndromes of the brain?
(A) Immune-mediated by autoantibodies or cytotoxic T cell-related mechanisms.
(B) Limbic encephalitis is most common clinical paraneoplastic syndrome.
(C) About 10% of patients with systemic cancers develop paraneoplastic syndrome.
(D) Most common tumor is small cell lung carcinoma.
??
**Ans: C**
副腫瘤症候群(paraneoplastic syndrome)發生於高達「15%」（非10%）之癌症病人，故本敘述錯誤。機轉為自體抗體或細胞毒性T細胞相關之免疫媒介反應、邊緣系統腦炎(limbic encephalitis)為最常見之臨床副腫瘤症候群、最常見相關腫瘤為小細胞肺癌，皆為正確描述。
