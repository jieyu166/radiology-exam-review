#交換 #2018交換 #MSK Stener lesion is when the ulnar collateral ligament of thumb is superficial to _____, which may need surgery
(A) Radial collateral ligament
(B) Adductor aponeurosis
(C) Extensor hood
(D) Flexor pollicis longus
??
**Ans: B**
