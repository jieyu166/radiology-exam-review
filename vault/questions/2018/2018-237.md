#交換 #2018交換 #NR Classical findings of intracranial hypotension include the following except?
(A) Downward displacement of brain through incisura ("slumping" midbrain)
(B) Diffuse dural thickening/ enhancement
(C) Veins/ dural sinuses shrinkage
(D) Subdural hygromas/ hematomas
??
**Ans: C**
顱內低壓典型影像所見助記為「SEEPS」：Subdural fluid collections、Enhancement of the pachymeninges、Engorgement of the venous sinuses（靜脈竇「充血擴大」，非「萎縮」）、Pituitary hyperemia、Sagging brain。靜脈/硬膜竇為擴大充血而非皺縮，故本敘述錯誤；腦幹經天幕裂孔下垂("slumping")、瀰漫性硬膜增厚強化、硬膜下積液/血腫皆為典型表現。
