#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Which of the following is NOT correct about Meigs syndrome?
(A) Presence of ascites and pleural effusion
(B) Associated with ovarian tumors
(C) Presence of peritoneal nodules
(D) Tumor is typically hypointense on T2WI
??
**Ans: C**
Meigs症候群為良性病程，「不」合併腹膜結節(peritoneal nodules，該表現提示惡性/腹膜轉移)，故本敘述錯誤。合併腹水與肋膜積液、與卵巢腫瘤(典型為fibroma)相關、腫瘤(fibroma)因富含膠原纖維組織於T2WI典型呈低訊號，皆為正確描述。
