#交換 #2018交換 #ABD A 40-year-old man with hyperpigmentation, arthalgia and diabetes mellitus is clinically suspected to have primary hemochromatosis. What are the most likely findings on liver MRI in this condition?
(A) normal appearances of the liver
(B) decreased signal intensity on T1W and T2W images
(C) decreased signal intensity on T1W and increased signal intensity on T2W images
(D) increased signal intensity on T1W and decreased signal intensity on T2W images
(E) increased signal intensity on T1W and T2W images
??
**Ans: B**
