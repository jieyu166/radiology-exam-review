#交換 #2018交換 #Physics What statement of Iodinated contrast media adverse reactions is **wrong** :
(A) Anaphylatic type reaction may occur for < 1% in cases.
(B) Risk factors included asthma, anxiety and previous allergic reaction.
(C) Severe reactions that usually represent a progression of the moderate symptoms and are life-threatening.
(D) Delayed reaction happened after 15 minutes after injection.
??
**Ans: D**
