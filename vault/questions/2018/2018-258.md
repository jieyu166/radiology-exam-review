#交換 #2018交換 #CV Which of the following statements does NOT apply?
(A) Myocardial edema affects both myocardial T1 and T2.
(B) The presence of additional water leads to an increase in T2 and a decrease in T1.
(C) Myocardial edema accompanies both acute ischemic and acute inflammatory myocardial injury.
(D) Increases in myocardial T1 are not specific to myocardial edema.
??
**Ans: B**
心肌水腫(edema)造成組織內額外水分增加，會使T2「與T1皆上升」（非T2上升同時T1下降），故本敘述錯誤。心肌水腫同時影響T1與T2、急性缺血性與急性發炎性心肌損傷皆可合併水腫、心肌T1上升並非水腫特異性表現(纖維化/類澱粉沉積等間質擴張亦可致T1上升)，皆為正確描述。
