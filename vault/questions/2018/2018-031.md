#交換 #2018交換 #CH Which is the **least** encountered type of constrictive bronchiolitis?
(A) bronchiolitis obliterans syndrome
(B) rheumatoid arthritis
(C) inflammatory bowel disease
(D) idiopathic constrictive bronchiolitis
??
**Ans: C**
Aside from bronchiolitis obliterans syndrome, rheumatoid arthritis and idiopathic constrictive bronchiolitis are the most commonly encoun-tered types of constrictive bronchiolitis.
