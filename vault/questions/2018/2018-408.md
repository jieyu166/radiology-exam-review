#交換 #2018交換 #MSK 下列關於Sclerosing Bone Dysplasias何者錯誤?
(A) Osteopoikilosis shows numerous small foci of sclerotic bone in medullary cavity
(B) Osteopathia striata shows linear pattern of medullary sclerosis and adjacent soft tissues
(C) Melorheostosis means bone formation is along the cortex and/or adjacent soft tissues
(D) The three type of Sclerosing Bone Dysplasias mention above can appear together in mixed patterns
??
**Ans: B**
Osteopathia striata僅呈線狀髓質硬化條紋，不侵犯鄰近軟組織(軟組織受累為melorheostosis特徵)，故B錯。
