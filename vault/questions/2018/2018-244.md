#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Which of the following statements about ultrasound of the kidney is incorrect.
(A) Autosomal dominant polycystic kidney disease has a characteristic sonographic appearance of enlarged kidneys with multiple cysts of variable sizes.
(B) Simple cysts have characteristic features of well-defined, anechoic round structures that produce posterior acoustic enhancement..
(C) Typical sonographic appearance of an angiomyolipoma is a well-defined echogenic mass within the renal cortex.
(D) If an echogenic mass has a hypoechoic halo, AML is more likely than RCC.
(E) RCC can be solid or cystic in ultrasound
??
**Ans: D**
超音波上迴音性腫塊若合併「低迴音暈輪(hypoechoic halo)」，「腎細胞癌(RCC)較AML更可能」（非AML較可能），此敘述錯誤——低迴音暈輪常反映假包膜(pseudocapsule)或周圍壓迫之腎實質，為RCC較常見之特徵。體染色體顯性多囊腎典型表現、單純囊腫特徵、AML典型呈腎皮質內邊界清楚迴音性腫塊、RCC可為實質性或囊性，皆為正確描述。
