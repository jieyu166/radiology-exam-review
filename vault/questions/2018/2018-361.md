#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Which of the following may cause adrenal hemorrhage?
(A) Blunt abdominal trauma
(B) Adrenal vein sampling
(C) Hypoxia due to prematurity
(D) Underlying adrenal tumor
(E) All of the above
??
**Ans: E**
blunt abdominal trauma(成人單側最常見)、adrenal vein sampling、早產缺氧(新生兒)、潛在腎上腺腫瘤皆可造成腎上腺出血。
