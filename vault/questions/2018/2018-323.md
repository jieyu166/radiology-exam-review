(A) Tis
(B) T1a
(C) Tx
(D) T stage is not necessary because LCIS is considered a risk factor rather than a malignancy.
??
**Ans: A**
LCIS屬原位癌，與DCIS、不伴侵犯之乳頭Paget同歸類為Tis。
