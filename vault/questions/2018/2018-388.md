#交換 #2018交換 #GU About MRI of prostate gland, which of the following is correct?
(A) To define the junction of peripheral zone and transitional zone, T1WI has higher resolution than T2WI.
(B) Focal areas of low intensity in peripheral zone in T2WI are always cancer.
(C) When performing dynamic contrast-enhancing MRI of prostate, fast T1-weighted sequence has higher spatial resolution than slow T1-weighted sequence.
(D) On ADC maps, prostate cancer often shows lower ADC than the surrounding healthy peripheral zone tissue.
??
**Ans: D**
攝護腺癌於ADC map呈較周邊帶健康組織更低之ADC。A(應為T2WI高空間解析度)、B(PZ的T2低訊號不一定是癌)、C(DCE快速T1強調時間解析度非空間解析度)皆為偽。
