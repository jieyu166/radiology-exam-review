#交換 #2018交換 #NR The term "fogging of a cerebral infarction" refers to:
(A) Difficulty in diagnosing an infarction on CT scans within the first 24 hours.
(B) The loss in T2 signal on MR or hypoattenuation on CT during the second week post-ictus.
(C) Diffuse parenchymal enhancement of the brain following transient occlusion a cerebral vessel with reperfusion
(D) It occurs in chronic infarction.
??
**Ans: B**
亞急性期(約發病第2週)梗塞區於CT呈一過性等密度、MR T2訊號一過性減低而看似消失。
