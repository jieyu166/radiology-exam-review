#交換 #2018交換 #CH About pulmonary tuberculosis, which of the following is not **true**?
(A) Primary tuberculosis has radiologic findings include lymphadenopathy, consolidation, pleural effusion, and military nodules.
(B) Postprimary tuberculosis has consolidations that are predominant in the apical and upper lung zones, nodules and cavitation.
(C) Classic nontuberculous mycobacterial infection is characterized by lower lobe cavitary lesions and centrilobular and tree-in-bud nodules.
(D) None of the above.
??
**Ans: C**
典型（空洞型）非結核分枝桿菌感染特徵為上肺葉（非下肺葉）空洞病灶合併中心小葉與樹芽狀結節，影像可與後原發性結核幾乎無法區分。
