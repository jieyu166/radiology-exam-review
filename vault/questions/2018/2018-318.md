#交換 #2018交換 #NR Which disease does not show T1 shortening at basal ganglia
(A) Cirrhosis of liver
(B) Hypoglycemia
(C) Parenteral nutrition use
(D) Hemorrhage
??
**Ans: B**
B: hypoglycemia造成bilateral areas of increased signal on both T2 and FLAIR，侵犯posterior limb of internal capsule、cerebral cortex（尤其parieto-occipital與insula）、hippocampus與basal ganglia，並非T1 shortening；restricted diffusion可為更早期敏感之表現，多可回復。
T1 shortening at basal ganglia之常見原因：肝硬化(manganese沉積)、長期全靜脈營養(manganese)、出血(methemoglobin)等。
