#交換 #2018交換 #NR Which of the following MR imaging technique or image is the BEST for differentiating Epidermoid cyst and Arachnoid cyst located in the cerebellar pontine angle cistern?
(A) T2 FLAIR image
(B) T2 Fast spine echo image
(C) IV Gd contrast enhancement
(D) DWI (diffusion weighted imaging)
??
**Ans: D**
epidermoid cyst於DWI呈高訊號(限制擴散)，arachnoid cyst隨CSF無限制擴散，DWI為兩者鑑別最佳序列。
