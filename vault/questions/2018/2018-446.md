#交換 #2018交換 #CH Which of the following combination of “Radiological sign – Possible etiology” is incorrect?
(A) Hilum convergence sign – Sarcoidosis
(B) Luftsichel Sign – LUL collapse
(C) Cervicothoracic sign – Intrathoracic goiter
(D) Comet tail sign – Round atelectasis
??
**Ans: A**
**Hilum convergence sign – Sarcoidosis（A）**不正確——hilum convergence sign 用於區分擴大肺動脈與縱膈腫塊（對應**肺動脈擴大**），非 sarcoidosis。Luftsichel–LUL collapse(B)、cervicothoracic–intrathoracic goiter(C)、comet tail–round atelectasis(D)皆正確。
