#交換 #2018交換 #NR Which is not present in neuropsychiatric systemic lupus erythematosus?
(A) New infarcts
(B) Focal areas of hyperintensity
(C) Extensive, reversible white matter change
(D) Brain abscess
??
**Ans: D**
Brain abscess非ACR NPSLE定義項目(雖可因心臟瓣膜黴菌性栓子續發)。new infarcts/focal hyperintensity/extensive reversible white matter change皆為NPSLE表現。
