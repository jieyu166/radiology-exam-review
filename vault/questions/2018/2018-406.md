#交換 #2018交換 #MSK 下列何者為Kienböck disease在MRI的表現?
(A) Scaphoid, low signal on T1 and T2
(B) Scaphoid, low signal on T1 and high signal on T2
(C) Lunate, low signal on T1 and T2
(D) Lunate, low signal on T1 and high signal on T2
??
**Ans: C**
Kienböck disease為月狀骨(lunate)缺血性壞死，進展期T1與T2皆呈低訊號。Preiser disease才是舟狀骨(scaphoid)AVN。
