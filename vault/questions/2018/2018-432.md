#交換 #2018交換 #GU About PET imaging in prostate cancer, which one is **correct**?
(A) FDG is limited because of the relatively low glucose metabolism of most prostate tumors.
(B) FDG may have a role in response assessment of osseous disease in metastatic castration-resistant prostate cancer and as a prognostic indicator.
(C) 11C-Choline and 18F-choline are sensitive and specific for disease detection in high-risk staging and biochemical relapse after radical therapy in patients with high PSA levels.
(D) All of the above.
??
**Ans: D**
**All of the above（D）**——FDG因多數攝護腺腫瘤葡萄糖代謝相對較低而受限（A對）；FDG於轉移性去勢抗性攝護腺癌骨骼病灶之治療反應評估與預後判斷仍有角色（B對）；11C/18F-choline PET/CT對高風險分期與生化復發偵測敏感且特異（C對），三者皆正確。
