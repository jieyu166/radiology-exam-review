#交換 #2018交換 #Breast Which statement below is incorrect about papillomas?
(A) Solitary papilloma – from a large central duct, common in perimenopausal age, presenting with bloody or clear nipple discharge.
(B) Atypia is more common in central papilloma.
(C) Multiple papilloma – from terminal duct lobular units, peripheral lesions, in younger women, presenting mass.
(D) Papilloma with atypia can be upgraded to malignancy in 67%.
??
**Ans: B**
異型性(atypia)於「周邊型(peripheral)」乳突瘤較中央型(central)乳突瘤更常見（非中央型較常見），故本敘述錯誤。單發性乳突瘤源自大中央導管、好發停經前後年齡、以血性或清澈乳頭分泌物表現；多發性乳突瘤源自終末導管小葉單位、為周邊病灶、好發較年輕女性、以腫塊表現；合併異型性之乳突瘤最終upgrade為惡性之比例可達67%，皆為正確描述。
