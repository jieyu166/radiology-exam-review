#交換 #2018交換 #IR Concerning transhepatic arterial chemoembolization (TACE) of hepatocellular carcinoma, which one of the following is **TRUE**?
(A) TACE is the only treatment option for hepatocellular carcinoma.
(B) TACE will prolong survival compared to systemic infused chemotherapy.
(C) The effectiveness of TACE depends greatly on the choice of drugs used.
(D) Thrombosis of the portal vein will increase the effectiveness of TACE
??
**Ans: B**
TACE 相較全身輸注化療可顯著延長不可切除 HCC 病人的存活期。TACE 並非唯一治療選項（尚有手術/消融/移植/全身治療等）、療效非主要取決於藥物選擇、門脈血栓為相對禁忌而非增效因子。
