#交換 #2018交換 #Physics Which of the following statements about sonography artifact is **wrong**.
(A) Tissue harmonic imaging is helpful with large body habitus or "noisy" tissues such as an echogenic liver.
(B) Reverberation is useful in creating the "comet tail" artifact characteristic of adenomyomatosis of the gallbladder wall.
(C) Color Doppler can be used to accentuate the reverberation effects of adenomyomatosis.
(D) Acoustic shadowing is the result of nonuniformity of the ultrasound beam at the periphery of the transducer.
(E) Mirror image artifact is the result of the ultrasound beam "bouncing" off two strong specular reflectors before returning to the transducer.
??
**Ans: D**
探頭周邊聲束不均勻造成的偽影為「側葉偽影(side lobe artifact)」，並非聲影(acoustic shadowing)——聲影是高衰減/強反射結構後方訊號顯著減弱之現象，本敘述將兩者混淆，故錯誤。組織諧波成像有助於大體型/回音雜訊多的組織(如脂肪肝)、殘響造成腺肌症comet-tail偽影、彩色都卜勒可加強腺肌症殘響效應、鏡像偽影源自聲束於兩強反射面間反彈，皆為正確描述。
