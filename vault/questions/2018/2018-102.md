#交換 #2018交換 #PED A 2 years old child present with iron deficiency anemia. Chest CT shows a 9-cm posterior mediastinal mass with calcification. The both lungs are clear. Which one of the following is most likely the diagnosis.
(A) Neuroblastoma
(B) Lymphoma
(C) Lipoma
(D) Extramedullary hematopoiesis
??
**Ans: A**
2歲小兒缺鐵性貧血合併9公分後縱膈腫塊併鈣化、雙側肺野清晰，最符合神經母細胞瘤(neuroblastoma)。神經母細胞瘤好發後縱膈、常見鈣化，可合併副腫瘤症候群導致缺鐵性貧血相關表現；淋巴瘤通常無鈣化(治療前)；脂肪瘤不會造成貧血；髓外造血(extramedullary hematopoiesis)雖亦好發後縱膈，但鈣化極不典型(atypical)。
