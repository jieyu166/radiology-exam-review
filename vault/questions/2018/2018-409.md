#交換 #2018交換 #GU 關於髓質空洞與尿路攝影顯影，下列何者錯誤?
(A) Medullary cavities resulting from papillary necrosis fill with contrast at retrograde pyelography.
(B) Medullary cavities resulting from papillary necrosis fill with contrast at antegrade pyelography.
(C) Cavities presenting with medullary sponge kidney opacify during retrograde pyelography
(D) Cavities presenting with medullary sponge kidney opacify during antegrade pyelography
??
**Ans: C**
