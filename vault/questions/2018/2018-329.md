#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Which of the following MR contrast agents have significant amount excreted from hepatobiliary system?
(A) Gd-DTPA
(B) Gd-DOTA
(C) Gd-EOB-DTPA
(D) Gd-DO3A
??
**Ans: C**
Gd-EOB-DTPA（gadoxetate disodium）為肝膽特異性對比劑，約50%經肝膽排泄；其餘皆為一般細胞外對比劑，主要經腎排泄。
