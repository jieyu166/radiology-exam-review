#交換 #2018交換 #Unknown Which of the following is **not** the predisposing factor for osteosarcoma?
(A) Paget disease
(B) Fibrous dysplasia
(C) External ionizing irradiation
(D) None of above.
??
**Ans: D**
Paget disease、fibrous dysplasia、體外游離輻射線照射(external ionizing irradiation)皆為已知續發性骨肉瘤之危險因子(predisposing factor)，故「以上皆非」為錯誤敘述(即三者皆為危險因子，非「無」危險因子)。
