#交換 #2018交換 #Unknown Normal red marrow has what appearance on MR images?
(A) Markedly hyperintense to skeletal muscle on both T1-weighted and fluid-sensitive images.
(B) Hypointense to skeletal muscle on both T1-weighted and fluid-sensitive images.
(C) Hyperintense to skeletal muscle on T1-weighted images and iso- or hyperintense to skeletal muscle on fluid-sensitive images.
(D) Hypointense to skeletal muscle on T1-weighted images and hyperintense to skeletal muscle on fluid-sensitive images.
??
**Ans: C**
![ADD red yellow marrow](data/images/obsidian/add-red-yellow-marrow-52dc4768.png)
- The chemical composition of red marrow in young adults is approximately **40% water**, 40% fat, and 20% protein.
- Yellow marrow has fewer hematopoietic components and a greater proportion of fat, being approximately 15% water, **80% fat**, and 5% protein.
- Red marrow characteristically has **higher** signal intensity than yellow marrow on T2-weighted fat-saturated or **STIR** images and **lower** signal intensity on **T1**- weighted images
