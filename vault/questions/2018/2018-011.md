#交換 #2018交換 #Breast Which of the following of Digital mammography(DM) and digital breast tomosynthesis(DBT) is **incorrect** ?
(A) If a cancer is visible on only one view, it is more likely to be visible on the MLO view than the CC view.
(B) Not all cancers are easily detected, so suspicious lesions seen on only one view should be thoroughly evaluated.
(C) Although the false-negative rate of DBT screening is less than that of screening with DM alone, some cancers are still difficult to detect or nondetectable with DBT.
(D) None of the above.
??
**Ans: A**
From : Review of True-Positive and False-Negative Results at Digital Breast Tomosynthesis Screening
A:
If a cancer is visible on only one view, it is more likely to be visible on the CC view than the MLO view.
For example, the axillary tail of the breast is more optimally viewed on the MLO view than the CC view, but the inferior medial breast is less well seen on MLO views. Portions of the breast high on the chest wall, as well as the most lateral portions of the breast, are less well seen on CC views
11% of cancers were seen better or only on the DBT MLO view.
15% of lesions were seen better on the DBT CC view.
