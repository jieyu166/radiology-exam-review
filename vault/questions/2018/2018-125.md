#交換 #2018交換 #Unknown Which of the following statement is **false**?
(A) The anterior division of the renal artery supplies both upper and lower portions of the kidney.
(B) The segmental branches divided into interlobar arteries between the pyramids.
(C) The arcuate arteries anastomose extensively with each other at the base of the pyramids.
(D) The renal vein is anterior to the renal pelvis.
(E) The left renal vein is anterior to the aorta.
??
**Ans: C**
They form individual arcs which do not anastomose.
