#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Regarding the epithelial ovarian tumors, which of the following statement is **false**?
(A) Mucinous cystadenomas tend to be larger than serous cystadenomas at presentation.
(B) Epithelial tumors with low malignant potential demonstrate more proliferation of papillary projections than benign cystadenomas and often seen in younger patients.
(C) The majority of clear cell carcinomas are stage I disease whose prognosis appears to be better than other ovarian cancers.
(D) Psammoma bodies (calcifications) are seen at histologic analysis in up to 30% of malignant serous tumors.
(E) Bilaterality and peritoneal carcinomatosis are seen more commonly in mucinous cystadenomas than serous type.
??
**Ans: E**
雙側性(bilaterality)與腹膜癌病變(peritoneal carcinomatosis)於漿液性(serous)囊腺瘤較黏液性(mucinous)囊腺瘤「更」常見（非黏液性較常見），故本敘述錯誤。黏液性囊腺瘤診斷時通常較大、低度惡性潛能腫瘤乳突增生較良性囊腺瘤多且好發較年輕病人、多數透明細胞癌為stage I且預後較佳、砂粒體見於高達30%惡性漿液性腫瘤，皆為正確描述。
