#交換 #2018交換 #CV Which CT artifacts of coronary CT will not be improved by increased tube potential?
(A) Beam hardening
(B) Metal or streak artifact
(C) Quantum mottle
(D) Slab or banding
??
**Ans: D**
Slab/banding artifact 成因為掃描期間對比劑強化變化，與 tube potential 無關；beam hardening、metal/streak artifact、quantum mottle 皆可由增加 tube potential 改善。
