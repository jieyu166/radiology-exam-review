#交換 #2018交換 #CV What should be the main reason to obtain a chest CT in the setting of possible acute traumatic aortic injury?
(A) Abnormal chest radiograph
(B) Mechanism of trauma
(C) Patient request
(D) Chest pain
??
**Ans: B**
**Mechanism of trauma（B）**——高能量減速傷之受傷機轉本身即為安排胸部CT評估主動脈損傷的主要理由。異常胸部X光(A)、胸痛(D)僅為輔助線索、敏感度特異度不足；病人要求(C)非臨床適應症。
