#交換 #2018交換 #CV Which of the following descriptions is **correct** for hypertrophic cardiomyopathy?
(A) One of the most important diagnostic criteria is a mycaridal thickness equal or more than 15mm in end-systolic phase of cardiac imaging.
(B) The pressure gradient of the left ventricular outflow tract equals two times the peak velocity in meter across the left ventricular outflow tract.
(C) Formation of left ventricular apical aneurysm is associated with good prognosis.
(D) A left ventricular outflow tract pressure gradient equal or more than 50 mmHg may consider septal ablation procedure.
??
**Ans: D**
- A. 在end diastole時測量~~systolic~~
- B. Peak pressure gradient 根據白努力定律= $4*(peak收縮流速)^2$ 。休息> 30mmHg or 運動 > 50mmHg有意義
- C. Poor prognosis
- D. Alcohol ablation indication
	- 有症狀，藥物refractory
	- NYHC class III-IV heart failure
	- LVOT obstruction. 有影像學證據且 Pressure gradient >=50mmHg
