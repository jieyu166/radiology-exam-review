#交換 #2018交換 #NR Which of the following is thought to have different embryonic origin from the rest?
(A) Colloid cyst
(B) Neurenteric cyst
(C) Dermoid cyst
(D) Rathke cleft cyst
??
**Ans: C**
A, B, D為endodermal origin；dermoid cyst為先天性包涵囊(畸胎瘤性、含發育成熟實質組織)，胚胎來源不同。
