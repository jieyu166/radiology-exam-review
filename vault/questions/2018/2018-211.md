#交換 #2018交換 #Physics Which the following one could be the adverse reactions to GI tract water-soluble iodinated contrast media?
(A) Allergic-like reactions
(B) Alterations in thyroid function
(C) Bacteremia
(D) All of the above
??
**Ans: D**
腸胃道水溶性碘顯影劑可能之不良反應包括：過敏樣反應、甲狀腺功能改變(碘吸收所致)、菌血症(灌腸操作相關的暫時性菌血症，文獻報告發生率不一)，三者皆有可能發生。
