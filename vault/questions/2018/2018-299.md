#交換 #2018交換 #MSK Which of the following imaging findings not typical for renal osteodystrophy?
(A) Transverse sclerotic lines paralleling the growth plates.
(B) Salt and pepper appearance of skull.
(C) Bone resorption and cortical tunneling of long bone.
(D) Formation of Brown tumor
??
**Ans: A**
與生長板平行之橫向硬化線為「佝僂病(rickets)」之典型表現，非腎性骨營養不良(renal osteodystrophy)之典型影像特徵，故本敘述錯誤。顱骨鹽和胡椒樣外觀(salt and pepper skull)、長骨骨吸收合併皮質隧道化(cortical tunneling)、棕色瘤(brown tumor)形成，皆為腎性骨營養不良(常合併續發性副甲狀腺機能亢進)之正確典型表現。
