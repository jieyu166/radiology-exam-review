#交換 #2018交換 #CV Which of the following statements applies to ECV and late gadolinium enhancement (LGE)?
(A) LGE is more sensitive to the presence of myocardial fibrosis than ECV.
(B) Areas without hyperenhancement on LGE images always show normal ECV.
(C) The calculation of ECV requires the acquisition of LGE images.
(D) Areas with high ECV might not show hyperenhancement on LGE
??
**Ans: D**
瀰漫性纖維化或微觀性間質纖維化可能無法被LGE偵測，但可於ECV影像上顯示——故高ECV區域未必於LGE呈現過度強化，此為正確敘述。LGE對心肌纖維化之敏感度非優於ECV(A錯)、無LGE過度強化區域未必ECV正常(B錯)、ECV計算不需LGE影像(僅需pre/post-contrast T1 mapping計算R1值，C錯)。
