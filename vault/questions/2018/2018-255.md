#交換 #2018交換 #Breast Which statement below is incorrect about Radial Scar (RS) and Complex Sclerosing Lesions (CSL)?
(A) Radiating scar without atypia usually needs to wide excision.
(B) Benign proliferative lesions characterized a fibro-elastic core containing entrapped ducts with an intact myoepithelial layer and ductules radiating outwards.
(C) A radial scar which larger than 1cm termed as a complex sclerosing lesion.
(D) It may co-exist with benign pathologies, such as cysts, sclerosing adenosis; or atypical hyperplasia, DCIS and early stage invasive carcinoma.
??
**Ans: A**
不具異型性(atypia)之放射狀疤痕(radial scar)通常僅需「保守密切追蹤(conservative close follow-up)」，並非需要廣泛切除(wide excision)，故本敘述錯誤。良性增生病灶特徵為纖維彈性核心包夾陷入之導管、伴完整肌上皮層與放射狀排列之小管、大於1公分稱複合硬化性病灶(CSL)、可與囊腫/硬化性腺病或異型增生/DCIS/早期侵襲癌並存，皆為正確描述。
