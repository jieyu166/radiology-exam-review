#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Which of the following is correct considering androgen insensitivity syndrome?
(A) 46, XX karyotype with female phenotype
(B) Absent or rudimentary uterus
(C) Normal ovary
(D) All of the above are correct
??
**Ans: B**
雄性素不敏感症候群(androgen insensitivity syndrome, AIS，又稱睪丸女性化症候群)之病人子宮通常缺如或呈殘跡狀態(absent or rudimentary uterus)，為正確描述。核型為46,XY(非46,XX)、性腺為雙側隱睪(非正常卵巢)，兩者皆為錯誤描述。
