#交換 #2018交換 #2019交換 #CH An 88-year-old male came to ER for persistent epigastric pain for one week. CT showed diffuse IHD dilatation with obstruction level at liver hilum, diffuse **pancreas** swollen with **sausage** appearance. What’s your impression and next work-up?
(A) Pancreatic head cancer, MRI and CA19-9 further evaluation
(B) Mirizzi syndrome, check sonography and MRI
(C) Klatskin tumor, MRI and surgical exploration.
(D) IgG4-related sclerosing disease, check IgG4 level.
??
**Ans: D**
香腸狀瀰漫腫大胰臟（sausage appearance）＋肝門膽管狹窄致肝內膽管（IHD）擴張，符合自體免疫胰臟炎／IgG4-related sclerosing disease，下一步應檢測血清 IgG4 濃度。非胰頭癌（A）、Mirizzi syndrome（B）或 Klatskin tumor（C）。
