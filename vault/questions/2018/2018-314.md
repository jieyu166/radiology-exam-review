#交換 #2018交換 #ABD 有關於成人腸套疊，下列何者敘述錯誤：
(A) It is often a transient benign incidental finding, largely unappreciated before the widespread availability of CT
(B) Terminal ileum to cecal segment is the most common site of involvement in adults.
(C) Proximal obstruction often accompanies clinically significant intussusception.
(D) If the length of the intussusception measures less than 3 to 5 cm and no proximal dilatation is present in an adult, it is unlikely to be of clinical significance.
??
**Ans: B**
成人腸套疊「無」固定好發部位分布模式——絕大多數病例存在導引點病灶(lead point lesion)，套疊發生位置取決於該病灶所在位置，故「迴腸末端至盲腸段為最常見侵犯部位」之敘述錯誤。常為短暫良性偶然發現(CT廣泛應用前多未被察覺)、臨床顯著套疊常合併近端阻塞、若套疊長度<3-5公分且無近端擴張則不太可能具臨床意義，皆為正確描述。
