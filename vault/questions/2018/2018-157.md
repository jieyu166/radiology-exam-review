#交換 #2018交換 #ABD 下列關於Dual Gradient-Echo In-Phase and Opposed-Phase Hepatic MR Imaging的敘述，何者錯誤?
(A) A dual gradient-echo technique is very helpful in the visualization of T2* effects and allows to detect hemochromatosis or hemosiderosis.
(B) The displayed intensity on opposed-phase images is the absolute value of the difference between fat and water signals. So tissue that consists of mostly fat protons (eg, subcutaneous fat) shows only little or no obvious drop in signal intensity.
(C) Metallic objects demonstrate a larger susceptibility artifact on the image with shorter echo time.
(D) 以上敘述皆正確
??
**Ans: C**
磁化率偽影大小與 TE 成正比（Susceptibility Artifact Size ∝ TE），TE 越長偽影越大，故「較短 TE 偽影較大」為錯誤敘述。
