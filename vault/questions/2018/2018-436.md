#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Which one is NOT correct about GIST (gastrointestinal stromal tumor)?
(A) Large gastric GIST with central necrosis and ulceration should be differentiated with gastric lymphoma.
(B) The common sites of GIST location are stomach > colon > retroperitoneum > mesentery > small intestine.
(C) Lymphadenopathy is uncommon for GIST.
(D) GIST responding to Imatinib therapy shows cystic change with myxoid degeneration and scarring.
??
**Ans: B**
**好發部位排序（B）**錯——正確應為**胃 > 小腸 > 大腸直腸 > 其他 > 食道**，小腸為第二常見，非大腸/後腹腔/腸繫膜。大型胃GIST合併中央壞死潰瘍須與胃淋巴瘤鑑別(A對)、淋巴結轉移少見(C對)、Imatinib反應後可呈囊性變合併黏液樣退化與疤痕化(D對)皆正確。
