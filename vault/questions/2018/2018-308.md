#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Which of the following ultrasonographical finding is **true** when we try to differentiate between epididymitis and testicular torsion?
(A) Both epididymitis and testicular torsion shows enlarged epididymis size
(B) Both epididymitis and testicular torsion shows enlarged testis size.
(C) Both epididymitis and testicular torsion shows increased flow to epididymis.
(D) Both epididymitis and testicular torsion shows increased flow to testis.
??
**Ans: B**
⚠️官方答案標示B，惟頁緣加註「A應該也對」——因扭轉(torsion)本身即會造成睪丸「與」附睪皆腫大(increase in the size of the testis and epididymis)，故理論上A(附睪腫大)與B(睪丸腫大)皆為兩者共同表現，考官對單一正解B表達存疑，建議醫師對照原文獻確認。附睪炎(epididymitis)特徵為附睪血流增加(epididymal PSV>15cm/sec，患側/健側比值>1.7，RI<0.5)；扭轉(torsion)特徵為睪丸與附睪血流皆下降、RI>0.75，兩者血流方向相反，故C、D(血流皆增加)不成立。
