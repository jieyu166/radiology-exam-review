#交換 #2018交換 #NR Which one is correct about colloid cyst?
(A) Most at foramen of Monro.
(B) 2/3 hyperdense on non-enhanced CT.
(C) 2/3 hyperintense on T1WI.
(D) All of above.
??
**Ans: D**
膠樣囊腫(colloid cyst)大多位於Monro氏孔(foramen of Monro)、約2/3於非顯影CT呈高密度、約2/3於T1WI呈高訊號，三者皆為正確描述。
