#交換 #2018交換 #US Ultrasound diagnostic criteria for renal artery stenosis can be divided into direct signs and indirect signs. Which of the following belongs to the direct signs?
(A) Doppler US artifacts caused by turbulence (aliasing)
(B) Pulsus parvus et tardus
(C) Acceleration index < 300 cm/sec²
(D) Acceleration time (time to peak systole) > 70 msec
??
**Ans: A**
因紊流造成的Doppler US假影（aliasing）為狹窄處本身的直接徵象；pulsus parvus et tardus、acceleration index、acceleration time皆為狹窄遠端的間接徵象。
