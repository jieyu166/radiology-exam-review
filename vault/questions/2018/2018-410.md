#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Which uterus anomaly is associated with in utero diethylstilbestrol (DES) exposure?
(A) Didelphys
(B) T-shaped endometrial cavity
(C) Bicornuate uterus
(D) Mayer-Rokitansky Küster-Hauser (MRKH) syndrome
??
**Ans: B**
子宮內DES暴露特徵為T形子宮腔(T-shaped endometrial cavity)。didelphys/bicornuate為融合缺陷、MRKH為Müllerian發育不全，皆非DES相關。
