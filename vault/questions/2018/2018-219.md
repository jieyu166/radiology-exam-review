#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Which of the followings is **wrong** about pheochromocytomas in VHL disease compared with those in general population?
(A) They occur at younger age.
(B) They tend to be multiple lesions.
(C) High proportion of malignancy.
(D) Ectopic site can be at carotid body.
??
**Ans: C**
VHL疾病相關嗜鉻細胞瘤惡性比例「極低」(約1-5%，非高比例，與一般常提及之10%法則相比更低)，此為本題錯誤敘述。VHL相關嗜鉻細胞瘤發病年齡較輕、多發性病灶、可出現異位部位(如頸動脈體)皆為正確描述。
