#交換 #2018交換 #PED Which of the followings is not the medullary neoplasm of adrenal glands in children?
(A) Neuroblastoma
(B) Ganglioneuroblastoma
(C) Teratoma
(D) Ganglioneuroma
??
**Ans: C**
Teratoma為生殖細胞腫瘤，非腎上腺髓質/神經母細胞腫瘤。neuroblastoma/ganglioneuroblastoma/ganglioneuroma皆為neuroblastic(髓質)腫瘤譜系。
