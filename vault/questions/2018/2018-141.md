#交換 #2018交換 #IR Concerning dysfunctional dialysis access grafts, the underlying abnormality is MOST frequently found:
(A) at the arterial anastomosis
(B) mid graft
(C) at the venous anastomosis
(D) involving central veins
??
**Ans: C**
透析用人工血管（AV graft）功能不良最常見的病灶位置是靜脈吻合端（venous anastomosis）的內膜增生狹窄，遠多於動脈吻合端、graft 中段或中心靜脈。
