#交換 #2018交換 #2019交換 #CH Which of the following CT feactures is the most accurate indication of a high risk acute [[Pulmonary embolism]]?
(A) Inferior vena cava contrast reflux
(B) Main pulmonary arterial size > 3.0 cm
(C) Free anterior right ventricular wall > 4 mm thickness
(D) Right ventricle/Left ventricle ratio > 1.0
??
**Ans: D**
正確答案為 **D**。CT 上判斷高風險急性肺栓塞最準確的徵象是 **RV/LV ratio > 1.0**，反映右心室功能障礙，為預測早期死亡率最可靠之CT徵象；主肺動脈徑擴大、右室游離壁增厚（正常應<5mm）等徵象相對較不特異。詳見 [[acute-pe-ct-severity]]。
