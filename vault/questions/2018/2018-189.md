#交換 #2018交換 #CV Which of the advantages for CT imaging in infective endocarditis is not **true**?
(A) When prosthetic material, central lines or devices are present
(B) Complex infections with air or gas
(C) Coronary evaluation or mycotic aneurysms
(D) Complete hemodynamic imaging or repeated follow-up imaging
??
**Ans: D**
完整血流動力學評估或重複追蹤影像是 MRI（而非 CT）在感染性心內膜炎的角色；人工材料存在、含氣感染、冠脈/黴菌性動脈瘤評估皆為 CT 之優勢情境。
