#交換 #2018交換 #MSK Which of the following about osteochondroma is **wrong**?
(A) Cartilage cap
(B) Possible malignant change but rare
(C) Usually stop growing after bone maturity
(D) Most common involved site: epiphysis
??
**Ans: D**
骨軟骨瘤最常好發部位為「幹骺端(metaphyseal region)」，典型背向骨骺生長，並非好發於骨骺(epiphysis)，故本敘述錯誤。具軟骨帽、惡性變機率低但可能發生、骨骼成熟後通常停止生長，皆為正確描述。
