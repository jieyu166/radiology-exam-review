#交換 #2018交換 #CH Which statement of innominate artery compression syndrome artery is **wrong**?
(A) Also known as brachiocephalic artery compression syndrome.
(B) May cause tracheal stenosis in pediatrics.
(C) Suggested surgical repair if tracheal luminal stenosis exceeds 70% in symptomatic patients.
(D) None of above.
??
**Ans: D**
A/B/C皆為正確描述(別名brachiocephalic artery compression syndrome、嬰幼兒可致氣管狹窄、症狀性氣管狹窄>70%建議手術修補)，故答案為D none of above(無錯誤選項)。
