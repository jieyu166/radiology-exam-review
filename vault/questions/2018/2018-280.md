#交換 #2018交換 #PED Which one of the followings about TB infection in pediatric patients is correct?
(A) Children are amongst the low-risk groups for developing tuberculosis worldwide.
(B) Diagnostic and treatment algorithms of tuberculosis for children are similar to those of adults.
(C) Subpleural nodules (“tree-in-bud” appearance) on CT scan are suggestive of active tuberculosis.
(D) Radiologists have an important role to play in early diagnosis of TB infection because bacteriologic confirmation of the disease is often difficult in children
??
**Ans: D**
兒童結核病之細菌學確診(如痰液採檢、培養)常較成人困難，故放射科醫師於早期診斷上扮演重要角色，為正確敘述。兒童實為全球結核病發展之「高」風險族群之一(A錯)；兒童結核病之診斷與治療流程與成人「不」完全相同(B錯)；「tree-in-bud」表現實為「小葉中心性(centrilobular)」結節，非subpleural結節(C錯，此為考點常見混淆處)。
