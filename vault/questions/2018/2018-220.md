#交換 #2018交換 #ABD The retrocaval ureter occurs when the IVC is formed by which of the followings?
(A) right posterior cardinal vein,
(B) right supracardinal vein
(C) right subcardinal vein
(D) vitelline vein
??
**Ans: A**
下腔靜脈正常發育由subcardinal與supracardinal靜脈系統形成，right posterior cardinal vein正常應退化消失；若異常由right posterior cardinal vein殘留參與形成下腔靜脈腎段，輸尿管會被夾在此異常靜脈與正常靜脈之間，形成retrocaval ureter(輸尿管走行於下腔靜脈後方)。
