#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Which of the following about gestational trophoblastic disease (GTD) is correct?
(A) 46 chromosomes in partial hydatidiform mole (PHM), and 69 chromosomes in complete hydatidiform mole (CHM).
(B) Both PHM and CHM are surrounding by myometrium, and myometrium is relatively low signal to the mass on T2WI.
(C) Under ultrasonography, the CHM looks like a cluster of grapes, and some fetal parts may present in it.
(D) Under contrast-enhanced CT, moles are strong hyperintensity mass than myometrium.
??
**Ans: B**
PHM與CHM皆被肌層包繞、肌層於T2WI相對mole呈低訊號。A染色體顛倒(CHM 46/PHM 69)、C(CHM胎兒部分應缺如)、D(CECT mole為低衰減併薄強化分隔非強強化)皆為偽。
