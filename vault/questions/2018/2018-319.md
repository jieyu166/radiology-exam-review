#交換 #2018交換 #NR Which statement about MR spectroscopy is correct?
(A) Lactate resonates at 1.3 ppm chemical shift with a double peak at short TE
(B) N-acetylaspartate (NAA) resonates at 3.0 ppm
(C) Canavan disease shows decreased NAA
(D) Choline is a precursor of acetylcholine (ACH), a component of cell membranes
??
**Ans: D**
A: Lactate為double peak出現在long TE（非short TE），且與lipid band重疊，可用intermediate TE如144ms使lactate反轉以資鑑別，故A錯。
B: NAA共振於2.02 ppm（非3.0 ppm），B錯。
C: Canavan disease為NAA增加（非減少），C錯。
D正確：Choline為acetylcholine（細胞膜成分）之前驅物。
