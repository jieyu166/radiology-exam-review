#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Which of the following is NOT the common complication of ADPKD?
(A) infection
(B) hemorrhage
(C) urolithiasis
(D) adhesion
??
**Ans: D**
ADPKD 常見併發症為囊腫感染、囊內出血、尿路結石；沾黏（adhesion）並非 ADPKD 病理本身的典型直接併發症。
