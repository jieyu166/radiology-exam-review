#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Which description about the pancreatic features of von Hippel–Lindau syndrome is **wrong**?
(A) Pancreatic cysts are the most common finding and usually multiple and asymptomatic.
(B) Serous cystadenomas do not communicate with the pancreatic duct.
(C) VHL-associated pancreatic NETs can be multiple.
(D) VHL-associated pancreatic NETs manifest at an older age compared with that of sporadic NETs.
??
**Ans: D**
VHL相關胰臟神經內分泌腫瘤(NET)好發於「較年輕」年齡(非較年長)，此為與偶發性(sporadic)NET的重要差異，故本敘述錯誤。胰臟囊腫為最常見表現(多發且無症狀)、漿液性囊腺瘤不與胰管相通、VHL相關NET可為多發，皆正確。
