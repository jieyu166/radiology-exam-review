#交換 #2018交換 #Breast On breast MR, which one of the followings will show “rim-enhancement”?
(A) Papilloma
(B) Hemorrhage
(C) DCIS
(D) Invasive carcinoma
??
**Ans: D**
DCIS most commonly appears as non-mass enhancement, most commonly with a
- segmental or linear distribution and clumped or heterogeneous internal enhancement pattern
- clustered ring internal enhancement pattern is most specific for malignancy, usually DCIS
