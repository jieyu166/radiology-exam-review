#交換 #2018交換 #Breast On breast MR, which one of the followings will show “rim-enhancement”?
(A) Papilloma
(B) Hemorrhage
(C) DCIS
(D) Invasive carcinoma
??
**Ans: D**
invasive carcinoma因中心壞死/纖維化+周邊血管新生而呈rim-enhancement。papilloma/DCIS/hemorrhage皆非典型rim enhancement。
