#交換 #2018交換 #IR Regarding to acute upper gastrointestinal tract bleeding, which of the following statement is **true**?
(A) For acute nonvariceal upper gastrointestinal tract bleeding with correctable unstable vital sign, trans-arterial embolization is the first-line treatment.
(B) For acute nonvariceal upper gastrointestinal tract bleeding with stable vital sign, trans-arterial embolization is the first-line treatment.
(C) For acute nonvariceal upper gastrointestinal tract acute rebleeding after endoscopic treatment, trans-arterial embolization is the first-line treatment.
(D) For acute upper gastrointestinal tract bleeding with suspected esophageal varices rupture, endoscopy treatment is the first-line treatment.
??
**Ans: D**
疑似食道靜脈瘤破裂之急性上消化道出血，內視鏡治療為首選第一線治療，為正確描述。生命徵象可矯正之不穩定病人仍應先嘗試內視鏡治療(非TAE為首選，A錯)；生命徵象穩定之非靜脈瘤性上消化道出血首選為內視鏡治療(非TAE，B錯)；內視鏡治療後急性再出血，首選應先「重複內視鏡治療(repeat endoscopy)」，而非直接TAE(C錯)。
