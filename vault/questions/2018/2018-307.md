#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Which of the following statement regarding to nonobstructive mimics of obstructive hydronephrosis is **false**?
(A) Renal AVM could be differentiated from hydronephrosis on Doppler ultrasonography.
(B) Renal sinus cyst could be differentiated from hydronephrosis on delayed phase contrast-enhanced ultrasonography (CEUS).
(C) Staghorn calculus typically accompanys xanthogranulomatous pyelonephritis
(D) Hydronephrosis is commonly seen in pregnant woman in 3rd trimester.
??
**Ans: B**
腎竇囊腫(renal sinus cyst)可模擬水腎症，主要應以CT IVP等排泄性影像鑑別(顯示集尿系統正常或受壓變窄/移位但「未」擴張)，而非以延遲期對比增強超音波(CEUS)鑑別，故本敘述錯誤。腎臟動靜脈畸形可以都卜勒超音波與水腎症鑑別、鹿角狀結石常伴隨黃色肉芽腫性腎盂腎炎、妊娠第三孕期常見水腎症(右側較明顯)，皆為正確描述。
