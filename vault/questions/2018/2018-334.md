#交換 #2018交換 #NR Which is not the factor included in intracranial hemorrhage (ICH) score?
(A) Glasgow Coma Scale score.
(B) Age >/=80 years.
(C) Infratentorial origin of ICH.
(D) Presence of intraventricular hemorrhage.
(E) Location of ICH.
??
**Ans: E**
正式因子為更具體之「infratentorial origin」而非泛稱的「location of ICH」。ICH score五因子：GCS score、ICH volume(≥30cm³)、IVH、infratentorial origin、age≥80，總分0–6。
