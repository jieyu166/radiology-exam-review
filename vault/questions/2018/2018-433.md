#交換 #2018交換 #GU Concerning congenital ureteropelvic junction (UPJ) obstruction, which one of the following is **TRUE**?
(A) It is an uncommon cause of hydronephrosis in children.
(B) Urinary tract infection is the most common presentation.
(C) Females and males are affected equally.
(D) The presence of crossing vessels decreases the success rate of pyeloplasty.
??
**Ans: D**
**The presence of crossing vessels decreases the success rate of pyeloplasty（D）**——crossing vessel 僅見於約 15%–20% 病例，但顯著降低 pyeloplasty 成功率，故多主張術前 CT 評估。A 誤（UPJ obstruction 為兒童 hydronephrosis **最常見**病因）；B 誤（最常以腹部腫塊/側腹痛/成長遲滯表現，UTI 非最常見）；C 誤（男性多於女性約 2:1）。
