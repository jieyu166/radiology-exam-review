#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Which of the following descriptions about Fournier gangrene is **WRONG**?
(A) lethal necrotizing fasciitis of scrotum
(B) usually predisposed with diabetes
(C) CT is essential to identify the extent of disease
(D) In most cases, primary focus of infection could not be identified.
??
**Ans: D**
多數 Fournier gangrene 病例的原發感染源「可被找到」，最常見為肛門直腸來源（perianal fistula/abscess），其次泌尿生殖道或會陰創傷；故「大多數找不到原發灶」為錯誤敘述。
