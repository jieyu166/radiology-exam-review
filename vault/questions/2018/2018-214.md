#交換 #2018交換 #CV Which of the following descriptions about pericardial germ cell tumors is **wrong**?
(A) They arises from pericardium
(B) Most are located anteriorly and to the left
(C) They can be diagnosed in utero.
(D) Calcifications are common seen.
??
**Ans: B**
心包性生殖細胞瘤(pericardial germ cell tumor，多為畸胎瘤teratoma)好發於心包前方偏「右」側，而非偏左側，故「most located anteriorly and to the left」為錯誤敘述；腫瘤源自心包、可產前診斷、常見鈣化(依常見度：多囊性>脂肪>鈣化)皆為正確描述。
