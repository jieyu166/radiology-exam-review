#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Which of the followings is **wrong** about renal cell carcinoma (RCC) in von Hippel-Lindau disease (VHL)?
(A) RCC can arise from renal cysts in VHL disease.
(B) There is no correlation between cyst size and malignancy.
(C) Ultrasound is more sensitive than CT to detect smaller RCC (<2cm).
(D) RCC occurs in VHL disease at a younger age than general population.
??
**Ans: C**
偵測較小腎細胞癌(<2cm)時，CT較超音波更敏感(非超音波較敏感)，此為本題錯誤敘述。VHL疾病中RCC可源自腎囊腫、囊腫大小與惡性度無相關性、VHL之RCC發病年齡較一般族群年輕，皆為正確描述。
