#交換 #2018交換 #CV About anomalous origin of the coronary artery from the opposite sinus(ACAOS) and the risk of sudden cardiac death (SCD), which description is **wrong**?
(A) Left ACAOS is three times more commonly encountered than right ACAOS, but has a weaker association with SCD.
(B) An interarterial course, particularly when associated with an intramural segment, is the abnormality most consistently associated with SCD.
(C) The risk of SCD is minimal to nonexistent for retroaortic and prepulmonic courses.
(D) Patients with ACAOS and interarterial course can be asymptomatic.
??
**Ans: A**
- A. Right ACAOS 比 left ACAOS常見，和sudden cardiac death(SCD)關連較低。
	- Right-ACAOS: 0.92% incidence; Left-ACAOS: 0.15%. (Total incidence: 1.07% for ACAOS)
	- 64 cardiac deaths, 21 (33%) were related to left-ACAOS and that no other CAAs resulted in cardiac death.
Circulation. 2007;115:1296–1305
- B. **Malignant** abnormalies (SCD↑):
	- ALCAPA/ARCAPA(Anomalous LCA/RCA from PA)
	- **Interarterial** course(Aorta, PA之間)
	- **Intramural** course(在aorta壁裡)
- C. Retroaortic, prepulmonic, **septal(subpulmonic)** ... 都是 benign
- D. can be...當然可以
