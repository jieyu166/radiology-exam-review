#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Which of the statement regarding to adnexal tumor is **false**.
(A) When searching for ovary, it is often useful to follow the round ligament away from the tapered cornual region of the uterus because this often points toward the ovary.
(B) The claw sign is not useful in determining whether a mass originates from the ovary or the uterus
(C) Pedunculated lesions from the uterus (usually a fibroid) should have a vascular pedicle extending from the uterus to the mass that can usually be found with imaging.
(D) If ovarian follicles are not visible, or if an abnormality is so large as to distort normal anatomy, tracing the ovarian veins from the inferior vena cava (right) or left renal vein (left) down into the pelvis is often effective.
??
**Ans: B**
爪徵(claw sign)「有助於」判斷腫塊是否源自子宮(而非卵巢)——腫塊與正常子宮肌層間呈爪狀交界，提示子宮來源(如平滑肌瘤)，故「爪徵無助於判斷」為錯誤敘述。尋找卵巢時沿圓韌帶自子宮角錐狀區向外追蹤常指向卵巢、帶蒂之子宮病灶(通常為肌瘤)應有可見之血管蒂連接子宮與腫塊、卵巢濾泡不可見或異常過大扭曲正常解剖時可沿卵巢靜脈(右側自下腔靜脈、左側自左腎靜脈)向下追蹤至骨盆，皆為正確描述。
