#交換 #2018交換 #IR 關於Hereditary haemorrhagic telangiectasia的描述，下列何者為非?
(A) Also known as Osler-Weber-Rendu syndrome.
(B) It may affect mucous membranes, skin, lung, brain, GI tract.
(C) Up to 60% of patients with pulmonary AVMs have hereditary hemorrhagic telangiectasia.
(D) Less than 10% of patients with hereditary hemorrhagic telangiectasia have pulmonary AVMs.
??
**Ans: D**
at least half of HHT patients have pulmonary involvement；實際上至少一半HHT病人有肺部侵犯/PAVM，非少於10%。
