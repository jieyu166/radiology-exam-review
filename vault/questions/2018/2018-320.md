#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Which of the following statement regarding to contrast medium used in liver imaging is **false**?
(A) SPIO uptake by Kupffer cells, hypointense on MRI
(B) Sonazoid microbubbles uptake by Kupffer cells, hyperechoic on US
(C) Gadoxetate disodium (Primovist) uptake by Kupffer cells, high signal on MRI
(D) Extracellular gadolinium-based contrast distributes in liver sinusoid, high signal on MRI
??
**Ans: C**
C為偽：Gadoxetate disodium (Primovist)實際由hepatocytes（非Kupffer cells）經OATP1（organic anion transporting polypeptide-dependent transporter）攝取。
