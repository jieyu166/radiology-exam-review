#交換 #2018交換 #CV Which of the following statements does NOT apply to myocardial T1?
(A) Native myocardial T1 is influenced by both intracellular and extracellular processes.
(B) Postcontrast T1 values are higher than native T1 values of the myocardium.
(C) For extracellular volume (ECV) measurements, postcontrast T1 mapping should be performed at steady state, which can be assumed 10-15 minutes after application of the contrast agent.
(D) The isolated use of postcontrast T1 as a biomarker is not recommended due to its sensitivity to confounders including contrast material dose and time point of postcontrast image acquisition.
??
**Ans: B**
顯影劑注射後(postcontrast)之T1值「較原生(native)T1值為低」（非較高）——顯影劑造成T1縮短(T1 shortening)效應，故本敘述錯誤。原生T1受細胞內外因素影響、ECV量測應於顯影劑注射後10-15分鐘穩態時進行、單獨使用postcontrast T1易受顯影劑劑量與擷取時間點等干擾因子影響故不建議單獨作為生物標記，皆為正確描述。
