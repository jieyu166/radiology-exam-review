#交換 #2018交換 #CV Which of the following abnormalities of right ventricle is **not** in the MR imaging criteria for ARVC (arrythmogenic right ventricular cardiomyopathy) according to 2010 Revised Task Force criteria?
(A) Increased end diastolic volume of right ventricle
(B) Regional right ventricular akinesia
(C) Decreased right ventricular ejection fraction
(D) Increased diameter of right ventricular outflow tract
??
**Ans: D**
依2010 Revised Task Force準則，ARVC心臟MR影像診斷準則(major/minor)以RV EDV(以BSA校正)與RVEF為依據；RVOT diameter僅為2D心臟超音波準則之一部分，非MR準則所採用之參數，故「RVOT徑增加」不在MR診斷準則內。
