#交換 #2018交換 #MSK Which of the following imaging findings not typical for hypophosphatemia? (應該是指Hypophosphatasia)
(A) Diffuse sclerotic changes of bones.
(B) Beaten-copper appearance of skull
(C) Short stature.
(D) Pseudo-fracture of long bone.
??
**Ans: A**
低磷酸酶症(hypophosphatasia)之典型骨骼表現為「瀰漫性骨質疏鬆(generalized/usually irregular osteoporosis)」而非「瀰漫性硬化性變化」，故本敘述錯誤。顱骨敲擊銅樣外觀(beaten-copper appearance，因功能性顱縫早閉、若嬰兒存活顱縫可能永久融合致顱內壓升高)、身材矮小、長骨假性骨折，皆為正確描述。
