#交換 #2018交換 #ABD When performing biliary sonography, what is the anatomical relationship of the following structures at hepatic hilum, from anterior to posterior? 1. Hepatic artery, 2. Main portal vein, 3. Common bile duct.
(A) 321
(B) 312
(C) 123
(D) 213
??
**Ans: B**
肝門處由前至後解剖排列順序為：總膽管(3)在最前方、肝動脈(1)居中、主門脈(2)在最後方，故排列順序為312。
