#交換 #2018交換 #CH 有關 CXR for acute dyspnea，下列何者為非？
(A) 由cardiac size, vessel distribution, pleural effusions 來區分cardiogenic 或 non cardiogenic pulmonary edema.
(B) pneumothorax was more obvious in forced expiratory film.
(C) CXR had important role in diagnosis of pulmonary embolism, usually abnormal.
(D) lateral view was more sensitive than AP view to show pleural effusion.
??
**Ans: C**
CXR 對肺栓塞診斷既非敏感亦非特異，不能單靠 CXR 通常異常來診斷 PE，故本敘述錯誤。
