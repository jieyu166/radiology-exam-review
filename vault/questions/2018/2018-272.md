#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Which of the following is NOT a typical ultrasound finding of chronic cholecystitis?
(A) Thickening of gallbladder wall
(B) Hyperemic changes of gallbladder wall
(C) Contracted gallbladder
(D) Gallstones
??
**Ans: B**
膽囊壁充血性變化(hyperemic changes)較典型見於急性膽囊炎(反映急性發炎充血)，非慢性膽囊炎之典型超音波表現。慢性膽囊炎典型表現為膽囊壁增厚(纖維化，多平滑均勻)、膽囊縮小(contracted)、膽結石(幾乎必有)。
