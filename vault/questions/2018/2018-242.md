#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Which of the following statements about the ultrasound of the liver is incorrect.
(A) No specific sonographic findings are found in acute hepatitis.
(B) Starry sky pattern means increased periportal echoes coupled with decreased parenchymal echogenicity is sensitive and specific for the detection of acute viral hepatitis.
(C) Marked gallbladder wall thickening is sometimes present in patients with acute hepatitis.
(D) Severe fatty infiltration of the liver often results in hepatomegaly and diffusely increased echogenicity.
(E) Flow reversal in portal vein and collateral venous flow is specific finding for liver cirrhosis.
??
**Ans: B**
Starry sky pattern(暗肝配亮門脈)對急性病毒性肝炎之偵測敏感度高但特異度「差」，並非「敏感且特異」，故本敘述錯誤。急性肝炎本身無特異性超音波所見、可合併膽囊壁明顯增厚、嚴重脂肪浸潤常致肝腫大合併瀰漫性回音增加、肝硬化可見門脈血流逆轉合併側枝循環，皆為正確描述。
