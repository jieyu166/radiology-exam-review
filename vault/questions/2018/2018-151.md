#交換 #2018交換 #Breast Complex cystic breast masses 下列何者為非？
(A) Complex cystic breast masses demonstrate both anechoic (cystic) and echogenic (solid) components at ultrasonography.
(B) Common benign findings include fibrocystic changes, intraductal or intracystic papilloma without atypia, and fibroadenoma.
(C) Common atypical findings include atypical ductal hyperplasia, atypical papilloma, atypical lobular hyperplasia, ductal carcinoma in situ and lobular carcinoma in situ.
(D) Malignant findings include, infiltrating ductal carcinoma, and infiltrating lobular carcinoma.
??
**Ans: C**
常見異型（atypical）表現應為 ADH、非典型乳突瘤、ALH、LCIS，不含原位乳管癌（DCIS）；DCIS 應歸類為惡性表現。
