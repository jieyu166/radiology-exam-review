#交換 #2018交換 #PED A neonate presents with vomiting and failure to pass meconium within 24 hour. Which one of the following radiographic features is favoring meconium ileus?
(A) Contrast enema refluxes into the narrow terminal ileum, where pellets of meconium are outlined.
(B) Ultrasound showing target sign.
(C) Contrast enema showing diffusely dilated colon.
(D) KUB showing coffee bean sign.
??
**Ans: A**
胎便性腸阻塞(meconium ileus)典型灌腸攝影可見顯影劑逆流至狹窄的迴腸末端，並勾勒出胎便糰塊(pellets of meconium)輪廓，同時可見未使用之微結腸(microcolon)；target sign為腸套疊之超音波特徵；瀰漫性結腸擴張非本病典型(反因遠端阻塞而呈microcolon)；coffee bean sign為乙狀結腸扭轉之KUB特徵。
