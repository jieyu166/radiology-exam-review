#交換 #2018交換 #Physics Which of the following statements about sonography artifact is **wrong**.
(A) Acoustic enhancement refers to a zone of increased signal seen deep to a structure with low acoustic impedance.
(B) Doppler can e used to increase conspicuity of tissue reflectivity in the form of the color comet tail artifact, also called twinkle artifact.
(C) Tissue harmonic imaging is helpful for thin patient.
(D) Acoustic shadowing is commonly seen with urinary or biliary calculi.
(E) Side lobe artifact is often seen as an apparent septations extending from the side of the gallbladder, the urinary bladder or a simple cyst.
??
**Ans: C**
組織諧波成像(tissue harmonic imaging)有助於「肥胖(fat)」體型或回音雜訊多之病人，而非瘦(thin)病人，故本敘述錯誤。聲增強、彩色都卜勒之twinkle(comet tail)偽影、聲影常見於泌尿/膽道結石、側葉偽影於膽囊/膀胱/單純囊腫呈現假性分隔，皆為正確描述。
