#交換 #2018交換 #IR May-Thurner syndrome means?
(A) Entrapment of the left renal vein between the superior mesenteric artery and the aorta
(B) Third portion of the duodenum is compressed between the superior mesenteric artery and the aorta
(C) Compression of the celiac artery by the median arcuate ligament
(D) Left common iliac vein is compressed between the right common iliac artery and fifth lumbar vertebra
??
**Ans: D**
May-Thurner syndrome：左髂總靜脈被夾於右髂總動脈與第五腰椎間受壓。A為nutcracker syndrome、B為SMA syndrome、C為median arcuate ligament syndrome，皆為不同壓迫症候群。
