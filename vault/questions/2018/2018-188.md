#交換 #2018交換 #IR Which of the following is not the factors influencing the development of extra-hepatic collateral arteries supplying HCC?
(A) Tumor size greater than 5cm
(B) Multifocal lesions
(C) Prior surgery
(D) Extrahepatic tumor infiltration
??
**Ans: B**
多發病灶（multifocal lesions）並非肝外側枝動脈發展的公認因子；腫瘤>5cm、曾手術、肝外浸潤皆為公認因子。
