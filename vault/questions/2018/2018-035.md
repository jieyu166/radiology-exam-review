#交換 #2018交換 #CH Which choice is **false** about thoracic complications of precision cancer therapies?
(A) Drug-related pneumonitis during cancer therapy can be due to cytotoxic injuries, oxidative stress, or immune-mediated effects.
(B) Anticancer agents can not cause pulmonary edema or pleural effusions.
(C) In patients with cancer treated with VEGF inhibitors, major complications include hemorrhage and embolism.
(D) Pneumothorax is a rare but known complication in patients treated with antiangiogenic therapy.
(E) The imaging manifestations of immune checkpoint inhibitor–related pneumonitis include the AIP/ARDS pattern, the COP pattern, the NSIP pattern, and the hypersensitivity pneumonitis pattern
??
**Ans: B**
錯誤。看起來硬加not進去。文中有一段是Pulmonary Edema  and Pleural Effusion due to  Molecular Targeting Therapy，常見藥物有BCR-ABL kinase inhibitors。
D. 文中表格Tyrosine kinase inhibitor，VEGF inhibitor可能引起pneumothorax.
