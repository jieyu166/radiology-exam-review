#交換 #2018交換 #CH About description of pulmonary vasculitis, which one is **wrong**?
(A) Temporal (giant cell) vasculitis was classified as large vessel disease.
(B) Behcet's Disease was classified as small vessel disease.
(C) Wegener's Granulomatosis was also classified as immune complex associated vasculitis.
(D) Microscopic Polyangiitis was also classified as immune complex associated vasculitis, usually P-ANCA is present in 80%
??
**Ans: B**
Behcet's disease 侵犯動靜脈側任何口徑血管，屬獨立的 variable vessel vasculitis 類別，非小血管疾病。
