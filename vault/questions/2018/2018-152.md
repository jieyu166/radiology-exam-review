#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Segment 5 of liver may drain into the following veins, except?
(A) Middle hepatic vein
(B) Right hepatic vein
(C) Accessary inferior right hepatic vein
(D) Left hepatic vein
??
**Ans: D**
Segment 5 可引流入 MHV、RHV、accessory inferior RHV，不引流入左肝靜脈（LHV，負責左外側段引流）。
