#交換 #2018交換 #PED A 2 years old child presents with beta thalassaemia, and skeletal survey is performed. Which one of the following radiographic features is favoring thalassaemia?
(A) Hair-on-end appearance with widening of the diploic space is the skull.
(B) Enlargement of the frontal sinuses
(C) Lucent metaphyseal bands
(D) Medial displacement of the orbits.
??
**Ans: A**
重型海洋性貧血(thalassaemia major)因骨髓代償性造血增生，顱骨呈「hair-on-end」放射狀骨針樣外觀合併板障間隙增寬，為典型影像特徵。額竇擴大、乾骺端透亮帶、眼眶向外側(非向內)移位皆非典型海洋性貧血特徵。
