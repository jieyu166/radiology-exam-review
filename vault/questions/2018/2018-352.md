#交換 #2018交換 #IR Which of the followings is not reducing radiation exposure of the patient during angiography?
(A) Maximize the use of filters
(B) Minimize the use of geometric magnification
(C) Collimate as much as possible.
(D) Keep filming rates as low as possible.
??
**Ans: A**
官方標示答案A。⚠️惟增加濾片(beam filtration)通常會降低病人劑量，此標示物理上存疑；B減少幾何放大、C盡量準直、D降低攝影張數皆明確降劑量。待醫師確認。
