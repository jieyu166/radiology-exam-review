#交換 #2018交換 #Breast 關於breast MRI判讀，以下何者為**非**？
(A) 最好於動態顯影MRI之early phase判讀(早期顯影系列).
(B) Clustered ring是non-mass enhancement (NME)之一種表現形式，是沿著duct而成的一串聚集的ring enhancement, 可能為惡性表現.
(C) Clumped亦為NME的一種內在顯影形式，形成鵝卵石或葡萄串之型態，可能為惡性表現.
(D) 當一個rim enhanced lesion,呈現均勻且薄之顯影，其內容物在non-fat suppressed之T1WI為high signal, 則最可能為metastasis.
??
**Ans: D**
Rim enhancement is enhancement mainly concentrated at the periphery of the mass.
- This type of enhancement is frequently a feature of **high-grade invasive ductal cancer, fat necrosis, and inflammatory cysts**.
- Not a typical cyst, 40% chance of malignancy.
