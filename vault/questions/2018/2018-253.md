#交換 #2018交換 #Breast Which statement below is incorrect about ADH?
(A) This is likely that ADH and DCIS are two close histopathologic entities.
(B) Any biopsy technique, limited or partial sampling probably the major factor in underestimated of ADH diagnosed on US guided core needle biopsy.
(C) A re-biopsy using a vacuum-assisted biopsy device under US guidance might result in decreasing the upgrade rate of ADH on final surgery.
(D) ADH is effective at predicting upgrade malignancy by MG and US features.
??
**Ans: D**
目前尚「無法有效」單憑乳房攝影(MG)與超音波(US)影像特徵預測ADH最終病理是否會upgrade為惡性，故本敘述錯誤（應為ineffective而非effective）。ADH與DCIS為組織病理學上相近之兩個實體、任何切片技術之取樣不完整為US導引粗針切片低估ADH之主因、以真空輔助切片(VAB)重新切片可能降低最終手術upgrade率，皆為正確描述。
