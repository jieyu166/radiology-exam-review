#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Which of the following about retroperitoneal fibrosis is **wrong**?
(A) About 56-100% of idiopathic retroperitoneal fibrosis cases will suffer from obstructive uropathy.
(B) In T1WI, the fibrosis area is low signal intensity. After administration of gadolinium contrast material, some early soft-tissue enhancement may happen.
(C) Carcinoid tumor can induce retroperitoneal fibrosis without direct metastasizing to the retroperitoneal area.
(D) In benign retroperitoneal fibrosis, anterior displacement of aorta and IVC is usually seen.
??
**Ans: D**
良性RPF為浸潤性包繞而非移位，貼脊椎不前抬主動脈；主動脈前移(floating aorta sign)反而提示惡性/淋巴瘤，故D錯。
