#交換 #2018交換 #MSK Which one of the following radiographic features on radiography is NOT usually seen in patients with an enchondroma?
(A) Presence of sclerotic rim
(B) Annular or punctate calcifications in the matrix
(C) Scalloping of the inner cortical margin
(D) Entirely radiolucent in short bones
??
**Ans: A**
邊緣硬化緣(sclerotic rim)並非內生軟骨瘤(enchondroma)之典型平片表現，故本敘述錯誤。基質內環狀或點狀鈣化、內側皮質緣扇形凹陷(scalloping)、於短骨(如手指骨)完全呈透亮性表現，皆為內生軟骨瘤之正確典型表現。
