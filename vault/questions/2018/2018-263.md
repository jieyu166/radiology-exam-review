#交換 #2018交換 #CH Which is the typical CT feature of lymphoid interstitial pneumonitis?
(A) diffuse lung consolidation with ground-glass opacities
(B) ground-glass opacities, often in combination with cystic lesions
(C) centrilobular nodules and ground-glass opacities
(D) patchy peripheral or peribronchovascular consolidation
??
**Ans: B**
淋巴球性間質性肺炎(LIP)典型CT表現為毛玻璃樣混濁(ground-glass opacities)，常合併囊性病灶(cystic lesions)——雙側毛玻璃、邊界不清之小葉中心性結節、小葉間隔輕度增厚、支氣管血管束增厚(約85%)、周邊小結節(約85%)、薄壁囊腫(約70%)為其特徵組合。
