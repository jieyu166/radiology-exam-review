#交換 #2018交換 #NR Concerning hemimegalencephaly, which of the followings is INCORRECT?
(A) defect of cellular organization and neuronal migration
(B) enlargement of a part or all of a hemisphere
(C) progression of myelination
(D) decreased signal of white matter on T1-weighted MR images caused by reduced myelination
??
**Ans: D**
hemimegalencephaly因加速髓鞘化，白質於T1呈增高訊號、CT值增高(非減少)，故D不正確。
