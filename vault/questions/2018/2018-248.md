#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Which of the following statement about the renal tract is **wrong**?
A. Perirenal fat allows the renal outline to be seen on plain radiographs.
B. A high kV radiograph optimizes the detection of calcification.
C. The ureters are projected over the tips of the transverse processes of L2 to L5.
D. On computed tomography, the renal upper pole is posterior to the lower pole.
E. Prone views aid mid ureteric filling during IVP examination.
(A) Perirenal fat allows the renal outline to be seen on plain radiographs.
(B) A high kV radiograph optimizes the detection of calcification.
(C) The ureters are projected over the tips of the transverse processes of L2 to L5.
(D) On computed tomography, the renal upper pole is posterior to the lower pole.
(E) Prone views aid mid ureteric filling during IVP examination.
??
**Ans: B**
偵測鈣化應使用「低kV」攝影（非高kV），高kV會降低腎實質對比，故本敘述錯誤。腎周脂肪使腎輪廓可於平片顯示、輸尿管投影於L2-L5橫突尖端、CT上腎上極較下極偏後、俯臥姿勢有助IVP檢查中段輸尿管充盈，皆為正確描述。
