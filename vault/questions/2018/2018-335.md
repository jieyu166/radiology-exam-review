#交換 #2018交換 #CH According to AJCC 8th edition, what is the clinical staging of HPV-associated oropharyngeal cancer with one 3.6cm tumor mass at tongue base, extending to lingual surface of glottis and one 4.5cm metastatic lymph node at right neck level III?
(A) T3N1
(B) T2N2a
(C) T2N1
(D) T3N2a
??
**Ans: A**
腫瘤侵犯會厭舌面使T分期升至T3（不受3.6cm大小限制）；單一同側4.5cm淋巴結（未超過6cm）為N1。
