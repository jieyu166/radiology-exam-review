#交換 #2018交換 #ABD Which of the following is less likely to be the indications of CEUS(contrast-enhanced ultrasound) on FLL(focal liver lesion.)?
(A) CEUS is performed to characterize observations detected at surveillance US.
(B) CEUS can be performed to characterize observations detected on prior CT / MRI if the observations are visible as distinct nodules on pre-contrast gray-scale ultrasound.
(C) To characterize observations (generally >10mm and visible as distinct nodules at pre-contrast grayscale US) in patients at risk for HCC and establish a diagnosis of HCC.
(D) To differentiated between different metastases.
??
**Ans: D**
區分「不同種類轉移病灶彼此之間」並非CEUS於局部肝病灶(FLL)之常見適應症，故較不可能為適應症。CEUS常見適應症為：確認監測超音波所偵測到之異常表現、確認先前CT/MRI偵測到且於灰階超音波上呈明顯結節之異常表現、於HCC高風險病人中確認一般>10mm且灰階超音波呈明顯結節之異常表現以建立HCC診斷。
