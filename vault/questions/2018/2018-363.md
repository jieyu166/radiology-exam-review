#交換 #2018交換 #ABD In a 67-year-old female patient with jaundice and gallbladder wall thickening on ultrasound scan, which feature on CT favors a diagnosis of xanthogranulomatous cholecystitis rather than gallbladder carcinoma?
(A) pericholecystic fat infiltration
(B) intramural hypoattenuating nodules throughout the gallbladder
(C) biliary obstruction
(D) hepatic extension
(E) regional lymphadenopathy
??
**Ans: B**
膽囊壁內低衰減結節(intramural hypoattenuating nodules，壁內肉芽腫/膿瘍)傾向XGC。其餘徵象於XGC與膽囊癌皆可見無法區分。
