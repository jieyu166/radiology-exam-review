#交換 #2018交換 #NR 有關 Progressive multifocal leukoencephalopathy 下列敘述何者錯誤?
(A) A demyelinating disorder caused by John Cunningham (JC) virus.
(B) Around 80% of normal population have antibodies of JC virus but are disease-free due to normal immune response.
(C) Involving periventricular to subcortical white matter, more common at frontal and occipital locations .
(D) Usually presence of mass effect but rarely contrast enhancement.
(E) None of above
??
**Ans: D**
There is little, or no mass effect or enhancement and the subcortical U-fibers are commonly asymmetrically involved subcortical frontal and parieto-occipital regions
- Milky way sign: multiple punctate high T2 signal lesions surrounding the main area
- Barbell sign: parieto-occipital signal abnormality crossing the splenium
