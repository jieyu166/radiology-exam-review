#交換 #2018交換 #H&N Which is **true** for Le Fort I fracture?
(A) Separation at nasofrontal suture.
(B) Fracture of the inferior orbital rim.
(C) Fracture of zygomatic arch
(D) Fracture of anterolateral margin of the nasal cavity.
??
**Ans: D**
Le Fort I為水平上頜骨折(floating palate)，骨折線通過齒槽脊、鼻腔外側壁(鼻腔前外側緣/梨狀孔)、上頜竇下壁。A鼻額縫=III、B下眶緣=II、C顴弓=ZMC非Le Fort。
