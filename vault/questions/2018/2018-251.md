#交換 #2018交換 #MSK A 10-year-old girl presents with right hip pain. The following X-ray shows sclerosis of femoral head epiphysis. MRI study shows double line sign at femoral head, what's the most likely diagnosis?
(A) SUFE
(B) Panner disease
(C) Bone infarct
(D) Perthes disease
(E) OCD
??
**Ans: D**
股骨頭骨骺硬化合併MRI「double line sign」（代表缺血性壞死之肉芽組織/硬化交界）為Perthes disease（股骨頭缺血性壞死）之典型MRI表現。SUFE為骨骺滑脫、Panner disease侵犯肱骨小頭、bone infarct好發長骨幹骺端而非骨骺、OCD好發關節面局部軟骨下骨。
