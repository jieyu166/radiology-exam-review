#交換 #2018交換 #NR A 13-year-old male presents with recurrent epistaxis. CT shows a highly vascular mass in the nasopharynx, with widening of the pterygopalatine fossa and invasion of the sphenoid sinus. What's not your possibly suggestion?
(A) TAE for active bleeding
(B) Hormonal therapy
(C) Radiotherapy
(D) Biopsy
(E) Consult surgeon for surgery
??
**Ans: D**
青少年鼻咽血管纖維瘤(JNA)為富血管腫瘤，切片(biopsy)因高出血風險而「不建議」作為處置選項，故本題答案為D。栓塞(TAE)可用於控制活動性出血、荷爾蒙治療與放射治療為文獻報告過之輔助處置選項、外科手術照會為確定性治療方式，皆屬合理建議。
