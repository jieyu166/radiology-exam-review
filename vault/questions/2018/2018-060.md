#交換 #2018交換 #CV Which of the following statements best describes the prognostic value of T1 mapping?
(A) ECV may represent a risk marker for death, hospitalization for heart failure, or both.
(B) It has been shown that the prognostic value of native myocardial T1 is higher than that of ECV.
(C) ECV correlates with left ventricular ejection fraction.
(D) The prognostic value of ECV stands against scientific evidence as myocardial fibrosis does not affect mechanical, vasomotor, or electrical function of the heart.
??
**Ans: A**
正確。用一些統計方法，可用ECV算出病人at risk for death, hospitalization for heart failure, or both.
B: 錯誤。文中提到 Data regarding the prognostic value of native T1 are limited. 而ECV至少在組織學的correlation比native T1更好。文中提到在預後和LVEF不相上下。所以T1 higher應該是錯的。也有人認為ECV跟正常人難以區分，其預後價值仍待商確。
C: 錯誤。ECV和LVEF在預測預後都有效果。但文中沒提到兩者是否有correlation。
D: 錯誤。myocardial fibrosis is **associated** with mechanical, vasomotor, and electrical dysfunction.
