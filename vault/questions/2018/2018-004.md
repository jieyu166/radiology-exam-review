#交換 #2018交換 #Breast Which one of the followings is “not” or “least likely”a malignant ultrasonographic characteristic of breast nodule?
(A) Microlobulation
(B) Purely echogenic nodule
(C) Angular margins
(D) Hypoechoic texture
??
**Ans: B**
純高回音結節(purely echogenic nodule)為良性特徵(脂肪/纖維化)，最不可能為惡性。microlobulation/angular margins/hypoechoic texture皆為惡性特徵。
