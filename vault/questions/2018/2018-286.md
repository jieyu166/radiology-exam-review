#交換 #2018交換 #NR Which one is **WRONG** about schwannomatosis?
(A) Incidence similar to NF2.
(B) Germline mutation of SMARCB1 gene.
(C) Multiple schwannomas of peripheral nervous system with involvement of vestibular nerves.
(D) Peak incidence between ages 30-60.
??
**Ans: C**
神經鞘瘤病(schwannomatosis)之診斷準則要求「不合併」前庭神經(vestibular nerve)侵犯，若合併前庭神經腫瘤則應歸類為NF2，而非schwannomatosis，故本敘述錯誤。其發生率與NF2相近、可有SMARCB1基因生殖細胞突變、好發年齡通常>30歲(30-60歲區間)，皆為正確描述。
