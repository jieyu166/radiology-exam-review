#交換 #2021交換 #MSK Which of the following statement about avulsion fractures of the knee is **WRONG** ?
(A) Stieda fracture - Avulsion injury at medial femoral condyle
(B) Segond fracture - Avulsion injury at lateral tibial plateau
(C) Gerdy tubercle - Avulsion injury at tibial tuberosity
(D) Larsen-Johansson syndrome - Avulsion injury at inferior pole of the patella
??
**Ans: C**

- [[Osgood-Schlatter disease]] - Avulsion injury at tibial tuberosity
- Gerdy tubercle is the eponymous name for the lateral condyle of the proximal tibia (where it is located anterolaterally). It is where the iliotibial band and anterior tibialis muscle inserts.
