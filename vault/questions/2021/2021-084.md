#交換 #2021交換 #Physics In diagnostic radiology, As Low As Reasonably Achievable (ALARA) may be taken to mean ensuring:
(A) Patient doses low
(B) Unnecessary radiation is eliminated
(C) Costs are minimized
(D) Examinations are cost effective
??
**Ans: B**
- A不夠全面。除了病人，我們人員的輻射劑量也要盡量減少。
- 輻射防護要防止確定效應的發生，並使機率效應的發生降低至合理可接受的低水平。除了降低病人劑量，也要考慮輻射源本身的屏蔽，以降低一般大眾或工作人員的劑量。(醫學影像物理學Chp.31)
- 目標ALARA: As Low As Reasonable Achievable合理抑低。
- 考書中文字。The principle is that the residual risk shall be reduced as far as reasonably practicable. Unnecessary radiation is eliminated. (Review of radiologic physics 4th Ed.)
