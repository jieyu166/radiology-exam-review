#交換 #2021交換 #MSK Which of the following is NOT a radiographic feature of melorheostosis?
(A) Typically involve the metaphysis.
(B) Dripping candle wax sign.
(C) Limb length discrepancy.
(D) Associated with osteopoikilosis.
??
**Ans: A**
Common in diaphysis of appendicular skeleton (lower > upper extremity); usually monomelic with at least 2 bones involved in dermatomal distribution. Rare in axial skeleton.
