#交換 #2021交換 #H&N Which description about orbital cavernous venous malformation is **wrong**?
(A) Most common adult orbital mass lesion with characteristic feature as patchy dynamic enhancement.
(B) MR appearance showing septations on T2WI is more specific than CT in distinguishing from other orbital masses
(C) Macroscopic calcifications or phleboliths are typical.
(D) Slow, progressive enlargement over years. On average 10-15% volume growth per year. Faster growth during pregnancy.
??
**Ans: C**
- A. (Ref Handbook of Head and Neck Imaging)
	-  Choroidal melanoma是最常見的 primary ocular tumor，Inflammatory pseudotumor是最常見的painful orbital mass。但如果是patchy CE(+)只能是cavernous venous malformation.
- C. 書中沒提到phleboliths. 和其他地方hemangioma不太相同，眼睛的hemangioma不太會鈣化。 (Radiopaedia) Sclerosing hemangiomas有時會有鈣化。
