#交換 #2021交換 #Physics Which iodinated contrast media is the non-ionic dimer?
(A) Iopromide.
(B) Iohexol.
(C) Iothalamate meglumine.
(D) Iodixanol.
(E) Iopamidol.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**。**Iodixanol（Visipaque）為 non-ionic dimer**（等滲）。Iopromide/Iohexol/Iopamidol 為非離子單體；Iothalamate meglumine 為離子單體。
