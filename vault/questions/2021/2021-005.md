#交換 #2021交換 #Breast Which one of the following managements about probably benign (BIRADS 3) lesions is **not true** ?
(A) It is usually deﬁned as speciﬁc lesion types with ≤2% risk of malignancy
(B) In breast sonography, BIRADS 3 can be used directly on screening
(C) Biopsy is recommended for circumscribed solid mass with increased diameter ≥30% in 6 months or other suspicious ﬁndings
(D) Speciﬁc lesion can be considered BIRADS 3 on baseline mammograpm after work-up.
??
**Ans: C**
要biopsy就是4以上
- B. Screening  Echo 做完可直接下 BIRADS 3 診斷，但 Screening  Mammo 不行。原因是如果Echo 有看到東西 ，通常在同一次就會做Mammo. (BIRADS 敘述的邏輯)
- D. Screening mammography不可直接下BIRADS 3，需診斷mammo才行(小陳主任, after workup)
