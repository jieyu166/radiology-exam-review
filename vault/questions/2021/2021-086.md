#交換 #2021交換 #Physics Which space(s) would iodinated contrast media distribute after intravenous injection in a patient with normal renal function? (1) Vascular space (2) Extracellular space (3) Intracellular space
(A) 1
(B) 1+2
(C) 1+2+3
(D) 1+3
(E) 2+3
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B（1+2）**。含碘對比劑分子量低（<200 Da），靜脈注射後**自由交換於血管內與細胞外間隙**，不進入細胞內。
