#交換 #2021交換 #Breast Which of the following statement about digital breast tomosynthesis (DBT) is **wrong**?
(A) In general, the larger angular range of the x-ray tube motion results in more tomographic information and yields better section separation or vertical (z-axis) resolution.
(B) The increase in screening-detected cancers achieved with DBT is mostly due to an increase in the detection of invasive cancers rather than ductal carcinoma in situ.
(C) DBT is associated with a lower recall of asymmetries and focal asymmetries.
(D) In DBT, the resolution of each individual calcification may be compromised depending on the optical density of the calcification, the DBT imaging acquisition, and motion during the longer DBT image acquisition.
(E) None of the above.
??
**Ans: E**
Larger angle range = better resolution.
DBT screening increases detection of invasive cancers rather than DCIS.
DBT lower recall rate for asymmetries and focal symmetries.
DBT higher recall rate for masses and distorsions.
