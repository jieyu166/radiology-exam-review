#交換 #2021交換 #Unknown About Osteoarthritis (**OA**), which of the following is **not** appropriate?
(A) Osteoarthritis (OA) is a progressive degenerative disease that is the most frequent cause of pain in facet joints
(B) OA cannot be found in adults under 30 years of age
(C) OA can be found in almost all elderly people after age 60 years
(D) The prevalence and degree of OA being higher at the L4-L5 level, followed by L3-L4 and L5-S1
??
**Ans: B**
<30歲年輕人已有半數有OA的情形。
退化性關節炎（OA）是一種漸進性退化性疾病，是椎間facet joints疼痛最常見的原因之一。根據 Eubanks 等人的研究，30歲以下的成年人中已超過一半有 OA 的跡象，而60歲以上的長者幾乎全部都有，且以 L4-L5 節段最常見，其次是 L3-L4 和 L5-S1。Ref: [2021 RG Differential Diagnosis of Facet Joint Disorders](https://pubs.rsna.org/doi/full/10.1148/rg.2021200079)

## [[Gout]]
- Gout: 代謝疾病，monosodium urate crystal沉積在synovium跟關節旁軟組織
- 常見於30-50歲中老年人
- involve **peripheral joints**較多，但也會出現在axial bones，在脊椎L4-5最多
- 可能合併CPPD/pseudogout
- 長時間不治療會引起tophi沉積
- X光: sensitivity較低，慢性變化有osteophytes, periarticular erosions, subchondral cysts, and bone scleroses,可能和tumors, abscesses, tuberculosis, and degenerative spinal diseases搞混
- CT: 看tophi引起的bony erosion好用。50%病人有
	- DECT: Sensitivity 90%, Specificity 83%。但對spine 敏感度較低，無法排除。
- MRI: 有時不容易區分gout跟感染，腫瘤。
	- Tophi T1: low-iso intensity，T2: low-high intensity。會有homogeneous or peripheral enhancement
