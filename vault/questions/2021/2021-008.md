#交換 #2021交換 #Breast Which of the following statement is **wrong**?
(A) The main indications of breast MRI are staging of known cancer, screening for breast cancer in women at increased risk, and evaluation of response to neoadjuvant chemotherapy.
(B) Lesion detection on breast MRI is primarily performed by using the postcontrast images.
(C) Using MRI to assess tumor size for invasive lobular carcinomas is less accurate than ultrasonography.
(D) The depiction of DCIS components related to invasive cancers on breast MR is much better than that with mammography.
??
**Ans: C**
幾乎沒有遇過US 比 MRI 好的情況. Tissue contrast 差太多
[[2019 R Breast MRI State of the Art.pdf]]
- A. Indication 沒錯
- B. 沒錯，主要打contrast看血管。現在還會再看T2WI, DWI
- C. Lobular carcinoma: MRI 能看得比mammo/US清楚，評估tumor size也更準，減少re-excision rate。故眾多guideline indication都有lobular carcinoma (原文Table 2)
- D. MRI 看tumor sensitivity是最高的。但以microcalcification呈現的DCIS要用mammo看，MRI沒有比較好。
	- 看invasive cancer旁邊的DCIS，MRI比其他工具好。
	- MRI看 large DCIS ，Sensitivity approaches 100%.
	- MRI看 DCIS，特別是 high-grade 效果也是很好 (原文Fig 4)，但以microcalcification呈現的DCIS可能會看不到，看microcalcification還是mammography比較強
