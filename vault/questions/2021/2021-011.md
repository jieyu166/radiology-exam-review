#交換 #2021交換 #Breast According to the ACR BI-RADS Atlas® 5th Edition, which description of the Kinetic Curve Assessment is **not** **true**?
(A) In initial phase, slow is deﬁned as an increase in signal intensity of less than 50% within the ﬁrst 2 minutes
(B) In initial phase, fast is an increase of more than 100%
(C) During the delayed phase, persistent is deﬁned as signal intensity that does not change over time after the initial rise
(D) During the delayed phase, washout is deﬁned as a decrease of more than 10% from the highest signal intensity during the initial rise. Washout curves are speciﬁcally denoted “suspicious.”
??
**Ans: C**
「持續性」（persistent）的定義是：在初始上升後，信號強度繼續**緩慢增加>10%**，而不是「不隨時間變化」。
