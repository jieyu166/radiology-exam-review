#交換 #2021交換 #Breast To identify an ultrasound non-mass lesion for a mammographic correlation, which of the following mammographic finding is less likely?
(A) Group or segmental microcalcifications
(B) Focal asymmetry
(C) Developing asymmetry
(D) Spiculated mass
(E) Architectural distortion
??
**Ans: D**
Mammographic lesions that most often appear as nonmass findings on US images include calcifications, a focal or developing asymmetry, and architectural distortion.
