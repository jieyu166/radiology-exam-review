#交換 #2022交換 #NR Which following structure is most commonly involved in locally invasive thyroid cancer?
(A) Carotid artery
(B) Esophagus
(C) Larynx
(D) Recurrent laryngeal nerve
(E) Trachea
??
**Ans: D**
根據Head Neck. 1994;16(2):165–72.的review，最常被 invasive thyroid cancer侵犯的是 **strap** muscles (53%)，接著是 **recurrent laryngeal nerve** (47%), trachea (37%), esophagus (21%), lateral neck structures including the great vessels and vagus nerve (30%), and larynx (12%). From [Invasive thyroid cancer: management of the trachea and esophagus](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC2750808/)
