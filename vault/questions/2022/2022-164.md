#交換 #2022交換 #2023交換 #NR Regarding the 2021 WHO Classification of Tumors of the Central Nervous System (The 5th-edition), which one is **NOT** within the classification of the Gliomas, Glioneuronal Tumors, and Neuronal Tumors ?
(A) Adult-type diffuse gliomas
(B) Pediatric-type diffuse low-grade gliomas
(C) Circumscribed astrocytic gliomas
(D) Ependymomas (now classified by site as well as histological and molecular features).
(E) Choroid Plexus Tumors,
??
**Ans: E**
WHO2021 CNS brain tumor 分 13 類，Glioma 又分下列幾種
- 大人小孩分類不同，因為基因不同(以往小兒並未分出，WHO2016 新增 Diffuse midline glioma, H3 K27-altered，WHO2021 又更加細分)
- Circumscribed: Pilocytic astrocytoma, Subependymal giant cell astrocytoma (SEGA), Pleomorphic xanthoastrocytoma(PXA)等
- Glioneuronal: 近年題目較常出現 Multinodular and vacuolating neuronal tumour (MVNT)
- Ependymomas
- Choroid plexus tumor 病理不一樣，不在 glioma 分類中。答案選 E
