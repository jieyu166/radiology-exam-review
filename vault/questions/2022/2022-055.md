#交換 #2022交換 #ABD Which description about papillary RCC is **right**?
(A) Papillary RCCs typically appear hypovascular and homogeneous on imaging studies.
(B) Papillary RCCs and oncocytomas share similar ontogenic features, histologic features and some imaging findings.
(C) It appears heterogeneous with areas of necrosis and hemorrhage
(D) All of above
??
**Ans: A**
正確。
B. Oncocytoma 跟 Chromophobe RCC 合稱 Oncocytic tumor
C. necrosis/hemorrhage 是 Clear cell RCC 的特徵，會有 low T1。
