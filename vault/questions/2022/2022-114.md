#交換 #2022交換 #NR Which one of the followings is the most **inappropriate** describing atlantoaxial rotatory subluxation (AARS)?
(A) Children with AARS often have a delayed presentation with neck pain and torticollis
(B) Falling-down is a common injury mechanism.
(C) In patients with Down syndrome, inflammatory process or an intrinsic defect in collagen fibers are associated with AARS.
(D) The space available for the spinal cord of less than 14 mm, measured from the posterior aspect of the dens to the anterior aspect of the posterior atlantal ring, predicts the development of paralysis.
(E) The atlantodental interval (ADI) predicts the development of paralysis.
??
**Ans: E**
(D) The distance between odontoid and posterior arch of C1 -> 有文獻稱這段為 posterior atlantodental interval (PADI)，< 14mm 時 spinal cord compression 的機率會上升
(E) 不是很確定，不過一般講的 ADI 指的是 anterior atlantodental interval (AADI)，能預測 cord compression 的是 PADI
