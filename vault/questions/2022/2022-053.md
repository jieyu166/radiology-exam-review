#交換 #2022交換 #ABD Under the 8th AJCC staging of prostate cancer, which of the following region is mostly **not** involved on radiological staging T3aN1Mx?
(A) Seminal vesicle
(B) Pseudocapsule
(C) Pelvic lymph node(s)
(D) Transition zone
??
**Ans: A**
- A. 有侵犯到就是T3b。此為答案
- B. Pseudocapsule: a visible rim of low T2 signal intensity around all or part of the tumor margin that was not abutting the true capsule of the prostate gland or the pseudocapsule of the central gland. (Clin Imaging. 2016 May-Jun;40(3):365-9.)
- 和prostate capsule無關
- C. N1
- D. Transition zone跟peripheral zone跟分期無關
