#交換 #2022交換 #NR The following description about imaging feature of Neuropsychiatric Systemic lupus erythematosus(NPSLE) , which one is **wrong**?
(A) NPSLE vasculopathy affects predominantly arterioles and capillaries
(B) Posterior reversible encephalopathy syndrome (PRES), progressive multifocal leukoencephalopathy (PML) are known as CNS complications related to NPSLE
(C) Intracranial calcifications have been associated with NPSLE. The most frequently involved location is cerebral white matter such as cerebellum
(D) The white matter lesions of demyelinating syndrome in NPSLE are mainly distributed in the periependymal regions
??
**Ans: C**
Intracranial calcifications 時常可見。最常見位置是**basal ganglia** (30%)，較少在cerebral white matter and cerebellum.
