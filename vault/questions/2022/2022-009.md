#交換 #2022交換 #Breast Which is **incorrect** with regarding to the trade-offs in the design of DBT (digital breast tomosynthesis) systems?
(A) There are two types of x-ray tube motion in DBT acquisition: continuous and step-and-shoot.
(B) Continuous tube motion may result in a focal spot blur.
(C) Continuous tube motion requires a longer acquisition time, which may in itself compromise image quality due to patient motion.
(D) Narrower sweep angles reduce focal spot blurring, relaxing the requirements on short pulse widths.
??
**Ans: C**
Continue 時間較短，Step-and-shot scan 時間較長。
A. **正確**。Tube motion 有連續和 step-and-shoot 兩種方式。![ADD DBT Continue tube motion](data/images/obsidian/add-dbt-continue-tube-motion-dc37a0f9.png)較快速 ![ADD DBT Step-and-shot](data/images/obsidian/add-dbt-step-and-shot-16e7a7bf.png)照-停-照-停  時間久，病人易動有 motion artifact
BD. 連續運動可能會造成 Focal spot blur 焦點模糊，影響影像解析度，使用 extremely short x-ray pulse widths 可改善，但會導致 noise、組織穿透度和劑量等問題，也要大電流的發射器。減少 sweep angles 也有幫助，可併用。
-  Focal spot blur：X-ray 至 detector 的距離不同，在影像周圍產生模糊輪廓，會影響sharpness,主要由 continuous scan 產生。 ![ADD Focal spot blur](data/images/obsidian/add-focal-spot-blur-637a532e.png)
