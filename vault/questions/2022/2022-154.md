#交換 #2022交換 #NR Ectopic thyroid is a well-defined oval structure that is occasionally hyperemic on color Doppler. What is the **most common** form of ectopic thyroid?
(A) Lingual thyroid
(B) Suprahyoid sublingual thyroid
(C) Hyoid thyroid
(D) Mediastinal thyroid
??
**Ans: A**
Ectopic thyroid glands 可以在很多地方：lingual, suprahyoid sublingual, hyoid, infrahyoid, double ectopic, mediastinal, and rarely lateral cervical regions.
- Lingual thyroid: most common form of thyroid dysgenesis and contains the only functioning tissue in 75% of congenital hypothyroidism cases.
- Thyroid tissue 有功能，新生兒篩檢可能驗不出來，但之後會有 hypothyroidism
- Retrosternal, endolaryngeal or endotracheal ectopic thyroids 超音波看不到
