#交換 #2022交換 #PED Which of the following statements is **wrong** about pancake kidney?
(A) It is a kind of rare congenital fusion anomaly of upper urinary tract.
(B) Its arteries should originate from abdominal aorta only.
(C) It is usually located at the pelvic cavity.
(D) The collecting systems are normally to face anteriorly
??
**Ans: B**
可能源於aorta或common iliac artery
### Rotation
- Nonrotation or incomplete rotation: 腎臟沒有正常地轉90度。最常見。
- Reverse rotation: 旋轉方向相反。也不少。血管會在前側，collection system會在外側。
- Hyperrotation: 也不少。腎臟轉了超過180度。**血管會在後方** (放PCN要注意)
- Sagittal rotation: 罕見，只有case report。
