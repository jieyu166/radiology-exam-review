#交換 #2022交換 #CH As compared to normal group, what kind of associated condition is more likely to be observed in intubated patients with COVID-19 infection?
(A) Pneumothorax
(B) Pneumomediastinum
(C) Pneumopericardium
(D) All of the above
??
**Ans: D**
如果考細一點，答案會是 pneumomediastinum最多。 B應該也可以。
- COVID-19 病人，用呼吸器 barotrauma 機率是 24%，非 COVID-19 病人是 11%。Barotrauma 不影響 overall mortality
- The most common event was pneumomediastinum followed by pneumothorax.
