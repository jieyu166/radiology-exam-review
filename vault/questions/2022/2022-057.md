#交換 #2022交換 #2023交換 #ABD Regarding splenic/liver hemangioma: Which one is **not** the common imaging features on MRI?
(A) Hyperintense on T1-weighted images
(B) Hyperintense on T2-weighted images
(C) Can show nodular centripetal enhancement
(D) Can show uniform enhancement at delayed imaging
(E) Can show homogeneous enhancement
??
**Ans: A**
iso or hypo-intense T1
Radiopaedia
- T1: iso to hypo-intense to normal splenic parenchyma
- T2: hyperintense to splenic parenchyma
- CE: most demonstrate early nodular centripetal enhancement and uniform enhancement at delayed imaging. Smaller lesions may, however, show homogeneous enhancement on immediate post-contrast acquisitions remaining enhanced on delayed images.
