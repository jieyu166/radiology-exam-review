#交換 #2022交換 #ABD Which of the following is **wrong** about bladder rupture?
(A) The propensity for bladder injury is related to the degree of bladder distention at the time of impact.
(B) Bladder contusion or interstitial bladder injury may be treated conservatively.
(C) Intraperitoneal rupture the leakage of contrast around bowel loops and mesenteric folds, and requires surgical treatment.
(D) Extraperitoneal rupture is the leakage of contrast into perivesicle space and may dissect into fascial planes.
(E) None of the above.
??
**Ans: E**
全對。全對。
A. 根據2000 RG，正確。
B. Type 1 (UB contusion), type 3 (interstitial) 會以保守治療，正確。
C. Type 2 (Intraperitoneal) 需要開刀治療，正確。而2000 RG提到，大多可靠Foley 引流保守治療。
D. **正確**。
