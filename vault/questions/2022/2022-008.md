#交換 #2022交換 #Breast About the ultrasonographic findings of the breast DCIS (ductal carcinoma in situ), which is **wrong**?
(A) The ultrasound pattern of breast DCIS vary widely
(B) DCIS can present as a non-mass lesion on ultrasound
(C) DCIS more likely shows non-parallel (taller than wide) in orientation on breast ultrasound
(D) The high-grade DCIS more likely shows non-mass pattern with calcifications.
??
**Ans: C**
A.	**正確**。根據JABTS的統計，有很多種型態，主要是non-mass跟small mass，而non-mass表現多樣。
B.	**正確**。Non-mass grade統計上較高。
C.	文獻少提到taller than wide
D.	**正確**。和high grade有關的feature：
- Non-mass lesions were associated with high-grade DCIS and the presence of comedo necrosis.
- Microcalcifications were significantly associated with high-grade DCIS, the presence of comedo necrosis, an elevated Ki-67 , and Her2(+).
