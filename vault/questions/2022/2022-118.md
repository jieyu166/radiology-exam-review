#交換 #2022交換 #MSK Which of the following statements regarding the rotator interval is **true**?
(A) Contains the coracohumeral ligament and the superior and middle glenohumeral ligaments.
(B) Coracohumeral and superior glenohumeral ligaments form the "biceps pulley."
(C) Does not have a role in shoulder range of motion
(D) Resides between the infraspinatus and teres minor tendons.
??
**Ans: B**
A. **錯誤**。包含 CH ligament, SGHL。不含 MGHL
B. **正確**。另外還有 distal attachment of the subscapularis tendon.
C. 有功能。sling to stabilize the intraarticular portion of the long head of the biceps tendon.
D. **錯誤**。在 supraspinatus tendon, subscapularis 之間。
