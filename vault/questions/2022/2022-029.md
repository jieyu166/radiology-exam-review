#交換 #2022交換 #ABD Regarding extra-uterine high grade serous carcinomas, which of the following is **incorrect**?
(A) The spectrum includes ovarian serous carcinoma, primary peritoneal serous carcinoma, and primary fallopian tube carcinoma.
(B) They have the common origin from the fimbriated segment of the fallopian tube.
(C) FIGO uses a unified a staging system for serous ovarian, peritoneal and fallopian tube carcinomas, and guides appropriate management.
(D) Contrast-enhanced CT of the abdomen and pelvis is the main imaging examination for the staging in both the pretreatment and posttreatment periods.
(E) None of the above
??
**Ans: E**
AB 正確。
C 正確。這是 2014 版的重大改變。
D 正確。原文引用 2013 ACR Appropriateness Criteria: PreTx, Post-Tx 可用 contrast-enhanced CT of the abdomen and pelvis 評估。如果equivocal 可使用 CE-MRI。初步評估通常用 US，但 staging 幫助有限。posttreatment surveillance 可用 PET-CT。
