#交換 #2022交換 #2023交換 #NR Which of the following description about [[Medulloblastoma]] is **false**?
(A) Medulloblastoma is the most common CNS embryonal tumor and most common pediatric CNS malignancy
(B) The tumor is most commonly located in the cerebellum, and frequently grows into the fourth ventricle
(C) At CT, the tumor is a hyperattenuating enhancing mass and hydrocephalus is infrequently appearance.
(D) Leptomeningeal spread from the craniospinal axis is common.
??
**Ans: C**
但 AC 都可給分
A. **正確**，copy 自 paper。但台灣小兒最常見的 CNS tumor 是 Astrocytoma ➔ GCT ➔ Medulloblastoma 是第三名 (TPOG 2021)
B. **正確**。The tumor is most commonly located in the cerebellum (94%), particularly the vermis (>75%), and frequently grows into the fourth ventricle.
C. infrequently At CT, a hyperattenuating well-defined enhancing mass located in the cerebellar vermis with surrounding edema and frequent hydrocephalus is the classic appearance, but variability is common (40%), frequently from cyst formation and calcification.
D. **正確**。Leptomeningeal spread from the craniospinal axis is common(33%). Foraminal extension from the fourth ventricle is uncommon.
