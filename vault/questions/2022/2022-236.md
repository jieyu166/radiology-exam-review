#交換 #2022交換 #NR Which of the following pharyngeal arch – derivative structure pairs are **correct**?
(A) First pharyngeal arch - maxilla and mandible
(B) Second pharyngeal arch - trigeminal nerve
(C) Third pharyngeal arch - laryngeal cartilage and muscles
(D) Fourth pharyngeal arch - hyoid cartilage
??
**Ans: A**

- 1st arch 也有mandibular body, ramus。CNV 來自1st arch，2nd arch 是CN7。
- Hyoid 來自2, 3rd arch
- Laryngeal cartilage 來自4,6th 。
