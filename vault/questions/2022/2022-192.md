#交換 #2022交換 #NR Which of the follow statement are **not** correct for ultrasound features in ACR TI-RADS:
(A) When describing echogenicity of nodule ‘hypoechoic’ is when the nodule is hypoechoic compared to the strap muscles
(B) When describing echogenicity of nodule ‘very hypoechoic’ is when the nodule is hypoechoic compared to the strap muscles
(C) In nodules with calcifications that cause strong acoustic shadow assign it is best to assume the nodule is solid
(D) Microcalcifications are < 1mm, lack posterior shadowing and may show small comet-tail artifacts
??
**Ans: A**
Hypoechoic 和thyroid parenchyma比較。very hypoechoic 和strap muscle 比較。A錯B對。
C. **正確**。如果鈣化的acoustic shadowing造成評估困難，最好假定 **nodule is solid** and assign 2 points for composition and 1 point for echogenicity.
D. **正確**。Punctate echogenic foci: smaller than macrocalciﬁcations, **non-shadowing**, may have small comet-tail artifacts.
