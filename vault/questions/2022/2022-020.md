#交換 #2022交換 #ABD About the role of CT in two-stage liver surgery, which statement is **wrong**?
(A) This technique includes the conventional TSH procedure with surgical ligation or portal vein embolization (PVE) and the more recently adopted ALPPS (associating liver partition and portal vein ligation for staged hepatectomy) procedure.
(B) Multidetector CT is considered the imaging method of choice, because of its wideavailability, excellent temporal and spatial resolution, accuracy for detection and characterization of tumors, and three-dimensional (3D) reconstruction and liver volumetric abilities.
(C) The first objective is to detect and characterize all hepatic lesions, both primary neoplasms such as cholangiocarcinomas or hepatocellular carcinomas and metastases.
(D) The most important estimation in CT volumetry is the volume of hepatectomy.
??
**Ans: D**
CT volumetry 主要目的是評估手術切除後剩餘的肝臟大小足以支撐身體的需求，常用的指標是FLR-to-TLV ratio (肝臟無疾病者希望要在 25-30%以上，肝臟有疾病者希望要在 40%以上)，重點在於**預期剩餘的肝臟體積**而非切除掉的肝臟體積
FLR 指的是 future liver remnant (預期手術後剩餘的肝臟體積)
TLV 指的是total liver volume (原本肝臟的全部體積)
TSH 指的是 two-stage hepatectomy (兩階段切肝，第一階段是為了增加 FLR 所做，第二階段才做真正的肝臟切除)
