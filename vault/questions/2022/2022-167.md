#交換 #2022交換 #2023交換 #NR What condition may result from abnormal inclusion of surface ectoderm in the spinal cord?
(A) Lipoma.
(B) Epidermoid or dermoid cyst.
(C) Neurenteric cyst.
(D) Type II glomus arteriovenous malformation
??
**Ans: B**
Ectoderm 外胚層形成神經跟皮膚，想到皮膚/皮下常見的(epi)dermoid cyst
- Neurenteric cyst: 定義是 heterotopic endodermal tissue。來自 endoderm
- Intramedullary lesion 中比較常見/常考的有 Astrocytoma, Pilocytic astrocytoma, Ependymoma, Hemangioblastoma 有興趣可找該 paper 來看
