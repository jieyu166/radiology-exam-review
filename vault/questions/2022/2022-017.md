#交換 #2022交換 #Breast 下列何者為**錯**?
(A) The relative contraindication for DBT is similar to that for patients who cannot tolerate traditional mammography.
(B) In a combination-mode study, a slightly longer compression time is required to obtain both sets of images, and some patients may not tolerate the additional imaging time.
(C) At DBT, the imaging features and visibility of breast cancers will be slightly different from those at conventional mammography.
(D) Architectural distortion is not a common manifestation of cancers noted at DBT.
??
**Ans: D**
Architectural distortion is a subtle imaging finding that often is better visualized at DBT than at conventional mammography.
