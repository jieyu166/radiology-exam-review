#交換 #2022交換 #CV Which of the following entities in the differential diagnosis of acute chest pain can be distinguished at cardiac MRI by identifying a subendocardial or transmural scar that follows the distribution of a typical coronary artery territory during LGE?
(A) MI with nonobstructive coronary arteries (MINOCA).
(B) Myocarditis.
(C) Takotsubo cardiomyopathy.
(D) Cardiac contusion.
??
**Ans: A**
- Follows the distribution of a typical coronary artery territory是重點，會想到MI，那選項只有A可以選。
- MINOCA：MRI LGE可看到沿著coronary territory的subendocardial/transmural scar。答案選A。T2WI可再區別acute, subacute, chronic disease
- Myocarditis：MRI可診斷，但不能分辨出underlying。ECV quantification +LGE診斷比Lake Louise criteria來的好。Lake Louise criteria：下列有兩個即可診斷：(1) myocardial edema at T2WI, (2) hyperemia at early post contrast T1WI, and fibrosis during LGE, usually with a nonischemic (ie, midwall or subepicardial) pattern that does not follow a coronary artery distribution
- Takotsubo：MRI是US suboptimal時的二線檢查。可看到和LV dyskinetic portion相關的edema，和coronary territory無關。LGE基本上沒finding，但22-40%會有 low threshold focal or patchy nonischemic pattern，其和edema有關和myocardial necrosis無關。
- Contusion沒特別提，但應該和coronary血管分布無關。
