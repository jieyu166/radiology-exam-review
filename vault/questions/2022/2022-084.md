#交換 #2022交換 #CH About the diagnosis of the pulmonary arterial hypertension (PAH), which ONE of the following is **WRONG**?
(A) On chest X-ray, PAH presents a prominent pulmonary trunk shadow overlays on left pulmonary hilum with a size larger than aortic arch.
(B) On cardiac CT, the maximal diameter of the pulmonary trunk is larger than 1.3x diameter of the ascending aorta makes the PAH diagnosis.
(C) On chest X-ray, early stage of the PAH presents a cephalization of the bilateral lung markings.
(D) On cardiac CT 4-chamber view, the maximal width of the middle right ventricle is larger than 1.3x width of the middle left ventricle makes the PAH diagnosis.
??
**Ans: D**
(B) pulmonary trunk to aortic ratio 在成人身上超過 1:1 要考慮 PAH，在小孩身上有文獻指出 cutoff value 可以設在1.3:1
(D) reference 文章中沒有給出明確的 cutoff value，radiopaedia 上是寫 right ventricle to left ventricle diameter 大於1:1 (測量 midventricular level)
