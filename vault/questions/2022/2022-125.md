#交換 #2022交換 #MSK Which description about proximal femoral fracture is **wrong** ?
(A) Can result in fracture nonunion or AVN
(B) Osseous support of the proximal femur are strongest when exposed to longitudinal compression forces and weakest when exposed to tension and shear forces.
(C) Fractures involving the lateral margin of the femoral head-neck junction create a high risk of critical vascular injury.
(D) None of the above
??
**Ans: D**
A. can通常是正確的。Intracapsular fracture可能影響femoral head血流供應，尤其容易導致nonunion and/or AVN等併發症
B. **正確**。強度取決於負載的方向，縱向壓縮力時最強，而在張力和剪切力作用下最弱。![ADD femoral neck 1](data/images/obsidian/add-femoral-neck-1-83caa016.png)
C. **正確**。Lateral epiphyseal artery通常由股骨頭頸交界處的外側邊緣lateral margin of the femoral head-neck junction進入，這邊的骨折造成high-risk vascular injury風險最高。沿股骨頸越往下，風險逐漸降低。
