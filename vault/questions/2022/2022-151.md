#交換 #2022交換 #NR In terms of posterior fossa abnormalities, which one is **wrong**?
(A) Blake pouch cysts rarely present with hydrocephalus since most of these lesions are with CSF fenestration.
(B) Isolated inferior vermian hypoplasia is with better disease outcome than Dandy-Walker malformation.
(C) Rhombencephalosynapsis may be nonsyndromic.
(D) Pontocerebellar hypoplasia may present with a so-called “dragonfly“ appearance.
??
**Ans: A**
- Blake pouch cysts: caused by **lack** of fenestration of the Blake pouch, resulting in absence of communication between the fourth ventricle and the subarachnoid space and leading to tetraventricular hydrocephalus. Blake pouch沒有fenestration導致obstructive **hydrocephalus**全部腦室都會變大。A是錯的。
	- occurs sporadically, no recurrence risk. 多以Hydrocephalus and macrocephaly表現。預後看shunt是否有complication基本很好。
	- cyst導致choroid plexus位移到vermis下方/Blake pouch cyst前上方，顯影可看到
- Isolated inferior vermian hypoplasia: vermis下半部部分缺失。有些人當作Dandy-Walker variant，但Paper認為這是錯的。除非合併Mendelian syndromes，不然不會復發。在胎兒時期，要18周後才能診斷，18周以前不一定是異常。>75%病人預後不錯，可能會有些許功能缺失
	- 嚴重程度 DWM > DW variant > BPC > mega cisterna magna(MCM) 因inferior vermian hypoplasia算是DW variant所以B是對的。
- Rhombencephalosynapsis: Vermis不存在+ 兩側小腦半球相連。
	- 大多是nonsyndromic。但卻是Gómez-López-Hernández syndrome的典型特徵(parietal alopecia, trigeminal anesthesia, and craniofacial dysmorphic signs)，也可能在VACTERL病人上看到。復發風險低。C是對的。
- Pontocerebellar hypoplasia影響小腦遠大於vermis，看起來像蜻蜓Dragonfly sign。D是對的。
	- Autosomal recessive，至少有10種以上subtype。
	- 在一些malformations, disruptions, metabolic disorder也會有同樣的影像表現。
