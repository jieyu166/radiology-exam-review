#交換 #2022交換 #NR Which of the following statement about brain injury is **wrong**?
(A) Arterial EDH does not cross suture lines, and may cross dural reflections(falx tentorium), in contradistinction to SDH.
(B) In contradistinction to EDH, SDH is no consistent relationship to the presence of skull fractures.
(C) DAI is due to axonal disruption from impact forces of acceleration/ deceleration.
(D) Initial CT of brain contusion is often normal; later on, low-density lesions with or without blood in them develop.
??
**Ans: C**
DAI is due to axonal disruption from **shearing forces** of acceleration/deceleration.
