#交換 #2022交換 #ABD Which of the following statement about FNH-like lesion is **wrong**?
(A) FNH-like lesion are macroscopically, microscopically, and immunohistochemically identical to FNH but occur in a background of abnormal liver.
(B) Myriad liver conditions predispose patients to formation of FNH-like lesions, all of which share a vascular disturbance as the inciting cause
(C) FNH-like lesions carry no risk for malignant degeneration.
(D) None of the above.
??
**Ans: D**
ABC皆正確
- FNH-like lesion指在肝臟異常背景下與FNH相同的病變(macroscopically, microscopically, and immunohistochemically identical to FNH)
- 病理與FNH相同，所以不是惡性的。carry no risk for malignant degeneration
- 肝臟異常背景：都有**血管異常**作為成因。all of which share a vascular disturbance as the inciting cause.
| 異常在...         |                                                                                                          |
| ----------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Outflow**       | Budd-Chiari syndrome, Fontan-associated liver disease, other forms of cardiogenic congestive hepatopathy |
| **Inflow**        | Congenital absence of portal vein, cavernous malformation, hereditary hemorrhagic telangiectasia         |
| **Microvascular** | Cirrhosis, nodular regenerative hyperplasia, chemotherapy induced                                        |
