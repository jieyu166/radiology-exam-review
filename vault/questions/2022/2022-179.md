#交換 #2022交換 #PED Which of the following description is **false**?
(A) Rhabdomyosarcomas are the most common malignant tumors of the urinary bladder in children
(B) Bladder rhabdomyosarcomas are typically large and nodular and frequently associated with urinary tract obstruction
(C) 14 to 16% of these patients have a 3-year event-free survival, compared with an overall 3-year event-free survival rate of approximately 30%
(D) Operation is normally the first line of treatment for rhabdomyosarcoma, before chemotherapy or radiation therapy
??
**Ans: D**
但C也是錯的
- C. Copy自paper但主詞不明，這段原文也亂寫。根據其引用的J Clin Oncol 2008; 26(14): 2384–2389.，有meta的病人3年存活率34%，event-free survival (EFS) 27%。病人有0-1項risk factor EFS為44%，兩項以上為14%。Risk factor含：
	- (1) Age <=1 or >=10 (RR=1.6)
	- (2) 在unfavorable site長出(limb及其他罕見位置) (RR=1.4)
	- (3) cortical bone or bone marrow involvement (RR=1.4)
	- (4) meta至3處以上 (RR=1.4)
- 侵犯骨頭僅為其中一項risk factor，且須有兩項以上存活率才降至14%左右。
D.	**錯誤**。通常化療為第一線，之後會開刀或radiation therapy
