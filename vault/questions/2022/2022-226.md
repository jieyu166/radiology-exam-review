#交換 #2022交換 #Unknown Which of the following statement for hepatic lymphoma is **correct**?
(A) Primary hepatic lymphoma is common
(B) D/Dx primary or 2nd lymphoma is easy
(C) Transient increased perilesional enhancement is common on MRI
(D) All of above
??
**Ans: C**
- (A) Primary hepatic lymphoma (PHL) **很罕見** (approximately 100 described cases)，要發現 liver tumor 後 6 個月內沒有出現 distant lymphadenopathy, splenomegaly, bone marrow disease, leukemia 這些情形才能下這個診斷
- (B) Primary 和 secondary hepatic lymphoma 影像上難以區分
