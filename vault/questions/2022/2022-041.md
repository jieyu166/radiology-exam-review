#交換 #2022交換 #ABD Regarding imaging detection of HCC, which of the following statements is **true**?
(A) Ultrasound is the most sensitive imaging modality
(B) Gadoxetic acid enhanced MRI provides the best sensitivity in detecting HCC
(C) LI-RADS 5 lesion means 100% HCC
(D) HCC not possibly belongs to the LR-RADS M category
??
**Ans: B**
 DWI+HBP準確度最高，很多文獻也支持，算是共識了
(A)  診斷HCC，傳統的hyperenhancement+washout specificity雖然很高，sensitivity 其實不太好。<2cm Sensitivity大家都很差，且超音波最差。MRI比CT稍微好一點，AUC稍微高一些
(C) LR-5 lesions有 95%是 HCC and 98% 是 malignant tumor。
(D)可以是表現不典型的 HCC，不過英文文法有點怪。
