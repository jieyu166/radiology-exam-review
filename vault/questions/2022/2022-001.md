#交換 #2022交換 #Breast Which is **incorrect** with regarding to the trade-offs in the design of DBT (digital breast tomosynthesis) systems?
(A) Wider sweep angle may improve the depiction of masses and architectural distortion.
(B) Wider sweep angle may be superior for depicting microcalcification morphology.
(C) Wider sweep angles incur the penalty of longer imaging times and may be associated with increased patient motion artifact.
(D) Wider sweep requires a greater number of projections.
??
**Ans: B**
- 增加掃描角度 sweep angle 可以提高垂直解析度 Vertical (z-axis) resolution，提高重疊組織結構的解析度， 對判讀 masses and architectural distortion 有幫助。
- 看 microcalcification 減少 sweep angle 會較好看。也減少motion artifact。
