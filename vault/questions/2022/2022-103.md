#交換 #2022交換 #CV Regarding the time of rescan for individuals with a coronary artery calcium score of 0, which of the follow description is **true**?
(A) 7-10 years for individuals with low 10-year cardiovascular disease risk (<5% risk).
(B) 5-7 years for individuals with intermediate risk (5%-20% risk)
(C) 3 years for individuals with high risk (>20% risk) with diabetes mellitus
(D) No need to rescan since individual with a coronary artery calcium score of 0 will never have atherosclerotic cardiovascular disease.
??
**Ans: C**
From Radiology: Cardiothoracic Imaging 2021; 3(1):e200484
A. **錯誤**。5-7 years
B. **錯誤**。3-5 years
C. **正確**。
D. **錯誤**。~~never~~
