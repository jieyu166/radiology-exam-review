#交換 #2022交換 #MSK 有關 Multiple myeloma 下列何者有**誤**?
(A) FDG-PET might be useful to assess extent of disease
(B) Associated POEMS syndrome (polyneuropathy, organomegaly, endocrine disorders, monoclonal gammopathy, skin changes)
(C) Bone destruction with prominent new bone formation, and bone scan usually increased uptake
(D) Punch-out lytic lesions in ribs and skull of adults usually due to multiple myeloma
??
**Ans: C**
Vast majority of the lesions are purely lytic. Bone scans usually do not contribute significant information due to potential lack of osteoblastic activity.
