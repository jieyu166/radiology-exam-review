#交換 #2022交換 #NR Regarding to spinal cord infarction, which of the following statements are **wrong**?
(A) Spinal angiography is modality of choice for diagnosis.
(B) It can be normal on MRI in the first few hours.
(C) Typical presentation is sudden onset paraplegia.
(D) Treatment is usually supportive.
??
**Ans: A**
Spinal angiography 只有在 noninvasive vascular imaging 有懷疑其他病灶(vascular lesion)時才會考慮，否則 MRI 通常已經足以診斷
