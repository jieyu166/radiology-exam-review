#交換 #2022交換 #CV The latest guidelines recommend cardiac MRI early after an acute myocardial infarction (MI) for which of the following indications?
(A) Always performed after an acute MI.
(B) As a first-line modality for evaluation of patients in unstable condition.
(C) For adenosine stress cardiac MRI to be performed before reperfusion therapy.
(D) As an alternative when echocardiography is suboptimal
??
**Ans: D**
- AB不符合常規。懷疑STEMI會直接做cath(不要delay病人)，除非懷疑aortic dissection或tamponade不然不會用noninvasive imaging，而超音波是最常用的。近年coronary CTA可用在low-intermediate risk的病人，可參考CAD-RADS相關文章。MRI在急性期角色仍是limited role。
- Stress testing, including adenosine stress cardiac MRI, can be used to evaluate myocardial ischemia and viability, including multivessel disease. 這段是放在early after PCI，不是before reperfusion therapy(急性期作stress test?) C是錯的
- PCI 1周內算是early，排影像的目的有measure ventricular function, detect early postacute MI complications, and exclude LV thrombosis。當心超影像suboptimal quality or inconclusive，MRI可以是alternative。D是對的
