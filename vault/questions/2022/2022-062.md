#交換 #2022交換 #CH About the situs, which ONE of the following is **WRONG**?
(A) Visceral situs solitus means liver at the right side and the stomach, spleen at the left side of the upper abdomen.
(B) Symmetrically early branching of the bronchi indicates bilateral left-sidedness of the bronchopulmonary situs.
(C) Bilateral symmetrical pyramid shape right atrial appendages are the presentation of bilateral right-sidedness of the atrial situs.
(D) Situs ambiguous represents by discordance between visceral, bronchopulmonary and atrial situs.
??
**Ans: B**
bronchus 的分支位置可以根據與 pulmonary artery 的相對位置分成 eparterial (高於 PA 就分支)和hyparterial (低於 PA 才分支)，eparterial bronchus 其實就是 right superior lobar bronchus，所以兩側都出現 eparterial bronchus 的話表示兩邊都是右邊的肺(trilobed)，也就是 right isomerism
