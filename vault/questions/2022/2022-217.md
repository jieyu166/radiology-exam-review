#交換 #2022交換 #Unknown 在成人天先性心臟病中，repaired TOF 是最常見的診斷，這些病人在幼年時接受tetralogy of Fallot （TOF）的全矯正手術，約在中年時開始因為長期嚴重的 pulmonary regurgitation(PR) 和心肌瘢痕而開始出現 arrhythmia 甚至 sudden cardiac death （SCD）。下列敘述合者為真？
(A) Cardiac magnetic resonance imaging is the gold standard imaging modality for quantification of right ventricular size and function in patients with repaired TOF.
(B) RV end-diastolic volume index =<160mL/m2 indicates severe RV dilation.
(C) Risk factors for SCD do not include LV diastolic dysfunction.
(D) In patients with repaired TOF and moderate or greater PR who are undergoing cardiac surgery for a separate lesion (RVOT aneurysm, VSD, aortic root replacement…), it is not reasonable to concurrently perform pulmonary valve replacement.
??
**Ans: A**
(A) 正確，copy自paper。CMR是看RV size, function的gold standard。也可以看valve regurgitation and pulmonary and systemic flows, delineating pulmonary artery anatomy and detection of scar tissue in the ventricular myocardium.
(B) RV EDV index **>=** 160mL/m2:  severe RV dilation.
(C) Additional risk factors for SCD include:
- a. **LV systolic or diastolic dysfunction**
- b. Nonsustained VT, QRS duration ≥180 ms
- c. Extensive RV fibrosis by CMR
(D) In patients with repaired TOF and moderate or greater PR who are undergoing cardiac surgery for a separate lesion (eg, RVOT aneurysm, TR, branch PA stenosis, residual VSD, arrhythmia ablation, coronary artery revascularization, aortic root replacement), it may be **reasonable** to concurrently perform pulmonary valve replacement.
