#交換 #2022交換 #Breast Which choice is **not** a BI-RADS category 3 lesion?
(A) A noncalcified solid masses with a round or oval contour and at least 75% circumscribed margins.
(B) A benign-appearing solid mass demonstrates growth, and interval change supersedes benign morphology.
(C) A solitary group of round microcalcifications.
(D) Developing calcifications that appear to be most likely, but are not definitely, vascular.
(E) Focal asymmetries without associated calcifications or architectural distortion.
??
**Ans: B**
ACE. **適合短期追蹤的乳房攝影發現**：包括（a）無鈣化的實心腫塊，其形狀為圓形或橢圓形，且至少有75%的邊緣清晰，（b）一組孤立的圓形微鈣化，以及（c）無相關鈣化或結構扭曲的局部不對稱
B. supersede取代。當benign mass有growth, interval change讓你擔心時。根據2016 RG此時應該要biopsy，要biopsy就是BI-RADS 4。 If a benign-appearing solid mass demonstrates growth, interval change trumps or supersedes benign morphology, and biopsy is warranted despite probably benign features
