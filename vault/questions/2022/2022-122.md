#交換 #2022交換 #MSK Which description about characteristics of [[Osteochondroma]] at thorax is **true**?
(A) Corticomedullary continuity with the parent bone is one of the diagnostic imaging features.
(B) It usually shows significant in-phase signal loss at chemical shift imaging.
(C) Patients with multiple osteochondromas have about 70% lifetime risk of malignant degeneration.
(D) More commonly occurs in the scapula than in the ribs.
??
**Ans: A**
A. **正確**。
B. **錯誤**。~~in-phase signal loss~~ Radiopaedia提到T2 hyperintense cartilaginous cap，沒提到in/out phase
C. **錯誤**。2.7-5.0%
D. **錯誤**。根據[[2022 RG Diagnostic and Imaging Approaches to Chest Wall Lesions.pdf]]，rib比scapula常見，最常見在 **costochondral junction**
