#交換 #2022交換 #Unknown Match the following radiographic findings or patient history with the most closely associated cause of interstitial lung disease. Which is **wrong**?
(A) Lymphangioleiomyomatosis– chylous pleural effusion
(B) Lymphocytic interstitial pneumonia– Sjogren Syndrome
(C) Scleroderma–esophageal stricture
(D) Asbestosis– chryostile exposure
??
**Ans: C**
(A) LAM: 可能合併 chylous pleural effusion and pneumothorax; a/w Tuberous sclerosis → 可能同時表現 multifocal micronodular pneumatocyte hyperplasia(MMPH): small nodule
(B) LIP: 25% Sjögren syndrome 相關
(C) Scleroderma: 80% 有 esophageal **dilatation**，肺部多以 NSIP 表現(peribrnochial reticulation, traction bronchiectasis +/- GGO, in subpleural and lower lung distribution)
(D) Asbestosis: chrysotile(白石棉) exposure
