#交換 #2022交換 #NR Which of the following muscles is located posterior to the thyroid lobes?
(A) sternohyoid
(B) omohyoid
(C) longus colli
(D) sternocleidomastoid
??
**Ans: C**
Platysma是最表面的Superficial fascia的一部分，往下是deep cervical fascia，其superf. layer含有SCM，trapezius，parotid and submaxillary glands，接著的middle layer，前半是strap muscles=infrahyoid muscles，包含sternohyoid, sternothyroid, thyrohyoid or omohyoid muscles，後半包覆thyroid。Deep fascia的deep layer在prevertebral muscle之前。longus coli在脖子後側，spine周圍。 From Surgical Anatomy and Technique (Skandalakis 5th)
