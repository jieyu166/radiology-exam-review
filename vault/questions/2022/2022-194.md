#交換 #2022交換 #Physics About delayed adverse reactions (DARs) to iodinated contrast media (ICM), which of the following are **not** correct?
(A) DARs to iodinated reactions are thought to be B-cell mediated.
(B) Typically present as mild to moderate maculopapular rash, which are often self-limiting.
(C) DARs tend to manifest more quickly in people who have had a prior reaction to ICM.
(D) A higher rate of DARs with dimeric iso-osmolar ICM compared with monomeric low osmolality ICM.
??
**Ans: A**
T-cell
B 應該是對的。 DAR 最常見表現為皮膚反應。表現: rash and itching, deemed to be a macular to maculopapular exanthema。
C 是對的。 皮膚反應第一次通常要 2-10 天，Reexposure 只要 1-2 天。
D 是對的。Delay reaction risk factor 有非離子性二聚體(dimer) ；Acute reaction risk factor 有離子性高張對比劑。
 AJR 2014;203: 1163-1170. 2020 對比劑手冊
- 顯影劑是藥物引起皮膚反應的第三大原因，僅次於抗生素跟 NSAID。
- 機轉可能和 T cells 有關。Hapten concept: Ag-hapten pair 被 APC 辨認引起過敏反應。
- 顯影劑半衰期約 1.5 小時。
- Delay reaction 大部分在 3 小時~2 天發生。
	- 皮膚反應第一次通常要 2-10 天，Reexposure 只要 1-2 天。
	- DAR 最常見表現為皮膚反應。表現: rash and itching, deemed to be a macular to maculopapular exanthema。
	- Delay reaction risk factor 有非離子性二聚體(dimer) ；Acute reaction risk factor 有離子性高張對比劑。
