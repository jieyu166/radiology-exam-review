#交換 #2022交換 #Unknown Which description about orbital infantile hemangioma is **wrong**?
(A) It is the most common benign orbital tumor of infancy
(B) It is a kind of vascular malformation and high flow arteriovenous shunt.
(C) When it accompanied with cerebrovascular and posterior fossa anomalies, PHACE syndrome should be considered.
(D) Not present at birth, rapid initial growth in infancy, then slow, spontaneous regression
??
**Ans: B**
A. H&N infant最常見的tumor，正確。
B. 非AV shunt，也是high flow
C. **正確**。PHACE：**P**osterior fossa malformations, **H**emangiomas, **A**rterial cerebrovascular abnormalities, **C**ardiovascular anomalies, **E**ye abnormalities
D. **正確**。出生不會有，數周後才會顯現。在嬰兒期有增生，長大後在childhood自然消退
