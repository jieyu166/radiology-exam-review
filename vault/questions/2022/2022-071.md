**Ans: C**
Probable 沒有honeycombing
## 組織學
Pathological features of UIP/IPF pattern:
- dense fibrosis, which causes remodelling of lung architecture with frequent honeycomb fibrosis;
- fibroblast foci, which are typically scattered at the **edges** of dense scars;
- patchy lung involvement;
- frequent subpleural, paraseptal, and peripheral acinar distribution.
