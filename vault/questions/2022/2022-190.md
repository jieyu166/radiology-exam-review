#交換 #2022交換 #NR Which of the following ultrasound characteristic is **NOT** included in ACR TI-RADS assessment of thyroid lesions:
(A) Width and height of the lesion
(B) Presence of a hypoechoic halo
(C) Presence of macrocalcifications
(D) Extra-thyroidal extension
??
**Ans: B**
A. 決定是否taller than wide. CD都有。
- Echogenic halo 文章中認為不具有區別性，和其他margin也不互斥，TI-RADS不鳥他。量大小時，halo應該一起測量。
