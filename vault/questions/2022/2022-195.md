#交換 #2022交換 #2023交換 #Physics Which of the following statement for adverse drug reaction of iodinated contrast media (ICM) is **wrong**?
(A) The majority of adverse side effects to low-osmolality contrast media are mild no-life-threatening events that usually require only observation, reassurance, and/or supportive measures.
(B) Severe and potentially life-threatening adverse events continue to occur rarely and unpredictably.
(C) Nearly all life-threatening contrast reactions occur within the first 20 minutes after contrast media injection.
(D) The frequency of allergic-like and physiologic adverse events related to the intravascular administration of ICM is low and has decreased considerably with changes in usage from ionic high-osmolality contrast media to nonionic low-osmolality contrast media.
(E) Allergy reactions to ICM manifest similarly to allergic reactions seen with other drugs and allergen, an antigen-antibody response could be always identified.
??
**Ans: E**
**答案 E——類過敏(allergic-like)反應雖表現類似其他藥物過敏,但「一定能找到 antigen-antibody response(抗原-抗體反應)」為錯誤**:類過敏反應不一定能找到抗原抗體證據、與劑量無關。(A) 低滲對比劑多數副作用輕微、僅需觀察安撫支持、(B) 嚴重致命反應仍罕見且不可預測、(C) 幾乎所有致命反應在注射後 20 分鐘內、(D) 由 ionic HOCM 改用 nonionic LOCM 後不良反應頻率明顯下降——皆正確。
