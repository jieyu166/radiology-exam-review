#交換 #2022交換 #NR According to the 2018 Thyroid Imaging Reporting and Data System (TI-RADS), which Committee of the American College of Radiology (ACR) published, which of the following statement is **wrong**?
(A) ACR TI-RADS is founded on the evaluation of US features in five categories—composition, echogenicity, shape, margin, and echogenic foci—in which each feature is assigned 0–3 points.
(B) The nodule’s point total determines its risk level, which ranges from TR1 (benign) to TR5 (highly suspicious).
(C) Because the threshold diameters for mildly and moderately suspicious nodules (TR3 and TR4) are smaller than in other systems, adherence to ACR TI-RADS will result in more biopsies of benign nodules.
(D) The reporting process must be efficient and account for circumstances such as patient or referring physician preferences, previous biopsies, interval growth, and lymphadenopathy.
??
**Ans: C**
threshold larger 那biopsy的數量應該較少。~~more~~ fewer
- ACR TI-RADS對於>=1cm的nodule和其他guideline是一致的。但對mildly suspicious,  moderately suspicious nodules的threshold分別為 2.5和 1.5 cm。比其他guideline高(ATA, Korean Society of Thyroid Radiology)(文章Size Thresholds for FNA段落)
