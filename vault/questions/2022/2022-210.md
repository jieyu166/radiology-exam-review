#交換 #2022交換 #Unknown Which of the following is **NOT** characterized by multiple small 1- to 5-mm pulmonary nodules?
(A) Metastases
(B) Asbestosis
(C) Tuberculosis
(D) Silicosis
(E) Langerhans cell histiocytosis
??
**Ans: B**
(A) Metastasis: hematogenous spread, random distribution nodules
(B) Asbestosis: 以 interstitial and pleural change 為主(**fibrosis**)
(C) TB: miliary TB, random distribution nodules
(D) Silicosis: 屬於 pneumociniosis, **perilymphatic** nodules
(E) Pulmonary LCH: nodules and cysts 表現為主, typically 1-5mm nodules
