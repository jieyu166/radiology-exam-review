#交換 #2022交換 #CV About coronary artery anomaly, the presence of an intramural segment can be inferred by
(A) The length of inter-arterial course
(B) an elliptical cross section of the artery
(C) location of origin
(D) All above are true
??
**Ans: B**
The spatial resolution of CT is not high enough to evaluate the outer wall of the aorta, overlying the compressed intramural segment. However, the presence of an intramural segment can be inferred by **an elliptical cross section of the artery**.
