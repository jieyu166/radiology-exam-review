#交換 #2022交換 #NR For pediatric thyroid sonography, cystic lesions exhibiting an echogenic dot and posterior comet tail artifact are referred to as colloids. What are they called when they are ≦0.3 cm?
(A) Colloid cysts
(B) Colloid follicles
(C) Colloid blebs
(D) None of the above
??
**Ans: B**
Colloid follicles: ≦0.3 cm ；Colloid cysts: 0.3~1 cm.
- Polycystic thyroid disease: Rare. Multiple cysts, either coalescing or separate.
- True epithelial-lined cysts: Rare. Entirely cystic, anechoic thin-walled lesions.
