#交換 #2022交換 #2023交換 #NR Which of the following statement about the 2021 WHO Classification of Tumors of the Central Nervous System (the 5th-edition) is **wrong**?
(A) WHO CNS5 has changed all CNS WHO tumor grades to Arabic numerals.
(B) There is neither a CNS WHO grade 1 IDH-mutant astrocytoma nor a CNS WHO grade 4 meningioma
(C) An NOS suffix indicates that the diagnostic information (histological or molecular) necessary to assign a specific WHO diagnosis is not available, providing an alert to the oncologist that a molecular work-up has not been undertaken or failed technically
(D) An NEC suffix, indicates that the necessary diagnostic testing has been successfully performed but that the results do not readily allow for a WHO diagnosis
(E) None of the above.
??
**Ans: E**
A. 命名：病理, 基因, CNS WHO grade 12345 改用阿拉伯數字，以前用羅馬數字
C. NOS 如果醫院無法驗基因診斷沒做完，要標 NOS
D. NEC 驗基因跟目前分類配不起來。如 diffuse glioma 驗出來是 IDH-wild type
B. Astrocytoma, IDH mutant 沒有 grade 1，只有 2-4。Grade 2 diffuse，Grade 3 anaplastic，Grade 4 原本的 secondary GBM 跟 CDKN2A/B homozygous deletion
Meningioma 沒有 grade 4 ，只有 1-3(一般, atypical, anaplastic)。
