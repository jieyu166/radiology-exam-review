#交換 #2022交換 #Unknown Which is the most common complication of acetabular dysplasia?
(A) Chondral and labral damage
(B) Subchondral fracture
(C) Hip instability
(D) Early hip osteoarthritis
??
**Ans: D**
- Acetabular Dysplasia是DDH的一種形式
- 25-50%會有early hip OA，其他complication包含chondral and labral damage, subchondral fractures, hip instability, hip subluxation or dislocation (Radiopaedia)
### Acetabular labral tear
- 最常見於 FAI 病人，通常破在 anterior-superior。下方沒有 labrum 而是transverse ligament
- 有 intrasubstance, chondrolabral junction tear 兩種
- Secondary findings: paralabral cyst formation, chondral injury, and subchondral edema, osteoarthritic changes
- Mimics DDx: sublabral sulcus(no secondary degenerative changes, 打藥不會擴張 labrum width)
