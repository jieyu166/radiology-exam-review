### Renal lymphoma
Core Radiology, Diagnostic ultrasound. 比例來自DI。
- Most common high-grade B-cell, non-Hodgkin比Hodgkin多。雙側比單側多。3-8% lymphoma病人會involve 腎臟
- 可能是血行性或來自retroperitoneum。Primary非常少(<1%)。腎臟沒有lymphoid tissue。
- 有數種型態
	- Most common: multiple lymphoma. mild enhancement. (50%)
	- Solitary mass
	- Diffuse infiltration, enlarged kidney (10%)
	- Extension of retroperitoneal LNs (25%)
