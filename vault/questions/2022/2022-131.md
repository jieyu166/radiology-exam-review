#交換 #2022交換 #NR In terms of craniosynostosis, which one is **wrong**?
(A) Frontal plagiocephaly may result in Harlequin eye deformity.
(B) Syndromic synostoses may present with vertebral anomalies.
(C) Occipital (Posterior) plagiocephaly is the most common type.
(D) Apert syndrome is one of the syndromic synostoses.
??
**Ans: C**
A. 單側丹鳳眼也算
B. May 通常是對的。Crouzon 可能有 cervical fusion，多跟 C 有關：Chiari I(~70%), hydroCephalus, Callosal agenesis, cervical fusion (Radiopaedia)。Apert可能有vertebral fusion，比較常提到的症狀有 Brachycephaly(Coronal), Syndactyly
C. **錯誤**。最常見是 scaphocephaly
D. 比較常考的有 Apert, Crouzon syndrome
