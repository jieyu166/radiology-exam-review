#交換 #2022交換 #ABD Which of the following description is **NOT** for PI-RADS score 4 of peripheral zone lesion on T2 weighted imaging?
(A) homogenous moderate hypointense focus/ mass
(B) < 1.5 cm in diameter
(C) Linear or wedge-shaped hypointensity or diffuse mild hypointensity
(D) Circumscribed and confined to prostate
??
**Ans: C**
是 T2 PZ PI-RADS 2.；D. 超出 prostate =有 EPE 算 PI-RADS 5，所以 D 是 PI-RADS 4

## Enhancement
![ADD PI-RADS CE 1](data/images/obsidian/add-pi-rads-ce-1-2908d2b7.jpg)
- 左邊是typical curve，現在只看前面的斜率(early enhancement)
- washin map很亮/紅色，或不晚於peripheral zone enhancement就算是early enhancement
- map比較好看，沒有就看dynamic study ![ADD PI-RADS CE 2](data/images/obsidian/add-pi-rads-ce-2-533b8356.jpg)
- dynamic腳色目前還好，現在只看一開始的斜率wash-in
- PIRADS是看early enhancement，其實也不是wash in
- 理論上tumor進去出來都比別人快。Ktrans contrast 進去 Kep contrast出來 AUC是指下面面積通常較大 現在都是參考 如果當major criteria那sensitivity會下降所以PI-RADS不放入
- BPH會enhancement，不是cancer ![ADD BPH 1](data/images/obsidian/add-bph-1-0c87ac5a.jpg)

## EPE
| Grade             | 不同grade切下病理EPE比例 |
| ----------------- | ------------------------ |
| ![ADD PI-RADS EPE 1](data/images/obsidian/add-pi-rads-epe-1-606c641a.jpg) | ![ADD PI-RADS EPE 2](data/images/obsidian/add-pi-rads-epe-2-912c1403.jpg)        |
- 超過prostate capsule叫EPE，講EPE因為prostate其實沒有capsule
	- tumor 在posterior peripheral zone要小心看是否involve neurovascular bundle
- EPE grade
	- Grade 1: margin >1.5cm OR 稍微凸出來。切下來真的有EPE機會是1/4
	- Grade 2: margin >1.5cm + 稍微凸出來，大約40%有
	- Grade 3: 很明顯凸出。目前部長只把grade 3算EPE
		- EPE=T3a理論上不能開刀
		- 這樣false negative機會較低
- ESUR有自己的score也很接近，但上面用的文章較多
## PI-RADS
Prostate 解剖分區 ![ADD Prostate anatomy](data/images/obsidian/add-prostate-anatomy-442291d6.png)
| PI-RADS | likelyhood |
| ------- | ---------- |
| 1       | <5%        |
| 2       | low        |
| 3       | 12-33%     |
| 4       | 70%        |
| 5       | 95%        |
|         |            |
- 不到4分impression會打No mpMRI evidence of clinical significant cancer，但我們還是會寫最高的lesion。
- PI-RADS3還是有1/3的機會是，可能要開刀或active survillence
- PI-RADS4-5就有機會是真正的prostate ca
	- PI-RADS5還是有可能不是。之前年會閱片分析 去年/前年BCG granuloma在prostate會被當成PI-RADS 5
