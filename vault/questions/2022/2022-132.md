#交換 #2022交換 #NR Which following syndrome is characterized by craniosynostosis (premature fusion of calvarial sutures) and syndactyly?
(A) Joubert syndrome
(B) Klippel-Feil syndrome
(C) Apert syndrome (type I acrocephalosyndactyly)
(D) CHARGE syndrome
(E) Gorlin syndrome
??
**Ans: C**
A. Joubert: 第四腦室像蝙蝠(batwing 4th V)，上小腦腳像臼齒(Molar sign)
B. Klippel-Feil spectrum/syndrome: C-spine 不正常的 fusion。segmentation failure of ≥ 2 cervical vertebrae ± thoracic, lumbar segmentation failure。典型 triad (33-50%): Short neck, low posterior hairline, and limited cervical motion(DI: Pediatrics)
C. 如表格。Apert, Pfeiffer 可能有 syndactyly
D. CHARGE: C = Coloboma and cranial nerves (眼器官先天裂開與腦神經缺損) ；H = Heart defects (心臟缺損)；A = Atresia of the choanae (後鼻孔閉鎖) ；R = Retardation of growth and development (生長與發育遲緩) ；G = Genital and urinary abnormalities (生殖泌尿道系統異常) ；E = Ear abnormalities and/or hearing loss (耳朵異常或聽力喪失)
E. Gorlin-Goltz syndrome= Basal cell nevus syndrome。罕見，Autosomal dominant disorder。影像可能有 multiple odontogenic keratocysts(下巴 multiple lytic pericoronal cysts), basal cell carcinoma(BCC), medulloblastoma, intracranial dural calcifications, bifid ribs (DI: H&N)
