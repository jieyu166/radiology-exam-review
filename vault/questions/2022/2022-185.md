#交換 #2022交換 #US 有關 elastography 的敘述下列何者為**非**?
(A) Strain elastography also named static elastography or compression elastography.
(B) Shearwaves travel faster in harder tissues.
(C) Maximum scale often sets to 7.7 m/s (180kPa) for breast condition.
(D) Diagnostic performance of shearwave elastography and strain elastography are similar
(E) We can reclassify soft BI-RADS 4B masses as BI-RADS 3.
??
**Ans: E**
BI-RADS 3 or 4A masses 可看 elastography 來 upgrade/downgrade。而從4B降到3降太多，所以錯誤。
- 但是DI: Breast裡面對4B lesion是說possibly 4B, even 4B。或許日後會有變動
- 4B以上lesion不能因elastography免除biopsy，而BI-RADS 2 lesion也不能因elastography很硬而直接切片。(Should not be used to avert biopsy of masses assessed as BI-RADS 4B, 4C, or 5 on B mode , nor to prompt biopsy of BI-RADS 2. )
- 被 upgrade 的可能是 high-grade, often triple-negative IDCs
- 被 downgrade 的可能是 fibroadenomas
- Complicated cysts 可從 BI-RADS 3 downgrade 到 BI-RADS 2
