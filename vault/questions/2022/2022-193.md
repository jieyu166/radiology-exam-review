#交換 #2022交換 #US Which of the following concerning gray-scale imaging is **incorrect**?
(A) By increasing gray-scale gain settings, the brightness of the entire image increases.
(B) By increasing power output, the brightness of the entire image increases.
(C) Increasing the field of view decreases the frame rate.
(D) Higher dynamic range produces an image with higher contrast.
??
**Ans: D**
A. Echogenicity 增加，正確
B. Echogenicity 增加，正確。但 artifact 也可能增加。
C. 可以指寬度跟深度，正確。
D. Less compression (i.e., a higher dynamic range) allows for distinction of subtle differences in echo amplitude and produces an image that appears smoother.細節增加但對比減少，錯誤。
