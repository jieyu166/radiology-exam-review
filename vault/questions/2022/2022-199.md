#交換 #2022交換 #Physics Gadoxetic acid應用在診斷hepatic mass lesions漸顯重要，下列何者**不是**它的缺點？
(A) Lower arterial phase and venous phase enhancement
(B) Higher frequency of imaging artifacts during arterial phase due to transient dyspnea and/or transient severe motion
(C) Decreased uptake in liver parenchyma due to liver dysfunction and/or fibrosis
(D) Low detectablility of small lesions during hepatobiliary phase
??
**Ans: D**
High detectability
缺點如ABC。給藥時病人反應較大A phase，可能會晃動。另外Primovist濃度較低，也讓A phase較不明顯。
