#交換 #2022交換 #Unknown Which of the following sequence combinations is considered the mainstay of a cardiac MRI protocol in patients with acute chest pain?
(A) T2-weighted, early gadolinium enhancement (EGE), and myocardial feature tracking sequences.
(B) Cine steady-state free-precession (SSFP), EGE, and first-pass perfusion sequences.
(C) Cine SSFP, T2-weighted, and late gadolinium enhancement (LGE) sequences.
(D) T2-weighted, first-pass perfusion, and EGE sequences
??
**Ans: C**
Cine SSFP, T2-weighted, and LGE sequences 是核心 protocol。
- Cine SSFP 解析度高，tissue contrast 好，可評估心肌 motion 和 volume。也可看 valvular disfunction, thrombosis, and pericardial effusion
- T2WI 看 edema，代表有 acute injury。Chronic MI 不會有 edema。最常用black-blood T2 STIR FSE sequence
- LGE: 正常 myocardium 信號被 null，fibrosis, necrosis 會有高訊號。其看的 no flow phenomenon/intramyocardial hemorrhage 是 late MVO(microvascular obstruction)。Late MVO 評估預後比較好。
