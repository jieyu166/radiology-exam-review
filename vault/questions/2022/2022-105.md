#交換 #2022交換 #CV About Eisenmenger syndrome, which is **wrong**?
(A) The is a complication of an uncorrected high-flow, high-pressure congenital heart anomaly leading to chronic pulmonary arterial hypertension and shunt reversal.
(B) Ventricular septal defect (VSD) is the most cause
(C) Never apparent in atrial septal defects (ASD) patient
(D) Related to atrioventricular septal defect (AVSD) or patent ductus arteriosus (PDA).
??
**Ans: C**
The Eisenmenger syndrome is a complication of an uncorrected high-flow, high-pressure congenital heart anomaly leading to chronic pulmonary arterial hypertension and shunt reversal (原本 left-to-right shunt, 因為 Pul hypertension 變成 right-to-left
shunt).
最常見原因為 ventricular septal defect(VSD, 最常見), atrioventricular septal defect(AVSD), patent ductus arteriolus(PDA)。atrial septal defect(ASD)少見但仍有案例
