#交換 #2022交換 #CV Which feature is **not** included in the classic hypertrophic cardiomyopathy phenotypes in cardiac MRI?
(A) Hypertrophied, nondilated and hyperdynamic LV (left ventricle)
(B) Myocardial crypts
(C) LV outflow tract obstruction
(D) Diastolic dysfunction
??
**Ans: B**
Myocardial clefts or crypts are congenital abnormalities related to myocardial fibre or fascicle disarray and have been described in healthy volunteers, as well as those with classic HCM (< 5%) →錯
Diastolic dysfunction is one of the early hallmark features of HCM, and it is related to myocardial disarray and fibrosis, even in the absence of LV hypertrophy. →正確
