#交換 #2017交換 #CH Which is the **most common** primary osseous malignancy of the chest wall?
(A) Osteosarcoma.
(B) Myeloma.
(C) Ewing Sarcoma.
(D) Chondrosarcoma.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**。**Chondrosarcoma 為胸壁最常見原發惡性骨腫瘤**（好發肋軟骨交界、含軟骨基質鈣化）。osteosarcoma、Ewing sarcoma、myeloma 較少。
