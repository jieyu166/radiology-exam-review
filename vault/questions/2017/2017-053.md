#交換 #2017交換 #MSK Stable features of an intertrochanteric fracture include which of the following entites?
(A) Loss of medial buttress
(B) Comminution
(C) Reverse obliquity fracture line prientation
(D) Facture line extending from the greater to lesser trochanter
??
**Ans: D**
- A. ![ADD femoral neck 2](data/images/obsidian/add-femoral-neck-2-c1ac5702.png) (圖 From [Orthogeriatrics](https://link.springer.com/chapter/10.1007/978-3-030-48126-1_9)) medial buttress: inferomedial femoral neck cortex最厚的地方，旁邊這些很厚的cortex叫做calcar femorale，支撐femoral neck，implant釘在這也會比較穩。所以受損的話會不穩定。
- B. 可能是Type III, IV, V都是 unstable. 3-4 parts
- C. A rare variant. 很不穩定，adductor muscle會讓displacement惡化。
- D. Type I, II. 2 parts. reduction效果好，很穩定。
