#交換 #2017交換 #2018交換 #2022交換 #2023交換 #ABD Features of gastrinomas, which one is **wrong**?
(A) 30% of pancreatic neuroendocrine tumors
(B) Most common hyperfunctioning neuroendocrine tumor
(C) Associated with Zollinger-Ellison syndrome
(D) 60-80% lesions are malignant
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**（錯誤）。**insulinoma 才是最常見的亢進性/功能性 NET**,非 gastrinoma。gastrinoma 占胰 NET 約 20–30%(A 約略)、與 Zollinger-Ellison 相關(C)、60–80% 惡性(D)皆正確。
