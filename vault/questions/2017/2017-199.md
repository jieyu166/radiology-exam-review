#交換 #2017交換 #ABD 以下關於 renal cell carcinoma (RCC) 的敘述，何者正確?
(A) Clear cell RCC is the most common histologic subtype, accounting for 70% of all RCCs.
(B) Clear cell RCCs typically show hypervascularity on contrast-enhanced studies including computed tomography (CT), magnetic resonance (MR) imaging, and catheter angiography.
(C) Papillary RCCs typically appear hypovascular and homogeneous on imaging studies.
(D) 以上皆正確
??
**Ans: D**
三項皆正確。
(A) 正確:clear cell RCC 為最常見亞型,約占 70%。
(B) 正確:clear cell RCC hypervascular,CT/MR/導管血管攝影皆呈明顯強化。
(C) 正確:papillary RCC 乏血管、均質、低度漸進強化。
(D) **以上皆正確（正解）**。
