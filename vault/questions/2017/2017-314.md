#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Some hepatocellular carcinoma show paradoxical uptake in hepatobiliary phase in liver MRI, which statement for these lesions is **wrong**?
(A) Higher expression of MRP3 transporter
(B) Lower expression of OATP8 transporter
(C) Impairment of bile secretion by tumor compression
(D) None of above
??
**Ans: B**
**B為偽**——矛盾攝取HCC之機轉為OATP8表現量『升高』(非降低),搭配MRP2表現量降低致對比劑細胞質滯留。
(A) MRP3表現量升高:真。
(B) **正解(偽)**:應為OATP8升高非降低。
(C) 腫瘤壓迫致膽汁分泌障礙:真。
(D) 不適用。
