#交換 #2017交換 #CH Regarding idiopathic interstitial pneumonias (IIP), which one of the followings is **true**?
(A) Smoking-related IIPs included hypersensitivity pneumonia (HP), desquamative interstitial pneumonia (DIP) and respiratory bronchiolitis interstitial lung disease (RB-ILD).
(B) RB-ILD is characterized by bronchial wall thickening, centrilobular nodules, patchy ground-glass opacity and often distributed in lower lung zones.
(C) Tree-in-bud appearance in CT is suggestive of IIP entity.
(D) Cryptogenic organizing pneumonia is characterized by subpleural distribution.
??
**Ans: D**
**Cryptogenic organizing pneumonia（COP）典型呈胸膜下／支氣管周圍分布**。
(A) 錯:吸菸相關 IIP 為 RB-ILD 與 DIP;**HP（過敏性肺炎）非吸菸相關、亦非 IIP**。
(B) 錯:RB-ILD **上肺為主**（非下肺）,呈中央小葉結節＋毛玻璃。
(C) 錯:tree-in-bud 提示**小氣道感染／發炎**,非 IIP 的特徵。
(D) **為真（正解）**:COP 以胸膜下／支氣管旁實質化為特徵,可游走。
