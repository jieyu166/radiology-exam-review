#交換 #2017交換 #NR Which one of the following description concerning lacunar infarction is **wrong**?
(A) Ischemia of deep perforators from arterial circle of Willis, the anterior choroidal artery, or the basilar artery
(B) The most frequently affected perforating arteries include the lenticulostriate, the thalamoperforate and the perforators arising from the anterior choroidal artery
(C) Maximum size of the infarction in the acute stage is 15 mm (some sources say 20mm)
(D) Most first-ever symptomatic lacunar infarcts are located in the area supplied by the anterior choroidal artery (posterior limb of internal capsule)
(E) Most lacunar infarcts are asymptomatic
??
**Ans: C**
⚠️ 官方答案為C,但官方詳解本身亦標註此數值文獻不一(15mm或20mm),建議醫師核對原文確認真正錯誤理由。
(A)(B) 深部穿通動脈供血:真。
(C) **正解(標示為錯)**:最大範圍數值有爭議。
(D) 內囊後肢為首次症狀性腔隙梗塞好發部位:真。
(E) 多數無症狀:真。
