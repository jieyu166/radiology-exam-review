#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Multiple patterns of renal and perinephric involvement by lymphoma have been described, 以下何者正確?
(A) The most common pattern, which involves multiple parenchymal lesions, is observed in 50%–60% of patients and is most often unilateral.
(B) A solitary mass is observed in 10%–25% of patients; in these cases, it may be easy to distinguish this condition from renal cell carcinoma.
(C) Lymphomas can also preferentially affect the renal sinuses by mimicking the appearance of urothelial carcinomas.
(D) Diffuse parenchymal infiltration by neoplastic cells may result in enlargement with disruption of the renal contour, but this is usually associated with homogeneous enhancement.
??
**Ans: C**
**淋巴瘤可優先侵犯腎竇,酷似 urothelial（移行細胞）carcinoma**。
(A) 錯:最常見的多發實質腫塊型多為**雙側**（非單側）。
(B) 錯:單發腫塊（10-25%）常**難**與 RCC 區分（非容易）。
(C) **正確（正解）**:可侵犯腎竇酷似 urothelial ca。
(D) 錯:瀰漫浸潤致腎腫大輪廓破壞,多伴**不均（heterogeneous）強化＋局部病灶**（非均質）。
