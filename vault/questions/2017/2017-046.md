(A) Longitudinal fractures lead to bridge formation more often than do horizontal fractures.
(B) The use of an intravenous gadolinium contrast agent is recommened because it makes the fracture more conspicuous at imaging.
(C) Factures that occur in older childen have a worse prognosis than those in younger children.
(D) The degree of comminution of a fracture does not affect the prognosis.
??
**Ans: A**
