#交換 #2017交換 #CV For arrhythmogenic right ventricular dysplasia (ARVD), the typical criteria that can be demonstrated with MRI are:
(A) Fatty infiltration of the RV myocardium.
(B) Aneurysms of the RV and RVOT.
(C) Dilatation of the RV and RVOT.
(D) All of the above.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**（以上皆是）。ARVD 的 MRI 典型表現含 RV 心肌脂肪浸潤(A)、RV/RVOT 動脈瘤(B)、RV/RVOT 擴張(C)。
