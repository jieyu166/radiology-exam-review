#交換 #2017交換 #CV About KAWASAKI disease, which following is not **true**
(A) Mostly involve superficial temporal artery (Giant cell arteritis)
(B) Mostly involve medium-sized arteries
(C) May cause coronary dilatation with aneurysm
(D) Up to 30% of patients exhibit involvement of the CNS
??
**Ans: A**
**A為偽**——川崎病並非主要侵犯顳淺動脈(此為giant cell arteritis的特徵)。
(A) **正解(偽)**。
(B) 屬中血管血管炎:真。
(C) 可致冠狀動脈擴張伴動脈瘤:真。
(D) 中樞神經系統侵犯比例:另一常見誤答陷阱選項。
