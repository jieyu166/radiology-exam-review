#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Pelvic congestion syndrome (PCS) is a cause of chronic pelvic pain; its typical imaging findings include all of the following EXCEPT:
(A) Dilated, tortuous, enhancing tubular structures near the uterus and ovary.
(B) On T1-weighted images, varices may appear as flow voids.
(C) Ultrasound shows slow (<3 cm/s) venous flow in the cranial direction in the left ovarian vein.
(D) More than 50% of women with PCS have associated cystic ovaries.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**（除外）。PCS 的 US 為慢血流（<3 cm/s）且**逆向 caudal/retrograde**（尤左卵巢靜脈），非 cranial 方向。擴張迂曲強化管狀結構(A)、T1WI flow void(B)、>50% 囊性卵巢(D)皆正確。
