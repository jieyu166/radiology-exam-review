#交換 #2017交換 #NR Moyamoya disease is related to following disease except
(A) Sickle cell anemia
(B) Down syndrome
(C) Marfan syndrome
(D) Coarctation of the aorta
(E) All above are related
??
**Ans: E**
**E（All above are related）**——Sickle cell anemia、Down syndrome、Marfan syndrome、主動脈弓縮窄皆與moyamoya病有關聯記載(縮窄關聯性文獻報告較少見但仍存在),故答案為全部皆有關。
