#交換 #2017交換 #Breast Which the following statement is **wrong** about breast MRI? (T2 signal intensity)
(A) T2-weighted signal hyperintensity within the enhancing portion of the lesion is highly suggestive of benign histology.
(B) The assessment of lesions on T2-weighted images is most helpful in younger patients.
(C) Medullary cancers tend to occur in younger women and may have a circumscribed appearance.
(D) The signal intensity of the lesion on T2-weighted images can aid in the differential diagnosis of myxoid fibroadenoma and medullary cancer.
??
**Ans: D**
**T2 訊號無法可靠區分 myxoid fibroadenoma 與 medullary cancer**——髓質癌也常呈 T2 等/高訊號（約 60%），與黏液性纖維腺瘤重疊。
(A) 正確:強化部分 T2 高訊號偏良性組織。
(B) 正確:T2 評估在年輕病人最有幫助。
(C) 正確:髓質癌好發年輕女性、可呈邊界清楚外觀。
(D) **錯誤（正解）**:T2 訊號無法可靠區分二者（髓質癌也常 T2 高）。
