#交換 #2017交換 #NR Which of the following is not correct about Rhombencephalosynapsis?
(A) Absence of the vermis and continuity of the cerebellar hemispheres, dentate nuclei, and superior cerebellar peduncles.
(B) The most common clinical manifestations include truncal and/or limb ataxia, abnormal eye movements, head stereotypies, and delayed motor development.
(C) The majority of patients are nonsyndromic.
(D) It is not associated with other central nervous system anomalies.
??
**Ans: D**
**Rhombencephalosynapsis 常合併其他中樞神經系統異常**，並非孤立不合併。
(A) 正確:小腦蚓部缺失＋半球/齒狀核/上小腦腳融合。
(B) 正確:典型臨床表現為共濟失調、異常眼動、頭部搖擺、動作遲緩。
(C) 正確:多數為非症候群性（但確有部分為症候群性，如 Gómez-López-Hernández 或 VACTERL）。
(D) **不正確（正解）**:常合併視丘融合、下丘融合、透明隔缺失、腦室擴大、顱縫早閉等其他 CNS 異常。
