#交換 #2017交換 #PED Regarding congenital hydronephrosis, which one of the following is the MOST inappropriate?
(A) Regardless of etiology, the goal of management is preservation of renal function.
(B) In most cases, surgical reconstruction cannot be delayed after a period of observation.
(C) The majority of antenatally detected hydronephrosis is non-obstructive and resolves or stabilizes.
(D) A minority of prenatally detected hydronephrosis necessitates surgical intervention in persistently severe cases.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**（最不恰當）。多數產前腎水腫**非阻塞性、可觀察**，手術保留給腎功能下降/反覆感染，非「不可延後」。保存腎功能為目標(A)、多數非阻塞會緩解(C)、少數持續嚴重才手術(D)皆正確。
