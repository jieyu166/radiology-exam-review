#交換 #2017交換 #2019交換 #ABD Which of the following diseases is NOT associated with increased risk for renal cell carcinoma?
(A) ADPKD.
(B) von Hippel-Lindau disease.
(C) Acquired cystic kidney disease associated with chronic renal failure.
(D) All of the above.
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**。**ADPKD 不增加 RCC 風險**（雖 60 歲約 50% 末期腎病）。von Hippel-Lindau(B)、acquired cystic kidney disease(C) 皆增加 RCC 風險。
