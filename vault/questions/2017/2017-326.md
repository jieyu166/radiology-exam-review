#交換 #2017交換 #MSK Which one is **wrong** about evaluation of anterior cruciate ligament repair using MRI?
(A) The inferior portion of the femoral tunnel should be located at the intersection of the lines along the posterior cortex of the femur and the roof of the intercondylar notch on sagittal MRI images
(B) The femoral tunnel should open at the superolateral anterior margin of the intercondylar notch on coronal MRI images (posterior)
(C) The tibial tunnel should be oriented parallel to the Blumensaat line on sagittal MRI images
(D) The opening of the tibia tunnel should be completely posterior to Blumensaat line
??
**Ans: B**
**B為偽**——股骨隧道應開口於髁間窩的後上方(posterior),非前上方(anterior)。
(A) 矢狀面股骨隧道下緣定位法:真。
(B) **正解(偽)**:應為posterior非anterior。
(C) 脛骨隧道與Blumensaat線平行:真。
(D) 脛骨隧道開口完全位於Blumensaat線後方:真。
