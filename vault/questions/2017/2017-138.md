#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Which statement best describes adrenal cortical nodular hyperplasia?
(A) It does not occur with adrenal cortical adenomas.
(B) It increases in prevalence with age.
(C) It is most commonly focal.
(D) It is most commonly unilateral.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**。腎上腺結節性增生**隨年齡盛行率增加**。可與腺瘤並存(A 誤)、多瀰漫非局灶(C 誤)、多雙側非單側(D 誤)。
