#交換 #2017交換 #MSK Which ONE of the following image findings is NOT commonly seen in a Brodie abscess on radiograph?
(A) Well-demarcated margin.
(B) Sclerotic margin.
(C) Center in the epiphysis.
(D) Extending into the growth plate.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**。Brodie abscess（亞急性骨髓炎）典型位於**幹骺端(metaphysis)**，非骨骺(epiphysis)。well-demarcated margin(A)、sclerotic margin(B)、可延伸跨越生長板(D)皆常見。
