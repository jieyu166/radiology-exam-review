Mammography reveals a 7cm multilobulated soft tissue density mass in the left upper quadrant. On ultrasound the mass is solid with cystic areas. Posterior acoustic enhancement is demonstrated. What is the most likely diagnosis?
(A) Phyllodes tumor
(B) Adenoid cystic carcinoma
(C) Melanoma metastasis
(D) Fibromatosis
??
