#交換 #2017交換 #Breast A 40-year-old woman presents with a rapidly enlarging left-sided breast mass. A large, firm, non-tender discrete mass is noted on clinical examination. Mammography reveals a 7cm multilobulated soft tissue density mass in the left upper quadrant. On ultrasound the mass is solid with cystic areas. Posterior acoustic enhancement is demonstrated. What is the most likely diagnosis?
(A) Phyllodes tumor
(B) Adenoid cystic carcinoma
(C) Melanoma metastasis
(D) Fibromatosis
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**。**Phyllodes tumor**——40 歲、快速增大、大而多分葉,US 為實心含裂隙狀(cleft-like)囊腔、後方回音增強。adenoid cystic carcinoma、melanoma metastasis、fibromatosis 較不符。
