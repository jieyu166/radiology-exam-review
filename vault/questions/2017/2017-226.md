#交換 #2017交換 #CH Which of the following is **wrong** about autoimmune-featured ILD?
(A) Comprehensive and systematic evaluation will identify these patients.
(B) Patients with ANA titer ≥ 1:1,280 may have improved survival.
(C) Seen in patients with UIP on HRCT scan and pathology.
(D) Tobacco cessation is strongly recommended.
??
**Ans: D**
**「強烈建議戒菸」是針對 rheumatoid arthritis（RA）相關 ILD，非 autoimmune-featured ILD**。
(A) 正確:全面系統性評估有助辨識這類病人。
(B) 正確:ANA titer ≥1:1280 者可能存活較佳。
(C) 正確:可見於 HRCT 與病理呈 UIP 型者。
(D) **錯誤（正解）**:強烈建議戒菸屬 RA-ILD 的處置,非 AIF-ILD。
