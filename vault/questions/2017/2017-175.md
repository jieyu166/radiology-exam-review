#交換 #2017交換 #Breast Which one is not **true** for breast cancer during pregnancy?
(A) In pregnant women, the background parenchymal enhancement is increased due to hormonal stimulation.
(B) Overall the prognosis is better than in non-pregnant-related carcinoma due to age of diagnosis.
(C) Radiotherapy is usually avoided during pregnancy.
(D) None of above.
??
**Ans: B**
孕婦乳癌**預後並非較好**——各分期存活率可能比非孕婦更差,主因常較晚期才診斷。
(A) 正確:孕期 BPE 因荷爾蒙刺激而增加。
(B) **不正確（正解）**:孕婦乳癌各分期存活可能更差,而非因診斷年齡而較好;常因症狀被孕期變化掩蓋而延遲診斷。
(C) 正確:放療於孕期通常避免（胎兒輻射）。
