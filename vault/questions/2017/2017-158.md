#交換 #2017交換 #CH Which one of the following HRCT findings is NOT consistent with UIP pattern?
(A) Subpleural and basal predominance.
(B) Reticular abnormality.
(C) Honeycombing with traction bronchiectasis.
(D) Honeycombing without traction bronchiectasis.
(E) None of the above.
??
**Ans: E**
正解 **E**（none of the above）。A-D 皆符合 UIP pattern：subpleural/basal predominance、reticular abnormality、honeycombing（伴或不伴 traction bronchiectasis）。
