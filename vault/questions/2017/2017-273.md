#交換 #2017交換 #Breast A 60-year-old woman presents with a palpable breast lump, which on mammography corresponds to a speculated/stellate soft tissue density seen on both MLO and CC views with associated pleomorphic microcalcification. Wide bore core needle biopsy confirms a malignant lesion. She has no family history of breast cancer. Which of the following histological types in most likely in this case?
(A) invasive ductal carcinoma
(B) mucinous tumor
(C) intracystic carcinoma
(D) papillary carcinoma
??
**Ans: A**
**Invasive ductal carcinoma 為最常見的乳癌組織學型態**，典型呈 spiculated/stellate mass ± pleomorphic microcalcification。
(B) mucinous tumor 呈圓形邊界清楚（非毛刺）。(C)(D) 相對少見型態，非此典型表現的最可能型態。
(A) **正解**。
