#交換 #2017交換 #NR Which one of following entities is characteristic with the pulvinar sign (hockey-stick sign)?
(A) Herpes simplex encephalitis
(B) Variant creutzfeldt-Jakob disease
(C) Progressive multifocal leukoencephalopathy
(D) Rasmussen encephalitis
??
**Ans: B**
vCJD：最主要是在後方的Pulvinar，加上兩邊的 Dosomedial 變成V型Hockey stick sign。
Pulvinar sign: 會想到vCJD。其他DDx有Fabry disease(T1高訊號)，thalmic infarction，ADEM，癲癇發作等(Radiopaedia)
