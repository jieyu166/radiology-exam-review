#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Which is the most common primary nonhematopoietic malignant tumor of the spleen?
(A) Littoral cell tumor
(B) Hemangioendothelioma
(C) Angiosarcoma
(D) Lymphoma
??
**Ans: C**
From Sabiston Textbook of Surgery
- Lymphoma是spleen**最常見**的tumor，其他primary/secondary都很rare
- Hematopoietic: Lymphoma, Leukemia
- Nonhematopoietic Primary通常是血管相關的
	- Hemangiomas 良性 常見
	- Splenic hamartomas 良性 rare。 usually composed of malformed disorganized red pulp elements.
	- **Angiosarcomas** (or hemangiosarcomas) 惡性 rare。usually occur spontaneously but have been linked to environmental exposures, such as to thorium dioxide and monomeric vinyl chloride. Patients with angiosarcomas may present with splenomegaly, hemolytic anemia, ascites, pleural effusions, or even spontaneous splenic rupture. These tumors are aggressive and have a poor prognosis.
	- Lymphangiomas, 良性 are endothelium-lined cysts that come to attention because of splenomegaly secondary to cyst enlargement.
	- Lymphangiosarcoma 惡性 has been found within lymphangiomas. Splenectomy is appropriate for the diagnosis, treatment, and palliation of these conditions.
