#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Which of the following descriptions about urogenital tuberculosis is **WRONG**?
(A) Fewer than 5% of patients with urogenital TB have concomitant active pulmonary TB.
(B) Only 30-50% have evidence of pulmonary abnormality on chest radiograph.
(C) Urine culture is the most definite method, and the false negative rate is less than 3%.
(D) Imaging is essential to assess extent/severity, monitor treatment, and discover complications.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**（錯誤）。尿培養**敏感度約 65%（偽陰性 ~35%）、特異度 100%**，偽陰性率非 <3%。<5% 同時活動性肺 TB(A)、30–50% 胸片異常(B)、影像評估必要(D)皆正確。
