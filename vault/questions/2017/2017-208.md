#交換 #2017交換 #NR About IA thrombectomy, which statement is not correct?
(A) Patients eligible for intravenous r-tPA should receive intravenous r-tPA even if endovascular treatments are being considered.
(B) To ensure benefit, reperfusion to TICI grade 2b/3 should be achieved as early as possible.
(C) Observing patients after intravenous r-tPA to assess for clinical response before pursuing endovascular therapy is recommended.
(D) Use of stent retrievers is indicated in preference to the MERCI device.
??
**Ans: C**
依 2015 AHA/ASA,**不建議在 IV r-tPA 後先觀察臨床反應再決定是否血管內治療**（Class III）——應儘快進行,不可因觀察等待而延誤取栓。
(A) 正確:符合條件者應給 IV r-tPA,即使同時考慮血管內治療（Class I）。
(B) 正確:reperfusion 達 TICI 2b/3 應儘早。
(C) **不正確（正解）**:觀察 tPA 反應再做取栓「不建議」。
(D) 正確:stent retriever 優先於 MERCI device。
