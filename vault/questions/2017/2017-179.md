#交換 #2017交換 #CH Regarding “Guidelines for Management of Small Pulmonary Nodules Detected on CT scans” from Fleischner Society, which of the statements is not **true**?
(A) For high-risk patient with incidentally found pulmonary nodule ≤ 4 mm, follow-up CT after six months is recommended.
(B) For low-risk patient with incidentally found pulmonary nodule ≤ 4 mm, no follow-up is needed.
(C) For low-risk patient with incidentally found pulmonary nodule > 8 mm, follow-up CT at 3, 9, 24 months are recommended.
(D) For high-risk patient with incidentally found pulmonary nodule > 8 mm, follow-up CT at 3, 9, 24 months are recommended.
??
**Ans: A**
依 **Fleischner 2005**,高風險病人偶見 **≤4 mm 結節為 12 個月**複查,非 6 個月。
(A) **不正確（正解）**:高風險 ≤4 mm → 12 個月複查（低風險不需追蹤）;題述「6 個月」錯。
(B) 正確:低風險 ≤4 mm 不需追蹤。
(C)(D) 正確:>8 mm 於 3、9、24 月追蹤（或考慮 PET／切片）。
⚠️ 本指引 2017 已更新（門檻提高、6 mm 起追蹤），作答依題目所依 2005 版。
