#交換 #2017交換 #Breast Which one is the definition of focal asymmetry in BI-RADS 2013 for mammography?
(A) visible on only one mammographic projection and lacking convex margins
(B) visible on different mammographic projections, lacks the convex-outward borders
(C) convex margins that is visible on two orthogonal views
(D) large amount of fibrogranular tissue over a substantial portion of breast
(E) new, larger, or more conspicuous than on a previous examination.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**。focal asymmetry＝**在不同(兩個)投影皆可見、缺 convex-outward 邊界（邊界 concave-outward）**。單一投影＝(one-view) asymmetry(A)、有 convex 邊界＝mass(C)、範圍大＝global(D)、新/變大＝developing(E)。
