#交換 #2017交換 #Unknown Which of the following statement regarding the sonographic findings of gallbladder disease is **WRONG**?
(A) Typical gallstones manifest as echogenic foci with acoustic shadow and gravitational dependence.
(B) Sludge produces low-amplitude, nonshadowing echoes with layering.
(C) A gallbladder wall with thickness greater than 3 mm is usually regarded as thickened.
(D) Cholesterol polyps present as mobile, hypoechoic structures attached to the gallbladder wall with posterior acoustic shadow.
??
**Ans: D**
A non-shadowing polypoid ingrowth into gallbladder lumen, which is usually immobile unless there is a relatively long pedunculated component.
