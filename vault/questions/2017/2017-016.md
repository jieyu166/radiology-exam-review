#交換 #2017交換 #Breast Regarding mammography, which of the following statement is **NOT** correct?
(A) In digital mammography, mean glandular dose received is around 2 mGy per exposure.
(B) Radiation dose is lower in thinner than in thicker breasts.
(C) Most commonly used anode/filter combination is Mo/Mo. (Mo: molybdenum)
(D) Typical kVp for mammography are 80-100 kVp for rhodium (Rh).
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**（不正確）。乳攝用**低 kVp(25–35)**,「銠 80–100 kVp」錯誤。MGD ~2 mGy(A)、薄乳劑量低(B)、Mo/Mo 最常用(C)皆正確。
