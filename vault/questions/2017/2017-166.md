#交換 #2017交換 #PED Regarding intestinal malrotation and midgut volvulus, which one is the MOST inappropriate?
(A) The potential of malrotation with midgut volvulus demands emergent imaging evaluation.
(B) Every patient suspected of malrotation demands emergent imaging evaluation and intervention.
(C) Less than 5% of children with congenital duodenal obstruction have a second distal obstruction.
(D) Duodenal atresia or other partial duodenal obstructions are often indistinguishable clinically and radiographically.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**（最不恰當）。僅合併 **midgut volvulus/症狀者**需緊急介入，非每個疑似旋轉不良。volvulus 需緊急影像(A)、<5% 合併遠端阻塞(C)、duodenal atresia 與部分阻塞難分(D)皆正確。
