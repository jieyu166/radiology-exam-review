#交換 #2017交換 #Physics Which is **wrong** about nephrogenic systemic fibrosis ?
(A) Observed in patients with renal failure
(B) Most have been associated with Ominscan
(C) Some associated with OptiMARK and Magnevist
(D) Frequently reported since 2009
??
**Ans: D**
(2017 p.32)
- A. 在第四期或第五期慢性腎臟病受檢者，注射含釓對比劑後，約有 1-7% 產生腎因性全身纖維化。
- B. Omniscan 病例數最多。C. 這兩個藥均為高危險性
- D. 於 2006 年被確認與 MRI 含釓對比劑相關。2009年後沒有新案例被報導 suspect reflects use of more stable GBCAs and limitation of use of GBCAs in patients with renal failure.
- The overall incidence of NSF was 36.5 cases per 100 000 gadolinium-enhanced magnetic resonance (MR) examinations between 2003 and 2006
- 4 cases/100 000 gadolinium-enhanced MR examinations between 2007 and 2008 after screening for NSF risk was instituted (Fisher exact test, P = .001)
