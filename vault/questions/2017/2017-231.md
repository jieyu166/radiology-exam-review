#交換 #2017交換 #H&N Which of the following statements is **wrong** about site classification for lymph nodes from American Head and Neck Society and American Academy of Otolaryngology-Head and Neck Surgery?
(A) Levels II and III are divided by hyoid bone.
(B) Levels III and IV are divided by inferior border of cricoid cartilage.
(C) Levels IIA and IIB are divided by the medial margins of carotid arteries.
(D) Level V is posterior to the posterior border of sternocleidomastoid muscle.
??
**Ans: C**
**Level IIA 與 IIB 的分界是『內頸靜脈後緣（posterior margin of internal jugular vein）』，非頸動脈內緣**。
(A) 正確:Level II/III 以舌骨體下緣分。
(B) 正確:Level III/IV 以環狀軟骨下緣分。
(C) **錯誤（正解）**:IIA/IIB 以內頸靜脈後緣分,非頸動脈內緣。
(D) 正確:Level V 位於胸鎖乳突肌後緣之後。
