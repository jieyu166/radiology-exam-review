#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Which MRI image finding of uterine cervical cancer does NOT affect cancer TNM staging?
(A) Tumor invasion to the uterine body.
(B) Tumor invasion to the parametrium.
(C) Hydronephrosis.
(D) Pelvic lymphadenopathy.
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**。**侵犯子宮體（uterine body）不改變子宮頸癌的 FIGO/TNM 分期**。parametrium 侵犯(→IIB，B)、hydronephrosis(→IIIB，C)、pelvic lymphadenopathy(N 分期，D)皆影響分期。
