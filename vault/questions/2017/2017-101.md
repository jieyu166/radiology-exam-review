#交換 #2017交換 #NR About demyelinating disease, which of the following was INCORRECT?
(A) Most multiple sclerosis (MS) plaques are supratentorial.
(B) Active MS plaques may show ring enhancement.
(C) Neuromyelitis optica (NMO) preferentially involves the spinal cord and optic nerves.
(D) NMO patients do not have T2/FLAIR hyperintensities in the cerebral white matter.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**（不正確）。**NMO 常有腦白質病灶（亞洲 60–80%、歐洲 25–40%）**，鏡像 aquaporin-4 分布。MS 多 supratentorial(A)、活性斑環狀強化(B)、NMO 偏好視神經/脊髓(C)皆正確。
