#交換 #2017交換 #Breast Which statement is not **true** for background parenchymal enhancement (BPE) at breast MR imaging?
(A) BPE commonly begins in the peripheral breast tissue and gradually becomes apparent in more central breast tissue.
(B) Non-urgent breast MR imaging should be scheduled during the 3rd week of the menstrual cycle (days 7-15).
(C) Bilateral similar areas of enhancement, regardless of distribution, are more characteristic of benign enhancement than malignancy.
(D) BPE is proportional to mammographic density.
??
**Ans: D**
**BPE 與乳房攝影緻密度不必然成正比**——extremely dense 可僅輕微 BPE,scattered 也可 marked。
(A) 正確:BPE 常始於周邊乳組織、漸向中央明顯。
(B) ⚠️ 官方標 D 但註記 B 也不完全對:非急迫乳房 MRI 宜排於月經週期第 2 週（約 7-14 天）BPE 最弱時,題述「第 3 週」措辭有爭議,建議醫師確認。
(C) 正確:雙側對稱相似強化較傾向良性。
(D) **不正確（正解）**:BPE 與纖維腺體量/乳攝緻密度不必然相關。
