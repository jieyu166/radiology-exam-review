#交換 #2017交換 #PED Which one is not the correct description about "infantile hypertrophic pyloric stenosis(IHPS)?
(A) Male predominance (Males: females 4:1 to 6:1)
(B) It typically occurs soon after birth (symptoms usually begin between 3 and 5 weeks of age, and very rarely occur after 12 weeks of age)
(C) Manifested by non-bilious vomiting
(D) Radiographic findings include marked gastric dilatation disproportionate to small bowel gas
??
**Ans: B**
**B為偽**——IHPS並非出生後不久發病,症狀典型於出生後3-5週開始,極罕見於12週後才發病。
(A) 男性好發4:1至6:1:真。
(B) **正解(偽)**。
(C) 非膽汁性嘔吐:真。
(D) X光顯著胃擴張與小腸氣體量不成比例:真。
