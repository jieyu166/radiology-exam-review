#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Which one of the follow is not **true** for urachal tumor?
(A) The urachus represents the embryologic remnant of two embryologic structures: the cloaca and the allantois.
(B) The urachus extends from the anterosuperior surface of the bladder to the umbilicus and lies in the extraperitoneal space of Retzius between the transverse fascia and parietal peritoneum.
(C) Calcifications are present in 70% of the cases and are mostly seen at the center of the urachal mass.
(D) All of the above are correct
??
**Ans: C**
**Urachal carcinoma 的鈣化雖見於約 70% 病例，但多位於腫塊『周邊（peripheral）』，非中央**。
(A) 正確:urachus 為 cloaca 與 allantois 的胚胎遺跡。
(B) 正確:自膀胱前上緣延伸至臍,位於 Retzius 腹膜外間隙。
(C) **非真（正解）**:鈣化多位於周邊,非中央。
