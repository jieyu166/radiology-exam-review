#交換 #2017交換 #CH Regarding pulmonary Kaposi sarcoma, which statement is **WRONG**?
(A) Single or multiple 1-3 cm pulmonary nodules.
(B) Almost always preceded by cutaneous or visceral involvement.
(C) Coarse linear opacities emanating from the hilum.
(D) It is the most common pulmonary malignancy in AIDS patients.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**。1-3 cm 結節(A)、幾乎都先有皮膚/內臟病灶(B)、粗線狀影自肺門放射(C)皆為肺 KS 典型;KS 為 AIDS 最常見相關惡性腫瘤、淋巴瘤次之。
