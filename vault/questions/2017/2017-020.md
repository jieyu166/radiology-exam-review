#交換 #2017交換 #ABD 關於The Prostate Imaging Reporting and Data System (PI-RADS) Version 2的敘述，以下何者**錯誤**？
(A) For peripheral zone abnormalities, the apparent diffusion coefficient (ADC) map in combination with diffusion-weighted imaging (DWI) performed with high b values (≧1400 sec/mm2) is the dominant parameter for clinically significant cancer.
(B) For transition zone abnormalities, T2-weighted MR imaging is dominant for overall lesion assessment.
(C) MR spectroscopic (MRS) imaging has ancillary roles in the characterization of lesions considered equivocal for clinically significant cancer.
(D) Assessment with dynamic contrast-enhanced MR imaging is simplified, being considered positive or negative on the basis of qualitative evaluation for a focal area of rapid enhancement matching an abnormality on DWI-ADC or T2-weighted MR images.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**（錯誤）。**PI-RADS v2 不納入 MRS**（已移除、無 ancillary 角色）。PZ 以 DWI/ADC 主導(A)、TZ 以 T2WI 主導(B)、DCE 簡化為陰/陽性(D)皆正確。
