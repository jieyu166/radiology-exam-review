#交換 #2017交換 #MSK Which of the following is **TRUE** about ankle Mortise-view radiograph?
(A) It is an AP-view taken with a 90 degrees endorotation of the foot
(B) The technologist turns the foot outwards (inward) until the medial malleolus is at the same height as the lateral malleolus
(C) This view visualizes both the lateral and medial joint spaces
(D) The talus overlaps a portion of the lateral malleolus, obscuring the lateral aspect of the ankle joint (in AP view)
??
**Ans: C**
**C（同時顯示內外側關節間隙）為真**。
(A) 為15-20度內轉oblique,非90度AP旋轉。
(B) 應為內轉(inward)非外轉(outwards)。
(C) **正解**:Mortise view核心優勢。
(D) 此為標準AP view的特性(距骨遮蔽外髁)，非Mortise view的敘述。
