#交換 #2017交換 #2018交換 #Breast Which statement about “mass” detected on mammography is **WRONG**?
(A) A fat containing mass is almost always benign
(B) A mass with indistinct margin usually implies a suspicious finding
(C) A mass with three undulations should be described as irregular in shape
(D) A mass with margin of short cycle undulations usually implies a suspicious finding
(E) None of the above
??
**Ans: C**
Oval shape: ellipitical or egg-shaped (may include 2/3 undulations) 所以C是oval shape。
B. Indistinct margin是suspicious，如果覺得是良性會用obscured。
C. Irregular shape: 不是 round, oval就叫做irregular。
D. Microlobulated margin: characterized by short cycle undulations. suspicious.
