#交換 #2017交換 #US Which statement is not **true** for elastography?
(A) The main objective is to provide an imaging representation of lesion stiffness to improve diagnostic confidence and increase specificity of the ultrasonography.
(B) Malignant lesions tend to be stiffer than benign lesions.
(C) With static elastography, the tissue stiffness can be measured by the Young modulus.
(D) Shear-wave based elastography is able to provide quantitative elastic information in real time.
??
**Ans: C**
**靜態（strain）彈性造影只提供相對形變、無法以 Young modulus 定量組織硬度**;能定量者為 shear wave 彈性造影。
(A) 正確:目的為將病灶硬度影像化,提升診斷信心與特異度。
(B) 正確:惡性病灶通常較良性硬。
(C) **不正確（正解）**:static（strain）elastography 給的是相對形變/壓縮,不能用 Young modulus 定量;許多硬度增加的腫瘤無法正確編碼於彈性圖、不能量測 Young modulus。
(D) 正確:shear-wave elastography 可即時提供定量彈性資訊。
