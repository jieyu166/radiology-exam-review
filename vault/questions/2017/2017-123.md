#交換 #2017交換 #NR Which statement is **TRUE** about intraventricular tumors?
(A) Ependymomas occur in any age group but are more common in elderly patients.
(B) The classic scenario of subependymoma is a middle-aged man with an incidental intraventricular neoplasm at imaging.
(C) Central neurocytomas never contain calcification or hemorrhage.
(D) The most common location of choroid plexus neoplasm is the third ventricle.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**。**Subependymoma 經典為中年男性偶發、無症狀的腦室內腫瘤**。ependymoma 偏年輕(A 誤)、central neurocytoma ~50% 含鈣化可出血(C 誤)、choroid plexus 成人最常見側腦室房部非第三腦室(D 誤)。
