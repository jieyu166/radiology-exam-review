#交換 #2017交換 #MSK Which one of the following conditions is usually associated with a cross-over sign on the anteroposterior radiograph of the hip?
(A) Perthes disease
(B) Developmental dysplasia of hip
(C) Femoroacetabular impingement
(D) Slipped capital femoral epiphysis
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**。**Crossover sign（髖臼前緣越過後緣）＝髖臼後傾，屬 pincer 型 femoroacetabular impingement**。Perthes、DDH、SCFE 非以 crossover sign 為特徵。
