#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Which of the following can be classified as adreniform masses? (intrinsic to the adrenal preserving the overall shape, adreniform)
(A) Adrenal cortical hyperplasia
(B) Adrenal hemorrhage
(C) Pheochromocytoma
(D) A and B
??
**Ans: D**
**D（A and B）**——cortical hyperplasia與adrenal hemorrhage皆保留腺體整體外形僅瀰漫增大。
(A)(B) 保留腺體外形:真,屬adreniform。
(C) Pheochromocytoma:扭曲/取代腺體外形,不屬adreniform。
(D) **正解**。
