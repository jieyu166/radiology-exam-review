#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Which description about Intraductal papillary mucinous neoplasm (IPMN) is **wrong**?
(A) IPMNs can be classified anatomically as main duct (MD-IPMN), branch duct (BD-IPMN), or mixed.
(B) The majority of MD-IPMNs arise in the pancreatic head, whereas most BD-IPMNs arise in the uncinated process.
(C) BD-IPMNs have a much lower rate of malignant transformation than MD-IPMNs.
(D) All above are correct
??
**Ans: D**
A、B、C 三項敘述**皆正確**,無錯誤者,故答案為 D「以上皆正確」。
(A) 正確:IPMN 分 MD/BD/mixed。
(B) 正確:多數 MD-IPMN 在胰頭、多數 BD-IPMN 在鉤突。
(C) 正確:BD-IPMN 惡性轉變率（~5%, 2-10%）遠低於 MD-IPMN（~60%）。
(D) **正解**:以上皆正確。
