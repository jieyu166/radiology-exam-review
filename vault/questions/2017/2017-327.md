#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Which of the following description concerning with myelolipoma is **true**?
(A) An uncommon benign tumor composed of mature adipose tissue and hematopoietic tissue
(B) It can be large and symptomatic secondary to spontaneous hemorrhage
(C) It may present as homogeneous, hyperintense masses on T1-weighted images with intermediate signal intensity on T2-weighted images
(D) All of the above
??
**Ans: D**
**D（All of the above）**——myelolipoma為少見良性腫瘤(成熟脂肪+造血組織)、可因自發性出血致症狀性增大、MR呈T1均質高訊號/T2中等訊號皆為真實描述。
(A)(B)(C) 皆為真。
(D) **正解**。
