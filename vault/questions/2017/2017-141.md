#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Which of the following conditions is most likely to manifest as well-defined bilateral adrenal masses or enlargement?
(A) Adrenal cortical carcinoma.
(B) Adrenal cortical adenoma.
(C) Adrenal infection.
(D) Adrenal oncocytoma.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**。**Adrenal infection（如組織漿菌、結核）常造成雙側腎上腺腫塊/腫大**。adrenal cortical carcinoma(A)、adenoma(B)、oncocytoma(D)多為單側。
