#交換 #2017交換 #Breast Regarding proper mammography technique, which of the following statement is **NOT** correct?
(A) Fat is seen posterior to the fibroglandular tissue.
(B) In MLO view, pectoralis muscle is convex and but not extends inferior to the posterior nipple line.
(C) The length of the posterior nipple line on CC view should be within 1cm of its length on the MLO view.
(D) In MLO view, the inframammary fold is open, with lower breast and partial upper abdominal wall included.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**（不正確）。足夠的 MLO 胸大肌應呈 convex 且**延伸至或低於 posterior nipple line（PNL）**;「convex 但未延伸至 PNL 下方」屬定位不足。脂肪在纖維腺體後方(A)、CC PNL 在 MLO 1 cm 內(C)、IMF 打開(D)皆正確。
