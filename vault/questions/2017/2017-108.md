#交換 #2017交換 #CH Which of the following structures is the most reliable landmark for the thoracic inlet at axial CT?
(A) First thoracic vertebra.
(B) Thyroid gland.
(C) Scalene muscles.
(D) First ribs.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**。**First ribs（第一肋骨）** 為軸狀 CT 上界定 thoracic inlet 最可靠的標誌。T1 椎體、甲狀腺、斜角肌較不可靠。
