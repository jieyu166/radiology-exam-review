#交換 #2017交換 #ABD About premalignant precursors to the development of cholangiocarcinoma, which of the following is the most common?
(A) intraductal papillary neoplasm
(B) congenital choledochal cyst
(C) biliary serous cystic neoplasm
(D) solid papillary epithelial neoplasm
??
**Ans: A**
**Intraductal papillary neoplasm of bile duct（IPNB）為膽管癌最常見的癌前病灶**——膽管內乳頭狀增生、被覆膽道上皮＋中央纖維血管核。
(A) **正解**:IPNB 為最常見前驅。
(B) congenital choledochal cyst:增加風險但非最常見前驅。
(C) biliary serous cystic neoplasm、(D) solid papillary epithelial neoplasm:皆非。
