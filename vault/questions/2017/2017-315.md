#交換 #2017交換 #CH Which of following vasculitis is large-vessel involved vasculitis?
(A) Polyarteritis nodosa
(B) Giant cell arteritis
(C) Granulomatosis with polyangiitis
(D) Eosinophilic granulomatosis with polyangiitis
??
**Ans: B**
**B（Giant cell arteritis）**——大血管炎僅含Takayasu arteritis與giant cell arteritis。
(A) Polyarteritis nodosa:中血管炎。
(B) **正解**。
(C)(D) GPA/EGPA:皆屬ANCA associated小血管炎。
