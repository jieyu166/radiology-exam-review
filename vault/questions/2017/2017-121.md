#交換 #2017交換 #CV About the coronary circulation, which pair of vessels has a compensatory relationship?
(A) LAD & LCX.
(B) RCA & PDA.
(C) LAD & PDA.
(D) LAD & RCA.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**。**LAD 與 PDA 互為代償**——LAD 繞心尖供應下壁心尖,一者長則另一者短。
