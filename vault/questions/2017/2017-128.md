#交換 #2017交換 #ABD When evaluating a hepatic lesion on CT or MRI, all of the following image features favor HCC, EXCEPT:
(A) Tumor within the lumen of a vein.
(B) Arterial phase hyperenhancement.
(C) Portal venous or later phase hypoenhancement (washout).
(D) Without a capsule or pseudocapsule.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**。**「無 capsule/pseudocapsule」不利 HCC**——HCC 典型『有』包膜。有利者：tumor in vein 瘤栓(A)、動脈期 hyperenhancement(B)、portal/later washout(C)。
