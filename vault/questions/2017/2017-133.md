#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Which of the following is NOT a typical image finding of endometrial cancer on MRI?
(A) Isointensity compared to myometrium on T1WI.
(B) Hyperintensity compared to myometrium on T2WI.
(C) Hyperenhancement compared to myometrium on post-contrast images.
(D) All of the above are typical image findings.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**（不是典型）。子宮內膜癌 post-contrast 為**強化低於肌層（hypoenhancement）**，非 hyperenhancement。T1WI 與肌層等訊號(A)、T2WI 高於肌層(B)皆為典型。
