#交換 #2017交換 #CV What is **TRUE** regarding truncus arteriosus?
(A) In Type II truncus arteriosus, the pulmonary arteries arise independently from the posterior aspect of the truncus.
(B) In Type I truncus arteriosus, the pulmonary arteries arise from the descending thoracic aorta.
(C) Other congenital cardiac anomalies are uncommon.
(D) Patients usually present after 2 months of age.
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**。Collett-Edwards **Type II：兩肺動脈各自從動脈幹後方發出**。Type I 主肺動脈自共同幹（非降主動脈，B 錯）、幾乎都合併 VSD 且常合併他異常（C 錯）、出生即發紺非 2 月後（D 錯）。
