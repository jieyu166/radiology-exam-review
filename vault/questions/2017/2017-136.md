#交換 #2016交換 #2017交換 #ABD Which of the following is **WRONG** about adenoma malignum?
(A) The initial symptom is often vaginal bleeding.
(B) Multiple grape-like cysts within the cervical stroma.
(C) A subtype of mucinous adenocarcinoma.
(D) Has an unfavorable prognosis.
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**（錯誤）。Adenoma malignum 初症常為**水樣/黏液性分泌**，非陰道出血。子宮頸間質葡萄串狀囊腫(B)、黏液腺癌亞型(C)、預後差(D)皆正確。
