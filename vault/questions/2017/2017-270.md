#交換 #2017交換 #Breast Which of the following unusual benign breast tumor is most likely to be locally infiltrating, aggressive and proliferative, and consist of only well-differentiated fibroblasts?
(A) areolar leiomyoma
(B) granular cell tumor
(C) fibromatosis
(D) neurofibroma
??
**Ans: C**
**Fibromatosis（即乳房的 desmoid tumor）為局部侵犯性良性腫瘤，僅由分化良好纖維母細胞構成**。
(A) areolar leiomyoma、(B) granular cell tumor、(D) neurofibroma 皆為不尋常良性乳房腫瘤但非以局部侵犯性增生為特徵。
(C) **fibromatosis（正解）**。
