#交換 #2017交換 #H&N 有關 congenital sensorineural hearing loss 的敘述，下列何者為非?
(A) Mondini defect is associated with DiGeorge, Waardenburg, Alagille, Klippel-Feil, Wildervanck, and Pendred syndromes and chromosomal trisomies.
(B) MRI is preferred image study because it provides improved evaluation of the membranous labyrinth and endolymphatic sac and direct visualization of the cochlear nerve.
(C) Cochlear hypoplasia shows a small cochlea with a height less than 4 mm and fewer than two and a half turns.
(D) The upper limit of normal vestibular aqueduct on axial CT is 4 mm.
??
**Ans: D**
**前庭導水管（vestibular aqueduct）軸位正常上限約 1.5 mm**（>1.5 mm 即大前庭導水管 LVAS）,非 4 mm。
(A) 正確:Mondini defect 與 DiGeorge/Waardenburg/Alagille/Klippel-Feil/Wildervanck/Pendred 及染色體 trisomy 相關。
(B) 正確:MRI 為首選（膜迷路/內淋巴囊/耳蝸神經）。
(C) 正確:cochlear hypoplasia 高度<4 mm、不足 2.5 圈。
(D) **為非（正解）**:正常上限約 1.5 mm,非 4 mm。
