#交換 #2017交換 #MRI Which of the following statement about decidualized endometriotic cyst is **FALSE**?
(A) All decidualized components on the wall of the endometriotic cyst is considered as low-growing component along the cystic wall or the intracystic septa
(B) The solid component of decidualized endometriotic cysts has higher signal intensity on T2-weighted images as compared with those associated with carcinomas
(C) The ectopic decidua showed significantly higher ADC value than the carcinomas
(D) In the setting of gynecological pathology, a number of studies have sought to differentiate benign lesions from malignant lesions, both uterine and ovarian, using DWI values as a distinguishing parameter
??
**Ans: D**
⚠️ 官方答案為D。依所附段落，A（蛻膜化組件定義）、B（T2蛻膜化略高於惡性）、C（ADC值蛻膜化顯著高於惡性）皆為文獻支持的正確敘述。DWI高訊號本身對蛻膜化組織與惡性皆非特異，需靠ADC定量值鑑別（蛻膜化顯著較高）。D為研究背景性描述，確切被判偽理由建議醫師對照原文全段確認。
