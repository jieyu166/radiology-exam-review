#交換 #2017交換 #MSK About the classic signs of diffuse idiopathic skeletal hyperostosis (DISH), which one is **WRONG**?
(A) Flowing ossification along at least four contiguous vertebral levels.
(B) Relative preservation of disc height.
(C) Presence of apophyseal joint ankylosis.
(D) Prominent, sharply marginated bony excrescences at ligament and tendon attachments.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**（錯誤）。**Apophyseal(facet) joint ankylosis 與 sacroiliitis 屬僵直性脊椎炎(AS)，非 DISH**。流注狀骨化(≥4 椎節)(A)、椎間盤高度保留(B)、附著點骨贅(D)皆為 DISH。
