#交換 #2017交換 #CV Which of the following MR scans usually require injection of gadolinium
(A) Time-of-flight angiography for lower limb.
(B) Time-resolved MR angiography for lower limb.
(C) Cine steady-state free precession in cardiac MR.
(D) Navigator-gated, ECG-triggered 3D whole-heart MRI angiography.
??
**Ans: B**
Time-resolved MRA sequences, 如 TRICKS, TWIST 等。會在打藥後連續收數張影像
	- 一般的藥後影像問題是靜脈也會顯影，造成干擾。有很多技術嘗試解決，如subtraction, venous compression, 其他打藥的方式, 後處理...還有time-resolved MRA。
	- 參考 https://www.ajronline.org/doi/full/10.2214/AJR.11.7065
- TOF brain看過很多，不需要打藥。
- Cine SSFP也不用打藥。源自GRE，血液是亮的。(Bright blood)
- 一般的coronary MRA偏好不打顯影劑的方式 non-contrast bright blood techniques (參考 https://mriquestions.com/coronary-artery-mra.html)
	1) a single 3D-volumetric sequence covering the entire heart, or
	2) two shorter duration slab acquisitions targeting the right and left coronary systems respectively.
