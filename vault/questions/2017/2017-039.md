#交換 #2017交換 #CV Which of the following MR scans usually require injection of gadolinium
(A) Time-of-flight angiography for lower limb.
(B) Time-resolved MR angiography for lower limb.
(C) Cine steady-state free precession in cardiac MR.
(D) Navigator-gated, ECG-triggered 3D whole-heart MRI angiography.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**。**Time-resolved MRA（下肢，如 TRICKS/TWIST）需注射 gadolinium**。TOF(流入增強,A)、cine SSFP(C)、navigator 3D whole-heart(SSFP 血池,D)皆非對比、不需釓。
