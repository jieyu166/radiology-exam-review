#交換 #2017交換 #H&N Which of the following statements is **wrong** about neck lymph nodes?
(A) For head and neck cancer staging, it is generally more important to have higher specificity for nodal metastasis.
(B) Although mediastinal nodal calcifications are usually a marker of benign disease, cervical nodal calcifications are more frequently seen with malignancy.
(C) Imaging findings of extracapsular spread are irregular margins, fat stranding, and loss of fat planes with adjacent structures.
(D) In the setting of head and neck cancer, necrotic or cystic change, even in small nodes, is a specific marker of malignancy.
??
**Ans: A**
**頭頸癌淋巴結分期一般更重視高『敏感度（sensitivity）』，以抓出所有轉移結節，非高特異度**。
(A) **錯誤（正解）**:應重視高敏感度,非高特異度。
(B) 正確:縱膈鈣化多良性,頸部淋巴結鈣化偏惡性。
(C) 正確:ECS＝邊緣不規則、脂肪條紋、脂肪面消失。
(D) 正確:小結節壞死/囊變為惡性特異徵。
