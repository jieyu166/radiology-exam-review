#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Regarding recurrent pyogenic cholangitis, which one is **WRONG**?
(A) No risk of developing cholangiocarcinoma.
(B) Particularly in the lateral segment of the left lobe.
(C) Multifocal bile duct strictures.
(D) Associated findings include gallstones, splenomegaly and varices.
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**（為非）。**RPC 增加膽管癌風險**。好發左葉外側段(B)、多發膽管狹窄(C)、合併膽石/脾腫大/靜脈曲張(D)皆正確。
