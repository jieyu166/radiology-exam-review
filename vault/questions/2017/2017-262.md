#交換 #2017交換 #ABD About the Meckel's Diverticulum, which of the following description is correct?
(A) Meckel's diverticulums is remnants of the omphalomesenteric duct, therefore involves a connection to the body wall, and usually present a fistula opening through the umbilicus.
(B) CT scan is the modality of choice to make the diagnosis
(C) Meckel's Scans (Technetium-99m pertechnetate scintigraphy) have no false negatives.
(D) Meckel's Scans have false positive that occur if the person has a concomitant inflammatory disorder.
??
**Ans: D**
**若病人合併發炎性疾病，Meckel scan 可產生偽陽性結果**。
(A) 錯:憩室典型不涉及與臍部連接/瘻管開口。
(B) 錯:診斷首選為 Meckel scan 或腸繫膜血管攝影，非 CT。
(C) 錯:Meckel scan 敏感度有限（成人~60%），並非無偽陰性。
(D) **正解**:合併發炎性疾病可致偽陽性。
