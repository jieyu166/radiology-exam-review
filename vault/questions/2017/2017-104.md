#交換 #2017交換 #2019交換 #ABD Which of the following descriptions about acquired cystic kidney disease (ACKD) is **WRONG**?
(A) Develops in patients with chronic renal failure treated by dialysis.
(B) Not associated with increased risk of renal neoplasm.
(C) Renal hemorrhage is a common associated complication.
(D) The affected kidneys are usually small, but the cysts sometimes cause significant renal enlargement.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**（錯誤）。**ACKD 增加腎細胞癌風險**（增生性囊腫為 dialysis-associated RCC 的可能來源）。發生於透析病人(A)、腎出血常見(C)、患腎多小但可腫大(D)皆正確。
