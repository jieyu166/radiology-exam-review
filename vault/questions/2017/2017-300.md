#交換 #2017交換 #NR Which of the following statements about subependymomas is **FALSE**?
(A) It is thought that they arise from the subependymal glial layer surrounding the cerebral ventricles, but the exact histogenesis remains uncertain
(B) Enhancement is variable, with most lesions demonstrating no or minimal enhancement
(C) Invasion into the brain parenchyma usually occurs, and it is often for there to be adjacent T2 hyperintensity in the periventricular white matter (unlike ependymomas, no invasion into brain parenchyma occurs, and it is rare for adjacent T2 hyperintensity)
(D) No CSF dissemination occurs. Rarely, multiple subependymomas may be present
??
**Ans: C**
**C為偽**——subependymoma不侵犯腦實質,周邊白質很少出現T2高訊號(與ependymoma相反)。
(A) 組織起源不確定:真。
(B) 強化多變但多無/輕微強化:真。
(C) **正解(偽)**。
(D) 無CSF散播偶可多發:真。
