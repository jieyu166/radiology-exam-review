#交換 #2017交換 #ABD About MR imaging of hypervascular liver tumors, which one is **correct**?
(A) Hemangiomas are more T2 hypointense than most metastases.
(B) Small uniformly enhancing hemangiomas retain contrast and remain hyperintense, whereas hypervascular metastases show washout.
(C) Depiction of intralesional fat with fat-suppressed or opposed-phase imaging.
(D) Regenerative and dysplastic nodules typically display venous washout.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**。**小而均勻強化的血管瘤滯留對比、維持高訊號;hypervascular 轉移呈 washout**。血管瘤較轉移 T2 更高訊號(A 反)、regen/dysplastic nodule 不washout(D 誤)。
