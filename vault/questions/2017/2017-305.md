#交換 #2017交換 #ABD A 15-year-old boy presents with severe abdominal pain. He has a known history of a polyposis syndrome. KUB shows small bowel obstruction, a subsequent abdominal CT scan indicates that this is due to a small bowel intussusception. Which "polyposis" syndrome does he most probably have?
(A) Cronkhite-Canada syndrome (stomach, colon hamartomatous polyps)
(B) Familial adenomatous polyposis syndrome (colonic adenomatous polyps)
(C) Cowden syndrome (terminal ileum and colon hamartomatous polyps)
(D) Gardner's syndrome (colonic adenomatous polyps)
(E) Peutz-Jeghers syndrome (intestinal hamartomatous polyps)
??
**Ans: E**
**E（Peutz-Jeghers syndrome）**——錯構瘤性息肉體積大易誘發小腸套疊。
(A) Cronkhite-Canada:非遺傳性,好發胃/大腸,不伴惡性風險增加。
(B)(D) FAP/Gardner:腺瘤性息肉,非以套疊為主要特徵。
(C) Cowden:好發末端迴腸/大腸錯構瘤性息肉,非以套疊為主要特徵。
(E) **正解**:PJS息肉本身非癌前病變,但仍伴GI腺癌及腸外惡性腫瘤風險。
