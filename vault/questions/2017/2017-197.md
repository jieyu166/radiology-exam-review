#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Tuberculosis (TB) of the urinary tract has a wide spectrum of imaging findings, 以下何者正確?
(A) Nearly all urinary TB has multiple imaging findings.
(B) Putty kidney is regarded as a pathognomonic finding for renal TB.
(C) Ureteral stricture by TB is most commonly seen in ureteropelvic junction, followed by ureterovesicular junction.
(D) A small bladder without normal distension over time associated with an unusual anatomical change and obstruction on images should alert the readers of the high probability of a bladder TB.
??
**Ans: C**
**TB 輸尿管狹窄好發於 ureteropelvic junction（UPJ）,其次為遠端（ureterovesicular junction）**;結核狹窄好發 infundibulum、UPJ 與遠端輸尿管。
(A) 過度概括:並非「幾乎全部」皆多發影像表現（IVP 約 90% 異常但型態不一）。
(B) putty kidney（autonephrectomy）為**特徵性**但稱「pathognomonic」過度。
(C) **正確（正解）**:狹窄好發 UPJ、其次遠端輸尿管。
(D) thimble bladder 描述大致正確但非本題最佳答案。
