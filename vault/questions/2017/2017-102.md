#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Which of the following statements is **NOT** a characteristic of parasitic leiomyoma?
(A) Pedunculated soft tissue mass arising from the uterus.
(B) Arterial supply is best seen on MRA.
(C) Usually has large draining veins.
(D) Image characters on both CT and MRI are similar to that of uterine leiomyoma.
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**（非特徵）。Parasitic leiomyoma **與子宮分離**、自腹膜血管獲血供，非「由子宮長出的有蒂腫塊」。大引流靜脈(C)、影像似子宮肌瘤(D)、動脈血供 MRA 可見(B)皆正確。
