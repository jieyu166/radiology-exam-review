#交換 #2017交換 #MSK Which of the following is the most common location for posttraumatic myositis ossificans?
(A) Skull.
(B) Trunk.
(C) Proximal extremities.
(D) Distal extremities.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**。創傷後骨化性肌炎最常侵犯**四肢近端（前側肌群，股四頭肌與肱肌最常受累）**。
