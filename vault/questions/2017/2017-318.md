#交換 #2017交換 #NR Which statement of neurovascular compression syndrome caused trigeminal neuralgia is **wrong**?
(A) More often involving maxillary branch
(B) Left side of the face being affected more commonly
(C) Peak around 60 to 70 years old
(D) Involved artery: SCA > AICA > VA/BA
??
**Ans: B**
**B為偽**——右側臉部受累較常見(1.5:1,非左側),僅約3%為雙側。
(A) 上頷支最常受累:真。
(B) **正解(偽)**:應為右側較常見。
(C) 60-70歲高峰:真。
(D) 受累血管SCA>AICA>VA/BA:真。
