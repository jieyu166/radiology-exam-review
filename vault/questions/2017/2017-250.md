#交換 #2017交換 #ABD About CT urography for evaluation of the ureter, which one is correct? a. It can manifest as the following findings: congenital abnormality, filling defect, dilatation, narrowing, or deviation in course. b. Ureteral duplication is the most common congenital anomaly of the urinary tract and occurs in approximately 1% of the population. c. A dilated ureter is not necessarily obstructed; conversely, an obstructed ureter is not always dilated. d. A ureterocele is defined as a bulbous dilatation of the ureter as it passes through the bladder wall, and appears as a rounded filling defect that projects into the urinary bladder lumen.
(A) a+b+d
(B) b+c+d
(C) a+c+d
(D) a+b+c+d
??
**Ans: D**
a、b、c、d 四項敘述**皆正確**。
(a) 正確:CTU 可見先天異常、充盈缺損、擴張、狹窄、走行偏移等異常型態。
(b) 正確:輸尿管重複為泌尿道最常見先天異常,約占人口 1%。
(c) 正確:擴張輸尿管不必然阻塞、阻塞輸尿管不必然擴張。
(d) 正確:ureterocele 為輸尿管穿膀胱壁處球狀擴張,呈投射入膀胱腔的圓形充盈缺損。
(D) **a+b+c+d（正解）**。
