#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Which of the following statements about the syndromic pancreatic islet cell tumors (ICTs) is **wrong**?
(A) Syndromic ICTs are more likely to be cystic or necrotic than nonsyndromic ICTs
(B) Insulinoma is the most common syndromic ICT.
(C) Gastrinoma may related to Zollinger-Ellison syndrome
(D) People with vipoma may present with WDHA (watery diarrhea, hypokalemia, and achlorhydria).
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**（錯誤）。相反——**nonsyndromic ICT（尤其非功能性）較大、較易囊變/壞死**;syndromic（MEN1）多小而多發、早發現。insulinoma 為最常見 syndromic ICT(B)、gastrinoma 與 ZES 相關(C)、VIPoma 呈 WDHA(D)皆正確。
