#交換 #2017交換 #CH Which of the following connective tissue disease-associated interstitial lung disease is most commonly found with cysts on HRCT scan?
(A) Autoimmune-featured ILD
(B) Rheumatoid arthritis
(C) Sjögren syndrome
(D) Systemic sclerosis
??
**Ans: C**
**Sjögren syndrome 最常合併肺囊腫**——因其相關的 lymphocytic interstitial pneumonia (LIP) 典型產生肺囊腫。
(A) Autoimmune-featured ILD、(B) Rheumatoid arthritis:較少以囊腫為主。
(C) **正解**:Sjögren → LIP → 肺囊腫。
(D) Systemic sclerosis:多為 NSIP/UIP（纖維化為主，非囊腫）。
