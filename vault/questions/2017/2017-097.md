#交換 #2017交換 #Unknown Which of the following statements is **NOT** a characteristic of parasitic leiomyoma?
(A) Pedunculated soft tissue mass arising from the uterus.
(B) Arterial supply is best seen on MRA.
(C) Usually have large draining veins.
(D) Image characters on both CT and MRI are similar to that of uterine leiomyoma.
??
**Ans: A**
Parasitic leiomyoma: A type of **extra**-uterine leiomyoma and present as a peritoneal pelvic mass, **separate** from the uterus, with characteristics similar to uterine leiomyoma.
