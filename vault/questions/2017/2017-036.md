#交換 #2017交換 #CV About transcatheter aortic valve implantation (TAVI) for aortic stenosis 下列何者為**非**?
(A) 因為要得到好的影像品質, 用 ß-blocker 控制心跳是必需的
(B) 一般測量 aortic annulus 大小都在 cardiac cycle 中的 systolic phase
(C) 如 aortic annulus 至 coronary ostium 距離<10mm 可能在 TAVI 置放後有 risk of coronary occlusion
(D) aortic annulus 一般指 aortic valve leaflets insertion 最低點, 剛好在 left ventricular outflow tract (LVOT)之上
??
**Ans: A**
Pre-medication with beta-blocker or nitrates, which is standard for coronary CTA angiography,
- should be **avoided in patients with severe AS** at the time of scanning, in order to avoid haemodynamic complications.
