#交換 #2017交換 #CV About pulmonary hypertension (PH), which is CORRECT?
(A) pre-capillary type is more than post-capillary type
(B) mosaic perfusion pattern is less seen in idiopathic type than in chronic pulmonary thrombo-embolic type
(C) transverse diameter of main pulmonary artery > 26 mm is highly considered PH
(D) Unilateral enlargement of left pulmonary artery in young woman indicates idiopathic pulmonary artery hypertension
??
**Ans: B**
**Mosaic perfusion pattern 在慢性血栓栓塞型（CTEPH）比特發性（idiopathic）型更常見**（故 idiopathic 型較少見）。
(A) 錯:臨床上肺高壓最常見病因為 **postcapillary（Group 2，左心病變/PH-LHD）**,非 precapillary 比 postcapillary 更常見。
(B) **正確（正解）**:mosaic perfusion 在 CTEPH 較常見、idiopathic 較少見。
(C) 錯:主肺動脈徑 >26 mm 門檻已過時,較新研究提出 >29 mm 或 31.6 mm。
(D) 錯:左右肺動脈徑（正常上限約 16 mm）對 PH 存在與否是**較差**的指標,不可僅憑單側擴大診斷 idiopathic PAH。
