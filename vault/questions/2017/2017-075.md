#交換 #2017交換 #US Which of the following is **NOT** typical ultrasound presentation of seminoma in the testis:
(A) homogeneous
(B) hypoechoic
(C) lobulated margin
(D) internal cystic components and calcification
??
**Ans: D**
- Seminomas are hypoechoic compared with the background testis.
- They tend to be homogeneous and may be lobulated or multinodular.
- Densely echogenic areas or calcifications (30%) and cystic spaces (10%) are relatively uncommon.
