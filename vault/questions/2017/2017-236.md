#交換 #2017交換 #Breast Which of the following statement is **wrong** about architectural distortion on breast MRI?
(A) An architectural distortion that is nonenhanced may represent a radial scar.
(B) An architectural distortion that enhanced is highly suggestive of invasive cancer.
(C) Desmoplastic tethering (the hook sign) has been described as highly indicative of a benign lesion.
(D) The hook sign is not observed in the presence of noninvasive cancers.
??
**Ans: C**
**Hook sign（desmoplastic tethering，自病灶中心朝胸肌的毛刺狀牽引）高度提示『惡性』，非良性**（惡性 29.3% vs 良性 3.2%）。
(A) 正確:不強化的構造扭曲可能為 radial scar。
(B) 正確:強化的構造扭曲高度提示侵襲癌。
(C) **錯誤（正解）**:hook sign 提示惡性,非良性。
(D) ⚠️ 官方標 C 但註記「D 似乎也錯」——hook sign 亦見於約 9.5% 原位（noninvasive）癌,故「不見於 noninvasive cancer」亦不精確,建議醫師確認。
