#交換 #2017交換 #CH Which statement about Rasmussen aneurysm is not **true**?
(A) Rasmussen's aneurysm was first subscribed in 1968, known as pulmonary arterial pseudoaneurysm (PAP)
(B) Rasmussen's pseudoaneurysm is classified as a vascular complication of chronic puomonary tuberculosis, showed a 5% incidence.
(C) Tuberculous PAPs usually involve in lower lung fields.
(D) The most common bleeding source is bronchial circulation (90%).
??
**Ans: C**
**Tuberculous PAP 通常分布於周邊、超出主肺動脈分支範圍，並非侷限於下肺葉**。
(A) 正確:1868年首次描述（題幹「1968」應為筆誤），稱PAP。
(B) 正確:慢性肺結核血管併發症，約5%發生率。
(C) **不正確（正解）**:應為周邊分布，非下肺野。
(D) 正確:最常見出血來源仍為支氣管循環約90%。
