#交換 #2017交換 #Unknown 有關pulmonary infection的敘述，下列何者為**非**
(A) Monad sign describes air that surrounds a mycetoma in a pre-existing pulmonary cavity. It should not be confused with the air crescent sign which is seen in recovering angioinvasive aspergillosis
(B) Finger-in-glove sign describes allergic bronchopulmonary aspergillosis (ABPA) as mucoid impaction within bronchiectasis.
(C) Water-lily sign is seen in hydatid infections when a cyst membrane floats in residual fluid after cyst rupture
(D) Signet ring sign is early stage of pulmonary paragonimiasis infection as thin-walled crescent-shaped worm cysts
??
**Ans: D**
**late** stage worm cyst的cavity跟周圍的出血也許會像signet ring [[Paragonimiasis]]
但Signet ring sign多指bronchiectasis在CT的表現。擴張的bronchiectasis+血管，看起來像戒指 ![ADD signet ring bronchiectasis](data/images/obsidian/add-signet-ring-bronchiectasis-a16871cd.png)
Bronchiectasis: bronchoarterial ratio > 1. 也可能合併tram-track sign [[ADD tram-track sign.png]]
