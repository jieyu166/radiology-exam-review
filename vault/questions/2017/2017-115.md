#交換 #2017交換 #NR Which artery reaches the skull base and can on occasion have intracranial anastomoses through the foramen lacerum and other routes?
(A) Middle meningeal artery.
(B) Accessory pharyngeal artery.
(C) Ascending pharyngeal artery.
(D) Persistent stapedial artery.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**。**Ascending pharyngeal artery（升咽動脈）** 的小腦膜分支經 foramen lacerum 達顱底,可與顱內(ICA/椎基底)吻合。ICA 沿 foramen lacerum 上緣走但不穿過。
