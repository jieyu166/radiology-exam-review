#交換 #2017交換 #ABD About gastric lesions causing giant gastric folds on MDCT, which description is **WRONG**?
(A) Giant gastric folds can be caused by scirrhous carcinoma, B-cell lymphoma, acute gastric mucosal lesions, and Menetrier disease.
(B) MDCT is a poor modality of choice to differentiate the diseases causing giant gastric folds.
(C) Gastric lymphoma mostly originates from the submucosa.
(D) Loss of wall stratification is the best MDCT predictor of malignancy.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**（為非）。**MDCT 是鑑別巨大胃皺襞病因的『好』工具**（藉壁分層、強化型態），非差選擇。成因(A)、胃淋巴瘤源自黏膜下(C)、壁分層消失＝惡性最佳預測(D)皆正確。
