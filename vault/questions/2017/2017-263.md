#交換 #2017交換 #ABD About the Cross-fused renal ectopia, which of the following description is correct?
(A) The definition of Cross-fused renal ectopia is the kidneys fuse on the same side and fails to properly ascend during development.
(B) The most common type of Cross-fused renal ectopia is horseshoe kidney.
(C) Pancake kidney is one of the most common types of renal ectopia.
(D) Image study cannot differentiate inferior crossed fusion type and superiorly crossed fused type.
??
**Ans: A**
**Cross-fused renal ectopia 定義為兩腎融合於同一側，發育中未能正常上升就位**。
(A) **正解**。
(B) 錯:horseshoe kidney 為獨立的最常見腎融合異常（兩腎仍分居兩側），非 cross-fused ectopia 的一種。
(C) 錯:pancake（disc）kidney 為 cross-fused ectopia 中的少見型態，非最常見型（最常見為 inferior crossed fusion）。
(D) 錯:影像可區分不同融合型態。
