#交換 #2017交換 #MSK Which of the following about Multiple Myeloma is **FALSE**?
(A) Men much more common than women
(B) Mandible involved in 1/3 of patients with diffuse involvement (Bony metastases rarely involve mandible, distal axial skeleton)
(C) Sclerosis is usually seen only with treatment or fracture
(D) In spine, MM destroys body and pedicle
??
**Ans: D**
**D為偽**——脊椎MM典型**侵犯椎體但保留椎弓根**（骨轉移則常侵犯椎弓根，為鑑別重點）。
(A) 男性好發:真。
(B) 約1/3侵犯下頜骨:真。
(C) 硬化僅見治療後或骨折:真。
(D) **正解(偽)**:MM保留椎弓根,非破壞。
