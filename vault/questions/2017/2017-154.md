#交換 #2016交換 #2017交換 #CH Regarding lung malignancy, which of the following statement is NOT correct?
(A) About two thirds of adrenal masses identified by CT in lung cancer patients are benign.
(B) Most typical carcinoid tumors show calcifications on CT.
(C) Pleural effusion in non-Hodgkin lymphoma is most likely caused by central lymphatic obstruction.
(D) Pulmonary involvement in Hodgkin disease usually presents as ill-defined nodules along bronchovascular bundles.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**（不正確）。Typical carcinoid 鈣化**少見（約 20-30%，central 40%）**，非「大多顯鈣化」。肺癌病人腎上腺腫塊 2/3 良性(A)、NHL 胸水源自中央淋巴阻塞(C)、Hodgkin 沿支氣管血管束(D)皆正確。
