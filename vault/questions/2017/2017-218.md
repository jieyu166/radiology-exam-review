#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Abdominal CT shows Fitz-Hugh-Curtis adhesions around both lobes of the liver and adhesions within the pelvis. What is the probable reason for this condition?
(A) Perforated peptic ulcer
(B) Ruptured ovarian dermoid
(C) Ovarian torsion
(D) Ruptured ectopic pregnancy
??
**Ans: B**
本題為 **Fitz-Hugh-Curtis 症候群之非典型病因案例＝卵巢皮樣囊腫破裂（ruptured ovarian dermoid）**——破裂內容物致化學性腹膜炎,造成肝周＋骨盆黏連。
雖 FHC 典型源自骨盆腔發炎（PID, Chlamydia/Gonorrhea）,本案例（依所引案例報告）答案為 **B（卵巢皮樣囊腫破裂）**。其餘 A 穿孔潰瘍、C 卵巢扭轉、D 子宮外孕破裂非本案例之肝周黏連病因。
