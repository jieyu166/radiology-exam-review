#交換 #2017交換 #CV Causes of vascular rings and slings commonly include all of the following, EXCEPT:
(A) Double aortic arch.
(B) Right aortic arch.
(C) Aortic coarctation.
(D) Pulmonary sling.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**。**Aortic coarctation（主動脈窄縮）非血管環/吊帶成因**。double aortic arch(A)、right aortic arch(B)、pulmonary sling(D)皆為常見成因。
