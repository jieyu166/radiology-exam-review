#交換 #2017交換 #US Which statement regarding ultrasound diagnosis of testicular torsion is **WRONG**?
(A) Gray-scale ultrasound may be normal in the early phase of testicular torsion.
(B) Preserved vascularity in the painful testis excludes the possibility of testicular torsion.
(C) Para-testicular hyperemia is commonly seen on the affected side.
(D) Heterogeneous echogenicity in the affected testis is a sign of testicular infarction.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**（錯誤）。**保留血流不能排除扭轉**——不完全/部分扭轉可保留甚至增加血流；torsion-detorsion 反應性充血亦可誤判為 epididymo-orchitis。灰階早期可正常(A)、heterogeneous echogenicity 提示梗塞(D)皆正確。
