#交換 #2016交換 #2017交換 #CH Regarding lung tumors, which of the following statement is NOT correct?
(A) Leiomyoma is usually solitary, well-circumscribed, and may occasionally calcify.
(B) In diffuse infiltrative amyloidosis, fine linear, nodular, or reticulonodular pattern may be observed.
(C) Calcification is identified in approximately 75% of hamartoma on CT.
(D) Small cell lung cancer usually has massive bilateral mediastinal adenopathy.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**（不正確）。肺錯構瘤 CT 鈣化僅約 **30-40%**，非 75%。leiomyoma 孤立可鈣化(A)、瀰漫型 amyloidosis 呈 fine linear/nodular/reticulonodular(B)、SCLC 大量雙側縱膈淋巴腫(D)皆正確。
