#交換 #2017交換 #CH About interstitial fibrosis and emphysema of the lung, which is correct?
(A) emphysema is less common than interstitial fibrosis
(B) they are often coexistent and should be regard as a distinct disease entity
(C) emphysema is always localized at upper lung zone
(D) they are just two common disease occur together
??
**Ans: B**
**肺氣腫與間質纖維化常共存（尤吸菸者），應視為一個獨立疾病實體——CPFE（combined pulmonary fibrosis and emphysema）syndrome**，凸顯單純二分肺氣腫/纖維化的侷限。
(A) 錯:肺氣腫並非較纖維化少見。
(B) **正確（正解）**:CPFE 為獨立疾病實體。
(C) 錯:肺氣腫非總侷限上肺（雖常上肺為主）。
(D) 錯:非「只是兩種常見病剛好同時出現」,而是有共同（吸菸）易感性的獨立症候群。
