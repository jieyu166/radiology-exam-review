#交換 #2017交換 #Breast According to ACR BI-RADS 5th edition, which is **wrong** about the likelihood of breast cancer in each final assessment category?
(A) category 2, essentially 0%
(B) category 3, equal or less than 3%
(C) category 4a, equal or less than 10%
(D) category 4b, between 10% and 50%
(E) category 5, equal or more than 95%.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**（錯誤）。**BI-RADS 3 為 ≤2%**（非 ≤3%）。category 2 ~0%(A)、4a 2–9%(C)、4b 10–49%(D)、5 ≥95%(E)皆正確。
