#交換 #2017交換 #2020交換 #NR Regarding pediatric bacterial meningitis, which statement is the MOST inappropriate?
(A) Neonatal bacterial meningitis has different causative organisms from that in preschool children.
(B) The disease can progress over hours with rapid deterioration.
(C) Imaging should be recommended for all children with suspected meningitis.
(D) Antibiotic treatment with periodic follow-up of meningeal thickening is appropriate.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**（最不恰當）。**影像僅用於特定指徵**（免疫低下、局部神經缺損、papilledema、LP 延遲），非所有疑似兒童。致病菌依年齡不同(A)、病程可小時計惡化(B)皆正確。
