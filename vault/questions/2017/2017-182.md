#交換 #2017交換 #CH Concerning pulmonary septic emboli, which of the followings is INCORRECT?
(A) Most emboli originate in the heart.
(B) Staphylococcus aureus is the most common organism.
(C) Lemierre's syndrome refers to emboli originate from deep vein thrombosis.
(D) Thin-walled cavities are frequently seen on chest radiograph.
??
**Ans: C**
**Lemierre's syndrome 並非源自深部靜脈血栓(DVT)**,而是口咽感染（咽炎/扁桃腺炎±膿瘍）後的**頸內靜脈敗血性血栓性靜脈炎合併遠端轉移性敗血**。
(A) 正確:多數敗血性栓子源自心臟（如三尖瓣心內膜炎）/靜脈導管。
(B) 正確:Staph. aureus 為最常見致病菌。
(C) **不正確（正解）**:Lemierre 為頸內靜脈敗血性血栓性靜脈炎（非 DVT）。
(D) 正確:胸片常見薄壁空洞。
