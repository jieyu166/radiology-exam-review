#交換 #2017交換 #NR To differentiate radiation necrosis and brain tumor recurrence, which is not the characteristics for radiation necrosis?
(A) Radiation necrosis can be suspected by location of primary tumor and radiation port
(B) Radiation necrosis has higher lesion quotient in MR image
(C) Periventricular enhancing foci within the radiation port are typical of radiation-induced enhancement
(D) An enhancing area with a "cut green pepper" or "soap bubble" appearance may suggest radiation necrosis
??
**Ans: B**
**B為偽**——腫瘤復發的lesion quotient較放射線壞死『高』(復發>0.6、放射線壞死<0.3),非放射線壞死較高。
(A) 可依原發腫瘤位置與照野推測:真。
(B) **正解(偽)**。
(C) 腦室旁強化灶典型提示放射線引起:真。
(D) cut green pepper/soap bubble外觀可提示放射線壞死:真(但研究顯示不可靠鑑別)。
