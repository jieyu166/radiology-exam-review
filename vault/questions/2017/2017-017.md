#交換 #2017交換 #Breast Regarding mammoagraphy, which of the following statement is NOT correct?
(A) Placing the breast halfway between focal spot and image receptor would magnify the image by a factor of 1.4.
(B) A digital detector with 0.1mm pixel size has spatial resolution of 5 line pairs/mm.
(C) Magnification mammography uses smaller focal spot (0.1mm) than screening mammography to reduce image blurring.
(D) MLO view means x-ray source in the medial location, and image receptor in the lateral location.
??
**Ans: A**
~~1.4~~應為2.
The limiting spatial resolution of digital mammography systems is less (5–10 lp/mm), due to pixelization of the image by the digital image receptor.
In digital, a “line” is 1 pixel width, and a line pair is 2 pixels.
