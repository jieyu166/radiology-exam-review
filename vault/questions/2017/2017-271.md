#交換 #2017交換 #Breast Calcification is seen on a screening mammogram. Which of the following patterns is the most likely to be associated with a carcinoma?
(A) tortuous tramline calcification
(B) a cluster of 10 calcific particles, all less than 0.5mm
(C) eggshell, curvilinear calcification
(D) thick, linear, rod-like calcifications, some with a lucent centre
??
**Ans: B**
**群聚10顆、每顆<0.5mm的細小鈣化顆粒符合 fine pleomorphic 型態**，屬可疑型態（BI-RADS 4，PPV約29-58%）。
(A) tortuous tramline:血管鈣化，良性。
(B) **正解**:fine pleomorphic 型態，可疑。
(C) eggshell/curvilinear:rim鈣化（脂肪壞死/囊壁），良性。
(D) thick rod-like with lucent centre:secretory型，良性。
