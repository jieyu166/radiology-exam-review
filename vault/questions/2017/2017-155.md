#交換 #2017交換 #CH Regarding empyema necessitans, which statement is **WRONG**?
(A) It refers to empyema invading the bronchus.
(B) Mycobacterium tuberculosis is the most common causative organism.
(C) Patients commonly present with a red, swollen, warm, painful chest wall mass.
(D) First described by Guillaume de Baillou in 1640.
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**（為非）。Empyema necessitans 是膿胸**穿出胸腔、過胸壁進入軟組織**，非侵犯支氣管。M. tuberculosis 最常見(B)、胸壁皮膚紅腫熱痛(C)、歷史(D)皆正確。
