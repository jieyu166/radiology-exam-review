#交換 #2017交換 #CH 有關pulmonary Langerhans cell histiocytosis, 下列何者有誤
(A) In the early stage, pulmonary LCH is characterized by the presence of granulomas resulting in destruction of lung tissue.
(B) In the later stages, the cellular granulomas are replaced by fibrosis and lung cysts.
(C) Less than 10% of adults with LCH are smokers.
(D) HRCT demonstrates cystic air spaces, which are usually less than 10 mm in diameter.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**（有誤）。**up to ~95% 的成人 LCH 有吸菸史**,非 <10%。早期肉芽腫(A)、後期纖維化+囊腫(B)、HRCT 囊腔常 <10 mm(D)皆正確。
