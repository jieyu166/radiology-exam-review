#交換 #2017交換 #CH Which statement is **WRONG** about pulmonary interstitial emphysema?
(A) Sudden deterioration in patients during mechanical ventilation.
(B) Air-block phenomenon is particularly common in the elderly.
(C) Pseudocyst or pneumothorax may happen.
(D) Bilateral symmetrical distribution is typical.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**（錯誤）。PIE 的 air-block 現象好發**新生兒（早產、機械通氣）**，非老年。通氣中突然惡化(A)、可併 pseudocyst/氣胸(C)、雙側對稱(D)皆正確。
