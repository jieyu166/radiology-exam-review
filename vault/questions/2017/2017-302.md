#交換 #2017交換 #MSK Which of the following statements about Paget disease involving petrous apex is **FALSE**?
(A) Characterized by production of dense fragile bone formed by alternating episodes of excessive abnormal osteoclastic and osteoblastic activity
(B) The disease may be polyostotic or monostotic and typically will involve the facial bones (usually involves the calvaria, skull base, and temporal bones)
(C) It can divided into 4 phases: osteolytic, mixed, sclerotic and remodeling phase
(D) MR imaging shows heterogeneous signal intensity on T1- and T2-weighted images with heterogeneous enhancement in involved bone
??
**Ans: B**
**B為偽**——Paget disease典型侵犯顱蓋、顱底、顳骨,非「顏面骨」。
(A) 硬化/溶骨交替活動:真。
(B) **正解(偽)**:應為calvaria/skull base/temporal bones。
(C) 四期分類:真。
(D) MR訊號與強化不均:真。
