#交換 #2017交換 #IR When patient with abnormal low PT or INR on schedule for vascular intervention, best strategy for correction is fresh frozen plasma given at:
(A) 3 days before vascular intervention
(B) The night before vascular intervention
(C) Shortly before and/or during vascular intervention
(D) The second day after vascular intervention
??
**Ans: C**
**C（緊接手術前及/或手術中）**——FFP效果短暫,須緊鄰手術時機給予才能於操作當下提供足夠矯正效果。
(A)(B)(D) 皆因給予時機與手術操作時間相距過遠,凝血因子效果已消退或尚未發揮作用。
(C) **正解**。
