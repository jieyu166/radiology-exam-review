#交換 #2017交換 #NR Which one of following entities is clinical characteristic with arylsulfatase A deficiency?
(A) Adreno leukodystrophy
(B) Alexander disease
(C) Pelizaeus-Merzbacher disease
(D) Metachromatic leukodystrophy
??
**Ans: D**
- A. lack of oxidation of very long chain fatty acids (VLCFAs)
- B. the gene for glial fibrillary acidic protein (GFAP): mapped to chromosome 17q21.
Histologically the disease is characterised by the accumulation of large numbers of Rosenthal fibres and eosinophilic granular bodies (large accumulations agglomerations of astrocytic processes) in the degenerated (demyelinated) white matter which is a product of GFAP.
- C. derangement in the proteolipidprotein (PLP1) gene locus at Xq22
