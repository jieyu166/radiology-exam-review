#交換 #2017交換 #MSK Which one of the following tumors does NOT usually occur in a young patient?
(A) Ewing sarcoma.
(B) Alveolar soft part sarcoma.
(C) Rhabdomyosarcoma.
(D) Kaposi sarcoma.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**。**Kaposi sarcoma 好發年長/免疫低下（HIV/移植，HHV-8 相關）**，非年輕病人。Ewing sarcoma、alveolar soft part sarcoma、rhabdomyosarcoma 皆好發年輕族群。
