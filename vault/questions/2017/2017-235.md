#交換 #2017交換 #Breast Which of the following statement is **wrong** about breast MRI? (mass margin)
(A) Margin of a focal mass is the most predictive feature of the breast MR image.
(B) Irregular or spiculated margins have a positive predictive value (PPV) of 60%-70%.
(C) Features associated with malignancy include heterogeneous internal enhancement and enhancing internal septa.
(D) A segmental distribution of enhancement was associated with a 78% likelihood of cancer.
??
**Ans: B**
**不規則/毛刺緣（irregular/spiculated margins）的 PPV 約 84%-91%，非 60%-70%**。
(A) 正確:邊緣為腫塊最具預測性的特徵。
(B) **錯誤（正解）**:spiculated margin PPV 約 84-91%。
(C) 正確:不均質內部強化、強化內隔皆偏惡性。
(D) 正確:節段性分布強化約 78% 為惡性。
