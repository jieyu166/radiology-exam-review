#交換 #2017交換 #ABD 關於 inflammatory bowel disease 的描述，下列何者為非?
(A) Ulcerative colitis (UC) is mainly limited to the large intestine and affects only the intestinal mucosa.
(B) Crohn disease (CD) can occur at any location(s) along the GI tract and is often transmural.
(C) Nephrolithiasis is common extraintestinal IBD manifestation in IBD, particularly in CD with distal ileal involvement.
(D) Severe CD may exhibit "backwash" ileitis.
??
**Ans: D**
**Backwash ileitis 是 ulcerative colitis（UC）的特徵**（嚴重全結腸炎逆流入末端迴腸）,不是 Crohn disease。
(A) 正確:UC 侷限大腸、僅黏膜層。
(B) 正確:CD 全消化道任一段、常為全層（transmural）。
(C) 正確:草酸鈣腎結石為常見腸外表現,尤其末端迴腸受累的 CD。
(D) **為非（正解）**:backwash ileitis 屬 UC,非 CD。
