#交換 #2017交換 #CH About distribution of disease within the lung, which one is **WRONG**?
(A) Sarcoidosis shows upper lung zone predominance.
(B) Asbestosis shows upper lung zone predominance.
(C) Silicosis shows upper lung zone predominance.
(D) Coal worker's pneumoconiosis shows upper lung zone predominance.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**（錯誤）。**Asbestosis 為下肺周邊為主**，非上肺。sarcoidosis(A)、silicosis(C)、coal worker's pneumoconiosis(D) 皆上肺為主。
