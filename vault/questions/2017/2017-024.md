#交換 #2017交換 #2018交換 #CH About application of the Silhouette sign on chest plain film, which one of the following statements is **WRONG**?
(A) Loss of right heart border: right middle lobe or anterior pleura/mediastinum
(B) Loss of aortic arch: left lower lobe or middle/posterior mediastinum
(C) Loss of left heart border: ligula or anterior pleura/mediastinum
(D) Loss of right paratracheal stripe: right upper lobe
(E) None of the above
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**（錯誤）。**主動脈弓（knob）消失＝LUL（apicoposterior）或中/後縱膈,非 LLL**。右心緣＝RML/前胸膜(A)、左心緣＝lingula/前胸膜(C)、右氣管旁線＝RUL(D)皆正確。
