#交換 #2017交換 #Physics Which symptom is associated with Gadolinium deposition disease ?
(A) Headache
(B) Bone and joint pain
(C) Tightness of hands and feet
(D) All of above
??
**Ans: D**
Gadolinium in Humans: A Family of Disorders. AJR Am J Roentgenol. 2016 Aug;207(2):229-33
Diagnostic criterion for GDD 接受GBCA 注射**2月**內，出現下列至少3種症狀：
- peripheral neuropathic pain in either a “glove and stocking” or generalized distribution;
- joint stiffness, muscle spasms, buzzing sensation, and fatigue;
- headache;
- clouded mentation;
- distal extremity and skin substrate thickening, discoloration, and pain
