#交換 #2017交換 #MSK Which of the following about Foot Charcot Arthropathy is **FALSE**?
(A) Most common secondary to diabetes
(B) Foot frequently shows ulceration
(C) Usually first involves metaphysis of MT joint (reabsorption of the ends of the affected bone)
(D) Usually involves MTP joint with astonishing joint destruction on initial radiograph
??
**Ans: C**
**C為偽**——病變最初侵犯**1st MTP joint**，表現為受侵犯骨端**reabsorption**（非蹠骨metaphysis破壞）。
(A) 糖尿病最常見病因:真。
(B) 足部常見潰瘍:真。
(C) **正解(偽)**:最初侵犯MTP關節非metaphysis。
(D) MTP關節初診即顯著破壞:真。
