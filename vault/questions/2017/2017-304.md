#交換 #2017交換 #PED A 10-year-old girl presents with right hip pain. Which of the following radiographic features makes the diagnosis of SUFE more likely than Perthes' disease?
(A) Reduction in size of the proximal femoral epiphysis
(B) Loss of overlap between the medial femoral metaphysis and acetabulum (loss of triangular sign of Capener)
(C) Regional femoral demineralization
(D) A crescent of subcortical lucency in the femoral head
(E) Fragmentation of the femoral head
??
**Ans: B**
**B（內側股骨頸幹端與髖臼重疊消失，Capener三角徵消失）**——反映骨骺實質滑脫，為SUFE特異性徵象。
(A)(C)(D)(E) 骨骺縮小、局部去礦質化、軟骨下新月狀透亮、碎裂皆為Perthes disease缺血性壞死重塑過程的典型表現。
(B) **正解**。
