#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Which of following ligament is landmark between left subphrenic space and left paracolic gutter?
(A) Gastrosplenic ligament
(B) Phrenicocolic ligament
(C) Splenorenal ligament
(D) Gastrocolic ligament
??
**Ans: B**
**B（Phrenicocolic ligament，Hensing's韌帶）**——連結結腸脾彎曲至橫膈,為左膈下腔隙與左結腸旁溝的解剖界標。
(A)(C)(D) 皆非此界標韌帶。
(B) **正解**。
