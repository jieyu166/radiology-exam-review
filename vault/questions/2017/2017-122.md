#交換 #2017交換 #Breast Which of the following is NOT in the differential diagnosis of spiculated masses?
(A) Postbiopsy scar.
(B) Tubular cancer.
(C) Radial scar.
(D) Mucinous (colloid) carcinoma.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**。**Mucinous (colloid) carcinoma 呈圓形/邊界清楚腫塊**，非毛刺狀。postbiopsy scar(A)、tubular cancer(B)、radial scar(C)皆為 spiculated mass DDx。
