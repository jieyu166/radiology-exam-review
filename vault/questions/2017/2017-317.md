#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Which statement of abnormal placental attachment is **wrong**?
(A) Placenta percreta means villi partially invades myometrium
(B) Prior cesarean section and placenta previa are the two most important risk factors
(C) MR image can provide information on depth of invasion and more clearly depict posterior placentas than ultrasound
(D) MR imaging findings are uterine bulging, heterogeneous placenta, and placental bands
??
**Ans: A**
**A為偽**——Placenta percreta定義為絨毛侵犯至或超過子宮漿膜層(非部分侵入肌層,此為increta的定義)。
(A) **正解(偽)**。
(B) 前次剖腹產與前置胎盤為最重要危險因子:真。
(C) MRI較超音波更清楚顯示後壁胎盤並評估侵犯深度:真。
(D) MRI表現含子宮膨出/胎盤訊號不均/帶狀構造:真。
