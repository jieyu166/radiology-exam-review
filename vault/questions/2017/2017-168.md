#交換 #2017交換 #NR Which one of the following is the most appropriate description of spinal cord injury in children?
(A) Spinal cord injury in the pediatric population is relatively rare.
(B) SCIWORA in children is not an indication for spinal MRI examination.
(C) SCIWORA is rare in children with non-accidental trauma.
(D) Scoliosis is uncommon in patients with SCI before their adolescent growth spurt.
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**（兒童脊髓損傷相對少見）。SCIWORA 是 MRI 適應症(B 錯)、兒虐近 100% 為 SCIWORA(C 錯)、生長陡增前受傷 ~97% 發展脊柱側彎(D 錯)。⚠️ 官方答案鍵標「D A??」存疑，建議醫師確認。
