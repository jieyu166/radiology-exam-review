#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Which description about MR cholangiopancreatography (MRCP) is **wrong**?
(A) MRCP has been used more often for pre-operative bile ducts exploration to rule out choledocholithiasis in symptomatic patients and to exclude gall stone-associated disease.
(B) The use of the more invasive ERCP is preferred in subjects with low probability of needing therapeutic intervention.
(C) Furthermore, MRCP has the advantage of accurately demonstrating anatomical variants of the biliary system, which are a potential intra-operatively risk factor for iatrogenic injuries.
(D) All above are correct.
??
**Ans: B**
**侵入性較高的 ERCP 應用於『需治療介入機率高』者,而非低機率者**——低介入機率者優先用非侵入的 MRCP。
(A) 正確:MRCP 術前排除膽總管結石/膽石疾病。
(B) **錯誤（正解）**:應為高介入機率者用 ERCP;低機率者用 MRCP。
(C) 正確:MRCP 準確顯示膽道解剖變異（減少醫源性損傷）。
