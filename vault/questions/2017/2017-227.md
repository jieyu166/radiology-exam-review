#交換 #2017交換 #CV Which of the following is **wrong** about SVC syndrome (SVCS)?
(A) The dominant cause of SVCS has varied over time, and the majority of cases today are caused by malignancy, typically metastatic tumors.
(B) Benign processes, such as stenosis and thrombosis induced by central venous catheters, are responsible for an increasing minority of cases.
(C) The majority of patients with SVCS experience face, neck, and upper extremity swelling; dyspnea at rest; and cough.
(D) Endovascular therapy can offer rapid and enduring symptomatic relief and is an important adjunctive therapy.
??
**Ans: A**
**SVCS 的惡性病因最常見為『肺癌與淋巴瘤』，非籠統的『轉移腫瘤』**。
(A) **錯誤（正解）**:惡性主因為肺癌與淋巴瘤,非 metastatic tumors。
(B) 正確:中心靜脈導管致狹窄/血栓的良性病因佔比逐漸上升。
(C) 正確:多數有顏面/頸/上肢腫脹、靜息呼吸困難、咳嗽。
(D) 正確:血管內治療（stent）可快速持久緩解。
