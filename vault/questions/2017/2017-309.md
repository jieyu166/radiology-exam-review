#交換 #2017交換 #ABD A 58-year-old African woman developed right upper quadrant pain. Ultrasound showed a grossly cirrhotic liver with markedly hyperechoic septa separating areas of relatively normal liver, recognized as a 'turtle back' appearance. What is the most likely underlying diagnosis?
(A) Amoebiasis
(B) Hydatid
(C) Lymphoma
(D) Schistosomiasis
(E) Tuberculosis
??
**Ans: D**
**D（Schistosomiasis）**——高回音間隔分隔正常肝實質形成turtle back appearance,反映門脈周圍pipestem fibrosis。
(A) Amoebiasis:典型呈肝膿瘍非纖維化間隔。
(B) Hydatid:呈囊性病灶伴子囊。
(C) Lymphoma:呈局部/瀰漫低回音病灶。
(D) **正解**。
(E) Tuberculosis:可呈粟粒狀病灶或膿瘍非turtle back紋路。
