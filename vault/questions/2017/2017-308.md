#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Following a holiday in South-East Asia, a 33-year-old man presented with abdominal pain. Ultrasound demonstrated an echo-poor lesion within the right lobe of the liver which contained several smaller internal cysts. What is the most likely diagnosis?
(A) Amoebic abscess
(B) Echinococcus
(C) Pyogenic abscess
(D) Polycystic liver disease
(E) Haemangioma
??
**Ans: B**
**B（Echinococcus）**——子囊(daughter cysts,honeycomb/rosette狀)為肝包蟲病(CE2型)特徵性超音波表現。
(A) Amoebic abscess:無子囊結構。
(B) **正解**。
(C) Pyogenic abscess:臨床常伴發燒感染徵象。
(D) Polycystic liver disease:囊腫間無母子關係。
(E) Haemangioma:血管性病灶非囊性。
