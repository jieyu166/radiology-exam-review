#交換 #2017交換 #CV About hypertrophic cardiomyopathy (HCM), which statement is **wrong**?
(A) The typical pattern of late gadolinium enhancement in HCM is midwall patchy or punctate hyperenhancement in a thickened myocardium in a noncoronary distribution.
(B) The apical form is the most common morphologic variant of HCM and accounts for up to 60%–70% of cases.
(C) Specific complications of apical HCM include development of apical infarctions with resultant aneurysm formation
(D) The diagnosis of asymmetric septal hypertrophy is made when septal thickness is greater than or equal to 15 mm or when the ratio of the septal thickness to the thickness of the inferior wall at the midventricular level is greater than 1.5.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**（錯誤）。**apical 型非最常見——asymmetric septal 才是（60–70%），apical 僅 ~2%（日本人 ~15%）**。LGE 典型 midwall patchy(A)、apical HCM 併心尖梗塞/動脈瘤(C)、asymmetric septal 定義中隔 ≥1.5×(D)皆正確。
