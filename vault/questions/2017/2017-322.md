#交換 #2017交換 #MSK About Achondroplasia, which of following is not **true**
(A) Rhizomelic dwarfism
(B) The interpedicular distance widening distally (progressive decrease in the interpedicular distance in the lumbar spine)
(C) Trident hand
(D) Short and stubby fingers (bullet-shaped)
??
**Ans: B**
**B為偽**——腰椎椎弓根間距應由頭側向尾側漸進縮小(非漸進增寬,與正常脊椎相反)。
(A) Rhizomelic dwarfism:真。
(B) **正解(偽)**。
(C) Trident hand:真。
(D) Bullet-shaped短粗手指:真。
