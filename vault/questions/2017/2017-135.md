#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Which of the following is NOT a typical image finding of polycystic ovary syndrome on MRI?
(A) Enlarged ovaries.
(B) Multiple follicles at the central part of the ovary.
(C) Usually bilateral.
(D) All of the above are typical image findings.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**（不是典型）。PCOS 濾泡呈**周邊排列(string of pearls)**，非卵巢中央。卵巢腫大(A)、通常雙側(C)皆為典型。
