#交換 #2017交換 #Breast Regarding stereotactic vacuum-assisted breast biopsy (VABB), which is **WRONG**?
(A) Stereo pair and 0-degree scout view can calculate the 3D position of the target.
(B) About 99% can obtain a representative sample.
(C) Mainly for biopsy of mammographically visible lesions, especially calcifications.
(D) A tissue marker clip is placed for future surgical localization.
(E) If no residual calcification remains on the post-biopsy mammogram and pathology is malignant, surgical excision is not needed.
??
**Ans: E**
正解 **E**（為非）。即使 VABB 移除全部微鈣化，惡性者手術時仍約 **80%** 有殘餘癌（Liberman et al.）→ **仍需手術切除**。stereo pair 算 3D(A)、~99% 足夠樣本(B)、針對鈣化(C)、置 marker clip(D)皆正確。
