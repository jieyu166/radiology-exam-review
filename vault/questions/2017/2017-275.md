#交換 #2017交換 #CH Which statement about chest trauma is not **true**?
(A) Ribs 10 – 12 fracture is associated with lacerations of liver/spleen.
(B) Pulmonary Contusion I is most common finding in blunt chest injury.
(C) Pulmonary laceration is half solid and half cyst.
(D) Fracture of trachea and bronchus is due to penetration trauma.
??
**Ans: D**
**氣管與支氣管骨折主要由鈍傷造成**（好發距隆凸2.5cm內的遠端氣管），穿刺傷反而以頸部氣管最常受傷。
(A) 正確:肋骨10-12骨折提示肝脾撕裂。
(B) 正確:肺挫傷為鈍性胸創最常見表現。
(C) 正確:肺撕裂傷呈半實質（血腫）半囊狀（創傷性肺氣囊腫）。
(D) **不正確（正解）**:主要由鈍傷造成，非穿刺傷。
