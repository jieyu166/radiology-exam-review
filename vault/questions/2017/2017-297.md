#交換 #2017交換 #MSK Which of the following is **TRUE** about compartment syndrome?
(A) Necrosis of tissue may begin at interstitial pressure as low as 50 mmHg (above): pressure higher than 30 mmHg of the diastolic pressure is associated with compartment syndrome; for those with hypotension, a pressure of 20 mmHg higher than the intra-compartmental pressure is associated with compartment syndrome
(B) Compartment syndrome is not a life-threatening condition
(C) It progresses to rhabdomyolysis if untreated
(D) A fasciotomy procedure with incision in the skin and the muscle fibers (fascia) is necessary to release the pressure and regain normal function of the arteries
??
**Ans: C**
**C（未治療進展為橫紋肌溶解症）為真**。
(A) 診斷閾值應以delta pressure(舒張壓−腔室壓)<20-30mmHg為準,非單純腔室壓絕對值。
(B) 為危及生命之急症,非「非致命」。
(C) **正解**。
(D) 筋膜切開術切開皮膚與筋膜(fascia),非肌肉纖維。
