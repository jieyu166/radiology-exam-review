#交換 #2017交換 #CH Which statement about acute respiratory distress syndrome (ARDS), cardiogenic pulmonary edema, renal based pulmonary edema is **true**?
(A) The differential diagnosis of ARDS, included cardiogenic pulmonary edema, interstitial pneumonia, and renal based pulmonary edema.
(B) All three have Kerleys B line.
(C) Renal based pulmonary edema is peripheral in distribution mostly.
(D) Cardiogenic edema is even distribution mostly.
??
**Ans: D**
**心因性肺水腫約90%呈均勻分布**。
(A) 敘述本身描述ARDS鑑別診斷正確，但非本題最佳答案。
(B) 錯:ARDS幾乎無Kerley B lines（僅心因性與腎因性各約30%）。
(C) 錯:腎因性肺水腫多為**中央**分布（約70%），非周邊。
(D) **正解**:心因性約90%均勻分布。
