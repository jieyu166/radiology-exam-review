#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Which of the following groups should begin screening for colorectal carcinoma at age 50?
(A) Patients with polyposis syndrome
(B) Patients with family history of colon cancer
(C) Asymptomatic patients with no known risk factors
(D) Patients with prior history of colon cancer
(E) Patients with heme-positive stools
??
**Ans: C**
**一般族群（無症狀無風險因子）從50歲開始篩檢**。
(A) 息肉症候群:需自青少年期即開始篩檢。
(B) 家族史:通常提早開始。
(C) **正解**:一般族群篩檢起始年齡。
(D) 個人病史:依術後監測時程追蹤。
(E) heme-positive stools:症狀性,需立即檢查。
