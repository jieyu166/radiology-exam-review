#交換 #2017交換 #MSK Choose the **false** statement of giant cell tumor.
(A) On conventional radiographs show a lytic lesion that has a well-defined sclerotic margin.
(B) It is eccentric in location, and extends to the subchondral bone, and occurs in patients with closed physes.
(C) GCT may also have aggressive features, such as a wide zone of transition, cortical thinning, expansile remodeling, or even cortical bone destruction and an associated soft-tissue mass.
(D) GCT may also contain fluid-fluid levels due to secondary aneurysmal bone cyst formation.
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**（為假）。**GCT 典型為『非硬化緣』(narrow zone of transition)，非界線清楚的硬化緣**(<5% 可些微硬化)。偏心延伸至軟骨下/骨骺閉合後(B)、可有侵襲徵象(C)、可含 fluid-fluid levels（次發 ABC，D）皆正確。
