#交換 #2017交換 #NR Regarding intracranial dural arteriovenous fistula, which of the following descriptions is **WRONG**?
(A) The most common location is the transverse-sigmoid sinus.
(B) The most common clinical presentation is pulsatile tinnitus.
(C) Endovascular treatment is generally more effective than radiosurgery.
(D) It is considered a congenital disease.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**（錯誤）。**dAVF 多為後天性**（常伴靜脈竇栓塞），非先天。最常見於 transverse-sigmoid sinus(A)、最常見症狀 pulsatile tinnitus(B)、血管內栓塞常較放射手術有效(C)皆正確。
