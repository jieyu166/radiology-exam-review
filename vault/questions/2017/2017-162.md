#交換 #2017交換 #CV Which of the following is the cause of slab or banding artifact in cardiac CT?
(A) Helical mode acquisition.
(B) Beam hardening.
(C) Changes in contrast enhancement over the duration of the scan.
(D) Gadolinium-based contrast agent administration.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**。Slab/banding artifact 成因為**掃描期間對比強化隨時間變化**（多 slab 接合處）。
