#交換 #2017交換 #2018交換 #Breast Which statement about breast MRI is **WRONG**?
(A) A focus may be benign or malignant
(B) Multiple separated foci with intervening non-enhancing breast tissue should be considered as background parenchymal enhancement (BPE)
(C) Non-enhancing masses with benign morphology are nearly always benign
(D) Pre-contrast high signal in ducts on T1WI is a benign finding
(E) None of the above
??
**Ans: E**
- Focus (formerly defined as <5 mm): enhancement too small to be characterised, should be considered as BPE if symmetric and multiple
- Pre-contrast high signal in ducts on T1WI: may be blood or fat
