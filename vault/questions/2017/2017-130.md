#交換 #2017交換 #CH Regarding HRCT criteria for usual interstitial pneumonia (UIP) pattern, which one is NOT correct?
(A) Extensive ground-glass abnormality (more than reticular abnormality).
(B) Subpleural, basal predominance.
(C) Reticular abnormality.
(D) Honeycombing with or without traction bronchiectasis.
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**（為非）。**Extensive GGO（多於 reticular）屬『inconsistent with UIP』**。UIP 四特徵＝subpleural/basal(B)、reticular(C)、honeycombing±traction bronchiectasis(D)、無不符特徵。
