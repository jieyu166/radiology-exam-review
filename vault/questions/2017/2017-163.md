#交換 #2017交換 #CV Which of the following is the most common cause of sudden death in young athletes?
(A) Excessive caffeine intake before vigorous exercise.
(B) Anomalous right coronary artery coursing anterior to the RV outflow tract.
(C) Anomalous left coronary artery coursing posterior to the aorta.
(D) Hypertrophic cardiomyopathy (HCM).
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**。**Hypertrophic cardiomyopathy (HCM)** 為年輕運動員猝死最常見原因。冠狀動脈異常起源為次要原因。
