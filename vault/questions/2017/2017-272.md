#交換 #2017交換 #Breast Which of the following forms of calcification raises greatest suspicion of ductal carcinoma in situ (DCIS)?
(A) Coarse
(B) Dot-dash fine linear/linear branching
(C) Sedimented
(D) Egg-shell
??
**Ans: B**
**Dot-dash fine linear/linear branching 鈣化強烈提示乳管腔內沉積**，為 DCIS 典型表現，可疑度最高。
(A) Coarse:良性。(C) Sedimented（milk of calcium）:良性。(D) Egg-shell（rim）:良性。
(B) **正解**。
