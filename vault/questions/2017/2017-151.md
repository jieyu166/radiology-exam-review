#交換 #2017交換 #CH Regarding pulmonary hamartoma, which of the following statement is NOT correct?
(A) Hamartoma occasionally occurs endobronchially and produces obstructive symptoms.
(B) The characteristic calcification of hamartoma is a lamellated appearance.
(C) The presence of fat (-50 to -150 HU) is helpful in making this diagnosis.
(D) It may contain bone, vessels, and smooth muscle components.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**（不正確）。肺錯構瘤特徵鈣化為 **popcorn（爆米花）**，非 lamellated。偶支氣管內致阻塞(A)、脂肪密度(-50~-150 HU)助診斷(C)、含骨/血管/平滑肌(D)皆正確。
