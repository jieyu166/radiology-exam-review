#交換 #2017交換 #MSK The typical clinical course of CRMO (Chronic Recurrent Multifocal Osteomyelitis) is best characterized by which of the following?
(A) Severe long-term sequelae are seen in most patients.
(B) CRMO is often accompanied by fulminant systemic symptoms.
(C) CRMO has a prolonged course with periodic exacerbations.
(D) The predominant symptoms of CRMO are diffuse bone pain and swelling.
??
**Ans: C**
**CRMO 典型呈長期病程合併週期性惡化**。
(A) 錯:雖可致顯著病態，但非「多數病人有嚴重長期後遺症」的絕對化說法（CRMO 一般為自限性）。
(B) 錯:全身猛爆症狀少見，非典型特徵。
(C) **正解**:長期病程、反覆惡化緩解交替。
(D) 錯:主症狀為**多灶性**骨痛，非彌漫性；且此描述不若「反覆惡化」精確。
