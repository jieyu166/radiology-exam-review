#交換 #2017交換 #CH Regarding pulmonary amyloidosis, which of the following statement is NOT correct?
(A) Amyloid has typical birefringence with polarization microscopy.
(B) It can occur in the tracheobronchial tree as well as lung parenchyma.
(C) In nodular form, calcification occurs in 30-50% of patients.
(D) All of the above are correct.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**（以上皆正確）。amyloid 偏光顯微鏡有 birefringence(A)、可侵犯氣管支氣管樹與肺實質(B)、結節型約 30-50% 鈣化(C)，三者皆正確。
