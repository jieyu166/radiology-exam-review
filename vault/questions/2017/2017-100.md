#交換 #2017交換 #NR About brain tumors, which of the following was INCORRECT?
(A) Pilocytic astrocytoma most commonly occurs in the cerebellum.
(B) Pleomorphic xanthoastrocytoma is found almost exclusively in young adults.
(C) All diffusely infiltrating astrocytomas have an inherent tendency to malignant progression.
(D) Ganglioglioma most commonly occurs in the deep hemisphere.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**（不正確）。**Ganglioglioma 為皮質腫瘤、最常見於顳葉(~70%)**，非深部半球。pilocytic→小腦(A)、PXA→年輕成人(B)、瀰漫浸潤型有惡性進展傾向(C)皆正確。
