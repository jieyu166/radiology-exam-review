#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Regarding pseudomyxoma peritonei, which one is **WRONG**?
(A) Scalloping of the liver and spleen.
(B) May contain septa and calcification.
(C) More common in male.
(D) May originate from a lesion of the appendix.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**（為非）。Pseudomyxoma peritonei **女略多於男（M:F ≈ 9:11）**。scalloping of liver/spleen(A)、含分隔與鈣化(B)、源自闌尾(D)皆正確。
