#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Which of the below are not characteristic findings of endometriomas?
(A) A cystic lesion hyperintense on T1WI with "shading" or relative hypointense on T2WI images
(B) Multiple hyperintense lesions on T1WI
(C) Cystic lesions hyperintense on T1WI and hyperintense without shading on T2WI (old haemorrhage occasionally appears hyperintense)
(D) Flow voids within the cystic lesion
??
**Ans: D**
**D（囊內流空訊號 flow voids）非典型**——endometrioma為無血管性囊腫，內部不應出現流空訊號。
(A) T1高訊號伴shading或相對T2低訊號:典型。
(B) 多發T1高訊號病灶:典型。
(C) T1/T2高訊號無shading(舊出血):可見。
(D) **正解**:無血管性囊腫不應有流空訊號。
