#交換 #2017交換 #H&N A 13-year-old male presents with recurrent epistaxis. CT shows a highly vascular mass in the nasopharynx, with widening of the pterygopalatine fossa and invasion of the sphenoid sinus. Which arterial branch is the feeding vessel likely to be arising from?
(A) ascending pharyngeal artery
(B) facial artery
(C) Superficial temporal artery
(D) Internal maxillary artery
(E) Internal carotid artery
??
**Ans: D**
**D（Internal maxillary artery）**——JNA主要供血來自同側IMA遠端分支(尤其sphenopalatine、descending palatine、posterior superior alveolar)。
(A) Ascending pharyngeal artery:可提供次要供血,非主要。
(B)(C)(E) 皆非JNA主要供血動脈。
(D) **正解**。
