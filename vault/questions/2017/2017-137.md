#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Which of the following is **WRONG** about peritoneal inclusion cysts?
(A) Most commonly seen in postmenopausal women.
(B) Have a spider web appearance.
(C) Have no malignant potential.
(D) Have no enhancing solid components.
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**（錯誤）。腹膜包涵囊腫好發**停經前(premenopausal)**且有骨盆手術/PID/內膜異位史者，非停經後。spider web 外觀(B)、無惡性潛能(C)、無強化實質(D)皆正確。
