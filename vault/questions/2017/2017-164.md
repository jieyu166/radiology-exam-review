#交換 #2017交換 #Physics Regarding the use of MRI in pregnant patients, which statement is the MOST inappropriate?
(A) One should first consider whether ultrasound is sufficient for clinical evaluation.
(B) If contrast is necessary, consider whether termination of pregnancy before the exam is possible.
(C) For acute brain or spinal cord symptoms, MRI should be performed regardless of gestational age or prior ultrasound.
(D) For acute abdominal symptoms, MRI should be performed regardless of gestational age or prior ultrasound.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**（最不恰當）。急性腹症 **US 多足夠**，不宜逕用 MRI。先評估 US(A)、釓有必要時權衡(B)、急性腦/脊髓症狀用 MRI(C)皆恰當。
