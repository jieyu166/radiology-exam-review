#交換 #2017交換 #2018交換 #Breast Which statement about breast MRI is **WRONG**?
(A) Peak enhancement is usually reached at 2-3 minutes after bolus injection
(B) A washout kinetic curve is defined as a decrease of more than 20% signal intensity after peak enhancement is reached
(C) Many malignant lesions exhibit a washout kinetic curve
(D) Plateau kinetic curve can be seen with both benign and malignancy lesions
(E) Elevated choline on 1H-MRS is an indicator of increased cellular proliferation
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**（錯誤）。**washout 定義為峰後訊號下降『>10%』**,非 >20%。峰值 2–3 分(A)、washout 惡性常見(C)、plateau 良惡皆可(D)、choline 升高＝細胞增生(E)皆正確。
