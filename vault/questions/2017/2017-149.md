#交換 #2017交換 #NR Regarding hypoxic ischemic encephalopathy (HIE), which one is **WRONG**?
(A) Mild HIE may affect only the watershed zones.
(B) Hippocampi may be affected in severe HIE.
(C) White cerebellum sign suggests good prognosis.
(D) Delayed leukoencephalopathy may result in diffuse T2 prolongation in the subcortical white matter.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**（為非）。**White cerebellum sign 代表嚴重 HIE、預後差**，非預後好。輕度影響 watershed(A)、嚴重影響海馬(B)、delayed leukoencephalopathy 致皮質下白質 T2 延長(D)皆正確。
