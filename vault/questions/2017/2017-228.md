#交換 #2017交換 #MSK Which of the following statements is **wrong** about ADC values?
(A) Soft-tissue masses composed primarily of fluid (eg, ganglia or paralabral cysts) exhibit high ADC values.
(B) It is needed to consider myxoid and nonmyxoid tumors separately, since a myxoid matrix will result in increased ADC values.
(C) In malignant bone lesions, the ADC values are usually lower than those in normal fatty marrow.
(D) Hematomas may demonstrate low ADC values at DW imaging.
??
**Ans: C**
**骨內惡性/高細胞病灶的 ADC 值反而『高於』正常脂肪骨髓**（脂肪骨髓 ADC 極低），與軟組織惡性病灶 ADC 低相反。
(A) 正確:液性軟組織腫塊（ganglion/paralabral cyst）ADC 高。
(B) 正確:myxoid 基質使 ADC 升高,需與 nonmyxoid 分開判讀。
(C) **錯誤（正解）**:骨惡性病灶 ADC 高於正常脂肪骨髓,非低於。
(D) 正確:血腫可呈低 ADC。
