#交換 #2017交換 #Breast 以下何種描述 『**不**』符合BI-RADS 2013 lexicon for mammography finding?
(A) An oval circumscribed fat containing nodule
(B) Developing asymmetry
(C) Segmental fine linear calcifications
(D) Intramammary lymph node
(E) None of the above
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**（不符 lexicon）。**「nodule」非 BI-RADS 用語,應用「mass」**。developing asymmetry(B)、segmental fine linear calcifications(C)、intramammary lymph node(D)皆為合法 lexicon。
