#交換 #2016交換 #2017交換 #CH Which of the following statements about 2011 classification of the spectrum of adenocarcinoma of the Lung is **true** except for?
(A) Focal interstial fibrosis
(B) Adenocarcinoma in situ (AIS)
(C) Minimally invasive adenocarcinoma (MIA)
(D) atypical adenomatous hyperplasia (AAH)
??
**Ans: A**
**Focal interstitial fibrosis（局部間質纖維化）並非 2011 肺腺癌分類光譜的成員**。
2011 IASLC/ATS/ERS 肺腺癌光譜為 AAH → AIS → MIA → invasive adenocarcinoma。
(A) **非成員（正解）**:focal interstitial fibrosis 不屬此分類。
(B) AIS、(C) MIA、(D) AAH 皆為光譜成員。
