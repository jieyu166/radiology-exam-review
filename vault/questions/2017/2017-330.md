#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Which one is the characteristic of pheochromocytoma?
(A) GRE T1-weighted chemical shift out-of-phase imaging shows loss of signal intensity (pheochromocytomas do not contain a large amount of intracellular lipid)
(B) Most lesions show intense enhancement after the administration of contrast material
(C) Most lesions demonstrate low signal intensity on T2-weighted images (high, lightbulb sign)
(D) All of the above
??
**Ans: B**
**B（多數病灶顯著強化）**——pheochromocytoma不含大量微觀脂肪,反相位無訊號掉落;T2WI典型呈高訊號(lightbulb sign),非低訊號。
(A) 反相位不會訊號掉落:A敘述為錯誤描述。
(B) **正解**。
(C) T2應為高訊號非低訊號。
(D) 不適用。
