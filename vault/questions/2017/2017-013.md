#交換 #2017交換 #Breast 下列有關 the features of breast cancers detected in women with implants 何者為**非**?
(A) There is a higher rate of nonpalpable, invasive cancers in women with implants.
(B) The stage distribution of cancers in women with implants is similar to screening populations
(C) The majority of cancers appeared as irregular, non-circumscribed, enhancing masses.
(D) Tumor spread along the implant contour was more likely to be seen with subglandular implants than with subpectoral implants.
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**（非）。隆乳婦女因植入物降低乳攝敏感度,偵測到的癌**多為『可觸及』**,並非有更高比例的『不可觸及』浸潤癌。分期與篩檢族群相近(B)、多為不規則非清楚邊界強化腫塊(C)、subglandular 易沿輪廓擴散(D)皆正確。
