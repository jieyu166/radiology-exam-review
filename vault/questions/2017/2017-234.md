#交換 #2017交換 #Breast Which the following statement is **wrong**? (breast MRI sensitivity)
(A) The sensitivity of MR imaging for the detection of breast cancer is very high, with 90% being the value reported in most studies.
(B) The detection of ductal carcinoma in situ (DCIS), the sensitivity of MR imaging varies between 40% and 100%.
(C) The use of computer-assisted methods such as neural network clustering cannot aid in the assessment of contrast enhancement on breast MR images.
(D) The relatively low specificity of breast MR imaging is a disadvantage, and rigorous criteria have been proposed for the interpretation of breast MR images.
??
**Ans: C**
**電腦輔助方法（CAD／類神經網路 clustering）『可』輔助評估乳房 MRI 的強化**，並非不能。
(A) 正確:MRI 偵測乳癌敏感度高（多數研究約 90%）。
(B) 正確:對 DCIS 敏感度差異大（40-100%）。
(C) **錯誤（正解）**:CAD/類神經網路可輔助評估強化。
(D) 正確:乳房 MRI 特異度較低為缺點,故有嚴格判讀準則。
