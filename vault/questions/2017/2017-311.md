#交換 #2017交換 #ABD A 30-year-old female patient with pelvic pain and irregular periods underwent an ultrasound examination. In the left ovary there was a 4-cm diffuse homogeneous hypoechoic focal lesion with low-level internal echoes. What is the most likely diagnosis?
(A) Endometrioma
(B) Functional ovarian cyst
(C) Polycystic ovary (PCOS)
(D) Ovarian carcinoma
(E) Corpus luteum
??
**Ans: A**
**A（Endometrioma）**——均勻分佈的低位準內部回音(毛玻璃樣外觀)為endometrioma超音波典型特徵性表現。
(A) **正解**。
(B) Functional cyst:多為完全無回音。
(C) PCOS:多發周邊小濾泡非單一均質病灶。
(D) Ovarian carcinoma:常伴實質成分及血流。
(E) Corpus luteum:常呈環狀血流(ring of fire)。
