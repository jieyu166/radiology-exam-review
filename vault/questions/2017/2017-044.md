#交換 #2017交換 #H&N 關於retinoblastoma以下敘述何者為**非**?
(A) Retinoblastoma, which is the most common intraocular pediatric tumor,
(B) Hereditary retinoblastoma is usually bilateral and multifocal.
(C) Optic nerve involvement is a marker for aggressive and advanced tumors and is associated with a high risk of metastatic disease and consequent increased mortality rates
(D) Intraocular hemorrhages and calcifications are depicted best on DWI sequence
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**（為非）。**眼內鈣化以 CT（或 GRE/SWI）顯示最佳，非 DWI**。最常見兒童眼內腫瘤(A)、遺傳型多雙側多灶(B)、視神經侵犯為侵襲性/進展標記(C)皆正確。
