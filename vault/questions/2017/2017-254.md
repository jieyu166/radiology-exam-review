#交換 #2017交換 #MSK All of the following signs are associated with Paget disease EXCEPT:
(A) Blade-of-grass appearance in long bones.
(B) Hole-within-hole appearance in long bones.
(C) Cotton-wool appearance in the skull.
(D) Osteoporosis circumscripta in the skull.
(E) Ivory vertebra.
??
**Ans: B**
**Hole-within-hole appearance 是顱骨嗜伊紅性肉芽腫（Eosinophilic Granuloma, EG）的特徵，非 Paget disease 徵象**——因病灶對顱骨內外板侵犯程度不同，造成雙重輪廓/斜邊外觀。
(A) Blade-of-grass:Paget disease 長骨溶骨前緣。
(B) **Hole-within-hole（正解）**:EG 顱骨特徵，非 Paget。
(C) Cotton-wool:Paget disease 顱骨硬化期。
(D) Osteoporosis circumscripta:Paget disease 顱骨溶骨早期。
(E) Ivory vertebra:Paget disease 脊椎（非特異，也見於淋巴瘤/轉移性攝護腺癌）。
