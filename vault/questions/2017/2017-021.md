#交換 #2017交換 #ABD About solid and pseudopapillary tumor of pancreas, which description is correct:
(A) A common tumor of pancreas
(B) Usually found in young female patient
(C) Usually asymptom due to small size
(D) Highly malignant tumor and easy to metastasis
??
**Ans: B**
A. Rare (1-2% of exocrine pancreatic tumour)
B. Daughter tumor
C. median size ~8 cm. 不過典型是大的且有cystic change；太小會是solid
D. low malignant potential. 雖然大多良性，~15%是惡性的 Complete resection is associated with long-term survival even in the presence of metastatic disease.
From: 詳解, Core Radiology p.109, Radiology Review Manual 電子 p.2515
