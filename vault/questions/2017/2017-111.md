#交換 #2017交換 #US Which of the following is NOT a common ultrasound finding of testicular epidermoid cyst?
(A) Purely cystic nodule.
(B) Mural calcification.
(C) Well-defined margin.
(D) Onion-skin appearance.
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**。睪丸表皮樣囊腫為**實質、onion-skin 層狀腫塊**，非純囊性。mural calcification(~86%，B)、well-defined margin(C)、onion-skin appearance(D)皆為其表現。
