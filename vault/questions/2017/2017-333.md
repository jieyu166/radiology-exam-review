#交換 #2017交換 #IR Using balloon catheter for vascular intervention, which one of the following carry the greatest risk for vascular rupture?
(A) Low compliance balloon in small vessel
(B) Low compliance balloon in large vessel
(C) High compliance balloon in large vessel
(D) High compliance balloon in small vessel
??
**Ans: B**
**B（Low compliance balloon in large vessel）**——低順應性氣球充氣壓力增加時直徑變化小、可耐高壓，用於大血管時若尺寸誤判，破裂風險最高。
(A)(C)(D) 風險相對較低。
(B) **正解**。
