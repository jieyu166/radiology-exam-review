#交換 #2017交換 #2018交換 #Breast Which statement about “background parenchymal enhancement (BPE)” on breast MRI is **WRONG**?
(A) BPE is not necessarily related to the amount of fibroglandular tissue
(B) BPE is more prominent in the follicular phase of the menstrual cycle
(C) Younger patients with dense breasts are more likely to demonstrate BPE
(D) BPE usually demonstrates progressive enhancement over time
(E) None of the above
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**（錯誤）。**BPE 在黃體期(luteal)最明顯**,非 follicular phase。BPE 與纖維腺體量不必然相關(A)、年輕緻密乳房較易顯 BPE(C)、隨時間 progressive(D)皆正確。
