#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Which of the following description is **true**?
(A) GRE T1-weighted chemical shift in-phase and out-of-phase imaging is helpful for differential diagnosis of adrenocortical adenoma
(B) The pheochromocytoma shows the typical features of loss of signal intensity on GRE T1-weighted chemical shift out-of-phase image and intense enhancement on the contrast-enhanced image (no loss of signal on GRE/out of phase)
(C) Adrenal cysts are common and are often detected during radiologic investigation or at autopsy. The frequency of adrenal cysts seen at autopsy studies ranges from 3% to 5% (rare; 0.064-0.018%)
(D) None of the above
??
**Ans: A**
**A（GRE T1化學位移in/out-of-phase成像有助鑑別腎上腺皮質腺瘤）**——腺瘤含微觀脂肪致反相位訊號下降。
(A) **正解**。
(B) Pheochromocytoma不含脂肪,反相位無訊號掉落。
(C) Adrenal cysts屍檢盛行率罕見約0.064-0.018%(非3-5%)。
(D) 不適用。
