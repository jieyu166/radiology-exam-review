#交換 #2017交換 #IR Which one is NOT correct about applying TIPS in portal hypertension management?
(A) TIPS is indicated in management of hepatic and posthepatic portal hypertension but not indicated in prehepatic portal hypertension.
(B) TIPS is indicated in primary prophylaxis of variceal bleeding.
(C) Severe liver failure such as Child-Pugh class C, MELD>22, bilirubin >3 mg/dL is not indicated for TIPS
(D) The strongest evidence of indication of TIPS is secondary prophylaxis of variceal bleeding.
??
**Ans: B**
**TIPS 最強實證的適應症是次級預防（已出血過、治療失敗者）,並非初級預防**。
(A) 正確:TIPS 適用肝性/肝後性門脈高壓,不適用肝前性。
(B) **不正確（正解）**:TIPS 不用於初級預防,而是次級預防。
(C) 正確:嚴重肝衰竭（Child-Pugh C、MELD>22、bilirubin>3）不建議 TIPS。
(D) 正確:次級預防為 TIPS 最強實證適應症。
