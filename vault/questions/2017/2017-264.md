#交換 #2017交換 #ABD About the Henoch-Schonlein purpura (HSP), which of the following description is correct?
(A) HSP is the most common vasculitis of childhood and is an acute, systemic, progressive, small-vessel vasculitis.
(B) Bowel angina occurs in 100%.
(C) Bowel involvement confining to a single vascular territory.
(D) Long segments wall thickening of small bowel, fluid filled small bowel and ascites are diagnostic findings.
??
**Ans: D**
**長節段小腸壁增厚、充液小腸與腹水為 HSP 的診斷性影像表現**。
(A) 錯:早發型（early childhood onset）本質上為**非進行性**，非「進行性」。
(B) 錯:GI 症狀約發生於**一半**兒童，非100%。
(C) 錯:腸道侵犯呈**跳躍性病灶**，**不侷限**於單一血管領域。
(D) **正解**。
