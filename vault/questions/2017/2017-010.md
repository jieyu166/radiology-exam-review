#交換 #2017交換 #Breast Which statement is **not true** for background parenchymal enhancement (BPE) at breast MR imaging?
(A) BPE commonly begins in the peripheral breast tissue and gradually becomes apparent in the more central breast tissue.
(B) Non-urgent breast MR imaging should be scheduled during the 2nd week of the menstrual cycle (days 7-15).
(C) Bilateral similar areas of enhancement, regardless of distribution, are more characteristic of benign enhancement than of malignancy.
(D) BPE is proportional to mammographic density.
??
**Ans: BD**
正解 **D**（非真）。**BPE 與 mammographic density 不必然相關**（非成正比）;extremely dense 可僅輕微 BPE。BPE 始於周邊(A)、雙側相似強化偏良性(C)皆真。
