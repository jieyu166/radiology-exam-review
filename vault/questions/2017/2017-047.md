#交換 #2017交換 #MSK Which of the following statement about Baker’s cyst is **true**?
(A) They’re very rare, found in less than 1% of patient undergoing MRI of knee.
(B) They communicate with joint capsule via the gap between gracilis and sartorius tendons.
(C) On sonography, presence of fluid between the semimembranosus and medial gastrocnemius tendons is a helpful feature for the diagnosis.
(D) A popliteal lesion with internal echogenicity on sonography excludes the possibility of Baker’s cyst.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**。**US 上液體位於 semimembranosus 與 medial gastrocnemius 之間為診斷性**。Baker cyst 很常見(非 <1%,A 誤)、經 SM-GN 間相通(非 gracilis-sartorius,B 誤)、有內部回音不能排除(D 誤)。
