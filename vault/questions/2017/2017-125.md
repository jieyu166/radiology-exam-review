#交換 #2017交換 #NR Which is a component of the triangle of Guillain and Mollaret?
(A) Putamen.
(B) Caudate nucleus.
(C) Substantia nigra.
(D) Red nucleus.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**。Guillain-Mollaret triangle＝**red nucleus（紅核）＋inferior olivary nucleus（下橄欖核）＋contralateral dentate nucleus（對側齒狀核）**。putamen、caudate、substantia nigra 非其組成。
