#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Concerning the appendix, which of the following is **true**?
(A) Contains taeniae coli
(B) Most commonly retrocecal
(C) Directed toward the side of the ileocecal valve
(D) Supplied by branch of the inferior mesenteric artery
(E) Does not contain a serosal layer
??
**Ans: B**
⚠️ 官方答案標示存疑（原標C後改註B?），依解剖教材最支持者為**Most commonly retrocecal（B，約65-70%）**。
(A) 闌尾壁本身不含獨立teniae coli構造。
(B) **正解**:retrocecal最常見位置。
(C) 根部位於迴盲瓣下方2-3cm,非直接指向其側。
(D) 血供來自SMA,非IMA。
(E) 闌尾具漿膜層覆蓋。
