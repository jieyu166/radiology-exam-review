#交換 #2017交換 #Breast Which one is **false** in “male breast cancer”?
(A) Klinefelter's syndrome increase the risk of breast cancer.
(B) Overall prognosis is better than in women.
(C) Average age of diagnosis range 60-64 years, later than in women.
(D) Usually presented with a subareolar mass without calcifications on mammogram.
??
**Ans: B**
男性乳癌預後與**同齡、同分期女性相近,並非較好**;男性常較晚期診斷。
(A) 正確:Klinefelter 症候群增加乳癌風險。
(B) **為偽（正解）**:男性乳癌預後與同齡同期女性相近,且常因較少回報症狀而較晚期診斷,並非比女性好。
(C) 正確:平均診斷年齡 60-64 歲,晚於女性。
(D) 正確:典型呈乳暈下無鈣化腫塊（可被 gynecomastia 遮蔽）。
