#交換 #2017交換 #ABD What is the most likely cause of increased signal intensity within an adrenal pseudocyst on T1-weighted MR images?
(A) Fat.
(B) Hemorrhage.
(C) Calcification.
(D) Simple fluid.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**。Adrenal pseudocyst T1WI 內高訊號最可能源自**出血(hemorrhage)**（假性囊腫常為出血後）。非脂肪、鈣化或單純液。
