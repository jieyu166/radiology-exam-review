#交換 #2017交換 #NR Which statement of malignant melanoma in the head is **wrong**?
(A) Include the melanotic and amelanotic patterns
(B) High signal intensity on T1-weighted images of melanotic pattern
(C) Low signal intensity on T2-weighted images of amelanotic pattern
(D) None of above
??
**Ans: C**
⚠️ 官方原答案標示B、後更正為C為偽。
(A) 包含melanotic與amelanotic兩型態:真。
(B) Melanotic pattern T1高訊號:真(黑色素順磁效應)。
(C) **正解(偽)**:Amelanotic pattern於T2WI應呈高至等訊號,非低訊號。
(D) 不適用。
