#交換 #2017交換 #NR Which of the following statements about central neurocytoma is **FALSE**?
(A) Central neurocytomas are well-circumscribed, lobulated masses that frequently have cystlike areas
(B) The term central neurocytoma is exclusively reserved for neurocytomas that occur in the ventricular system
(C) At contrast-enhanced imaging, the enhancement pattern is variable, but moderate to strong enhancement is typically seen
(D) Recent evaluation with MR spectroscopy has revealed the presence of glutamine (glycine, 3.55 ppm) within central neurocytomas which help differentiate them from other intraventricular neoplasm
??
**Ans: D**
**D為偽**——MRS鑑別特徵峰為glycine(3.55 ppm),非glutamine。
(A) 邊界清楚分葉狀伴囊狀變:真。
(B) 命名專指腦室內病灶:真。
(C) 強化多變但典型中重度強化:真。
(D) **正解(偽)**:應為glycine非glutamine。
