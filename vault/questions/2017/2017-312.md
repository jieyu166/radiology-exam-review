#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Which statement of extraadrenal paraganglioma is **wrong**?
(A) Most arise in fourth or fifth decades in life
(B) Carney's triad consists of extraadrenal paraganglioma, pulmonary chondroma and gastrointestinal stromal tumor (gastric GIST)
(C) Hyperintense on T2-weighted MR images
(D) These tumors are frequently found in the organs of Zuckerkandl
??
**Ans: B**
**B為偽**——Carney triad正確組成為gastric GIST(非泛指一般消化道基質瘤)、pulmonary chondroma、extraadrenal paraganglioma。
(A) 多數40-50歲診斷:真。
(B) **正解(偽)**:應specifically為gastric GIST。
(C) T2高訊號:真。
(D) 好發Zuckerkandl器官:真。
