#交換 #2017交換 #PED Which following description about Congenital diaphragmatic hernia (CDH) is not **true**
(A) Bochdalek's hernia is usually involved left-posterior diaphragm
(B) Bochdalek's hernia is most common type of CDH
(C) Morgagni hernia is usually involved right-posterior diaphragm (anterior, right-sided ~90%, small, rare ~2% of CDH, at low risk of prolapse)
(D) CNS abnormality is associated
??
**Ans: C**
**C為偽**——Morgagni hernia為前方(非後方)缺損,好發右側(約90%)。
(A)(B) Bochdalek's hernia左後外側且最常見:真。
(C) **正解(偽)**:應為前方非後方。
(D) CDH可合併CNS異常(約10%非症候群性病例):真。
