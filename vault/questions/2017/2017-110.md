#交換 #2017交換 #Breast Regarding interpretation of breast MRI, which is **WRONG**?
(A) The most fundamental required pulse sequence is dynamic contrast-enhanced MRI (DCE MRI).
(B) Micrometastatic lymph nodes can be clearly seen on MRI.
(C) Malignant lesions may show washout, plateau, or even persistent kinetics on DCE MRI.
(D) DCIS may show linear or segmental clumped enhancement on MRI.
(E) Morphology and kinetics are the two major criteria for interpreting breast MRI.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**（為非）。**MRI 無法清楚偵測淋巴結微轉移**。DCE MRI 為必備序列(A)、惡性可呈 washout/plateau/persistent(C)、DCIS 呈 linear/segmental clumped(D)、morphology＋kinetic 為兩大標準(E)皆正確。
