#交換 #2017交換 #NR MRI is the most sensitive tool to detect brain metastasis, but not perfect. Which strategy can NOT increase sensitivity for detection of brain or leptomeningeal metastasis?
(A) Increased dosage of contrast medium.
(B) Add FLAIR image after contrast medium injection.
(C) Use 3D T1W imaging with thin-slice delayed image.
(D) Add DWI image after contrast injection.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**。**顯影後加 DWI 不提升腦/軟腦膜轉移偵測敏感度**。加大對比劑量(A)、post-contrast FLAIR(B)、3D T1W 薄切延遲(C)皆可提升。
