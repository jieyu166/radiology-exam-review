#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Which statement regarding ultrasound appearance of hepatic hemangioma is **WRONG**?
(A) Typically hyperechoic when the tumor is smaller than 2.5 cm.
(B) Increased echogenicity of the tumor following progression of fatty liver.
(C) Heterogeneous internal echoes are more commonly seen in larger tumors.
(D) May have a thin echogenic rim around the tumor.
??
**Ans: B**
正解 **B**（錯誤）。脂肪肝進展使肝背景變亮，血管瘤**相對回音『降低』**（可呈 hypoechoic）。<2.5 cm 多高回音(A)、較大者異質回音(C)、薄 echogenic rim(D)皆正確。
