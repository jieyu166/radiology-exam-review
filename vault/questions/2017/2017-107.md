#交換 #2017交換 #ABD About the TNM staging system for esophageal cancer (AJCC 7th edition), which statement is **WRONG**?
(A) T4b tumors are unresectable cancers invading adjacent structures such as aorta, carotid vessels, trachea, left main bronchus, or vertebral body.
(B) Regional lymph nodes include any paraesophageal lymph nodes from cervical to celiac nodes.
(C) Cancer location is determined by where the tumor occupies the largest mass volume, not the position of the upper edge.
(D) EGJ tumors are staged as esophageal cancer if the epicenter is within the proximal 5 cm of the stomach and the tumor extends into the esophagus.
??
**Ans: C**
正解 **C**（錯誤）。食道癌**位置由 tumor epicenter（中心）決定**，非「佔最大體積處」。regional node 範圍(B)、EGJ 近端 5 cm 規則(D)、T4 侵犯定義(A)皆正確。
