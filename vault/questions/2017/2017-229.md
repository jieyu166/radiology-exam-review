#交換 #2017交換 #MSK Which of the following statements is **wrong** about recurrent soft tissue sarcoma?
(A) The complete absence of fluid signal in the surgical bed has been suggested as an excellent sign to rule out the presence of recurrent tumor.
(B) A recurrent tumor is characterized by early and rapid arterial phase enhancement at DCE (dynamic contrast material-enhanced) MR imaging.
(C) A recurrent tumor is characterized by low ADC values at DW imaging.
(D) Postoperative inflammation and fibrosis never have a nodular or masslike appearance.
??
**Ans: D**
**術後發炎與纖維化『可』呈結節或腫塊樣外觀，並非『絕不（never）』**——故 D 為錯。
(A) 正確:手術床完全無液訊號為排除復發的良好徵象。
(B) 正確:復發腫瘤呈早期快速動脈期強化（DCE）。
(C) 正確:復發腫瘤（高細胞）呈低 ADC。
(D) **錯誤（正解）**:術後發炎/纖維化可呈結節/腫塊樣,「never」過絕對。
