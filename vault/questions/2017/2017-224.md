#交換 #2017交換 #ABD Which one of the following is not **true** for ureteral duplication?
(A) Ureteral duplication is more common in males.
(B) When bilateral and complete, all four ureters may open orthotopically on the bladder trigone.
(C) In keeping with the Weigert-Meyer law the upper pole ureter typically opens medially while the lower pole ureter opens laterally.
(D) Complete ureteral duplication may be associated with other congenital anomalies such as an upper moiety ureter with a ureterocele causing obstruction.
??
**Ans: A**
**輸尿管重複『女性較常見』，非男性**。
(A) **非真（正解）**:女性較常見,非男性。
(B) 正確:雙側完全重複時四條輸尿管可正位開於膀胱三角。
(C) 正確:Weigert-Meyer 法則——上極輸尿管開口偏內（下）、下極偏外（上）。
(D) 正確:完全重複可合併其他先天異常（上極 ureterocele 阻塞）。
