#交換 #2017交換 #Physics Which of the following adjustments would reduce sound attenuation in the body most?
(A) Increasing receiver gain
(B) Decreasing transmit power
(C) Increasing dynamic range
(D) Decreasing frequency
(E) Decreasing focus
??
**Ans: D**
聲波衰減係數隨頻率升高而增加,故**降低頻率最能減少體內聲波衰減**(代價為解析度下降)。
(A) Increasing receiver gain:只放大接收訊號(含雜訊),不改變組織衰減本身。
(B) Decreasing transmit power:降低發射能量,不改變衰減係數。
(C) Increasing dynamic range:改變灰階顯示範圍,與衰減無關。
(D) **Decreasing frequency（正解）**:attenuation coefficient 隨頻率上升,降頻 → 衰減減少、穿透加深。
(E) Decreasing focus:聚焦深度設定,與整體衰減無關。
