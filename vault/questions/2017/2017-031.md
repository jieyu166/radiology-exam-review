#交換 #2017交換 #CH Which following disease is IgG4 related disease:
(A) Kikuchi-Fujimoto disease
(B) Castleman disease
(C) Kimura disease
(D) Mikulicz disease
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**。**Mikulicz disease（淚腺/唾液腺）屬 IgG4-related disease**。Kikuchi-Fujimoto（壞死性淋巴結炎，A）、Castleman（B）、Kimura（C）皆非 IgG4-RD。
