#交換 #2017交換 #Unknown Which one of the followings is least likely to have **crazy paving sign** on HRCT images?
(A) Nonspecific interstitial pneumonitis
(B) Cryptogenic organizing pneumonia
(C) Pneumocystis pneumonia
(D) Respiratory bronchiolitis superimposed on interstitial lung disease
??
**Ans: D**
看Radiopaedia AD難以選擇. 根據 [[2003 RG Crazy-Paving Pattern at Thin-Section CT of the Lungs Radiologic Pathologic Overview.pdf]]有探討NSIP但沒提到RB-ILD，所以RB-ILD機會更低。
Pneumocystis=PcP=PjP
[Crazy paving](https://radiopaedia.org/articles/crazy-paving)
Common causes:
- Pulmonary edema 現實最常見
- ARDS, Acute interstitial pneumonia (ARDS of unknown etiology)
- [[Pulmonary alveolar proteinosis]] (PAP): rare, 但大部分PAP都有crazy paving
- bacterial pneumonia
Less common causes:
- pneumonitis (drug-induced, radiation)
- Pulmonary hemorrhage
	- Goodpasture syndrome
- Chronic eosinophilic pneumonia
- UIP + alveolar hemorrhage
-感染 (mycoplasma, TB, PjP, COVID-19)
- COP, formerly BOOP
- invasive mucinous adenocarcinoma of the lung
- sarcoidosis, esp. alveolar sarcoidosis
- lipoid pneumonia
- pulmonary veno-occlusive disease
