#交換 #2017交換 #CH Which one of the followings is least likely to have **crazy paving sign** on HRCT images?
(A) Nonspecific interstitial pneumonitis
(B) Cryptogenic organizing pneumonia
(C) Pneumocystis pneumonia
(D) Respiratory bronchiolitis superimposed on interstitial lung disease
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**（最不可能）。**RB-ILD 以氣道中心結節為主、非典型 crazy-paving**。NSIP(A)、COP(B)、PCP(C)皆可呈 crazy-paving。
