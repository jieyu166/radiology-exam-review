#交換 #2017交換 #CH Which one is **WRONG** about the typical morphological classification of bronchiectasis?
(A) Cylindrical.
(B) Varicose.
(C) Cystic.
(D) Round.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**。支氣管擴張典型形態分型為 **cylindrical、varicose、cystic** 三型;Round 非分型。
