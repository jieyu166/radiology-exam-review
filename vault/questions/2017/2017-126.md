#交換 #2017交換 #ABD According to UCSF and Milan criteria, which patient is suitable for liver transplantation?
(A) Single HCC (7.0 cm).
(B) 2 HCCs (5.0 cm and 1.0 cm).
(C) Single cholangiocarcinoma (3.0 cm).
(D) 3 HCCs (1.5 cm, 3.0 cm, 2.0 cm).
(E) 4 HCCs (1.0, 1.0, 2.0, 2.0 cm).
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**。3 顆 HCC（1.5/3.0/2.0 cm）符合 **Milan（≤3 顆、各 ≤3 cm）與 UCSF（總和 <8 cm）**。單一 7 cm(A)、含 5 cm 顆(B)、膽管癌(C)、4 顆(E)皆不符。
