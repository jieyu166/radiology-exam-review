#交換 #2017交換 #CV Which is **wrong** about hypertrophic cardiomyopathy?
(A) Hypertrophic cardiomyopathy (HCM) is a heterogeneous group of diseases related to sarcomere gene mutations exhibiting heterogeneous phenotypes with an autosomal dominant mendelian pattern of inheritance.
(B) The disease is characterized by left ventricular (LV) hypertrophy in the absence of another systemic or cardiac disease to account for the changes noted.
(C) The asymmetric septal form is the most common morphologic variant of HCM and accounts for up to 60%–70% of cases.
(D) The typical pattern of LGE in HCM is subendocardial hyperenhancement with coronary distribution.
??
**Ans: D**
正解 **D**（錯誤）。**HCM 的 LGE 典型為 midwall patchy/punctate（非 subendocardial 冠狀動脈分布；後者提示缺血）**。肌節疾病(A)、LV 肥厚無其他負荷(B)、asymmetric septal 最常見(C)皆正確。
