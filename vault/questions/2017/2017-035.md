#交換 #2017交換 #CV Which of the following modality most accurately measure right ventricular volumes and ejection fraction?
(A) Cardiac MR imaging.
(B) Cardiac echography.
(C) Cardiac CT.
(D) Catheter right ventriculography.
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**。**Cardiac MR imaging 為量測 RV 容積與 EF 的 gold standard**（cine 短軸堆疊、無幾何假設）。echo(幾何假設)、CT(輻射/時間解析)、導管 RV 攝影(侵入)皆較不準。
