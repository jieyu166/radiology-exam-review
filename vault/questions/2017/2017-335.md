#交換 #2017交換 #PED Which description about "neonatal lung disease" is incorrect?
(A) The cause of hyaline membrane disease (HMD) (or respiratory distress syndrome (RDS)) is immature type II alveolar cells
(B) Pleural effusions should not be seen with HMD
(C) Retained fetal lung fluid (transient tachypnea of newborn) is caused by inadequate clearance of lung fluid prior to first breath
(D) Meconium aspiration syndrome usual in preterm infants
??
**Ans: D**
**D為偽**——MAS好發足月或過期產嬰兒(非早產兒),發生率隨妊娠週數增加而上升(31週後遞增)。
(A) HMD因第二型肺泡細胞不成熟:真。
(B) HMD不應合併肋膜積液:真。
(C) TTN因出生前肺液清除不完全:真。
(D) **正解(偽)**。
