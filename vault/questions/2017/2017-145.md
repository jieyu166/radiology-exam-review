#交換 #2017交換 #MSK Which one of the following tumors does NOT usually involve the epiphysis of a long bone?
(A) Ewing sarcoma.
(B) Chondroblastoma.
(C) Giant cell tumor.
(D) Osteosarcoma.
??
**Ans: A**
正解 **A**。**Ewing sarcoma 好發骨幹/metadiaphysis（骨骺僅 1–2%）**，不常侵犯骨骺。chondroblastoma（骨骺未閉）、giant cell tumor（骨骺已閉）、osteosarcoma（可延伸）較會侵犯骨骺。
