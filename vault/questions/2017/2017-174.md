#交換 #2017交換 #Breast Which statement is not correct for mastitis?
(A) Most cases of mastitis occur during lactation, are pyogenic, and are caused by Staphylococcus aureus and Streptococcus species.
(B) If erythema and swelling are present without an abscess, skin punch biopsy is recommended to differentiate between infection and malignancy.
(C) Breast abscesses usually appear as noncalcified oval masses with indistinct margins on mammograms.
(D) MRI is the best imaging modality to assess breast abscesses.
??
**Ans: D**
懷疑乳房膿瘍時 **US 才是最有用的初始影像與監測工具,非 MRI**。
(A) 正確:多數乳腺炎發生於哺乳期、為化膿性,主因 Staph. aureus 與 Streptococcus。
(B) 正確:紅腫但無膿瘍時 punch biopsy 可區分感染與惡性（發炎性乳癌）。
(C) 正確:膿瘍常呈非鈣化、邊緣不清的卵圓形腫塊。
(D) **不正確（正解）**:US 為懷疑乳房膿瘍之首選影像,也是監測治療反應與緩解的首選,非 MRI。
